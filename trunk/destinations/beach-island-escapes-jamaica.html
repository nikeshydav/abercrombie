Travel To Jamaica


Addictive sun rays, sugary sands and pampered resort life. Welcome to Jamaica, located almost in the center of the Caribbean Sea. The third largest island in the Caribbean, Jamaica, with its varied and luxuriant landscape is considered by many to be one of the most beautiful. Nowhere else in the Caribbean is the similarity between Africa seen and felt more than in Jamaica. A melting pot of African, Asian, European and Middle Eastern cultures, this diversity can be experienced in its beliefs, performing arts and some very unique dining options. Not one to blend in easily with the rest of the islands in the Caribbean archipelago, Jamaica is set apart historically and culturally.

Take your holidays in Jamaica to new heights with Abercrombie and Kent as you embark on the journey of a lifetime. A holiday or a honeymoon to this beautiful country is a cut above, providing authentic tastes of local culture and cuisine, as well as the relaxed exclusivity of the finest beaches and hotels.

Unwind over a soothing cup of coffee at the Blue Mountains famed for having the world's best coffee. Rivers and waterfalls are home to some 250 bird species and 3,000 varieties of tropical plants. High above, the rainforests lie hidden in clouds whereas down below, secluded rocky coves shroud white sandy beaches.

Tropical treats like ice cold jelly from a colorful fruit stand or ackee and saltfish with breadfruit are must-haves. The national dish, ripened fruit from the ackee tree is combined with saltfish and seasonings to create a mouth-watering breakfast item. Treat yourself to some fine rum from Jamaica famed for its sugar plantation heritage. Hospitable fishing villages, gurgling waterfalls, buzzing, cosmopolitan cities, unforgettable sunsets. this country has it all. Love for the game of cricket, afternoon tea and colonial houses - some echoes from the British Rule can still be spotted - and blend in well with the legendary culture of the reggae imparting a distinctive face to this inimitable island. Travel to Jamaica, a country that has enough variety to comprise many utterly distinct vacations.
