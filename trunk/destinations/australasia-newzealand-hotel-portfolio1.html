Suggested South Island accommodation

  • Azur Resort

    Azur Resort in Queenstown gives you a unique experience. Awaken to the mountain sunrise in your private luxury lodge. This nine villa lodge is situated in the Sunshine Bay area offering sensational views of Lake Wakatipu, The Remarkables and Queenstown.

    Claremont Country Estate

    Claremont offers luxury lodge accommodation in spectacular countryside near Christchurch in Canterbury. Each room, including the guest bedrooms, is elegantly furnished with antiques. The complimentary two hour guided 4WD scenic tour of the lodge estate is not to be missed.

    Edenhouse

    Edenhouse was built in 2003 for Peter and Bobbie Martin who personally look after each guest. They moved to the Orinoco Valley in 2002 after living in England for 18 years.

    Peter, an Australian, traded financial markets and Bobbie, a Kiwi, established her own interior design business in Knightsbridge London after studying at the Inchbald School of Design. She has designed both Edenhouse and the gardens.

    Edenhouse is finished and furnished to a very high standard with an emphasis on comfort, views and sunshine. The 48-acre grounds currently farm 37 cattle and 24 sheep. Mount Crusader, which is in the vista from the front of the house, features in the film " Lord of the Rings".

    Kapitea Ridge

    Kapitea Ridge, located on the West Coast of New Zealand between Hokitika and Greymouth, is an intimate lodge with just six stylish individually decorated rooms. A fine collection of New Zealand art is show cased throughout the property.

    There are eight hectares of coastal land and indigeneous forests to explore by mountain bike or on foot or you may prefer to relax on the privacy of your balcony and take in the incredible views. The West Coast has plenty of activities available, including quad biking tours, adventure caving and scenic flights over this rugged landscape. Kapitea Ridge is an ideal stop enroute to or from the dramatic glaciers.

    Matakauri Lodge

    Spectacularly situated on Lake Wakatipu, Matakauri Lodge offers stunning views from every angle, taking in the Remarkable, Cecil and Walter Peak mountain ranges.

    Only a seven minute journey from Queenstown, Matakauri Lodge will provides a lakeside retreat nestled in one of the world's most serenely beautiful landscapes.

    The Lodge and adjoining villas, originally constructed in 2000 with a contemporary New Zealand design, reopened in August 2010 after undergoing extensive refurbishment and new interior design by acclaimed New Zealand Interior Designer, Virginia Fisher.

    Otahuna Lodge

    Otahuna was built in 1895 for prominent Canterbury pioneer Sir Heaton Rhodes. An enduring testament to his love for the finer things in life, the homestead is considered one of the best examples of unspoiled Queen Anne architecture in Australasia and is protected by a Category One listing from the New Zealand Historic Places Trust.

    Otahuna Lodge is an intimate, exclusive retreat offering seven individual suites. The 2 master suites and 5 suites retain grand architectural features from the 19th century such as ornate wood-burning fireplaces, carved inglenooks and stained glass windows.

    Remarkables Lodge

    Remarkables Lodge has all the facilities of a small luxury hotel but with the intimacy and hospitable ambience of a country estate, epitomising the New Zealand luxury lodge experience. Just seven individual suites with personal touches such as cookies at bedtime that will make your stay truly memorable.

    The George Hotel

    The George is a boutique hotel in the heart of the city. A member of 'Small Luxury Hotels of the World', the George has two chic and sophisticated award winning restaurants; Pescatore and 50 on Park. The 57 deluxe rooms and suites are indulgent and luxurious, a perfect base for this wonderful city.

    Timara Lodge

    Timara Lodge houses just four rooms and enjoys a charming country setting in the picturesque Marlborough wine region. Here you'll find a swimming pool, tennis court and a man-made lake.

    It is set amongst 600 acres of land, including 25 acres of one of New Zealand's most beautiful English style gardens and a five-acre, man-made lake. Timara Lodge's accommodation facilities ensure your stay is private, comfortable and peaceful. With a warm, unobtrusive welcome, delightful company and culinary delights, Timara Lodge is truly a 'slice of paradise' in Marlborough, New Zealand.

    Whare Kea Lodge

    On the shore of Lake Wanaka, deep in New Zealand's Southern Alps, Whare Kea Lodge is the perfect base for beautiful lakeside walks, fishing, horseriding, skiing, golf and vineyard touring.

    Each cottage or house is within easy walking distance of the main lodge, the heart of Wharekauhau. Modelled on an Edwardian country mansion, the lodge was designed by Queenstown architect Fred van Brandenburg, and it is here that clients dine and mingle. The fine design, sumptuous furnishings, exquisite tableware and outstanding cuisine all combine to satisfy the senses. Renowned for its cuisine, fresh, local produce is used, complemented by a quality wine cellar comprising the finest New Zealand wines.
