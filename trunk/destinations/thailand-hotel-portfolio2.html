Suggested Chiang Rai accommodation

  • Anantara Resort & Spa - Golden Triangle

    Perched on a ridge, amidst 160 acres of tropical jungle overlooking the winding Mekong River, Anantara is a luxury retreat surrounded by outstanding natural beauty. The resort's 58 deluxe guest rooms offer views of three countries from balconies with in-built sofas. For a slice of luxury, we recommend one of the 19 suites with hardwood floors, double balconies and open bathrooms.

    Start the day with a delicious breakfast and a northern Thai sunrise shrouded in mountain mist, then visit the spa or relax by the fabulous pool. Experience the thrill of a longboat ride up the Mekong and the exhilaration of elephant trekking through bamboo jungle.

    Four Seasons Tented Camp, Chiang Rai

    Reached by river boat through exotic bamboo jungles, the Golden Triangle Resort offers its own take on inimitable Four Seasons luxury. With just 15 luxurious tents, it is the height of intimate style and a sense of adventure hangs on the tropical air. Each tent is designed as an open space with a large outdoor deck.

    Furnishings are handcrafted in styles reminiscent of 19th century expecditions, right down to the hand-hammered copper bathtubs. The resort is perfect for active adults seeking once-in-a-lifetime adventure - from elephant mahout training to Golden Triangle excursions. Don't miss the stilt top bar to enjoy a sundowner by the river.
