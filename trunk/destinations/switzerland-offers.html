  • Rest of Europe

    Switzerland is a beautiful country offering a unique blend of French, German and Italian cultures against a backdrop of stunning scenery, magnificent mountains, lakes and meadows. The unspoilt nature and fairytale, picturesque villages can't fail to charm both the active adventurer and those seeking a more relaxing break.

    Our 11-day Tailor-Made Switzerland itinerary allows clients to experience the best of Switzerland's enchanting beauty, from the historic churches and architecture of Geneva and Lausanne to the famous cheese making villages in Gruyeres and the majestic lakes and mountains surrounding Zermatt and Lucerne. Travel by open-air railway, cable car, and the world's steepest cogwheel railway to capture the dramatic views of the Matterhorn, Monte Rosa and Mount Pilatus, spend time exploring the restaurants and museums in the pretty Swiss towns or simply relax and take advantage of the excellent facilities offered by your hotels.

    Clients can also enjoy the added incentive of 3 nights for the price of 2 at the stylish Palace Luzern and the Grand Hotel Zermatterhof as well as free rail travel and exclusive Concierge Rail service for the duration of the trip making this an ideal summer escape for couples, families and small groups alike.

    *Dates and Validity - All offers valid June 18th 2011 through to September 24th 2011 subject to availability.

    3 for 2 offer at the Grand Hotel Zermatterhof is valid for the Deluxe Rooms or Junior Suites
