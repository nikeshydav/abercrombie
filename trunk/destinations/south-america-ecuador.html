Travel to Ecuador


Ecuador is often known as the home to the "middle of the world", and thanks to its strategic location on the line of Equator complemented by the Pacific Ocean coastline this petite Andean land is blessed with a unique biodiversity and a stunning topography. Make your way to the Southern coastal desert from the snowcapped peaks of the Andes in the north and finally usher into the lush dense Amazon Rainforest basin which is home to Cofani, Shuar, Secoya and Hurani tribes. Travel to Ecuador and unfold a stirring experience enriched with the discovery of an Andean way of life, an adventure trip to Cotopaxi world's highest active volcano and an equally majestic Chimborazo in the south, explore many cities and sites recorded as UNESCO World Heritage Sites.

The second highest capital city in the world, Quito is an ideal location to start your expedition of Ecuador. This charming town is one of the most gorgeous capitals of South America showcasing the colonial charms from the past in its architecture and culture. Whole of Imbabura province not too far from Quito is another must visit to soak in the local colors and vibes. You can visit vintage market towns of Cuenca, Otavalo, Ibarra and Cotacachi and indulge yourself with the Andean traditions, cuisine and arts.

Ecuador Tour Packages by Abercrombie & Kent take you on a voyage of discovery at Galapagos Island. This prolific archipelago is a cluster of exotic volcanic islands that lie in the lap of the Pacific Ocean around the Equator and are famed for their incomparable natural beauty and a vast variety of atypical flora and fauna. The Islands were discovered in 1535 when a Spanish exploratory vessel drifted off its course and ended up at its shores. As a result of this, Galapagos Islands inherited a rich Spanish influence, even though it is one of those rare regions in the world which doesn't have its own indigenous population.
