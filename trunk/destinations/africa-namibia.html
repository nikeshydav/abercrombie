Namibia safaris & luxury holidays


A Namibia safari may not be top of your list but it should be. Those choosing Namibia holidays invariably return invigorated by the vastness of the landscape, its stark beauty and an unforgettable Namibia safari experience. Its dramatic scenery, rugged coastlines and ever-changing colours remain etched in the memory.

A Namibia safari with Abercrombie & Kent is a truly personal experience, taking in everything that makes a luxury Namibia safari special.

If you are looking for unspoilt natural beauty and a luxury safari, Namibia ticks all of the right boxes. Safaris in Namibia with A&K are made unforgettable by our exclusive access to people, places and wildlife.

Etosha National Park is one of the great wildlife-viewing areas, with perennial springs attracting giraffes, elephants, birds and the big cats. Sossusvlei is a photographer's paradise where some of the highest sand dunes rise majestically. Damaraland is home of the mystical desert elephant, ancient rock art sites and the wonderful Himba people.

The Skeleton Coast, covering an area of two million hectares is an eerie yet fascinating landscape. Walvis Bay Lagoon is regarded as the most important wetland for coastal birds in Southern Africa, with about 200,000 birds stopping here on their migration to and from the Arctic. The Caprivi Strip in the far north-eastern corner of Namibia is a lush wetland area and follows the Linyanti and Zambezi rivers, with an environment very similar to the Okavango Delta.

Namibrand Nature Reserve is located in the south, it was established to help protect and conserve the unique ecology and wildlife of the south-west Namib Desert. Holidays in Namibia at Namibrand Nature reserve will offer you the opportunity to experience how the reserve can maintain holistic biodiversity conservation balance with financial sustainability.
