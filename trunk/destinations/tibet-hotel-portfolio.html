Suggested Tibet accommodation

  • St Regis in Lhasa Resort

    The St. Regis Lhasa Resort steeps its guests in the beauty and serenity of Tibetan culture—from exquisitely crafted architectural details to the traditional healing Tibetan herbal treatments offered in the sanctuary of our Iridium Spa. The resort is configured to pay homage to this mystical and historic country in every way. A Meditation Garden offers a sacred space for quiet contemplation and an in-house Travel Agency stands ready to assist guests in further explorations, near or far.

    Guests are invited to join in morning yoga instructions and evening rituals when scheduled, including the St. Regis tradition of champagne sabering. Cooking classes taught by local masters offer hands-on immersion in the heart and soul of the cuisine and are available for everyone with advance reservations. Should guests need to keep up with work, the resort staffs a Business Center 24 hours a day. Bespoke St. Regis Butler Service is available to all guests any time, day or night.

    HIGHLIGHTS

    • Experience the tantalizing aromas, flavors and textures of Tibetan cooking with the chef at Social's open kitchen. We offer classes exclusively for two or more with advance reservations.
    • Guests are invited to enjoy a delightful evening ritual with champagne, canapés, and a taste of Tibetan culture in the courtyard.
    • Under the direction of our Head Concierge, the resort’s Concierge Desk serves as a central resource for guests' every need—from dining reservations to procuring tickets for array of cultural activities and tours.
    • Travel in and around Tibet is a fascinating adventure, but it does require some special knowledge of the need for permits and transportation options. The resort's Travel Desk is here to take care of all of our guests' travel needs.
    • Shimmering with the reflections of 24-karat gold leaves secured by crystals, the resort’s comfortably heated Golden Energy Pool is a crown jewel in the midst of the Iridium Spa. Lounge, wade, or enjoy a foot massage, poolside.
    • Experience the rare and extravagant at the Iridium Spa at The St. Regis Lhasa Resort. Reflecting the art and spirit of Tibet, our spa offers the utmost in personalized, holistic treatments in a splendid and tranquil sanctuary.
    • Fitness and exercise take on a different tone at our altitude of 3,680 meters above sea level. Here, we are pleased to offer inviting environments, state-of-the-art equipment, and expert instructors for the gentle practices of yoga and Pilates.
    • Internet access throughout the hotel keeps you connected.
    • We practice responsible luxury to enhance your experience and benefit the planet. Some of our environmental practices include better energy and water conservation, waste minimization, responsible purchasing and indoor environmental quality.
    • St. Regis Butler Service has been a treasured hallmark of the St. Regis experience for over one hundred years. From a perfectly pressed suit to a favorite book at hand, no request is too small or unattainable.
