Suggested Koh Samui accommodation

  • Banyan Tree Samui

    This brand new 78 all-pool Villas resort combines the best elements of a tropical getaway with white beaches and sun. The resort is nestled in a series of cascading terraces on a private hill cove on the south-eastern coast of Koh Samui, overlooking the sapphire waters of scenic Lamai Bay.

    Retreat from the bustle of the outside world and into this serene environment as your days pass away in a gentle flow of tranquillity, serenaded by the cool sea breezes off the Gulf of Thailand. Choose from iconic dining venues, the island's largest individual infinity pools in the villas, the award winning Banyan Tree Spa or The Rainforest, Koh Samui's only hydrotherapy wellness facility.

    Four Seasons Resort Koh Samui

    The Four Seasons Koh Samui is a tropical retreat, located in a hidden, private cove on the pristine Lam Yai Bay beach. Cascading down a hillside rich with coconut trees and lush gardens, the resort mixes sleek styling with traditional touches to create a luxurious environment that is in tune with its surroundings and offers beautiful views across the Gulf of Siam.

    Set the tone for a relaxed day with breakfast on La Tania's spacious terrace, then wander through tropical gardens to the secluded beach-side pool. For the ultimate in relaxation try the spa, which blends traditional Thai treatments with remedies drawing on the wisdom of ancient civilisations, from the Incas to the Romans.If you want to explore, take a trip on the SiamSeas speedboat, which is perfect for family trips to the picturesque islands of the Angthong National Marine Park. Afterwards, try the Once in a Blue Moon dinner experience, which offers outstanding cuisine in a romantic candlelit beach setting under a balmy tropical sky.

    Karma Samui

    Situated in a private hollow on terraces that rise gently from the quiet sandy beach, the Pool Villas at Karma Samui offer stunning views of the Gulf of Thailand. Elements of Thai and contemporary architecture combine in perfect harmony, while rich silks and hand-crafted artworks adorn the interiors.

    6 One, Two, Three and Four Bedroom Pool Villas are each set in walled tropical gardens stocked with frangipani and gardenia. Immaculate in every detail, they have private pools, outdoor dining areas and free-standing day bed pavilions for spa treatments or just a moment of quiet reflection.After a day's relaxation or exploration, settle into the Padma Restaurant where a combination of fabulous views, attentive service and excellent cuisine have resulted in the reputation as one of the best restaurants on Koh Samui. Alternatively, head to the beach for an intimate dining experience under star-lit skies.

    Six Senses Samui

    Six Senses Samui nestles on a gently sloping headland amongst 20 acres of indigenous vegetation. Perfectly located on the northern tip of Samui Island, it is the only resort to enjoy both sunset and and sunrise views over the Gulf of Siam.

    With just 66 villas, many with private infinity pools, Six Senses creates an atmosphere of calm seclusion. Pure white walls complement authentic Thai décor, including bamboo screens and thatched roofs, whilst large windows and outdoor areas create a sense of space and light. Our favourite are the Pool Villa Suites providing spacious accommodation, private sala decks and comfortable daybeds. Away from the hotel you will find a wealth of opportunity to explore and unwind, with sophisticated shops and restaurants, serene hikes, jungle drives and boat trips to deserted islands near at hand. Afterwards, indulge in a hot basalt stone massage at the Six Senses Spa, followed by wonderful New Asian cuisine at the signature 'Dining on the Rocks' restaurant.

    The Library

    The Library, a member of Design Hotels is a new conceptual hotel set amongst luscious tropical grounds and the rolling waves of stunning Chaweng Beach, Koh Samui.

    With its 26 suites and studio rooms scattered through the property in 13 individual units, The Library offers privacy and conveniences in one ultra-modern package. The concept behind The Library is to present a sleek modernist design while achieving a unique harmony with its natural surroundings.
