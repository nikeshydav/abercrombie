Zambia safaris & luxury holidays


If you're out to experience the real Africa, Zambia is undoubtedly that diamond in the rough. The land of glorious sunsets, legendary walking safaris, breathtaking Victoria Falls, the profuse and untamed Zambezi River, effervescent lakes, abundant wildlife and raw, pulsating wilderness, all thrown together in a heap! Sightseeing in Zambia is a treat for all the senses alike. Acknowledged as one of the safest countries in the world to visit, it fondly cradles savannahs, streams, marshlands and lakes within itself, making for an ideal luxury holiday prospect.

If it is adrenalin pumping adventure that you are looking for, trust Zambia to make it an experience of a lifetime; one that you can never let go, one that never lets go of you. Safaris here are luxury and adventure clubbed together. Here you will find some of the finest safari experiences on the planet. Expect face to face encounters with nature at its wildest best! Tourist places in Zambia is one thing you can never get enough of. You don't go look for them, they come to you! Mosi-Oa-Tunya National Park, Lower Zambezi National Park and the South Luangwa National Park are premier places to look out for. The thundering Victoria Falls is another major crowd puller and will leave you awestruck to say the least!
