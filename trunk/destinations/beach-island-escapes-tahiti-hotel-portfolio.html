Tahiti Hotel Portfolio

  • BORA BORA LAGOON RESORT

    This award-winning Orient Express Hotel sits on its own motu in the middle of Bora Bora's lagoon with panoramic views of the mystical volcanic peak of Mt. Otemanu.

    The resort features 80 Beach and Overwater bungalows. A truly romantic setting, the resort provides the ultimate in service and hospitality.

    FOUR SEASONS BORA BORA

    Located on the ancient island of Motu Tehotu, Four Seasons Resort Bora Bora is one of the most spectacular island resorts in decades. The spacious hideaways, comprising of Overwater suites and Beachfront Villas with pools, overlook the beautiful unspoiled lagoon and offer stunning views of Mount Otemanu and Mount Pahia.

    Just a 15 minute motor boat from Bora Bora airport, the resort has cleverly captured the spirit of French Polynesia whilst maintaining the ultra modern comforts and liberating service delivered by Four Seasons.

    Read more

    LE MERIDIEN BORA BORA

    Le Méridien Bora Bora is located on a Polynesian islet, surrounded by a turquoise lagoon. It offers guests luxurious over-water bungalows with glass-bottom floors or beach bungalows under coconut trees.

    Activities include water sports, swimming with turtles, deep-sea fishing and shark feeding.

    ST REGIS BORA BORA

    Edged by powdery sands and a tranquil lagoon, this 100 room resort represents the epitome of carefree elegance. Many of the rooms enjoy terrace whirlpools or a private swimming pool.

    The celebrated St. Regis Butler ensures that everything is possible to make a perfect stay in paradise.
