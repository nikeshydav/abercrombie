Zimbabwe safaris & luxury holidays


Close your eyes and imagine: A vast African river happily and peacefully gurgling through the infinite expanse of pristine tropical rainforest only to be suddenly met by an edge that sends it plummeting down one hundred and twenty eight meters into an endless gorge! This heart stopping fall is the famed Vitoria Falls and the drama unfolds right in front of your eyes, here at Zimbabwe!

A world heritage site and a major attraction, the falls, however, are not the only reasons that beckon you to the wonderful land. A striking and diverse landscape, cosmopolitan cities brimming with arts and culture, exotic wildlife teeming in its adventurous game parks and some very warm and friendly folk - that's Zimbabwe for you!

A high plateau with the central region liberally dusted with hills, Zimbabwe is nestled in the southern part of Africa, flanked by Mozambique in the east and the north-east, Zambia and Botswana in the west and South Africa in the south. The eastern parts are dotted with high mountain ranges called the Eastern Highlands, which grows most of the country's coffee!

Sightseeing in Zimbabwe is a pleasure to say the least. Learn of the history and culture of this wonderful country from the Great Zimbabwe Ruins and indulge in the scenic beauty of the Eastern Highlands. With a total of eleven national parks in the area, game viewing is a common activity. Revel your senses in the free flowing Victoria Falls, make friends with elephants at the Hwange National Park, home to one of the highest concentration of game especially elephants, enjoy the warm sun and soak in the pleasure of man-made wonderland of Lake Kariba and stroke the adventurous streak in you at the Mana Pools, showcasing the last remains of true wilderness with great views of the Zambezi Escarpment.
