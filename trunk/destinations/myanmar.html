Myanmar: Mystery embodied in a serene and pristine locale

Sitting prettily in the Asian landscape, Myanmar is a land of mystery and excitement. You will be marvelled by the criss-cross of hills, valleys, mystical spaces like temples and thick brunch of jungles once here. It shares the northern borders with Tibet in the Himalayan range while being heavily laden with teak wood trees on the Golden Triangle. Sharing Mekong's water with Thailand and Laos, Myanmar makes its plains along the Mandalay area fertile. Continuing its trail along the Ayeyarwaddy delta, Mekong flows into tropical islands of the Andaman Sea while ending its journey.

Full of rich history and culture, Myanmar is a house of ancient Asian civilisation and charm. One can visually observe an obvious difference between the world and the land of Myanmar in terms of a mystic charm. Keeping its old-world charm intact, Myanmar is now opening to the world for modernisation.

Geographically Myanmar is located on the Southeast zone of Asia while being its western-most point. Cinematically it can be touted as one of the most diversely rich country with beautiful landscapes and greatly entrenched spiritual connect to look forward to. Myanmar is a must visit for those who want to experience Asia the quiet and serene way. A great experience for all, all of Burma needs visitors to publicise the beauty of the land. With deeply ingested Buddhist culture set along pristine locale, Myanmar is a rare beauty.
