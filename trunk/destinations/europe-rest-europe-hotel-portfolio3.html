  • Baur au Lac Hotel

    Baur au Lac is Zurich's prestigious landmark of unequalled hospitality, with more than 165 years of hosting royalty, celebrities and international travellers. In its own private park on the lake shore, with a unique location at the upper end of the Bahnhofstrasse shopping and financial district, the hotel is only 10 minutes from most cultural and historical attractions.

    The hotel offers the ultimate in contemporary elegance, evoking the intimate atmosphere of a private home. The 120 rooms offer complimentary minibar and W-LAN internet access as well as free use of the fitness club with spectacular views overlooking the lake and the Alps. Internationally renowned, the completely redesigne restaurant Pavillon for contemporary fine dining and outstanding wines. New Bar & Restaurant Terrasse for open-air dining in the incomparable setting of Baur au Lac Park. Rive Gauche Restaurant & Bar, Zurich's most popular hot spot for casual fine dining.

    Beau-Rivage Palace Lausanne

    The beautiful Belle Epoque buildings of the Beau-Rivage Palace stand majestically, surrounded by 10 acres of stunning private gardens on the bank of Lake Geneva. This member of the Leading Hotels of the World, offers breathtaking views across the lake and the Alps.

    The guest rooms and suites are uniquely decorated, presenting utmost elegance and comfort. Beau-Rivage Palace's restaurants provide to all tastes, with their rich menus and high class cuisine. The signature Cinq Mondes Spa offers pure relaxation on over 1,500 square meters, welcoming you to discover beauty rituals from all over the world. Last but not least, Beau-Rivage Palace provides impeccable service featuring a special kids program to make your stay truly unforgettable.

    Palace Luzern

    Just walking distance from the Old Town and shops, right on the lakeshore, with magnificent views over the Alps, Hotel Palace Luzern offers atmosphere, tradition and distinctive style combined with the most modern facilities.

    Guest rooms are all individually decorated in a stylish, contemporary fashion, many offering fabulous views of the lake. Amenities include numerous restaurants, cocktail bars and sports facilities. A broad terrace at the lakeside provides a panoramic view of the city, lake and mountains. Two exclusive restaurants offer superb international cuisine. There is a Fitness Corner that offers massage, steam bath and sauna, ESPA facilities, including a Private Spa as well as solarium and gym.

    The Grand Hotel Zermatterhof

    The Grand Hotel Zermatterhof is an exceptional property situated in the centre of Zermatt. The historic hotel is uniquely positioned along the village's main street in a picturesque park-like setting.

    It offers a variety of comfortable and homely deluxe guest rooms as well as junior and large suites. Most rooms have fantastic views on the surrounding village and mountains, including the Matterhorn, and are decorated with fabrics, murals and wood panelling. The hotel's Vita Borni wellness zone combines the natural purity of the mountains with the healing power of water. A spacious pool, whirlpool, hot air sauna, steam bath, alpine grotto and ice grotto are all available as well as 5 treatment rooms. The hotel's exquisite restaurants include the Fine Dining Prato Borni and Restaurant Lusi offering gourmet delights and stunning views of the Matterhorn. The Stars bar and Rudenbar also offer a cosy space and piano entertainment.
