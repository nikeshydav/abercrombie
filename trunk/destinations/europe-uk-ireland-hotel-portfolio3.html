SUGGESTED EDINBURGH & SCOTLAND ACCOMMODATION:

  • The Balmoral Hotel Edinburgh

    The Balmoral Hotel is located at Number 1 Princes Street in the heart of Edinburgh, a minute's walk from the city's main shopping area, and is the top hotel in the city.

    The preserved grand public areas reflect the origins as a major railway terminus hotel, and the current more contemporary décor reflects the development a modern top class venue. There are 2 restaurants, The Number One restaurant and the less formal Hadrian's, where breakfast is served. There is also a tea lounge, The Palm Bar. Other Hotel facilities include a Health Club, which is open from 7.00am - 9.00pm, with swimming pool, fully equipped gymnasium, solarium and steam room.

    The Gleneagles Hotel

    The Gleneagles Hotel is a world class resort hotel set in 850 acres of Perthshire countryside and is a member of the Leading Hotels of the World. Famous for its three 18-hole golf courses and venue to the Ryder Cup 2014, Gleneagles is also home to an award winning destination spa and a host of country pursuit activities.

    With a fantastic reputation for cuisine, Gleneagles has four restaurants including the two Michelin-starred Andrew Fairlie and The Strathearn, offering a delicious array of the finest Scottish dishes. With such a choice available, Gleneagles is an ideal destination for both adults and children.
