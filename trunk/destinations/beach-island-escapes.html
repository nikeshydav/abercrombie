Beach & Island Escapes


Sun kissed beaches, pristine tepid waters, lush green foliage, all characterize the Indian Ocean destinations rightly. Perfect for honeymooners, seasoned couples or families and even those looking to relax after a long adventurous African safari experience, islands of the Maldives, Mauritius or Seychelles, Swahili Coast, the spice scented air of Zanzibar or hideaways of Mozambique are all here for your perfect Indian Ocean beach holiday.

Escape to Maldives, this is where time comes to a standstill and gives you a perfect get-away from a demanding city life. A dream world where you can unwind yourself amongst white sand beaches and sparkling blue lagoons, it no wonder that this stunning archipelago has been described as 'the Last Paradise on Earth'. A paradise where you can devour the nature's wonders, gentle breeze brushing against the columns of palm, glistening shoreline, turquoise waters and stay at the most luxurious resorts in the world.

Mauritius is another tempting haven of elongated stretches of sandy coast, delicious cuisine, friendly locals and loads of water sport opportunities, welcoming newlyweds and families alike. If Mauritius catches your fancy then all you need is to get in touch with one of our expert holiday planners.

Seychelles' picturesque 115 islands are scattered across the coast of Africa like gemstones in the clear blue expanse of the Indian Ocean. It is so easy not to get swept off your feet with the postcard perfect spellbinding beauty and mesmerizing scenery that surrounds you here. So go ahead and travel to a beach island in the gracious Indian Ocean and indulge yourself one sunset at a time!
