    BANYAN TREE MADIVARU

    Set upon the North Ari Atoll, Banyan Tree Madivaru is located just 30 minutes southwest by seaplane from Male International Airport. Madivaru, meaning 'Ray' in Dhivehi, is an island spanning an area of 16,000 square meters with white long beaches. Banyan Tree Madivaru comprises 6 Tented Pool Villas. Each Tented Pool Villa is a set of three individual tents, distinctively equipped to function as living, sleeping, and bath areas. The Banyan Tree Spa experience is conducted In-Villa for the ultimate island indulgence. Side-by-side treatment beds in the bath tent are transformed into an oasis of calm; receive facials or massages in the

    privacy of your own tent. Dining options are varied with the option to have a sandbank lunch or dinner, in villa dinning or the exquisite Boa Keyo restaurant where in the evening you may be able to spot eagle rays, turtles, dolphins or even manta rays in the Indian Ocean.

    BANYAN TREE VABBINFARU

    Cast away on a secluded coral atoll in the Maldives archipelago, Banyan Tree Vabbinfaru prides itself on being a charismatic island retreat. Just 20 minutes by speedboat from the airport, this island paradise provides a magical and exclusive hideaway, which has been famed for its unobtrusive service and omnipresent attention to detail, since it began attracting its first guests to the Maldives.

    Banyan Tree have set themselves the objective of researching and pioneering projects supporting marine life, as well as helping to increase the ecological awareness of guests. The environmental dives and eco-safaris are a must for all ages. The resident marine biologists conduct courses and dives in small groups so everyone receives individual attention. You will discover about the unique lotus and turtle research projects, alongside gaining a broader understanding of the ocean and its mysterious creatures. After a morning of discovery, select from the extensive menu of tropical treatments at the Banyan Tree Spa. Unwind in tranquil surroundings with a heavenly massage or refreshing facial. With the sun setting across the Indian Ocean, feel the romance in the air as you sweep your loved one off their feet to a moonlit dinner on a stunning private sandbank.

    BAROS

    Located in the shimmering waters of a shallow lagoon, the tropical island of Baros is ringed by a house reef, vibrant with marine life and excellent for snorkelling or diving. Elegant villas of timber and sandstone are positioned over the lagoon or on powder white sands, ensuring perfect privacy in a peaceful environment, undisturbed by the sounds of the modern world. Here you will find unique experiences, impeccable service, original overwater dining venues and architecture designed to preserve and celebrate the beauty of nature.

    Pack a picnic and head out on a dhoni, keeping an eye out for a pod of dolphins or a leaping sailfish. Or, enjoy an à la carte diving experience in this amazing oceanographic environment. The experienced dive team will choose the site according to the moon, tides and currents. Find yourself amongst colourful corals, graceful rays and inquisitive turtles. By night, join the Marine Environmentalist and discover a whole other world. There are many dining experiences to savour, but make sure you visit the overwater Lighthouse, a unique structure and the resort's signature restaurant. How about a yellow-fin tuna carpaccio and your pick from the glass-walled wine cellar? Afterwards step into the Lighthouse Lounge and listen to the light sounds of jazz accompanied by spectacular views of the Indian Ocean.

    COCOA ISLAND

    Floating elegantly on sparkling shallow lagoon waters, the timber dhonis and villas of Cocoa Island offer luxurious and ultimately private accommodation. Set between the excellent diving of the Kandooma Channel and sheltered snorkelling around the shallows, the hand-crafted suites and villas are linked by a timber walkway to a private island. Here you will find the COMO Shambhala Retreat Spa, a gourmet restaurant, swimming pool, all-day lounge and watersports centre.

    To ensure you return home feeling truly rejuvenated, let us pre-book you a spa package at the COMO Shambhala Retreat. You can choose from a purification, relaxation, rejuvenation or restoration path which will help you to rediscover a sense of mind-body balance. Combined with yoga tuition and healthy cuisine, you will feel the benefits of a complete return to real well-being. If an active path is more your choice, then diving in the Maldives is rated among the best in the world. Join a dive boat from the PADI centre to one of 20 different dive sites, in the undisturbed waters around this quiet atoll. Or, stay closer to home and snorkel straight from your room to the spectacular house reef. End the day with sand between your toes and a private romantic dinner for two on the beach.

    FOUR SEASONS RESORT AT LANDAA GIRAAVARU

    Discover a more intense experience of nature at Four Seasons Resort Maldives at Landaa Giraavaru, in the remote Baa Atoll. Fringed by one of the Maldives' most spectacular turquoise lagoons and surrounded by powder-soft, white-sand beaches, this 44-acre private island is home to some of the richest waters in the country. Snorkel in the Resort's pristine 2-kilometre lagoon and enjoy breathtaking encounters with turtles, dolphins, eagle rays, manta rays and juvenile lemon-sharks - a sight normally reserved for divers only. Spend your days in the lush surroundings of the multi-award winning Spa and Ayurvedic Retreat, diving in the aquarium-like waters or enjoying specialities such as teppanyaki, Lebanese mezze and tandoori.

    The multi-award winning Spa and Ayurvedic Retreat offers in-depth, life changing Ayurvedic programmes that range from a seven to 28 days in lush 2.5-acre setting that spans from the sea to the heart of the island. The retreat's resident Ayurvedic physician, yogi and Ayurvedic chef guide seekers of vibrant health and wellness towards balance and bliss through consultations, spa treatments integrated with yoga and Ayurvedic cuisine meal plans. The Resort's Marine Research Centre runs pioneering conservation projects on whale sharks, fish breeding, coral reefscaping and supports the only full-time manta ray research project in the Maldives. You will have the chance to go on an experience you will never forget, as you dive in and watch these graceful animals glide through the water. Children will enjoy learning about fragile yet breathtaking ecosystem of the Maldives with resident marine biologists, alongside a multitude of activities at the kids' club. Teenagers won't feel left out either, as they get to hang out at Majaa Maizan to watch a movie, play computer games or have a game of pool.

    VILLINGILI

    British author James Hilton's mystical 'Shangri-La' has become synonymous with any earthly paradise. This description could not be more apt for their first resort in the Maldives, opened July 2009. On the southernmost tip of the Addu Atoll, Villingili Resort and Spa stretches across the fine white coral sands of Villingili Island. Effortless luxury, peace and privacy are assured at this exotic hidden treasure.

    Exploration is the order of the day; learn about 45 different plant species as you follow one of the nature trails. Alongside the 17,000 coconut trees, you might also find the Noni plant, believed to be useful for the treatment of certain ailments. Search out a secluded cove, perfect for romantic picnics, or have lunch on the equator. If you are looking to explore further afield, take the boat shuttle to meet the locals on Gan Island or go diving among historic British royal relics. After an active day, unwind at the restaurant for an aromatic curry, plate of dim sum or mezze. Choose from one of the three distinctive Eastern-style rooms, representing the different cultures and cuisines from the Indian Ocean, South China Sea and Arabian Gulf.
