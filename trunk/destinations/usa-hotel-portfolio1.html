Suggested Deep South accommodation.

  • Forte Conde Inn Alabama, Mobile, Fort Conde Inn

    A boutique hotel exuding the charm and style of 19th century Mobile, Forte Conde Inn is the second oldest house in Mobile. The lavish interiors reflect its architectural vintage, with high ceilings, original heart pine flooring, and crystal chandeliers. It occupies a central position in Mobile, and is within walking distance of historical landmarks, museums and restaurants.

    This boutique hotel has just 10 bedrooms, meaning that service is impeccable and the house retains the feel of a private home. All of the large rooms have working fireplaces, WiFi and flat-screen televisions. The one bedroom luxury suite has a kitchen, and benefits from two open fireplaces, one in the bedroom and one in the sitting room.

    Renaissance Hotel Alabama, Montgomery, Renaissance Hotel

    In the heart of historic downtown Montgomery, you are within close distance of many of the sites and attractions, including the Rosa Park's Museum. The Montgomery Museum of Fine Arts and the Alabama Civil Rights Museum trail are also close by, making this hotel the perfect base from which to explore this historic state capital.

    NEED TO KNOW
    * Central position next to many sites and attractions
    * Just minutes from a championship golf course
    * Relax in the hotel's spa, and enjoy an up-to-the-minute treatment in one of the seven treatment rooms
    * For a taste of new Southern cuisine, eat at The House, where authentic Southern food is perfectly offset by the modern surroundings. Alternatively, head to Wintzell's Oyster House to try their delectable and infamous oysters

    Georgia, Atlanta, St Regis

    Located in the heart of Buckhead, the hotel is a perfect base from which to explore the exciting city of Atlanta. It has 151 rooms, including 31 suites, all of which benefit from the bespoke St Regis Butler Service, ensuring that you are impeccably looked after throughout your stay.

    Indulge at the Remede Spa, which offers a selection of highly acclaimed treatments. Also unwind at the Pool Piazza where you can enjoy a cocktail at the poolside bar or a game of billiards. There are five dining options within the hotel, so you will never be short of choice, including Paces 88 restaurant which benefits from stunning views and superb authentic southern cuisine.

    NEED TO KNOW
    * Located in the heart of elegant Buckhead
    * 151 rooms with butler service
    * The Remede Spa offers a selection of highly acclaimed treatments.

    Louisiana, Lafayette, Bois des Chenes Inn

    With just three guestrooms and two suites, this inn gives you a truly intimate experience. This 1820's Arcadian style plantation house has been restored to its original configuration, with period antiques complimenting its unique architectural heritage. It is listed on the Historic Register of Historical Houses, and was once the centre of a 3,000 acre cattle and sugar plantation.

    The three guestrooms are located in the carriage house and the two suites - one with an open fireplace - are in the main carriage house, known as the Charles Mouton House. Each room/suite contains cleverly-hidden modern touches including televisions and fridges.

    NEED TO KNOW
    * Central position in historic Lafayette
    * On the National Register of Historic Houses
    * Just three guestrooms and two suites

    Louisiana, New Orleans, Bocage Plantation

    Immerse yourself fin the history of this resplendent mansion, where Baccarat chandeliers drape from the ceilings, the walls are adorned with paintings by the likes of Rembrandt, Thomas Sulley and Peale, and furniture by great masters such as Mallard and Belter. This bed and breakfast contains the grand splendour of a stately home, while retaining the intimate feel of a private residence.

    NEED TO KNOW
    * The Bocage Plantation boasts an enviable position on the River Road on the bank of the Mississippi River. Located just 20 minutes from downtown Baton Rouge, and 50 minutes from the New Orleans French Quarter, this historic plantation provides an excellent base from which to explore the whole of South Louisiana
    * Built in 1840 by famous architect James Dakin, it is considered to be one of the most original interpretations of American Greek Revival architecture
    * The bedrooms are located on the second level of the mansion, and benefit from views out towards the Mississippi river. Step out onto the front gallery for panoramic views of the 110 acre plantation * Experience a place where the modern meets tradition. The décor preserves a traditional air, while wireless internet and flat screen televisions in every room bring it into the 21st century

    Monmouth Plantation Mississippi, Monmouth Plantation

    Monmouth Plantation is located in historic Natchez, a town that retains its sense of history while at once being vibrant and full of culture. All of the rooms and suites reflect the tradition of the area, complimented by modern inflections. The deluxe suites provide easy access to Monmouth's walking trail, which includes the private 19th century cemetery of the first owners.

    A morning guided tour of the historic house is included in the rate. Experience the taste of the true south in the gourmet 1818 restaurant. The Beau-Pre Country Club has an 18-hole golf course set amongst spectacular scenery, just 3 miles south of Natchez.

    NEED TO KNOW
    * Traditional plantation home located in historic Natchez
    * The deluxe suites have easy access to Monmouth's walking trail
    * Gourmet restaurant serving authentic southern food

    Tennessee, Blackberry Farm

    Occupying an idyllic position in the foothills of the Great Smoky Mountains of eastern Tennessee, Blackberry Farm is a haven for nature lovers, and simply for those looking to relax in the great outdoors. Set in 420,000 acres of pristine landscape, the activities on offer are endless, with biking, horseriding, hiking and fishing all on offer. All of the rooms, suites, houses and cottages are meticulously designed in order to ensure you have the most comfortable stay possible.

    Stay in a Hill Cottage for your own concierge and golf cart. Many of the suites benefit from fireplaces, perfect for unwinding after a day of adventure. The recently renovated spa will leave you feeling refreshed and revitalised. Yoga is also on offer, as well as an outdoor pool with stunning views of the Great Smoky Mountains.

    NEED TO KNOW
    * Idyllic position in the foothills of the Great Smoky Mountains
    * Plenty of activities on offer
    * Outdoor pool with stunning views of the Great Smoky Mountains
