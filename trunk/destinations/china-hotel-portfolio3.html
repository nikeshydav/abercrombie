Suggested Hong Kong accommodation

  • Mandarin Oriental, Hong Kong

    Nestled amongst designer stores and premier business locations, the Mandarin Oriental is situated at one of Hong Kong's most prominent addresses. Its name has, over the years, become synonymous with style.

    Located overlooking the glittering Victoria Harbour, the Mandarin Oriental has been at the epicentre of Hong Kong's social and business scene since 1963. Following a recent redesign, along with superb attention to detail, it is a haven of luxury in the heart of the city.

    Read more

    The Peninsula Hong Kong

    Set in the heart of Kowloon, The Peninsula has long been recognised as one of the finest hotels in the world, offering a choice of the classic comforts of the original building, or the more spectacular Victoria Harbour views from the rooms in the modern tower. We recommend the Grande Deluxe Harbour View room if the famous view of Hong Kong harbour is a must for you.

    Relax for the afternoon in ESPA, described as 'heaven on earth', or visit the gym offering one of the most spectacular location for a fitness centre. Be sure not to miss diningat Gaddi's, the Verandah or Chesa, some of the most iconic restaurants in Hong Kong.

    Read more

    The Upper House

    The Upper House is Hong Kong's latest and most talked-about hotel that combines five star luxury with boutique intimacy. The Upper House is exciting, quirky and unlike anything else in the city.

    Tucked away from the vibrant streets of Hong Kong, the hotel is designed by architect Andre Fu, who has created a vibrant refreshing environment, which might well tempt you to prefer a night in - as opposed to a night out in Hong Kong's busy urban landscape.
