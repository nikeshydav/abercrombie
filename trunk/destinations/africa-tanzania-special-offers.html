    Kilimanjaro- The Basic Facts:

    • Kilimanjaro is the highest mountain in Africa at 19340 feet (5,895 metres)
    • Kilimanjaro is the highest free-standing mountain (i.e. distance from the bottom of the mountain to the top) in the world.
    • Kilimanjaro is as high as you can go without requiring oxygen or specialist technical mountaineering skills.
    • Kilimanjaro has five distinct ecological zones - montane forest, Hagenia, heathland, alpine desert making it one of the most beautiful and varied peaks on Earth.
    • Kilimanjaro is the most accessible, the most exotic and the most do-able climb on Earth.
    • Kilimanjaro: Afromontane forest, glacial valleys and alpine desert, views of Mawenzi, ascent of Kibo and possible views of Kibo caldera and ash pit. Glaciers and the roof of Africa!

    • 1 Night Stay at Karanga Camp on double/twin occupancy inclusive of Full Board on Day 4.
    • After approx hiking of 3 Hrs to Barafu Camp, enjoy the desolate alpine desert and at times strong winds rip over this camp with evening splendid views of Mawenzi peak on Day 5.

    • 1 Night Stay at Karanga Camp on double/twin occupancy inclusive of Full Board on Day 6.
    • Hike for 4 to 5 Hrs to Barafu Camp at 15,200 Feet.
    • 1 Night Stay at Barafu Camp on double/twin occupancy inclusive of Full Board on Day 7.
    • At Midnight you'll make your way up a winding path flanked by Ratzel & Rebman glaciers about 6 Hrs of Hiking you'll reach Gilman's point at 18,630 Feet on the rim of Kilimanjaro's crater on Day8.
    • After reaching watch the sunrise & continue around the rim to Uhuru Peak at 19,340 Feet climb takes at least 2 Hrs & later descent to 10,200 Feet & your last campsite on the mountain on Day 8.
    • 1 Night Stay at Mweka Camp on double/twin occupancy inclusive of Full Board on Day 8.
    • After Breakfast you will descent to base of the mountain & arrive at approx 1300 Hrs where you will met by your A&K driver/guide & transfer to Serena Mountain Village Lodge for your lunch & for day use then transfer to Kilimanjaro Airport on Day 9.
