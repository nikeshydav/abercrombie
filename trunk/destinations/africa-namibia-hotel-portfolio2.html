Suggested Etosha Region Accommodation

  • LITTLE ONGAVA

    The lodge is set in a private 66,000 acre Ongava Game Reserve along the Southern Boundary of the Etosha National park. Little Ongava is an intimate, luxurious camp, consisting of just three large private suites, all en suite, with a plunge pool, outdoor shower and private sala.

    Activities whilst at the camp include game drives, game walks and visits to Etosha National Park. Game sightings include white & black rhino, elephants, lions and springbok.

    ONGAVA LODGE

    Ongava Lodge comprises of 13 rock and thatch chalets and one family room. Each chalet is en suite, has air conditioning and its own private covered veranda overlooking the plains. Dinner in the camp is either under the stars on the dining deck weather permitting otherwise inside the main dining area under thatch.

    Ongava Lodge has a swimming pool to relax by in the middle of the day, and there are plenty of seating areas in the main area to relax and take in the stuning views of the surrounding plains of this reserve.

    Most general game are present in the Ongava Game Reserve as well as within Etosha National Park: including springbok, gemsbok, wildebeest, Burchell's zebra, Hartmann's mountain zebra, waterbuck, red hartebeest, giraffe, eland and the endemic black-faced impala. Elephant can be found in the Park, with lion, leopard, and black and white rhino seen both there and on the reserve

    ONGAVA TENTED CAMP

    Ongava Tented camp provides private luxury, delicious food and an excellent safari experience. The small tented camp is in a different area of the reserve to its sister property Ongava Lodge.

    Accommodation comprises eight large comfortable walk-in tents, each with en-suite facilities, including flush toilet and a hot shower. Relaxation at Ongava Tented Camp typically takes place around the main area with its bar and swimming pool, watching the wildlife that comes to drink at the waterhole Ongava has resident White Rhino and Black Rhino, allowing guests the unique opportunity to see both of these rare species.

    ONGUMA PLAINS CAMP

    Onguma Plains Camp is located on the Eastern side of Etosha National Park in Namibia's Onguma Private Game Reserve. This large game reserve allows leopards, lions, cheetahs and black rhinos to roam freely, allowing perfect game viewing up close and from afar.

    The camp comprises of 12 private suites and the main Fort Suite, both with spacious en suites. Each suite has an outside area with loungers, providing the comfort for a relaxing rest in front of the remarkable views.

    Read more

    ONGUMA TENTED CAMP

    Onguma Tented Camp offers an exclusive safari experience in the Onguma Reserve. The private reserve is 20,000 hectares and is on the Eastern side of the Etosha National Park. The seven contemporary styled tents are equipped with a bath and shower and offer the perfect view of the waterhole.

    The views from your private deck over look the waterhole which is floodlit at night. The restaurant offers delicious food which can be enjoyed whilst viewing giraffe, zebra and antelope whilst they visit the watering hole.

    ONGUMA TREE TOP CAMP

    The Camp is built on wooden stilts amongst the tree tops with excellent views over the beautiful watering hole. This rustic private camp is perfect for small groups of families or friends who are looking to experience the bush in a relaxing and beautiful location in Onguma's Private Reserve in Etosha's National Park.

    The 4 thatched rooms with canvas walls can be opened at each side, an amazing way to relax and see the game in the distance. Each tent also has its own private wooden deck to unwind on after each game drive.

    THE MUSHARA OUTPOST & VILLAS

    Situated ten kilometres from the Von Lindequist gate of the Etosha National Park, The Mushara Outpost is nestled on the banks of an ancient river bed and accommodates 16 guests in custom made wood and canvas chalet/tents. The ethos at Mushara is of old fashioned hospitality with warm and friendly service.

    The main lodge building is styled on an old farm house and is extremely comfortable. Set on raised wooden decks and with floor to ceiling windows, the views into the bush are spectacular. Close by two exclusive villas are hidden in the bush, providing an extreme level of comfort, perfect for families.
