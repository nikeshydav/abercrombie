Suggested Entebbe Accommodation

  • Lake Victoria Serena Resort

    Located on the shores of Lake Victoria in the Kigo District just a short twenty minutes drive from Entebbe International Airport is the Lake Victoria Serena Resort. Built in the style of a Roman villa the hotel rises above the white-terraced rose gardens.

    All rooms are elegantly furnished in shades of terracotta and cream and feature, either a queen-sized or two twin beds, a dressing table, work station, seating area, and balcony and lavish marble bathroom with walk-in shower.

    • Visit The Uganda Wildlife Centre
    • Enjoy a refreshing with swim in the Lake View Pool
    • For those who are more active, take a day trip out to Jinja for white-river rafting or bunjee jumping
    • Take a tour of Kampala which takes in the National Museum as well as the African Craft Village

    IN THE KNOW
    A visit to the Ngamba Island Chimpanzee Sanctuary on Lake Victoria will never be forgotten.

    The Boma Entebbe

    Set in tropical gardens in a suburb just minutes form Entebbe International Airport, The Boma is one of Entebbe's original and most exclusive hotels. Keeping the charm and the character of the original home which dates back to the 1940's all rooms have been individually decorated in a mixture of African and European decor.

    The main house has a private dining area and living room for guests to enjoy.

    • Cool down in the swimming pool
    • Enjoy homemade meals in the restaurant
    • Tours around Entebbe can be arranged

    IN THE KNOW
    Entebbe in the local Luganda language means 'seat' and was probably named that because it was the place where a Baganda chief sat to adjudicate legal cases.
