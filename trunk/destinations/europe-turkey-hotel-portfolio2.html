SUGGESTED ISTANBUL ACCOMMODATION:

  • DIVAN ISTANBUL

    Divan Istanbul is where luxury accommodations, bold design, and innovative cuisine meet in a uniquely refined setting. A short walk along broad, tree-lined boulevards is all that separates you from the best culture, nightlife and entertainment that Istanbul has to offer.

    Featuring elegantly appointed guestrooms and the warm, attentive service combining the best of East and West that is the hallmark of Divan Hotels, the completely rebuilt Divan Istanbul has an over fifty-year heritage yet is also one of Istanbul's newest hotels.

    All of Divan Istanbul's spacious rooms combine comfort, elegant design and luxurious convenience. The elegantly appointed bathrooms are decorated in harmonious marble tones and feature both rain and hand-held showerheads. All rooms provide the comfort of a King-size bed with Ottoman-inspired hand-beaten brass finials adorning the headboards.

    The choice of three gourmet restaurants: Divan Lokanta for fine dining, Divan Pub perfect for breakfast and a more relaxed lunch or dinner and Maromi the Japanese restaurant and sushi bar. The sleek Divan Bar serving Turkish gourmet cheese platter washed down with a selection of wines.

    Experience a rejuvenating and cleansing tradition stretching back to Ottoman times and before as you luxuriate in the hammam. If you are looking for something more active, try a workout at the fitness centre, take a Pilates class, or swim laps in the half Olympic-size pool. Afterward, treat yourself to a relaxing session in one of the saunas or indulge in one of the wide selection of beautifying facial and body treatments that will leave you feeling refreshed.

    FOUR SEASONS ISTANBUL AT BOSPHORUS

    Outstanding in every regard, the Four Seasons is a converted 19th-century palace, with a fresh, eclectic spirit. From its grand façade to its sun washed outside spaces and flawless interiors, retaining their glorious original features, this hotel is an absolute delight. In addition, from its waterfront location, it is cooled by gentle breezes and is in the heart of the city's attractions.

    The 170 guest rooms, including 25 suites, are bright and airy. Based on a striking blend of Ottoman design and contemporary mahogany furnishings, décor is a rich palette of deep blue or terracotta, accented by shades of copper and burgundy. Ornate ceilings feature hand-painted traditional motifs, and lithographs representative of Turkish and Ottoman art adorn the walls. Overall they are elegant and welcoming, with the homely yet pristine feel of an exclusive residence.

    FOUR SEASONS ISTANBUL AT SULTANAHMET

    This extraordinary hotel has been created from a century-old Neo-Classical Turkish prison, steps from the fabled Blue Mosque and Topkapi Palace. Impressive on the outside, this hotel is an architectural delight on the inside, where huge archways soar above intricately inlaid marble floors to create a real sense of scale amongst the authenticity and attention to detail.

    The Hotel's historic building affords space enough for just 65 unique rooms and suites nestled around an enclosed courtyard. All have high ceilings and are furnished with a writing desk, chairs, a private refrigerated bar and an armoire with a television, DVD and Bose CD player. The marble bathroom includes a deep soaking tub and a separate shower.

    The landscaped courtyard is another highlight, offering a calm, tranquil space for reading and relaxation.

    PARK HYATT ISTANBUL

    Ideally located in the fashionable Nisantasi residential and shopping district, this hotel uniquely combines the historic architecture of an Art Deco building with innovative interior design. With only 90 rooms this hotel has a stylish and modern feel, yet the service is friendly and intimate.

    The Nisantasi Shopping Mall and Istanbul Fashion Academy are just a few steps from the hotel as Park Hyatt Istanbul-Maçka Palas is also within five minutes walking distance to the Dolmabahçe Palace.

    Housing Emporio Armani and Gucci boutiques on the ground floor, the Park Hyatt is within walking distance of many other upscale designer fashion houses as well as ultra-trendy bars and restaurants.

    The hotel spa is available exclusively for hotel guests. Featuring a separate male and female sections with two saunas, three luxurious treatment rooms and a relaxation area. The state-of-the-art fitness centre boasts high-tech equipment and the outdoor swimming pool is on hand for a dip.

    The Terrace is the place to choose in spring and summer to relax during the day or at night indulge in delicious tapas washed down with a cocktail or two.

    PERA PALACE

    When the worldwide famous Orient Express chose Istanbul as its last stop in the East The Pera Palace Hotel in Tepebasi, was founded in 1892.

    Rooms are classic and uniquely furnished with original 19th Century features, including precious "White Carrara" marbles and exquisite "Murano-glass" chandeliers make Pera Palace Hotel suites the ideal place to experience century - old heritage. At the same time, expertly installed state-of-the-art technology and new technological services guarantee the comfort of a luxury-class hotel. Deluxe Pera rooms overlook the legendary streets of the district and many boast a private balcony. Choose a Deluxe Golden Horn View Room for spectacular sunsets.

    Room 101 was the favourite room of Mustafa Kemal Atatürk, the great leader and founder of modern-day Turkey. In 1981 on the occasion of his 100th birthday anniversary, the great leader's room was turned into a museum in which many of his personal belongings are now exhibited.

    The Agatha Restaurant, pays tribute to the three major stops of the former Orient Express, - Paris, Venice and Istanbul - by serving a combination of French, Italian and Turkish tastes.

    Patisserie de Pera returns as a classic French patisserie with excellent service offering delicious Turkish and French inspired handmade cakes, gateaux and homemade chocolates in a nostalgic ambiance.

    The traditional Turkish bath Pera SPA boasts a classic heated marble platform and foam bath, the traditional Turkish bath takes you back to the bygone, slower paced days of Istanbul. The SPA also has a steam bath, jacuzzi, sauna and a fully equipped gym. The indoor jet-streamed swimming pool offers the healing power of water both in summer and winter.
