SUGGESTED NICOYA PENINSULA ACCOMMODATION

  • CASA CALETAS

    Located on the beautiful and unexplored Gulf of Nicoya in Guanacaste, Casa Caletas occupies a perfect spot on the beautiful beaches of Coyote, Caletas, Manzanillo and Ma Pais.

    The Casa itself is a wonderful building, traditionally constructed with brightly painted adobe walls, red tiles roofs and shaded verandas. Inside, the public areas, like the spacious lounge, are light and airy - again, with brightly coloured walls offset by wood and bamboo detailing. The rooms follow a similar theme and are lovely, homely spaces complete with large comfortable beds and enriched with local craft pieces.

    There is no shortage of things to do whilst staying at this romantic hideaway. Allow those adventurous urges to take hold, with a kayak tour following the beautiful coastline of Nicoya, or mangrove tours providing privileged access to this fascinating and diverse habitat.

    FLORBLANCA RESORT

    Florblanca Resort boasts one of the most beautiful settings in Costa Rica, its villas dotted along little pathways deep in attractive jungle gardens. This is exclusive, secluded luxury at its finest.

    The ten villas lie nestled in the jungle and are designed with a quiet elegance in mind, using local arts and crafts to add individual touches to each. All feature spacious open air living areas and sumptuous bathrooms in a garden setting and with outdoor, sunken baths. The air-conditioned bedrooms are fitted with king size beds adorned with fine linens. Two bedroom villas are equipped with two twin beds and a day bed in the second bedroom.

    Throughout the accommodation, and the public areas, no detail has been overlooked in creating fabulous spaces guaranteed to create as sense of peace and well-being. Meanwhile, given the setting, there is a huge range of activities to choose from - surfing, fishing, hiking, 4x4 tours, jungle excursions. The list goes on and on.

    FOUR SEASONS RESORT COSTA RICA

    The Four Seasons is Costa Rica's first true luxury resort and stands in a privileged location amongst a profusion of landscapes and nature. Spilling down a hillside to an expanse of golden sand, it creates an ambience of simple luxury in this pristine natural setting.

    The 153 rooms, suites and residences are all designed to capture the exotic character of Costa Rica, with comfortable rattan and bamboo furnishings, local artwork and indigenous wood and stone finishes. Each seamlessly blends indoor comfort with an open-air furnished terrace or veranda - ideal for soaking up the mesmerising views.

    Guest rooms are situated within three four-storey buildings, which are set on the water's edge on both sides of the isthmus. Canopy Suites are like tree houses on the hillside, offering views through the forest canopy down to the water, whilst residences provide all the conveniences of a private home, including full kitchen and laundry facilities.

    LATITUDE 10

    Latitude 10 is an exclusive tropical retreat with impeccable eco-credentials. Standing where the mighty Pacific Ocean merges with one of Costa Rica's greatest beaches, bringing together surf, white sand beaches and jungles, Latitude 10 is a unique Jungle Beach Resort.

    The resort's five open air luxury villas are beautifully shrouded by the jungle for the ultimate private, romantic hideaway. Inside the villas, an eclectic, early 20th century tropical-colonial atmosphere prevails, whilst touches such as the unforgettable open-sky bathrooms add a large helping of luxury.

    The outdoor areas are wonderful too. Hammocks are strung up between palm trees on the beach, whilst the reserve is home to tropical forests, world class surf and amazing sunsets over the Pacific Ocean.

    PUNTA ISLITA

    Standing in harmony with its neighbouring communities, Hotel Punta Islita is a premium beachfront getaway redolent with the charm, culture, and bewildering natural biodiversity of Costa Rica and the Guanacaste province. Stylish yet relaxed and packed with facilities, it is perfect for family vacations, romantic getaways, honeymoons, and relaxation escapes.

    The property's tiered layout ensures excellent ocean and cove views from all guestrooms, which are set in secluded 'cabin style' buildings. Accommodation choices range from spacious deluxe rooms and suites, to casitas and villas with private pools. All are beautifully put together, leaning heavily on cool, tiled floors and neutral wall colours, and enlivened by splashes of colour from freshly cut flowers, soft furnishings and mosaic tiles in the bathrooms. Meanwhile, exposed wood detail lends an authentic, beach cabin flavour.
