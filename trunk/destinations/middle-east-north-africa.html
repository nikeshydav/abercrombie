TRAVEL DestinationS

Middle East & north Africa


Time travel into the richness of Morocco's streets with A&K's special holidays. A place where the ancient-existence of the country is still intact, you must experience the labyrinth streets to get the feel of Morocco.

Old shops, tradesmen, women behind the shutters and souqs give a vivid feel of the Moroccan delight. Go on an enroute trip to Egypt and do a sightseeing of all the exotic locales, starting from the Pyramids of Giza to hot sand dunes, majestic Nile River to the calmness of the streets. With A&K's luxurious vessels- Sun Boat III and IV, you can experience the feel of a personalised vessel with private dock.
A trip memorable for all times to come, we make you travel the paths of Thesiger, the British explorer and show you the deserts, architecture and the Wahiba Sands in the Sultanate of Oman, the oldest civilisation in the Arabian Peninsula.
