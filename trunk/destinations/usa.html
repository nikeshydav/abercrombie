Travel To USA


United States of America, 'the Land of Dreams' has been luring travels and explorers from all over the world. From Christopher Columbus in the 15th century to modern day backpackers, people flock towards this nation offering an incredible diversity of landscapes, cultures, and traditions; The glowing coral archipelago of Florida Keys to the endless solitary roads in Nevada desert, the Great Lakes next to Canada or the grand open skies of Montana and the beautiful Pacific islands of Hawaii.

New England is where one can devour charming colonial remnants and coastal quaintness in the towns of Massachusetts, Connecticut, Maine and Vermont. The historical town of Aspen in Colorado is surrounded by a spellbinding landscape of snow capped mountains while the city centre features an upscale shopping area. The multicultural essence of USA is much evident in the names of a number of cities and states, such as the Spanish influence in Los Angeles and San Francisco and Native American roots in Yosemite National Park and Napa Valley.
