Italy - A Family Tour

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 10 days

    A 10 day tour of this stunning country, visit areas of natural beauty, Portofino National Park and the Marble Mountains together with the grand sights of Rome. A fabulous holiday with appeal for the whole family.

    Highlights: Portofino, Cinque Terre, PIsa, Marble Mountains, Balloon Flight, Wineries of the Val d' Orcia region, Rome, Colosseum

  • Day 1

    Arrive Portofino

    Arrive in Portofino, the glamorous jewel of the Italian Riviera.
    Enjoy your first family meal together this evening in Portofino's lively harbour, a great place for people-watching among the luxurious yachts.

    Overnight: Hotel Splendido
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 2

    Private Motor-Yacht in the Cinque Terre

    The Cinque Terre is one of Italy's most precious treasures. Its five lands, or villages; Monterosso, Vernazza, Corniglia, Manarola and Riomaggiore are linked by a rugged, 12 mile coastal path that winds past steeply terraced olive groves and vineyards, white-sand beaches, colourful higgledy-piggledy houses and the most spectacular rocky seascapes imaginable. The best way to explore the villages and the coast of the Italian Riviera is on-board a skippered motor-yacht. Spend the day sailing on the sparkling Mediterranean with your expert guide, taking in the beauty of the Cinque Terre.

    Overnight: Hotel Splendido
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    Hiking in the Portofino National Park

    The promontory of Portofino National Park is one of great natural beauty and a paradise for walkers. Ancient walking paths lead through olive groves and vineyards up to chestnut forests.

    Join your guide for an invigorating hike to the San Fruttuoso Abbey, only accessible by foot or by the sea. The abbey dates back more than 700 years and includes a defence tower to protect it from pirates!

    years and includes a defence tower to protect it from pirates!

    Overnight: Hotel Splendido
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    Off-road Experience in the Marble Mountains, Leaning Tower of Pisa, Pisa's Chocolate Valley

    The mountains between Portofino and Tuscany offer the most highly demanded of the famous white Italian marble, loved by the great sculptors such as Michelangelo and Bernini. Today we have a high octane 4x4 off road experience into the vast marble quarries. Your expert driver will bring you up the along steep, winding, roads. See trucks manoeuvring in the quarries at death-defying angles, and gaze in awe at dazzling landscapes. After climbing nearly 4000 feet you are rewarded with an amazing view, on a good day to the island of Corsica.

    Continue your journey onwards to the world famous city of Pisa with time for lunch and climbing the iconic leaning tower. The last visit of the day is in Pisa's 'chocolate valley' for tastings at an artisanal producer of fine Italian chocolate.

    The day ends at your exclusive private villa on the Castiglion del Bosco estate, pride of the esteemed Ferragamo family.

    Overnight: Castiglion del Bosco
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 5

    Siena Sightseeing, Evening Pizza Party at your villa

    Depart for Siena for a guided tour of the great city. Siena was built on three hills and is enclosed by a set of walls and gates. See the Piazza del Campo, one of the finest main squares in Italy, and the Palazzo Pubblico which has served as Siena's Town Hall since the 1300s. Visit Siena's Duomo, considered to be one of the finest Gothic cathedrals in Italy with unforgettable frescoes in the Piccolomini Library. A highlight of your day in Siena will be private access into one of the Contrada Museums, dedicated to one of the quarters of the city that participates in the bi-annual Palio horse race. Here you can gain an insight into the centuries of pomp, pride and pageantry behind today's spectacle.

    Return to your luxury villa in the afternoon with your private pool inviting you in for a dip. In the evening a pizzaiola will arrive to fire up the pizza oven and create a freshly baked private pizza party for you in the grounds of your villa!

    Overnight: Castiglion del Bosco
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 6

    Balloon Flight and Activity Day

    This morning, enjoy a special experience, floating above the rolling Tuscan countryside during a private hot air balloon flight (subject to weather conditions). The peace and serenity of exploring Tuscany in this way is one not to be missed. Return to the hotel in the for breakfast.

    The remainder of the day is at leisure to take advantage of the many on-site activities (payable locally) or simply relax and drink in the special atmosphere.

    Activities on the estate include:
    . Tours of the winery
    . Championship members/guests only golf course
    . Horseback riding
    . Hiking trails
    . Mountain biking trails
    . Extensive spa
    . Cooking school Academy
    . Kids Club

    Overnight: Castiglion del Bosco
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 7

    Pienza and Wineries of the Val d'Orcia

    Today visit the pretty town of Pienza. The center of Pienza was redesigned by Pope Pius II, who wanted to build a model Renaissance square in his native village. Nearby Montepulciano is an archetypal Tuscan hilltop town, set 605 meters above sea level and offering superb views over the surrounding countryside. Inside the ancient city walls and fortifications you will find winding streets and a wealth of Renaissance style palazzi and churches. During your excursion today you will have the opportunity to enjoy a tasting of some of the wonderful Brunello and Montalcino wines of this region.

    INSIDER ACCESS: The Real Italian Pecorino
    Today visit a typical Tuscan farmhouse, located in the hearth of Val d'Orcia, close to Pienza and Montepulciano, to enjoy a private cheese making demonstration. The owner will give you a brief introduction of what they do on their estate followed by an explanation about Pecorino and Ricotta making. You will be able to visit the laboratory and learn about the entire process of cheese making.

    Overnight: Castiglion del Bosco
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 8

    Tuscany to Rome: The Colosseum from a Gladiator's Perspective

    Travel through the countryside of Tuscany and Umbria today to the capital city of Italy, Rome.

    In the afternoon descend into the belly of the most famous and most visited ancient monument in the world, the Colosseum! Stories of the gladiatorial games will become more vivid as you visit where gladiators once held their final preparations before battling with wild beasts. These spaces are preserved in the condition they were in at the end of the fifth century AD, at which time they were covered over and only very recently opened up. Your guide will bring to life the atmosphere of Rome's pulsating heart, crowded with people from across the Roman Empire. Your guide will also explain the other nearby Emperor's forums; Traiana, Augusto and Cesare.

    Enjoy accommodations at Rome's newest urban resort, believed to be the birthplace of Emperor Nero and just a short walk from the Vatican City.

    Overnight: Gran Melia
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 9

    Underground Rome

    Take a journey back in time during your half day (4 hours) excursion accompanied by your expert guide and driver. Explore the unique church of St. Clemente as you descend down three levels and through two thousand years of history! The medieval church was built over the remains of a 4th century basilica which, in turn, covers buildings from the time of the emperor Nero and a sanctuary dedicated to the oriental divinity, Mithras.

    The medieval basilica houses one of the finest mosaics from the period, representing the Tree of Life. Continue to one of Rome's' most important catacombs, those of San Sebastiano. In burying their dead in underground cemeteries outside of the city walls, the early Christians were simply obeying the laws of time. They were not forced to use them because of persecution, as later popular myth has suggested. Many saints were buried here, and the catacombs later became shrines and places of pilgrimage.

    For your final evening in Rome, come together as a family once again with dinner on your own private terrace in an exquisite villa drinking in the panoramic views of the Eternal City.

    Overnight: Gran Melia
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 10

    Depart Rome

    Privately transfer to Rome Airport today.

    Meal Plan: B

CAPRI VIP ISLAND

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 4 days

    Visit the Island of Capri for the ultimate 4 day luxurious and tranquil break. This exclusive island off the coast of Italy is simply stunning and offers the perfect mini-moon destination.

    SPECIAL OFFER- Stay for an additional night at no extra charge in April, May, September and October if guests begin their stay on a Sunday.

    Highlights: Sail on your luxury private motor yacht, swim in the Blue Water Cave and enjoy plenty of time at leisure at your exclusive hotel.

  • Day 1

    ARRIVE CAPRI

    Upon arrival into Naples airport you will be Chauffeur driven to the hydrofoil pier. Cross the Mediterranean to the exclusive island of Capri before transferring to your luxury hotel. Caesar Augustus has the most romantic setting on this iconic and beautiful island sitting 1000 feet above the sea!

    Overnight: Caesar Augustus
    Meal Plan B

  • Day 2

    CAPRI

    Enjoy the wonderful facilities of your hotel today. Children under 10 are not permitted to stay meaning the hotel has a refined and tranquil atmosphere, perfect for relaxation. Why not enjoy the spectacular panoramic outdoor swimming pool, an al fresco massage or simply relax amongst the beautiful flowers which fill the gardens.

    Overnight: Caesar Augustus
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    CAPRI

    Experience Capri today in true style onboard your own luxury motor-yacht (8 hours). After passing Punta Gradola, reach the Blue Grotto where you will have the chance to take a swim in the famously romantic blue water cave. Enjoy time at leisure for shopping or perhaps a lazy swim from the boat before cruising back to your hotel. We can highly recommend the Marina Piccola for a lunch of fresh seafood and people-watching.

    Overnight: Caesar Augustus
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    DEPART CAPRI

    Your chauffeur will bring you back in style to Florence Airport today.

    Meal Plan: B

TUSCANY FINE WINE AND DINING

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 4 days

    A romantic 4 day break to the stunning Italian region of Tuscany. Relax and enjoy the luxurious spa facilities and indulge in a wine and dine experience.

    SPECIAL OFFER - Stay for an additional night at no extra charge throughout 2013.

    Highlights: Florence, Chianti, Tavarnelle, Passignano

  • Day 1

    ARRIVE TUSCANY

    You private chauffeur will drive you from Florence Airport to your hotel in the heart of the stunning Tuscan wine producing region of Chianti.

    The ideal choice for both relaxation and exploration, the luxury sanctuary Castello Del Nero is located within striking distance of Florence and Siena. The carefully restored 12th century castle offers the absolute latest in modern facilities whilst maintaining the atmosphere of its historic surroundings.

    Overnight: Castello del Nero

  • Day 2

    TUSCANY

    Today is at your leisure to enjoy the facilities of your hotel and the archetypal Tuscan village of Tavarnelle, just a short walk away. Your hotel is located in acres of secluded grounds and boasts a large outdoor pool, extensive ESPA signature treatments, thermal suites and a fitness centre as well as two floodlit tennis courts and a heated swimming pool. Other activities include trekking, hot air ballooning and golf.

    Overnight: Castello del Nero
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    TUSCANY - WINE AND DINE EXPERIENCE

    Your chauffeur will bring you into the heart of Chianti today and the fairy-tale location of Passignano. The finest of the renowned noble wines of the Antinori family are stored away in ancient cellars underneath the badia (monastery) which is entirely closed to the public - except for you!

    Enjoy an exclusive tour of the cellars followed by Michelin starred lunchtime dining with delicious accompanying fine wines from the Antinori cellar. The sommelier will be happy to share his in-depth knowledge with you. Fully satiated, your chauffeur will bring you back to the paradise of your hotel.

    Overnight: Castello del Nero
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    DEPART TUSCANY

    Your chauffeur will bring you back in style to Florence Airport today.

    Meal Plan: B

CROATIA BEACH

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 4 Days

    A luxurious and relaxing 4 day visit to the stunning Croatian coastal town of Split.

    Highlights: Explore the Ancient city of Split and enjoy plenty of time at leisure to make the most of your wonderful hotel facilities.

  • Day 1

    ARRIVE IN SPLIT

    Meet your Chauffeur at Split airport and transfer to your hotel which overlooks the bay of Split, the Adriatic sea and the iconic island of Brac.

    Le Meridien Lav, Split is located at Podstrana, five miles south of Split. The beautiful setting features 800 metres of beach frontage, gardens landscaped by award-winning designer Jim Nicolay of Hawaii, and fantastic sea views overlooking Split and the surrounding islands.

    Overnight: Le Meridien Lav

  • Day 2

    SPILT

    Today is at your leisure to enjoy the facilities of your beachfront hotel. The hotel facilities include an outdoor and an indoor pool, tennis courts and a Water Sports Centre which offers scuba diving, waterskiing, sailing, and more. The exclusive spa and wellness centre is a magnificent space offering treatment rooms, relaxation areas and a fitness centre, the perfect place to unwind and feel pampered.

    Overnight: Le Meridien Lav
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    SPLIT

    Today you may wish to explore the ancient city of Split, including the palace which was the 'retirement home' of the roman emperor Hadrian. Alternatively why not enjoy a romantic dinner of fresh oysters overlooking the marina.

    Overnight: Le Meridien Lav
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    SPLIT

    Your chauffeur will bring you back in style to Split Airport today.

    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 5

    Urubamba/Machu Picchu

    Board the deluxe Hiram Bingham train, the most luxurious way to travel the breathtaking landscape to Machu Picchu. Step on board, and you are immersed in an elegant world of polished wood, gleaming cutlery and glittering glass that evokes a romantic ambience. After a light lunch on board, arrive at the breathtaking Incan citadel and begin exploring the ruins in the company of your expert guide. Feel the pulse of this magical place as you visualize the lives of the priests, craftsmen, and servants who inhabited this seemingly uninhabitable citadel and then left, leaving no written records about its destruction. After a busy day of sightseeing, return to your beautiful mountainside retreat, high in the lush Andean clouds forest. It features the look and feel of a rural Andean village set in spectacular grounds with paths that wind past waterfalls and gardens, and trails that wander through wild forest. Snuggle under a thick alpaca blanket in your suite, which offers incredible views of the exotic gardens or the dramatic Vilcanota River.

    Overnight: Inkaterra Machu Picchu Pueblo Hotel
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 6

    Machu Picchu/Cusco

    Return to Machu Picchu with your guide this morning for a final look at the ruins - and for an even more meaningful event for you. A shaman joins you to perform a vow renewal ceremony for you. Stand before the spirits of the Incas and reaffirm your love in this magical place. Afterward, return to the hotel, renewed and refreshed, for lunch. This afternoon, return to Cusco by Vista dome train and settle in at your hotel, where you receive VIP amenities.

    Overnight: Hotel Monasterio
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 7

    Cusco

    A morning city tour takes you to the Cathedral on the Plaza de Armas and the Santo Domingo Temple, a Dominican church and convent built on the foundations of the Koricancha (place of gold), where the Incas worshipped the sun. Continue just outside Cusco to the ruins of Sacsayhuaman, a testimonial to the Incan architectural skills. Try to slip a piece of paper between the cracks of the massive stone blocks, cut and fitted together with incredible precision without a single drop of mortar to hold them in place. Your afternoon is at leisure. Make sure to visit the hotel spa for a relaxing couple's massage. This evening, enjoy a romantic dinner at Limo restaurant, overlooking the main plaza.

    Overnight: Hotel Monasterio
    Meal Plan: B,L

  • Day 8

    Cusco/Lima

    Fly to Lima and transfer to your hotel. The remainder of the day is at leisure to do some last-minute shopping, take a walk along the ocean or enjoy the views from your suite.

    Overnight: Miraflores Park Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 9

    Lima/India

    Transfer to the airport &Board your international departure flight.

    Overnight: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
    Meal Plan: B,L,D
