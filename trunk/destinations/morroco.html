Morocco: Medinas, Mountains and Monuments

On your Morocco travel with A&K India, where you are greeted warmly by an amalgamation of African, Arabic and European cultures. Known for its tolerant nature, the land of Morocco shows an easy blend of cultures and religions. Abercrombie & Kent India’s team will guide you through a first-hand insight into the mixed vibrant culture, delightful art, succulent food, soulful music, ancient architecture and generous hospitality on your Morocco tour.

A country where three M's- Medinas, Mountains and Monuments live in close contact, Morocco is a land of sheer joy for every traveller. Experience the everyday life at lively bazaars and glittering souks in the old medinas, while it rivets you with the rich legacy of historical monuments. Meet the friendly Moroccans, enjoy the ecstasy of stunning panoramas of the Atlas Mountains and stroll in the warm waters of the Atlantic Ocean coastline. Morocco’s main tourist attraction, the Sahara Desert is not to be missed. You may recognize it as a location for many Hollywood movies.

Enjoy a lavish stay at exclusive luxury hotels, private riads or in our adventure-laden mobile desert camps in Sahara Desert for that kick during your Morocco travel. Knowledgeable local guides will take you on a tour of the country where you can view the ancient monuments, splendid palaces and captivating mosques while experiencing the richness of Morocco's natural beauty. A Moroccan holiday with A&K India is truly an experience to cherish for years to come.
