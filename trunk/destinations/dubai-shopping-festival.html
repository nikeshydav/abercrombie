Dubai Shopping Festival 2015

Thursday, 1st January - Sunday, 1st February 2015


Celebrate 20 glorious years of Dubai Shopping Festival (DSF), the largest shopping and entertainment extravaganza in the Middle East! Drawing visitors from across the globe, each year DSF marks a grand beginning to Dubai's calendar of events with incredible shopping deals offered by the world's leading brands, headline-grabbing raffle draws and all-round family entertainment activities, events and promotions. From international concerts to musicals to outdoor and fashion events, Dubai Shopping Festival has something in store for everybody, from the youngest to the oldest of all!
