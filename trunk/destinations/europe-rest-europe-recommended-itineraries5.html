The Netherlands and Belgium

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 10 days

    A 10-day FIT programme taking in the highlights of the Netherlands and Belgium.

    Highlights: Amsterdam, Brussels, Waterloo, Ypres, Bruges.

  • Day 1

    ARRIVE AMSTERDAM

    Upon arrival in Amsterdam you will be met by your local representative and privately transferred to your hotel.

    The remainder of your day is at leisure.

    Overnight: Sofitel Legend The Grand Amsterdam

  • Day 2

    AMSTERDAM

    This morning your English-speaking guide will meet you at your hotel to take you on a half day tour of this beautiful city which will include a two hour private tour of the famous canals by water-taxi. Enjoy the view of the canals and the gabled houses of the mercantile quarter, where you will visit the house in which Anne Frank wrote her celebrated diary during the last war.

    This afternoon is at leisure.

    Overnight: Sofitel Legend The Grand Amsterdam
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    AMSTERDAM - ZAANSE SCHANS - MARKEN - VOLENDAM -AMSTERDAM

    Enjoy a full day sightseeing excursion into the countryside outside Amsterdam with your English-speaking driver-guide. Start your day with a visit to the Zaanse Schans Open-Air Museum. The buildings have been painstakingly moved here from all over the Zaan region, and you will be able to watch a clog-making demonstration, see how the famous Dutch cheeses are produced and watch pottery being hand-painted just like in Delft.

    Later, visit the beautiful villages of Marken and Volendam, two traditional fishing villages which have retained their typical Dutch character. Cosy tea shops, small private museums and fishing boats will set you back in time.

    Overnight: Sofitel Legend The Grand Amsterdam
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    AMSTERDAM

    This morning you will be transferred to the museum quarter for a half day tour. Your guide will take you on a tour of the Rijksmuseum, home to the largest and finest collection of Dutch paintings in the world. You may also wish to visit the Van Gogh Museum which displays many of his finest pieces.

    In the afternoon, see Amsterdam the Dutch way, by bike. Your private cycling guide will meet you for a three hour tour. See the major sights such as the harbour, Jordaan Quarter, Vondel Park and Rembrandt's House. Also see some of the less well known parts of the city such as the smaller canals and hidden courtyards.

    Overnight: Sofitel Legend The Grand Amsterdam
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 5

    AMSTERDAM - BRUSSELS

    Today you will be met at your hotel and privately transferred with your local representative to Amsterdam railway station. Here you will be shown to your pre-reserved 1st class seating and your luggage will be handled onboard for you. Upon arrival at Brussels railway station you will be met from the door of the train and your luggage will be handled for you. You will then be privately transferred to your hotel, Hotel Amigo, where you will be assisted with check-in.

    The remainder of the day is at leisure.

    The Hotel Amigo is one of the latest hotels to be completely refurbished in the Rocco Forte style. Located directly behind the Grande Place, it is ideal for sightseeing or an evening stroll before dinner. The bathrooms contain mosaic details in red or green to match the marble vanity tops, and figures of Herge's characters Tintin, Snowy the dog and Captain Haddock enliven the walls with a touch of humour.

    Overnight: Hotel Amigo
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 6

    BURGUNDY

    This morning your local English-speaking guide and driver will meet you at your hotel for a half day sightseeing tour of the city. Enjoy a walking tour of the old town, which will start with a visit to the Grand'Place. This magnificent square at the heart of the old quarter is considered to be one of Europe's most ornate market squares. Close to the Grand'Place is Brussels' most famous resident, the Manneken Pis, a bronze statue of a small boy watering the fountain. Your tour will then take you further afield to the Horta House, and finally to the Atomium, from the top of which you will enjoy a panoramic view of Brussels.

    This afternoon is at leisure.

    Overnight: Hotel Amigo
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 7

    BRUSSELS - WATERLOO - BRUGES

    After lunch at leisure, your day will continue with a threehour excursion to Waterloo by private vehicle with your English-speaking guide. Visit the battlefield where Napoleon was finally defeated over one hundred years ago. The plain where this battle took place is now dotted with monuments. You will also stop at the Visitor Centre which offers an audio-visual presentation of the progress of the battle and the Panorama Museum which features a vast, circular painting of the battle, executed with amazing perspective and realism.

    This afternoon you will be transferred directly to Bruges where you will check-in at your hotel, Die Swaene. This boutique hotel overlooks one of the most picturesque canals in Bruges.

    Overnight: Die Swaene
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 8

    BRUGES - YPRES - BRUGES

    Today you will leave Bruges for a full day of sightseeing with your English-speaking driver-guide to the First World War battlefields surrounding Ypres. The city of Ypres was virtually wiped off the face of the earth during the Great War. The magnificently re-constructed Cloth Hall houses an interactive museum where you can learn about the events of 1914-1918. Visit the Menin Gate, one of the most moving war memorials, before visiting some of the remaining trenches and cemeteries in the surrounding area.

    Overnight: Die Swaene
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 9

    BRUGES

    Today meet your English-speaking guide for a half day walking tour ofBruges, a beautiful medieval city of canals, often called the "Venice of the North". You will be taken on a tour of the most attractive of the town's waterways and houses. You will visit the market place, and see the celebrated belfry and gothic hall. Enjoy also a visit to the world renowned Groeninge Museum.

    This afternoon is at leisure. You may like to take a short boat trip or give into temptation in one of the town's many chocolate shops.

    Overnight: Die Swaene
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 10

    DEPART BRUSSELS

    This morning you will be privately transferred to Brussels Airport for your departure flight.

    Meal Plan: B
