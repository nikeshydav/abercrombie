Cambodia: The Heart of Indochina

The Kingdom of Cambodia is located in the heart of mainland Southeast Asia. Explore this mystical land rich with heritage, a glorious past and a concurrence of various cultural influences with A&K India on your Cambodia travel journey. Historically known as Kambuja, Cambodia was a capital of the Khmer Empire that went on to thrive in the region for over 600 years. It was also during this period when the remarkable Angkor Wat, the largest religious monument in the world, was built. It is a Hindu and later Buddhist temple built by king Suryavarman II in the early 12th century. Take an Angkor Wat tour with us and explore its grand gopuras, lush green gardens and intricately carved lintels and pediments. Despite having gone through a number of political upheavals in the recent past, Cambodian Holidays have remained one of the most attractive tours in southern Indochina.
