Tahiti Bora Bora

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 8 Nights

    Highlights:Tahiti, Bora Bora, Lagoon cruise with shark & ray discovery, Jet Ski around Bora Bora lagoon, Discovery of Bora Bora Island in a 4x4, Sunset & Romantic dinner.

  • Day 1

    ARRIVAL TAHITI

    Arrival to Tahiti. Meet & Greet with flower lei, document remittance and transfer to the Radisson Plaza Resort in a private car. Check in to Ocean view Jacuzzi room, 1 night with American breakfast included.
    If your arrival is on a morning flight, it is also possible to proceed directly to Bora Bora.

    Overnight: Radisson Plaza Resort

  • Day 2

    TAHITI - BORA BORA

    Transfer to Tahiti airport and take off on a 50 min flight to Bora Bora. Upon arrival, the St Regis Bora Bora staff will meet you directly at the airport and take you by boat to the resort.

    Check in to your Deluxe Overwater bungalow, 6 nights, American breakfast included for two.

    Facing the main island of Bora Bora and at 1,550 square feet, the Deluxe Overwater One Bedroom Villas are the largest fleet in the Pacific. Tastefully designed in exotic woods and marble; and featuring a private terrace and gazebo they easily translate to islands of tranquility.

    Overnight: St Regis Bora Bora

  • Day 3

    BORA BORA

    Suggested excursion: Lagoon cruise with shark & ray discovery
    A must on Bora Bora is to cruise around the lagoon, snorkel in coral gardens and admire the sting rays & sharks. The boat will take you to the most beautiful spots of the lagoon with the opportunity to feed rays, watch sharks & swim in a coral garden. It will also be a fantastic photo opportunity. Your morning will end with a stop on an islet for some fresh fruits and juice.

    Available by private boat or as a regular tour.

    Overnight: St Regis Bora Bora

  • Day 4

    BORA BORA

    Not to miss, The St. Regis Bora Bora 13,000 square foot world-class spa and fitness center is located on its own private island in the lagoon. The facility houses Tahitian and Pacific Rim treatments in seven luxurious spa treatment rooms and a 24-hour Techno gym fitness center. The use of natural herbs, extracts from the local beauty products as monoi oil, pure mother of pearl, and luminescent pearl powder, will create an unforgettable revitalizing experience.

    Overnight:St Regis Bora Bora

  • Day 5

    BORA BORA

    Suggested excursion: Guided tour by Jet Ski around Bora Bora lagoon
    This 2 hour circle island tour by Jet Ski is an opportunity to really enjoy yourself while discovering the most beautiful lagoon of the world. You will be amazed of the various shades of blue & turquoise of the lagoon while you follow your guide on your own Jet Ski (one or two persons per Jet Ski). A stop is included on a white sandy beach where some fresh fruits will be served. During a second stop on a ―motu‖ (small islet), enjoy a ―coconut show‖ – your guide demonstrates how to climb the coconut tree and how to open a coconut.

    Overnight:St Regis Bora Bora

  • Day 6

    BORA BORA

    Suggested excursion: Discovery of Bora Bora Island in a 4x4
    Discover the culture, history, fauna and flora of the island. Learn about the ancient temples of the great Tahitian seafaring society built around 1000AD & learn about the local legends visiting ancient archeological sites such as the Marae "temple", and the ―Turtle stone‖, located in Bora Bora's very own rain forest.
    In Faanui valley you'll spot small fruit plantations and learn about some of the local flora including medicine plants used by the ancient Medicine man, such as the Nono and the Hotu tree. Explore also the World War 2 sites of the Cannons and the bunkers and find out why Americans used Bora Bora as a staging area during WWII and their influence on the locals of Bora Bora. Other stops include various look out points around the island, such as "Top of the World " where you'll get to see Bora Bora on the West and East side and the Matira look out from where you'll see all various colors of the lagoon.

    Overnight:St Regis Bora Bora

  • Day 7

    BORA BORA

    Enjoy another relaxing day at the resort. In the evening we suggest another unforgettable tour :

    Sunset & Romantic dinner

    To end this very special holiday on Bora Bora, enjoy a sunset & a romantic dinner on an islet. As the day fades, a transfer by boat will take you directly from the pontoon of your hotel to a private islet. You will be comfortably seated in your lounge chair, facing the sunset, with a glass of punch. After this magical moment it’s time for a romantic dinner on a nicely dressed table. Maximum 6 persons (it is also possible to book a private evening).

    Menu:

    Appetizer : Cream of Uru (breadfruit)
    First course: Tahaa Vanilla Foie Gras with its fruit chutney
    Main Course: Plancha of fish, chicken, shripms (& lobster upon request - supplement)
    Dessert: Vanilla Creme Brulee
    One bottle of Champagne, wine, soft drinks, water, tea/coffee

    Overnight:St Regis Bora Bora

  • Day 8

    BORA BORA / TAHITI

    Take off to Papeete and connect to your international flight.

    • END OF ABERCROMBIE & KENT'S ARRANGEMENTS

    WE TRUST YOU ENJOYED YOUR TIME WITH US AND HOPE YOU COME BACK!

    Haere Ra!

    Goodbye!
