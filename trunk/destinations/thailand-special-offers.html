  • Private Islands & Beach Escapes - Exclusive Holiday Offers

    If you are looking for a quiet and relaxing holiday in combination with unseen islands, beautiful private beaches and white sands, Thailand has a variety of options for you! Just three kilometers southeast of Phuket, you can visit the Koh Hae (Coral Island) or head nineteen kilometers south of Phuket for the breathtaking Koh Racha with crystal Clear Waters.

    From the east coast of Phuket, one can also opt to escape to Koh Yao Yai and Koh Yao Noi, which houses some very high-end resorts. An hour drive from Phuket is the picturesque Phang Nga Bay known for emerald green waters. From the famous tourist town of Krabi, head south West for the spacious island of Koh Lanta known for its great snorkeling. Koh Mook in the South is known for the Emerald Cave (Morakot Cave) and natural surroundings. The sea around Koh Tao (off Koh Samui) provides some of the best diving in the Gulf of Thailand. A sand bar dividing the islands at low tide gives this setting true paradise status. Koh Chang in the Eastern coast of Thailand is one of Thailand's most beautiful islands with long white sandy beaches, most half deserted. The island and its vicinity are great places for snorkeling, diving and jungle hiking. Koh Kut, also in the east has some breath-taking beaches, Bang Bao Bay is known for its calm water. Koh Mak, situated in the East of the Gulf of Thailand, has many long sandy beaches, and a few hills. Koh Mak is popular amongst tourists for it's white sandy beaches and clear blue water. The island is small and flat with coconut plantations and mangrove vegetation along the coastline. Thung Wua Laen Beach in Chumphon Province, offers white-fine sand, broad area, and important diving spots (Koh Ngam Noi, Koh Ngam Yai, Koh Jorake), and also convenient facilities such as resorts and restaurants.
