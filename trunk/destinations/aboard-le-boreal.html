Aboard le Boreal

Cruise to the Arctic with confidence and style aboard the all-balcony ‘Le Boreal’

An A&K journey to the Arctic offers an opportunity to experience the very best of the polar region with the finest expedition staff and most comprehensive itineraries, all from on board the comfortable, secure and urbane ‘Le Boreal.’ Featuring all-balcony staterooms and suites, this incredible expedition vessel offers an ideal way to experience the Arctic with A&K.

‘Le Boreal’ Video Tour


Strength and Style

Sophisticated design, ground-breaking marine technology and a convenient layout aided by three passenger elevators are among Le Boreal’s many features. She offers a single-seating dining room, al fresco dining area, an outdoor heated pool and a theater, as well as a spacious gathering area and library. A full-service salon, steam room, spa and fitness center complete the amenities.

On other cruises to different destinations, she carries more than 264 passengers; on voyages with A&K, we limit her capacity to just 199 guests. Fewer passengers in turn allow for more shore excursions each day and cabins with private balconies for every guest.

Safety

Safety is first and foremost on board sturdy ‘Le Boreal.’ The ship features the latest communication, navigational and safety equipment. In addition, A&K’s English-speaking staff is readily available to help out and instruct on safety at all times during your cruise.

Environmentally Sound Operations

In keeping with A&K’s longstanding commitment to environmental conservation, ‘Le Boreal’ is equipped with the most eco-friendly technology available today.

Rest and Relaxation Redefined

Conclude each day of discovery – and recharge for the next day’s adventures – in your very own balcony stateroom or suite, an A&K exclusive on board ‘Le Boreal.’ All 108 cabins are fully equipped and feature bedding configurations that range from king-size beds to two single beds. Premium and Deluxe Two-Room Suites feature a pull out sofa that converts to a king-size bed, and the option to convert into a two-room cabin – perfect for families.

Cabin amenities include a mini bar, safe, flat-screen in-suite TV with movies on demand and on-board channels, iPod docking station, individually controlled heat/air conditioning, wireless Internet access and telephone. Bathrooms come equipped with a shower (or shower/bath), hair dryer, designer amenities and bathrobes. Laundry services and 24-hour room service are available throughout your cruise. ‘Le Boreal’ is also outfitted with a technologically advanced stabilizing system to minimize the effects of rough conditions and help keep passengers at ease at all times.

Space to Meet and Mingle

Spacious and elegant, ‘Le Boreal’s common areas invite casual conversation with fellow passengers, as well as private reflection and views in quiet corners. The Panoramic Lounge and Terrace offer spacious areas ideal for social gatherings, afternoon tea or just relaxing with ocean views. The Club provides the perfect evening place, offering an elegant atmosphere with live music and dancing. The Theatre is a show lounge with unhindered views for all and the ideal venue for nightly entertainment or a variety of special functions, such as enrichment lectures and movies. An exceptional library offers a wide range of titles with extended reading areas and passenger computer stations with Internet access.

International Cuisine

‘Le Boreal’ features two restaurants: The Restaurant offers gourmet and international cuisine while The Compass Grill delivers casual indoor and outdoor dining for breakfast, lunch and themed dinners. There is always a selection of light fare and vegetarian dishes, and most special diets can be accommodated with advance notice. In-cabin room service is also available 24/7.

Scheduled Bridge Visits

A visit to the bridge can be arranged by the captain.
