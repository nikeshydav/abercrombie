SUGGESTED ABU DHABI ACCOMMODATION

  • Desert Islands Resort And Spa By Anantara

    Desert Islands Resort & Spa by Anantara, originally the guesthouse of His Highness Sheikh Zayed, is a secluded hideaway where Arabic décor coupled with natural beauty evoke a unique mystique. Located on the beach with the azure of turquoise waters safe for swimming and snorkeling with the backdrop of the desert hills create this unique resort style hotel. Accessing the wonderful Desert Islands Resort is straightforward be driven to the jetty on the mainland and enjoy a scenic transfer by the resort's boat, or fly from Al Bateen Airport with one of the scheduled airlines, or private jet.


    Dining at the resort is exceptional with four different restaurants offering a variety of cuisines form authentic Middle Eastern, traditional afternoon tea and the most sumptuous fresh seafood. There is an endless list of activities from archery to discovering the wildlife sanctuary, indulging in the spa and keeping fit with a game of tennis or a swim. The hotel also boasts an excellent watersports centre offering PADI dive courses for the experienced or the novice.

    Hyatt Capital Gate

    Hyatt Capital Gate Abu Dhabi offers 189 five-star hotel rooms and luxury suites occupying the18th to 33rd floors of the Capital Gate building. The accommodation at the hotel is sleek and modern and offers commanding views of the city below. With five restaurants and a fabulous in room dinning service all created to indulge culinary senses in the epitome of luxury.


    The hotel's signature restaurant, 18°, invites guests seeking an exquisite experience to sample authentic creations while relaxing with panoramic views of the city. A unique pool deck with stunning views of the city is the perfect place to relax with a chilled drink and a good book. The architecture of this hotel is superb and is one of the main highlights of this ultra-modern hotel.

    InterContinental Abu Dhabi

    Located on the only hill in Abu Dhabi and offering great views, The Intercontinental Abu Dhabi is a great leisure property with outstanding dining options. The Beach area is ideal for Children's activities. The Health Club offers the weary a fast track to revitalization. A fully equipped gymnasium, Tennis courts and squash courts are also on hand if you are looking for a more energetic activity.


    Dining options at InterContinental Abu Dhabi are wide and varied from the Brazilian Churrascaria & Bar serving exquisite meats straight from the skewer to the accompaniment of live Latin music to The Yacht Club Bar & Galley with its stunning marina views and Asian cuisine any time of the day or night. Venturing out is made easy with the complimentary shuttle that takes you within the vicinity of the hotel.

    Park Hyatt Abu Dhabi Hotel & Villas

    Park Hyatt Abu Dhabi boasts 306 beautifully appointed rooms and suites; the accommodation is modern with Arabian flair and luxurious amenities including a Hans Groche rain shower and a free standing Toto bathtub. Each room or suite has beautiful oak flooring encapsulating the opulence that this lavish living space offers.


    Three different restaurants await to tantalize even the most seasoned traveller and all with a sea view and elegant surroundings offering cuisine from each corner of the world. Allow the team at Camp Hyatt the children's club to inspire with an excellent array of indoor and outdoor activities.

    St Regis Saadiyat Island

    The prestigious St Regis Saadiyat Island has an enviable location on the 9km protected white sandy beach of Saadiyat with views over the warm azure seas of the Persian Gulf. The pristine Saadiyat Island being 500 meters off the coast of Abu Dhabi is within a twenty minute drive of the International airport and a ten minute drive to the bustling centre of Abu Dhabi.


    The Mediterranean architecture offers sophisticated guest rooms and suites all with butler service, large balconies and elegant bathrooms. Superior Rooms have resort views, whilst the Superior Sea View Rooms boast commanding views of the turquoise ocean. Dining at the St Regis is extremely pleasurable with the restaurants offering Mediterranean, Arabian, Seafood and Asian cuisine, delight in the luxurious surroundings and the relaxed atmosphere that this beach resort offers.
