  • CASA TURIRE

    Casa Turire is an elegant boutique hotel reminiscent of a colonial plantation home. Surrounded by lush tropical gardens teeming with toucans, and parakeets, its 16 high-ceilinged rooms are colourfully decorated with locally sourced hardwoods and furnishings.

    All the rooms have beautiful wood floors, open ceilings and private balconies offering wonderful views of the hills. Each balcony is equipped with furniture made from wood cultivated in the plantations, whilst the beds are made of fine timber. Floral linens and stucco walls soften the overall feel.

    Casa Turire is set on a working hacienda, with its own stable of more than 15 horses among many other working animals. Take the opportunity to ride up to many breath taking lookout points with your genuine vaquero or local cowboy acting as a guide.

    PACUARE LODGE

    Pacuare Jungle Lodge is a superb eco-retreat tucked away deep in the heart of Costa Rica's most pristine rainforest environment, on the banks of the Pacuare River. Surrounded by nature in its purest and most primitive state, the lodge runs a great many initiatives designed to in minimising the environmental impact of local tourism - from recycling and renewable energy to community initiatives.

    The lodge's nineteen bungalows and main building were designed and constructed to blend with the surrounding environment. Indeed they have been recognized by the World Tourism Organization as one of only sixty-five examples throughout the world of good practice in sustainability and ecotourism.

    Constructed from local woods and palm thatch collected by Cabecar Indians, the eight cosy bungalows offer a simple, stylish luxury. Inside, they are light and airey spaces, using exposed woods, clean lined furniture and flowing fabrics to create a sense of stripped-back chic. For the ultimate in eco-luxury, the signature suites have private plunge pools.
