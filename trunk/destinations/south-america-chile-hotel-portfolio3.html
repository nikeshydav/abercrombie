Suggested Patagonia accommodation

  • Explora Patagonia

    Perched on the shores of Lake Pehoé in Torres del Paine National Park, Explora Patagonia was conceived as an exploration base and offers spectacular views of Patagonia's astonishing natural beauty.

    Explora Patagonia is the only hotel at the center of Torres del Paine National Park - home to diverse and pristine ecosystems and the place to be if you want to explore the true scale and beauty of Patagonia.

    In terms of architecture, the hotel is designed to integrate seamlessly with its surroundings. A long building intended to contrast with the vertical form of Macizo del Paine, it is painted white to blend in with the snow and ice, whilst the external cladding is constructed of cement and wood beams - a combination able to withstand the Patagonian climate's wide-ranging temperatures and strong winds.

    Remota Lodge

    Remota's huge windows bring the splendour of the Patagonian wilderness into the comfort of spacious and well-appointed rooms and guest areas. The hotel's Finnish sauna is the place to go to refresh tired limbs after a day of exploration.

    Remota brings designer chic and stunning, creative architecture to the rugged landscapes of Patagonia. The building is designed to stand in total harmony with its surroundings and is inspired by the large farmstead buildings of yesteryear. 'Inspired' is the word, because this hotel is far from traditional. Modern design brings together huge windows punctuated by soaring, asymmetric pillars and rich, dark wood - whilst gravel pathways connect the individual buildings.

    Termas de Puyuhuapi

    Amid dense green forest, along the banks of Dorita Bay and accessible only by dirt road or by sea lies a raw paradise overlooking a magnificent remote and pristine landscape. The Termas de Puyuhuapi has just 30 bay view rooms and a location to die for if you are looking for an escape from modern hustle and bustle.

    Explore the temperate rainforest of the Queulat National Park, mountain bike along the Carretera Austral, sea kayak through fjords and between islands, enjoy relaxing thermal baths in outdoor pools or simply luxuriate with Thalassotherapy and massages in the beautiful spa.
