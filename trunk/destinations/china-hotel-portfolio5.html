Suggested Shanghai accommodation

  • Four Seasons Hotel Shanghai

    With its superb location in downtown Shanghai, this elegant Four Seasons hotel brings 37 storeys of stretched glass tower, filled with lavish rooms and suites.

    Inside it is stylish and slick - from the décor and furnishings to the service. Its lavish rooms and suites are beautifully put together, combining warm tones and dark antique woods along with the renowned Four Season's bed. All have marble bathrooms with both deep soaking tubs and separate showers. Modern, uncluttered and supremely comfortable, they are oases of calm above the the bustling metropolis.

    Hyatt On The Bund Shanghai

    Situated on the banks of the Huang Pu River, this leading hotel has an unbeatable location at the northern end of the Bund with convenient access to major areas of the city.

    Modern and minimalist, yet plush and comfortable, rooms are decorated in a clean, simple style with quality furnishings and fittings and state-of-the-art facilities. All have marble bathrooms with rain showers and soaking tubs, but the stars of every room are the floor to ceiling windows and the spectacular views beyond.

    Read more

    JIA Shanghai

    Set in a stunning 1920s building in the heart of Shanghai's prime shopping area, JIA is a design-led masterpiece of urban chic. Taking its name from the Mandarin for 'home', this stylish hotel combines comfort and luxury to strike the perfect balance between a home and a hotel.

    All the rooms are superb studies in style. Reminiscent of upscale city apartments, they are packed with one-off furniture pieces and wonderful modern artworks. Décor combines pure whites to highlight sheer, clean lines, and muted earth tones to create an understated sense of sophistication. Meanwhile washed oak flooring and fabric wall paneling add warmth and individuality to each room. Finally, all feature fully equipped kitchenette/kitchen facilities and Bisazza-tiled bathrooms.

    The Langham

    One glance at this iconic art deco building confirms that this is a singular hotel in Shanghai. Beyond the enchanting facade are 96 exquisitely appointed rooms and suites - each designed to recapture the glamour and grandeur of the era when Shanghai was famed as the "Paris of the East".

    Here you will also find the 'service with poise' and magnificent surroundings that have become synonymous with The Langham since 1865. Encounter the charm of old Shanghai, now presented with every modern luxury at The Langham, Yangtze Boutique - a truly unique hotel in Shanghai..

    Read more

    The Peninsula Shanghai

    The Peninsula Shanghai offers 235 supremely comfortable and stylish rooms and suites. Each room takes inspiration from the Art Deco period and "Golden Age" when Shanghai was the Paris of the East, and blends traditional Peninsula standards of luxury and state-of-the-art technology with historical references.

    With street access and its own entrance from the Lobby, The Peninsula arcade features 25 leading luxury fashion and jewellery brands. The hotel is also just minutes from the heart of Shanghai's exciting shopping district Nanjing Lu. After a day of exploring, escape the fast moving city with a leisurely Spa journey at The Peninsula Spa by ESPA.

    The PuLi

    The PuLi Hotel & Spa is a new Urban Resort Concept that blends the immediacy and convenience of being in Shanghai's most central location with the quiet, emotional indulgences of a peaceful, luxurious resort. It's like being away from it all and right in the heart of it all at once.

    Simple and authentic touches of historic and cultural reference sit side by side with new benchmarks in innovation, leading edge technology and design that pervade throughout its contemporary urban spaces. The quality of the hotel's facilities is only matched by its service delivery. One that is handcrafted, genuine and complete and one that allows you to relive the traditions of Shanghai's famed hospitality.
