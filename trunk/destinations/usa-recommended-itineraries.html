CLASSIC CALIFORNIA

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 10 Nights

    Highlights: Vibrant San Francisco and LA, Drive the dramatic coastal Highway 1, Yosemite National Park

  • Day 1

    India/San Francisco

    Depart from India today to San Francisco. Surrounded on three sides by the Pacific Ocean and San Francisco bay, the city crowds the tip of the San Francisco Peninsula. Stay at The Mandarin Oriental San Francisco for two nights.

  • Day 2

    San Francisco

    Today you may like to take a boat ride out to Alcatraz, a journey on one of the famous cable cars, wander around Fisherman's wharf or take advantage of the excellent location of the hotel. Make your day complete with a spectacular evening performance at the San Francisco Symphony (schedule permitting).

  • Day 3

    Golden Gate/Sausalito

    Collect your rental car this morning and take the short drive across the magnificent Golden Gate Bridge to the luxurious Cavello Point Lodge. Nearby is the Mediterranean-like city of Sausalito - boutiques, cafés and yacht clubs create a feel of sophisticated charm.

  • Day 4

    Sausalito/Yosemite

    Today ascend into the Sierra Nevada en route to fabled Yosemite National Park. Experience the edifice of Half Dome, the granite wall of El Capitan and the vista of the Merced River. Your hotel is the legendary Ahwahnee Hotel, one of the most distinctive resort hotels in the USA.

  • Day 5

    Yosemite

    Full of giant sequoias, dramatic vistas, hikes and trails, Yosemite is a paradise for the nature lover. Spend the day in the park making the most of the wildlife viewing opportunities by going to some of our favourite hidden spots that many visitors never see.

  • Day 6

    Mariposa/Carmel

    Drive west today and pass through the 'Western' town of Mariposa before joining the famous Highway 1. Explore Monterey with its unique buildings before visiting the upmarket town on Carmel.

  • Day 7

    Big Sur

    Continue your drive along the most dramatic section of Highway 1, regarded as one of the world's most beautiful drives, where the road hugs the cliffs with the ocean crashing far below. Stop off regularly to make the most of the excellent views, before reaching the Post Ranch Inn on the cliffs of Big Sur, the ultimate luxurious escape for the night.

  • Day 8

    Santa Barbara

    Spend the morning exploring Hearst Castle on a private tour. Formerly the retreat of William Randolph Hearst, the castle is packed with European antiques. Continuing along Highway 1, the road reaches Santa Barbara - a wine lover's paradise.

  • Day 9

    Malibu/Los Angeles

    Pass through Malibu before arriving into Los Angeles and check into what the locals call 'The Pink Palace' or The Beverly Hills Hotel. A place to see and be seen, the hotel is the best spot in LA to catch famous faces or to revel in the splendid luxury.

  • Day 10

    Los Angeles

    Explore Beverly Hills, with all its sophistication, designer shopping and unique architecture. Take in Mulholland Drive that snakes through the Hollywood Hills and provides magnificent views. A private tour of a film studio can be arranged, or a performance at the incredible Disney Theatre.

  • Day 11

    Los Angeles/India

    The morning is free to enjoy your final hours in California before a drive to the airport in time for your overnight flight back to the INDIA.

HIDDEN YELLOWSTONE AND TETON

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 6 Nights

    Yellowstone is a haven for nature lovers. It is a chance to encounter black bears, elk, moose and pelicans, to name just a few of the unique creatures living within its boundaries, and to see the pointed peaks of the Grand Tetons and the stunning vistas provided by the vast national parks.

    Highlights: See the peaks of the Grand Tetons. Encounter Bison, Elk and Black Bears. Search for bears and wolves by sunset

  • Day 1

    Jackson Hole, Wyoming

    Arrive in Jackson Hole, a traditional 'Western' town on the edge of Grand Teton National Park. After settling in to the Four Seasons Resort, enjoy a welcome dinner and informal orientation.

  • Day 2

    Jackson Hole/Grand Teton National Park

    This morning see the magnificent mountains and wildlife of Grand Teton National Park, including elk, bison and eagles. In the afternoon sail along the Snake River as you look out for moose, pelicans and even black bears. After your adventures, continue to the Jenny Lake Lodge.

  • Day 3

    Grand Teton National Park/Yellowstone National Park

    After breakfast spend a few last hours searching for moose at Oxbow Bend, before continuing to the world's first national park, Yellowstone. The park was established in 1872 and is known for its many geothermal features - the Old Faithful Geyser is one of Yellowstone's most popular features. Spend the night at Old Faithful Inn, where you will have the chance this evening to explore the fumaroles and nearby hot springs.

  • Day 4

    Yellowstone National Park

    Spend the morning with the largest land mammal in North America, the bison. En-route to the Lamar Valley in the north east corner of the park, take time to stop and enjoy the Mammoth Hot Springs. This evening a sunset walk with a specialist guide will take you close to bears and wolves, before continuing to the rustic Alpine Inn in Cooke City, just outside the park.

  • Day 5

    Yellowstone National Park

    Your morning tour begins in Hayden Valley for encounters with herds of bison and elk, and continues to the 'Grand Canyon of Yellowstone' for remarkable views over the whole expanse of the park. After a wonderful lunch at the Lake Yellowstone Hotel, where you will spend the night, enjoy an afternoon of hiking and wildlife watching.

  • Day 6

    Yellowstone National Park/Jackson Hole

    This morning explore the shores of Lake Yellowstone and West Thumb Geyser Basin before travelling back to Grand Teton National Park. After lunch you will have a few hours to see Mormon Row and Antelope Flats, before a farewell dinner at the Wort Hotel, where you will spend your final night.

  • Day 7

    Jackson Hole/India

    This morning is yours to explore the town of Jackson Hole, before being driven to the airport in time for your return flight home. We can arrange flights via Chicago, Denver or Salt Lake City - why not extend your trip for a few days to explore one of these cities.
