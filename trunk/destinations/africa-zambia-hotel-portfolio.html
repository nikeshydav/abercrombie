Suggested Lower Zambezi accommodation

  • Chiawa

    Holding a prime position in the heart of Zambia's Lower Zambezi National Park, the Chiawa camp offers the best guiding in Africa. Blending effortlessly with its idyllic surroundings, the camp provides a retreat where guests receive the biggest of smiles, the friendliest of welcomes and the best of experiences. Sixteen guests are accommodated between five classic tents, one honeymoon tent and three superior tents, all sharing abundant comforts, conveniences and luxuries. Starting at a spacious 42 square metres, the hotel's rooms are the largest in Guilin, whilst its suites offer some of the most luxurious accommodation in the city.

    Enjoy Chiawa's renowned cuisine on the al-fresco dining verandah or relax by the open campfire with a cocktail in hand before retiring to the tents, each one an abode of luxury and peace. Setting new standards in Zambia for bush-luxury, these tents have timber floors, verandas and a plethora of other amenities and offer excellent views. Plush carpets and oversized, comfortable beds heighten the sense of luxurious retreat, while local artworks on the walls lend a sense of peace while adding notes of individuality.

    Enjoy the finest of Africa's safari camps and the wildlife adventure of a lifetime, only at Chiawa, one of the best lodges in Zambia.

    Old Mondoro Bush Camp

    A peaceful camp, ideal for those seeking somewhere really in the wilds, the Old Mondoro Bush Camp is a sister camp of Chiawa in the eastern side of the Lower Zambezi National Park in an open area of faidherbia forest.

    Uncomplicated and unpretentious, it provides guests with an authentic, intimate safari that concentrates on wildlife and bush experience while not forgoing any comforts or service. With no electricity, the camp is entirely lit up by paraffin lamps and candles which create a wonderful romantic ambiance. Overlooking a maze of hippo inhabited islands and exuding charm and charisma like nowhere else, this camp has just four en-suite chalets made of local pole and thatch construction. Set deep in the park it is surrounded with excellent game densities and activities include walking, game drives and boating.

    Sanctuary Zambezi Kulefu Camp

    Gathered around acacia tree shaded Bedouin-style central tent, this small and intimate camp is located on the edge of the Zambezi River. Kulefu means 'the faraway place' in the Gova language (a derivative of Shona). The camp is located in the Lower Zambezi Park dominated by the Zambezi River which channels game into a narrow floodplain, resulting in unparalleled game viewing.

    Sausage Tree Camp

    An intimate and beautiful safari camp nestled in the Lower Zambezi, the Sausage Tree Camp offers excellent food and the best of game viewing in luxurious surroundings. Completely rebuilt in 2008, this elegant camp is an epitome of luxury where sophisticated comforts come to life. Accommodation is in the form of luxurious Bedouin-style tents, discreetly positioned alongside the riverbank and often surrounded by wildlife. The large swimming pool is idyllically situated under shading Mahogany trees.

    Each of the five signature tents has a unique open air en-suite bathroom and sliding teak doors opening onto private decks with amazing views of the Zambezi River. Two lavish Honeymoon Suites each boast of indoor and outdoor bathrooms, dining area, bar and a king size bed overlooking the private infinity pool set into the expansive teak deck.

    Kigela House is the most recent addition to the Sausage Tree Camp and consists of two spacious bedrooms; Added in 2009, it is furnished with en-suite bathrooms complete with a bath tub, twin showers and basins. A fully equipped bar and a dining table for private dining are added highlights. A private butler, 24 hour power and abundant hot and colt treated water are other amenities. The camp serves delicious Africa inspired cuisine in a variety of unexpected locations complete with impeccable service.
