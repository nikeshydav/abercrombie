Suggested Amman accommodation

  • Four Seasons Hotel Amman

    Your contemporary yet classic styled room or suite offers Amman's finest setting, presenting breathtaking views of the cityscape. Take a swim in the indoor pool or relax with a dip in the adjacent whirlpool.

    Between the prestigious Al Sweifiyah residential area and the Shmeisani financial district, the hotel's dramatic hilltop location affords breathtaking views of the surrounding cityscape. The hotel interior is a dramatic mix of grand architecture and modern chic, with huge marble arches counterpointed by slick, modern decor.

    The rooms are bright and airy and decorated in a contemporary, residential style - all cool, natural colours and beautiful furnishings. Thanks to its ideal location, this hotel is within reach of a wide range of local attractions and cultural sites.

    Grand Hyatt Amman

    Sip an aperitif next to one of the large fireplaces in the fashionable lounge with novel designer furniture, the place to see and be seen. Dine in Indochine, where the beauty and sensuality of 1930s Vietnam is captured.

    Grand Hyatt Amman Hotel is a 5-Star luxury hotel located in the business and diplomatic district, Jabal Amman. We describe this hotel as a resort in the city. Inside, the hotel is slick and stylish - the Grand Club lounge is light and airy, whilst the 32° North Restaurant, with its dimmed lighting, is atmospheric and intimate. As you would expect, the rooms are immaculate and the hotel service is crisp and efficient.
