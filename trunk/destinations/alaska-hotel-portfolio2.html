SUGGESTED GUSTAVUS ACCOMMODATION

  • GLACIER BAY BEAR TRACK INN

    On the tranquil shores of Icy Straits in Gustavus is The Glacier Bay Bear Track Inn. Just 14 spacious guest rooms feature hand-crafted log furniture and details. The gracious hosts are eager to show-off their beautiful corner of the world, and the lobby has been made the focal point of the Inn, with 30-foot ceilings, and inspiring views of the forests and meadows, streams and diverse wildlife.

    The Inn's dining room has a wonderfully intimate atmosphere, the centrepiece is a walk-around fireplace surrounded by overstuffed suede couches, handmade tables, moose antler chandeliers - all handpicked to ensure just the right feel.
