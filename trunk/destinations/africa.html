Africa safaris & Luxury holidays


Africa is a continent that is unrivalled when it comes to scale, variety and pure, raw impact. Its natural history alone would make a multiple visits worthwhile. Can you think of any other place on earth where you can fall asleep to the sound of lions roaring, or watch a million flamingos take off from the waters of a secluded lake? Parts of Africa boast scenery so stunning they'll leave you awestruck. The golden continent of Africa has for long been known for its adventurous and luxurious safari experiences and with Abercrombie & Kent, India, you will be offered a great deal more besides this. Our team of experts aims to give you an enchanting African luxury holiday experience-delectable cuisine, cultural connect, exotic wildlife, thrilling adventure sports and luxury lodges; all combined to make you revel in luxury and comfort.

Get ready for a journey that will make you go deep into the Rift Valley, experience game watching in the Okavango Delta, vibrant beauty of the Table Mountains, gorilla trekking in Uganda, elephant spotting in Kruger National Park and witness the breath taking Victoria falls among hordes of other exciting activities. Even in the heart of Africa, the team at A&K ensures you are provided with the most immense of luxuries, delicious food and beautiful lodgings at one with nature.

Home to some of the best national parks and game reserves of Africa, Kenya is a world within itself. Witness a wealth of glittering lakes, exotic wildlife, wide plains, snow-capped mountains and a semi-arid desert. An addition to this list is the flamingo lakes, the white palm-fringed ocean beaches and the coral reefs. The country is also host to the annual wildebeest migration. Kenya offers a wide range of activities from biking to camel and horse riding safaris, underwater sports like snorkeling and diving, trekking at Mount Kenya, white water rafting, fishing and playing golf for the silent hearts.

Steep, towering and dramatic mountain ranges, exotic wildlife and palm-fringed sandy beaches greet you as you enter Tanzania. The wealth of culture, vibrant coral reefs and its idyllic climate are the reason Tanzania is a popular destination among travelers. For climbers and trekkers alike, a climb to the highest free standing mountain-Mt. Kilimanjaro is well worth all the travel. To witness a spectacular game of nature, come to Tanzania's northern parks of Tarangire, Serengeti, Ngorongoro and Lake Manyara. They are easily accessible and support a high concentration of predators and animals like zebra, elephants etc.

Nicknamed the "Pearl of Africa," Uganda has the finest scenery in Africa, mesmerizing travelers with towering mountains, sparkling bottomless lakes, dazzling lush greenery, abundant wildlife and the greatest natural treasure of all - half of the world's last remaining mountain gorilla population. Trek deep into the heart of Bwindi Impenetrable National Park to see these fascinating, rare creatures from closer than you ever imagined on an A&K Uganda gorilla safari.

South Africa is blessed with stunning scenery and natural landscapes - towering mountain peaks and passes, dense and prehistoric forests, miles of unblemished coastline, rivers to soothe or thrill you, and billowing flower fields and grasslands of every hue. Unbeatable wildlife encounters add up to safari adventures far superior than anything you imagined. First you start with the Big Five; African elephants, black rhinos, Cape buffaloes, lions and leopards and move on to whales, penguins, meerkats, wild dogs,

birds, dung beetles and many more. Cape Town on the other hand presents a classic image with its colorful harbor-dining, spectacular view from the Table Mountain, vast vineyards and Robben Island.

Discover Zambia's South Luangwa National Park, teeming with abundant wildlife, the first to introduce the concept of walking safaris. Witness the majestic Victoria Falls, and then head to Botswana's Chobe National Park, home to cape buffalo, impala, hyenas, leopards and Africa's largest concentration of elephants.

Another breath taking destination in Africa is the Okavango Delta in Botswana. A miracle of nature, the Okavango is the only inland delta the world over. It is a magical oasis of dramatic flood plains, palm-fringed islands and the mighty rushing river, right in the middle of the arid Kalahari Desert. In terms of pristine and untainted beauty, secluded location and rich diversity of animal and birdlife, the Okavango Delta is unparalleled.

Sightseeing in Zimbabwe is a delight to say the least. Learn of the history and culture of this fantastic country from the Great Zimbabwe Ruins and indulge in the picturesque beauty of the Eastern Highlands. With a total of eleven national parks in the area, game viewing is a common activity. Enjoy the warm sun and soak in the pleasure of man-made wonderland of Lake Kariba and stroke the adventurous streak in you at the Mana Pools, showcasing the last remains of true wilderness with great views of the Zambezi Escarpment..

Being incredibly diverse, holidays in Rwanda are the perfect mix of striking landscape and rich wildlife. Misty, temperamental rainforest in the south gives rise to lush, forested volcanoes in the north. Originally the domain of hunter gatherers, trekking gorilla in Rwanda is seen by many as the best adventure on the planet, and no trip to Africa would be complete without sighting gorillas in their mist in Rwanda's national parks.

Along with having a striking diversity of cultures and national origins, Namibia is a photographer's dream - it boasts wild seascapes, rugged mountains, lonely deserts, stunning wildlife and colonial cities. Encounter Namibia's treacherously stunning coastline, its towering sand dunes, incredible moonscapes and savanna grasslands teeming with wildlife. See the best of this spectacular region by canoeing down rivers, scaling sand dunes and walking through the desert.
