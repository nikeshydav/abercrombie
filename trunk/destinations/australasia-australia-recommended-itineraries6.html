MELBOURNE NOW

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 4 Nights

    "Experience the vibrancy and cultural diversity of Melbourne, recognized as one of the world's most livable cities" An adventure awaits you down every street, lane and alleyway with diverse cuisines, the arts, fashion precincts and history scattered throughout the city. Enjoy a change of pace on the city's fringe with serene valleys chequered with award winning wineries, spectacular coastlines and picturesque landscapes with unique Australian wildlife.

    Highlights: Melbourne, Phillip Island , Great Ocean Road.

  • Day 1

    Arrive Melbourne

    • Exciting Hummer ride from the airport
    • Visit iconic St Kilda Beach
    • Colonial Tram Car Lunch
    • Eureka Skydeck

    Experience the vibrant, cosmopolitan city of Melbourne.

    Upon arrival, meet the A&K guide and start your adventure with an exciting Hummer transfer to the hotel. Your driver will take you via the vibrant beach side suburb of St Kilda for a quick beach side stroll and photo stop before arriving at your hotel to check in and refresh.

    Board the iconic glossy, burgundy restaurants on wheels - the first travelling Tramcar Restaurants in the world. This is a charming way to orientate yourself with Melbourne's streets and take in the sights while enjoying a delightful 3 course luncheon with beverages (1pm - 3pm).

    This evening, stand at the top of the city at the Eureka Skydeck - the Southern Hemisphere's highest viewing platform. Eureka is perfectly positioned for 360 degree, floor-to-ceiling views encompassing Melbourne's CBD, sports precinct, Port Phillip Bay, Docklands and everything in between. See the sun set and the city lights begin to dazzle.

    This evening perhaps take the family to one of the many fabulous restaurants followed by a theatre show.
    • Chitty Chitty Bang Bang - 30th January - 24th February
    • War Horse 23 Dec - 10 Feb 2013

  • Day 2

    Phillip Island Full day tour

    • Helicopter flight to Phillip Island
    • Phillip Island Nature Park
    • Koala Conservation Centre

    Victoria's iconic wildlife island is just a short helicopter flight from Melbourne - this is the most incredible start to your Phillip Island adventure.

    Your luxury vehicle will collect you from your hotel and whisk you across the city to the airfield where your private helicopter awaits.

    Enjoy a spectacular flight over the city, along Cape Woolamai and down to Phillip Island, where your host is waiting to collect you for your exciting Phillip Island adventure.

    Get up close and personal with our most iconic of marsupials at the Koala Conservation Centre, while immersing yourself in the Australian bush.

    Dinner is an unforgettable experience - dine on delicious local produce including fresh seafood whilst enjoying the panoramic ocean views.

    End your unforgettable day with one of the world's great wildlife wonders - the Penguin Parade.

    Relax and recap on the comfortable drive home.

  • Day 3

    Great Ocean Road 2 day/1 night journey

    • Victoria's iconic surf Beach (Bells Beach) - option for surf or paddle board lesson
    • Otway Fly Treetop Walk

    Victoria's Great Ocean Road is an amazing experience at any time of the year.

    One of the most beautiful coastal roads in the world, the Great Ocean Road stretches 550 km along the southern coast of Victoria.

    Travel it and you will discover some of the world's most inspiring scenery, an ever-changing hinterland and charming towns and hamlets. Marvel at the huge cliffs, roaring seas, tranquil coves, magnificent waterfalls and secluded beaches.

    Get an insight into Australian surfing culture at Bells Beach, there will be time for a surf, kayak or stand up paddle board lesson if you are feeling active! Discover seaside villages such as the popular town of Lorne, where you can stroll on the stunning beach. See koalas in the wild at Kennett River.

    Visit the Otway Fly Treetop Walk is a 1 hour rainforest walk experience that is approximately 2km in total and features a 600mt long and 30mt high steel structured treetop canopy walkway that takes you right into the treetops. It's the longest and tallest walkway of its type in the world! Included in this walk is the kids Prehistoric Path - where there is a dinosaur around every corner! There is also the exhilarating Otway Fly Zip Line Tour is a 3.5 hour experience and includes 2.5 hours of Zip Line activity (including training and simulation), 8 cloud stations, 6 flights and 2 suspension bridges and 1 hour for the Treetop Walk. This Zip Line Tour is the only Zip Line Tour for Victoria!

    This evening overnight in Apollo Bay with dinner at local restaurant La Bimba.

  • Day 4

    Great Ocean Road to Melbourne

    • 12 Apostles
    • Moonlight Cinema at Royal Botanic Gardens with picnic hamper

    View the sculptured beauty of The Twelve Apostles today. Many of these stacks of flat-bedded limestone raise 65 metres above sea level and are an inspiring sight to see. Visit Loch Ard Gorge and hear stories from your host about the tragic and romantic shipwreck whilst marveling at the landmark. Visit the quaint seaside village of Port Campbell with sandy beaches and picturesque cliff tops before your journey home to Melbourne. You may like to do a scenic flight to view the incredible stretch of coastline from the air or re-board the vehicle and travel through farmland back to Melbourne.

    This evening, visit the Royal Botanic Gardens - Moonlight Cinema for an evening under the stars at an outdoor cinema screening new release, classic and cult movies. We will arrange a picnic and a blanket for you to enjoy your last evening in Melbourne.

  • Day 5

    Depart Melbourne

    Meet the A&K chauffeur for a private transfer to the airport for departure flight way back home.
