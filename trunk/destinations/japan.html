Travel To Japan

Holidays in Japan are like stepping into a completely different world. A country that lives life between two worlds, Japan is an intriguing blend of tradition and modernity and has a unique way of welcoming the new while still fondly embracing the old. Japan Holidays with Abercrombie and Kent allow you to truly savor the unique culture and dramatic landscape that defines this country. Japan holidays offer visitors diverse options and fresh discoveries, from scenic landscapes viewed from an outdoor hot spring to the bustling city of Tokyo lit with thousands of neon lights.

Japan envelopes in itself about 6,800 islands and the main island of Honshu boasts of the country's two main gateways, Tokyo, famed for its vibrancy and modern feel and Osaka, dubbed as the 'Kitchen of the Nation' for its rich food culture - and great food is always a prominent feature of a Japanese Holiday.

A city full of striking palaces, ornate shrines and exquisite gardens, Kyoto is the kind of city one would imagine in a dream. Once the capital city of Japan, here you will still find kimono clad geisha shuffling along dark, lamp lit streets. The capital city of Tokyo is by far the most modernized town with towering landscapes, flashing neon lights and crowds decked up in the wildest of fashions. Currently energetic with a cosmopolitan feel to it, the city of Hiroshima has risen from the ashes. The archipelago of Okinawa has a unique paradise to set it apart from mainland China. Japan's foremost natural sight, Mount Fuji is certainly not to be missed.

Travel to Japan with our expert guides at Abercrombie and Kent. We allow you to truly savor the unique culture and the dramatic landscape that are synonymous to this wonderful country. Our in-depth knowledge and experience ensures you have a highly personalized and memorable holiday in this beautiful country.
