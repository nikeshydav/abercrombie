Best of Brazil

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 8 Nights

    Highlights: Flamboyant Rio. Statue of Christ the Redeemer. Dinner with a local Carioca artist. Colonial architecture and beaches in Salvador. Spectacular waterfalls in Iguassu National Park. Brazilian & Argentine Falls.

  • Day 1

    Arrive Rio de Janeiro

    Arrive in Brazil's party capital and settle into your hotel with the afternoon at leisure.

    Overnight: Copacabana Palace (Deluxe Ocean View)

  • Day 2

    Rio de Janeiro

    Take in the city sights this morning including the famous cable car ride up Sugar Loaf for 360° views, Guanabara Bay and the legendary golden beaches of Copacabana, Ipanema and Leblon. After a typical Brazilian BBQ lunch visit the Statue of Christ the Redeemer, one of the seven 'new' wonders of the world. Atop Corcovado Hill the views from here are breathtaking. Time also to explore the hilly streets of Santa Teresa with its colonial architecture, home to many artists, intellectuals and handicraft studios

    Overnight: Copacabana Palace (Deluxe Ocean View)
    Meal Plan: B,L

  • Day 3

    Design Your Day Rio de Janeiro

    Plot your course this morning with a samba lesson under the guidance of a professional dancer or the chance to see the other side of Rio with a tour through the city's favelas (slum districts). Visit one of the largest in South America where around 150,000 people live. Observe the routine of the community here, visit local houses, day nurseries and schools. Walk through the small streets and watch life go by. This afternoon is at leisure before dinner at the popular Porcao Rio's.

    Overnight: Copacabana Palace (Deluxe Ocean View)
    Meal Plan: B,D

  • Day 4

    Rio - Salvador da Bahia

    A flight* north along the Atlantic coast to Salvador, Brazil's oldest city and one of the most beautiful and interesting in the country. Settle into the hotel and this afternoon is an historical orientation tour of the city taking in the church of Sao Francisco with its gold-leafed Baroque interior and the old town, Pelourinho, a UNESCO Heritage site with delightful pastel-coloured colonial architecture.

    Overnight: Villa Bahia (Deluxe)
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 5

    Salvador da Bahia

    Today you leave the city behind to explore the nearby farming hinterland, full of sugarcane plantations, peaceful colonial towns and busy country markets. Stop at the country market town of Santo Amaro and on through rolling hills to Cachoeira, the jewel of the Bahian hinterland nestled deep in the valley of the Paraguaçu River. After lunch take a walking tour of the town, with opportunities to browse for local wood carvings and see cigars being expertly rolled

    Overnight: Villa Bahia (Deluxe)
    Meal Plan: B,L

  • Day 6

    Salvador da Bahia

    This morning visit the peaceful family district of the lower city where life moves at a slower pace. Fishermen fish from dugout canoes, locals collect shellfish at low tide and schooners are at anchor. See the famous Bonfim church, one of the most important in Brazil and continue to the Monserrat district with its panoramic view of the city ending at Mercado Modelo, a thriving market for the finest Brazilian arts and crafts

    Overnight: Villa Bahia (Deluxe)
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 7

    Salvador da Bahia - Iguassu Falls

    A morning flight* south to Iguassu Falls and your hotel which is situated a short stroll from the Falls. This afternoon you are escorted on a walking tour of the Brazilian side of the falls which offers marvellous panoramic views of this natural wonder

    Overnight: Hotel Das Cataratas (Deluxe)
    Meal Plan: B,D

  • Day 8

    Iguassu Falls

    Another day spent with a visit to the Argentine side of the falls where the experience is a close-up one. There is an electric train ride through the park followed by a walking trail through the rainforest to a suspended platform at the edge of the Devil's Throat, the largest of the cataracts. Return by train and visit the upper Argentinian falls and if time permits a brief stop at the Three Borders point where Brazil, Argentina and Paraguay meet. Afternoon at leisure.

    Overnight: Hotel Das Cataratas (Deluxe)
    Meal Plan: B,L

  • Day 9

    Depart Iguassu

    A fond farewell as you depart Iguassu for onward arrangements

  • *Flight not included.

Buzios Extension

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 3 Nights

    Highlights: Atlantic Ocean seaside getaway. 26 beaches nearby. Boating, biking, snorkelling, shopping & spa. Restaurants, bars and nightlife.

  • Day 1

    Rio - Buzios

    A private 3 hour transfer east to Buzios and your luxury hotel. There's an afternoon orientation tour to acquaint yourself with the town layout.

    Overnight: Insolito Buzios (Superior Room)

  • Day 2-3

    Buzios

    Spend the next two days enjoying your boutique hotel and the nearby beaches or a boating expedition. The hotel is within easy reach of town and the 26 beaches dotted all around the peninsula, these are best accessed by beach buggy, the vehicle of choice in Buzios! The hotel also has a wellness centre, boats and bikes all available for guests

    Overnight: Insolito Buzios (Superior Room)
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    Buzios - Rio

    Return transfer to Rio and the airport for onward flight arrangements

    Meal plan: B

Kiaroa Extension

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 3 Nights

    Highlights: Sophisticated eco-resort on Atlantic Coast. Unspoiled pristine beach, tropical. Vegetation and coral reef. Laid back pace.

  • Day 1

    Salvador - Kiaroa

    Transfer by light aircraft half an hour south to Kiaroa, your luxury beachside resort. Rest of the day to settle in.

    Overnight: Kiaroa Eco-Luxury Resort (Tropical Deluxe Suite)
    Meal Plan: D

  • Day 2-3

    Kiaroa

    Spend the next two days in this remote area which is rich in natural attractions. There are semi-deserted beaches, lagoons, river outlets, mangroves and several waterfalls. Relax on the unspoiled beach or by the pool, indulge in a spa treatment or for the more active there is tennis, beach volleyball, boating or land excursions

    Overnight: Kiaroa Eco-Luxury Resort (Tropical Deluxe Suite)
    Meal Plan: B,D

  • Day 4

    Depart Kiaroa

    Return air transfer to Salvador for onward flight arrangements

    Meal Plan: B

Ponta dos Ganchos Extension

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 3 Nights

    Highlights: Exclusive coastal hideaway. Private peninsula on south Brazilian coast. Nature trails, island visits & snorkeling.

  • Day 1

    Florianopolis - Ponta dos Ganchos

    A private road transfer from Florianopolis the short distance north to your luxury retreat located between picturesque fishing villages

    Overnight: Ponta dos Ganchos (Deluxe Bungalow)
    Meal Plan: D

  • Day 2-3

    Ponta dos Ganchos

    Spend the next two days enjoying this exclusive property on its own private peninsula. There are 25 charming bungalows here with decks and hammocks overlooking the sea to complete the picture. There's a swimming pool, massage tents, SPA by Christian Dior, fitness centre and tennis courts plus nature trails, island visits and excellent scuba diving close by.

    Overnight: Ponta dos Ganchos (Deluxe Bungalow)
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 4

    Ponta dos Ganchos - Florianopolis

    Return transfer to Florianopolis and the airport for onward flight arrangements

    Meal Plan: B
