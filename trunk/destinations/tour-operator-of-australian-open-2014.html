Official Tour Operator of Australian Open 2014

The Australian Open is one of Melbourne's most iconic and exciting events celebrating Australia's love of the great outdoors with the fun and entertainment of Grand Slam® tennis.

Whether it's behind the baseline or court side directly behind the umpire, Abercrombie & Kent will bring guests centre stage to Rod Laver Arena over the duration of the two week tournament with premium seating options.

Enjoy the finest food and wine with a Grand Slam® Select Premium Finals Package for some of the most exciting tennis at the Australian Open. Guests will stay at A&K's preferred hotel and the Official Player's Hotel for the Australian Open - the Grand Hyatt*.

Stay in the city centre of luxury, located on Collins Street in the heart of Melbourne's most prestigious shopping, dining and theatre precinct and within walking distance to many tourist attractions.

The Grand Hyatt's signature restaurant, Collins Kitchen, offers guests an interactive dining experience with an open - show kitchen serving multiple food concepts, while the bar, RU-CO, presents the finest selection of wine.

Grand Hyatt Melbourne



Accommodation

Grand Hyatt Melbourne is the city centre of luxury, located on Collins St in the heart of Melbourne's most prestigious shopping, dining and theatre precinct and within walking distance to many tourist attractions.

This iconic Melbourne hotel includes striking upper and lower lobbies, a state-of-the-art health and fitness centre, City Club, and a luxury retail precinct with flagship stores Bulgari, Paspaley Pearls and Emporio Armani.

The hotel boasts truly grand modern spaces, framed by spectacular Australian artwork and complemented by magnificent marble finishings. Spacious guest rooms are furnished with Hyatt custom-made beds and Egyptian cotton linen, and floor-to-ceiling windows offer breathtaking Melbourne city views. The residential-style concept has been designed for even greater guest comfort and includes bespoke furniture and luxurious fabrics for a warm, relaxing mood. Guests can enjoy café-style coffee with Nespresso coffee machines in all Club Rooms and Premium Suites, whilst unwinding with their own personal music selection thanks to iPod docking stations in every guestroom.

The hotel's signature restaurant, Collins Kitchen, offers guests an interactive dining experience with an open-show kitchen serving multiple food concepts, while the bar, ru-co, presents the finest selection of wine.

All guests can enjoy access to the largest hotel gym in Australia - City Club Health and Fitness centre, or seek refuge at Sanctuary Spa, an urban retreat delivering exceptional massage, beauty and aesthetic treatments.

Sofitel Melbourne



Accommodation

With a prime city position at the top end of prestigious Collins Street, an exquisite Sofitel Melbourne On Collins immediately impresses through subtle luxuries, sophisticated elegance and a distinction for the finest service.

Complementing Melbourne's rich history of Art and Culture, a celebrated reputation is well deserved by Sofitel Melbourne On Collins, Melbourne's Hotel for the Arts.

A living art gallery born over 13 years, Sofitel Melbourne On Collins reflects an ever-changing landscape of scope, texture and colour, continually reinventing itself with each of the 16 major Art exhibitions held annually. From the gallery style Sofi's Lounge to the private dining room in No.35 and beyond, the discovery of fine art is an 'every moment' delight for those passionate about the visual.

Classic rooms feature between levels 36 and 47 offering breathtaking city views through floor to ceiling windows. Enjoy a restful night's sleep assisted by black out shutters and Sofitel MyBed. Rooms feature mood lighting, LED\LCD or Plasma televisions and iPod|MP3 connection. Marble bathrooms offer automated low level lighting, French toiletries and shower over bath.

Collins Street, a grand boulevard of architectural heritage, iconic fashion labels, gothic style churches and opulent theatres. Melbourne's premier commercial streetscape, a bustling inner city address surrounded by galleries and gardens, magical arcades and laneways and Fortune 500 companies. Breathtaking in each season, stroll from the Paris (top) end of Collins Street for a most distinguished collection of brand name boutiques, hidden cafes, Collins Place retail space, and time honoured theatrical landmarks, the Regent and Athenaeum Theatres.

On Level 35 is No35, offering dazzling views through floor to ceiling glass. Sophisticated. Elegant. Dark timbers, contemporary artworks and accents of gold, red and pink tones shape an ambient sense of taste. Memorable modern cuisine crafted with an eye for pure flavour and the best local produce to ensure the experience lasts.
