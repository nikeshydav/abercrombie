Suggested Ho Chi Minh Accommodation

  • Caravelle Hotel

    Overlooking Lam Son Square and the Opera House, the beautiful Caravelle enjoys a fantastic location in the shopping and entertainment centre of Ho Chi Minh City. Elegant and contemporary rooms, award-winning hospitality and friendly staff means that a stay at the Caravelle is synonymous with the very best this city has to offer.

    High above the city and located on the top floor is the Saigon Saigon Bar, the perfect escape from the perpetual flow of Ho Chi Minh City's busy traffic. Enjoy a quiet drink in this iconic rooftop bar, while admiring the views of the Saigon River and Vietnam's most bustling city.

    Park Hyatt Saigon

    The 252-room Park Hyatt Saigon is centrally located at Lam Son Square, in downtown Saigon. The colonial styled rooms feature lavish bedding, rich fabrics, graceful colonial era furniture and gazing views over Ho Chi Minh City, offering the guests a warmth and comfort that only comes from such elegant surroundings.

    The Park Hyatt Saigon is within walking distance of the elegant Opera House, business, shopping and entertainment districts ensuring that it is the perfect base from which to discover Ho Chi Minh City.

    Read more

    Six Senses Con Dao

    Occupying a one kilometre space of white sandy beach and surrounded by vivid mountain landscapes, Six Senses Hideaway Con Dao is the ultimate in a Vietnamese beach getaway. The property's remote island location and beautiful natural surroundings provide a sense of peace and privacy that will reinvigorate and re-energize.

    The 35 villas with private pools and 16 residential villas with two private swimming pools are built with Indochinese hardwood and Con Dao granite stones to ensure that the design and concept of each building matches with the beauty and grandeur of the island.

    Perched above the sand dunes the restaurant offers exceptional views of the nearby ocean and river. Those looking to relax, soothe and invigorate the body, choose from a holistic spa treatment at the Six Senses Spa set amidst the dramatic backdrop of the Lo Voi mountain and surrounded by tropical gardens. The resorts picturesque beachside setting, marvellous service and long list of facilities makes it a prime accommodation option for families and couples.

    The Jahan

    The Jahan is the newest member of the Heritage Line Collection, and a baby brother to the famous Jayavarman. It brings the singular touches of a luxury boutique hotel together with the leisurely pace of a river cruise and the opportunity to collect new experiences along the mighty Mekong.

    'The Lost Civilisation' upstream cruise and the 'Pearl of the Orient' downstream cruise for 8 days and 7 nights connects bustling Ho Chi Minh City with Siem Reap, home of the magnificent Angkor Wat temple. The 26 carefully designed staterooms and suites with floor to ceiling windows and sliding doors that open onto a private balcony offer privacy and uncompromised luxury.
