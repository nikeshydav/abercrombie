Suggested California accommodation.

  • LAKE TAHOE, THE RITZ CARLTON

    The Lake Tahoe region is the perfect base for visitors looking for outdoor adventure. A ski paradise in winter and a hiking and nature lover's destination the rest of the year. The Ritz Carlton Lake Tahoe is the premier resort in the region and offers distinctive service, great food and large rooms with wonderful views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.

    Most of the rooms have their own fireplaces and balconies. The nearby village of Northstar can be reached by gondola and is packed with restaurants, bars and shopping.

    LOS ANGELES, BEVERLY HILLS HOTEL

    Steeped in Hollywood history, the Beverly Hills Hotel is surrounded by 12 acres of lush gardens. Choose one of the classic pink bungalows for the ultimate Californian experience and don't miss lunch in the Polo Lounge - the favourite watering hole for generations of stars.

    The Beverly Hills Hotel has 210 rooms and suites including 23 one-of-a-kind bungalows. Guests may choose the comfortable elegance of the main building or the beauty of the bungalow gardens. The rooms and suites offer a variety of unique features such as private garden patios, kitchens, fireplaces, and balconies with gorgeous views of the palm-lined avenues of Beverly Hills.

    Inspired by the hotel's sought-after classic pink bungalows, the newest "ultra bungalows" blend the indoor and outdoor experience effortlessly creating the largest and most prestigious Presidential suites in Los Angeles.

    Read more

    LOS ANGELES, L'ERMITAGE BEVERLY HILLS

    On a quiet Beverly Hills street, L'Ermitage is famed for its privacy. Large contemporary rooms and the stunning rooftop pool and spa embody the three 'S's - smart, stylish and secluded. Dining in the hotel's celebrated restaurants and a drink on the rooftop poolside are an absolute must.

    Featuring a sophisticated Californian aesthetic with accents of the Far East, each of the rooms reflects the hotel's laid-back sensibility and cosmopolitan décor. We recommend the Beverly Suites, the newest one-bedroom suites that feature a living room, second full bathroom in addition to the master bedroom and bath, and two step-out balconies with French doors and views of the city and surrounding hills.

    Read more

    LOS ANGELES, SHUTTERS ON THE BEACH

    "Shutters" is all about beachfront living. Facing miles of golden sand in sunny Santa Monica, you can enjoy both city and beach in one trip - the ultimate LA boutique hotel.

    A seat in the hotel lobby is one of the most sought after spots in Los Angeles.If you'd prefer, skip the sand and soak in the sun by the poolside where spectacular ocean views await you at the largest beachside pool deck in the city. Stylish and spacious guest rooms and suites have newly redesigned interiors by the freshly appointed Obama White House designer Michael Smith - understated luxury with the feel of a private beach cottage. Each one offers all the comforts of home.

    Read more

    NAPA VALLEY, CALISTOGA RANCH

    The lodges of the Calistoga Ranch offer a retreat to escape and unwind. Hidden away in a canyon of the Upper Napa Valley, each lodge offers wonderful views, warming fireplaces and understated style. Calistoga captures the rich culture of food, wine and nature inherent in the region.

    Designed with relaxation in mind, the lodges have an abundance of indoor and outdoor spaces so you can fully appreciate the pristine setting - expansive cedar decks, rich polished woods and floor-to-ceiling windows that frame the tall pines beyond. Each guest lodge features a fireplace, original artwork, down duvets, Italian linens and signature botanical bath products.not to mention the chance to shower under the stars in your secluded outdoor shower garden.

    NAPA VALLEY, MEADOWOOD

    Meadowood has a calm, relaxed ambiance and gives off the feeling of a private lodge. We would recommend a room with either a fireplace or a private terrace. The atmosphere is one of a peaceful retreat and feels very secluded. The facilities are extensive.

    Meadowood offers a beautiful spa, fitness centre, seven tennis courts, croquet lawn, and a 9-hole executive golf course. There's a lap and a family pool, hiking trails and of course the area is famed for its local vineyards which are mostly open to the public. A peaceful and serene settings.

    NAPA VALLEY, POETRY INN

    The much sought after Poetry Inn commands a stunning spot overlooking Napa Valley and the small town of Yountville, home of the three Michelin starred restaurant The French Laundry. The inn is designed beautifully, with a look and feel straight from a style magazine. Every suite offers those amazing views across the valley.

    Life is centred around the open lounge area, the terrace and the small pool. With just 5 suites you will get to know everyone including the staff, which is a large part of the charm. The Inn even offers a couple's treatment room offering. This is the perfect choice for those looking for something more intimate.

    ORANGE COUNTY BEACHES, MONTAGE LAGUNA BEACH

    Montage Laguna makes for an ideal stop on a coastal drive between San Diego and LA, or as a permanent base for a week or two to visit Disneyworld, San Diego Zoo, Universal Studios and the Southern California beaches and cities, all of which are within 90 minutes drive. The resort has a relaxed laid-back feel with life centred around the pools, spa and beach.

    Guestrooms are large, all with ocean views. Laguna itself is a short walk away and is packed with unique shopping, cafes and restaurants. The whole area epitomises the Orange County 'Southern California' lifestyle with temperatures remaining high well into October. The Loft is the resort's informal dining experience and has terraces overlooking the ocean.

    ORANGE COUNTY BEACHES, PELICAN HILL, NEWPORT BEACH

    Pelican Hill is the perfect spot to choose for a family looking to explore Southern California from a relaxing base with the very best activities and food. The whole family is catered for with 2 golf courses, one of the largest spas in California, 5 restaurants and one of the most unique swimming pools in the USA.

    The feel is relaxed and embraces the warm year round temperatures. There's a choice between the smaller bungalows - all with their own parking - and the larger villas. Pelican Hill suits a longer stay.

    PACIFIC COAST HIGHWAY, BIG SUR, POST RANCH INN

    The rustic elegance that forms The Post Ranch Inn (sister hotel to Cavello Point in Sausalito) comes with some of the best views in America. Two private houses - Post House and South Coast House and an array of beautiful rooms make The Post Ranch Inn an excellent place to retreat after exploring the Californian coast. It is easily one of our favourite hotels in all of America.

    Post Ranch's award-winning spa takes relaxation to a new level. Enjoy the experts' therapies by yourself or with your partner in the setting you prefer - your private room or deck, the scenic spa treatment rooms, or the couple's Spa Suite. There's also morning yoga, guided hikes or saunters, stargazing with their celestial telescope, Chef Craig's cooking classes, and nearby activities like whale watching.
