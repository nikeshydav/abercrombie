    Deep South

    Deep South is the embodiment of the southern states of Alabama, Georgia, Louisiana, Mississippi and Tennessee where you'll experience world's best in music, history, culture and sports. Atlanta, the world's busiest travel hub is the 'Gateway to the South', offering a perfect start to your Deep South experience. Travel further into Montgomery in Alabama, where Dr. Martin Luther King endeavored for the civil rights.

    Tennessee is where you can serenade yourself with the blues, rock n roll at one of the many live music concerts. Travel into the exotic Louisiana, which is home to splendid plantation houses and a number of 17th century towns showcasing its legacy. Being the birthplace of jazz and a hub of diverse culture New Orleans is also a must visit.

    Florida

    Florida is a seductive paradise of white sunny beaches, beautiful people, alluring flora and fauna, diverse national parks, beautiful deco architecture. Appropriately christened, the Sunshine State has a range of activities on offer for travelers of all ages. Explore the state by chartering a deep sea fishing boat, across the subtropical wetlands of the Everglades or go bar hopping along the glamorous Ocean Drive in the South Beach.

    A stopover in the tempting Miami is a must visit. This buzzing city gives you more than just beaches, as you can stroll along the perfectly manicured lawns at Coral Gables or experience some Cuban hospitality in Little Havana and Calle Ocho.

    Abercrombie and Kent, offers you an exclusive peek in the life and culture of Florida and we await to plan your bespoke luxury holiday for 2013 here.

    Hawaii

    Say 'Aloha' to life and laughter! Hawaii's paradise of gorgeous volcanic islands, floral scent of its air, pristine beaches and friendly locals make it an ideal getaway for everyone. Capture the buzz in Honolulu, the bustling capital city, the glowing coastline of Waikiki and the lush highlands of the Lanai Island brimming with bright yellow hibiscus, kukui and red ohia.

    Dance to the unique beats of the Polynesian music with Hula dancers or dive in the tepid waters of crescent shaped Molokoni, spot the giant humpback whales or play a round of golf, or simply soak in the scenery of one of the white sand beaches.

    LAS VEGAS, UTAH & ARIZONA

    In Las Vegas everything is bigger and better. It is truly the most spectacular and dynamic city on earth. This is the city which is home to the world famous Las Vegas strip, famed casinos, fine restaurants and sold out live shows. This is the city where the hotels are the attractions, boasting international themes, Venetian canals, Egyptian pyramids, Eiffel Tower and much more.

    While its vibrant nightlife is usually the main draw for many, daytime is the perfect time to explore the natural wonders around it. Surrounding deserts is teeming with stunning landscapes such as the Death Valley, the Mojave Desert, and the Grand Canyon.

    If you're looking for some adventure in the nature and wilderness then Utah is the place to be, where the scenery is dominated by the canyons, mountains, rivers, and deserts. Zion and Bryce Canyon are a must visit and so are the Monument Valley and Arches National Park, making the region a perfect long term stopover.

    To unwind yourself from all the action, head to Arizona, where you can slow down the pace in Scottsdale which is also known as the 'Spa Capital of the World'.

    New England

    One of the best tourist places in USA, New England is the birthplace America and home to the original colonial heritage as well as the quaint coastal towns of Massachusetts, Connecticut, New Hampshire, Maine and Vermont.

    Boston is the gateway to New England, paying a grand homage to the imperial past of America. Also let yourself get charmed by the placid beachfronts and historical homes, and fresh seafood of Cape Cod, Martha's Vineyard and Nantucket. Make sure you visit the 'Berkshires' region to get a taste of the local culture and arts. While you're there also enjoy life at a leisurely pace at B&Bs, antique shops and terrific natural beauty. New England is a haven for witnessing the autumn foliage, during its world famous 'Leaf Peeping' phase, when the green transforms into a tapestry of bright fiery colors.

    Yellowstone

    Established in 1872, Yellowstone National Park is the first such park of America, an idea that would later find its way all over the world. Almost the of Wales, Yellowstone is an expanse rich with mountains, forests, wildlife and a majority of world's natural geysers.

    Old Faithful, a cone geyser, which erupts every 91 minutes, is a highlight of the park. Visitors can also explore a number of other geyser basins, hot springs, and mud pools in the 2.2 million acres of this expanse. It is also home to wolves, elk, pronghorn antelope, buffalo and moose that dwell in its nearly intact eco system.

    A guided adventure tour with Abercrombie & Kent's experienced personnel is highly recommended to witness the introverted wildlife and natural beauty of Yellowstone. Another ideal way of exploring the park is to simply drive through a park for which our North American experts can design a bespoke itinerary for you.
