Suggested Dead Sea accommodation

  • Evason Ma-In

    Evason Ma'in offers a unique location, built to create innovative and enriching experiences in a sustainable environment. Enjoy the therapeutic benefits of Ma'in hot spring waterfalls or swim in the pool

    Choose to dine on the clifftop restaurant with views over to the Dead Sea, serving home grown organic produce or sojourn to the olive oil area for a tasting. This property is located in Ma-in, 20 minutes drive from the Dead Sea. The hotel offers a complimentary shuttle to the Dead Sea for your convienence to experience a 'swim'.

    Kempinski Dead Sea

    Take a gentle swim under the shimmering moonlight of the one and only Dead Sea, situated at the lowest point on earth. Wander through the luxurious enclaves set amidst gardens, lagoons, winding rivers and waterfalls.

    Kempinski Dead Sea is beautifully appointed, based on a Babylonian-inspired theme - carved in natural stone and styled with contemporary fittings. There is a real sense that every detail has been pored over to create an immaculate living space.

    Movenpick Resort and Spa Dead Sea

    Consisting of 340 rooms and suites and several culinary outlets, this property combines a style of oriental flair with natural traditional local stone. The large spa offers the therapeutic benefits of its surroundings.

    The Mövenpick Resort & Spa Dead Sea is in a traditional village setting nestled amongst lush gardens. Set on the northern shores of the Dead Sea, the resort and its Spa create an oasis of tranquillity. On the outside, the building resembles a rough-hewn Moorish fort with stone and earthen walls. In fact the entire resort has a traditional, almost antique feel, with the addition of lavish furnishings.

    Rooms are dispersed throughout the main building, the village or on the beachfront with mountain, garden or sea views. All are air-conditioned and offer a balcony or terrace. There is a great range of facilities on offer at the resort for all ages and tastes. Make use of the beach with its infinity pool, the tennis courts. Children will delight in their own shaded swimming pool and the Dana Children's Activity Centre.
