TRAVEL DestinationS

Small Ship Cruising

For discerning travellers who enjoy an intimate experience with like-minded individuals in comfort and security, A&K presents a range of small ship cruises. These vessels all offer the highest standard of accommodation, guiding, dining, exceptional service and fabulous on-board camaraderie. Whether barging in France, cruising the Nile or onboard 'Road To Mandalay' in Myanmar, A&K's world on the water is a true experience.

Adventure Cruising

An adventure cruise immerses you in the exotic and the unspoiled. Each trip is a fresh, spontaneous, unscripted journey that is fundamentally different from a traditional cruise itinerary in all ways but one: comfort and service.

Each ship provides an on-board experience that is second to none. In addition, each is small enough to navigate the ports too small or remote for larger cruise ships, immersing you in the exotic and the unspoiled. With a small crew-to-passenger ratio, service is much more personalized and attentive, resulting in a perfect balance of exploration and comfort.

Canal & River Cruising

For a leisurely vacation in beautiful surroundings far from the usual tourist circuits, canal and river cruising has no equal. Abercrombie & Kent offers hotel vessels on escorted itineraries throughout France, Holland and Belgium, Germany, Britain and Ireland that include the most popular excursions and sightseeing in each region.

A barge cruise is a great value in today's European travel market. There is no need to pull out your wallet while aboard a canal or luxury river cruise. Pricing includes everything you need to make your holiday complete, from accommodations to three delicious meals daily with wine, as well as guided sightseeing and entry fees. Even transfers and bar drinks are included on most vessels.

A&K offers special theme cruises to suit your interests. Wine lovers might want to take the Wines of Burgundy (Grand Cru) Cruise aboard 'Amaryllis' and sample the 33 Grand Crus of Burgundy. If your passion is horticulture and gardens, consider a luxury river cruise on 'Magna Carta' in England, passing through lush green countryside and quaint towns along the Thames. Wherever your cruise takes you, rest assured you will experience the best of A&K travel in an escorted journey.
