TAILOR MADE SWITZERLAND

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 11 days

    This itinerary is ideal for first time visitors to the Alps and Switzerland, and those who enjoy picturesque views.

    Highlights: Geneva, Gothic Cathedral of Notre Dame in Lausanne, Gruyères Cheese Factory, Gruyères Castle, Montreux, Chateau de Chillon in Veytaux, Zermatt, Matterhorn, Lucerne

  • Day 1

    DEPART USA

    Overnight flight

  • Day 2

    ARRIVE GENEVA/LAUSANNE

    On arrival at Geneva airport you will be met by your English-speaking guide for a short sightseeing tour of Geneva in your private vehicle. Nestled between the Jura and the Alps, on the southwestern tip of Lac Lèman (Lake Geneva), Geneva was the home of the League of Nations. The Left Bank bristles with prestigious shops, monuments, parks and fountains. In the Old Town, visit the Church of St Peters and relax a little in one of the many sidewalk cafès before transferring to the lakeside town of Lausanne.

    Here your Guardian Angel will assist with check-in and run through the rest of your itinerary.

    Overnight: Beau-Rivage Palace - Deluxe Double/Twin with lake view
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    LAUSANNE

    This morning your driver and guide will take you up to the Old Town of Lausanne. The Gothic Cathedral of Notre Dame, an architectural treasure, is Switzerland's largest and finest church. The Hôtel de Ville is the seat of municipal and communal councils, and the painted medieval Fontaine de la Justice draws people to lounge on its heavy rim. This afternoon is at leisure for you to enjoy the relaxing facilities of the hotel, or pop along to the Olympic Museum, next door to the hotel. The more adventurous may like to take the ferry across the lake to the French town of Evian, famous for its water.

    Overnight: Beau-Rivage Palace - Deluxe Double/Twin with lake view
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    CHILLON & GRUYERES

    Today you will enjoy a full day excursion to Montreux and Gruyères with your private vehicle and English-speaking guide. Tour through some of the picturesque cheese-making villages, and visit the Gruyères Cheese Factory and Gruyères Castle. You will stop in Montreux, where you enjoy a guided tour of Château de Chillon, a 12th century castle that rises out of the water at Veytaux that was once used as a prison. This inspired Lord Byron to write his famous poem 'The Prisoner of Chillon'.

    Overnight: Beau-Rivage Palace - Deluxe Double/Twin with lake view
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 5

    LAUSANNE -ZERMATT

    Today you will be met at your hotel and privately transferred with your Guardian Angel to Lausanne railway station. Here you will be shown to your pre-reserved 1st class seating and your luggage will be handled onboard for you.

    In Visp our Zermatt Guardian Angel and driver will wait for you and transfer you the rest of the way to Zermatt by private vehicle.

    CONCIERGE RAIL SERVICE

    Travel by modern express rail in Europe is an excellent way of traveling between destinations in comfort, style and at speed. The benefits of rail travel in Europe are numerous, and in many instances, rail travel can be quicker than air travel so no waiting in airport departure lounges or lost luggage!

    1st Class rail travel is also a very cost effective and comfortable method of covering mid-distance travel when compared to air travel or road transfers.

    Our unique Concierge Rail means that you will be accompanied to your reserved 1st class seating on departure and met on the platform at the arrival terminal, whilst your luggage will be handled for you at both ends of the journey. For your journey, you will be provided with a journey bag containing an English language newspaper, bottled water and some snacks.

    Overnight: Grand Hotel Zermatterhof - Deluxe Double/Twin
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 6

    ZERMATT & GORNERGRAT

    Enjoy a 4-hour sightseeing tour with your local English-speaking guide. Travel up the Gornergrat by the highest open-air rail system in Europe. From the summit station there are majestic views of the Matterhorn, Monte Rosa and Corner Glacier. The remainder of today is at leisure.

    Overnight: Grand Hotel Zermatterhof - Deluxe Double/Twin
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 7

    ZERMATT

    Today is at leisure to enjoy the Alpine scenery and hospitality. You may want to take the cable car up to the Little Matterhorn, stroll through the Alpine meadows or relax and enjoy the facilities of your hotel which has an amazing spa with a large pool, steam room, sauna, Alpine and Ice grotto, fitness area and "Vita Borni" treatments (please pre-book).

    Overnight: Grand Hotel Zermatterhof - Deluxe Double/Twin
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 8

    ZERMATT - GLACIER EXPRESS - LUCERNE

    This morning you will be met by your Guardian Angel and privately transferred to Zermatt station. Here you will board the Glacier Express train bound for Andermatt. Enjoy the breathtaking scenery from the comfort of your reserved first class seats. Upon arrival at Andermatt train station, you will be met by your driver and taken by private vehicle to your hotel in Lucerne.

    Overnight: Palace Luzern - Deluxe Double/Twin with lake view
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 9

    LUCERNE

    After breakfast, you will take a walking tour of Lucerne with your local English-speaking guide. You will visit the Altes Rathaus (Old Town Hall), the Hofkirche that was first part of a monastery founded in 750, and the celebrated Glacier Gardens. The afternoon is at leisure, but on a nice day we suggest enjoying a relaxing public boat cruise on the lake.

    Overnight: Palace Luzern - Deluxe Double/Twin with lake view
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 10

    MOUNT PILATUS

    Meet your guide and driver this morning for your trip up Mount Pilatus. Your driver will take you to the station in Alpnachstad from where you and the guide will take the world's steepest cogwheel railway to Pilatus Kulm. During your journey, you will see lush meadows with Alpine flowers, mountain streams and fascinating rock faces. Your guide will point out anything of interest. Make your way back down the mountain by the aerial gondola. Those interested may like to visit the exhibition at Frâkmüntegg Station that tells the story of the gondola from concept through to completion. The remainder of today is at leisure for you to enjoy the lovely town of Lucerne, perhaps visit a museum or just sit along the promenade and watch the world go by.

    Overnight: Palace Luzern - Deluxe Double/Twin with lake view
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 11

    DEPART

    You will have a private car transfer to Zurich airport.

    Meal Plan: B

SWITZERLAND AND NORTHERN ITALY

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 10 days

    A 10-day FIT itinerary showcasing the beautiful highlights of Switzerland and Northern Italy.

    Highlights: Geneva, Lausanne, Zermatt, Chillon, Lugano, Lake Maggiore, St. Moritz, Lake Como, Interlaken, Bern, Lucerne.

  • Day 1

    ARRIVE GENEVA

    Upon arrival at Geneva Airport you are met by your local representative and accompanied to your hotel in Lausanne, where you are assisted with check-in.

    Overnight: Beau Rivage Palace

  • Day 2

    LAUSANNE

    Today you will enjoy a four hour city tour by private vehicle with your English-speaking guide. Your tour will include the Old Town. The Gothic Cathedral of Notre Dame, an architectural treasure, is Switzerland's largest church and probably its finest. The Hôtel de Ville is the seat of municipal and communal councils, and the painted medieval Fontaine de la Justice draws people to lounge on its heavy rim. You will also visit the Olympic museum.

    Overnight: Beau Rivage Palace
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    LAUSANNE - CHILLON - ZERMATT

    This morning, you will meet your driver-guide and transfer by private vehicle to Taesch. En route you will stop in Montreux for a guided tour of Château de Chillon, a 12th century castle that rises out of the water at Veytaux. The châteaux was once used as a prison and was the inspiration for Lord Byron's famous poem "The Prisoner of Chillon". Upon arrival in Taesch you will be met by your local representative and escorted by taxi and electric car to your hotel in Zermatt.

    Overnight: Grand Hotel Zermatterhof
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    ZERMATT

    Today enjoy a half day excursion with your English speaking guide up to Gornergrat (subject to weather conditions). The Matterhorn dominates the skyline, creating alpine vistas that are breathtaking. Have a quick stop before returning down to Zermatt and your hotel.

    Overnight: Grand Hotel Zermatterhof
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 5

    ZERMATT - LAKE MAGGIORE - LUGANO

    Leave Zermatt this morning with your driver and make your way across the border of Switzerland to Italy. First stop is in Stresa where you will enjoy a four hour private boat cruise during which you will visit the Borromean islands of Isola Bella and Isola Madre. Isola Bella is home to the 17th century Palazzo Borromeo and its splendid landscaped gardens, whilst much of Isola Madre is given over to a botanical garden. After the lake cruise continue onto your hotel located in Lugano.

    Overnight: Villa Principe & Leopoldo Residence
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 6

    LUGANO - LAKE COMO - ST. MORITZ

    Today make your way back into Switzerland as you travel along the old roman road, the Via Regina. Admire the beautiful scenery, with roads lined by the clear lakes and majestic mountains of the Lake Como region. You will overnight in the luxury resort town of St. Moritz.

    Overnight: Badrutt's Palace Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 7

    ST. MORITZ - INTERLAKEN

    Board the Glacier Express in St. Moritz for the scenic train journey to Andermatt. First class seating will be reserved in the panoramic carriage. Sit back and relax as you take in the breathtaking mountain landscape of the Swiss Alps. You will be met in Andermatt and transferred by private vehicle to your hotel in Interlaken.

    Overnight: Victoria Jungfrau Grand Hotel & Spa
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 8

    JUNGFRAUJOCH

    Meet your English-speaking guide at the hotel lobby for a full day excursion to Jungfraujoch. This excursion takes you to the very heart of the Bernese Alps - from the green valleys to the glistening, eternal snow fields and glaciers. You will travel by private car from Interlaken to Lauterbrunnen and board the train up to Jungfraujoch, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known as "the roof of Europe" at over 11,000 feet.

    The railways of the Jungfrau region are under unified management and comprise an electrified, highly efficient transport system. For those technically interested: there are different gauges with gradients up to 25%. The highlight, of course, is the spectacular ride through the tunnel (4.4 miles long) cut into the rocks of the Eiger. The travelling time from Lauterbrunnen to the Jungfraujoch is approximately two hours each way. At Jungfraujoch, the highest railway station in Europe, you can visit the Ice Palace, which was hewn into the glacier in 1934, and the Sphinx Terraces - reached by lift affording breathtaking views of the Aletsch Glacier, the largest river of ice in Europe. Return to your hotel after the tour, first by train back to Grindelwald, then by private transfer to Interlaken.

    Overnight: Victoria Jungfrau Grand Hotel & Spa
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 9

    INTERLAKEN - BERN - LUCERNE

    Depart Interlaken today with your private vehicle and English-speaking driver for Lucerne. Stop en route in Bern for a sightseeing tour of this ancient city. Bern, built between the 12th and the 18th centuries, still maintains much of its medieval town today and was designated a World Landmark in 1983 by the United Nations. It has the feel of an overgrown country town, with red geraniums on windowsills and a local market each week. The Kunstmuseum possesses the world's largest collection of Paul Klee's works, which combine abstract elements with recognisable forms in a fantasy of light colours and lines. Visit the famous clock tower and the interesting Albert Einstein house before continuing to your hotel in Lucerne.

    Overnight: Palace Hotel Lucerne
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 10

    DEPART ZURICH

    Private transfer with English-speaking driver to Zurich Airport for your return flight home.

    Meal Plan: B
