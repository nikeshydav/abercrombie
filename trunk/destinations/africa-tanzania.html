Tanzania safaris & luxury holidays


Tanzania welcomes you to a land that prides itself on being the home of steep and dramatic mountain ranges, palm fringed sandy beaches and wildlife that you have never heard of. Imagine millions of wildebeest running across the plains of Tanzania, migrating to a new place for food. This is what makes Tanzania special. Cunning predators wait for this moment making it a great place for game viewing. Rich culture, protected coral reefs and its idyllic climate are major crowd pullers. For climbers and trekkers alike, a climb to the highest free standing mountain-Mt. Kilimanjaro is worth all the travel.

To witness a spectacular game of nature, come to Tanzania's northern parks of Tarangire, Serengeti, Ngorongoro and Lake Manyara. They are easily accessible and support a high concentration of predators and animals like zebra, elephants etc. Home to large maned lions, you can also try the southern parks of the Selous or Ruaha. If its alone time that you want, then head to the pristine white beaches of Zanzibar where you can relax under the palms and indulge in a walking safari if you want.
