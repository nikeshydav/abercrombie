Suggested The Garden Route accommodation

  • Hunter's Country House

    Ideally located on the Garden Route, Hunter's Country House makes the perfect base from which to explore this stunning area. Hunter's is an exclusive family owned retreat set in magnificent gardens, surrounded by indigenous forest set against the backdrop of the Tsitsikamma mountains. Hunter's Country House is a member of Relais & Chateaux and therefore an excellent standard of service is assured.

    Extensive, manicured gardens house 21 individually decorated en suite cottages and suites. Each offer homely comforts and privacy. Breakfast is best enjoyed in the sun-drenched conservatory or perhaps relaxing by the pool.

    Within the grounds there is a relaxing pool area or, for the more energetic explore the forest trails and garden walks. Massage and reflexology can be arranged in your suite for any aching muscles. For the younger members of the family there is a fully supervised recreation area. Cuisine at Hunter's is one of the highlights of your stay - food is prepared combining fresh local ingredients, whilst picnics can also be arranged to ensure that you make the most of your days of exploration.

    Kurland
    The Garden Route , South Africa

    Where the foothills of the Outeniqua Mountains sweep down to the warm waters of the Indian Ocean, emerald-green pastures and immaculate polo fields enclose this luxurious country-house hotel. Just 21 kms from Plettenberg Bay, Kurland is ideally located on the Garden Route and can provide endless activities to keep the family entertained.

    The 12 individually-designed guest suites are tailored to suit your needs: open fires, objets d'art and original oil paintings adding character to comfortable rooms with every facility imaginable. Ten rooms have special loft rooms for children and some have private plunge pools for those hot summer evenings.

    Throughout the day, horseback riding, fishing and tennis are the active options, while quieter alternatives are on offer around the swimming pool or in the hotel spa. Children are also welcome, with their own swimming pool and a games room. Situated on a polo estate, there is also the opportunity to learn to play the game. Fine dining draws on the freshest local produce, beautifully presented in a fusion of traditional South African cuisine touched by the cultures of Europe, with a choice of the finest wines and brandies to make the perfect finale to every day.

    Pezula Resort Hotel & Spa

    Perched above the Indian Ocean on the Eastern Head, with views extending to the Knysna Lagoon and Outeniqua Mountains, lies Pezula Resort Hotel & Spa. The setting of this property opens up a wealth of activities from horse riding and nature walks in the indigenous forest or canoeing on the tranquil Noetzie River.

    The hotel has 76 luxurious suites and two magnificent Presidential Suites. Each has spectacular views towards the Knysna Lagoon or the golf course towards the Indian Ocean. All suites have a fireplace, walk-in wardrobe, satellite television and a DVD player as well as a private garden or balcony. Pezula Private Castles is located on one of the most beautiful and secluded beaches in the world, Noetzie Beach. Built into the rugged coastal cliff-face, the castle offers breathtaking panoramic views of the beach and ocean and is the ultimate in ultra-luxury beach accommodation.

    A challenging and rewarding round of golf can be experienced on the championship golf course. The Pezula Spa & Gym has nine therapy rooms with a comprehensive range of treatments. Guests have unlimited use of the state-of-the art gym equipment and the hot spa area. Many sport activities are also available on the estate. Zachary's Restaurant makes dining alfresco a rewarding experience, whilst at night it becomes a romantic and intimate setting. Helicopter or limousine transfers can be arranged, as can use of the Pezula private jet.

    St. James of Knysna

    The St James of Knysna is an ideal base for exploring the Garden Route. The property is an exclusive, owner run country house hotel located in a private three acre landscaped garden, directly on the shores of Knysna Lagoon. The St James of Knysna prides itself on its exclusivity and high standards of service.

    Accommodation is split between The Manor building and the Courtyard building. In total there are 13 luxurious suites with amazing views across Knysna Lagoon or over the immaculate gardens. All of the rooms and suites are equipped with an en suite bathroom, mini bar, tea/coffee making facilities, satellite television, safe, iron and ironing board, direct-dial telephone, bath robes and hairdryer.

    Take a stroll around the peaceful gardens and large Koi pond. There are two swimming pools and croquet is also available. From the private jetty there is the opportunity to go fishing, canoeing and also water-skiing. Sightseeing cruises across the lagoon can also be arranged. Breakfasts and light lunches are served on patios and terraces around the property. For an extra special dining experience drive a short distance to The Boma at Phantom Forest, where new age international cuisine has recently been rewarded by the Wine Society of South Africa. The St James of Knysna is an ideal base for exploring the Garden Route.

    The Plettenberg Hotel

    Built on a rocky headland overlooking two glorious bays, this palatial mansion, built at the turn of the century as a stately beach house, has been transformed into one of the country's finest hotels.

    Thirty five rooms and suites are set within the original mansion and the Blue Wing. All rooms have recently received upgrades to their bathrooms to ensure a fresh light and airy feel. Guest rooms and suites also benefit from under floor heating and air-conditioning for warm summer days. Two child friendly villas provide the perfect stop off for family holidays along the Garden Route.

    Relax in the infinity pool and gaze over the Indian Ocean, where, in winter, the whales arrive to mate and calve. Breakfast and lunches are served on the terrace or in the enclosed sunroom in the Blue Wing with panoramic views of the ocean and mountains. Enjoy a cocktail in the ultra-chic Sand bar and as night falls over the spectacular scene, experience a sumptuous meal at Sand restaurant. Browse The Collection Boutique, filled with local crafts, jewellery and new Polo clothing range. Don't miss a walk in the Robberg Nature Reserve, its beauty is astounding and only a short distance from the hotel. Boat trips, tennis, cycling, horse riding and golf are all available nearby.

    Tsala Treetop Lodge

    Tsala Treetop Lodge is ideally placed to explore the wonders of the marine world, though it has to be said that guests here find it hard to tear themselves away from the comfort of their suites, set high up in the trees. As a Hunter Hotels property and member of Relais & Chateaux, service and comforts levels are assured.

    Above the forest floor, the ten exclusive and private suites of Tsala Treetop Lodge are linked by meandering wooden walkways and share the lofty canopy of Tsitsikamma's primal forest in a symphony of wood, glass and water. Each suite has a private infinity pool, airy wooden decks and lavishly appointed bathrooms as well as outside showers.

    In the evening, the intimate sitting rooms and library, stocked with collectable books on natural history and tales from the past, form tranquil and atmospheric havens of quiet. Dine on open wooden platforms amongst the encompassing trees, or in the acclaimed dining room where carefully prepared and exquisitely presented dishes of both local and faraway flavour combines the tastes of Europe with the traditions of Africa.
