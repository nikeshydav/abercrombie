Aotearoa Cultural Encounter

  • Duration: 5 Days / 4 Nights

    Explore the vivid Aotearoa culture of New Zealand as you venture out to amazingly distinctive sights.

    Programme Overview

    Auckland – Rotorua

    A&K Advantage
    Wondrous Waitomo Glow Worm Cave
    Spectacularly-situated Solitaire Lodge
    Insider Access at Kiwi Encounter
    Thermal sightseeing including Lady Knox Geyser
    The cultural heart, Rotorua

    • Detailed Itinerary

  • Day 1

    ARRIVE AUCKLAND

    On arrival you will be met by a representative, transferred privately to your accommodation and be assisted with check-in. Unwind and relax from your lengthy flight and later explore the glittery pulsating, country’s largest city and commercial hub.
    Meet your guide and head for a private sightseeing tour which will include a scenic drive along the waterfront, the quaint Victorian village of Parnell and panoramic views from the extinct volcano - Mount Eden. At the Auckland Museum see a world-renowned collection of Polynesian artifacts.
    OR
    Visit the SKYCITY located in the heart of the city below the iconic Sky Tower. Get on top and experience spectacular 360 degree views of the city. You could walk around the pergola at SkyWalk or take a controlled free-fall to ground level at SkyJump.
    On conclusion you will return to your accommodation.

    Overnight: Hotel Sofitel or similar
    Meal Plan: None

  • Day 2

    AUCKLAND – ROTORUA

    This morning after breakfast, you will be assisted with a transfer to Rotorua. Enroute at Waitomo, explore the famous Glowworm Caves on a 45-minute tour, where you will marvel at the thousands of magical glow worms above you as you glide gently on a boat through the world-famous caves. The legend of Waitomo began more than 30 million years ago with the creation of limestone at the bottom of the ocean. Now these limestone formations stand as one of New Zealand's most inspiring natural wonders. Across Cambridge to “The Shire’s Rest” you can have the wonderful opportunity to see the original Hobbiton Movie Set. Hobbiton Movie Set, made famous by the movie trilogies “The Lord of the Rings” and “The Hobbit”. Continue south to Rotorua to your accommodation.

    Overnight: Solitaire Lodge / Peppers on the Point or similar
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    ROTORUA

    Visit the Rainbow Springs Nature Park, a wildlife park with a strong focus on conservation, and New Zealand's only 'open to view' kiwi hatchery and rearing facility. Learn about New Zealand's unique national icon on a private behind-the-scenes tour featuring this endearing flightless bird. Afterwards, ride high above Rotorua on the gondola cableway for panoramic views of this thermal city and its nearby lake. Later, enjoy an exclusive shearing demonstration, sheepdog trial and farm tour at the Agrodome working farm. The tour gives you the opportunity of hands-on experience with a variety of farm animals. Accompanied by an experienced guide, you can also look around the organic orchard and sample kiwifruit juice and local honey.

    OR

    Relax and enjoy some of the many activities of this natural playground. From the lakeside wharf there is access to motorized dinghies, kayaks and fishing gear. Black swans glide in front of you, tempting islands await your visit, and nearby bays bask in a silence that suggests that you are many miles from habitation. Spend some time taking in the scenery and listening to the birdcalls.Fishing, lake walks, floatplane and helicopter flights are also available (additional costs may apply).

    Overnight: Solitaire Lodge / Peppers on the Point or similar
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    ROTORUA

    This morning, drive from your lodge to the thermal wonderland of Wai-O-Tapu. Visit its world-famous Champagne Pool, marvel at huge volcanic craters and New Zealand's largest bubbling mud pool, and witness the spectacular morning eruption of the Lady Knox Geyser. The day's adventure continues with an exhilarating jetboat journey on the might Waikato River, including a visit to Orakei Korako. This magnificent thermal reserve features some of the world's largest silica terraces and the stunning Ruatapu Cave, which extends some 120 ft down to a hot-water pool. Then, thrill to an action-packed return jetboat ride before retiring for the day.

    Overnight: Solitaire Lodge / Peppers on the Point or similar
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 5

    DEPART ROTORUA

    Transfer to Rotorua Airport Australiafor your flight.
    OR
    Extend your stay to explore the wonders of South Island.

    Meal Plan: B

Wilderness Escape

  • Duration: 8 Days / 7 Nights

    Explore the remote landscapes of the southern part of New Zealand. An ultimate experience for nature lovers with a self-drive or chauffeured vehicle options to explore South Island.

    Programme Overview

    Christchurch – Dunedin – Invercargill – Stewart Island – Queenstown – Milford Sound

    A&K Advantage
    Enjoy an exclusive and personalised tour of Christchurch's International Antarctic Centre.
    A punt on the Avon River.
    Experience a close encounter with nature and the endemic species at Dunedin the ‘Wildlife Capital’ of New Zealand.
    Drive through a series of rural settlements on the Oreti River.
    Take a breathtaking scenic flight to the World Heritage listed Milford Sound.

    • Detailed Itinerary

  • Day 1

    ARRIVE CHRISTCHURCH

    On arrival at Christchurch, you will be met by a representative and privately transferred to your accommodation. Relax and unwind from the flight.

    You will be met by a guide for your orientation tour of Christchurch followed by a unique Christchurch experience at the Antarctic Centre. Join an Old Antarctic Explorer on an exclusive and personalised tour of Christchurch's International Antarctic Centre discovering New Zealand's Scott Base, the Snow & Ice Experience and the Antarctic Storm where you will experience extreme Antarctic conditions. Post a light lunch at the center; enjoy a cruise to Antarctica on board an Antarctic Cruise Ship in the brand new 4D Extreme Theatre or enjoy watching the penguins being fed in the New Zealand Penguin Encounter. At the end of your 3-hour personalised Antarctic experience you leave with a signed copy of an Antarctic book which will be yours to keep.

    End your day with 30minute calming punt on the Avon. Relax and admire the beautiful landscape as you float down the river - an iconic Christchurch experience not to be missed.

    Overnight: The George Christchurch
    Meal Plan: L

  • Day 2

    CHRISTCHURCH – DUNEDIN

    On check out, self-drive vehicle will be handed over in case you choose to self-drive; alternately depart in a private chauffeured vehicle towards Timaru; known for its extensive Edwardian and Victorian architecture, before you continue to Dunedin.

    Driving Distance: 361 kms approx.
    Duration: 6 hrs approx.

    First settled by the Scottish, Dunedin is renowned as the "Edinburgh of the South". Relax and settle into your lodgings for the night.

    Overnight: Corstorphine House
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    DUNEDIN

    The Otago Peninsula within the city boundaries of Dunedin is internationally renowned for its wildlife reserves and is considered the ‘Wildlife Capital’ of New Zealand. It is renowned for its albatross colony, Yellow-Eyed Penguin and Little Blue Penguin colonies along with various species of seals and pelagic birds.

    This day you exclusively experience a close encounter with nature and unrivalled viewing of penguins, fur seals and sea lions on your private full day guided tour. Travel past lush pastures, small bays and inlets, rugged hills and white sandy beaches. Witness the rarest penguin in the world waddle up the beach after a day feeding at sea, bedlam in a fur seal breeding colony and majestic albatross above the headland.

    Overnight: Corstorphine House
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    DUNEDIN – INVERCARGILL

    We recommend another memorable experience unique to this region. Board the Dunedin Silver Fern Railcar for the Taieri Gorge Railway experience. Considered to be a highlight of the rail network in New Zealand, this luxury trains operates three times daily and each 90minute tour gives you an opportunity to experience stunning views along the Otago Harbour the dazzling Blue Skin Bay.

    Later, travel south through the stunning scenery and plethora of wildlife towards Invercargill.

    Driving Distance: 205 kkms approx.
    Duration: 3 hrs approx.

    On arrival at Invercargill, stop by the famed Estuary Walkway located a couple of kilometers south of Invercargill. It is a walking/cycling loop track where the New River Estuary and the Waihopai River meet. The area used to be a landfill and has now benefited from a community regeneration project. It now provides a rich habitat for wildlife and people to enjoy.

    Overnight: The Lodge at Tikana
    Meal Plan: B, D

  • Day 5

    STEWART ISLAND OR FIORDLAND EXPLORATION

    A day of adventure and exploration lies ahead! You could begin with a breathtaking 20 minute scenic flight from Invercargill to forested Stewart Island to learn about its life, history, places of interest and scenery followed by an in-depth walk around Ulva Island Nature Sanctuary. Enjoy close encounters with native birds and hear exquisite bird song.

    Alternately, you could take a helicopter tour from the lodge and fly towards the majestic Fiordland, a world Heritage area. Soak in the wilderness experience as you fly over the breath taking scenery of amazing lakes, waterfalls, lush mountain slopes, hanging valleys and perhaps even panoramic views over the Te Anau Basin.

    Overnight: The Lodge at Tikana
    Meal Plan: B, D

  • Day 6

    INVERCARGILL – QUEENSTOWN

    Your journey continues as you drive through a series of rural settlements on the Oreti River. After Lumsden the scenery becomes gradually more alpine. Take a break in Kingston, the home of a remarkable vintage steam train. From here the road hugs the incredibly picturesque Lake Wakatipu nearly all the way to Queenstown.

    Driving Distance: 187 kkms approx.
    Duration: 2 hrs 45 mins approx.

    Overnight: Matakauri Lodge
    Meal Plan: B, D

  • Day 7

    QUEENSTOWN

    Enjoy the vibrancy of your Queenstown base before being whisked away for an exclusive experience as you fly to Milford Sound by private helicopter. Soar over mountains, age old glaciers and prehistoric rainforests in a habitat seldom frequented by man. Stroll on an uninhabited beach and enjoy lunch at a remote alpine lake. This relaxed and unhurried expedition distills the essence of New Zealand's most dramatic landscapes with many outstandingly notable sights.
    At Milford Sound you could visit the Milford Discovery Center and the Milford Deep Underwater Observatory for a guided dive allowing you access to sights rarely revealed to human visitors.

    Overnight: Matakauri Lodge
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 8

    QUEENSTOWN

    Another day of memorable moments is in store, as you wake at the crack of dawn today for an experience of a lifetime. Enjoy a hot air balloon ride at sunrise. Share an incredible morning with the whole family as you rise to high altitudes and drift with the breeze, you will witness quite possibly the best panoramic views of Queenstown and its surrounds. Views of the spectacular Southern Alps, lakes, rivers, NZ's finest Golf Courses and much more! On landing, enjoy your champagne breakfast.

    Later in the day, maybe you could head towards an exclusive jet boat experience on the Dart River. From the comfort of your fully equipped vessel discover the spectacular beauty that makes up this unforgettable river.

    OR

    You could take a ride up the skyline gondola to the Bobs Peak. You will be totally awed by the panoramic picturesque views of Queenstown.

    OR

    You could take the most exhilarating experience in Queensland - the NZONE Skydive! Get harnessed to an experienced qualified jumpmaster and jump out of an aircraft into thin air for an experience that will change you forever. Perhaps follow it up with a parachute jump over the spectacular Remarkables mountain range or Lake Wakatipu.

    Overnight: Matakauri Lodge
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 9

    DEPART QUEENSTOWN

    At Queenstown airport, handover your self-drive vehicle in case you were self-driving. Alternately, you will be privately transferred to the airport to board your flight.

    Meal Plan: B
