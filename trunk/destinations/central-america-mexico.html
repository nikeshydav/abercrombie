Travel to Mexico


Mexico is one of most visited tourists' destinations in the world. Famed for its gifts to us such as Tequila, the Aztecs, Mayans, Corona, Salma Hayek, Day of the Dead festival as well as it much celebrated cuisine, the Mexican culture celebrates life in all walks. One can't help but enjoy various contrasts in traditions, landscapes, and lifestyles that Mexico has on offer. Mexico City, the capital is one of the biggest metropolitan areas in the world, whereas the dense jungles of Chiapas showcase mesmerizing scenery, which equally matches the glorious Mayan sites of Teotihuacán and Uxmal.

Abercrombie & Kent luxury holidays in Mexico aren't just about adventure or relaxation, rather enriching and inspiration. A tailor-made travel to Mexico takes you on a glorious discovery of cultural and archaeological highlights on Hispanic and pre-Columbian eras, where you can also explore the pristine and crystal clear Pacific and Caribbean coastline. Mexico's sun kissed beaches, bays and lively reefs embrace within a world of luxurious retreat in the Riviera Maya, on the other hand, Pacific Coast boasts of biologically diverse waters where nature enthusiasts can witness the rare marine life such as whales and dolphins.

Also, noteworthy is a visit to the Colonial Heartland lying in the central highland of Mexico, which houses many UNESCO's World Heritage centres in charming cities such as Guanajuato, San Miguel. Not too far from the Colonial Heartland is Oaxaca City, where visitors can marvel over the famous Day of the Dead festival. If you need your adrenaline fix then, head over to the Copper Canyon, the biggest in North America or Baja California, which is hallowed with a stunning diverse geographical landscape.
