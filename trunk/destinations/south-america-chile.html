There are a number of places to visit in Chile that you absolutely cannot miss out on. The Chilean Lake District is an idyllic setting for a holiday, with the water merging seamlessly together with land, making for a dazzling view. Santiago is the capital of Chile, as well as a gateway to the ski areas and sandy beaches.
