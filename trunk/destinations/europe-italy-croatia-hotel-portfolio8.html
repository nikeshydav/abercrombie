Suggested Tuscany accommodation

  • Castel Monastero

    The ideal holiday for art lovers, foodies and wine connoisseurs, Castel Monastero boasts a superb location, luxurious furnishings, endless relaxation and cuisine deserving of its five stars.

    Located just 23km from Siena, the hotel sits in the medieval village of Monastero dell'Ombrone and is furnished with simple Tuscan pieces alongside masterpieces from the area, radiating pure elegance and a typical Italian ambiance.

    Castiglion Del Bosco

    Situated in the heart of Tuscany's Brunello winemaking region, Castiglion del Bosco is one of Tuscany's largest contiguous estates. It is located in the Val d'Orcia, 12 km from the Province of Siena and just 95 km south of Florence. During your stay here you will experience sophisticated yet relaxed living, sumptuous suites and villas combined with a seamless and unobtrusive service.

    The estate is beautifully situated and has seen many changes in its 800-year history, yet in essence it has changed very little. Scattered across the property are remnants of a rich past: a circular Etruscan stronghold dating back to 600 BC, a 12th century castle fortress, ruins of an early Gothic chapel (c. 900 BC) and Chiesa di San Michele Arcangelo, with its spectacular Lorenzetti fresco.

    Grand Hotel Continental

    Stay at the Grand Hotel Continental within the city's walls and benefit from Siena's delights on your doorstep. This 17th Century palazzo was built originally as a wedding gift and became the preferred residence for those on the Grand Tour. The Grand Hotel is an architectural masterpiece of vaulted ceilings, stone arches and, at its heart, a soaring, glass roofed atrium.

    This hotel oozes opulence and warmth. The room too are works of art, almost literally. Since the time of its original construction, the palazzo has been repeatedly embellished by artists, craftsmen and decorators. They have made each room its own unique palette of frescoes, paintings, terracotta floors, mirrors with inlaid silver frames, delicate silk, Chinese porcelain lamps and Carrara marble bathrooms.

    HOTEL CASTELLO DI CASOLE

    Hotel Castello di Casole offers 41 suites in a range of styles and sizes to accommodate visitors in grand style. From junior suites in the main hotel building to apartment-style olive grove suites a short walk away, these enticing spaces have been painstakingly restored using original materials found on the estate. Authentic details - wood-beamed ceilings, stone walls and hand-plastered finishes - are gently coaxed into the modern era with luxurious fabrics, fixtures and furnishings, an alluring blend of old and new.

    In addition to the suites, choose from The Bargagli Penthouse - a highlight of the main hotel. A two-story, 3-bedroom masterpiece where every detail is a work of art. Families may be interested in renting one of the Hotel Villas just steps away or for a bit more privacy, the Farmhouses, set on hillsides throughout the 4,200-acre property. No matter the choice, one will enjoy the comforts of home, amazing views of the Tuscan hills and exceptional services.

    Castello di Casole has everything to make life in the countryside a delight. There's fine and casual dining, a luxurious spa, pools and fitness centers, and adventures throughout the estate during every season.

    Home to remarkable dining experiences, a collection of fine and casual dining venues serving traditional Italian cuisine with a twist. From a perfect cappuccino as the Tuscan sun sets, to a romantic picnic lunch in the vineyards, to dining al fresco with friends and family, food and drink are an essential part of everyday.

    The focus is on simple, rustic food in the classic Italian tradition. Expect handmade pastas, fresh organic vegetables, fresh fish and game from local sources - all paired with fine wines from the best wineries in Italy and around the world. Whether you choose to dine in your private residence, order room service at the hotel, take Italian cooking classes from master chefs, or visit one of the restaurants, every day in Tuscany stirs the soul in some new way.
