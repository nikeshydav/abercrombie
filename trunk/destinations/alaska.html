Introduction to Alaska


Vast stretches of bare land, sparkling blue lakes, spectacular natural landscape and unique Native culture are what characterize the largest state of United States. However Alaska remains largely an underexplored and undiscovered strip of land because of its jagged and enormous topography.

Much of the journey across the state takes place in floatplanes which is perhaps the best way to get an exclusive bird's eye view of the widespread wilderness here. The beauty here is endless and so are the odds of discovery, rejuvenation and experiencing a once in a lifetime Holiday in Alaska, the largest state of the US.

For adrenaline junkies, opportunities galore await with numerous rugged hiking trails, hidden caves and rivers suitable for kayaking and other water sports. Abercrombie & Kent's Tour to Alaska takes you on an intimate discovery of this diverse land brimming with majestic towering mountains, glaciers, spectacular flora and fauna.
