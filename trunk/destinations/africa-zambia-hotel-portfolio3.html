Suggested Victoria Falls, Zambia accommodation

  • Islands of Siankaba

    Located 24 miles upstream from Victoria Falls is the perfect haven for those wanting total peace and relaxation. Spread across two densely forested islands in the Zambezi River and linked by a wooden suspension bridge, the lodge caters for just fourteen guests in seven tree-top suites, giving the feeling of exclusivity and intimacy.

    With fantastic views of the Zambezi River, guests can enjoy the shaded plunge pool and recline on the terrace, watching the wildlife pass by. Meals are best enjoyed outside in this wonderfully romantic setting. Bird watching trips, sunset cruises and canoe excursions are all available from the lodge.

    Royal Livingstone

    On the forested banks of the Zambezi River, upstream of the mighty cataract where it thunders into the Batoka Gorge, is The Royal Livingstone. The epitome of luxury in the heart of the African bush, the Royal Livingstone provides 173 spacious, en suite air-conditioned luxury guest rooms each with private veranda.

    Luxuriously furnished, with crisp white linen and large comfortable beds, bathrooms feature spacious showers and Victorian style baths. Wildlife pencil sketches adorn the cool ivory-washed walls, reflecting the textures and moods of the Zambezi River. Its cool lounges and shaded verandas exude the elegance and luxury of an earlier, more courteous age.
