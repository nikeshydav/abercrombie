Suggested Serengeti Accommodation

  • Dunia Camp

    Hidden away between the central and Southern Serengeti, this semi-permanent luxurious camp is in a private location amongst the Moru Kopjes. 7 spacious en suite tents are equipped with comfortable beds, a large sitting area and contemporary furniture.

    Wildlife can be viewed on incredible game drives and can also be heard from your tent or when sitting around the fire at night.

    • Delicious food, excellent service, knowledgeable guides and luxurious tents.
    • The open sided vehicles for a natural safari experience
    • With its peaceful and intimate setting, perfect place to relax

    In the know
    Spend time with the guides in search of the endangered black rhino which can be found in the area.

    Grumeti River Camp

    Offering exceptional year-round game viewing, the camp lies in peaceful seclusion in a remote river valley. This is your comfortable haven from which to experience the beating heart of Africa.

    Every year during the Great Migration, hundreds of thousands of animals cross the Grumeti River, risking the jaws of some of the world's largest crocodiles. The excitement is mirrored in the ten vibrantly designed canopied safari tents set along the riverbank, each with an en suite bathroom and romantic al fresco shower open to the sun and stars. The sparkling pool and domed bar are captivating, while the cuisine is a marvel.

    Read more

    Kirawira Serena Tented Camp

    Kirawira is the epitome of luxurious colonially-styled safari camps. Commanding panoramic views over the endlessly rolling reaches and volcanic reefs of Serengeti National Park, it recalls a bygone era of explorers and pioneers.

    Personal valet service and superb cuisine make for the most satisfying of experiences. The 25 deluxe individual tents are set on wooden platforms, each graced with a large, opulently decorated double bedroom and bathroom. Best of all a private veranda affords spectacular views over the shimmering plains. A great variety of large game can be viewed in the lush vegetation around the camp.

    Klein's Camp

    Klein's Camp is located on a private ranch of 25,000 on the north-eastern boundary of Tanzania just south of the Mara plains. The camp is set on the edge of the Kuku Hills which offers panoramic views of the valley below.

    The 10 tastefully furnished cottages which are built in local rock with traditional thatch-roofs have private verandas with sweeping views of the valley below. The dining and bar facilities are in separate rondavels with commanding views of the Grumeti River Valley. Meals are prepared to satisfy the most discerning palate, and include fresh vegetables from the ranch.

    • Enjoy the excitement and adrenaline of a night safari
    • A walking safari in the game rich valley below the camp will sure to be a highlight
    • Have a chance to witness the migration heading North across the border in July/August or heading back South again in October/November
    • Cool off in the lodge pool after your mornings game drive

    IN THE KNOW
    Bush breakfasts and dinners in spectacular locations is a speciality of Klein's Camp and not to be missed.

    Sanctuary Kusini Luxury Camp

    Sanctuary Kusini sits in exclusivity in a remote area of these "endless plains" of legend, affording a rare opportunity for secluded game drives well away from the normal tourist trail. Relaxed and welcoming in atmosphere, here you can make your days and nights as lively or tranquil as you wish, with or without children.

    Stay in a permanent camp that blends seamlessly into the delicate environment of the predator-rich plains of the Serengeti. One of the oldest ecosystems on earth, this is where early Man appeared. Today, witness the thundering and awe-inspiring migration of over a million wildebeest and some 200,000 zebra - and the majestic big cats that prey on them. Expert guides enhance your visit by sharing their knowledge and experience acquired during a lifetime in the bush. Luscious banquets in the wild and sundowner cocktails add even more memories. Exclusive in location, luxurious in setting and rich in experience, Kusini offers a dreamy encounter with the real Africa.

    Accommodation
    Few holiday locations pay homage to the surrounding environment with the same astonishing effect. Your temporary home in the bush will be one of only 12 well-appointed tents with highly polished floors and elevated wooden terraces overlooking the dramatic panorama of short grass plains. Attended by a personal butler and positioned in amongst the rocky outcrops to ensure your privacy, these first-rate pavilions feature an en suite bathroom with shower and comfortable furnishings throughout. Gather for elegant candlelit meals and safari tales in the dining tent, and perhaps take a book from the library for a quiet read on the veranda - or back on your own personal deck.

    Sayari Camp

    Tanzania's stunning Sayari Camp is situated in the superb location of Northern Serengeti. Fifteen lavish tents overlook the vast Serengeti plains, making it the perfect location to witness the great migration crossing the Mara River.

    The modern tents are all en-suite with large comfortable king-size beds and a free standing bath tub situated in front of a sliding window. All tents are positioned well for perfect privacy.

    Delicious meals including authentic African and European cuisine are most satisfying after a day on safari or when enjoying a relaxing evening meal. August and September are the perfect time to view the amazing migration cross the river.

    • Witness Hippos, zebras, lions, elephants and leopards in their own habitat on a walking or game drive safari
    • Feel welcomed with friendly and knowledgeable staff offering an outstanding service
    • Enjoy homemade cookies and a hot water bottle before bed to relax yourself after a breath-taking day at safari

    IN THE KNOW
    Children are more than welcome at Sayari and the Askaris will spend time with them teaching them how to shoot with bows and arrows on targets made out of boxes.

    Serengeti Migration Camp

    Situated in the North-West of Serengeti, this small luxury camp has been set up on the side of a hill, in a rock formation. On the south side of the Grumeti River, west of Lobo, Migration Camp makes the most of a high location on a kopje.

    It's a particularly comfortable camp with 20 high-quality canvas rooms raised on a polished wooden deck. Each is very spacious and stylish with a fan, a writing desk, well-chosen fabrics, leather furniture and plenty of thoughtful touches. The extensive dining and bar areas are open to the view, and have excellent food and service.

    • Have a chance to witness the amazing migration during the months of June/July
    • Watch the sun setting over the Serengeti while enjoying an ice cold glass of champagne
    • Spend a full day game viewing in the Serengeti, stopping off for a sumptuous lunch in the bush
    • Relax by the swimming pool after your morning

    IN THE KNOW
    Enjoy a romantic candle lit dinner on the private deck of your tent.
