SUGGESTED MUNICH ACCOMMODATION

  • Mandarin Oriental Hotel

    Formerly the Rafael, The Mandarin Oriental Hotel is one of the most exclusive hotels in the city, offering guests an excellent service in intimate and elegant surroundings. The Hotel is a beautifully renovated neo-Renaissance building and all the bedrooms are individually furnished in a style of Mandarin Oriental.

    Buffet breakfast and dinner are served in the main restaurant, and there are two further outlets for more informal dining. Other facilities include a rooftop outdoor heated freshwater pool (open in the summer) as well as an in-house Fitness Room. The hotel is excellently located for exploration of the city centre, with many sights and stores located in the vicinity.

    The Charles Hotel Munich

    The Rocco Forte Charles Hotel, opened in October 2007, and is located in the centre of Munich within the Old Botanical Gardens. The hotel has its own personality with paintings from Franz von Lenbach, Munich's famous 19th century artist, in all public areas.

    The contemporary designed rooms are at least 40 square metres in size, and offer views over the city, the Alps, or the gardens. The Hotel Restaurant offers Italian cuisine with views over the Old Botanical Gardens, and a small bar.
