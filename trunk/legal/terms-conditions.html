The payments made for the travel arrangements are to be in accordance with the applicable guidelines issued by Reserve Bank of India (RBI) and Government of India, and any other applicable law for foreign exchange transactions.

Note*: Passports issued with validity for 20 years are no longer accepted or recognized by some of the foreign missions in India for the purpose of grant of visas.
