Luxury Holidays by Abercrombie & Kent



Abercrombie & Kent has been successfully perfecting the art of tailor-made luxury travel for over five decades. We are pioneers in experiential luxury holidays since 1962, with a network of 50 offices in six continents and more than 2,200 employees, we help our discerning clients experience the world from a unique perspective. Each journey is flawlessly tailored to provide the traveler a hassle free experience. Our exceptional guides and local experts, sumptuous food, premium wines and luxurious properties all form part of our customized itineraries. We work hard to get to know each and every client and partner with them to design the ultimate itinerary that will be exciting, unforgettable, life changing and etched into their memories forever. Being the world's leading authority on luxury and experiential travel, we do not compromise on our standards and impeccable personal service is a guarantee. Our experts on the ground don't just receive our clients at their destination; they also take a keen interest in educating them about history, culture, traditions of the destination and showing the local way of life in a manner that makes them feel like they are in their own home.


Safaris, Cruises, Photography Tours, Family Holidays, Journeys by Private Jets, Luxury Trains, Extreme Adventures, Group Journeys or Honeymoons whatever your passion, Abercrombie & Kent will take you on a trip of a lifetime.
