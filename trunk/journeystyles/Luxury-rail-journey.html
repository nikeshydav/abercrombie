Luxury rail journeys

Luxury train conjure up images of handsomely crafted compartments, elegant dining cars serving gourmet meals on fine porcelain, silver cutlery, rich, white tablecloth and an attendant waiting by your side. Evoking a strong sense of association as in history, heritage and decadence of a leisure ride and offering a classy journey, luxury train travel is all things stylish and graceful.

Enjoy a journey on the Royal Scotsman, evoking gracious hospitality of gentler times. Regarded as one of the world’s most classy and lavish train, life on board is relaxed and indulgent, with gastronomic dining and hospitality in the air. And, being one of the 35 preferred guests onboard helps. Or perhaps, you would like to take the famous Orient-Express through the magical land of poets, lovers and writers and traverse through Europe or the exotic South-East Asia.

Whatever your choice, embark on a magical and luxurious train journey only with Abercrombie & Kent.
