Romantic Fiji Honeymoon

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 7 Days

    3 Nights Castaway Island, Fiji /3 Nights Outrigger on the Lagoon Fiji.

    • ITINERARY:

  • Day 1

    Arrive Fiji

    Arrive at the Nadi International Airport. Your English speaking local representative will welcome you with a “Bula” and escort you to their office where you will receive a chilled bottled water and minted towels to cool off plus your Fiji Travel documents and directed to your onward transfer.

    LUXURY PRIVATE VEHICLE TRANSFER NADI AIRPORT TO DENARAU MARINA

    Enjoy luxury private air conditioned vehicle transfer from Nadi airport to Denarau Marina. You are assured your short (approx 30mins) trip to the Denarau Marina will be a safe and pleasant one

    LAUNCH TRANSFER DENARAU MARINA TO CASTAWAY ISLAND

    Indulge yourself with a relaxing luxury catamaran transfer from Denarau Marina to Castaway Island Resort which is located just west of the largest Island of Fiji. It is approximately a 2hour transfer from the mainland to the island. Enjoy the cruise with the amazing fresh sea breeze and breathtaking views along the way to the island . Departs Port Denarau at 0900hrs and arrives Castaway Island.

    Overnight: Castaway Island, Fiji

  • Day 1 to 4

    Castaway Island, Fiji

    Castaway Island is 174 acres , covered in rich tropical rainforest, surrounded by white sand beaches, vibrant coral reefs and azure waters.. As you arrive at this paradise island resort; you will be welcomed by their friendly staff with a chilled welcome drink. For rest of the stay, simply lay back on the beach or in a hammock or treat yourself to an island style massage. Or enjoy many of the activities available for the young and the young at heart. Complimentary activities include Catamaran sailing, Coral viewing in glass bottom boat , Kayaks , Paddle boards , Snorkeling –beach etc while Banana boat rides , Island hopping excursion , Jet skiing , Knee boarding etc come at a charge. A Dolphin Safari Island Hop by speedboat for 2 persons is included in this package.

    Overnight: Castaway Island, Fiji

  • Day 4

    Helicopter Transfer from Castaway Island, Fiji To Outrigger On The Lagoon Fiji

    After breakfast , its time to board a helicopter transfer from Castaway Island to the Outrigger on the Lagoon. This will be an amazing opportunity for you to view the stunning scenes of the mainland and the Mamanuca groups from a perfect, bird’s eye view.

    Overnight: Outrigger on the Lagoon Fiji

  • Day 4 to 7

    Outrigger on the Lagoon Fiji

    Upon arrival, at Outrigger on the Lagoon Fiji , check in and fill your day with activities or simply unwind and do nothing at all . This five star luxury beach resort features modern air-conditioned hotel rooms and buresthat come together in a style reminiscent of a traditional Fijian Village. Enjoy beach services with complimentary use of kayaks, paddle boards, snorkeling equipment . Other services include Spa , Cultural Activities , Fijian FirewalkingShows, Fitness centre, Massage & beauty services etc , Diving (some come with a fee to be paid directly). Or ask concierge desk to book you on one of the exciting tours from the Coral Coast. A 1 hour Spa Treatment per person at Outrigger’s stunning Bebe Spa is included.

    Overnight: Outrigger on the Lagoon Fiji

  • Day 4 to 7

    Luxury Private Vehicle Transfer Outrigger on The Lagoon Fiji To Nadi Airport

    Check out of the hotel and you will be picked up from your resort approximately 4 hours before your flight departure time by luxury air-conditioned vehicle and transferred to Nadi Airport to board your flight back home or onward destination.

    “End of Services”

Luxury Honeymoon Brazil

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 7 Days

    Highlights: Enjoy Rio de Janeiro sightseeing & cable car ride up Sugar Loaf Mountain & train to Corcovado summit. Top properties in each city: The best boutique hotel in Rio, the best beach resort in Brazil.

    • ITINERARY

  • Day 1

    Arrive Rio de Janeiro / Sugar Loaf

    Welcome to Rio de Janeiro, the city of mountains and sea, samba and carnival, and a relaxed way of life. You are transferred to your hotel along Copacabana Beach. This afternoon, your orientation begins with a cable car ascent of Sugar Loaf Mountain, a tour of city sights, and a ride along the world-famous beaches. Bottle of champagne in the room to celebrate your first night in Rio.

    Overnight: Fasano, Rio De Janeiro

  • Day 2

    Rio de Janeiro / Corcovado & Lunch at Confeitaria Colombo

    This morning, the breakfast will be served in the room. You will be taken to one of Wonders of the World: The statue of Christ the Redeemer, which crowns the top of the Corcovado mountain with stunning views of Guanabara Bay, Sugar Loaf and the beaches of Copacabana and Ipanema below. The perfect way to reach the summit is on the toy train which zig zags its way up through the lush rainforest of the Tijuca Forest where it is possible see monkeys and parrots in the trees. Once on top you will have ample time to admire the views. You will have lunch at Confeitaria Colombo, a 100 year old Tea Room / Restaurant (closed on Saturdays afternoon, Sundays and public holidays) that has been kept in pristine conditions and it is a pleasure to enjoy. It is one of the most traditional restaurants in Rio and it is part of the city's cultural heritage.

    Overnight: Fasano, Rio De Janeiro

  • Day 3

    Rio de Janeiro / Samba Class & Shall we dance

    Samba Dance Class at the prestigious dancing school of Carlinhos de Jesus. Rio de Janeiro is the birthplace of samba. It's impossible to resist the most Brazilian of all beats. You listen to it, and before you notice your feet are tapping, your fingers snapping! If only you knew how to dance it the way locals do... Well, you can. With a 2-hour lesson you'll be ready to do the basic samba steps and body movements, and before you know it you will be dancing really well! Tonight dinner will be at Rio Scenarium which if there was an award for most beautiful bar, it would certainly deserve it. Located in a renovated warehouse on the edge of Lapa, it is an antique store-turned-bar which serves a wide range of seafood and traditional Brazilian dishes. There is also a stage on the main floor where live Brazilian samba and choro bands will be playing as well as dance floor in case you want to samba the night away. This is the perfect place to practice the moves you learnt earlier today.

    Overnight: Fasano, Rio De Janeiro

  • Day 4

    Rio de Janeiro-Florianopolis

    Morning at leisure to relax on the beach or linger at sidewalk cafes or take in an optional tour. In the afternoon, fly to Florianopolis. Upon arrival at Florianopolis airport, you will be met by your guide who will take you to paradise. The destination is "Ganchos". That's how it was baptized by its founders: Azorean immigrants who settled on this lush, mountainous, cove-studded peninsula, more than two hundred years ago at the height of the whaling boom. Some decades ago, the small city was renamed as Governador Celso Ramos. Protected from people's curiosity as by its topography, Ganchos has to this day remained one of the last surviving secrets of the Brazilian coast.

    More than just natural beauty, a whole way of life has been preserved here. The colonists descendants still make their living from the sea – nowadays cultivating oysters, scallops and other shellfish.

    You leave the main road behind and eight miles further on you find yourself winding along through a succession of villages strung along the north-facing peninsula as though frozen in time. Slow down. The coast road passes hard by the picturesque colonial style houses, as calm little bays come into view one by one, the water dotted with the simple boats and mollusk farmers colored buoys.

    At the end of an unassuming lane, a gate will open to reveal the most amazing spot on the whole peninsula.

    Welcome to Ponta dos Ganchos.

    Be warned in advance: it’s not easy to leave the bungalow. Well, of course, the instant you call for one, an electric car will appear at your door in a flash. Nevertheless, sometimes it is going to be difficult to want to leave.
    It might be because of the therapeutic mattress or perhaps, it is because there are just too many threads in the Egyptian cotton sheets. Sometimes the espresso machine or the organic snacks next to it in the mini-bar are to blame.
    Other times it will be a combination of things – your private wine cellar will join forces with the view of the sunset over the bay to prevent you from venturing beyond the terrace. This is the reason why breakfast is like a miniature gourmet festival – a parade of delicacies served in dainty portions. Providing a little competition for the terrace of your bungalow is a little stone island just in front of the beach that you can reach swimming or along a catwalk.

    Overnight: Ponta dos Ganchos, Florianopolis

  • Day 5

    Ponta Dos Ganchos - Governador Celso Ramos

    Once you've become accustomed to the idea that the bungalows, with its creature comforts and its view, is always going to be there waiting for you, you will become more enthusiastic about venturing out. There is a wide selection of short trips, lasting from a couple of hours to a whole afternoon and that don’t involve leaving the peninsula. or going too far away from your whirlpool bath.
    A quick chat with the concierge will provide you with enough activity choices to keep you busy for this visit such as: visit to oyster farm, a walk along deserted beaches, a visit to the dolphins, beaches and forest on horseback, trip to waterfall by bike, diving with biodiversity, sport fishing and more.
    All activities are optional and not included in the program.

    Overnight: Ponta dos Ganchos, Florianopolis

  • Day 6

    Ponta Dos Ganchos - Governador Celso Ramos

    Day at leisure.

    Overnight: Ponta dos Ganchos, Florianopolis

  • Day 7

    Governador Celso Ramos

    Around noon you will be taken back to Florianopolis airport for your flights back home.

    • END OF ABERCROMBIE & KENT'S ARRANGEMENTS

    WE TRUST YOU ENJOYED YOUR TIME WITH US AND HOPE YOU COME BACK!

    Haere Ra!

    Goodbye!

Honeymoon at Zimbabwe

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 7 days

    Highlights: Experience the Helicopter Ride over the Victoria Falls & enjoy the Game Cruise on the Upper Zambezi. Itinerary offers stay at luxury Hide Safari Camp with Elephant interaction.

    • ITINERARY

  • Day 1

    Arrival at Victoria Falls Airport// Hwange

    On arrival at Victoria Falls Airport off a flight to be advised you will be road transferred to The Hide Safari Camp in Hwange.

    The Hide Safari Camp: -The Hide Safari Camp is situated on the Eastern boundary of the famous Hwange National Park well known world over for its size and its wildlife population of buffalo, zebra, giraffe, rhino and all the big cats and the elephant population hence the game drives are the order of the day.

    Later in the day go for an evening Game Drive & dinner at the Camp.

    Overnight Stay: Hide Safari Camp

  • Day 2

    Enjoy Bush Breakfast, Game Drive & Spend time in the hides close to the watering hole

    You will spend the whole day at The Hide Safari Camp – enjoy bush breakfast, game drives, spend time in the hides close to the watering hole, enjoy lunch in your own private sitting area.

    Enjoy the evening game walk

    Overnight Stay: Doves Nest or Tree House

  • Day 3

    Hwange//Victoria Falls

    After Lunch you depart the Hide by road for Victoria Falls Imbabala Lodge.
    Imbabala River Lodge: - Imbabala Zambezi Safari Lodge is situated on a riverine fringe of the mighty Zambezi River where Zimbabwe, Botswana, Zambia and Namibia converge. Located 80 kilometers west of Victoria Falls

    After Check- in, have tea at the lodge. & later in the evening go on a Game Cruise on the Upper Zambezi with Dinner at the Lodge.

    Overnight Stay: The Imbabala River Lodge

  • Day 4

    Full Day at Hide Safari Camp

    You will spend the whole day at lodge on a Fully Inclusive Basis i.e. accommodation, Meals, Game Drives and Game walks with Dinner at the Lodge

    Overnight Stay:: The Imbabala River Lodge

  • Day 5

    Imbabala Lodge//Elephant Camp

    After Breakfast you depart Imbabala lodge for an hour’s drive to The Elephant Camp. On arrival at the Elephant Camp you will check-in and spend the whole afternoon at leisure

    Later in the day you will go for Elephant Interaction at the and subsequently on a Sunset Cruise on the Mighty Zambezi River wit Dinner at the Boma

    Overnight Stay: The Elephant Camp

  • Day 6

    Experience helicopter flight

    After breakfast, you will go on a helicopter flight over the mighty Victoria Falls

    The Afternoon is open for Optional Activities for Honeymooners
     White water rafting  George swing/Zipline/Fly fox  Bungee jumping from the bridge  Canoe Safari  Walk with the lions  Helicopter flights

    Dinner at the Hotel

    Overnight Stay: The Elephant Camp

  • Day 7

    Depart

    After Breakfast you will check-out at the Hotel and road transferred to Victoria Falls Airport for a flight to be advised.

Luxury Honeymoon in Zimbabwe

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 7 Days

    Highlights: Enjoy the romantic Sunset cruise at the Zambezi River, experience the smoke that thunders at Victoria Falls & relax at the luxury lodges i.e. Victoria Falls, Hide Safari Camp & Changa Safari Camp and experience the best game drives safari experience with bush walk.

    • ITINERARY

  • Day 1

    Arrival at Victoria Falls Airport

    On arrival at The Victoria Falls Airport off a flight to be advised you be road transferred to The Victoria Falls Hotel.

    Later in the afternoon go for a romantic Sunset Cruise at the Mighty Zambezi River & Dinner at the Hotel.

    Overnight Stay: The Victoria Falls Hotel

  • Day 2

    Visit Victoria Falls & Enjoy Optional Activities

    After breakfast, you will go on a guided tour of the falls.
    The Mighty Victoria Falls: -Victoria Falls present a spectacular sight of awe-inspiring beauty and grandeur on the Zambezi River, forming the border between Zambia and Zimbabwe. It was described by the Kololo tribe living in the area in the 1800’s as ‘Mosi-oa-Tunya’ – ‘The Smoke that Thunders’. In more modern terms Victoria Falls is known as the greatest curtain of falling water in the world.

    The Afternoon is open for Optional Activities
     White water rafting  George swing/Zipline/Fly fox  Bungee jumping from the bridge  Canoe Safari  Walk with the lions  Helicopter flights

    Later in the evening dinner at the Hotel

    Overnight Stay: The Victoria Falls Hotel

  • Day 3

    Victoria Falls//Hwange

    After breakfast and check-out at the Hotel, you will be Road Transferred to Hwange where you will stay at The Hide for 2 nights.

    The Hide Safari Camp:- The Hide Safari Camp is situated on the Eastern boundary of the famous Hwange National Park well known world over for its size and its wildlife population of buffalo, zebra, giraffe, rhino, all the big cats and the elephant population hence the game drives are the order of the day.

    Later in the afternoon tea you will go on a Game Drive & Dinner at camp then enjoy a night safari.

    Overnight Stay: The Hide Safari Camp

  • Day 4

    Game driver & Bush walk

    Today you may do a bush walk in the morning along with a bush breakfast

    More than just natural beauty, a whole way of life has been preserved here. The colonists descendants still make their living from the sea – nowadays cultivating oysters, scallops and other shellfish.

    You will spend the whole day at The Hide Safari Camp. Enjoy Game Drives and Game walks, and spend time at the ‘hides’ where you can observe the animals at the watering hole.

    Spend the night at the tree house called Dove’s Nest & later enjoy the game drive with dinner at tree house.

    Overnight Stay: The Doves Nest

  • Day 5

    Hwange//Kariba//Game Drive

    On this day after breakfast you will be Charter Transferred to Kariba where you will stay at The Changa Safari Camp for 2 nights on fully inclusive basis.

    The Changa Safari Camp: -is Located on the shores of Lake Kariba in Zimbabwe and provides the unique experience of combined Game and fishing Safaris.

    After check-in you will have afternoon Tea then go on evening Game Drive & Dinner at Changa Safari Camp

    Overnight Stay: Changa Safari Camp

  • Day 6

    Enjoy Canoe Rides, Game Walk & Drives and Fishing on Lake Kariba

    You will spend the whole day at The Changa Safari Camp, enjoying canoe rides, Game Walks, Game Drives and Fishing on Lake Kariba.

    Overnight Stay: Changa Safari Camp

  • Day 7

    Depart

    After breakfast at a time to be advised you will be charter transferred to Harare International Airport for your outbound flight.
