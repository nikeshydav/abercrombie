ROMANCE IN AUSTRIA & CZECH REPUBLIC

Programme Overview:

Vienna, Salzburg, Prague & Karlovy Vary

  • Duration:11 Days/10 Nights

    Highlights
    Romantic Crystal Dinner
    Individual Viennese Waltz lesson
    Opera show reservations
    Michelin starred restaurant reservation for meals
    Private Picnic lunch in the Vienna woods
    Travel in a Horse drawn carriage ride to the Opera like a true royalty
    Swarovski Crystal Megastore visit
    Luxury Cruise on river Vltava in Prague
    Ride like a Local from Vienna to Prague aboard a high–speed train, a popular mode of travel in Europe

    A&K Advantage
    Augustiner Brau Brewery Private Visit
    Reservations at Staatsoper Opera
    A complimentary rose petal bath and breakfast in bed at Augustine Hotel, Prague
    Special offer for couple massage treatments
    Reservations at Duplex, the most exclusive club in Prague
    Vintage Tram Ride
    Trip to Karlovy Vary for a private romantic spa at the beautiful spa town

    Private arrival and departure airport transfers
    The services of an Abercrombie & Kent Guardian Angel

    * On arrival in each country, you are welcomed by your A&K Guardian Angel who conducts a briefing on your time in the country. A Guardian Angel is always at hand to provide unparalleled 24/7 support, be it local travel tips or reconfirming arrangement details, to providing recommendations for restaurants and shopping. Should you ever need help or advice, dial your Guardian Angel’s mobile phone for prompt and courteous service at any time.

  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE VIENNA–Crystal Dinner

    Upon arrival at Vienna Airport you will be met by your Guardian Angel and transferred to your hotel where you will be assisted with check–in.

    Day is at leisure.

    Reservations will be in place for a Romantic Crystal Dinner in the crystalline luxury cabin. A romantic candlelight dinner for 2 over the rooftops of Vienna is an unparalleled experience. Enjoy a select three–course romantic dinner in the crystalline Wagon of the Vienna Giant Wheel. The light reflections at dusk immerse the wagon in a magical light. The striking crystalline decoration by Swarovski Vienna creates a luxurious, sparkling ambience.

    Overnight: Ritz Carlton
    Meal Plan: D

  • Day 2

    VIENNA–Visit Schönbrunn Palace

    Meet your English–speaking local guide today and travel outside the city center by private vehicle for a half–day excursion (up to 4 hours) to the magnificent Schönbrunn Palace with its gorgeous grounds. Schönbrunn Palace was the enormous summer residence of the Habsburg’s (Habsburg family ruled the Austro–Hungarian Empire until 1918) and takes its name from a beautiful spring found on the estate. The splendor of the strict architectural symmetry is further enriched by its gardens with fountains, trees and alleyways. Your guide will escort you through many of the palace staterooms including the 'Hall of Mirrors' in which Mozart performed for the Empress Maria Theresa when he was only six years old, and the gothic St Stephen’s Cathedral, whose soaring structure enshrines the heart of Vienna. You may also be able to see a training session of the famous Spanish Riding School (timetable and ticket availability permitting).

    Return to the hotel for a relaxing spa session or walk along the famous 'Ringstrasse' with its 19th century buildings or venture out on the streets of Vienna’s famous Kärtnerstrasse on a shopping spree.

    Overnight: Ritz Carlton
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    VIENNA–Private picnic in the woods & Viennese Waltz lesson

    Leave the city of Vienna behind this morning with your English–speaking chauffeur–guide and private vehicle for a sightseeing excursion to the charming Vienna Woods. We will pack you a scrumptious picnic basket with a bottle of wine to enjoy a private lunch in the woods with your loved one.

    On the return trip to Vienna stop at the Seegrotte, a former mine and now a mesmerizing underground lake, where you can take a motor boat trip for the finest view of the reflections before returning back to your hotel.

    Vienna is the world’s capital of music and one of its most sought–after “export goods” is the Viennese Waltz, the dance in three quarter time that waltz king Johann Strauss victoriously took across the globe. This evening you will have the chance to take a private Viennese Waltz lesson in an exclusive baroque ballroom. After your introductory lesson you will have a traditional Viennese “pause” with coffee or sparkling wine and cake. At the end of the lesson you will receive a certificate authenticating your efforts.

    Overnight: Ritz Carlton
    Meal Plan: B, L

  • Day 4

    VIENNA–Trip to Swarovski Megastore, Opera and Dinner at Steirereck

    Enjoy a special crystalline experience at the enormous Swarovski Megastore, captivating with an impressive facade and a fascinating interior in the heart of Vienna. Enjoy several stories of shimmering wonders from the world of crystals and experience the inspiring interplay of design, culture and visions of crystals, all merging into a extravagant shoppers' landscape. While there, you may visit their Moët & Chandon Bar on the first floor and enjoy a prickling glass of champagne.

    Treat yourselves to sweet treats at the Altmann & Kühne, producers of handmade bonbons for more than 100 years with their undisclosed recipe. You may buy fresh, handmade chocolates, marzipan and nougat bonbons.

    Nothing is quite as exquisite in Vienna as a night at the Staatsoper. Constructed in 1869, this extravagant auditorium is the scene for world–class opera and ballet and the celebrated Vienna Philharmonic. The reservations will be made and a Horse drawn Carriage will transport you like royalty for the night to follow.

    Dinner reservations at the Steirereck, Vienna’s only double–Michelin–starred restaurant, an intimate and appealing spot within the Stadtpark, serves classic Viennese dishes alongside modern Austrian and international cuisine, complemented by wines from an exceptional cellar of 35,000 bottles.

    Overnight: Ritz Carlton
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 5

    VIENNA–DANUBE VALLEY–SALZBURG–Sightseeing

    This morning you will transfer with your chauffeur–guide and private vehicle towards Salzburg along the beautiful Danube Valley. En route sightseeing will include the town of Krems where you will visit a 14th century former cloister, then on to Durnstein which is arguably the most beautiful of the region’s villages and finally to Melk where you will see the magnificent abbey.

    The Goldener Hirsch is a personally run hotel, rich in tradition. The hotel consists of four townhouses, one of which is across the street from the others. Guestrooms are tastefully furnished with antiques and enjoy all modern services. Other facilities at the hotel include a gourmet restaurant, a traditional tavern and a bar.

    Time at leisure.

    Overnight: Hotel Goldener Hirsch
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 6

    SALZBURG–SALZKAMMERGUT–SALZBURG–Walking tour, sightseeing of the lakes and evening at Club Five

    This morning you will enjoy a walking tour of the city. Begin at your hotel with an English–speaking local guide who will show you the important sights in this “Mozart City” such as the Getreidegasse with Mozart’s birthplace, the “Pferdeschwemme” (Royal Horse Trough) and St Peter’s Abbey. You will also visit the Mirabell Palace and Gardens, renowned for being part of the background to “The Sound of Music”.

    After lunch enjoy sightseeing with your chauffeur–guide and private vehicle to the Salzkammergut Lake District, located outside Salzburg where you can romance around the beautiful lakes. Begin in Fuschl on Lake Fuschl, which is known for its pure blue deep waters. Then travel on to St. Wolfgang, which is set against a dramatic backdrop of mountains and home to the White Horse Inn where you may wish to savor a coffee and apple strudel with cream on the terrace. You may also enjoy a boat ride on picturesque Lake Wolfgang. The last would be the town of Bad Ischl, best known for its fine tortes (pastries).

    An evening at the Club Take Five is scheduled for you. Club Take Five just newly opened in Old Town Salzburg but it is becoming a trendy evening all around entertainment spot in town. Sultry, sexy, sleek, and stylish are just a few words that can describe its entirety.

    Overnight: Hotel Goldener Hirsch
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 7

    SALZBURG–PRAGUE–Sightseeing

    You will be privately transferred to the station and your local representative will show you to your pre–reserved seating in the First Class carriage of the train bound for Prague.

    Upon arrival into Prague you will be met from the train door by your Guardian Angel and privately transferred to your hotel where you will be assisted with check–in.

    Explore this delightful city in the afternoon with a visit to Prague's Castle District with your English–speaking local guide. You will travel by private vintage tram from a tram stop near your hotel to a stop near Prague Castle. Hradcany Castle was the seat of Bohemian kings and Holy Roman emperors. Stroll along the Golden Lane, a row of tiny houses dating from the 16th century.

    Time at leisure.

    In the evening enjoy a quiet drink in the hotel's intimate St Thomas brewery bar, housed in the cellar or you may enjoy your dinner at Kampa Park, Prague's premium fine dining establishment. A favorite amid local and celebrities, this upmarket restaurant delivers lavish gourmet paired with over 150 types of wines. Diners can select between sophisticated dining rooms or the intimate riverside terrace overlooking Charles Bridge or Certovka stream.

    Overnight: Augustine Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 8

    PRAGUE–Sightseeing and hot air ballooning

    Meet your English–speaking local guide and enjoy a half–day walking tour (4 hours) of the Old Town with its winding cobbled streets and fairy–tale charm and the fascinating Jewish Quarter, birthplace of Franz Kafka. See the striking Gothic Town Hall in Old Town Square, famous for its unique astronomical clock, and visit the Jewish Museum which houses the largest collection of Judaica in Europe, the Old–New Synagogue and the very moving Jewish Cemetery. You may also enjoy a walk across the romantically picturesque 14th century Charles Bridge.

    This afternoon post lunch you will be transported for a hot air balloon flight. A thrilling, unforgettable and romantic experience post which you will celebrate with a bottle of Champagne and refreshments.

    Overnight: Augustine Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 9

    PRAGUE–KARLOVY VARY–PRAGUE–Exotic Spa treatment and night at Duplex club

    A day trip to the Spa town planned especially for you. You will be transported by a private vehicle to Karlovy Vary which is a small & beautiful spa town located approx. at a distance of 2 hours from Prague.

    The spa began in the 14th century, seeing its first major influx of international guests in the 17th century. Spa building grounds include geysers as well as thermal springs which are natural and not man made, which is a unique point for the Karlovy Vary and reason it’s so famous for natural spa therapies.

    A reservation for a private couple therapy and an hour in the Relaxation pool exclusively for you will be made. Unwind and relax in each other’s company along with a bottle of Champagne and strawberries.

    Evening you will be transferred back to the hotel.

    Reservations at Duplex for the night will be in order. Duplex is the élite club in Prague, with outstanding views to match its status. Perched on the rooftop of one of the tallest buildings, the Duplex club is designed to be an enormous glass cube. The multi–level space is contemporary and classy, with glass walls and mood lighting. The terrace balcony provides memorable views of the city. Dancers cheer up the atmosphere and Duplex’s fine resident DJ’s vie with international names to entertain the crowd. It is known to be the best music venue in the city that attracts the most elite gentry.

    Overnight: Augustine Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 10

    PRAGUE–Personalised shopping & Luxury cruise on Vltava

    Enjoy a complimentary rose petal bath & breakfast in bed before meeting your Guardian Angel who will accompany you to Wenceslas Square for shopping.

    Tonight you are booked on a luxury cruise with an Á la carte menu. You can look forward to a delightful experience on board a luxury boat, during which you will enjoy live piano composition and excellent culinary delights. Add to it exceptionally memorable views of shimmering river Vltava and charismatic architectural monuments of the capital city to complete your perfect night out.

    Overnight: Augustine Hotel
    Meal Plan: B, D

  • Day 11

    DEPART PRAGUE

    This morning you will be privately transferred to Prague airport for your departure flight.

    Meal Plan: B

ROMANTIC HONEYMOON IN TUSCANY

Programme Overview

Florence, Chianti, Siena and Montepulciano

  • Duration: 7 Days/6 Nights

    Highlights
    Vineyard visit in Classico Chianti Region
    Hot air ballooning
    Wine tasting and Michelin starred lunch reservations
    Dinner at Michelin Starred restaurant

    A&K Advantage
    Florence – Oltrarno – South of the River Artisans & Chocolate!
    A Half day walking tour of Florence with a “Guardian Angel*”

    Private arrival and departure airport transfers
    The services of an Abercrombie & Kent Guardian Angel

    * On arrival in each country, you are welcomed by your A&K Guardian Angel who conducts a briefing on your time in the country. A Guardian Angel is always at hand to provide unparalleled 24/7 support, be it local travel tips or reconfirming arrangement details, to providing recommendations for restaurants and shopping. Should you ever need help or advice, dial your Guardian Angel’s mobile phone for prompt and courteous service at any time.
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE TUSCANY–At leisure, massage and a 4 course candlelit dinner

    Your private chauffeur will drive you from Florence Airport to your hotel in the heart of the stunning Tuscan wine producing region of Chianti in a private vehicle.

    The ideal choice for both relaxation and exploration, the luxury sanctuary Castello Del Nero is located within striking distance of Florence and Siena. The carefully restored 12th century castle offers the absolute latest in modern facilities whilst maintaining the atmosphere of its historic surroundings.

    Settle in & indulge in a Castello Del Nero Relaxation Ritual; a Balinese massage to help you unwind from the arrival journey.

    Time at leisure to soak in the surroundings; followed by a 4–course candlelit dinner with tasting menu “The Tuscan Tradition” & selected beverages.

    Overnight: Castello Del Nero
    Meal plan: B

  • Day 2

    TUSCANY–Wine & Dine Experience

    Wake up at leisure and get ready to enjoy your day in the amazing Tuscan countryside!

    Your chauffeur will bring you into the heart of Chianti and perhaps one of the most beautiful wine regions in the world, Chianti Classico Wine Region is an area that has miles and miles of hill topped medieval villages and valleys covered in vines, all between the two beautiful cities of Florence and Siena.

    You will begin in the vineyards and receive an explanation of the territory – the combination of local climate, soils and grape varietals that are the components that make wines so unique from region to region.

    Enjoy an exclusive tour of the cellars; which could be followed by a Michelin starred lunchtime meal with accompanying fine delicious wines from the cellar. The sommelier will be happy to share his in–depth knowledge with you. Fully satiated, your chauffeur will bring you back to the paradise of your hotel.

    Post lunch; enjoy the drive through the countryside. You could return to your hotel & perhaps stroll into the archetypal Tuscan village of Tavarnelle, just a short walk away.

    Overnight: Castello Del Nero
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    TUSCANY–Hot Air Ballooning & Countryside

    This morning by 5am you will be picked up and transferred to the launch site by your private vehicle between Florence and Siena.

    You will soar over the renowned Chianti landscape with its hilltop towns, castles, valleys, famous vineyards, and mixed woodlands. On clear days at a distance you can view Florence to the North and the Apennine mountain range in the background; to the West you will see the Elsa valley, San Gimignano, Certaldo, and Volterra and the sea in the far distance; to the South, Poggibonsi, Colle Val d'Elsa and Siena and Monte Amiata in the distance; to the East the higher Monti del Chianti and mixed oak woodlands.

    The flight is scheduled early morning just after dawn. Inflation takes about 30 minutes, and the flight approximately 1 hour depending on wind and weather conditions. After landing, the balloon will be loaded back onto the retrieving vehicle trailer that follows the balloon throughout the flight. Post the flight you enjoy a marvelous champagne breakfast with local produce.

    Although flights are organised all year round, the "ballooning season" is approximately late spring to early autumn with annual variations. Weather in Chianti can be tricky and unpredictable, as all flights are subject to wind and weather, obviously these winter flights are not as regular as during the ballooning season.

    After breakfast, you could wander throughout the countryside of Chianti and stop by Siena and Montepulciano.

    Siena is possibly Italy's prettiest medieval city and a trip to Siena cannot be missed when in Tuscany. Siena's center of attraction is its central piazza known as Il Campo, famous worldwide for its Palio run here; a horse race run around the piazza twice every summer.

    Enjoy the city & perhaps have lunch at another Michelin Star restaurant.

    Post lunch, drive to the hill town of Montepulciano. Of all the region's small historic hill towns, Montepulciano has to be one of the most compelling. Standing imperious on its high tufa ridge, Montepulciano appears to have been artistically planned by a designer of Renaissance opera sets. Architecturally it is the most integral and unified historic centers of any Italian town: apart from a few restorations, no sizable construction work has taken place since 1580. You will find some good restaurants serving authentic hearty southern Tuscan fare, couple rustically elegant hotels, and the region which develops Tuscany's most serious, full–bodied red wines. But the town also has a certain discretion about it, a private side to its character that is very fascinating.

    You could end the day with a lovely sundowner at a renowned restaurant overlooking the vineyards & soak in the amazing views of the Tuscan countryside at sunset.

    Return to Castello Del Nero for the evening.

    Overnight: Castello Del Nero
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    FLORENCE–Walking tour of the Oltrarno & Private tasting of artisan chocolates

    Morning is at leisure.

    This afternoon meet your Guardian Angel for a half day walking tour (4 hours) of the Oltrarno – the delightful South side of the River Arno, where the medieval streets are lined with artisan workshops. From your hotel, cross over the Ponte Vecchio to the south side of the river admiring the gold and jewelry shops that line this famous bridge. Once in the Oltrano your English Speaking guide will lead you to some of the lovely workshops and boutiques where you can admire hand–made pieces such as the bold and bright costume jewelry designs* of Angela Caputi.
    *Subject to availability.

    There are still a few chocolatiers in Florence who are committed to the tradition of artisan chocolate making. Their dedication to quality comes through in the flavor of their chocolate creations. Visit the best chocolatiers of Florence* (up to 1 hour) for a private tasting of artisan delights!

    Spend some time at leisure followed by dinner & return to the hotel for the night.

    Overnight: Castello Del Nero
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 5

    TUSCANY–Dinner at a Michelin starred restaurant

    Day is at leisure to explore the hotel facilities and local surroundings.

    This afternoon, you could either enjoy a self–drive through the area or plan to spend the day by one of the spectacular beach sides. Maybe do a private boat tour of Tuscany´s islands and the Monte Argentario peninsula.

    This evening, you could enjoy dinner at Trattoria Toscana, the only Alain Ducasse restaurant in Italy with a Michelin star. The interiors of the restaurant have a sense of discreet elegance and intimacy, with wooden beams, exposed brickwork, a fireplace and cotto tile flooring. It leads onto an enchanting terrace that faces the kitchen gardens and surrounding hills, an ideal setting for a private romantic dinner.

    Overnight: Castello Del Nero
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 6

    TUSCANY–Spa & Thermal treatments

    Day is at leisure.

    You could spend a day with your partner in the natural hot springs. Tuscany has great geothermal activity underneath its surface and hot springs are the most enjoyable result of this phenomenon.

    You can spot several SPA and thermal treatment outlets throughout Tuscany. You may try the natural–hot water pools and waterfalls such the ones in Saturnia, Bagno Vignoni, Petriolo or San Filippo. During winter it’s weird to bathe in 40°C waters when outside its cold, but it’s undoubtedly an extremely pleasant experience, whichever season you’ll choose. Invite your partner to bathe with you in one of the small pools and surprise him/her lighting up some candles around during the night hours: you’ll always remember those magical moments!

    Overnight: Castello Del Nero
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 7

    DEPART TUSCANY

    Your chauffeur will bring you back in your private vehicle to Florence Airport today.

    Meal Plan: B

  • Possible Extensions:

    Option 1:

    3 Days VENICE

    Day 1

    VENICE–Cruise to a Torcello for a romantic dinner

    After breakfast, enjoy your drive to Venice.

    On arrival, you are met by a Guardian Angel and transferred to your hotel, where you will be assisted with check–in. Speed across the lagoon to your palace hotel, conveniently located on the city's Grand Canal. A 14th–century Venetian palace adorned with masterpieces by the likes of Tiepolo – by private jetty.

    This evening, cruise on Venice’s famous canals as you pass the Santa Maraidella Salute Church and into the lagoon to toast the sunset and your romantic holiday with a glass of Veneto Prosecco. Reach the beautiful island of Torcello, where a table is set for you in the enchanting gardens at the Locanda Cipriani. Savor one of their specialties: Adriatic fresh oysters.

    Overnight: Ca' Sagredo
    Meal Plan: B, D

  • Day 2

    VENICE–Explore La Serenissima & a Gondola ride

    End your romantic adventure in one of the world's gorgeous cities & explore the less–visited side of "La Serenissima" (a fitting nickname for this most serene of Italian cities) alongside your Guardian Angel. Lose yourselves in the charming labyrinth of narrow streets and passageways in the old artisans' quarter, where postcard–perfect scenes await around every corner. Wander through the Frari Church, which houses the breathtaking masterpieces of Titian, Bellini and Canova.

    This evening, your private gondolier rows and serenades you through the Grand Canal and under the Rialto Bridge on to a restaurant of your choice.

    Overnight: Ca' Sagredo
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    DEPART VENICE

    Your chauffeur will transfer you in a private vehicle to the airport in Venice.

    Meal Plan: B

  • Option 2:

    3 Days LAKE COMO

    Day 1

    LAKE COMO–Self exploration or enjoy a picnic

    After breakfast, board a flight to Milan.

    Arrive in Milan and then privately transfer to your luxury hotel on the banks of stunning Lake Como. Take a refreshing swim in the pool or a relaxing stroll in the nearby park. Alternately you could do a self–drive with a picnic hamper & spend the day by the stunning location.

    Overnight: Villa d'Este Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 2

    LAKE COMO–Sightseeing on a private boat cruise

    Post breakfast; meet your local guide for a half–day private boat cruise on Lake Como, Europe’s deepest lake. Stop in the delightful village of Bellagio, an old retreat for the nobility of Lombardy, and visit the elegant Villa Carlotta at Tremezzo with its beautiful gardens. The gardens of Villa Carlotta are worthy a visit any time of the year. They feature old varieties of camellias, century–old cedars and sequoias, the rock garden and the ferns valley, the rhododendrons wood and the bamboos garden. All offer wonderful views of the lake.

    You may enjoy your lunch at Silvio in Bellagio, where reservations can be arranged. A rustic restaurant on the little harbour inlet of Loppia just beyond the Villa Melzi. Restaurant owner, Cristian Ponzini is one of the few privileged to hold fishing rights on the lake—a privilege that has been passed down through his family for generations. He sails out each morning to catch lavarello and other native fish to serve at his glass–walled restaurant.

    Lake Como is home to some of the very finest Italian cuisine with a number of world–class restaurants. Explore your options and enjoy your dinner. We recommend Villa Crespi and Piccolo Lago, both holding two Michelin stars each.

    Overnight: Villa d'Este Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    DEPART COMO

    After breakfast, your chauffer will drive you in a private vehicle to Milan where you either board your return flight; or spend a couple of nights in this fashionable city!

    Meal Plan: B

HONEYMOON IN FINLAND

Programme Overview

Helsinki, Kittilä Levi and Rovaniemi

  • Duration: 11 Days/10 Nights.

    Highlights
    Dog sledding
    Visit a Reindeer Farm
    Visit an exclusive handicraft workshop where you can make your own souvenir guided by a local family
    Visit Santa Claus Village with an opportunity to take photos with Santa Claus
    A Day trip on an Arctic Ice Breaker
    Porvoo city tour
    Visit the Suomenlinna fortress

    A&K Advantage
    Overnight in a luxury Glass Igloo for your unforgettable experience
    Private cruise from the Port of Ajos in Kemi

    Private arrival and departure airport transfers
    The services of an Abercrombie & Kent Guardian Angel

    * On arrival in each country, you are welcomed by your A&K Guardian Angel who conducts a briefing on your time in the country. A Guardian Angel is always at hand to provide unparalleled 24/7 support, be it local travel tips or reconfirming arrangement details, to providing recommendations for restaurants and shopping. Should you ever need help or advice, dial your Guardian Angel’s mobile phone for prompt and courteous service at any time.
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE HELSINKI–At leisure & dinner at Nokka

    Upon arrival into Helsinki Airport, you will be welcomed by your Guardian Angel, who will look after you throughout your time in Finland & assist you with your check in at the hotel.

    Time is at leisure to unwind from the flight.

    Enjoy a 3–course dinner at restaurant Nokka. At Nokka, the story is influenced by Chef Ari Ruoho and his unadulterated ingredients & this promises to be a great start to your Finnish honeymoon!

    Overnight: Hotel Haven
    Meal Plan: D

  • Day 2

    HELSINKI–KITTILÄ LEVI–Northern Lights view

    This morning, your private guide will meet you for a private tour of the Helsinki city. You will visit Senate Square and the White Cathedral. Continue to Market’s Place, the coastal streets of Helsinki. Photo stops at Sibelius Monument, created by Eila Hiltunen in honour of composer, Jean Sibelius. Visit the Temppeliaukion Church, which is built out of solid rock.

    Afternoon, take a private charter flight (Approx.1h15min) to Kittilä. On arrival you will be welcomed by your Guardian Angel & be transferred to your hotel by a private vehicle for an unforgettable experience.

    You are booked to stay in a luxury Glass Igloo. Spend a memorable night under the stars & try your luck to catch the world’s most amazing natural phenomenon: Aurora Borealis! As per record, Northern Lights occur about 200 nights on an average in Kittilä region every year, thereby significantly increasing your prospect to view this spectacular view.

    Overnight: Golden Crown Levi Igloos
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    KITTILÄ LEVI–Dog sledding and Reindeer safari

    After a delightful breakfast, it is time to fire up the engines. You will be taken for dog sledding. In order to meet your team of dogs who will take you on the sledding trip, you will visit the kennels. All equipment will be provided and you will be taught how to maneuver your team, after which you will be taken out in to the wilderness. On starting the dogs make a lot of noise, but soon enough they calm down and then the only sound is from the feet of the dogs hitting the snow.

    Afternoon you are scheduled for a visit to the Reindeer Farm. The reindeer herders dressed in traditional Lappish costumes will explain you about reindeers and their everyday routine. Enjoy the Reindeer Sledge safari in wilderness.

    Dinner at Ice restaurant and overnight stay at Ice Hotel.

    Overnight: Ice Hotel
    Meal Plan: B, D

  • Day 4

    LAPLAND–LEVI / ROVANIEMI–Exclusive handicraft workshop and Aurora hunting safari on Snowmobile

    This morning, visit an exclusive handicraft workshop where you can produce your own souvenir with guidance of a local family as a memorabilia.

    Evening head out for Aurora Hunting Safari by Snowmobile.*

    Experience the Arctic night by driving through the snow–covered forest directly and onto the frozen river. By the grace of lady luck, you may be able to see the bright sky, moon and stars, even the Aurora Borealis – Northern Lights – guiding you all the way.

    Overnight in Lapland – Rovaniemi

    **The Northern Lights are a natural phenomenon, therefore sighting cannot be guaranteed and subject to suitable weather conditions.

    ROVANIEMI–Santa Claus village visit

    Morning you will be taken for a visit to the Santa Claus Village where you will have an opportunity to post a postcard from the main post office. All postcards mailed from here will get a special Arctic Circle Stamp on them.

    At the end of your morning tour, collect the original Arctic Circle Certificate with your name and the special Arctic Circle stamp with date to substantiate that you have crossed the Arctic Circle.

    You may also get an exclusive opportunity to take pictures with Santa Claus.

    Return to the hotel for the night.

    Overnight: Santa Claus Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 6

    ROVANIEMI–Ranua wildlife park visit

    Visit the distinctive Ranua Wildlife Park & get the chance to come in close contact with the Nordic and Arctic animals. If you are lucky, you might get to feed animals by yourself, instructed and monitored by the animal keepers.

    Time at leisure

    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 7

    ROVANIEMI–KEMI–ROVANIEMI–Trip to ice breaker & private cruise

    Enjoy a day trip on an Ice Breaker.

    Take a 4–hour private cruise from the Port of Ajos in Kemi. During the cruise you will have a guided tour on board the icebreaker. Visit its facilities from the engine room all the way up to the captain’s bridge. You will be served a delicious soup–lunch in the estaurant on board the ship during your journey. After lunch you’ll have the chance for an exciting experience of swimming in freezing cold waters, dressed in warm impermeable survival suits, it is one of the most memorable experience.

    On arrival at the port, the captain will honor both of you with a Sampo Ice breaker Certificate.

    Meal Plan: B, L

    ROVANIEMI–HELSINKI–Dinner at Savoy

    Transfer to airport for a short domestic private charter flight to the capital of Finland – Helsinki.

    You would be welcomed on arrival & transferred to your hotel.

    Time at leisure to unwind

    Evening, enjoy a 3–course dinner at restaurant Savoy, whose dining hall is an essential part of Finnish design history. Savoy has been the flagship of Finnish gastronomy and restaurant culture for decades. Their kitchen, well–known for its classics, is also a trailblazer in the modern–day gastronomy with prominence on healthy eating.

    Their head chef is dedicated to provide the best menu using the best produce and ingredients. These are sourced from the very best sellers with no compromise to ensure that the right ingredients for a great menu are available all–year long.

    Overnight: Hotel Haven
    Meal Plan: B, D

  • Day 9

    HELSINKI–PORVOO–HELSINKI–City tour & Dinner at Savotta

    This morning, proceed for a Porvoo city tour. Porvoo is most famous for its charismatic cathedral, and for the picturesque Old Town. Sloping cobbled streets and lovely town square dominate the Old Town Hall. The square and surrounding streets are packed with attractive speciality stores selling handicrafts and works by local artisans. Porvoo's stone streets and painted wooden houses are wonderfully anachronistic and rustic; the town is even reachable by 1950s–style train from Helsinki.

    Time at leisure

    Dinner organised at an authentic Finnish restaurant, Savotta. The Restaurant Savotta transports its guests directly from Senate Square to a nostalgic view of Finland from past decades. Both the authentic interior and the dishes, served in original style, have been inspired by the Finnish forests and thousands of lakes, with a dash of Finnish “sisu” and logging traditions.

    The old china designed by the world–renowned Arabia, the hundred–year–old floorboards, the old chairs and tables and many old artifacts have been brought from all over Finland. Each of them tells its own story from past decades and takes you back in time to the logging camps of the old days.

    Overnight: Hotel Haven
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 10

    HELSINKI–Visit Suomenlinna fortress

    Today, visit the island fortress, Suomenlinna fortress, which was built in 1748. Suomenlinna is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Built on a cluster of rocky skerries, the fortress comprises of an irregular network of bastions. During the Russian era, the fortress was extended both towards the sea and on the mainland. Suomenlinna has played a key role in many turning points in Finland’s history. The fortress was handed over to civilian administration by the military in 1973 with the exception of Pikku Mustasaari Island, where the Naval Academy is still located.

    A visit to this fortress will leave you truly spell bound.

    Evening at leisure & overnight at the hotel

    Overnight: Hotel Haven
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 11

    DEPART HELSINKI

    Your private chauffer will meet you at your hotel & transfer you to the Helsinki Airport for your onward connections.

    Meal Plan: B
