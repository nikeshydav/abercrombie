Connections: Heart Of Europe: Cruising The Rhine, Main & Danube

Programme Overview

Embark on a 17-day odyssey through Holland, Germany, Austria, Slovakia and Hungary. Enjoy a concert in Vienna, and enjoy A&K-exclusive visits to Schloss Mespelbrunn and a World War II art bunker.

  • Duration: 17 days/ 16 nights

    Highlights: Amsterdam | Volendam | Edam | Cologne | Koblenz | Rüdesheim | Miltenberg | Wertheim | Würzburg | Bamberg | Nuremberg | Kelheim | Regensburg | Passau | Salzburg | Linz | Melk | Vienna | Bratislava | Budapest

    A&K Advantages

    • Cruise for 14 nights on the Rhine, Main and Danube Rivers, and enjoy one night each in Amsterdam and Budapest in centrally located hotels
    • Savor Edam cheese in the city where it originated
    • Marvel at Cologne's magnificent Gothic cathedral
    • See legendary Lorelei Rock along the scenic Rhine Gorge
    • Embark on an A&K-exclusive excursion to Schloss Mespelbrunn
    • Tour a World War II art bunker in Nuremberg, only with A&K
    • Enjoy a night-time cruise along the Danube in Budapest
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    Arrive Amsterdam

    Upon arrival in Amsterdam you will be met by an A&K representative, who will assist with transfer to the hotel

    This evening, join your fellow guests and A&K group leader for a welcome dinner.

    Sofitel Legend The Grand Amsterdam/Dinner

  • Day 2

    Volendam & Edam

    Start your morning with a scenic drive to Volendam, a beautiful fishing town beside the Zuiderzee.Visit the its stunning waterfront and traditional folklore before you travel to Edam for a cheese tasting session. Later, return to Amsterdam for a private boarding on the 'Amadeus Silver’, where you will be served with a refreshing cocktail prior to dinner.

    Amadeus Silver/Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner

  • Day 3

    Amsterdam

    Today, visit Amsterdam’s Royal Palace and one of the city's main churches ,Nieuwe Kerk (New Church). Continue to explore the city with a boat cruise through the city's historic district, the flat polder landscape of southern Holland before you enjoy your delicious lunch aboard.

    This evening, join your fellow guests for a specially organized pre-dinner cocktail party in the ship's Amadeus Club.

    Silver/Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner

  • Day 4

    Cologne

    Arrive in Cologne and explore the culture of the place through a walking tour of the old city. Go ahead for a dazzling architectural journey inside the 13th century Gothic cathedral, with its intricately designed architectural features.

    Amadeus Silver/Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner

  • Day 5

    Koblenz & Rüdesheim

    Reach Koblenz this morning and choose either from an optional walking tour or explore independently.

    This afternoon, enjoy a relaxing cruise on a picturesque stretch of the Rhine, viewing the hilltop castles, terraced vineyards and charming villages on your way. Conclude your day at Rüdesheim to visit Siegfried's Mechanical Music Museum.

    Amadeus Silver/Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner

  • Day 6

    Miltenberg & Wertheim

    Today, depart for the historic city of Miltenberg, famous for its timber-frame houses.

    You may explore the city with an optional walking tour along narrow cobbled streets with gabled, half-timbered houses. Continue to visit the charming Wertheim.

    Later, board to cruise through the Seine River followed by dinner at a local restaurant.

    Amadeus Silver/Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner

  • Day 7

    Würzburg & Heidelberg

    This morning avail the privileged opportunity as an A&K guest to visit Heidelberg, a lively university town that sits beside an old turreted bridge at the foot of a magnificent castle.

    Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant before you head towards private wine tasting at a family-owned estate.

    Amadeus Silver/Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner

  • Day 8

    Bamberg

    After your arrival in Bamberg explore the city with an optional walking tour of the old quarter, with its intricately designed Gothic cathedral and intriguing town hall (Altes Rathaus), built on a bridge above the River Regnitz. Later this evening, cruise on the Iconic Main-Danube Canal Amadeus, while you learn more about its significance abroad.

    Silver/Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner

  • Day 9

    Nuremberg

    Arrive in Nuremberg today and explore the place with a walk through the altstadt (old town) .Take sight of the 900-year-old ramparts and castle. En-route visit the Palace of Justice, site of the War Crimes Tribunal of 1946.

    Continue the journey for a private tour, A&K exclusive tour of a World War II art bunker. Later visit fascinating Documentation Centre Museum.

    Amadeus Silver/Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner

  • Day 10

    Kelheim & Regensburg

    After your arrival in Kelheim today, you may opt for an excursion to the world's oldest monastery brewery in Weltenburg Abbey to sample the famed rich dark beer. Further, travel to Regensburg, where you may either choose an optional walking tour of this preserved medieval city or explore it at your own convenience.

    This evening, enjoy a special performance of Bavarian musical entertainment aboard ship.

    Amadeus Silver/Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner

  • Day 11

    Passau, Salzburg & Linz

    Today there is a choice of tours: enjoy walking tours in picturesque town of Passau and Linz or a full-day excursion to Salzburg that includes the beautiful Salzkammergut region.

    Amadeus Silver/Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner

  • Day 12

    Melk & Vienna

    Upon your arrival in Melk/Emmersdorf, you may perhaps choose for an optional excursion into the Wachau District for a tour of the 900-year-old Benedictine abbey and a wine tasting.

    This afternoon, enjoy a relaxing lunch followed by a cruise to Vienna. Continue for an optional evening drive to the mountain of Kahlenberg and marvel the stunning views of Vienna at night.

    Amadeus Silver/Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner

  • Day 13

    Vienna

    Begin your morning with a drive through Ringstrasse, passing Karlskirche and the charming Opera House. Enjoy the journey with the views of the Hundertwasser apartment block and St. Stephan's Cathedral en-route. Further, you may opt for a tour of Schönbrunn Palace, once residence of Habsburg monarchs. Later, attend "Sound of Vienna" concert with priority category A seating in the splendid Kursalon, where Johann Strauss the younger directed his orchestra.

    Amadeus Silver/Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner

  • Day 14

    Bratislava, Slovakia

    This morning get a welcomed arrival in Slovakia's capital, Bratislava. Begin to visit its old quarter,St. Martin's Cathedral and Grassalkovich Palace.

    Tonight, join the captain and your fellow guests a gala dinner.

    Amadeus Silver/Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner

  • Day 15

    Budapest

    Arrive in Budapest, one of Central Europe's most popular tourist attractions. Experience the place and its culture with a walk through the cobbled streets of Buda and the wide avenues of Pest. Later, you may choose an optional excursion to Szentendre, a lively artists' town with a Mediterranean flair.

    Board on to cruise through Budapest, beneath the illuminated Chain Bridge for floodlit city views along the Danube, and enjoy a Hungarian folklore program on board.

    Amadeus Silver/Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner

  • Day 16

    Budapest

    Disembark for a drive to Lake Balaton. Here, you visit Tihany, a quaint town with narrow, cobblestone streets. The beautiful drive will take your through stunning panaromic views of the surrounding countryside of Mount Badacsony. Upon your return travel to Budapest, visit the Herend Porcelain Factory en route for a demonstration on the manufacture of their hand-painted, gilded creations.

    Corinthia Hotel Budapest /Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner

  • Day 17

    Depart Budapest

    After breakfast. You will be given an assisted transfer to the airport for your onward flight.

    Breakfast

  • Pricing & group size

    As a guide this 17 day journey through Amsterdam, Germany, Austria and Hungary will cost from £5,209 per person based on two adults sharing excluding flights. The maximum group size for this itinerary will be 24.

    DATES & PRICES FROM

Cruising Holland & Belgium in Bloom

Programme Overview

Glide through landscapes of spring blossoms and windmills, view millions of blooms at Keukenhof Gardens, visit the Royal Greenhouses in Laeken and exclusively tour the Diamond Pavilion in Antwerp.

  • Duration: 9 days/ 8 nights

    Highlights: Amsterdam | Arnhem | Middelberg | Ghent | Bruges | Brussels | Antwerp | Holland

    A&K Advantages

    • Cruise for seven nights on 'Amadeus Brilliant' and enjoy a night in Amsterdam at the centrally located Sofitel Legend the Grand
    • Visit the Black Tulip Museum and lovely Delft, famous for traditional blue and white porcelain
    • Tour Amsterdam's historic canal district, a UNESCO World Heritage Site
    • Take an exclusive excursion In Belgium to visit the Palace of Laeken and the Antwerp Diamond Pavilion.
    • Experience Bruges, a medieval jewel suspended in time
    • Take in the beauty of millions of blooming flowers at spectacular Keukenhof Gardens
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    Arrive Amsterdam

    On your arrival Arrival in Amsterdam you will be met by an A&K Representative who will conduct a briefing on your time in the country and assist your transfer to the hotel.

    This evening, enjoy a welcome dinner with your fellow guests.

    Overnight: Sofitel Legend the Grand Amsterdam
    Meal plan: D

  • Day 2

    Amsterdam

    Begin your journey in the beautiful city of Amsterdam with a visit to the Museum of the Black Tulip in Lisse, an old town surrounded by vast fields of iridescent blooms. Continue your travel to Delft, a town graced with small canals and humpbacked bridges, and famous for its traditional blue and white porcelain. Once you return back to Amsterdam make way for an A&K-exclusive priority embarkation aboard your spacious, stylish riverboat. Enjoy a welcome cocktail prior to dinner on board with your fellow travelers onboard.

    Overnight: 'Amadeus Brilliant'
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 3

    Amsterdam

    Explore Amsterdam, viewing the Royal Palace and Nieuwe Kerk (New Church), followed by a scenic canal boat cruise through the city's historic 17th-century canal district.

    After lunch, take your time to discover Volendam at leisure or perhaps take an optional tour that includes a dairy specializing in Dutch cheeses and a visit to Edam.

    Overnight: 'Amadeus Brilliant'
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 4

    Arnhem

    This day cruise to Arnhem, where you will be given the option to choose from three optional excursions. Visit the site of the "Market Garden," an Allied airborne military operation in 1944. Then, continue to either the Open Air Museum of historic homes, farmhouses and mills; the Hartenstein Airborne Museum; or the sculpture garden at the Kröller-Müller Museum.

    Overnight:'Amadeus Brilliant'
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 5

    Middelberg

    Today the 'Amadeus Brilliant' will moor in Middelburg, sleepy capital of Zeeland. Take an optional walking tour of the city or an excursion to the Delta Works, designed to protect land from the encroaching sea, returning via the lovely town of Veere. Later, enjoy a private pre-dinner and cocktail party hosted by your A&K Tour Director in the ship's Amadeus Club.

    Overnight: 'Amadeus Brilliant'
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 6

    Ghent & Bruges

    Today cruise to Belgium for an optional visit to Ghent to experience the vivacity of its historic old town center. Later, visit the splendid medieval city and one of Belgium's crown jewels, Bruges.

    Bruges is often called as the 'the Venice of the North' for its romantic canals.

    Overnight: 'Amadeus Brilliant'
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 7

    Brussels & Antwerp

    Today enjoy an A&K exclusive excursion. Travel to Brussels for a short walking tour, continuing to the Royal Greenhouses on the grounds of the Palace of Laeken (subject to visiting hours).

    After your arrival in Antwerp, enjoy lunch at a local restaurant before you visit the Diamond Pavilion. Here, learn about the history of diamond trade in Belgium. Continue to Rubenshuis, the former home and studio of 17th-century painter Peter Paul Rubens. Return to the ship to end your day with a gala dinner onboard.

    Overnight: 'Amadeus Brilliant'
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 8

    Holland

    Venture out today for an optional walk at Kinderdijk to see Holland's largest collection of period windmills. Further, visit stunning Keukenhof Gardens, the world's second largest flower garden also known as the Garden of Europe. Take a walk through a wide diversity of blooming flowers with an unparalleled array of colors its 79 acres of area, especially during the spring season.

    Overnight: 'Amadeus Brilliant'
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 9

    Depart Amsterdam

    After breakfast, you will be assisted with transfer to the airport for your onward flight.

    Meal plan: B

India: Tigers & the Taj Mahal

Programme Overview

Explore the vibrant places of India. A rickshaw ride through Delhi's Chandni Chowk market, waking up to sunrise at the magnificent Taj Mahal, Camel safari to a desert oasis; also seek the elusive Bengal tiger in Rathambore, witnessing nine UNESCO World Heritage Sites along the way.

  • Duration: 13 days/ 12 nights

    Highlights: Delhi | Jodhpur | Jaipur | Ranthambor | Agra

    A&K Advantages

    • Take a thrilling rickshaw ride through vibrant Chandni Chowk during a Market Connection
    • Embark on a private elephant safari on a Rajput farm
    • Set out on a camel-back safari to a private desert oasis, and then journey by 4x4 to a dinner with a Maharaja's grandson
    • Seek the elusive Bengal tiger in Ranthambore National Park
    • Visit nine UNESCO World Heritage Sites on a single itinerary, including Humayun's Tomb, Qutub Minar and Jantar Mantar
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE IN DELHI

    After your arrival at the Delhi airport, you will be welcomed by an A&K representative and assisted with transfer to your hotel.

    Overnight: The Taj Palace Hotel

  • Day 2

    DELHI

    Begin with your exploration with a visit to Old Delhi’s Jama Masjid, the largest mosque in India (Friday Mosque).Travel through the bustling Chandni Chowk market with a rickshaw ride to visit the Red Fort, the residence of the Mughal emperor of India for nearly 200 years later rebuilt with red sandstone. Further, visit the site of Mahatma Gandhi's cremation, Raj Ghat.

    Next, set out to visit Delhi’s beautiful historic monuments, The Humayun’s Tomb and Qutub Minar.

    Later, drive through the pond-flanked Rajpath, viewing India’s pride The India Gate, built in memory of the 90,000 Indian soldiers who laid down their lives during World War I. Also visit the beautifully structured Rashtrapati Bhawan, residence of the President of India.

    Tonight, gather for a welcome cocktail followed by dinner.

    Overnight: The Taj Palace Hotel
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 3

    JODHPUR

    This morning, depart for Jodhpur. After arrival start the journey with the picturesque desert town Osian, where you will enjoy your stay in a private, mobile, tented desert camp. Later, enjoy the picturesque sunset amid the dunes on a camel-back safari ,followed by sundowner cocktails. Set out on a 4x4 vehicle for an amazing experience of folk entertainment and candlelit dinner with the grandson of Maharaja Ratan Singh.

    Overnight: Exclusive Mobile Camp
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 4

    JODHPUR

    Rise early this morning to witness the breathtaking sunrise view from your desert camp. Further, travel to Jodhpur, Also known as the ‘Sun city’ is famous for historic grandeur, exquisite handicrafts, folk dances & music. Check into your luxurious hotel, with the rest of the day at leisure.

    Enjoy the evening experiencing the rich culture in Jodhpur’s Old Town through a local tuk-tuk ride .Walk through its vibrant bazaars and markets.

  • Day 5

    This day, drive to Salawas for an exciting morning safari, interacting with local potters, weavers and the Bishnoi people.

    Later, explore the magnificent Jodhpur Meharangarh Fort, Continue to visit the architectural landmark, Jaswant Thada monument, a white marble memorial. Also view the Maharaja Jaswant Singh memorial, a cenotaph displaying Maharajas’ portraits.

    Overnight: Vivanta by Taj Hari Mahal
    Meal plan: B (Day 4); B, L, D (Day 5)

  • Day 6

    JAIPUR

    Today, enjoy a full day drive to the “Pink City” of Jaipur, with its majestic palaces, forts and incredible arts and crafts.

    En-route visit the Jain temple near Osian. Upon arrival, check into your hotel for a good night’s sleep.

    Overnight: Jai Mahal Palace
    Meal plan: B, L, D (Day 6); B, L (Day 7)

  • Day 7

    JAIPUR

    Begin this day with a visit to the Amber Fort, set in picturesque rugged hills with a fascinating blend of Hindu and Mughal architecture; later, drive to Dera Amer for an elephant safari.

    Continue to explore Jaipur, visiting the renowned Hawa Mahal Palace and the Jantar Mantar Observatory, an equinoctial sundial with a collection of architectural astronomical instruments.

    End the day with a visit to the spectacular City Palace.

    Overnight: Jai Mahal Palace
    Meal plan: B, L, D (Day 6); B, L (Day 7)

  • Day 8-9

    RANTHAMBORE

    Take a beautiful drive to Ranthambore, where you check into your hotel. Spend the next two days exploring the Ranthambore National Park, one of the largest national parks in northern India and a UNESCO World Heritage Site with lake palaces, fortifications and the centuries-old Ranthambore Fort. Amid the ruins, watch for tigers, leopards, hyenas, sloth bears, barking deer and nilgai, Asia’s largest antelope.

    Overnight: Vivanta by Taj Sawai Madhopur Lodge
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 10

    AGRA

    Today, board a train to Bharatpur and proceed to Agra, where you visit Fatehpur Sikri, a red sandstone city “ghost town” built by Emperor Akbar as his capital and palace.

    This evening, visit the magnificent Taj Mahal to enjoy the mesmerizing sunset view of the epitome of Mughal art and one of the most famous buildings in the world.

  • Day 11

    AGRA

    This morning, explore the grandeur Agra Fort, a massive red-sandstone fort on the bank of the Yamuna River, once Akbar’s citadel.

    Spend the afternoon at leisure after returning to your hotel. Enjoy a refreshing evening with cocktails followed by a farewell dinner with the fellow guests.

    Overnight: ITC Mughal, Agra
    Meal plan: B, L (Day 10); B, D (Day 11)

  • Day 12

    DELHI

    Drive to Delhi and transfer to the airport hotel for the night’s stay.

    Overnight: JW Marriott Aerocity
    Meal plan: B

  • Day 13

    DEPART DELHI

    Take a quick transfer to the airport for your onward flight.

Connections: Discovering The Rhine

Programme Overview

Cruise for seven nights on the Rhine and Moselle Rivers visiting the Rijksmuseum and Cologne's cathedral, tour Strasbourg, enjoy an A&K-exclusive tour of Burg Eltz and visit a chocolatier's workshop.

  • Duration: 10 days/ 9 nights

    Highlights: Melbourne | Cairns | Sydney

    A&K Advantages

    • Cruise for seven nights on the Rhine, and enjoy one night in Amsterdam and Basel in centrally located hotels
    • Visit the Anne Frank House and the Diamond Museum in Amsterdam
    • Enjoy an A&K exclusive in Holland, visiting Gouda and De Waag, the city's famous 17th-century weigh house
    • Cruise the Rhine Gorge, one of the river's loveliest stretches
    • Visit historic cathedrals in Cologne, Strasbourg and Basel
    • Tour a chocolatier's workshop or a gingerbread bakery including a delicious treat
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    Arrive Amsterdam

    On arrival at the Amsterdam airport, you will be greeted by an A&K representative who will assist with transfer to the hotel.

    This evening, enjoy a welcome dinner with your fellow guests.

    Overnight: Sofitel Legend the Grand Amsterdam
    Meal plan: D

  • Day 2

    Amsterdam

    Start your journey of Amsterdam with a private canal boat cruise. Continue to visit the Anne Frank House, a historic house and biographical museum dedicated to Jewish wartime diarist Anne Frank.

    Then, visit the Diamond Museum with its glittering displays of priceless gems. This afternoon, enjoy priority boarding on 'Amadeus Silver. In the evening, enjoy the hospitality with a refreshing welcome cocktail followed by dinner.

    Overnight: Amadeus Silver
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 3

    Gouda

    Today, take a tour in Gouda exclusive to A&K guests. Begin by visiting De Waag, a characteristic monumental structure with a museum dedicated to local crafts. Stroll through the market square and later, head for an afternoon cruise to Utrecht.

    Overnight: Amadeus Silver
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 4

    Cologne

    Arrive in Cologne and explore the culture of the place through a walking tour of the old city. Go ahead for a dazzling architectural journey inside the 13th century Gothic cathedral, with its intricately designed architectural features.

    Overnight: Amadeus Silver
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 5

    Cochem

    Today, arrive in Cochem on the Moselle River, where you may opt for a walking tour that includes a wine tasting session or explore the place at your own pace.

    Later, enjoy a specially arranged private pre-dinner cocktail party in the ship's Amadeus Club Amadeus

    Overnight: Amadeus Silver
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 6

    Koblenz & Rüdesheim

    Reach Koblenz this morning and choose either from an optional walking tour or explore independently. This afternoon, enjoy a relaxing cruise on a picturesque stretch of the Rhine, viewing the hilltop castles, terraced vineyards and charming villages on your way. Conclude your day at Rüdesheim to visit Siegfried's Mechanical Music Museum.

    Overnight: Amadeus Silver
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 7

    Mannhiem & Speyer

    This morning, you may pick one from the optional excursions, a walking tour of Mannheim, with its landmark 19th-century wasserturm (water tower) or a tour of the lively university town of Heidelberg. Enjoy your lunch before you board on for a leisure cruise to Speyer and further for a walking tour. Marvel at the 180-foothigh Old Gate or appreciate the beauty of the Imperial Cathedral. End your day with a gala dinner with the captain on board.

    Overnight: Amadeus Silver
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 8

    Strasbourg

    This day is for you to enjoy a tour of the Strasbourg, capital of France's Alsace region. Visiting the Gothic cathedral with its intricate façade, beautiful stained glass windows and marvelous astronomical clock, also make a visit to the city's historic center (or Grand Île), a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

    Overnight: Amadeus Silver
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 9

    Basel

    After breakfast, take a travel to discover the charming city of Basel. Visit the majestic cathedral and stroll through the local market where you may dine at a local restaurant for lunch or visit a chocolatier for a workshop tour or tasting. This evening, enjoy a farewell dinner with your fellow travelers.

    Overnight: Hilton Basel/Hotel Euler
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 10

    Depart Basel

    After breakfast, get an assisted transfer to Basel airport and depart for your onward flight.

    Meal plan: B
