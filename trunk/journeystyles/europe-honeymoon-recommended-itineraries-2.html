ROMANTIC BOHEMIAN PARIS

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 4 Days/3 Nights

    A special 4 day tour of the French capital; exploring the main sights of one of the most romantic cities in the world.

    Programme Overview : Eiffel Tower, Arc de Triomphe, Champs Elysées, and Notre Dame Cathedral

    Highlights : Sightseeing of Paris city including major highlights
    Evening enjoy a romantic four Course dinner cruise
    Tickets to the Moulin Rouge show

    A&K Advantage :Behind the scenes tour at the Eiffel Tower
    Visit to a renowned "Parfumeur" for a unique private workshop
    Personalised shopping in Paris
    Private arrival and departure airport transfers
    The services of an Abercrombie & Kent Guardian Angel

    * On arrival in each country, you are welcomed by your A&K Guardian Angel who conducts a briefing on your time in the country. A Guardian Angel is always at hand to provide unparalleled 24/7 support, be it local travel tips or reconfirming arrangement details, to providing recommendations for restaurants and shopping. Should you ever need help or advice, dial your Guardian Angel’s mobile phone for prompt and courteous service at any time.

  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE PARIS–At leisure & Dinner Cruise on Seine

    Upon arrival at Paris airport you will be met in the arrival hall by your chauffeur and privately transferred to your hotel.

    On the Rue de Rivoli overlooking the beautiful Tuileries Gardens, the Hotel Meurice boasts an excellent location in the heart of Paris as it is nestled between the Place de la Concorde and the Grand Louvre. Also nearby are the Opéra Garnier, the Jewellery stores on the rue de la Paix, and the Place Vendôme. Original antiques and paintings are featured throughout the hotel and every floor is decorated in a distinctive style.

    Time is at leisure to unwind from the flight.

    This evening discover Paris, the city of lights and enjoy a dinner cruise on the Seine.

    Board the completely glass–encased boat for your romantic dinner which includes live music. Your 4–course menu includes some wine and a glass of champagne with dessert.

    (Special Offer for the Honeymoon Couple – Bouquet of Flowers and Bottle of Champagne)

    Overnight:Hotel Le Meurice
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 2

    PARIS–Sightseeing, Exclusive viewing & Dinner at Le Jules Verne

    This morning you will enjoy a half day (4 hours) sightseeing tour in Paris with your private vehicle and English–speaking guide. This overview tour will include famous Parisian sights such as the Eiffel Tower, Arc de Triomphe, Champs Elysées and the Ile de la Cité, where you will visit the beautiful Notre Dame Cathedral.

    Time at leisure & later a behind the scenes tour can be organized at the Eiffel Tower. View the internal machinery room located under the structure. You will be provided entry to the old secret military bunkers sited under the Champs des Mars, previously dedicated to radio and telegraph equipment. The tour then finishes on the 2nd floor, on the roof of the Michelin star restaurant, Le Jules Verne.

    Enjoy your dinner in this glorious setting & return to your hotel for the night.

    Overnight: Hotel Le Meurice
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    PARIS–Personalized shopping & Moulin Rouge show

    Explore the best of Paris’s vintage boutiques with your personal shopping guide who will show you true hidden gems in one of the fashion capitals of the world. Meet specialist sellers and receive private access to boutiques to find something truly unique to remind you of your trip to Paris.

    This afternoon your guide will take you to a renowned "Parfumeur" for a unique private workshop (4hrs). You will step behind the scenes and learn about the key stages in the composition of a fragrance. You will discover the techniques and the ingredients before becoming a "nose" yourself! You will design your own personal blend of notes with a selection of high quality ingredients. Also plan the name and label of your 17.5 fl oz. bottle of personal fragrance and carry it with you as a souvenir of this factory experience.

    9.00 PM – enjoy the cabaret theatre show at the Moulin Rouge. See the world–renowned showgirls and French Cancan dancers on the Moulin Rouge's historic stage.

    We recommend you have your dinner prior to the show.

    Return to the hotel after the show and you may lounge in the cosy sophisticated atmosphere of the hotels in house bar. Bar 228 is well known to be “the rendez–vous” of Parisians.

    Overnight: Hotel Le Meurice
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    DEPART PARIS

    Today you will be transferred to the Paris airport by private vehicle to board your flight.

    Meal Plan: B

ROMANCE IN LEISURELY CROATIA

Programme Overview:

Split

  • Duration: 4 Days/3 Nights

    A luxurious and relaxing 4 day visit to the stunning Croatian coastal town of Split

    Highlights: Explore the Ancient city of Split and enjoy plenty of time at leisure to make the most of your wonderful hotel facilities. Special couple’s spa session.

    A&K Advantage Private arrival and departure airport transfers
    The services of an Abercrombie & Kent Guardian Angel

    * On arrival in each country, you are welcomed by your A&K Guardian Angel who conducts a briefing on your time in the country. A Guardian Angel is always at hand to provide unparalleled 24/7 support, be it local travel tips or reconfirming arrangement details, to providing recommendations for restaurants and shopping. Should you ever need help or advice, dial your Guardian Angel’s mobile phone for prompt and courteous service at any time.
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE SPLIT–At leisure

    Meet your chauffeur at Split airport and transfer to your hotel in a private vehicle which overlooks the bay of Split, the Adriatic Sea and the iconic island of Brac. The beautiful setting of the hotel features 800 meters of beach frontage, gardens landscaped by award–winning designer Jim Nicolay of Hawaii, and fantastic sea views overlooking Split and the surrounding islands.

    Overnight: Le Méridien Lav

  • Day 2

    SPLIT–Enjoy hotel facilities & Spa

    Today is at your leisure to enjoy the facilities of your beachfront hotel. In terms of activities the hotel facilities include an outdoor and an indoor pool, tennis courts and a Water Sports Centre which offers scuba diving, waterskiing, sailing, and more. The exclusive spa and wellness centre is a magnificent space offering treatment rooms, relaxation areas and a fitness centre, the perfect place to unwind and feel pampered.

    This afternoon, enjoy a one hour special couple’s spa & totally unwind from the wedding’s hectic schedule!

    This evening, you could enjoy Split's most scenic terrace, Vidilica, perched atop Marjan hill. It is the most scenic spot, and can be reached by trekking up the steep but worthwhile climb. The large café–bar is tastefully covered sepia images of how Split looked 100 years ago. To get the worth of the trek up to the café, occupy one of the outside tables and admire the panoramic view from the other side of a stone wall guarding the steep drop.

    Overnight: Le Méridien Lav
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    SPLIT–Sightseeing

    Today you may wish to explore the ancient city of Split, including the palace which was the 'retirement home' of the roman emperor Hadrian.

    You may enjoy a romantic dinner of fresh oysters overlooking the marina along with Croatian wine.

    Overnight: Le Méridien Lav
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    DEPART SPLIT

    Your chauffeur will bring you back in style to Split Airport today.

    Meal Plan: B

  • Possible Extensions:

    Option 1:

    3 Days DUBROVNIK

    Day 1

    DUBROVNIK–Leisure and view of the city

    On arrival in Dubrovnik, check in to your hotel and then relax at leisure. Discover the city's pretty pebble beaches or people watch in the beautiful Old Port.

    Fascinating view of the Dubrovnik’s sunset attracts romantic couples. The beauty of the sunset is such that it can be adored from practically any spot in town. A stroll along Dubrovnik’s walls will give you the view of sun merging or rather melting into the blue sea. Alternately if you travel up the hill of Srdj by a cable car, you will be delighted with a breathtaking panoramic view of the entire city bathed in soft orange light.

    Enjoy a horse drawn carriage ride at sunset with a bottle of champagne & strawberries as you watch the legendary sunset.

    Dinner is followed by a return to the hotel for the night.

    Overnight: Hilton Imperial Dubrovnik
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 2

    DUBROVNIK–Old Town walking tour

    After breakfast in your hotel, meet your private guide in the center of town for a leisurely paced 2–hour walking tour around Dubrovnik’s UNESCO World Heritage–listed Old Town. Begin your tour from Pile Gate as you go along sophisticated Stadrun Street, passing Onofrio Fountain – a spectacular dome surrounded by 16 taps – and see the Franciscan monastery with its Romanesque–Gothic architecture.

    While strolling across to Sponza Palace learn about the city’s history and culture. Sponza Palace is undoubtedly Dubrovnik’s most stunning monument; the palace is a fusion of Renaissance and Gothic architecture, simple yet majestic. Look out for distinguished attractions like the Church of St Blaise and Rector’s Palace while winding through the pristine streets of Old Town. Reach the main square to view the 18th–century Cathedral of the Assumption of the Virgin. You may walk back to the start point where to end the tour or spend the rest of your day at leisure in the town.

    You could step in for Croatian wine and lunch at likes of Arsenal Wine Bar which is located in an old boatbuilding warehouse, which used to be the city’s covered boatyard in medieval days. The ambience with low lighting and a view through two ancient arches makes it a romantic venue for a meal.

    Enjoy a romantic dinner at one of the restaurants. You may visit Nautika, sophisticated setting which serves a blend of classic and modern cuisine.

    Overnight: Hilton Imperial Dubrovnik
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    DEPART DUBROVNIK

    This morning, your personal chauffer will transfer you to the airport for your onward connections.

    Meal Plan: B

  • Option 2:

    3 Days Hvar

    Day 1

    HVAR–Stroll around on this pedestrian only island at leisure

    Take your private boat to the Queen of the Croatian Dalmatian islands – Hvar! Hvar is a pedestrian only island and does not allow any cars. Rustic cobblestone streets with all the hotels located right on the crystal blue water, makes it one of the most romantic town.

    Modern interiors combine with classic 1940s prints and neon lighting! You can enjoy a delicious coffee at the waterfront terrace of the hotel before setting off to discover the region in your private yacht.

    In the evening appreciate the spectacular sunset by the sea, sipping a cool cocktail to the sound of DJs.

    Overnight: Lesic Dimitri Palace
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 2

    HVAR–At leisure & private sunset picnic at Sv. Nikola

    Morning at leisure to explore Hvar at your pace.

    You could perhaps enjoy a couple’s spa at the hotel this morning or walk around this amazing Island.

    We will pick you up at your hotel & take you via your boat to the islands highest peak, hiking escort to our romantic sunset picnic place, basket with chilled bottle of sparkling wine/regular wine, strawberries and island delicacies.

    Enjoy the most amazing sunset in the Mediterranean and enjoy a wonderful picnic! Perched atop the island’s highest point, Sv. Nikola, you and your loved one can experience the magic of watching the sun slip into the sea together, alone.

    Overnight: Lesic Dimitri Palace
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    DEPART HVAR

    This morning, your chauffer will transfer you back in style to Split for your onward connections.

  • Option 3:

    4 Days SVETI STEFAN

    Day 1

    SVETI STEFAN–Relaxing massage and at leisure

    Fly on a private charter across the Adriatic Sea to Podgorica, capital of Montenegro. Upon arrival, you will be welcomed by your Guardian Angel and transferred to the magical island of Sveti Stefan, one of Europe's newest and most exclusive luxury destinations.

    On arrival, enjoy a special relaxing couple's massage in one of the private cottages on the island.

    Evening, explore the island at your pace and enjoy at one of its restaurants in the charming Piazatta, with romantic views over the clear blue waters of the Adriatic.

    Overnight at the resort in your deluxe cottage, you may upgrade to a suite, which features a functional fireplace to add to the romance.

    Overnight: Aman Resort Sveti Stefan
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 2

    SVETI STEFAN–Cruising the Adriatic

    Set out on a romantic private cruise on board your luxury yacht with a personal skipper. You plan your day with your captain, opt to discover a sheltered beach cove, admire the magnificence of the Kotor fjord or cruise up to Dubrovnik for a romantic lunch. Return to Sveti Stefan by the afternoon.

    Evening at leisure, we suggest you dine in nearby medieval Budva.

    Overnight: Aman Resort Sveti Stefan
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    SVETI STEFAN–Explore Skadar Lake

    Today enjoy the harmony and splendor of Skadar Lake National Park in the morning.

    Explore the lake by yourselves on a private boat or canoe, and experience a true escape. Skadar is a haven for wildlife and nature, home to 270 bird species. In the neighboring region, there are 20 monasteries, churches, villages, fortresses and sacred monuments for you to explore.

    Return to Sveti Stefan by late afternoon, rest of the day at leisure. You may relax at the resort's private beach cove.

    Overnight: Aman Resort Sveti Stefan
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    DEPART SVETI STEFAN

    Privately transfer to Podgorica Airport for your onward connection.

ROMANTIC FRENCH HONEYMOON

Programme Overview

Paris, Burgundy region, Vézelay, Puligny Montrachet, La Bussière sur Ouche, Dijon & Beaune

  • Duration: 9 Days/8 Nights

    Highlights: Sightseeing of Paris city including major highlights and evening enjoy romantic four Course dinner cruise Dinner and private cooking class with Marc Ménaud Visit to the Chateau du Clos de Vougeot Full day (8 hour) sightseeing tours of Dijon, Beaune and a private hot air balloon flight

    A&K Advantage : Visit to a renowned "Parfumeur" for a unique private workshop
    Personalized shopping in Paris
    Private arrival and departure airport transfers
    The services of an Abercrombie & Kent Guardian Angel

    * On arrival in each country, you are welcomed by your A&K Guardian Angel who conducts a briefing on your time in the country. A Guardian Angel is always at hand to provide unparalleled 24/7 support, be it local travel tips or reconfirming arrangement details, to providing recommendations for restaurants and shopping. Should you ever need help or advice, dial your Guardian Angel’s mobile phone for prompt and courteous service at any time.

  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE PARIS–Dinner cruise on the Seine

    Upon arrival at the Paris airport, you will be met and transferred by your Guardian Angel and private vehicle with an English–speaking chauffeur to your hotel in Paris, where you will be assisted with check–in.

    Day is at leisure

    This evening discover Paris, the city of lights and enjoy a dinner cruise on the Seine. 8:30PM board an entirely glass–encased boat for your romantic dinner including live musical animation. Your 4–course menu will include wine and a glass of champagne with dessert.

    Special Offer for the Honeymoon Couple – Bouquet of Flowers and Bottle of Champagne .

    Overnight: Sofitel Paris Le Faubourg
    Meal Plan: D

  • Day 2

    PARIS–Unique experience of blending your own personal fragrance & Moulin Rouge show

    This afternoon your guide will take you to a renowned "Parfumeur" for a unique private workshop (4hrs). You will step behind the scenes and learn about the key stages in the composition of a fragrance. You will discover the techniques and the ingredients before becoming a "nose" yourself! You will design your own personal blend of notes with a selection of high quality ingredients. Also plan the name and label of your 17.5 fl oz. bottle of personal fragrance and carry it with you as a souvenir of this factory experience.

    9.00 PM – enjoy the cabaret theatre show at the Moulin Rouge. See the world–renowned showgirls and French Cancan dancers on the Moulin Rouge's historic stage.

    We recommend you have your dinner prior to the show.

    Return to the hotel after the show and you may lounge in the elegant comfort of the hotels in house bar. The bar is stocked with over 100 whiskies and liqueurs to choose from.

    Overnight: Sofitel Paris Le Faubourg
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    PARIS–Personal shopping guide, behind the scenes tour of the Eiffel tour

    Explore the best of Paris’s vintage boutiques with your personal shopping guide who will show you true hidden gems in one of the fashion capitals of the world. Meet specialist sellers and receive private access to boutiques to find something truly unique to remind you of your trip to Paris.

    Time at leisure & later a behind the scenes tour can be organized at the Eiffel Tower. View the internal machinery room located under the structure. You will be provided entry to the old secret military bunkers sited under the Champs des Mars, previously dedicated to radio and telegraph equipment. The tour then finishes on the 2nd floor, on the roof of the Michelin star restaurant, Le Jules Verne.

    Enjoy your dinner in this glorious setting & return to your hotel for the night.

    Overnight: Sofitel Paris Le Faubourg
    Meal Plan: B,D

  • Day 4

    PARIS–CHABLIS–VÉZELAY–Burgundy region tour

    This morning, you would be met by your English–speaking wine expert guide, who will accompany you through your tour of Burgundy.

    On your way to Burgundy stop at Chablis, the most northerly and insular of ‘Burgundian Wine regions’; known for the Chardonnay grape and the clay soil which produce delicate wines with considerable finesse. There are seven different Grand Cru vineyards in the Chablis appellation. At Domaine William Fèvre enjoy tasting of the distinct wines of the region. The Domain is set within 47 hectares of skillfully harvested vineyards in the heart of Chablis.

    Afterwards drive deeper into the countryside of Burgundy, stopping to walk in the valley of the River Serein, before arriving this evening at the foot of the ‘holy’ hill of Vézelay to check into your celebrated hotel, Hotel de l’Espérance.

    This evening sample the cooking of Marc Meneau, owner of the hotel and one of the founders of la nouvelle cuisine française. Marc Meneau was born in the village and knows all the flavors of the region. You may also enjoy a local Vezelay wine to accompany your meal.

    Overnight: Hotel de L’Espérance
    Meal Plan: B,D

  • Day 5

    VÉZELAY–Sight–seeing & private cooking class with Marc Ménaud

    Your private vehicle with chauffeur and an English–speaking wine expert guide will be at your disposal for a half day. Visit the beautiful setting of Vézelay and the magnificence of its glorious Basilica. Follow the footsteps of medieval pilgrims with a visit to the ‘sacred hill’ of Vézelay. Best known for its mighty Basilica of Ste–Madeleine, where St Bernard preached the Second Crusade in 1146, and whose carved capitals are one of the greatest Romanesque treasures of Europe.

    Early this afternoon enjoy a private cooking class with Marc Ménaud, sous chef. You will be able to create some of Marc’s original recipes yourself followed by a feast of your creations.

    The remainder of the afternoon is at your leisure.

    Overnight: Hotel de L’Espérance
    Meal Plan: B,L

  • Day 6

    VÉZELAY–PULIGNY MONTRACHET–LA BUSSIÈRE SUR OUCHE–Wine tasting

    Your private vehicle with chauffeur and an English–speaking wine expert guide will be at your disposal for a full day transfer to your hotel in the beautiful location of la Bussière Sur Ouche.

    You will stop for a wine workshop (not available on Sundays) and a lunch at Olivier Leflaive’s estate. In 1984 Olivier Leflaive, with help from his uncle Vincent Leflaive, set up his own wine business, which he continues to manage to this day. With the help of winemaker Jean–Marc Boillot, Olivier Leflaive produced wines of quality from the outset. Quality has continued to improve over time, and Olivier Leflaive has established a reputation of high quality. He is also renowned for his Table–d'Hôtes, where you will be invited today for lunch and a wine tasting.

    Continue on your journey to the Chateau de la Bussière, which is superbly located in the Cote d’Or.

    Table reservation can be made this evening for you to enjoy dinner at your hotel’s gastronomic restaurant.

    Overnight: Abbaye de La Bussière
    Meal Plan: B,L

  • Day 7

    DIJON–Walking tour & visit to vineyards

    This morning travel to Dijon with your chauffeur and an English–speaking wine expert guide for a full day (8 hour) sightseeing tour. On arrival in Dijon, the historical capital of Burgundy, enjoy a walking tour of the Old Town, with its splendid half–timber houses. Burgundian are truly obsessed with good wine and food and there is nowhere better to experience this than the local market (Tuesday & Friday, on Thursday the market is only inside the Halls), where you can sample the wonderful local delicacies and produce.

    This afternoon you will be treated to a visit to the Chateau du Clos de Vougeot that lies at the heart of one of the largest of all the Grand Cru vineyards. The chateau is particularly renowned for its historic wine–making barn. Vougeot is also ‘the home of the celebrated Chevaliers du Tastevin’.

    Time permitting, you may also like to visit Le Gevrey Chambertin vineyard, which is known as the crown of the Côte de Nuits. At the end of the afternoon you will be transferred back to your hotel.

    This evening we would be happy to make dinner reservation at L’Ecluse de la Charme restaurant in Saint–Victor–sur–Ouche.

    Overnight: Abbaye de La Bussière
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 8

    BEAUNE–MEURSAULT–Wine tasting & Hot air ballooning

    This morning, with your chauffeur and an English–speaking wine expert guide, visit Beaune for a full day (8 hour) sightseeing tour.

    Beaune is world famous as the capital of the Burgundy wine region. Located in the heart of Burgundy, its numerous medieval buildings with their colourful roofs are a reminder of a wealthy past. You will drive out into the countryside of the Cote de Beaune that surrounds the town. Cote de Beaune, southern part of the Cote d’Or, is home to some of the world’s finest dry white wines produced from the Chardonnay grape, and also well–known red wines from Pinot Noir. You will also be taken to visit one of the noted domains in Meursault, a village that is famed for its production of white wine. You will have the opportunity to taste the typical wines from the village as well.

    Later today, enjoy a private hot air balloon flight (subject to good weather conditions) over the châteaux, villages and vineyards of Burgundy. This breathtaking experience takes place at sun set and provides you with unforgettable memories. View some of the places you have visited during the past few days from the sky. (Hot air balloon flight – is also available in the morning – at sun rise.)

    This evening we suggest you enjoy dinner at the restaurant L’Eire du Temps in Barbirey–sur–Ouche

    Overnight: Abbaye de La Bussière
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 9

    DEPART PARIS

    Enjoy your drive back to Paris in your private vehicle. On arrival, you would be taken to the airport for your flight.

  • Possible Extension:
    5 Days NICE & MONTE CARLO

    Day 1

    PARIS–NICE–At leisure

    Your private charter will fly you from Paris to Nice

    Upon arrival in Nice you will be met by your local Guardian Angel and transferred to your hotel in Nice.

    Afternoon is at leisure for you to enjoy the city.

    Overnight: Le Méridien
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 2

    NICE–CANNES–At the beachside at your pace

    Cannes, host of the glamorous and stylish annual film festival is also famous for luxury shops. Cannes is a boisterous and sparkling seaside destination, with a spread of luxury hotels, gourmet dining and appealing beaches.

    Spend the day in this beautiful beach side at your pace.

    Return to Nice for the night.

    Overnight: Le Méridien
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    NICE–MONTE CARLO–Sightseeing of Nice, Monaco & Monte Carlo

    Today meet your private local English–speaking guide for half–day sightseeing (4hrs) in Nice. You will explore the beautiful Old Town and colourful open–air market and take the lift up to the Château Hill (Colline du Château) area for unrivalled views of the city.

    After lunch, with your private vehicle and English–speaking guide, set off to spend the afternoon exploring the Principality of Monaco. This exceptional city–state is a land steeped in history and also a Mediterranean mini Las Vegas. The capital, Monaco, is a fairy tale city standing on a rocky headland almost surrounded by sea, and contains the palace complete with guards, the tropical gardens offering excellent views, and the museum containing Roman antiquities and prehistory collections.

    En route you could also stop by the village of Eze which is renowned for its Perfume factory. Located only 8 km from Monaco, the medieval fortified village of Eze makes for a wonderful day trip for anyone looking to experience the real charm of Cote d’Azur. Perched on a rock high above the Mediterranean Sea, the enchanting town consists of a picture–perfect cluster of narrow winding streets that open out into pretty little squares and patios full of charm and serenity.

    Look around the Place du Casino, possibly the most famous casino in the world, in the center of Monte Carlo. Go on to the well–known Rock, crowned by the Palais Princier (open April through October) , where the royal family lives, seeing the extravagance of the 16th and 17th century frescoes, as well as tapestries, gilt furniture and paintings.

    Time is at leisure to enjoy the amazing principality of Monte Carlo.

    Overnight: Hermitage
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    MONTE CARLO–Shopping

    Day is at your own pace.

    You could either take a private yacht tour for the day or indulge in some retail therapy.

    Monaco offers a wide–ranging shopping experience; from distinctive shops and souvenir stalls of the Old Town to the exquisite designer boutiques and splendid chocolatiers found in famous Carre d’Or. Luxury brands such as Dior, Gucci, Hermes or Chanel are featured at the prestigious Golden Square, which includes the place du Casino, avenue des Beaux Arts and avenue Monte Carlo. Undoubtedly, it is a favourite shopping location with the well–heeled fashionistas and celebrities.

    A table will be reserved for you at Le Louis XV. Chef Alain Ducasse’s restaurant is committed to good taste and fantasy; authenticity and culinary sophistication have been sensitively maintained since its inauguration more than 25 years ago. In a sophisticated setting of light and gold, suggestive of Versailles Grand Siècle, all the hands on the clocks are stopped on 12 to indicate that within this Palace of Pleasure, time is of no significance. A dream come true for wine aficionados' as the wine cellar is stocked with 400 000 bottles. A timeless enchantment.

    Overnight: Hermitage
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 5

    Departure

    Your chauffer will transfer you to Nice in your private vehicle for your onward private charter flight.

ROMANTIC PRAGUE: A FAIRYTALE CITY

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 5 Days/4 Nights

    Romance in the enchanting town of Prague – one of the most beautiful cities in the world, and a UNESCO World Heritage site!

    Highlights : Luxury Cruise on river Vltava in Prague
    Hot air balloon ride with Champagne
    Trip to Karlovy Vary for a private romantic spa at the beautiful spa town

    A&K Advantage
    Reservations at Duplex, the most exclusive club in Prague
    Private Vintage Tram Ride when on sightseeing of Prague's Castle District
    Discover a jewel of Europe and experience its artistic and cultural traditions alongside local A&K experts
    Private arrival and departure airport transfers

  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE PRAGUE–Vintage tram ride

    Upon arrival at Prague you will be welcomed by your Guardian Angel and privately transferred to your hotel where you will be assisted with check–in.

    Explore this delightful city in a half day (4 hour) sightseeing tour in the late afternoon to Prague's Castle District with your English–speaking local guide. You will travel by private vintage tram from a tram stop close to your hotel to a stop near Prague Castle. Hradcany Castle was the seat of Bohemian kings and Holy Roman emperors. Stroll along the Golden Lane, a row of tiny houses dating from the 16th century.

    In the evening enjoy a quiet drink in the hotel's intimate St Thomas brewery bar, housed in the cellar or you may enjoy your dinner at Kampa Park, Prague's premium fine dining establishment. A favorite amid local and celebrities, this upmarket restaurant delivers lavish gourmet paired with over 150 types of wines. Diners can select between sophisticated dining rooms or the intimate riverside terrace overlooking Charles Bridge or Certovka stream.

    Overnight: Augustine Hotel
    Meal Plan B

  • Day 2

    PRAGUE–Walking tour of the Old town & Hot air ballooning

    Meet your English–speaking local guide and enjoy a half–day walking tour (4 hours) of the Old Town with its winding cobbled streets and fairy–tale charm and the fascinating Jewish Quarter, birthplace of Franz Kafka.

    See the striking Gothic Town Hall in Old Town Square, famous for its unique astronomical clock, and visit the Jewish Museum which houses the largest collection of Judaica in Europe, the Old–New Synagogue and the very moving Jewish Cemetery. You may also enjoy a walk across the romantically picturesque 14th century Charles Bridge.

    This afternoon post lunch you will be transported for a private hot air balloon flight. It is a thrilling, unforgettable and a romantic experience. Celebrate with a bottle of Champagne and refreshments later.

    Overnight: Augustine Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    PRAGUE–KARLOVY VARY–PRAGUE–Exotic Spa treatment and night at Duplex club

    A day trip to the Spa town planned especially for you. You will be transported by a private vehicle to Karlovy Vary which is a small & beautiful spa town located approx. at a distance of 2 hours from Prague.

    The spa began in the 14th century, seeing its first major influx of international guests in the 17th century. Spa buildings grounds include geysers as well as thermal springs which are natural and not man made, which is a unique point for the Karlovy Vary and reason it’s so famous for natural spa therapies.

    A reservation for a private couple therapy and an hour in the Relaxation pool exclusively for you will be made. Unwind and relax in each other’s company along with a bottle of Champagne and strawberries.

    Evening you will be transferred back to the hotel.

    Reservations at Duplex for the night will be in order. Duplex is the élite club in Prague, with outstanding views to match its status. Perched on the rooftop of one of the tallest buildings, the Duplex club is designed to be an enormous glass cube. The multi–level space is contemporary and classy, with glass walls and mood lighting. The terrace balcony provides memorable views of the city. Dancers cheer up the atmosphere and Duplex’s fine resident DJ’s vie with international names to entertain the crowd. It is known to be the best music venue in the city that attracts the most elite gentry.

    Overnight: Augustine Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    PRAGUE–Personalized shopping & Luxury cruise on Vltava

    Meet your Guardian Angel who will accompany you to Wenceslas Square for shopping.

    Tonight you are booked on a luxury cruise with a Á la carte menu. You can look forward to a delightful experience on board of a luxury boat, during which you will enjoy live piano composition and excellent culinary delights. Add to it exceptionally memorable views of shimmering river Vltava and charismatic architectural monuments of the capital city to complete your perfect night out.

    Overnight: Augustine Hotel
    Meal Plan: B, D

  • Day 5

    DEPART PRAGUE

    This morning you will be privately transferred to Prague airport for your departure flight.

    Meal Plan: B
