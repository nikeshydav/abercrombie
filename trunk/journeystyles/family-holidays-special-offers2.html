  • Special Upgrade Offer at KASBAH TAMADOT, MARRAKESH MOROCCO

    Full Board Offer
    Valid until 21 December 13

    Full Board: All meals included i.e. breakfast, lunch & dinner.
    From just MAD 800 per person per night guests staying at Kasbah Tamadot can opt for our full board supplement including lunch and dinner from either the à la carte menu or the special menu of the evening. After a swim in the infinity pool order lunch at the pool terrace, and later get a taste for some authentic Moroccan dishes in Kanoun Restaurant. These rates are in addition to the nightly bed and breakfast rates.

    UPGRADE TO FULL BOARD FOR ONLY MAD 800
    When is the offer available?
    From 2nd January until 21st December 2013

    How much?
    For MAD 800 supplement per person per night guests staying at Kasbah Tamadot can opt for our full board experience.

    Children's rates
    Infants under the age of 6 years are free of charge and children between the ages of 6 - 12 years are charged MAD 400 per child per night.

    What's included?
    Lunch either on an à la carte basis, or for guests who wish to go out and explore we will pack a picnic lunch.

    Dinner can be chosen from the à la carte menu in Kanoun Restaurant or on selected themed evenings when a selection of traditional and international dishes are served buffet style.

    Drinks are charged additionally.

    Terms and Conditions

    • This rate is priced in Moroccan Dirham (MAD), is per person and is in addition to the nightly bed and breakfast rates.
    • Offer is valid when booking any available tactical offers, however the supplement will still be payable each night.
    • This offer does not include drinks which are charged additionally.
    • On some evenings a set menu will be served buffet style. This includes a Berber Buffet, International Buffet and a weekly 6-course tasting menu.
    • Offer is subject to availability and applies to any new bookings of full board upgrades made for arrivals from 18th February until 21st December 2013, however, blackout dates may apply.
    • All our normal booking terms and conditions apply.
    • This offer can be combined with selected other offers and special packages.
