FLAVORS OF ITALY: VENICE, TUSCANY & LAKE GARDA

Programme Overview :

Venice, Verona & Lake Garda

  • Duration: 8 Days/7 Nights

    Highlights
    Cruise to a Torcello for a romantic dinner
    Visit to family home of Romeo & Juliet and Opera

    A&K Advantage
    Private gondolier rows and serenades through the Grand Canal
    Private moment together on Juliet's balcony in Verona

    Private arrival and departure airport transfers
    The services of an Abercrombie & Kent Guardian Angel

    * On arrival in each country, you are welcomed by your A&K Guardian Angel who conducts a briefing on your time in the country. A Guardian Angel is always at hand to provide unparalleled 24/7 support, be it local travel tips or reconfirming arrangement details, to providing recommendations for restaurants and shopping. Should you ever need help or advice, dial your Guardian Angel’s mobile phone for prompt and courteous service at any time.

  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE VENICE–Cruise to a Torcello for a romantic dinner

    On arrival, you are met by a Guardian Angel and transferred to your hotel, where you will be assisted with check–in. Speed across the lagoon to your palace hotel, conveniently located on the city's Grand Canal. A 14th–century Venetian palace adorned with masterpieces by the likes of Tiepolo – by private jetty.

    This evening, cruise on Venice’s famous canals as you pass the Santa Maraidella Salute Church and into the lagoon to toast the sunset and your romantic holiday with a glass of Veneto Prosecco. Reach the beautiful island of Torcello, where a table is set for you in the enchanting gardens at the Locanda Cipriani. Savor one of their specialties: Adriatic fresh oysters.

    Overnight: Ca' Sagredo
    Meal Plan: D

  • Day 2

    VENICE–Explore La Serenissima & a Gondola ride

    End your romantic adventure in one of the world's gorgeous cities & explore the less–visited side of "La Serenissima" (a fitting nickname for this most serene of Italian cities) alongside your Guardian Angel. Lose yourselves in the charming labyrinth of narrow streets and passageways in the old artisans' quarter, where postcard–perfect scenes await around every corner. Wander through the Frari Church, which houses the breathtaking masterpieces of Titian, Bellini and Canova.

    This evening, your private gondolier rows and serenades you through the Grand Canal and under the Rialto Bridge on to a restaurant of your choice.

    Overnight: Ca' Sagredo
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    VENICE–VERONA–Visit to family home of Romeo & Juliet and Opera

    You will be transported by a private vehicle to the Roman city of Verona, the site for Shakespeare's Romeo and Juliet. A visit to the family homes of the famous fictitious lovers will give you the chance for a private special moment together on Juliet's balcony. Possibly even a chance to renew your wedding vows at this truly romantic and special location. We could even create an extra special moment for you by arranging for a private serenade!

    Front–row seats at an epic opera performance al fresco in the Roman Arena, such as La Traviata (summer only) have been reserved especially for you.

    Retire for the night in the lavish surroundings of a 13th–century palace.

    Overnight: Due Torri
    Meal Plan: B, D

  • Day 4

    VERONA–LAKE GARDA–Sight seeing

    Travel by your private vehicle through central Italy and the Tuscan countryside, a land of rolling vineyards and charming villas. Explore the region around Cortona, setting similar to a romantic novel and film. Enjoy the spectacular beauty of Tuscany with your beloved.

    Another captivating place is at the border between Trentino, Veneto and Lombardy, the northern end of the lake which exhibits the evocative overhanging rocks of the Lombardy coast and the wonderful lakeshores of Veneto. Nature offers breathtaking views amidst high tree tops reflected in the deep blue of the lake.

    Time is at leisure to explore Lake Garda’s eclectic identity.

    Overnight: Hotel Lido Palace
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 5

    LAKE GARDA–Day at leisure and picnic

    Positioned on the dazzling gulf at the foot of Mt. Brione is Riva Del Garda. Explore its landscape and wide beaches which make it an ideal destination for a vacation.

    Spend time in the city center at leisure. The city center’s impressive architecture maintains a link with the past. Riva Del Garda is one of the most important historical centres on the shores of Garda.

    Lunch, head out to your own private spot with a picnic hamper in this truly amazing setting.

    Evening at leisure

    Overnight: Hotel Lido Palace
    Meal Plan: B, L

  • Day 6

    LAKE GARDA–Adventure activities

    Indulge in some Sailing & Windsurfing! The water facing the hotel and the town of Riva is prohibited for motor boats and reserved exclusively for navigation by sailboats and windsurfing; ensuring you enjoy the pleasure of the wind and the view all year long. You may take a short cracker course to get on for windsurfing.

    Afternoon is at leisure to explore the cities intriguing locations.

    Overnight: Hotel Lido Palace
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 7

    DEPART LAKE GARDA–MILAN

    After breakfast, you will be privately transported to the airport for your departure flight.

    Meal Plan: B

  • Possible Extension:
    3 Days MILAN

    Day 1

    MILAN–Private shopping tour

    Upon arrival you will be met by your guide & personal shopper for a private tour of the most renowned fashion houses of Milan. Visit Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II & indulge in retail pleasures!

    Museo Del Novecento, the sensational art museum of 20th–century houses the Giacomo Arengario. Since its opening in December 2010 it has rapidly established itself as a popular hotspot for Milan's art–and–fashion crowd.

    This evening, we could reserve space for you at Giacomo Arengario’s bar for dinner.

    Overnight: Four Seasons
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 2

    MILAN–Shopping & visit to The Martini Bar

    Spend the day at Corso Como. You will need at least half a day to look around Carla Sozzani's store. The store houses a vast assortment of fashion labels for both men and women, which is this ex–magazine editor’s collection. Collection of antique buttons, obscure perfumes and home wares is immaculate. They also offer bespoke service of an in–house tailor. It is a complete amalgamation of a music shop, art gallery, bookshop and restaurant.

    This evening you could visit Dolce & Gabbana’s famous Martini Bar which flaunts refined interiors and serves awesome aperitivi, including decorative capers kebabs and fresh bruschetta.

    Overnight: Four Seasons
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    DEPART MILAN

    This morning your chauffer will transfer you to the airport for your onward flight by your private vehicle.

    Meal Plan: B

TURKEY: TURKISH DELIGHT

Programme Overview

Istanbul, Izmir, Ephesus, Sirince and Cappadocia

  • Duration: 7 Days/6 Nights

    Highlights
    Reservations service at special restaurants
    Buyukada trip in a private boat
    Hands on Turkish cooking lesson with a local family
    Renewal of marriage vows in front of the Celsus Library
    Hot-air balloon ride over Cappadocia
    Dinner at Sur Balık Restaurant, Cappadocia
    A special massage in one of the hidden and mystic valleys of Cappadocia

    A&K Advantage
    Private sunset Bosphorus cruise followed by drinks and dinner on cruise with dinner
    Picnic barbecue lunch in Cappadocia via horse cart

    Private arrival and departure airport transfers
    The services of an Abercrombie & Kent Representative

    * On arrival in each country, you are welcomed by your A&K Representative who conducts a briefing on your time in the country. An A&K Representative is always at hand to provide unparalleled 24/7 support, be it local travel tips or reconfirming arrangement details, to providing recommendations for restaurants and shopping. Should you ever need help or advice, dial your A&K Representative’s mobile phone for prompt and courteous service at any time.

  • Detailed itinerary

    Day 1

    ISTANBUL–At leisure

    On Arrival at Istanbul, you will be met by your A&K Representative, who will provide VIP service for customs & immigration. You will then be privately transferred to your hotel and be assisted with check-in. En route to your hotel drive by the periphery of the world’s most striking city walls. Constructed between 413 and 447 by Theodosius II, they were the greatest fortifications built up to their time, stretching more than four miles from the Golden Horn in the north to the Sea of Marmara in the south. Four Seasons, Bosphorus is a palace beautifully modified into a chic retreat, stunningly landscaped & surrounded by the azure waters and the mountain tops.

    Day at leisure

    Dinner reservations can be made at Maiden’s (or Leander’s) Tower, where you may enjoy drinks and dinner on the first-floor restaurant or the café, Kiz Kulesi. Dinner could be followed by some wine with your loved one on the top floor of this light house with awe inspiring views against the dark at the Kuledebar. Rising up on an islet in the middle of the Bosphorus, this former Ottoman lighthouse has inspired not one, but two legends. We strongly recommend a visit for a truly romantic experience.

    Overnight: Four Seasons Hotel, Bosphorus
    Meal Plan: None

  • Day 2

    ISTANBUL–Sightseeing & Private sunset cruise

    Explore some of Istanbul's masterpieces of Byzantine and Ottoman architecture, beginning with the sixth-century Basilica Cistern. Visit the timeless Hagia Sophia, one of the greatest living examples of Byzantine architecture. Originally constructed as a church in 360, it has been a museum since 1935. The Turks governed most of the Mediterranean and western Orient from the 15th-century Topkapi Palace. Later proceed to the Pera district, the present-day heart of Istanbul.

    Early this evening, share memorable moments on a private sunset cruise of the Bosphorus. As you cruise along, view of the beautiful skyline is stunning with its splendid mansions, Judas blossoms, forts and palaces. To complete the day enjoy each other’s company over some wine, followed by dinner and entertainment all specially arranged for you on the cruise.

    Overnight: Four Seasons Hotel, Bosphorus
    Meal Plan: B, D

  • Day 3

    ISTANBUL–Trip to Buyukada & sightseeing

    You will be transported to Buyukada in a private boat. Buyukada is one of the Princes Islands in the Sea of Marmara. Cuddle up with your sweetheart in a horse-drawn buggy (private vehicles are not permitted on the island). Or stroll along the leafy boulevards lined with Ottoman-Victorian colonial houses. Enjoy a glass of wine at the café on the grounds of the famous St. George’s Church.

    After lunch, you will be taken on a private guided half day sightseeing tour (4 hour) with your A&K Representative. The 16th century was the Golden Age of the Ottoman Empire, and the era's master architect was Sinan. Visit his Suleymaniye Mosque, dominating the skyline with its magnificent dome. In the Ottoman world, pious mosque-school-bathhouse complexes sustained the social life of the community, and magnificent markets and bazaars powered the financial life. Visit Sinan's splendid Rustem Pasa Mosque, well-known for its Iznik tiles, located close to the eventful Spice Bazaar. Perhaps you can locate a souvenir or two in its 88 vaulted rooms. After strolling around the bazaar, stop for a special treat, taste of Istanbul's best baklava and the chance to meet the master pastry chef. No mall is as alluring as the 3,500-stall Grand Covered Bazaar, and you have an opportunity to walk through it looking for your own treasures.

    This evening, you could venture out to enjoy the famed nightlife of Istanbul.

    Overnight: Four Seasons Hotel, Bosphorus
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    ISTANBUL–IZMIR–EPHESUS–SIRINCE–Sightseeing, local cooking & renew your vows

    Fly to Izmir, the birthplace of Homer, and visit the remains of the great Ionian city of Ephesus, lying at the foot of Mount Pion. Unequaled as an archaeological site, the absolute size and grandeur of the ruins make it the best-preserved classical city in the eastern Mediterranean. Wander around the archaeological park, local museum and temple, and attend a private opening of the breathtaking Terrace Houses.

    For lunch, join a local family in their country restaurant for a hands-on lesson in Turkish cooking.

    Move ahead to the beautifully preserved village of Sirince, resting on a curved gorge that overlooks never-ending olive groves extending towards the Aegean Sea. Spend your night in an authentic cottage with brilliant views over the town, valley and surrounding mountains.

    Return to Ephesus in the evening for a delightful opportunity and poignant event: a chance to renew your marriage vows in front of the Celsus Library. Authenticated by a local priest with the Izmir State Symphony in the background, your wedding blessing becomes a romantic and memory to be cherished for a lifetime.

    Overnight: Nisanyan Houses
    Meal Plan: B, L

  • Day 5

    SIRINCE–IZMIR–CAPPADOCIA–Picnic lunch & romantic dinner

    Your private chauffer will drive you to the airport in Izmir for your private charter flight to Cappadocia. Millennia-long volcanic activity and erosion turned the volcanic remains into a soft porous stone named tuff, which form the stunning landscape of Cappadocia. The valleys of tuff occupied with "fairy chimneys" were developed by expert carvers into cave dwellings, monasteries, chapels and cellar villages.

    On arrival, you would be met & assisted to your luxurious traditional Cappadocia hotel, which is also partly carved into the tuff. Appreciate the antiques, elegant furniture, handmade wool and silk carpets that form a part of the interior décor throughout the property.

    At your own pace sightsee the rock-carved churches of Göreme, now an open-air museum displaying fine examples of ninth and tenth-century Byzantine wall paintings.

    Lunch, ride by horse cart to a quaint place in the country for a picnic barbecue. Feast on a mezze of salads, kebabs, fried chicken and lamb chops.

    We suggest a romantic dinner at Sur Balık Restaurant, which offers new tastes in Cappadocia. Talented executive chefs concoct the Anatolian specialties beside the famous Red River of Avanos.

    Overnight: The Museum Hotel
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 6

    CAPPADOCIA–Hot air ballooning

    Wake with sunrise and get on board for a thrilling hot-air balloon ride over Cappadocia, looking down over the magical landscapes. When you land, celebrate with a champagne toast. After a late breakfast, explore another wonder of the region: the "underground city" at Kaymakli. The wide network of rock-carved subterranean tunnels and hallways, storage and sleeping cells, airshafts and chapels, is more than 140 feet deep. With your private guide, follow a lighted route and descend to 70 feet to explore the mysterious passageways and dwellings.

    Lunch; join a local farmer and his family at their home for a taste of authentic and rustic cuisine.

    This afternoon, visit Göreme Village Weavers Association, where young girls weave traditional rugs at home.

    Or
    Evening, you can experience a special private massage session in one of the hidden and mystic valleys as part of our Valley Experiences. At offer are a variety of exclusive massages, such as our Ehl-i Massage, Cariye's Secret, Asian Silence, Sultan's Pleasure, Secret of Aphrodite, and Cappadocian Roses from menus (placed in your room)—all carried out with a professional and personal touch.

    Overnight: The Museum Hotel
    Meal Plan: B, L

  • Day 7

    DEPART CAPPADOCIA

    Board a private charter flight to Istanbul, where you connect with your departure flight home.

    Meal Plan: B
