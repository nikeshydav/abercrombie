Private Jet Travel with Abercrombie & Kent

Explore the Wonders of the World in Comfort and Style

I am delighted to announce the return of private jet travel with Abercrombie & Kent. Africa: Across a Continent by Private Jet represents the quintessential A&K travel experience: luxurious, authentic and packed with exclusive activities and encounters made possible through our local offices and longstanding connections in Africa. Our privately chartered jet takes off and arrives at your convenience, not on an airline's timetable, and your friendly cabin crew makes the jet aircraft a welcoming home away from home.

Making this journey even more extraordinary, I will host a three-day Serengeti safari, personally introducing you to the African lands where I grew up, and where A&K was born.

Whether you experience the wonders of a single continent or the entire world, you can count on a journey of uncompromising style and boundless inspiration. I hope to welcome you soon on an A&K journey by private jet.





Geoffrey Kent
Founder, Chairman & CEO
