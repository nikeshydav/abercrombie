Morocco: Medinas, Mountains and Monuments

Morocco is home to a delightful combination and mixture of African, Arabic and European cultures. Opening doors to the Mediterranean Sea, Africa and Europe find it easiest to connect via Morocco. Known for its tolerant nature, the land of Morocco shows an easy blend of cultures and religions. Holidays in Morocco will give you an insight into its mixed culture, art, food, music, architecture and their Moroccan hospitality.

A country where three M's- Medinas, Mountains and Monuments live in close contact, Morocco is a sheer delight for every traveller. Travel to Morocco to experience the everyday life at bazaars and souks in the old medinas while absorbing the rich legacy of historical monuments. Meet the friendly Moroccans and enjoy the ecstasy of stunning views of the Atlas Mountains, stroll in the warm waters of the Atlantic Ocean coastline and don't forget to see Sahara Desert as it has served as location for many Hollywood movies.

We at A&K ensure that our Morocco itineraries showcase the most historically-rich and significant aspects of the region's heritage. Stay at selected luxury hotels, exclusive riads or in our adventure-laden mobile desert camps in Sahara Desert for that kick. Locally-trained and friendly guides will take you on a tour of the country where you can view the ancient monuments, palaces and mosques while experiencing the richness of Morocco's natural beauty. Infusing the traditional with the contemporary, we create personalised itineraries.
