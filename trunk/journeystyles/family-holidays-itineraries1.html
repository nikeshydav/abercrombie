Family Kenya Safari

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 9 days/ 8 nights

    On Family Kenya, children enjoy an array of special programming and activities - such as mask-making, soccer games and kite-making - designed especially for their ages and interests.

    Highlights: NAIROBI| NANYUKI| SAMBURU GAME RESERVE| MASAI MARA GAME RESERVE

    A&K advantages

    • Enjoy the company of an A&K Young Explorers Guide, who travels with your group throughout the journey and arranges captivating activities for all ages
    • Explore Daphne Sheldrick's Orphanage, where you see young elephant lovingly interact with doting handlers
    • Hand-feed endangered giraffes at the Giraffe Center
    • Visit Kenya's only chimpanzee sanctuary in the Ol Pejeta Conservancy, which is also one of the largest rhino sanctuaries in East Africa
    • Meet the local Samburu people, who share their knowledge of fire starting, spear throwing and animal tracking, as well as bead and leather work
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    Arrive Nairobi

    Upon arrival in Nairobi, you are met by your A&K representative and assisted with transfer to your hotel.

    Overnight: Nairobi Serena Hotel or similar
    Meal Plan: None

  • Day 2

    Nairobi

    Begin your journey through the wilds of Kenya. Visit the Giraffe Center, a sanctuary for the rare Rothschilds giraffe. Feed them while perched upon a platform, watching as they gently snatch it from your hand with their long, blue tongues.

    Proceed to Daphne Sheldrick’s Orphanage, home to small elephants , who are reintroduced to the Tsavo National Park. Enjoy the views of elephant calves bathing in the mud hole and being bottle-fed by human surrogate mothers.

    Next is an Artisan visit at Kazuri, a jewelry workshop created by and for women with the mission of employing disadvantaged members of Kenyan society.

    This evening, join your fellow guests for a gala welcome dinner.

    Overnight: Nairobi Serena Hotel or similar
    Meal Plan: B D

  • Day 3

    Nanyuki

    Today, travel to the Sweetwaters Reserve within the Ol Pejeta Conservancy at the foot of Mount Kenya. Embark on a safari to view the exquisite animals in their natural habitat. You may also choose to take an escorted bush walk or thrilling night game drive at an additional cost.

    You may view game from your tent, which overlooks a flooded watering hole and salt-lick frequented by the local wildlife. Sweetwaters is an excellent place to see two of Africa’s most endangered species: black rhino and chimpanzee and can be viewed when you visit a chimpanzee and rhino sanctuary, set in conservancy.

    Behold the view of a sweet Baraka, a black rhino that you may feed from a protected platform. Enjoy the evening in your private veranda, viewing the nocturnal creatures moving in and out of the shadows to quench their thirst at the water’s edge.

    Overnight: Sweetwaters Serena Camp or similar
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 4

    Samburu Game Reserve

    Begin the day with a tour of the bush of Samburu Game Reserve, where you settle in a comfortable tented camp perched along the banks of the Ewaso Nyiro River. This river is the lifeline of the reserve and animals congregate along its banks.

    Led by the Young Explorers Guide, the young guests get a lot of interesting options to explore between game drives, including tree identification, mammal behavior studies and identification, an animal obstacle course, fire-making techniques and bush treasure hunts.

    Overnight: Samburu Intrepids or similar
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 5

    Samburu Game Reserve

    Start early today for a game drive; embark on a camel ride across the Ewaso Nyiro River. Watch for towering giraffes or tiny dik-diks.

    Arrive at the Samburu village, home to the nomadic Samburu people who live a pastoral, herding life. Here, experience the tribe’s unique culture and traditions.

    Return back to the camp and later, you may choose to take a guided game-tracking walk to explore the mesmerizing subtleties of the terrain. The young explores may enjoy themselves with entertainment activities of Soccer, treasure hunts, painting etc.

    Overnight: Samburu Intrepids or similar
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Days 6-8

    Masai Mara Game Reserve

    This day, take a flight to Masai Mara, an outstanding game reserve. After arrival, settle in your luxury tented camp.

    For the next three days enjoy your stay in the magnificent savanna country. Explore the game reserve with morning and afternoon game drives in search of Africa's "Big Five" - buffalo, elephants, rhinos, lions and leopards.

    Further, get the opportunity to learn the art of bead and leather work, as well as basic bush survival skills. Moving further, you may embark on an optional hot air balloon safari for a memorable experience.

    Later, visit a primary school supported by A&K Philanthropy, where your family may interact with students when school is in session.

    Tonight, experience the culture with a traditional Maasai song and dance performance. Conclude your journey with a special farewell dinner.

    Overnight: Mara Intrepids or similar
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 9

    SYDNEY

    Depart from the Coral Sea and take off for Sydney. Check into your hotel, which is strategically located near shopping-hubs, parks and museums. In the morning, receive a lesson in surfing at famed Bondi Beach, on an all-ages outing.

    Overnight: Sofitel Wentworth Sydney or Similar
    Meal Plan: B D (Day7), B L (Day8)

  • Day 9

    Depart Nairobi

    Today, travel back to Nairobi. After arrival, you will be escorted to your hotel for the day.

    Later this evening, proceed to the airport for your onward flight.

    Overnight: Nairobi Serena Hotel (dayroom) or similar
    Meal Plan: B

Family Tanzania Safari

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 8 days/ 7 nights

    On this all-ages adventure, enjoy game drives in Tanzania's greatest wildlife refuges, while children take part in a customized program of activities specially tailored to their ages and interests.

    Highlights: ARUSHA| LAKE MANYARA NATIONAL PARK| NGORONGORO CRATER| SERENGETI NATIONAL PARK

    A&K Advantages

    • Experience the best of northern Tanzania while staying in classic, family-friendly safari lodges set amid landscapes teeming with wildlife
    • Enjoy the services of a dedicated Young Explorers Guide, who travels with the group and organizes activities for all age levels
    • Embark on a family treasure hunt through a local market in the Ngorongoro Highlands
    • Visit a Maasai village where you meet with a village elder to learn about the tribe's traditional, nomadic people and also experience an Artisan Visit with a bead work demonstration
    • Children may participate in a special kids-only game drive during their stay in the Serengeti
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    Arrive Arusha

    After your arrival in Arusha, transfer to your lodge set on the leafy hillsides of Mount Meru, with views over coffee plantations and Lake Duluti.

    Depending on your arrival time, you will be joined by your A&K Young Explorers Guide, specially chosen for his or her rapport with young guests.

    Overnight: Lake Duluti Serena Hotel or similar
    Meal Plan: None

  • Day 2

    Lake Manyara National Park

    Begin your exploration with a travel overland to Lake Manyara National Park, a wildlife paradise. Embark on a thrilling morning game drive, with a chance to view hippos wallowing along the shore, pelicans scanning the surface, monkeys leaping between the trees and giraffes under the shade of a 1,000-year-old baobab.

    Proceed to your lodge, atop an escarpment overlooking the vast expanse of the Great Rift Valley.

    This afternoon, explore the place with an intriguing Market Visit to Mto wa Mbu, where the young explorers enjoy a treasure hunt through the market, setting off with money and a list of “treasures” to purchase; those who return with the most items (and remaining funds) are the winners.

    Later, return to your lodge for an exciting cooking demonstration, where families can participate with the chef and sample the local dishes they create together.

    Further, children may cool off and join the Young Explorers Guide for a swimming competition at the lodge pool. This evening, join fellow families and guests for a special welcome dinner.

    Overnight: Lake Manyara Serena Safari Lodge or similar
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 3

    Ngorongoro Crater

    This day, take a drive across the Tanzanian border to visit Ngorongoro Crater, a UNESCO World Heritage site, an extinct volcano, now a bucolic sanctuary supporting a wide array of wildlife.

    En-route enjoy lunch at the Gibb’s Farm followed by an exciting walk to nearby waterfalls. Proceed to your lodge perched on the rim of a collapsed, once-active volcano.

    Enjoy the enchanting scenic beauty of the 14-mile-wide caldera, home to the world’s highest-density of lions, also a haven for elephants, zebras, gazelles and hyenas. Children may join the Young Explorers Guide for crafts and games.

    Overnight: Ngorongoro Serena Safari Lodge or similar
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 4

    Ngorongoro Crater

    Today, descend the crater and its short grass prairies in search of lions, wildebeests and even rare, endangered black rhinos. Enjoy a picnic lunch before you set off for a visit to Maasai village. Here, you get an opportunity to know in-depth the tribe’s traditional, nomadic lifestyle and coexistence with wildlife as you interact with a village elder. Later, you may observe a demonstration of traditional bead work to appreciate the artistic work of local artisans.

    Overnight: Ngorongoro Serena Safari Lodge or similar
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 5

    Serengeti National Park

    Drive to Serengeti National Park, recently proclaimed a 7th world wide wonder. During the journey take a tour of the Olduvai Gorge, home to many significant archaeological discoveries.

    Later, settle in your lodge, set high on a hill with stunning views of the plains, and unwind in your comfortable, hillside accommodations. Later, take a game drive through the rambling park, site of the Great Migration. Here you may witness wildebeests and zebras in abundance, searching for fresh grass during the migration periods.

    Overnight: Serengeti Serena Safari Lodge or similar
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 6-7

    Serengeti National Park

    The next two days are for you to explore the arid, wildly beautiful Serengeti plains.

    Rise early for a first-light game drive across the atmospheric park to observe the animals in their natural habitat undertaking their morning regime. Look out for a sight of hippos returning to water after miles spent foraging for food overnight, elephants rustling through trees in search of breakfast, and sated lions, arriving back from a hunt with wide, yellow eyes.

    The young explorers may enjoy a special kids-only game drive, led by the Young Explorers Guide. They may also choose to take part in other activities such as butterfly catching, soccer and mask making.

    Spend an exploring afternoon with a game drive in search for elusive cheetahs, crocodiles and the striking leopard.

    Enjoy your last night here with your fellow guests at the gala farewell dinner.

    Overnight: Serengeti Serena Safari Lodge or similar
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 8

    Depart Arusha

    This day, depart for Arusha and proceed to relax in your dayroom before boarding your onward flight.

    Overnight: Lake Duluti Serena Hotel (dayroom) or similar
    Meal Plan: B

Amsterdam, Paris & London: A Family Journey

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 10 Days/ 9 Nights

    Enjoy kid-friendly activities in these three European capitals. Visit the house where Anne Frank lived, make a brief stop at the Louvre and dress up like a character from one of Shakespeare's plays.

    Highlights: Amsterdam| Paris| Belgium| London | HOLLAND

    A&K Advantages

    • Cruise the famous canals of Amsterdam for an engaging Neighborhood Connection
    • Sample traditional Dutch pancakes for lunch on a Culinary Connection
    • Learn how traditional wooden clogs are made
    • Join a local artist in Paris, who teaches you how to sketch
    • Travel in comfort on the Eurostar train between Paris and London, riding as the locals do
    • Take a thrilling flight on the London Eye
    • Visit Shakespeare's Globe Theater and dress up in costume as a character from one of the Bard's plays
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE AMSTERDAM

    Upon your arrival in Amsterdam, you are met and assisted with transfer to your hotel by an A&K representative. The rest of the day is at leisure.

    Tonight, you and your fellow travelers receive a warm welcome followed by dinner.

    Overnight: Sofitel Legend The Grand Amsterdam
    Meal Plan: D

  • Day 2

    AMSTERDAM

    Begin your exploration with a walking tour of the beautiful city of Amsterdam. Later, cruise through its canals. Continue to visit The Anne Frank House, a historic house and biographical museum dedicated to Jewish wartime diarist Anne Frank.

    Later, relish a lunch of Dutch pancakes .This afternoon, explore the place at leisure.

    Overnight: Sofitel Legend The Grand Amsterdam
    Meal Plan: B L

  • Day 3

    HOLLAND

    Today, take an excursion to Zaanse Schans Open-Air Museum, full of extraordinarily well preserved architecture and traditions. Witness marvelous examples of Holland’s signature windmills and also attend a wooden clog-making demonstration.

    Later, learn how the enormously popular Dutch cheeses and hand-painted pottery are produced. Return to Amsterdam for an afternoon at leisure.

    Overnight: Sofitel Legend The Grand Amsterdam
    Meal Plan: B D

  • Day 4

    PARIS

    Today, travel to Ghent, Belgium. After your arrival, enjoy lunch at a local restaurant. Explore the place with a short tour before you set off for Paris, the City of Light. Enjoy the interesting journey as you learn French from your Tour Director along the way.

    Overnight: Hotel du Louvre
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 5

    PARIS

    This morning, begin to explore Paris, where you enjoy the stunning sight of the Eiffel Tower. Continue to visit Arc de Triomphe, the most monumental of all triumphal arches and Champs Elysées, probably the most famous avenue in the world. Later, visit the Notre Dame Cathedral, the most famous of the Gothic cathedrals.

    Spend the rest of the day to explore Paris at your own convenience.

    Overnight: Hotel du Louvre
    Meal Plan: B L

  • Day 6

    PARIS

    Start your day with a visit to the awe-inspiring Louvre, originally a royal palace but now the world's most famous museum. Stroll through a local food market to experience the culture of the place.

    Later, board to cruise through the Seine River followed by dinner at a local restaurant.

    Overnight: Hotel du Louvre
    Meal Plan: B D

  • Day 7

    LONDON

    Today travel the north of Paris to reach the Montmartre District, Paris's most charming and quirky places to wander. Take a leisurely stroll in its winding back streets and later, join a local artist for a sketching lesson.

    Further, board the high-speed Eurostar to London.

    This evening, relish the famous fish and chips for supper at a local restaurant.

    Overnight: St. James Court, A Taj Hotel
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 8

    LONDON

    Explore London, starting with a tour of the Westminster Abbey, a Gothic church and continue to visit the Houses of Parliament and Buckingham Palace, the Queen's official London residence.

    Spend some time at the Trafalgar Square, located at the very heart of London, and move further to take a flight on the London Eye for spectacular panoramas of the city.

    Further, set off for an intriguing tour of the reconstruction of Shakespeare’s Globe Theater. Here, you learn about the significance of clothing in Elizabethan society and later, enjoy dressing up as a character from a Shakespeare play.

    Overnight: St. James Court, A Taj Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 9

    LONDON

    Today, visit the historic castle Tower of London and catch a glimpse of some of the dazzling jewels. Continue to view the Tower Bridge, one of the most recognizable bridges in the world, before visiting St. Paul’s Cathedral.

    Take a leisure stroll in the lively Covent Garden; also visit a sweet shop to observe the making of the rock candy.

    Tonight, celebrate the farewell dinner with your fellow travelers

    Overnight: St. James Court, A Taj Hotel
    Meal Plan: B D

  • Day 10

    DEPART LONDON

    After breakfast, you are assisted with transfer to the airport for your onward flight.

    Meal Plan: B

Antarctica, South Georgia & the Falkland Islands

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 17 days/ 16 nights

    Embark on luxurious 'Le Boreal' for an adventurous expedition across the Southern Ocean, stopping at wildlife-rich South Georgia, the unspoiled Falklands and the vast, wild shores of Antarctica.

    Highlights: Buenos Aires| Ushuaia| Falkland Islands| South Georgia| Antarctica

    A&K Advantages

    • Cruise for 15 nights aboard the luxury all-balcony expedition ship 'Le Boreal' on an exclusive A&K charter
    • Gain a deep understanding of the region with enriching lectures by award-winning experts, including scientists, authors, historians and professional photographers
    • Learn about the fascinating life of one of Antarctica's greatest explorers, Ernest Shackleton, when you visit his gravesite in Grytviken
    • Visit an active research station and observe preservation efforts to protect this one-of-a-kind habitat
    • Set off for exciting shore excursions in swift Zodiacs (sturdy inflatable boats), where closer views of the land and wildlife reveal the remarkable biodiversity of the Southern Ocean
    • Choose from two unique departures: a Family Holiday voyage that includes unforgettable winter holiday celebrations and landings, or a Photographic Expedition led by wildlife photographer Richard Harker and designed for all levels of interest and skill
    • Experience the benefits of all-inclusive cruising: on-board gratuities, unlimited house drinks and all port charges on 'Le Boreal' are included
    • Enjoy convenient A&K charter flights to and from Ushuaia
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    Arrive Buenos Aires

    Upon your arrival in Argentina’s capital city, you will be escorted by an A&K representative to your hotel. This afternoon, set off for an optional city tour of Buenos Aires exploring its wide boulevards and classical buildings.

    Begin with a visit to Plaza de Mayo, the main square; continue to the stately Casa Rosada. Later visit the Cabildo, once the royal government house, now a museum. Then, visit the fascinating Metropolitan Cathedral.

    Continue with a drive through the San Telmo neighborhood, the birthplace of tango to the colorful La Boca district.

    This evening, get to know your fellow guests at a welcome cocktail party.

    Overnight: Sofitel Buenos Aires or Four Seasons Buenos Aires (Categories 5-8)
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 2

    'Le Boreal'

    Today, Depart for Ushuaia, a busy port and adventure hub located at the southern tip of South America. Enjoy lunch after arrival and proceed for a guided trek (time and weather permitting) in the Cerro Alarken Nature Reserve or you may choose to simply stroll through the vibrant streets of Ushuaia.

    Further, board the luxurious ‘Le Boreal’ cruise. Settle in your cabin, with a private balcony to enjoy the spectacular views. Walk the decks and explore the common spaces as you familiarize yourself with the luxury cruiser, your home for the next 15 nights at sea.

    This evening, you may raise a toast with your fellow guests at the on-board bar.

    Overnight: 'Le Boreal'
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 3

    'Le Boreal'

    This day, sail eastwards to the Falkland Islands. Enlighten yourself with the expert informal series of lecturers and discussions regarding the flora, fauna, geology and history of the region. The lectures in the past have covered diverse topics such as seabirds and penguins of the Southern Ocean, the formation of sea ice over thousands of years, and personal accounts of living and working in Antarctica.

    On-board, you will be assisted by a professional photography enrichment coach, helping you with specific camera and composition queries, and delivering lead lectures on various skills.

    The cool waters in these parts are rich in krill and thus a food favorite with local wildlife. Alongside your naturalist guides at The Le Boreal’s deck, enjoy the view of various species of whales and seabirds

    Overnight: 'Le Boreal'
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 4

    'Le Boreal'

    Today, explore the Falklands. Depending on your itinerary, you either visit Stanley, the Falklands’ capital and stroll through its charming streets or set off to explore the surrounding wilderness areas.

    You may also choose to visit the southeast Falkland Islands of Sea Lion or Bleaker, home to unspoiled wildlife habitats.

    Overnight: 'Le Boreal'
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 5-6

    'Le Boreal'

    This day, as you cruise towards South Georgia, you experience a frequent deck-side view of whales and other wildlife. Enjoy an informative cruising journey with informative lectures and casual talks with your guides.

    On Day 6, scan the seas like an expert, as the ship cruises the rich waters along shag rocks.

    Overnight: 'Le Boreal'
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 7-9

    Ngorongoro Crater

    Begin with your exploration of South Georgia, a nearly submerged continuation of the Andes mountain range. Discover its glacier-carved coves and rugged shores. Proceed for a visit to Salisbury Plain, St. Andrews Bay or Gold Harbour, all home to vast colonies of king penguins as well as fur and elephant seals.

    If weather permits, you may visit the gravesite of Ernest Shackleton, the Antarctic explorer, in Grytviken guided along by your onboard historian, an expert on the great explorer’s life.

    Overnight: 'Le Boreal'
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 10-11

    'Le Boreal'

    Today, cruise the Scotia Sea, rich with marine mammals and fish. Recap the adventures in South Georgia with your expert lecturers onboard. Later, indulge in some more enriching insights on the soon approaching Seventh Continent, from the terrain to the hardy wildlife that inhabits this dramatic environment.

    Overnight: 'Le Boreal'
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 12-14

    'Le Boreal'

    This day, land on the Antarctic Peninsula, regarded by many visitors as one of the most beautiful places on earth. Also visit a number of South Shetland Islands, which may include Half Moon, Penguin and Deception.

    Other possible island visits, depending on your itinerary, may include Cuverville, Paulet and Goudier. You may also sail through Paradise Bay and the Lemaire Channel, often referred to as “Kodak Alley”; enjoy its breathtaking and specular beauty.

    Later, you may indulge in additional opportunities for exploration, including a stop at a scientific research station. Look out for splendid and rare views of whales and birdlife from the deck or your own private balcony.

    Overnight: 'Le Boreal'
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 15-16

    'Le Boreal'

    Today, conclude your exploration of Antarctica as you cruise back to Ushuaia through the Drake Passage. En-route you find more opportunities to observe marine and birdlife. Reflect on your incredible journey with fellow guests and new friends.

    Overnight: 'Le Boreal'
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 17

    Depart Buenos Aires

    This day, enjoy your breakfast and then disembark ‘Le Boreal’. Further, transfer to the airport for your onward flight.

    Meal Plan: B

    *Please note: This itinerary is provisional and subject to change due to ice, weather, wildlife and other conditions beyond A&K's control. The final itinerary will be determined at the sole discretion of the Captain and Expedition Leader.

  • Iguazu Falls Pre-Tour Extension

    Extension available on the Dec 19 and Jan 13 departures.

    Day 1

    Arrive Iguazu Falls

    Fly to Iguazu Falls, undoubtedly one of the most imposing natural attractions in Argentina and explore the Brazilian side.

    Later, join your guide on a tour through the national park to a catwalk within arm’s reach of the falls.

    Overnight: Hotel Das Cataratas

  • Day 2

    Iguazu Falls

    Today, you discover the Argentine side of the park as you walk through the trails above and along the falls.

    Further, you may opt to venture out on a catwalk that extends 3,600 feet over the tropical basin of Devil’s Throat. This afternoon is for you to explore or spend time at leisure.

    Overnight: Hotel Das Cataratas
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    Depart Iguazu Falls

    This day, transfer to the airport for your return flight to Buenos Aires.

    Overnight: Hotel Das Cataratas
    Meal Plan: B
