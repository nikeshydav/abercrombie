Turkey: Istanbul & the Aegean Coast

Programme Overview

Visit Hagia Sophia and Basilica Cistern, explore Topkapi Palace, see Cappadocia's "fairy chimneys," which you may choose to balloon over, boat on the Aegean Sea and enjoy the Whirling Dervish dance.

  • Duration: 11 days/ 10 nights

    Highlights: Istanbul | Cappadocia | Anakara Izmir | Ephesus

    A&K Advantages

    • Be treated to Istanbul's best baklava in the Spice Market and coffee in a local coffeehouse
    • Experience the Whirling Dervish, a frenetic dance designed to access the mystical realm
    • Visit one of Istanbul's historic culinary landmarks, the Vefa Boza
    • Travel like a local, aboard the local ferry, tram and funicular
    • Enjoy private boat ride on the beautiful Aegean Sea
    • Have lunch in a wonderful country restaurant with the local cuisine
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    Arrive Istanbul

    Upon your arrival in Istanbul you will be greeted by an A&K representative and assisted with transfer to your hotel

    Overnight: Grand Hyatt Istanbul or similar
    Meal plan: D

  • Day 2

    Istanbul

    Begin your travel in Istanbul with a visit to the Basilica Cistern, largest of several hundred ancient cisterns that lie beneath the city of Istanbul .Later, visit the magnificent Hagia Sophia, a great architectural beauty. Pass by the city walls that turned back Attila the Hun. Tour Chora Church (now a museum) before you make a leisure stroll in Pera to view its historic iconic buildings, In the evening, experience the culture by visiting a local coffeehouse and learning about the history of coffee .

    Overnight: Grand Hyatt Istanbul or similar
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 3

    Istanbul

    Today you’ll get the opportunity to explore the beautiful Topkapi Palace, largest and oldest palace in the world to survive to our day. Further, visit the extraordinary Dolmabahçe Palace, one of the most glamorous palaces in the world.

    At sunset, you may opt to take the ferry across the Bosphorus to enjoy the stunning panoramic views. Later, visit Kadikoy, to roam its atmospheric public market.

    Overnight: Grand Hyatt Istanbul
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 4

    Istanbul

    Explore the magnificent Suleymaniye Mosque an imperial mosque also visit the Vefa Boza, a local culinary landmark. Next on the schedule is your travel to Rustem Pasha Mosque and savor the city’s best baklava amid the aromas of the Spice Bazaar. Continue to one of the largest and oldest covered markets in the world, Grand Covered Bazaar.

    Later, enjoy a ride to Taksim, a major tourist and leisure district famed for its restaurants, shops, and hotels.

    Overnight: Grand Hyatt Istanbul
    Meal plan: B

  • Day 5

    Cappadocia

    Today depart for Cappadocia .After your arrival travel to visit buildings carved from "fairy chimneys." Enjoy a tour of Goreme Open Air Museum and an exclusive visit to the Dark Church. Later, closely observe the art of pottery at a potter's workshop and end the day with a Whirling Dervish performance.

    Overnight: Yunak Evleri
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 6

    Cappadocia

    Rise early today for an optional early morning trip in a hot air balloon. Afterward, visit the intriguing underground city of Kaymakli. Enjoy lunch with a local family. Later, get an opportunity to interact with the Goreme Village Weavers Association followed by a lecture on rug weaving in Turkey.

    Overnight: Yunak Evleri
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 7

    Anakara

    This morning, begin with a drive to Ankara admiring the journey through the ancient Silk Road, pausing at an authentic travelers rest. In Ankara, visit the Anatolian Civilizations Museum and tour the Old Citadel. Visit the Memorial of Ataturk, memorial to the first leader of the Republic of Turkey

    Overnight: Lugal, Ankara
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 8

    Izmir

    Fly this morning to Izmir and enjoy at a local winery. Further experience a culinary excursion and relish a country lunch and sample the region's famous olive oil. If time permits visit Clazomanae to witness the world's oldest olive oil press. Later, relax with a boat ride on the Aegean Sea and marvel the scenic beauty of the place.

    Overnight: Swissôtel Grand Efes
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 9

    Travel to Sardis to explore the ancient city. Behold the ruins of the Temple of Artemis, Continue to Tire and visit the local farmer's market. Where you can experience the local flavor and treats: kofte for lunch and tea at a kiraathane (tea house).

    Overnight: Swissôtel Grand Efes
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 10

    Ephesus / Wonders of the Ages

    Continue Istanbul’s excursion to visit the archaeological site of ancient Ephesus, also visit the Temple of Diana. Further, make a special permission exclusive visit to the recently excavated Terrace Houses to view their mosaic floors and frescoed walls. Later, enjoy a delicious lunch at a country restaurant while you meet and interact with the local couple that created a success story from their own kitchen. This evening join your fellow travelers for a farewell dinner.

    Overnight: Swissôtel Grand Efes
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 11

    Depart Izmir

    After breakfast, you will be assisted with transfer to the airport for your onward flight.

    Meal plan: B

Central Europe: Budapest, Vienna & Prague

Programme Overview

A charming journey on a private tour of Budapest's famed architectures, cruising through the Danube by twilight. Enjoy intriguing experiential sessions to learn about the art and culture and explore the cobbled streets in Prague.

  • Duration: 10 days/ 9 nights

    Highlights: Budapest | Vienna| Prague

    A&K Advantages

    • Enjoy a private visit to the Opera House in Budapest.
    • Take an evening cruise along the Danube River.
    • Learn the art of apple strudel baking at a demonstration by a professional pastry chef.
    • Attend a music lecture with Edward Kudlak, retired violist with the Vienna Philharmonic Orchestra.
    • Practice your waltz with a lesson in one of Vienna's most prestigious dance schools.
    • Take a private tour of the ornate library of the Strahov Monastery in Prague.
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE BUDAPEST

    On your Arrival in Budapest, you will be met by an A&K representative, who will assist with your transfer to the hotel.

    This evening, join your host and interact with your fellow travelers at a welcome dinner in the evening.

    Overnight: InterContinental Budapest or similar
    Meal plan: D

  • Day 2

    BUDAPEST

    Begin your exploration of Budapest, starting with a private tour of the palatial State Opera House.Further, Visit the ornate Dohány Street Synagogue, the vast Heroes' Square and St. Stephen's Basilica, the city's largest church.

    Head for lunch and thereafter ascend to Castle Hill, where the former Royal Palace gracefully towers over the Danube. Visit the Matthias Church, one of the best known landmarks of the city.Enjoy the mesmerizing views of the city and the river.

    Overnight: InterContinental Budapest or similar
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 3

    BUDAPEST

    This morning explore the place with a leisure stroll through the streets among the merchants and local shoppers in the Central Market Hall, at the Pest end of Liberty Bridge.

    After spending the afternoon at leisure, look forward for an enjoyable and relaxing private cruise along the Danube.

    Overnight: InterContinental Budapest or similar
    Meal plan: B

  • Day 4

    VIENNA

    Today, depart from Budapest for Vienna. During this beautiful travel you will visit Pannonhalma, where your local guide leads you on a tour of the 1,000-year-old Benedictine abbey.

    Later, enjoy lunch in the abbey's restaurant while sampling a range of their wines. Reach Vienna in the afternoon.

    Overnight:The Ritz Carlton Vienna or similar
    Meal plan: B

  • Day 5

    VIENNA

    Today, experience the fabulous imperial city of Vienna through a walking tour, along the famous Ringstrasse with its 19th-century buildings. Continue to visit the Hofburg Palace, once center of the gigantic Habsburg Empire.

    Further discover the Gothic St. Stephen's Cathedral, whose magnificent structure enshrines the heart of Vienna. The afternoon is for an experiential culinary session for an apple strudel baking and tasting with a professional pastry chef at Schönbrunn Palace.

    Overnight: The Ritz Carlton Vienna or similar
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 6

    VIENNA

    Celebrate the morning at leisure. Later, join Edward Kudlak, retired Vienna Philharmonic Orchestra violist, for an intriguing session on Vienna's incomparable musical heritage.

    The performance is but a prelude to this evening, where you take part in a private Viennese waltz lesson in one of the city's most prestigious dance schools.

    Overnight: The Ritz Carlton Vienna or similar
    Meal plan: B

  • Day 7

    PRAGUE

    Take a morning departure for Prague, En route visit the medieval Czech town of Telc, famous for its fine Gothic, Renaissance and Baroque buildings, where you may visit the abbey at your leisure.

    Further, proceed to Prague for an afternoon at leisure or you may take a short orientation walking tour with your Tour Director.

    Overnight: InterContinental Prague or similar
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 8

    PRAGUE

    Explore the scenic place with a walking tour of the Old Town through its cobbled streets and Jewish Quarter, birthplace of Franz Kafka. Continue to visit the famed Gothic Town Hall in Old Town Square.

    Later, visit the Jewish Museum, which houses the largest collection of Judaica in Europe, along with the Jewish Cemetery.

    Book your afternoon for an exciting excursion to Prague's Castle District and the Cathedral of St. Vitus. Accomplish your sightseeing tour with a visit to the Strahov Monastery, with its glorious Baroque decorations. Here, you’ll enjoy a private tour of the ornate library.

    Overnight: InterContinental Prague or similar
    Meal plan: B

  • Day 9

    PRAGUE

    This day, you will have the opportunity to experience in-depth two of the Czech Republic's best-known products: Bohemian crystal and Pilsner Urquell beer, where you are could relax and explore on your own.

    Tonight, savor the special farewell dinner with your fellow travelers.

    Overnight: InterContinental Prague or similar
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 10

    DEPART PRAGUE

    After breakfast, get assisted with your transfer to the airport for your flight home.

    Overnight: InterContinental Prague or similar
    Meal plan: B

World War II on the Rhine: 70 Years After Victory

Programme Overview

Cruise on the historic and exuberant Rhine River for seven nights. Experience a noted European art historian, disembarking for A&K-exclusive excursions to Arnhem, Cologne, Remagen and Heidelberg.

  • Duration: 10 days/ 9 nights

    Highlights: Basel | Strasbourg | Heidelberg | Remagen | Cochem | Cologne | Amsterdam

    A&K Advantages

    • Cruise for seven nights on the historic Rhine River, World War II's most strategic natural barrier, including four poignant A&K exclusive shore excursions
    • Travel with esteemed resident A&K Tour Director, Dr. James McKenzie-Hall, a noted European art historian and lecturer
    • Visit the Resistance Museum and the historic Anne Frank House in Amsterdam
    • Explore Arnhem and Remagen, sites of crucial bridgehead battles, and Cologne, painstakingly restored after the war
    • In Heidelberg, see a hidden amphitheatre and visit the cultural center to learn the wartime plight of the Sinti and Roma peoples
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE AMSTERDAM

    Upon arrival in Amsterdam you will be met by an A&K representative, who will assist with transfer to the hotel

    This evening, join your fellow guests and A&K group leader for a welcome dinner.

    Overnight: Sofitel Legend The Grand Amsterdam
    Meal plan: D

  • Day 2

    AMSTERDAM

    Start your day with a visit to view exhibits depicting life during World War II in Amsterdam at the Resistance Museum.

    Later, take a walk through the Jewish Quarter, Continue to visit the Anne Frank House, a historic house and biographical museum dedicated to Jewish wartime diarist Anne Frank.

    Break for lunch before you explore the National Monument of Dam Square, commemorating the city’s liberation in 1945.

    Overnight: 'Amadeus Silver II'
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 3

    AMSTERDAM

    Today, take an A&K-exclusive excursion to tour the site of Operation “Market Garden” at Arnhem.

    Later, visit the Hartenstein Airborne Museum, dedicated to the Battle of Arnhem in 1944 and Oosterbeek War Cemetery, Commonwealth War Graves Commission cemetery

    Overnight: 'Amadeus Silver II'
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 4

    COLOGNE

    Set forth on an A&K-exclusive excursion to once war-ravaged Cologne.

    Explore the place with a visit to its restored Gothic cathedral and a former Gestapo prison. Cologne is a major cultural center for the Rhineland. Appreciate the Roonstrasse Synagogue, one of the five pre-Nazi synagogues of the locality, now the only Jewish house of prayer restored to its previous splendor.

    Overnight: 'Amadeus Silver II'
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 5

    COCHEM

    Today, after your arrival in Cochem on the Moselle River, either take an optional walking tour with a wine tasting session or explore the city at your own pace.

    Later, enjoy the evening with an exclusive cocktail party in the ship’s Amadeus Club.

    Overnight: 'Amadeus Silver II'
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 6

    REMAGEN

    Begin with an A&K-exclusive journey from Koblenz to Remagen, site of the bridgehead captured by the Allies in 1945. Later, discover the Peace Museum housed in the two remaining bridge towers.

    Continue to Rüdesheim, where you may opt to embark on a musical adventure into the world of historical automated instruments at the Siegfried’s Mechanical Music Museum.

    Overnight: 'Amadeus Silver II'
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 7

    HEIDELBERG

    Today, enjoy another A&K-exclusive shore excursion with a travel to Heidelberg. Explore a hidden amphitheater built in 1935 for party rallies.

    Experience the old town before you relish lunch at a local restaurant.

    Continue your journey to see the cultural center and learn about the wartime persecution of the Sinti and Roma peoples.

    Conclude your day with a gala dinner aboard ship with the captain and your fellow travelers.

    Overnight: 'Amadeus Silver II'
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 8

    STRASBOURG

    This morning, you may explore the historical and cultural sights of Strasbourg with an optional city tour.

    Take a walk through the charming old town and also visit its magnificent cathedral.

    Overnight: 'Amadeus Silver II'
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 9

    BASEL

    Upon your arrival in Basel, visit its splendid cathedral and vibrant local market.

    Enjoy your lunch and then, savor chocolate tasting at a local chocolatier workshop.

    Tonight, relish a farewell dinner along with your fellow travelers.

    Overnight: Hotel Euler
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 10

    DEPART BASEL

    After breakfast, Set off for a transfer to Basel airport for your onward flight.

    Meal Plan: B

    NOTE: Timings and pre-/post-cruise sightseeing may vary. Please contact A&K for details.

Sri Lanka: Pearl of the Indian Ocean

Programme Overview

Marvel at Sri Lanka's many stunning UNESCO World Heritage Sites; explore the Galle Fort with a local author; stroll through Pettah Bazaar with a Colombo resident; and wander through the Royal Botanic Gardens alongside a botanist.

  • Duration: 11 days/ 10 nights

    A&K Advantages

    Discover your destination through these local connections.

    • Visit several UNESCO World Heritage Sites on a single itinerary
    • Explore the Galle Fort alongside a published author
    • Join a Colombo native on a walking tour through the Pettah Bazaar
    • On our Artisan Connection, hear a unique perspective on island life during a visit with an urban artist
    • Explore the Royal Botanic Garden alongside a botany professor
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE IN COLOMBO

    After your arrival at the Colombo airport, you will be escorted by an A&K representative to your hotel. Tonight, enjoy a warm welcome followed by dinner with your fellow travelers.

    Overnight: The Kingsbury
    Meal Plan: D

  • Day 2

    COLOMBO / Traditional Village Life

    Begin your journey to experience the traditional culture of Srilanka with a visit to a mini replica of a traditional Sri Lankan village.

    Continue for a guided walking tour of Colombo, visit bustling Pettah Bazaar, a series of open air bazaars and markets. Next, take a tour of the Dutch colonial mansions observing its gambrel roofs with curved eaves along the length of the house. Continue to the Colombo Fort and the Old Dutch Hospital, now an entertainment and dining complex.

    Overnight:The Kingsbury
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    GALLE / Dutch Fortress

    Today, explore the marvelous city of Galle, a UNESCO World Heritage site, visiting its Dutch fortress alongside a resident author. Later, interact with the merchant families to learn about the spice-trading history.

    Next is a visit to the inland tropical rainforest of Sinharaja, where you check into your eco-retreat.

    Enjoy the evening with a dinner in the serene surroundings along with your fellow travelers.

    Overnight:The Rainforest Ecolodge
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 4

    SINHARAJA / Lush Rainforest

    This day, explore the splendid Sinharaja, a UNESCO Biosphere Reserve with rare and endemic mammals and butterflies, prolific birdlife and an endless array of insects, reptiles and amphibians.

    This afternoon, you may go ahead to form an authentic neighborhood connection by visiting and interacting with a local family in the village over a cup of tea.

    End your day with dinner at the hotel.

    Overnight: The Rainforest Ecolodge
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 5-6

    KANDY / The Royal Botanical Gardens

    This day, take a beautiful drive through the hills to arrive at the cultural heritage city of Kandy.

    The next day, explore the orchid-rich Royal Botanical Gardens with a botany professor. Further, visit the stunning 17th-century Sacred Temple of the Tooth, home to the Sacred Tooth Relic of Lord Buddha. Later, interact with a professional urban artist to share a unique perspective on the island. Continue to explore the local market, form a culinary connection by attending a cooking class.

    Overnight: Amaya Hills
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 7

    Koblenz & Rüdesheim

    Reach Koblenz this morning and choose either from an optional walking tour or explore independently. This afternoon, enjoy a relaxing cruise on a picturesque stretch of the Rhine, viewing the hilltop castles, terraced vineyards and charming villages on your way. Conclude your day at Rüdesheim to visit Siegfried's Mechanical Music Museum.

    Overnight: Amadeus Silver
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 8

    NUWRA ELIYA / Scenic City

    Today, travel to the famous tea region of Nuwara Eliya, one of Sri Lanka’s highest towns surrounded by lush green hills. Visit its produce markets before you view the picturesque Gregory Lake.

    Later, visit the sacred Seetha Amman Temple. Further, hop on a train for a scenic ride from Nanuoya to Peradeniya, en-route admiring the lush green tea estates. Arrive at your hotel and enjoy dinner with fellow guests.

    Overnight: Amaya Hills
    Meal Plan: B, D

  • Day 8

    MATALE / Heady Spices

    This morning, explore the ancient spice city, Matale. After arrival, check into your hotel in Dambulla.

    Set out to hike the Sigiriya Rock Fortress, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Admire its astonishing feat of engineering and construction along with the marvelous frescoes, Lion Gate, water gardens and boulder gardens.

    Overnight: Amaya Lake Hotel
    Meal Plan: B, D

  • Day 9

    POLONNARUWA / Seat of the Ancient Kingdom

    Begin the day for a journey to Sri Lanka's splendid medieval capital, Polonnaruwa, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Continue to Minneriya National Park, site of a prolific bird population as well as “The Gathering,” an annual wildlife spectacle, during which elephants arrive en masse to feast.

    Overnight: Amaya Lake Hotel
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 10

    SEEDUWA / Tranquil Relaxation

    This day, travel to Seeduwa. After arrival, continue to explore a peaceful coconut plantation along the Negombo Lagoon.

    Spend the remaining day to explore the place at leisure. Tonight, join your fellow guests for a farewell dinner.

    Overnight: The Gateway Hotel
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 11

    DEPART SEEDUWA

    After breakfast, transfer to the airport for your onward flight.

    Meal Plan: B
