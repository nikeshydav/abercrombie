Romantic Fiji Honeymoon

Programme Overview

3 Nights Castaway Island, Fiji /4 Nights Taveuni Island Resort & Spa, Fiji

  • Duration: 8 Days/7 Nights

    Highlights
    A private dinner under the stars will be set up for you with local dancers & musicians
    Island hopping excursion and a Dolphin Safari
    Private picnic on a secluded beach
    Sundowner at the deck and a special couple’s massage
    Private charter flight from Castaway Island to Taveuni Island Resort & Spa
    Tour of the West Coast and a visit to the dateline
    A four wheel drive to Lake Tagimoucia - an extinct volcanic crater

    A&K Advantage
    Be serenaded at a private dinner under the stars

    Private arrival and departure airport transfers
    The services of an A&K Representative

    * On arrival in each country, you are welcomed by your A&K Representative who conducts a briefing on your time in the country. A A&K Representative is always at hand to provide unparalleled 24/7 support, be it local travel tips or reconfirming arrangement details, to providing recommendations for restaurants and shopping. Should you ever need help or advice, dial your A&K Representative’s mobile phone for prompt and courteous service at any time.

  • Detailed Itinerary:

    Day 1

    ARRIVE FIJI

    Arrive at the Nadi International Airport. Your English speaking local representative will welcome you with a “Bula” and escort you for your transfer to Denarau Marina. You are assured your short (approx 30mins) trip to the Denarau Marina will be a safe and pleasant one.

    Launch transfer Denarau Marina to Castaway Island

    Indulge yourself with a relaxing luxury catamaran transfer from Denarau Marina to Castaway Island Resort which is located just west of the largest Island of Fiji. It is approximately a 2hour transfer from the mainland to the island. Enjoy the cruise with the amazing fresh sea breeze and breathtaking views along the way to the island.

    Overnight: Castaway Island, Fiji

  • Day 2

    CASTAWAY ISLAND, FIJI

    Castaway Island is surrounded by white sand beaches, vibrant coral reefs with azure waters & also covered in rich tropical rainforest and is spread over 174acres! As you arrive at this paradise island resort; you will be welcomed by their friendly staff with a chilled welcome drink.

    For rest of the day, you could simply lay back on the beach or in a hammock and soak in the beauty & tranquility of the island which almost seems surreal. Alternately, you could enjoy many of the activities available for the young and the young at heart. With unrestricted use of all non-motorised water sports equipment including snorkeling equipment, catamarans, kayaks, windsurfers, paddleboards, spy boards and more, you would be spoilt for choice!

    You could also indulge in a catamaran sailing later this afternoon & view the coral through a glass bottom boat.

    This evening, a private dinner under the stars will be set up for you with local dancers & musicians who will serenade you through the evening, making this one of your most romantic dinners!

    Overnight: Castaway Island, Fiji
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 3

    CASTAWAY ISLAND, FIJI

    This morning, join the Island hopping excursion and a Dolphin Safari by speed boat exclusively for you.
    Enjoy a private picnic that would be set up for you at a secluded beach. Indulge in some local delicacies with champagne & enjoy some great quality time together.

    Enjoy a sundowner at the deck and return to your room for a special couple’s massage to end a perfect day of fun in the sun!

    Overnight: Castaway Island, Fiji
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 4

    TAVEUNI ISLAND RESORT & SPA, FIJI

    Helicopter Transfer from Castaway Island to Taveuni Island Resort & Spa

    After breakfast, it’s time to board a private charter flight from Castaway Island to Taveuni Island Resort & Spa, considered to be the Jewel of the Pacific. This will be an amazing opportunity for you to view the stunning scenes of the mainland from a perfect bird’s eye view.

    Known as the ‘Garden Island of Fiji’, Taveuni is Fiji’s third largest island. It is 62.4 kilometres long, 11.2 kilometres wide and 1,218 metres high and offers some amazing experiences like the Cascading waterfalls at Bouma and Levena, Scenic lookouts, Rainbow Reef corals with Great White Wall dive sites & Lagoons. In fact this is also famed for being the location of the second Blue Lagoon movie!

    There are blowholes and a fantastic natural rock waterslide - one will never cease to be amazed by the beauty of this island!

    Overnight: Taveuni Island Resort & Spa, Fiji
    Meal plan: B

  • Day 5

    TAVEUNI ISLAND RESORT & SPA, FIJI

    Taveuni has the unique distinction of being the first place on this planet to experience the birth of the New Millennium. Start your day with a tour of the West Coast which begins with a visit to the dateline (180 meridians) - where you can stand in both today, and yesterday! Take a four wheel drive to Lake Tagimoucia which is an extinct volcanic crater at the highest point of Taveuni.

    This evening, you can choose to dine privately… either enjoy an intimate romantic dinner for two at the lagoon bure, under the stars by the pool, on the beach with a personal waiter and musician, or in the privacy of your own luxury bure.

    Overnight: Taveuni Island Resort & Spa, Fiji
    Meal plan: B

  • Day 6

    TAVEUNI ISLAND RESORT & SPA, FIJI

    Explore the eastern side of the island today and visit the amazing Bouma National Heritage Park.
    Walk along & see three amazing waterfalls with lush vegetation and an unbelievably beautiful grotto. Take a swim in the huge natural rock pool: dive under, into and behind this large volume of cascading water. On your way up to the second waterfall, there is a lookout which gives you a 360 degree to view the coast and the islands of Matagi, Qamea and Laucala, and up into the high peaks and ranges of Taveuni mountainous landscape creating a vista of lush tropical beauty.

    Enjoy a picnic lunch on the rock & make your way to the beautiful white sand beach of Lavena. You could hike through the stunning coastal scenery and enjoy the beauty of this wild foreshore. You come across a swinging bridge that crosses a deep river canyon. You could swim to a cool deep pool surrounded by high rock walls and lush rainforest. As you swim deeper into the grotto, two beautiful waterfalls cascade. You could climb up onto them and slide or jump into the deep water below.

    Overnight: Taveuni Island Resort & Spa, Fiji
    Meal plan: B

  • Day 7

    TAVEUNI ISLAND RESORT & SPA, FIJI

    The day is yours to explore at your own pace!
    You could either choose to indulge in some more soft adventure activities or just relax & enjoy some of the resorts other facilities & features.

    Overnight: Taveuni Island Resort & Spa, Fiji
    Meal plan: B

  • Day 8

    Depart

    Luxury Private Vehicle Transfer Taveuni Island Resort & Spa to Nadi Airport.

    Check out of the hotel and depart on your private charter for a 90 minute flight directly to Nadi Airport and connect to your onward flight.

Tahiti Bora Bora

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 8 Days/7 Nights

    Highlights
    Lagoon cruise with shark & ray discovery
    Jet Ski around Bora Bora lagoon
    Pearl Diving and tour of Pearl farm
    Gourmet Picnic
    Helicopter tour
    Discovery of Bora Bora Island in a 4x4
    Sunset & Romantic dinners

    A&K Advantage
    Private arrival and departure airport transfers
    The services of an A&K Representative

    * On arrival in each country, you are welcomed by your A&K Representative who conducts a briefing on your time in the country. An A&K Representative is always at hand to provide unparalleled 24/7 support, be it local travel tips or reconfirming arrangement details, to providing recommendations for restaurants and shopping. Should you ever need help or advice, dial your A&K Representative’s mobile phone for prompt and courteous service at any time.

  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE TAHITI–At leisure

    On arrival you will be welcomed by your A&K Representative with flower lei, document remittance and transfer to your resort in a private vehicle. Check in to your exclusive room.

    Relax and treat yourselves to a private couple’s traditional Polynesian massage. Many of the exotic spa preparations used employ native, natural ingredients like indigenous fruit, flowers and Tahitian vanilla, as well as the mineral-rich black sand of Matavai Bay.

    Based on the time of arrival you may proceed directly to Bora Bora

    Overnight: Radisson Plaza Resort
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 2

    TAHITI–BORA BORA–Welcome with champagne and canapés

    You will be transferred to Bora Bora by a private charter. Upon arrival, the resort staff will welcome you directly at the airport and take you by a private boat to the resort. The resort overwater villas face the main island of Bora Bora and are the biggest area wise in the Pacific. The villas are designed aesthetically in exotic woods and marble; and feature a private terrace and gazebo which convert the villas into islands of tranquility and romance.

    At sunset, saber a bottle of Champagne on the terrace of your villa. Your dedicated waiter will serve your along with an assortment of cold canapés to add to the romance of the view.

    Overnight: St Regis Bora Bora
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    BORA BORA–Lagoon cruise with shark & ray discovery and Lobster Champagne lunch on a motu

    Today morning you will be taken for a private cruise around the lagoon. Explore the lagoon and to add to the adventure you will go snorkeling. The boat will cruise you to the most stunning spots of the lagoon where you may feed rays, watch sharks & swim in a coral garden. It will also be an amazing opportunity to capture these beautiful sights in a camera. Your morning will end with a stop on an islet for some fresh fruits and juice

    Cruise ahead for the tour of the island. While getting enticed by the island’s exotic charm stop by for a Lobster Champagne lunch on a motu.

    Arrive at the villa at leisure.

    Overnight: St Regis Bora Bora
    Meal Plan: B,L

  • Day 4

    BORA BORA–Pearl diving

    Today you will visit the pearl farm located at the main island for a unique pearl diving experience. You will have the opportunity to find the symbol of your eternal commitment as you dive to the bottom of the lagoon to find a pearl. This will be your own pearl, yours to keep and symbolic of your eternal love. You will be taken on a guided tour around the farm.

    You can also get a unique, unduplicated creation of jewelry designed and created just for you on the spot. You hand pick the design, mounting and the precious metals that will mirror your personality, sit back and watch your peerless, original Tahitian pearl jewelry take shape.

    Evening at leisure

    You may enjoy a romantic and an Intimate candlelit Gazebo dinner on our main beach which will be setup privately and exclusively for you.

    Overnight: St Regis Bora Bora
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 5

    BORA BORA–Guided tour by Jet Ski, Gourmet picnic & helicopter tour

    Explore the island in a Jet Ski. You will be amazed by the stunning views of the world’s most gorgeous and romantic lagoon. See the water take on various shades of blue & turquoise while you follow your private guide on your own Jet Ski*.

    Stop by on a white sandy beach where some fresh fruits, assortment of macaroons, chocolate-dipped strawberries with whipped cream will be served.

    On the second stop on a motu (small islet), a coconut show will be set up by your guide who will demonstrate how to climb up the coconut tree and crack coconut. Relax and enjoy a private romantic gourmet picnic lunch along with freshly opened coconut water.

    Early evening take your loved one on a private helicopter flight over Bora Bora. You may surprise your partner and add an element of romance by requesting for maybe a “Love You” to be written with coconuts on a white sand beach which you both will fly over and discover.

    *One or two persons per Jet Ski

    Overnight: St Regis Bora Bora
    Meal Plan: B,L

  • Day 6

    BORA BORA–Discovery of Bora Bora Island in a 4x4

    At your pace take a tour of the land side of the island in a 4X4. Explore the culture, history, fauna and flora of the island. Discover the ancient temples built around 1000AD & get to know from the natives about the local legends visiting ancient archeological sites such as the Marae "temple", and the Turtle stone, located in the rain forest.

    When you visit Faanui valley you’ll be able to identify small fruit plantations. Explore the local flora including medicine plants used by the ancient Medicine man, such as the Nono and the Hotu tree. Bora Bora was used by the American as a staging area during WWII. This not only influenced the locals but there are major remnants of the same. Sightsee the WWII sites of the Cannons and the bunkers. While travelling around the island sightseeing you will come across many look out points which will give stunning picturesque views of the lagoon.

    Tonight relish an exclusive A la Folie dinner at the Spa Beach of the resort under the star lit sky.

    Overnight: St Regis Bora Bora
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 7

    BORA BORA–Rediscover romance on a secluded corner of paradise

    Enjoy a complimentary Bubble bath with rose petals, and candles. To make the day special you may enjoy your breakfast in bed.

    Noon you will be whisked away on a private boat to a secluded corner of paradise (30 minutes from the resort) providing you complete intimacy. You will start off with a hobbie cat ride over the turquoise lagoon.

    On return Sushi Champagne lunch on the private islet will await you.

    Later an outdoor couples’ massage will be all set at the islet. You and your partner can relax and rejuvenate before returning to the resort. Jet Ski or a private boat may be used for a ride back to the resort at sunset.

    On return to the hotel room you will be welcomed with candles and roses petals for a romantic ambience.

    Overnight: St Regis Bora Bora
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 8

    DEPART BORA BORA

    You will be privately transferred to Papeete in style for your flight.
