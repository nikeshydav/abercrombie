Russia: A Family Journey

Programme Overview

Explore Russia, enjoying activities for all ages. Visit the Kremlin Armory Museum before hours, go backstage at the Moscow Circus and learn to paint Fabergé-style eggs in St. Petersburg.

  • Duration: 8 days/ 7 nights

    Highlights: Moscow | St. Petersburg

    A&K advantages

    • Join your Tour Director for a lesson in speaking Russian, and then practice your language skills during a ride on the renowned Moscow Metro
    • Take part in a re-creation of a traditional Russian wedding ceremony, and be entertained by a folk group with authentic Russian songs and games for a fun Musical Connection
    • Go on a backstage tour of the Moscow Circus, and then enjoy the performance from your VIP seat
    • Attend a master class in painting Fabergé eggs taught by a famous St. Petersburg artist
    • Avoid the crowds with an exclusive before-hours visit to the Kremlin Armory Museum
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE MOSCOW

    Upon your arrival in Moscow, capital of Russia, you are met and assisted with transfer to your hotel by an A&K representative.

    Tonight, you and your fellow travelers receive a warm welcome followed by dinner.

    Overnight: Marriott Moscow Royal Aurora
    Meal plan: D

  • Day 2

    MOSCOW

    Begin your journey with a tour of Moscow. Start with an exclusive (before official hours) visitto the Kremlin and witness the State Armory. Discover its impressive collection of carriages, royal gala clothes and fabulous Fabergé eggs.

    Later, visit two majestic Kremlin cathedrals: Assumption and Archangel. Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant and set forth to attend a Russian language lesson with your Tour Director.

    Continue to explore your newfound language skills while you take a ride on the Moscow Metro.Appreciate the beauty of the metro stations,with their reflective marble walls, high ceilings and grandiose chandeliers.

    Overnight: Marriott Moscow Royal Aurora
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 3

    MOSCOW

    Today, visit the stunningKolomenskoye Estate, a unique historic place in the most scenic corner of Moscow. Later, take a leisure walk to the nearby Peter the Great's cottage and join in a re-creation of a Russian wedding ceremony.

    Further, depart for the Moscow Circus in the afternoon. Here, you interact with the artists in their dressing rooms and watch gymnasts during warm ups.Look forward toenjoy the show on your especially reserved private box seats.

    Overnight: Marriott Moscow Royal Aurora
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 4

    MOSCOW

    This day, explore the New Maiden Cemetery,the best-known cloister of Moscow and the last resting place of many Russian luminaries.

    Further,embark on a high-speed train,Sapsan, to reach St. Petersburg,often called “Venice of the North”. The lunch will be served onboard.

    On arrival, you will be assisted with transfer to your hotel,where you will be served your dinner Later, experience the beauty of the place with a cruise of the rivers and canals of St. Petersburg.Pass by the Church of the Resurrection of Christ,a fine example of late 19th-century Russian architecture, and marvel its extraordinary exteriors.

    Overnight: Marriott Moscow Royal Aurora
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 5

    ST. PETERSBURG

    Start your exploration for the day with a tour of the exalting Hermitage, home to three million pieces of artwork dating back to the Stone Age.

    Enjoy your lunch and set off to attend an intriguing lesson in painting Fabergé-style eggsby a famous St. Petersburg artist. Later, you get the opportunity to learn the history and try your hand at the unique Russian art form.

    Continue with a stroll ofthe famous NevskyProspekt, then visit the Palace Square, a magnificent square surrounded by monumental buildings and further explore the Vasilevsky Island.

    Visit St. Isaac’s Cathedral to appreciate its gilded dome, sculptures and red granite columns that grace its façade. Explore the place by observing its interiors, intricately designed and decorated with mosaics, icons and columns of malachite and lapis lazuli.

    Overnight: KempinskiMoika 22
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Days 6

    ST. PETERSBURG

    This morning, take a beautiful drive to Peterhof, perhaps St Petersburg's most famous and spectacular Imperial estate. Take an exclusive tour of the Grand Palace, which is opened for you before official operating hours, so you avoid the crowds.

    Later, embark on a hydrofoil for a return journey to St. Petersburg, where you enjoy your lunch. Next, tour the fascinating Peter & Paul Fortress. Its cathedral is the burial place of many tsars and tsarinas, including Peter the Great.

    Overnight: KempinskiMoika 22
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 7

    ST. PETERSBURG

    Today, travel to TsarskoeSelo , the most beautiful suburb of St.Petersburg. Continue to visit the Catherine Palace, with its formal parks that stretch to Rastrelli’s Hermitage building, and the world-famous Amber Room, now restored to its original glory.

    Relish a traditional, hearty Russian lunch at Podvorie Dacha restaurant .Later, return to St. Petersburg where you join your Tour Director and fellow travelers for a farewell dinner.

    Overnight: KempinskiMoika 22
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 8

    DEPART ST. PETERSBURG

    After breakfast, transfer to the airport for your onward flight.

    Meal plan: B

Family Alaska

Programme Overview

View Alaska's pristine wilderness (populated by moose, bears, humpback whales and sea lions) on an adventure-packed journey that includes, fishing, geocaching and taking a scenic flight over Denali.

  • Duration: 8 days/ 7 nights

    Highlights: • See calving glaciers and marine wildlife, such as humpback whales, orcas, sea lions and harbor seals, on a Kenai Fjords cruise
    • Take a scenic train ride on the Alaska Railroad GoldStar Dome train from Seward to Girdwood
    • Experience a private wildlife rafting trip and riverside barbecue dinner at Kenai National Wildlife Refuge
    • See wild bears as they fish for spawning salmon - a unique opportunity - at Redoubt Bay
    • Embark on a private salmon fishing trip on the Susitna River
    • Flight-see over Denali National Park, landing on a glacier at the base of Mount McKinley
    • Meet with Martin Buser, a four-time Iditarod champion, and his team of huskies

  • Day 1

    Anchorage, Alaska/Kenai Peninsula

    Arrive in Anchorage to begin your adventure, complete with glacier trekking and wildlife encounters in America's unspoiled wilderness. At a welcome reception this evening, meet and get to know the families who share your journey.

    Overnight: Hotel Alyeska

  • Day 2

    Kenai Peninsula

    Visit the Alaska Wildlife Conservation Center en route to the Kenai River. This nonprofit organization rehabilitates injured or orphaned animals and returns them to the wild. During your visit, you may see local wildlife, including bears, moose, bison, elk and a variety of birds. This afternoon, take a float trip through Kenai National Wildlife Refuge. Beavers and otters make their homes along the water, and bears and moose are commonly spied on shore. Tonight, sit down to a barbecue dinner, followed by a riverside campfire and s'mores roast.

    Overnight: Hotel Alyeska
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 3

    Kenai Peninsula

    Cruise the Kenai Fjords National Park, and witness the crash of calving glaciers. The park occupies more than half a million acres and is covered by an ice pack extending more than 700 square miles. You may see humpback whales, orcas, puffins and sea lions at close range; look to the skies for a glimpse of bald eagles overhead. Ride the double-decker Alaska Railroad GoldStar Dome train, which is adorned with original Alaskan art and features an upper-level, open-air platform and a glass-covered observation deck offering sensational views of the countryside.

    Overnight: Hotel Alyeska
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 4

    Kenai Peninsula/Redoubt Bay/Talkeetna

    Travel by float plane over Cook Inlet to Redoubt Bay Bear Sanctuary, where a large population of bears prowl the river in search of a meal. Upon arrival, you are briefed on proper "bear etiquette" before taking to the creeks on pontoon boats. Watch safely from your pontoon boat as they snatch salmon from the water - sometimes even in mid-leap. Continue to the town of Talkeetna, where your lodge is poised on a hillside, with stunning views of Mount McKinley (conditions permitting).

    Overnight: Talkeetna Alaskan Lodge
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 5

    Talkeetna

    The Susitna River teems with some of the world's best salmon and trout, and this morning, you set off on a fishing trip. Afterward, enjoy a riverside picnic lunch to take in the beauty of the surrounding landscapes. Although you may not rake in as many fish as the bears you saw yesterday did, with the supplied fishing equipment - tackle, boots and rain gear - you should have a very rewarding morning nonetheless. This afternoon, go on a geocaching adventure, a high-tech treasure hunt using handheld GPS devices, on the grounds of your lodge.

    Overnight: Talkeetna Alaskan Lodge
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 6

    Talkeetna

    Continue your exploration of the Denali area with a kayak trip on the placid waters of Byers Lake (equipment is provided). Take a gentle but invigorating hike on a well-maintained forest trail; your guide introduces Alaska's plant and animal life along the way. This afternoon, you may explore the quaint downtown of Talkeetna, a three-square-block area centered on the Village Park; 13 of its buildings are on the National Register of Historic Places. Step out to the banks of the Talkeetna River to look for wildlife and enjoy spectacular views of Mount McKinley and the Alaska Range. You may also visit Denali National Park's Talkeetna Ranger Station, the last stop for climbing expeditions headed up Mount McKinley.

    Overnight: Talkeetna Alaskan Lodge
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 7

    Talkeetna/Anchorage

    Take a thrilling flight over the peaks and glaciers of Denali National Park. Fly over Don Sheldon Amphitheater (a natural formation named for a renowned Alaskan pilot), the two-mile-deep Great Gorge and the Kahiltna Glacier, a popular route for climbers attempting the summit of Mount McKinley. Your pilot, linked to you by two-way headset throughout the flight, explains the highlights you see. Land on a glacier's surface, and disembark to experience the majesty of Alaska's beauty, with snow-veiled peaks surrounding a vast snowfield stretching in every direction. This afternoon, meet and have lunch with four-time Iditarod champion Martin Buser, and meet his Alaskan husky team.

    Continue to Anchorage and enjoy a brief city tour, including the downtown district, and a demonstration of the traditional Alaska Ulus. Celebrate your Alaskan adventure at a farewell dinner tonight.

    Overnight: Hotel Captain Cook
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 8

    Depart Anchorage

    Board your homebound flight.

    Meal plan: B

South Africa: A Family Safari

Programme Overview

Together, experience the treasures of South Africa - its historic cities, breathtaking scenery and vibrant culture. Discover rousing wildlife on game drives, a river cruise, and a visit to an endangered species center.

  • Duration: 11 days/ 10 nights

    Highlights: Cape Town | Greater Kruger | Johannesburg | Victoria Falls

    A&K Advantages

    • Ascend Table Mountain and spend a full day exploring Cape Point, where you find the Boulders African penguin colony
    • Set off on safaris in Ngala Private Game Reserve, situated in the Greater Kruger Area and known for its populations of elephants, rhinos and wild African dogs
    • Visit the Hoedspruit Endangered Species Centre, where cheetahs, wild dogs and other species are rehabilitated
    • Connect with the local children of South Africa when you visit the rural community of Welverdiend
    • Understand South Africa's deep history in Johannesburg when you visit Hector Pieterson Museum & Memorial, see the former home of Nelson Mandela, and explore the Apartheid Museum
    • Explore incredible Victoria Falls - one of the Seven Natural Wonders of the World - on a private walking tour
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE CAPE TOWN

    After your arrival in the seaside city of Cape Town, you are met by an A&K representative and transferred to your boutique hotel.

    Spend the remainder of the day at leisure.

    Overnight: Vineyard Hotel & Spa

  • Day 2

    CAPE TOWN

    Today, drive through the slopes of Table Mountain, and later board on a cable car (weather permitting) to reach the peak. On arrival, you enjoy the stunning panoramic views of the city and sea.

    Continue to travel towards the historic Cape Town, en-route viewing theserene Company Gardens.Further visit the Castle of Good Hope,South Africa's oldest surviving colonial building, where you appreciate the castle’s notorious dungeon.

    Next, Amaze and delight yourself with beautiful handcrafted wire and bead gifts from streetwires, followed by an afternoon at leisure.

    This evening, join your fellow travelers for a Djembe drum session and 14-course welcome feast at Gold Restaurant.

    Overnight: Vineyard Hotel & Spa
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 3

    CAPE TOWN

    Today, enjoy a beautiful drive along the Atlantic coast to Cape of Good Hope Nature Reserve,a nature lover's paradise. En-route view the fishing harbors and the Clifton and Camps Bay .After arriving atthe rocky tip of the Cape Peninsula, you may choose to ride the Flying Dutchman funicular up to the lighthouse for panoramic aerial views of the rocky coastline. Admire the southern right whales breaching the water in spring and early summer.

    Continue to the Boulders Beach,a sheltered beach made up of inlets between granite boulders, famous for its charming colony of African penguins. Proceed to explore the indigenous Kirstenbosch National Botanical Garden at the slopes of Table Mountain.

    Overnight: Vineyard Hotel & Spa
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 4

    GREATER KRUGER

    This day, depart forHoedspruit and then continue to drive to the Ngala Private Game Reserve, aluxury Safari Lodge located in South Africa's Kruger Park. Settle in your charming lodge under a dense canopy of tamboti trees.

    Later, enjoy the game viewing activities on offer at leisure.

    Overnight: Ngala Safari Lodge
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 5-6

    GREATER KRUGER

    The next two days at Ngala Private Game Reserve, enjoying a relaxed serene atmosphere set in the wilds of the African bush. Each day you may choose your game viewing activities through Ngala, home to all of Africa’s “Big Five,” including: the elusive leopard, several prides of lionand notable packs of the rare and endangered African wild dog. Explore the place with the Local rangers and guides, who share with youthe folk tales, art of fire making and traditional bow hunting.

    Enjoy an exciting culinary experience as you indulge in an interactive chef experience to learn and prepare local dishes. Experience the local culture by meeting and interactingwith the local Welverdiend community, also visiting its schools and nurseries when in session.

    The next day explore the Hoedspruit Endangered Species Centre, with a large bio-diversity. Get an opportunity to closely observe cheetahs, wild dogs, caracals and various other rehabilitated species.

    Overnight: Ngala Safari Lodge
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 7

    JOHANNESBURG

    Depart for Johannesburg, on arrival at the airport, you will be met at by an A&K representative and transferred to your hotel.

    Overnight: 54 on Bath
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 8

    JOHANNESBURG

    This day, embark on a guided tour of Soweto, Infused with the history of struggle and abuzz with the energy of the city of gold.Continue to visitthe former home of Nelson Mandela, now a museum and Archbishop Desmond Tutu,still occupied by family.

    Later view the Apartheid Museum, a museum complex in Johannesburg.

    Overnight: 54 on Bath
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 9-10

    VICTORIA FALLS

    Take a morning flight to Victoria Falls. Upon arrival you are met and escorted to your hotel set on a plateau adjacent to Zambezi National Park. After settling down, board on a sundowner cruise on the charming Zambezi River. Enjoy the cruise with spectacular views of hippos, crocodiles and antelope at the water’s edge. Also catch sightof Victoria Falls rising at a distance.

    Overnight: Victoria Falls Safari Lodge
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 10

    VICTORIA FALLS

    Today, take a guided walking tour of Victoria Falls to explore the falls, dropping at a massive 1.2-mile-wide curtain of water over the edge of the falls, with plumes of mist, rainbows and a thrilling roar.

    Go on to shop for locally made crafts and wares at the Falls Market before you join your fellow guests in the evening for a special farewell dinner. Later, your family may opt for a private guided cycling tour to appreciate the scenic beauty of the town and falls.

    On your last night, join fellow guests for a farewell dinner.

    Overnight: Victoria Falls Safari Lodge
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 11

    DEPART JOHANNESBURG

    After breakfast, depart for Johannesburg, where you check in andrelaxat the comfortable Premier Lounge before boarding for your onward flight.

    Meal plan: B

Spain: A Family Journey

Programme Overview

Explore sunny Spain, enjoying exciting activities for all ages. Witness a display of fine horsemanship at a traditional Andalusian ranch, hunt for treasure in a museum and learn to dance flamenco.

  • Duration: 10 days/ 9 nights

    Highlights: Barcelona | Granada | Marbella | Seville | Córdoba | Madrid

    A&K Advantages

    • Discover your destination through these local connections.
    • Explore sunny Spain from seaside Barcelona to heartland Madrid, enjoying kid-friendly experiences designed to inspire
    • Join a local chef to learn how to make some classic Spanish dishes and then enjoy them for lunch on a Cultural Connection
    • Take a dramatic cable car ride in Gibraltar for spectacular views
    • Visit a traditional Andalusian ranch to witness a display of horsemanship that is a tribute to Spain's sporting heritage
    • Take part in an exciting scavenger hunt through the Thyssen-Bornemisza Museum in Madrid
    • Ride in a horse-drawn carriage to an authentic flamenco show for a Musical Connection
    • Connect with people as you learn to dance flamenco in Seville
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE BARCELONA

    On arrival at the Barcelona airport, you will be greeted by an A&K Representative who will assist with your transfer to the hotel.

    Later, enjoy a welcome dinner along with your fellow guests.

    Overnight: El Palace Hotel Barcelona
    Meal plan: D

  • Day 2

    BARCELONA

    Begin your day with a culinary excursion to explore one of the finest food market in all Europe-The Boquería.

    Continue the journey to visit the majestic cathedral,SantJaume Square and the elegant Eixample district. Later, take a tour of AntoniGaudí’s sensational SagradaFamilia Basilica .Next, indulge yourself in a Spanish gastronomy experience, where you learn to make some classic Spanish dishes with a local chef.

    Overnight: El Palace Hotel Barcelona
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 3

    GRANADA

    Today, depart for Granada. Begin to explore Spain’s Islamic Heritageby visiting the Alhambra, an example of one of the finest Islamic architecture in the world. Continue the exploration to visitthe magnificent district of Albaizín. A UNESCO World Heritage Site, characterized by cobble stoned streets with white washed houses.

    Overnight: Hotel Alhambra Palace
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 4

    MARBELLA

    This morning, travel to Marbella, En-route visitingMalaga, where you observe the ruined fortress of Gibralfaro. Next, is a visit to the famed Picasso Museum, houses one of the most extensive collections of artworks by the 20th-century Spanish artist Pablo Picasso.

    After your arrival in Marbella, you will be assisted with your hotel check-in. The afternoon is at leisure to explore the place.

    Overnight: Hotel Fuerte Marbella
    Meal plan: B

  • Day 5

    MARBELLA

    Start your day with a travel westwards to reach the Rock of Gibraltar. Here,you enjoy the scenic beauty while you ride on a cable car to reach the peak. Gaze and admire across the straits to the coast of Morocco.

    Later, visit St. Michael’s Cave,a network of limestone caves. View therare Gibraltar’s Barbary macaques, the only wild monkey population in the European continent.

    Conclude your day by exploring the streets of Marbella at your convenience.

    Overnight: Hotel Fuerte Marbella
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 6

    SEVILLE

    Today, journey into the heart of Andalucía to Campo Abierto, a traditional ranch, where you see the livestock of fighting bulls in the andalusian countrysideand displays of fine horsemanship.

    Enjoy your lunch before you take a travel to reach Seville and your hotel.

    Tonight, join your fellow guests on a tapas tour through Seville’s finest taverns and dine for an amazingCulinaryexperience.

    Overnight: Hotel Alfonso XIII
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 7

    SEVILLE

    Begin your day to the experience the beautiful city of Seville .Visit the Royal Palace of the Alcázar, a magnificent complex of patios and halls in different architectural styles. Continue to explore the majestic Gothic cathedral and of course, the flower-filled Santa Cruz district.

    Next, is anopportunity to learnthe cultural classic flamenco dancing through an excitinglesson.

    Later, embark on a horse-drawn carriage to celebrate a musical connection atTablao El Arenal. End your day with an unforgettable performance of flamenco dancing and singing.

    Overnight: Hotel Alfonso XIII
    Meal plan: B

  • Day 8

    CÓRDOBA / Streets of Flowers

    Today, visit the extraordinary Mezquitaat Córdoba .Explore the cathedral and former great mosque of Córdoba, and walk its flower-lined streets.

    Continue your travel on a high-speed AVE train to reach Madrid.

    Overnight: Hotel Ritz by Belmond
    Meal plan: B

  • Day 9

    MADRID

    Today, you enjoy an exciting scavenger hunt at the magnificent Thyssen- Bornemisza Museum, where the clues provided eventually lead to a treasure.

    Later, visit the Plaza Mayor for some mouthwatering churros dunked in hot chocolate. Continue to the marvelous San Miguel market, where you savor its mouth-watering ensemble of tapas bars for lunch at leisure.

    This evening, enjoy a farewell dinner with your fellow guests.

    Overnight: Hotel Ritz by Belmond
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 10

    DEPART MADRID

    After breakfast, you will be assisted with transfer to the airport for your onward flight.

    Meal plan: B
