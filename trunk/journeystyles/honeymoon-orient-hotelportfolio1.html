Suggested Thailand Accommodation

  • AMANPURI

    With sculptured roof-lines under tropical skies, Amanpuri is a tranquil oasis in a secluded natural coconut grove above a soft, crescent shaped beach. Elevated walkways weave through statuesque palms to reach a cool reception area and a large slate-blue pool.

    Stone steps sweep down to the beach and the mesmerising Andaman Sea, where the gentle fragrance of the frangipani flower delicately scents the sea air. Cruise to Shark Point, where crystal clear waters and shallow diving await. Famous for its tame leopard sharks, the area is also home to bright-purple sea anemones, snappers and schools of moray eels. If you are looking for total escape, take a stroll to The Spa, a holistic sanctuary shaded by whispering coconut palms. Pamper yourself with an impossibly soothing body wrap treatment.For an unusual dining experience, head for the Naoki restaurant, where authentic Kaiseki fare blends the finest French techniques with the artistry of Japanese cuisine. Round off the day in style, sitting on the terrace to watch the sunset over the darkening Andaman Sea.

    ANANTARA BO PHUT KOH SAMUI RESORT & SPA

    Anantara Koh Samui Resort & Spa has 106 airy rooms and suites each has either a terrace or a balcony to take in the view. Traditional Thai architecture and contemporary Anantara style are blended seamlessly here, both in the communal areas and the guestrooms.

    There are two restaurants at Anantara a traditional Italian restaurant, Full moon offering ocean-view dining on an elevated terrace overlooking the infinity pool and beachfront. High Tide is the Thai and international restaurant serving buffet breakfast, island-style and Thai cuisine. There are also two bars to relax by in the middle of the day and in the evening to enjoy a pre-dinner cocktail or an after dinner liqueur.

    There are two outdoor swimming pools one being a dedicated family pool. Children will love to spend time in the Baan Ling Noi Kids' Club. There is an array of leisure activities on offer at the hotel and guests can be as busy or as relaxed as they choose to be. The hotel is a 15 minute drive from Koh Samui airport.

    CONRAD KOH SAMUI

    Conrad Koh Samui is situated on the Phang Ka Peninsula and is the only west-facing luxury resort in Koh Samui with stunning sunset views from every villa. There are 80 unique ocean facing private villas 14 with 2 bedrooms each villa is contemporary in style with traditional Thai elements and all have a 10 metre infinity pool, private balcony and luxurious marble bathroom. At Conrad Koh Samui every luxury has been thought of and no detail has been left to chance here is a place where you can enjoy the beauty of your surroundings and truly relax.

    There are a selection of activities on offer diving, sailing, snorkelling or simply relax at the oversized infinity pool. The resort has an interactive children's club called the Turtle Club who organise games and activities with excellent staff. The resort also has a fully equipped gymnasium with the latest cardiovascular and weight training equipment and a yoga deck on the beach. The spa at Conrad Koh Samui is a complete wellness destination located on a dramatic cliff face, there are 10 treatment rooms each with their own private deck and offering a range of treatments from the contemporary to the traditional.

    Conrad Koh Samui has 4 restaurants including the fine dining Jahn where contemporary Thai cuisine is created in an exclusive candle-lit dining room for up to 28 guests. The Azure Bar/Grill is the relaxed dining option and located by the pool and the beautiful landscaped gardens. The unique Zest is where you can breakfast and has outdoor and indoor seating as well as being home to a unique art display The Food Library where the chefs display their ingredients before creating their dishes. The final dining area is the wine cellar which is an intimate dining experience as there is only one table but for wine lovers the perfect end to a day. The professional Sommelier can pair a superb vintage wine with every meal. The wine cellar hosts a collection of over 3000 bottles of vintage wines.

    SALA SAMUI

    SALA Samui has a beautiful location and has 80 metres of white sandy beach in front of the property. The accommodation at SALA Samui is split into seven different categories from the deluxe room with balcony (with no pool) to the beautifully appointed two bedrommed Presidential suite. Each room or suite has an outside bathroom and private deck or terrace.

    SALA Samui boasts two beachfront swimming pools, including a stunning 25 meter lap pool surrounded by 4 massage salas "floating" in a beautiful lotus pond. With a choice of two restaurants, a beach bar, a wine cellar and the option for in villa dinning guests are spoilt for choice with the cuisine being a mix of traditional Thai and modern western food. The hotel also has a gymnasium and a library and excursions can be arranged for snorkelling, diving, golf and elephant trekking.

    SALA Samui is just 15 minutes from the Samui airport, 20 minutes from Bo Phut's quaint Fisherman's Village, and only 15 minutes from Chaweng Beach shopping and nightlife making it the perfect choice of hotel in Koh Samui.

    Santiburi Beach Resort, Golf & Spa

    THE EASTERN & ORIENTAL TRAIN

    This luxurious train provides you with the perfect surroundings in which to relax and become immersed in the splendour of the ever-changing scenery in South East Asia.

    The two night journey takes you north-east from Singapore crossing Malaysia over the straits of Johor, onwards to Kuala Lumpur. The following morning, enjoy a guided tour of Georgetown, capital of Penang, before moving onwards to Thailand. Prior to arriving in Bangkok, experience a tour on the River Kwai.

    On-board, enjoy the views from the Observation Car and head to the bar for an aperitif before dinner. Relax to the sounds of the resident pianist before eating a sumptuous meal prepared by the on-board chefs. Retire to your private cabin, decorated in a style that hearkens back to a colonial era.

    The luxury train provides a year round scheduled service through the diverse countryside of Singapore, Malaysia and Thailand, including the following journeys between Bangkok - Singapore, Bangkok - Vientiane, Chiang Mai - Bangkok - Singapore.

    THE TONGSAI BAY

    The Tongsai Bay was the first hotel of its kind to open its doors on Koh Samui back in 1987 and has always been aware of their carbon footprint, not one tree has been removed to accommodate the hotel, and they continue their environmental mission today with a variety of successful initiatives.

    The accommodation is a variety of elegant suites, cottages and villas with a range of options there is the perfect choice for everyone. The lead in suites are the Beachfront Suites with a spacious bedroom and bathroom and private terrace with sun loungers, the suites on the first floor have an outdoor bath tub. The cottages are mostly free standing units with a couple being semi- detached, the cottages offer more privacy and space. The villas are the ultimate in luxury and space both indoor and outdoor with the Guvnor villa being 2 adjoining villas and can accommodate up to eight people.

    Dining at Tongsai Bay is outstanding with the Po-Lad beach bar and Bistro and the traditional Chef Chom's restaurant offering exquisite Thai cuisine. For that special treat have a meal designed especially for you by the chef and dine in the privacy of your own terrace or on the beach itself. The Tongsai Bay is a 10 minute drive from Samui airport.

    TRISARA

    Trisara, in Sanskrit meaning the "Third Garden in Heaven" is South East Asia's most intimate and exclusive resort and spa. Nestling in a private bay just 15 minutes from Phuket International Airport, the resort is hidden amongst lush tropical jungle. Although covering over 2 km of private coastline, Trisara feels small, intimate and supremely luxurious.

    The thirty nine pool villas and suites sit above the bay, adjacent to the beachfront restaurant decks and bar and the 18 larger residences are a celebration of tropical luxury. The Ocean View pool rooms have interconnecting doors, the perfect choice for any family. Each of the residences is a private compound styled property, with between two and six bedrooms, private kitchens, 20 meter pools and dedicated cooks and service staff who provide a true sense of Thai hospitality.

    Trisara's dining experience is all created from the superb local produce such as seafood, fruit and vegetables and the kitchen team know how to prepare them so that they retain all their flavour. There is also an in-house bakery making sure that the best bread and pastries are created fresh every day as well as the most amazing desserts.

    W SAMUI

    The accommodation is beautifully appointed with custom designed furniture, private patios and pools, breath-taking views and spa ready bathrooms. There is a selection of accommodation options starting with the Jungle Oasis at 163sq metres and the spectacular WOW Ocean Haven over 833sq metres split over two floors this is the ultimate beach residence with all the space and privacy of a villa in a resort environment.

    W Koh Samui boasts an array of facilities from their SWEAT gymnasium with state of the art equipment, W library offers a range of books, dvds, magazines and games, a selection of watersports are available, an outdoor swimming pool to cool off in the middle of the day or simply relax by and there are tennis courts. At W Koh Samui there is plenty to do for the active traveller or you can simply enjoy the beautiful beach and coastline that this stunning destination has to offer.
