PARIS, BURGUNDY AND A SOUTH OF FRANCE CRUISE

Programme Overview

Stay two nights in Paris and one in Beaune, before a seven-night cruise through the south of France on the Saône and Rhône Rivers. Enjoy lunch at a family-run farm in Provence - an A&K-exclusive.

  • Duration: 11 days

    Highlights:: Beaune| Burgundy| Giverny | Paris| Mâcon| Chalon-Sur-Saône| Lyon | Avignon| Sénanque | Viviers| Lyon

    A&K Advantage

    • Cruise for seven nights on the Saône and Rhône Rivers, and enjoy pre-cruise hotel stays in Paris (two nights) and Burgundy (one night)
    • Tour the sights of vibrant Paris and visit the Musée d'Orsay, which is noted for its collection of Impressionist art
    • Tour the home of Claude Monet and his gardens at Giverny
    • Experience an A&K exclusive excursion, including the Cistercian Abbey of Sénanque, Saint Paul-de-Mausole and the Roman amphitheater in Arles
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    Arrive Paris

    As soon as you arrive in Munich, you will be met by a member of the A&K team and transferred to your hotel. In the evening join your A&K group leader and fellow guests for a warm welcome dinner.

    Overnight: Hotel Westminster
    Meal Plan: D

  • Day 2

    Paris/ Giverny

    Start the day with a lovely morning by travelling to Giverny and exploring Claude Monet's house and the beautiful lush gardens that inspired many of his Impressionist masterpieces. Next, take your return travel to Paris for a tour of the iconic sights and a visit to the Musée d'Orsay, to observe its remarkable collection of Impressionist art.

    Overnight: Hotel Westminster
    Meal Plan: B L

  • Day 3

    Sorrento

    Enjoy a morning demystifying Paris at your leisure. This afternoon, travel to Beaune, the wine capital of Burgundy.

    Overnight: Hôtel Le Cep
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    Beaune / Wine Capital of Burgundy

    Explore the place by visiting a traditional family-run mustard mill for an exclusive tour and tasting. Then, visit the cellars of Domaine du Lycée Viticole, a French secondary school where the students make award-winning wines. Next, Travel to Lyon for priority boarding on the beautiful 'Amadeus Symphony.' Enjoy the hospitality along with your fellow travelers with a welcome cocktail & dinner.

    Overnight: Amadeus Symphony
    Meal Plan: B D

  • Day 5

    Mâcon / Beaujolais Wine Tasting

    Begin your day with a cruise through the Saône River to Mâcon for a journey into the lovely Mâconnais region. Tempt your taste buds with an enticing tasting session of the region's world-famous Beaujolais vintages . Make a return journey to Amadeus Symphony for lunch. You may choose to take an optional afternoon excursion to discover Cluny.

    Overnight: Amadeus Symphony
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 6

    Dock in picturesque Chalon-sur-Saône for a morning at leisure to explore this delightful riverside town with its graceful cathedral and photography museum. After lunch on board, visit medieval Brancion, continuing to Château de Cormatin and its turrets, wide moat and graceful gardens.

    Chalon-Sur-Saône / Vintages of Burgundy

    Today Amadeus Symphony will moor in picturesque Chalon-sur-Saône and return to Beaune to visit the Hôtel-Dieu, a 15th-century charity hospital. Relish a wine tasting session at Le Cellier de la Cabiote. The lunch will be served on board. Have a relaxing afternoon at your cruise to Tournus, or choose an optional excursion to medieval Brancion, moving to Château de Cormatin with its turrets and elegant lush gardens.

    Overnight: Amadeus Symphony
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 7

    Lyon / Historic Old Quarter

    After your arrival in Lyon go on to discover the historic old quarter, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. In the afternoon take a cruise of the Rhône River and in the evening enjoy a private pre-dinner cocktail party hosted by your A&K group leader in the ship's Amadeus Club.

    Overnight: Amadeus Symphony
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 8

    Avignon / Magnificent Palais des Papes

    Begin your day to explore Avignon, renowned for it’s marvelously conserved medieval walls. Tour the Magnificent Palais des Papes, once home to the Catholic popes. You may opt to spend the afternoon with an optional tour to Pont du Gard .Finally, visit the lovely town of Uzès with a meticulous cityscape.

    Overnight: Amadeus Symphony
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 9

    Sénanque & Arles / An A&K Exclusive Experience

    Today, take the privilege of being an A&K guest with an exclusive tour. Begin with a tour of the Cistercian Abbey of Sénanque and admire its magnificent 12th-century architecture. Followed by a tour of the nearby village of Gordes with its mesmerizing cobblestone lanes and breath taking views of the countryside. In Saint Paul-de-Mausole, view the asylum where Van Gogh voluntarily interned for a year. Take a stop at a Provençal farm for a healthy lunch made from home-grown vegetables. Conclude the day in Arles with its massive Roman amphitheater, beautiful basilica and Place du Forum, where Van Gogh painted Café Terrace at Night.

    Overnight: Amadeus Symphony
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 10

    Viviers / Pont d'Arc and Gala Dinner

    On your arrival in Viviers this morning, you may choose an optional excursion to the Ardèche gorge to view Pont d'Arc, a famed natural stone bridge of the region. Afterward, cruise to Lyon. Gather tonight for a gala dinner with the caption and your fellow travelers.

    Overnight: Amadeus Symphony
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 11

    Depart Lyon

    Enjoy your breakfast and take a transfer to Lyon airport for your onward flight home.

    Meal Plan:

  • Pricing & group size

    As a guide this 11 day journey in France will cost from £3,415 per person based on two adults sharing excluding flights. The maximum group size for this itinerary will be 24.

Peru: From Machu Picchu to Lake Titicaca

Programme Overview

  • View the beautiful sunrise over Machu Picchu and explore it with a local guide, view Sacsayhuaman's massive ruins, meet residents of the Floating Island of Uros and enjoy two nights on beautiful Suasi Island.

    Duration: 12 days/ 11 nights

    Highlights: LIMA |CUSCO| URUBAMBA| MACHU PICCHU | PUNO| SUASI ISLAND

    A&K advantages

    • Watch day break over the spectacular Machu Picchu
    • Learn of the mysteries of Machu Picchu's temples, fortresses and sanctuaries from a noted local expert guide
    • View pre-Columbian art at the Larco Herrera Museum
    • Tap your feet to a performance of Andean music
    • Cruise the world's highest navigable lake and visit the Uros people who live on its waters
    • Enjoy two nights on beautiful, privately owned Suasi Island
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE IN LIMA

    Arrive in Lima, where an A&K representative will welcome you to the capital of Peru and assist with transfer to the hotel.

    Later, enjoy a welcome dinner along with your fellow guests.

    Overnight: Ramada Costa del Sol Lima Airport

  • Day 2

    CUSCO

    This morning, fly to Cusco and enjoy lunch after your arrival. Continue with a drive to the sacred valley of the Incas, Urubamba. Here, you get an opportunity to visit a camelid breeding center to learn about the ancient weaving techniques still prevalent in the area. Experience the culture by meeting the locals wearing their colorful traditional dress.

    Transfer to your hotel for dinner with the fellow guests.

    Overnight: Aranwa Sacred Valley Hotel & Wellness
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 3

    URUBAMBA

    Today, visit a local home for a traditional culinary excursion to observe how chicha, a traditional Andean drink, is made. Later, visit Ollantaytambo, archaeological site in southern Peru and the site of the Incas’ greatest victory over the Spanish. Further, visit an intricately designed magnificent church built over Incan ruins in Chincheros

    Later, visit Petra, renowned for its charm and ancient cultural heritage

    Overnight: Aranwa Sacred Valley Hotel & Wellness
    Meal Plan: B L

  • Day 4

    MACHU PICCHU

    Begin your day with a beautiful journey to Machu Picchu on a Vistadome train. Explore the place in depth with an expert guide, who will guide you through this breathtaking Incan citadel.

    After discovering the mystical place, enjoy lunch at Machu Picchu Sanctuary Lodge Hotel.

    Overnight: Inkaterra Machu Picchu Pueblo Hotel
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 5

    MACHU PICCHU

    Today, Awaken to sunrise and take a stroll through Machu Picchu to witness the beautiful sunrise view.

    You may also choose to hike Inti Punku, Sun Gate, or Huayna Picchu with your guide to experience the stunning panoramic views.

    Enjoy your lunch and head for a return travel to Cusco.

    Overnight: Aranwa Cusco Boutique Hotel
    Meal Plan: B L

  • Day 6

    CUSCO

    This day, tour Cusco, visiting the Cathedral on the Plaza de Armas and the Santo Domingo Temple. Experience the cultural fusion of the Spanish and the Incan by interacting with the locals at the market of San Pedro.

    Later, travel to Sacsayhuaman, one of the most amazing Incan constructions for tourists. Marvel its monumental stones precisely cut and fitted together without mortar.

    This evening, enjoy an Andean music presentation by Tito Leon. Continue for diner at one of the city’s finest local restaurant, MAP Café.

    Overnight: Aranwa Cusco Boutique Hotel
    Meal Plan: B D

  • Day 7

    PUNO

    Begin your day with a travel to Puno, a beautiful port city. Along the way visit the Iglesia San Pedro Apóstol in Andahuaylillas, at times referred to as the Sistine Chapel of Peru.

    Later, visit ruins of an Incan shrine in Raqchi, once one of the holiest shrines in the Inca Empire. Proceed to pass through La Raya. Meet and interact with an artisan in Pucara before you go ahead for your lunch.

    Overnight: Casa Andina Private Collection Hotel Puno
    Meal Plan: B L

  • Day 8-9

    SUASI ISLAND

    Travel to Lake Titicaca,where you ride a boat to reach the man-made Floating Island of the Uros. Continue to the island of Taquile, renowned for its fine, hand-woven textiles.

    Later, travel to the beautiful, privately owned Suasi Island, where you enjoy a grilled barbecue lunch.

    The next day, you may choose to relax amongst the scenic beauty or continue to explore this picturesque island at leisure.

    Overnight: Casa Andina Private Collection Hotel Suasi
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 10

    LIMA

    Along the way of your travel to Juliaca, view a church famous for its paintings and sculptures.

    After your arrival in Juliaca, take a flight to Lima.

    Overnight: Casa Andina Private Collection Hotel Lima
    Meal Plan: B L

  • Day 11

    LIMA

    Begin your exploration with a city tour, starting with the Plaza Mayor. Visit the historic Cathedral and appreciate its architectural intricacies. En-route view the Presidential Palace, Continue to visit the Church of San Francisco.

    After the historic exploration, experience the modern Lima .Tour the fascinating Larco Herrera Museum to view an extensive private collection of pre-Inca ceramics, one of the largest collections of its kind in the world!

    This evening, discover an extraordinary archaeological site. Later, enjoy a farewell dinner comprising of the contemporary Peruvian cuisine at Huaca Pucllana Restaurant.

    Overnight: Casa Andina Private Collection Hotel Lima
    Meal Plan: B D

  • Day 12

    DEPART LIMA

    After breakfast, transfer to the airport for your onward flight.

    Meal Plan: B

Connections Russia: Treasures Of The Tsars

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 8 Days/ 7 Nights

    Explore historic Russia as you view priceless artifacts at the Kremlin, help re-create a traditional wedding ceremony, meet an expert on the Romanovs and tour the Grand Palace at Peterhof before hours.

    Highlights:Moscow|St. Petersburg|PETERHOF|PUSHKIN

    A&K Advantages

    • Enjoy a talk by a Russian scholar on Russia's past and present
    • Take part in a re-creation of a traditional Russian wedding ceremony and be entertained by a folk group with authentic Russian songs and games
    • Discover the Moscow metro, famed for the splendour of its stations
    • Travel on the high-speed Sapsan train from Moscow to St. Petersburg, enjoying lunch on board
    • Meet Zoya Belyakova, an expert on the Romanovs, to learn about the fascinating history of Russia's last ruling dynasty
    • Savour a hearty Russian lunch at Podvorie Dacha restaurant, housed in a traditional Russian log house
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    Arrive Moscow, Russia

    After your arrival in Moscow, you are welcomed and assisted with transfer to your hotel by an A&K representative.

    The evening is for you to enjoy a welcome dinner with you fellow guests.

    Marriott Moscow Royal Aurora | Dinner

  • Day 2

    Moscow / The Stately Capital

    Begin your exploration of Moscow, starting with a visit to the Kremlin Armory, one of the oldest museums of Moscow with a vast collection of priceless artifacts. Closely observe its impressive collection of carriages, royal gala clothes and fabulous Fabergé eggs. Continue to visit two Kremlin cathedrals: Assumption and Archangel.

    Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant and set forth to attend a talk by a Russian scholar.Experience the culture and traditions through a leisure stroll in Red Square, en-route visiting Lenin's Tomb and St. Basil's cathedral.

    Later, take a short journey on the Moscow metro to appreciate the beauty of the metro stations, with their reflective marble walls, high ceilings and grandiose chandeliers

    Marriott Moscow Royal Aurora | Breakfast/Lunch

  • Day 3

    Moscow / Kolomenskoye Estate

    Today, visit the stunning Kolomenskoye Estate, a unique historic place in the most scenic corner of Moscow. Later, take a leisure walk to the nearby Peter the Great's cottage and join in a re-creation of a Russian wedding ceremony.

    Enjoy your lunch before you set off to view some of the famous sites in Moscow.

    Continue the journey to New Maiden Cemetery, a cluster of sparkling domes behind high walls and battlements.

    Marriott Moscow Royal Aurora /Breakfast/lunch

  • Day 4

    St. Petersburg/ Venice of the North

    This day, embark on a high-speed train, Sapsan, to reach St. Petersburg, often called “Venice of the North”. The lunch will be served onboard.

    On arrival, you will be assisted with transfer to your hotel, where you will be served your dinner

    Later, experience the beauty of the place with a cruise of the rivers and canals of St. Petersburg. Pass by the Church of the Resurrection of Christ, a fine example of late 19th-century Russian architecture, and marvel its extraordinary exteriors.

    Kempinski Hotel Moika 22/Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner

  • Day 5

    ST. PETERSBURG / A Dynasty’s Legacy

    Today, you get an opportunity to meet and interact with Zoya Belyakova, a history expert, to learn about the history of the Romanovs – Russia’s last ruling dynasty. Continue the journey to take a tour of St. Petersburg. Visit Nevsky Prospect, Palace Square, the Winter Palace and Vasilevsky Island.

    Later, visit St. Isaac’s Cathedral to appreciate its gilded dome, sculptures and red granite columns that grace its façade. Explore the place by observing its interiors, intricately designed and decorated with mosaics, icons and columns of malachite and lapis lazuli.

    Experience the culture in-depth with a visit to a local farmer’s market before you break for lunch at a local restaurant.

    Conclude your day with a tour of the majestic Hermitage in the Winter Palace, home to three million pieces of artwork dating back to the Stone Age.

    Overnight: Kempinski Moika 22
    Meal Plan: B L

  • Day 6

    PETERHOF / Private Grand Palace Tour

    Start your day with a beautiful drive to Peterhof, perhaps St Petersburg's most famous and spectacular Imperial estate. Take an exclusive tour of the Grand Palace, which is opened for you before official operating hours, so you avoid the crowds.

    Later, embark on a hydrofoil for a return journey to St. Petersburg, where you enjoy your lunch. Next, tour the fascinating Peter & Paul Fortress. Its cathedral is the burial place of many tsars and tsarinas, including Peter the Great.

    Overnight: Kempinski Moika 22
    Meal Plan: B L

  • Day 7

    PUSHKIN / Lunch, Russian Style

    Today, travel to Tsarskoe Selo , the most beautiful suburb of St.Petersburg. Continue to visit the Catherine Palace, with its formal parks that stretch to Rastrelli’s Hermitage building, and the world-famous Amber Room, now restored to its original glory.

    Relish a traditional, hearty Russian lunch at Podvorie Dacha restaurant .Later, return to St. Petersburg where you join your Tour Director and fellow travelers for a farewell dinner.

    Overnight: Kempinski Moika 22
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 8

    DEPART ST. PETERSBURG

    After breakfast, transfer to the airport for your onward flight.

    Meal Plan: B

Morocco Imperial Cities

Programme Overview

Explore mysterious Morocco, dining in an authentic riad; navigating Marrakech by horse-drawn carriage; sitting down to a meal with a local family; and meeting the Berber people in their village beneath the Atlas' peaks.

  • Duration: 8 Days/7 Nights

    Highlights: CASABLANCA| FEZ| RABAT| MARRAKECH

    A&K Advantage

    Discover your destination through these local connections.

    • Enjoy a nostalgic welcome dinner at Rick's Café in Casablanca
    • Dine in an authentic riad, where a special guest speaker shares an insider's view of life in Morocco
    • Get to know Marrakech on a horse-drawn carriage ride
    • Experience Moroccan hospitality when you dine with a local family in their Marrakech home
    • Stop at a village in the Atlas Mountains to meet the local Berber people and visit a family in their home
    • Stay close to the action in traditional, charming accommodations located within a walking distance of the souks
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE CASABLANCA

    On your Arrival in Casablanca, you will be welcomed by an A&K Representative, who will assist you with transfer to the hotel.

    Gather tonight for a cocktail reception and welcome dinner at Rick’s Cafe, a tribute to the classic Casablanca "gin joint".

    Overnight: Sofitel Casablanca Tour Blanche
    Meal Plan: D

  • Day 2

    CASABLANCA

    Begin the day with a visit to the Hassan II Mosque, the largest mosque in Morocco and Africa and the 7th largest in the world. Enjoy your lunch and head for a beautiful drive to the bustling modern city of Meknes, built in the 17th century. Visit Volubilis, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and home to Morocco’s largest and best-preserved Roman ruins with excellently preserved mosaics.

    End your day as you arrive in Fez and check into your hotel.

    Overnight: Palais Ommeyad
    Meal Plan: B L

  • Day 3

    FEZ

    Today, experience the Middle Ages era in the largest living medieval city in the world Fez el-Bali (“Old Fez”), the cultural heart of Morocco. Stroll through the 9,000 lanes, alleys and souks that comprise its old quarter, where locals conduct their day-to-day business.

    Later, visit the Nejjarine Square, en-route admire the beauty of the elaborate mosaic-tiled fountain.

    Head for lunch and continue to explore the 13th century Fez el-Jadid (“New Fez”), which boasts of traditional Jewish architecture of wrought iron-decorated windows and carved wood balconies.

    This evening, enjoy the dinner at a traditional Moroccan riad with a special guest who shares insight into Moroccan life.

    Overnight: Palais Ommeyad
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 4

    RABAT

    This morning, drive to Rabat, the capital of Morocco. Begin your journey with a visit to the 12th-century Moorish Hassan Tower, this superbly preserved monument is an incomplete minaret of a great mosque. Continue to visit the city’s highlights, view the mausoleum of Mohamed V, founder of modern Morocco.

    Later visit Oudaya Kasbah, built on a bluff overlooking the Atlantic, showcasing one of the most beautiful Moorish carvings in the Moorish world.

    Overnight: Sofitel Rabat Jardin des Roses
    Meal Plan: B L

  • Day 5

    MARRAKECH

    This afternoon, you arrive in Marrakech after an overland travel. Begin to explore the beautiful place often called “Pearl of the South,” admiring its architecture with distinctive hues of brick buildings.

    Next, board a train to visit Vernazza, a gorgeous seaside town located on the west coast of Italy consisting of tall, colorful houses and a church set beside a sheltered cove. Continue to the Manarola coast, a fishing village nestled amid terraced vineyards.

    Later, if time permits visit Riomaggiore, a secluded medieval village.

    Overnight: The Pearl
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 6

    MARRAKECH

    Today, tour the Marrakech’s ancient medina, the old walled section of the city. Visit the Saadian Tombs exhibiting intricate stucco and Granada-style carvings. Later, travel to the Dar Si Said, once a palace and now the Museum of Moroccan Arts.

    Further, explore the El Badi Palace and the 12th-century Koutoubia Minaret, one of the finest examples of Islamic religious structure in North Africa. Walk through the vibrant Djemaa el- Fna square where fire-eaters, mime artists and street musicians perform.

    Later, ride on a private horse-drawn carriage ride for a return journey to your hotel. Tonight, experience the local culture and food as you dine with a local family.

    Overnight: The Pearl
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 7

    MARRAKECH

    You may choose to enjoy your last day in Marrakech by either exploring Marrakech at leisure or take a full-day excursion to the High Atlas Mountains, where you explore a picturesque village at Imlil Valley. Also get the opportunity form an authentic neighborhood connection by visiting and interacting with the local Berbers at their home.

    Tonight, enjoy the farewell dinner along with the fellow guests at a charming traditional riad.

    Overnight: The Pearl
    Meal Plan: B L D

  • Day 8

    DEPART MARRAKECH

    After breakfast you will be assisted with a transfer to the airport for your departure flight.

    Meal Plan: B
