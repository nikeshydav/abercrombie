The acclaimed True North Australia adventure cruise ship features luxury throughout, allowing passengers to explore the rugged outback in absolute style. With a crew of 18 and a passenger limit of 36, the personal service and fine dining will delight. The on-board helicopter and 6 dedicated expedition boats allow True North Australia to provide unmatched flexibility and exclusivity.

The purpose built vessel explores the upper reaches of shallow river systems and passengers need only step outside their cabins to experience the wilderness. Explore secluded beaches, bays and natural harbours as we weave among thousands of deserted islands. Whatever your cause, adventure, honeymoon, romance or eco-tourism, this getaway wilderness cruise has it all.

Kimberley Wilderness Cruise (13 nights or 6/7 nights Broome - Wyndham & Wyndham - Hunter River)

The vast, exciting Kimberley region is one of the world's last great wildernesses. Situated in the remote far north of Western Australia, it is like nowhere else on earth: Wildly beautiful landscapes of rugged mountain ranges, spectacular river gorges and majestic waterfalls.

A typical day might involve exploring an unknown estuary, trying to land the evening meal, swimming in rock pools beneath towering cascades, or viewing ancient aboriginal cave paintings. The region is steeped in curious stories of legendary human endeavor - of shipwreck, survival and tragedy. Experience the power of the largest tidal range in the world. Like everything in the Kimberley the contrasts are extreme.
