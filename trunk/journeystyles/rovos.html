Rovos Rail

The international acclaim it receives for creating truly world class travel experiences is rightly deserved by Rovos Rail, a perfect blend of period ambiance and modern features. The spacious train suites are the epitome of luxury. Refurbished using traditional and sympathetic techniques, the wood paneled coaches reflect the romance of a bygone era and set the scene for a truly bewitching journey through Africa's divinelandscape.Hauled by steam, diesel and electric locomotives, only 72 privileged passengers can take the pleasure of enjoying this elegant mode of travel at a time.

Possessing the largest train suites in the world, comfort is assured aboard the sleeper carriages. All the amenities you would expect in a modern five star hotel are provided. Each suite boasts of period Edwardian features, complete with en suite bathroom as well as double or twin beds. For an extra special experience, the Rovos Rail has Royal Suites, each of which take up half a carriage and possess their own private lounge area, full bathroom with Victorian bath and separate shower, all to make your luxury train journey an unforgettable experience.

Accommodation

There is no love sincerer than the love of food. Indulge in Rovos Rail’s fine cuisine as you travel past breathtaking scenery. Dress code is smart-casual during the day and jacket and tie for the evenings. One of three trains, the 'Pride of Africa' links some of Africa's most exciting destinations, from the 48-hour journey between Cape Town and Pretoria to the 13 day journey linking Cape Town to Dar-es-Salaam in Tanzania. Other journeys include the game reserves of Mpumalanga, the mighty Victoria Falls, desert landscapes of Namibia, the lush canefields of KwaZulu Natal, the glorious Garden Route, but perhaps the most impressive - the Cape to Cairo.
