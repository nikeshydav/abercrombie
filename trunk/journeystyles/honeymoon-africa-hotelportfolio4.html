  • BIRKENHEAD VILLA

    Located alongside Birkenhead House, Liz and Phil Biden have created a luxurious house for all the family to enjoy. Birkenhead Villa, set high on the cliffs above Walker Bay has quickly established itself as one of the most refined family friendly properties in the region.

    Perfect as an exclusive use property, the villa can also be booked on a suite by suite basis. If taken exclusively the property benefits from six bedrooms as rooms 2a and 2b are inter-leading, perfect for a young family. Defining the Birkenhead Villa experience, original artwork, exquisite cuisine and attentative personalised service. The décor is relaxed and neutral with an air of opulence. The ocean views are stunning from the suites, swimming pool or sun deck.

    Villa staff are on hand to make your stay a very special one, therefore if adults travelling with children would like a night to themselves, babysitters can be arranged and adults are welcome to use the facilities of Birkenhead House. Simple candle-lit dinners to a more elaborate private experience can always be arranged upon request. Picnic lunches can be packed for a day spent on the beach, two of Hermanus' finest swimming beaches are just a short stroll away.

    CAPE GRACE

    Nestled on a private quay between the working harbour of Cape Town's bustling Victoria & Alfred Waterfront and the serenity of an international yacht marina, the recently refashioned Cape Grace is a welcome and gracious sanctuary of traditional sophistication whilst emanating progressive style and comfort. The luxurious space within the 120 custom decorated guest rooms and suites, diversity of amenities and the unsurpassed standards in service that is both proficient and personal ensures Cape Grace is a favourite for many returning guests.

    A new addition to the hotel is "Spirit of the Cape" a privately owned luxury yacht available for the exclusive use of Cape Grace guests. She is a 56ft luxury motor yacht berthed in the yacht marina in front of the Bascule Whisky Wine & Cocktail Bar. The Bascule bar has a collection of over 400 whiskies from nearly every producing region including Scotland, Ireland, USA and Canada,

    Revitalisation of body and mind can be achieved at the African-inspired spa located on the top floor. Other facilities include: library, heated outdoor pool, hair salon and in-room workout facilities. Children are always welcome at Cape Grace. Signal Restaurant offers creative Cape cuisine that's fresh and inventive drawing on influences from the many nationalities and customs that have shaped this region since being settled in 1652. The library is the perfect place to relax during the day and serves sumptuous afternoon tea.

    The Cape Grace offers you the following complimentary added extras: use of the Hot Spa area: steam room, sauna, rain and body shower, complimentary wireless/high speed Internet access, morning tea and coffee with wake-up calls, luxury chauffeur transfers within a 20km radius from 07h00 to 23h00, subject to availability and Cape orientation and wine tasting.

    CYBELE FOREST LODGE AND HEALTH SPA

    The first Relais & Chateaux property in South Africa, this exquisite sanctuary of relaxation is nestled amongst 300 acres of forest and mountains. Here lies Cybele Forest Lodge and Health Spa. A perfect addition to any South African adventure, Cybele has so much to offer.

    Cybele is situated 420km east of Johannesburg in the heart of Mpumalanga between White River and Hazyview. It is 40km from the Phabeni Gate into the Kruger National Park and close to many of the exclusive private game reserves in the Sabi Sands. Limited to a maximum of 28 guests, housed in a variety of individual suites and cottages, privacy and fine service is assured. Original works of art adorn the walls, underfloor heating and open fires ensure that the atmosphere is cosy, particularly during the cooler winter months.

    Cybele is all about relaxation. There are superb walks on the property down through the forest to the river and along to the waterfall. With their own horses, enjoy some breathtaking rides through the forest, where experienced grooms are on hand to lead you on an adventure of discovery. The fully-equipped gym overlooks the garden and forest whilst the heated swimming pool is just a short walk away. On entering the spa you will find yourself in a completely private and peaceful zen courtyard, where lemon trees and mint bushes gently scent the air. A wide range of treatments have been designed to rejuvenate the body and soul. The cuisine at Cybele is created from the freshest of locally grown organic produce and is complemented by an award-winning wine list.

    GROOTBOS

    Located close to the seaside town of Gansbaai and set in the hills with views towards the ocean, Grootbos is unique in every sense. Set amongst indigenous fynbos and milkwood trees, the two five star lodges at Grootbos, Garden and Forest, offer stylish accommodation that is sensitive to its surroundings.

    Grootbos delivers the basics with ease, refined international cuisine, fine wines and stunning lodge accommodation along with some more refined touches. There are telescopes in the main lounge area for armchair naturalists and boat trips for those who want to get a little closer. Within the reserve itself highly-qualified resident research botanists offer fascinating guided tours around the unique ecosystem, by vehicle or on the superbly trained thoroughbred horses from Grootbos' own stables. Community projects are important at Grootbos and guests are actively encouraged to learn and engage in local township life.

    Grootbos has carved a unique niche in the upmarket eco-lodge sector by specialising in the Cape region's world-renowned flora and marine biodiversity. The multi-award winning 1,750 hectare reserve is home to more than 740 different species of plants and is a nature lover's paradise. Revered conservationist, David Bellamy has described Grootbos as 'The best example of conservation of biodiversity I have ever seen - Grootbos says it with flowers'. This is a truly exceptional nature destination and is linked with the Eden project in Cornwall.

    KURLAND

    Where the foothills of the Outeniqua Mountains sweep down to the warm waters of the Indian Ocean, emerald-green pastures and immaculate polo fields enclose this luxurious country-house hotel. Just 21 kms from Plettenberg Bay, Kurland is ideally located on the Garden Route and can provide endless activities to keep the family entertained.

    The 12 individually-designed guest suites are tailored to suit your needs: open fires, objets d'art and original oil paintings adding character to comfortable rooms with every facility imaginable. Ten rooms have special loft rooms for children and some have private plunge pools for those hot summer evenings.

    Throughout the day, horseback riding, fishing and tennis are the active options, while quieter alternatives are on offer around the swimming pool or in the hotel spa. Situated on a polo estate, there is also the opportunity to learn to play the game. Fine dining draws on the freshest local produce, beautifully presented in a fusion of traditional South African cuisine touched by the cultures of Europe, with a choice of the finest wines and brandies to make the perfect finale to every day. Time is of no consequence at Kurland, and guests set their own pace. Kurland aims to provide comfort, serenity, and great hospitality and ensures that guests have the pleasure of engaging in country pursuits and new experiences.

    KWANDWE PRIVATE GAME RESERVE

    Kwandwe means "place of the Blue Crane" in the Xhosa language and is home to a population of these splendid endangered birds. 25,000 hectares of farmland have been restored to pristine wilderness and more than 7,000 animals have been released into the reserve as part of the programme of reintroducing indigenous game to the area.

    The six suites of Ecca Lodge offer guests the ultimate in stylish and sophisticated hospitality - described as "funk meets futuristic farmhouse", all are extremely spacious. The Great Fish River Lodge is set along the aloe-studded banks of the Great Fish River, nine suites feature private plunge pools and spacious thatched game viewing decks with magnificent views of the wild. Uplands Homestead, a sensitively restored 1905 farmhouse comprising of three spacious en suite bedrooms, is available for exclusive use. The latest addition is Melton Manor a contemporary Eastern Cape homestead with four en suite rooms.

    Skilled rangers lead twice-daily game drives, in search of the 'Big Five' and other preditors like African wild dog. Optional adventures are available to explore rock paintings, battlefields and the scenic flight of the blue crane explores the pristine coastline of the region. An interactive kitchen and stylish lap pool are alternative diversions for those wishing to totally unwind. Excellent service and hospitality is guaranteed, as a member of Relais & Chateaux.

    LA RESIDENCE

    Nestled within a 30 acre private wine and olive estate in the Franschhoek Valley, a mere forty-five minutes drive from Cape Town. At La Residence, world-class service is the order of the day.

    Although from the exterior the building has been designed along the lines of a rustic Tuscan Villa, La Residence offers 11 individually furnished suites, each with palatial bathrooms, exquisite linen, silks, plush Persian rugs, and an eclectic mix of objets d'art. A property of extreme opulence, the Marharani Suite is complete with white porcelain bed and antique tapestry headboard.

    French panache extends to every corner of La Residence. Comfortable sofas, majestic oriental hardwood antiques, and soothing silk tones create an air of grace and elegance. Expansive patios and landscaped gardens offer magnificent opportunities to relax, admire the views from the infinity pool over the olive grove and torwards the mountains. Every fine detail has an air of magic, giant open fireplaces blaze in the main lodge during winter and enormous four metre high patio doors open wide in summer to let in the cool mountain breeze. Food is of the highest quality, locally sourced fine produce is used to create bountiful banquets at breakfast and sumptuous romantic dinners.

    LION SANDS IVORY LODGE

    The Lion Sands Ivory Lodge safari experience has an ever-changing, magical rhythm in a world of glimmering landscapes and unparalleled wildlife. Set on the banks of the Sabie River, a stay at Ivory Lodge ensures the ultimate private safari experience.

    There are six ultra luxurious suites with en suite bathroom, outdoor and indoor shower, courtyard, lounge with fireplace, wooden viewing deck, patio and private plunge pool. The bedroom and bathroom areas are air-conditioned and also have overhead fans. Each of the suites has breathtaking views over the Sabie River, where you can view game from the luxury of your own private deck, telescopes are provided if you don't have binoculars to hand.

    If you can manage to leave the luxury of your suite, you can accompany a ranger and tracker on a bush walk or view the 'Big Five' from your own private safari vehicle. The vehicles at Ivory Lodge are well designed with individual seats and storage boxes complete with binoculars, wildlife books and beanbags for steadying cameras. Private dining can be arranged in the comfort of your suite or at the indoor or outdoor dining areas. Enjoy a visit to the Lalamuka Health Spa - "Lalamuka" means unwind in Shangaan. For convenience, treatments can be offered on the deck of your suite overlooking the Sabie River. Ivory Lodge is an experience that promises to thrill, to revive the spirit and enliven long-dormant senses.

    MADIKWE SAFARI LODGE

    Madikwe Safari Lodge comprises of three lodges Lelpa Lodge, Kopano Lodge and Dithaba Lodge, each lodge is run separately and has its own main area and kitchen each with their own traditional pizza oven. Lelapa Lodge which translates from the local dialect to "family", means exactly that, the lodge has a home from home feel with lovely luxurious touches and the lodge welcomes all ages the lodge has twelve suites.

    Kopano translates to "small gathering" comprises only four suites offering a very private experience or perfect for a group of friends or family to have exclusively. Dithaba Lodge translates to mountain; the lodge is located higher up on the mountain and has incredible views, again only offering four suites an exclusive experience and perfect for couples wanting privacy. The guides at Madikwe Safari Lodge are highly trained and offer amazing safari experiences eager to share their wealth of knowledge of this incredible eco-system.

    MAKANYANE SAFARI LODGE

    Designed by a team of award winning architects, the key focus was for the lodge to blend in with nature - this has been achieved with great success. Makanyane lies hidden in the lush river-side forest of the Madikwe Game Reserve. Situated on 1,800 hectares of private ground within Madikwe's 75,000 hectares, the lodge affords spectacular views over the tranquil Marico River.

    The main lodge building, set on the edge of a ravine with views down to the waterhole has a wonderful thatched roof supported by ancient leadwood trees. A great place to relax, the lounge has two enormous rock fireplaces and opens out onto a huge wooden deck, again with spectacular views of the bush and a well stocked bar. Eight suites are linked to the main lodge via shaded winding pathways. Glass walled suites allow uninterrupted views of the river and surrounding bush, as do the sizeable private sundecks. Lavish bathrooms benefit from free standing baths, heated floors and indoor and outdoor showers.

    Guided game drives and walks through some of the reserves 75,000 hectares are sure to unveil a vast array of incredible wildlife. Alternatively sit and observe from one of Makanyane's two bush hides. For the more adventurous experience the star bed, set in the bush on a secure elevated deck. Tucked away in the forest, a spa and gym are always on hand to offer tired travellers a little pampering, or to work up an appetite for a sumptuous dinner. Priding itself on fine cuisine, executive chef, Mauritz Greeff, and his excellent team blend European and African flavours, using only the freshest of produce.

    SINGITA SWENI

    Immediately below Lebombo and set on the banks of the Sweni River, Sweni Lodge is the smallest of the Singita Lodges. Six luxurious suites, identical to Lebombo's are tucked away amongst the trees. The central area is decorated in a sea of chocolate and avocado fabrics.

    Sweni Lodge was influenced by the structures of dens, warrens and lairs found in the area. Flanking the Sweni River, this tranquil sanctuary is elevated on stilts with decks of timber. Even when you're indoors, you'll have the sensation of being close to the river and trees.

    You can travel to Singita Sweni all year round, rainy season is from December to February. May to August is peak safari season as the land is dry and animals congregate around water sources making game viewing more productive. Singita Game Reserves are involved in a number of community projects that clients canget involved with if they wish to from local schools to cookery classes that have changed people's lives.

Accommodation is exclusively limited to 48 spacious rooms, including 5 Junior Suites, the luxurious Royal Pool Suite with its own private swimming pool and the Presidential Suite. There are three dining areas in the hotel where guests can choose from a variety of cuisines.

The Spa and wellness area in the hotel is the perfect place to enjoy personal treatments and relax. A range of treatments are on offer from facials to massages. Children are welcome and the hotel is a 30 minute drive from Cape Town International Airport.
