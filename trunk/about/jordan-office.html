Founded in 1998, A&K Jordan is a division of the Abercrombie & Kent Group of Companies offering the finest and most deluxe ground operations available in Jordan. With an office in Amman, we have a full-time staff of 41, as well as numerous seasonal staff to handle larger groups and monthly fluctuations. An office of this size means that not only are we big enough to handle any request, but also small enough to offer the specialised and personal service required by today's travel professionals.

From the finest tailor made FITs to groups, incentives and cruise ship operations, we are always pleased to guarantee a unique and high level of customer service. The A&K Jordan team is multilingual with staff speaking fluent English, French, Russian, Ukrainian and Spanish, all of them with unparalleled experience in the tourism industry. We are particularly proud of our immediate response to requests for quotations, itineraries or other services. Due to our superior buying power, we are always in a position to give the most competitive quotations in the market. Coupled with our dedication to providing the highest quality transport, drivers and multilingual guides in the market, we are confident that nobody in Jordan offers the quality or value synonymous with Abercrombie & Kent across the globe.
