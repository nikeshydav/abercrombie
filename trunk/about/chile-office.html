A&K Chile was founded in 2001 with the opening of our office in Santiago, the third A&K office to open in South America following those in Lima and Cusco. Our team currently comprises 22 full-time professionals and is managed by Pedro Barraza who, with almost 20 years of experience in the Chilean tourism industry, boasts unrivalled knowledge and a long list of extremely satisfied clients. The size of our team means that we are big enough to handle the largest of operations, such as congresses, incentives and cruise operations, but we are also small enough to offer a specialised, tailor-made and personal service without losing sight of the finer details. We always seek to provide a proactive approach to the booking process, as well as a unique operational approach to the destination through exclusive services such as our meet and greet service behind customs and immigration in Santiago, or our private lake crossing in the Lake District.
