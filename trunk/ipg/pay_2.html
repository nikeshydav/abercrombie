Make your online payment to Abercrombie & Kent in 3 simple steps:

At Abercrombie & Kent, customer convenience is given utmost priority. So in order to facilitate your payment, we now offer the online payment option.
Step 1. Provide your online payment details
 Enter your Abercrombie & Kent Reference Number, Name, Email Id, Mobile Number, Payment Amount and click on "PAY".
 Verify your details and click on "Confirm".
 You will be securely redirected to the bank payment interface of your chosen net banking option.
Step 2. Confirm payment (at Bank)
 Enter your authentication details [viz Card details]
 Confirm your payment amount.
Step3. Receive online confirmation
 You will receive an online transaction confirmation and a Transaction Reference Number.
 If your transaction is successful, your account will get debited online.
