UAE Highlights & Sightseeing

  • Abu Dhabi

    Abu Dhabi, the capital city of the United Arab Emirates (UAE) is also the largest of the seven individual emirates that make up the country. A true representative of the ancient culture and heritage of Arabia, Abu Dhabi has a strong tradition of desert hospitality and welcomes every visitor with a genuine warmth and friendliness.

    Abu Dhabi boasts of a coastline studded with uninhabited islands, thus making it the famous 'island city' of the Gulf. Admire the beautiful tree-lined boulevards, lush green gardens and the Abu Dhabi fountain bordering the Corniche walkway, fascinating to explore on foot. This shopper's paradise also offers a world class culinary experience along with the best entertainment opportunities.

    Venture inland and discover the dramatic desert, mountains and wadis on a safari. Combining year round sunshine and superb leisure and recreational facilities, Abu Dhabi offers everything from a global cultural experience, an action packed holiday to a relaxing escape.

    Dubai

    Strategically located on the southern shore of the Persian Gulf, Dubai is thus called 'Pearl of the Persian Gulf'. It is the second largest Emirate and one of the most multicultural cities in the world, with the expatriate community constituting 85% of the population.

    Discover the magnificent skyscrapers, including the world's current tallest building, The Burj Khalifa. Hop on for a magical balloon ride over giant red sand dunes, emerald green oases, gazelles and wandering camels; be welcomed by the warmth of the desert people on landing. Set off to create memorable adventure filled memories with sand skiing and dune driving or feast in the desert whilst watching a belly dancer perform.

    A shopaholic's dream, Dubai is ranked as the world's second most popular destination for shopping after the United States and ahead of many other popular shopping destinations. Dubai, a city with traditional past interlinked with modern day, is definitely a unique travel destination to explore.
