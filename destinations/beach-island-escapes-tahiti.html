Travel To Tahiti


Tahiti conjures up visions of a paradise of earth. Picture perfect white sand beaches, turquoise blue lagoons, lush green mountains typify 118 exotic islands of Tahiti.

Abercrombie & Kent Holidays in Tahiti bring you the integral romance with nature in these exquisite islands spreading over 2 million square miles.

Appropriately known as the Queen of the Pacific, the main island of Tahiti is the usual point to start your travel to Tahiti and is majestically surrounded by mountains and adorned with deep valleys, waterfalls, and tropical flowers in the interior. Moorea, the magical Island, fittingly epitomizes a South Sea island with its colorful tapestry, souring green peaks and sparkling waters.

The Romantic Island of Bora Bora, mesmerizes with its striking rich tropical slopes, white sand beaches not so far from underwater coral gardens brimming with marine life. Huahine is another sultry getaway where you can experience Polynesian way of life with friendly locals and their culture. Taha'a is serene island shaped like a flower, is aptly dubbed the Vanilla Island, thanks to its subtle vanilla scented breeze across the coasts and lagoons.
