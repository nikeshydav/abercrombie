Sanctuary Yangzi Explorer

Yangzi River , China

The Sanctuary Yangzi Explorer is one of only two five star vessels operating in the astonishing Three Gorges region of China. Elegant and stylish, it is perfect for relaxed cruising through spectacular and ever-changing landscapes. A large sun deck offers outstanding photo opportunities and is complemented by the Viewing Lounge and Cigar Bar, which is the place to soak up the evening scenery over a relaxed pre-dinner drink.

The Sanctuary Yangzi Explorer sets a new standard for luxury on one of the most spectacular waterways on earth. On-board facilities include a huge spa and fitness centre, a two-tiered theatre, coffee lounge and a multi-language library.

Embark on shore excursions in the company of experienced and knowledgeable guides, to learn about the river's history and ecology. After a day's exploration, return for sundowners in the Viewing Lounge or on the Sun Deck. For a romantic and intimate meal, we recommend the vessel's exclusive private dining rooms for two, where you will enjoy delicious à la carte food and a selection of French and new world wines.

Accommodation

The Sanctuary Yangzi Explorer's complement is limited to 124 passengers, allowing her staterooms to set a new standard in spaciousness and elegance, from a roomy Deluxe Cabin to the palatial, exclusive suites.

The Explorer's 62 air-conditioned cabins, including 24 suites, all have balconies, panoramic windows and private bathrooms. Each is equipped with a shower, hairdryer, satellite television with in-house movies, a CD player, direct dial international telephone and mini bar.

Our favourite are the four stunning Speciality Suites - the 732 square foot Jade and Mandarin Suites and 829 square foot Celestial and Imperial Suites - which are beautifully furnished and the largest available anywhere on the Yangzi River.
