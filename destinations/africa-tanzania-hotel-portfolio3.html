Suggested Katavi Accommodation

  • Chada Katavi

    Katavi National Park is as dramatic as anywhere you'll ever visit in one lifetime - one of the greatest wildlife experiences of Africa. Miles from anywhere, it has an almost mythical status and a colossal density of mammals, from elephant and cheetah to crocodile and hippo.

    Almost everywhere you look, there's something grazing, bellowing, fighting, mating or hunting. Holing up at Chada Katavi, the only camp in a million wild acres, is a rather civilised event, the perfect retreat after a day of thrilling safari adventure and discovery on wheels or on foot. Creature comforts abound in this untamed world. Hidden in the acacia and tamarind trees are just six romantic roomy tents with fine wooden furniture, large shade net windows that let in light and air, and private de luxe bathrooms. Fabulous bush cuisine is served in your remote hideaway at dinnertime.

    Climb kilimanjaro

    Kilimanjaro is the highest mountain in Africa, standing at 19,340 feet (5,896m). Located in Northern Tanzania, bordering Kenya, it also spans five different ecological zones, making it one of the most varied peaks on Earth to climb.

    Nearly 800 clients climb Kilimanjaro with A&K every year and we are the only international tour operator with our own dedicated Kilimanjaro climbing operation. We use only the finest mountain apparatus, safety equipment, communications network and specialist tents on the mountain. Safety is our priority, therefore we are one of the few operators carrying oxygen bottles, altitude chambers, first aid kits and multiple communication measures on every climb. All of our guides are trained in first aid, mountain craft, natural history as well as ecological awareness, ensuring a once in a lifetime experience.

    The Routes
    There are a number of routes to choose from for the climb. The durations detailed are the minimum number of days to climb, however we strongly advise additional acclimatisation days where possible. In addition to the detailed Machame and Marangu routes, we can also guide you on the Umbwe and Rongai routes.
