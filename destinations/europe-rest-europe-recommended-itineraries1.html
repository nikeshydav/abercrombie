Paris Fashion Tour

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 4 Days

    A 4 day glamorous visit to the French Capital to experience fashion and shopping at its best. Tour the main sights of Paris then treat yourself with a visit to the main fashion houses and boutiques including Chritstian Dior, Coco Chanel and Louis Vuitton

    Highlights: Paris, Eiffel Tower, Arc de Triomphe, Champs Elysées, Ile de la Cité, Notre Dame Cathedral, Fashion Houses and boutiques-Christian Dior, Coco Chanel and Louis Vuitton

  • Day 1

    Arrive Paris

    Upon arrival at Paris airport you will be welcomed to France by your local Guardian Angel as you exit customs into the arrivals hall, and transferred to your hotel where you will be assisted with check-in procedures and you will also receive your luxurious welcome hamper upon arrival at your hotel.

    On the Rue de Rivoli overlooking the Tuileries Gardens, the Hotel Meurice boasts an excellent location in the heart of Paris as it is nestled between the Place de la Concorde and the Grand Louvre. Also nearby are the Opéra Garnier, the jewelry stores on the rue de la Paix, and the Place Vendôme. Original antiques and paintings are featured throughout the hotel and every floor is decorated in a distinctive style.

    Overnight: Hotel Le Meurice

  • Day 2

    PARIS

    Today you will enjoy a half day (4 hours) sightseeing tour in Paris with your private vehicle and expert private English-speaking guide. This overview tour will include famous Parisian sights such as the Eiffel Tower, Arc de Triomphe, Champs Elysées and the Ile de la Cité, where you will visit the beautiful Notre Dame Cathedral.

    This afternoon your private fashion guide will open for you the doors of legendary Parisian fashion houses such as Christian Dior and the famous

    Coco Chanel, allowing you a privileged look inside the world of French high fashion. On your VIP visit you will be greeted by the boutique director and given a personalized behind-the-scenes tour.

    Throughout the tour, your expert guide will share with you the history behind the famous names of this luxury industry. The grand finale of your half-day fashion experience, time and energy permitting, will be a visit to the world-renowned Louis Vuitton flagship boutique with its spectacular views over the rooftops of Paris. Your custom-designed program with exclusive access to the top design houses, selected according to your expressed interests, will be arranged by special appointment. Smart dress and comfortable shoes are recommended for this foray into the world of supreme elegance.

    Overnight: Hotel Le Meurice
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    Paris

    Today your own specialist guide opens the doors to the most talked-about boutiques and takes you behind-the-scenes into the workshops of the new generation of cutting-edge designers. She introduces you to these young talents who will take the time to explain to you their design inspiration and techniques while presenting their collection. Meet upcoming design stars like Valentine Gauthier. Visit her workshop to learn more about her creative process, and then enjoy a presentation of her new collection (upon availability).

    Discover the new generation of trend-setting designers in the world's fashion capital, such as Korean designer Moon Young Hee, and visit her superb atelier and recent collections. Learn about the personal inspiration behind some of the most creative design in Paris. Explore the fashion showrooms and art galleries that line the picturesque Rues de Charlot, Poitou and Bretagne, and visit the Hotel du Petit Moulin decorated by Christian Lacroix. This tour can be customized to suit your personal fashion interests

    Alternatively you could participate in this 'Sex & the City' tour:

    Designed for Carrie Bradshaw lovers, for those who wish to relive her romantic escapade in Paris, our private tour will take you to her favorite fashion spots in the City of Light. Your own specialist guide will meet you for coffee at the Plaza Athénée, one of Paris' most luxurious "palace" hotels where Carrie stayed with Alec Petrovsky. She will open the doors of Chanel, Christian Dior and Sonia Rykiel, the legendary Parisian fashion houses where our heroine literally fell head over heels for French design.

    A lunch reservation will be made for you at L'Avenue, a restaurant popular with Parisian fashionistas.In the afternoon your personal fashion guide will take you to Colette, the trendiest fashion shop in Paris with the latest 'it' designs, on the Faubourg St. Honoré. Once you have tried on some of the most glamorous and funky looks, you will then discover Christian Louboutin's flagship store and his gorgeous and sexy red-soled shoes. Conclude the day on a sweet note at the delicious pastry shop (patisserie) where Carrie takes refuge from the rain and indulges herself with to-die-for French pastries.

    Overnight: Hotel Le Meurice
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    Depart Paris

    Today you will be privately transferred to Paris airport.

    Meal Plan: B

BOHEMIAN PARIS

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 4 Days

    A special 4 day tour of the French capital, exploring all the main sights of one of the most romantic cities in the world.

    Overnight: Eiffel Tower, Arc de Triomphe, Champs Elysées, Notre Dame Cathedral, Versailles

  • Day 1

    ARRIVE PARIS

    Upon arrival at Paris airport you will be met in the arrival hall by your driver and privately transferred to your hotel. You will find a complimentary bottle of champagne in your room upon your arrival to celebrate the beginning of your mini-moon.

    On the Rue de Rivoli overlooking the beautiful Tuileries Gardens, the Hotel Meurice boasts an excellent location in the heart of Paris as it is nestled between the Place de la Concorde and the Grand Louvre. Also nearby are the Opéra Garnier, the Jewellery stores on the rue de la Paix, and the Place Vendôme. Original antiques and paintings are featured throughout the hotel and every floor is decorated in a distinctive style.

    Overnight: Hotel Le Meurice

  • Day 2

    PARIS

    This morning you will enjoy a half day (4 hours) sightseeing tour in Paris with your private vehicle and English-speaking guide. This overview tour will include famous Parisian sights such as the Eiffel Tower, Arc de Triomphe, Champs Elysées and the Ile de la Cité, where you will visit the beautiful Notre Dame Cathedral.

    This afternoon is at your leisure - you may like to explore Montmartre. This district is forever associated with the idealistic Bohemian lifestyles of the late 19th and early 20th centuries. Parisians call it the 'Butte' (knoll) as it is the highest point in the city. Discover Montmartre's romantic charms along the winding back streets, small squares and terraces. Many artists paint quick-fire portraits in the 'Place du Tertre'. Walk up to the Sacré-Coeur Basilica for spectacular views over the city. Other famous landmarks of Montmartre include the 'Moulin Rouge', a 19-century windmill transformed into a world-famous cabaret and immortalized by the painter Toulouse-Lautrec, the 'Place des Abbesses', a picturesque square which is now a hub of the fashion world, or the Dalí Centre, a unique exhibition entirely devoted to the master of surrealism, and more particularly to his sculptures and engravings.

    Overnight: Hotel Le Meurice
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    PARIS - VERSAILLES - PARIS

    Enjoy a full-day (up to 8 hours) excursion tour to Versailles with your private vehicle and English-speaking guide. Situated to the west of Paris, Versailles is the location of the huge palace that was constructed in the 17th century for Louis XIV, the 'Sun King'. It became the model for grand houses of royalty all over Europe. Take a tour of the opulent State Apartments that culminate in the magnificent Gallery of Mirrors.

    Enjoy a stroll in the formal ornamental gardens that are embellished with many fountains, and see the Grand Canal that stretches into the distance across the park; a beautiful spot to indulge in an intimate picnic for two which has been especially prepared for you. Return to Paris following your visit.

    Overnight: Hotel Le Meurice
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    DEPART PARIS

    Today you will be transferred to the Paris airport by private vehicle to board your flight.

    Meal Plan: B

Paris and the Loire Valley

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 9 days

    A 9-day private tour showcasing the historical and cultural highlights of Paris and the Loire Valley during the Fête de la Musique.

    Highlights: Paris, Versailles, Loire Valley, Onzain, Chartres, Amboise

  • Day 1

    ARRIVE PARIS

    Upon arrival at Paris airport you will be welcomed to France by your local Guardian Angel as you exit customs into the arrivals hall, and transferred to your hotel where you will be assisted with check-in procedures.

    You will be confirmed for 5 nights at the Relais Christine.

    Overnight: Relais Christine

  • Day 2

    PARIS

    Today you will enjoy a half day (4 hours) sightseeing tour in Paris with your private vehicle and English-speaking guide. This overview tour will include famous Parisian sights such as the Eiffel Tower, Arc de Triomphe, Champs Elysêes and the Ile de la Citê, where you will visit the beautiful Notre Dame Cathedral and see the Sainte Chapelle, a pearl of Gothic architecture.

    The rest of the day is at leisure.

    Overnight: Relais Christine
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    PARIS

    Today you will enjoy a half day (4 hours) sightseeing in Paris to include the world-famous Louvre Museum. Originally the Royal residence of the Kings of France, the sprawling Louvre palace contains one of the most important art collections in the Western world, spanning from remotest antiquity to the mid 19th cent, with the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo as perhaps its two most famous exhibits, and the upturned glass pyramid by I.M.Pei at the entrance adding a contemporary touch.

    Overnight: Relais Christine
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    PARIS

    This afternoon and this evening your Guardian Angel and private vehicle will be at your disposal to enjoy the free events at the Fête de la Musique. The annual street music festival takes place in Paris where musicians gather in the open air or public buildings to give free performances. Visitors can enjoy concerts of all styles, from classic and jazz to pop and contemporary (as this event is held on specific dates we have included it to give you an illustration of the type of inclusions/events we can offer, if you have clients who are travelling at a different time of year but want a similar itinerary please do contact us to discuss the options).

    Overnight: Relais Christine
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 5

    VERSAILLES

    Today you will enjoy a half day (5 hours) sightseeing tour to Versailles with your private vehicle and English-speaking guide. You will drive out to Versailles to admire the huge palace of the great Sun King Louis XIV, visiting the State Apartments and decorative gardens.

    The rest of the day is at leisure.

    Overnight: Relais Christine
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 6

    PARIS - ONZAIN

    You will be met this morning by your private vehicle and English-speaking driver-guide for your transfer to Onzain. You will drive to Rambouillet to visit the Chateau surrounded by forests and an extensive park. Continue to Chartres where you will see the magnificent Gothic cathedral with its world-famous stained glass windows, before arriving at your hotel.

    You will be confirmed for 2 nights at the Domaine des Hauts de Loire.

    Overnight: Domaine des Hauts de Loire
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 7

    LOIRE VALLEY

    This morning you will meet your English-speaking driver-guide for a full-day (8 hours) sightseeing tour in the Loire Valley by private vehicle. Visit the Chateau de Chenonceau that straddles the river and is one of the best-known Chateaux of the Loire region. Continuing on to Amboise you will visit the Clos Lucê, a handsome Renaissance manor where Leonardo da Vinci spent the last four years of his life. The museum contains working models of some of da Vinci's inventions. This afternoon enjoy the Chateau d'Ussê, sometimes described as the original "Sleeping Beauty Castle", and the 16th-century chapel in the garden.

    Overnight: Domaine des Hauts de Loire
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 8

    ONZAIN - PARIS

    Spend the morning at leisure, relaxing and enjoying the facilities at your hotel. This afternoon you will be privately transferred to your hotel at Charles de Gaulle Airport for the last night of your tour.

    You will be confirmed for 1 night at the Sheraton Airport Hotel at Charles de Gaulle Airport.

    Overnight: Sheraton Airport Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 9

    PARIS

    Please make your own way to your check-in desk for your departure flight.

PARIS, DIJON AND VENCE

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 10 days

    A 10 day tour including a city break to Paris followed by a relaxing retreat to Dijon and Vence.

    Highlights: Paris, Dijon, Beaune, Burgundy, Vence

  • Day 1

    ARRIVAL IN PARIS

    Upon arrival at Paris airport, you will be met by your representative and transferred by private vehicle to your hotel in Paris, where you will be assisted with check-in.

    You will be confirmed at the Hôtel Balzac for 3 nights.

    Overnight: H?tel Balzac, Paris

  • Day 2

    PARIS

    After breakfast, enjoy a full-day sightseeing tour with your private vehicle and English-speaking guide in Paris. Visit the Marais, one of Paris's most historic districts. This area houses many former residences of aristocratic families, which have now been restored and are now museums. You will see the Place des Vosges, lined with pink brick and covered arcades. You will have the opportunity to visit the fascinating Carnavalet Museum, dedicated to the history of Paris itself. The museum is housed in a 1548 town house and a 17th-century mansion featuring objets d'art, furniture, paintings and sculptures. Continue to the Ile de la Cité, where you will visit the breathtaking Sainte Chapelle, a gem of Gothic architecture built in 1248 by Louis IX.

    This afternoon experience Montmartre, a different quarter of Paris forever associated with the Bohemian lifestyles of the late 19th and early 20th centuries. Parisians call it the "Butte" (knoll) as it is the highest point in the city. Discover Montmartre's charms along the winding back streets, small squares and terraces. You will see many artists painting quick-fire portraits in the "place du Tertre" and have stunning views from the Sacré-Coeur.

    You may wish to visit the Dalí Exhibition, a unique permanent exhibition in France entirely devoted to the master of surrealism. The nearby Place des Abbesses, one of the capital's most picturesque squares, is also worth exploring. You will find original boutiques selling clothes, accessories or vintage furniture.

    This evening why not soak up the atmosphere of a Parisian jazz club or have a romantic dinner in a trendy restaurant?

    Overnight: Hôtel Balzac, Paris
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    GIVERNY & AUVRES-SUR-OISE

    Meet your driver and English-speaking guide for a full day excursion to Giverny and Auvers-sur-Oise. First head to Giverny and visit to the Fondation Claude Monet. The house, where the Master of Impressionism lived for over 40 years, is decorated in the original color schemes he admired and in the gardens you will see the famous Japanese bridge and water lily pond, his greatest source of inspiration. Continue your journey and explore the charming riverside village of Auvers-sur-Oise. Many painters found inspiration there: Cézanne, Van Gogh, Pissarro and Daubigny. Van Gogh only stayed there seventy days but he painted seventy-two pictures. You will see the famous Auberge Ravoux, the inn where he sojourned, the famous church and the castle, now a museum dedicated to the artists who made the village famous.

    Overnight: Hôtel Balzac, Paris
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    PARIS - DIJON

    Today you will be met at your hotel and privately transferred with your representative to the Paris railway station to begin your assisted rail journey. Here you will be shown to your pre-reserved 1st class seating and your luggage will be handled onboard for you. Upon arrival at the Dijon railway station you will be met from the door of the train and your luggage will be handled for you.

    After lunch at leisure, enjoy a walking tour of Dijon. Once the capital city of the mighty Dukes of Burgundy before coming under the control of the French crown, Dijon is still the capital of the Burgundy region and its wonderful old quarters are among the most interesting in France. Stroll through a network of narrow streets and small squares with fine 15th-16th century mansions. The medieval Verrerie street with its half-timbered houses is particularly remarkable. Admire the 13th-century gothic church of Notre-Dame before coming up behind the Ducal Palace, which houses a prestigious art collection.

    After your tour you will be transferred to the stunning Abbaye de la Bussière.

    Overnight: Abbaye de la Bussière, La Bussière-sur-Ouche
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 5

    BEAUNE AND THE VINEYARDS

    This morning meet your driver and expert English-speaking guide for a full day sightseeing tour to include the wine country south of Dijon and a visit to the Château de Chassagne-Montrachet.

    Visit the wealthy town of Beaune, capital of wine, with its 15th century Hospices famous for its tiled roof, which has become an icon of Burgundy. The Hospices are better known as Hôtel-Dieu and were founded as a hospital to provide free care for the poor, the orphans, the elderly or pregnant women of the town.

    You will have a guided tour at the Château de Chassagne-Montrachet which produces some of the best crus of Côte de Nuits, Côte de Beaune and C?te Chalonnaise fine wines. Interestingly the Château hosts a rolling programme of exhibitions by contemporary artists. Weather permitting, your guide will take you on a short walk through the vineyards surrounding the Château.

    Overnight: Abbaye de la Bussière, La Bussière-sur-Ouche
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 6

    BURGUNDY

    You'll spend the morning at leisure, exploring the region of Burgundy.

    This afternoon enjoy an unforgettable drive on board a Citroèn 2CV. in a true French style. Various options are available: you can either have your own chauffeur or, if you feel adventurous, drive the 2CV yourself and explore at your own pace (the vehicles are equipped with pre-programmed GPS).

    Overnight: Abbaye de la Bussière, La Bussière-sur-Ouche
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 7

    DIJON - NICE - VENCE

    This morning you will be transferred to Dijon train station for your direct journey to Nice by TGV. Upon arrival at the Nice railway station you will be met from the door of the train and transferred to your hotel in the heart of the French Riviera.

    Overnight: Château Saint Martin and Spa
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 8

    VENCE

    Today will yours to relax and enjoy the hotels spa and other facilities (swimming pool, tennis court, pétanque court, fitness room, etc)

    Optional sightseeing: Meet your local English-speaking guide and private vehicle for a half-day sightseeing tour the medieval village of Saint-Paul-de-Vence. It is still a mecca for contemporary artists who keep the artistic vocation alive. Visit the Fondation Maeght, a small modern art museum set on a wooded clifftop. This private foundation contains one of the most important collections in Europe, with paintings, sculptures drawings and other works by Braque, Calder, Chagall, Giacometti, Léger or Miró.

    Overnight: Château Saint Martin and Spa
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 9

    VENCE

    You will have another leisurely day for relaxation.

    Optional sightseeing: This morning you will be met by your private vehicle and English-speaking driver to be transferred into Nice. Upon arrival, spend the morning tasting delicacies in the Old Town, learn all about Provençal olive oils and have a light Mediterranean meal prepared with only the freshest ingredients from the market. Your guide will be a food critic and cookbook author who is fascinated with Nice and its unique culinary tradition, a result of its history and climate. After 2pm you will be free to spend time at leisure in the city for sightseeing or shopping before being transferred back to your hotel.

    Overnight: Château Saint Martin and Spa
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 10

    DEPARTURE

    Today you will be privately transferred to the airport to board your homebound flight.

    Meal Plan: B
