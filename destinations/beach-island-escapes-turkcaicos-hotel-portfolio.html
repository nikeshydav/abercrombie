Turks & Caicos Hotel Portfolio

  • AMANYARA

    The resort derives its name from aman, the Sanskrit word for 'peace', and yara, the word for 'place' in the language of the Arawak Amerindians - and lives up to its billing effortlessly. Enjoying a pristine location well removed from much of the development on the eastern side of the island, it is a private and exclusive hideaway alongside turquoise waters - the perfect opportunity to savour an authentic tropical paradise.

    Pavilions and villas are scattered amongst tropical vegetation and ponds, perched on rugged stone headlands and are steps from an endless fine-sand alabaster beach. In terms of interiors, the scheme is undeniably contemporary, with elegant living spaces giving way to outdoor areas where timber sun decks extend over tranquil ponds.

    The Amanyara really is a slice of luxury island life at its finest and most relaxed.

    GRACE BAY CLUB

    Mediterranean in design, Grace Bay Club is a member of 'Leading Hotels of the World'. Located on beautiful Grace Bay, pampering, service and privacy come naturally to this excellent hotel, which includes The Hotel - 21 adult-only suites - and Villas, with its 38 family-friendly suites and villas.

    In terms of ambiance, this resort is all about relaxed sophistication with an authentic, Caribbean flavour. The accommodation is set within an expansive 11-acre property, which creates a real sense of space, even though the resort is directly on the renowned shores of Grace Bay Beach.

    The suites at The Hotel at Grace Bay Club are spectacular. Ranging from Junior Suites to Two Bedroom Suites, each with views of the ocean and a private terrace, they provide stylish, spacious accommodation with all the mod cons. The Villas at Grace Bay Club range from Junior to Four Bedroom Penthouse Suites and are the very definition of barefoot luxury.

    PARROT CAY AND COMO SHAMBHALA RETREAT

    Devotees of intimate Caribbean retreats should seriously consider the seductively beautiful Parrot Cay. With a low-key ambience and Zen-like tranquillity it is an absolute delight on a private island bordered by pure, white-sand beaches and crystal-clear turquoise water. This 50-acre resort is accessible only via a half-hour boat ride from Providenciales.

    The resort's two and three bedroom beach villas boast their own private swimming pools and butler service, whilst one-bedroom villas and beach houses offer their own private plunge pools. Interiors are whitewashed with teak finishing, whilst four-posters beds are wrapped in soft cottons that billow with the breeze - all this combines to create a pared down aesthetic that is unerringly sleek.

    Simplicity and space are the watchwords throughout - though all rooms are enriched by clean-lined furniture, open-air day beds, cavernous bathrooms and the highest quality linens.

    REGENT PALMS

    Regent Palms sets the standard for luxury on the beautiful Turks & Caicos Islands, 365 miles of stunning coral reef, beaches, cays and islets. Inspired by the classic estates of the Caribbean and Oliver Messel in the overall style of architecture and design, Regent Palms offers maximum pampering, relaxation and indulgence.

    An ultimate treat is The Penthouse Residence, boasting an expansive terrace, a 'Sun Suite' with boundless ocean views and an outdoor shower garden and well equipped kitchen. Dining is a highlight and excels at the fine dining restaurant Parallel23, while an array of liquors and cocktails are enjoyed in The Green Flamingo Bar, often accompanied by live music from local musicians.

    The world class Spa, the infinity pool, the12 acres of lushly landscaped gardens, the spacious luxurious residences, and the beautiful beach of Grace Bay go just some way to explaining why this hotel is so delightful.

    THE VERANDA RESORT & RESIDENCES

    The Veranda is an all-inclusive resort set on the pristine white sands of Grace Bay Beach which is managed by the award winning Grace Bay Resorts. There are 123 suites and villas with sea or garden views, as well as the island's only private beach-front homes - all of which have their own private pools. The property's relaxed feel invites you to unwind. There are several dining options to suit your mood, whether you're looking for a family-friendly international experience or an authentic and romantic dinner for two. The seafront bar was designed by a cadre of international architects and has a contemporary design and laid back atmosphere.

    Tranquility awaits at Sabai Spa, which has an extensive menu including everything from pedicures and massages to aromatherapy. The Veranda also has a fitness centre with a gym and floodlit tennis courts, where private yoga instruction and group classes are on offer. The resort's water sports pavilion offers some of the world's best sailing, snorkelling, and sea kayaking right at your doorstep. There are three swimming pools, one of which is reserved solely for adults. The children's club offers a variety of activities for children between 3 and 12 which are designed to be interactive, educational, eco-friendly and fun. Additionally, children can sign up for afternoon "kids only" excursions. Teens can sign up for extreme excursions including kite boarding, scuba diving, and parasailing. Babysitting is also available on-site.

    Enjoy some of the best scuba diving in the world or a private romantic trip to a deserted beach.
