Suggested Phuket accommodation

  • Amanpuri

    With sculptured roof-lines under tropical skies, Amanpuri is a tranquil oasis in a secluded natural coconut grove above a soft, crescent shaped beach. Elevated walkways weave through statuesque palms to reach a cool reception area and a large slate-blue pool.

    Stone steps sweep down to the beach and the mesmerising Andaman Sea, where the gentle fragrance of the frangipani flower delicately scents the sea air. Cruise to Shark Point, where crystal clear waters and shallow diving await. Famous for its tame leopard sharks, the area is also home to bright-purple sea anemones, snappers and schools of moray eels. If you are looking for total escape, take a stroll to The Spa, a holistic sanctuary shaded by whispering coconut palms. Pamper yourself with an impossibly soothing body wrap treatment.For an unusual dining experience, head for the Naoki restaurant, where authentic Kaiseki fare blends the finest French techniques with the artistry of Japanese cuisine. Round off the day in style, sitting on the terrace to watch the sunset over the darkening Andaman Sea.

    Anantara, Phuket

    Sitting between sun-blessed beaches and verdant hills, Anantara Phuket is just steps away from the waters of the Andaman Sea. Its 82 One and Two Bedroom Villas echo southern Thai villages, with locally inspired architecture to complement their indigenous garden setting. Open spaces and private pools take advantage of the cool sea breeze, whilst stylish interiors embrace contemporary Thai design, using local prints, textiles and paintings in relaxed lounge spaces.

    Take time to explore in style, with a luxury yacht cruise through the mysterious karsts of Phang Nga Bay, or wander the viewpoints and waterfalls of the island's unseen hinterlands with a local guide. Visit our favourite, Sea.Fire.Salt restaurant, to feast on freshly grilled seafood, enhanced to perfection by the suggestions from the resident salt sommelier. Round the day off in romantic style with a magnificent Margarita in the fabulous Treehouse Bar.

    Andara Resort and Villas

    Andara Resort & Villas offers the ultimate Phuket holiday experience. Nestled on a gentle hillside overlooking the pristine Andaman Sea and blending seamlessly into the natural tropical surroundings, Andara is the perfect answer for every premium holiday occasion.

    Each and every property enjoys a breathtaking sea view, private pools, and a modern design accented by Thai influence and complimented with lush tropical landscaping.

    Banyan Tree Phuket

    Banyan Tree Phuket is an oasis of tranquillity, bordered by pristine golden sands and the gentle waves of the Andaman Sea.

    While away your days by the private pool of your villa, be pampered with indulgent treatments at Banyan Tree Spa, or practice your swing at the 18-hole championship Laguna Phuket Golf Course. Watch the sunset in style with a romantic dinner cruise for two on board Sanya Rak, the resort's traditional long-tail boat.
