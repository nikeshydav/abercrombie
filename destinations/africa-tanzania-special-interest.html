Tanzania Safari and Zanzibar

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 7 days

    From the bush to the beach, discover the wilderness of Tanzania as well as the palm-fringed island of Zanzibar.

    Highlights: Arusha, Ngorongoro Crater, Serengeti National Park, Zanzibar.

  • Day 1

    Arrive Arusha

    Upon arrival at JRO Kilimanjaro International Airport you will be met by your Tanzanian driver and transferred to Kia Lodge only a short drive away.

    Overnight: Kia Lodge

  • Day 2

    Arusha - Ngorongoro Crater

    After breakfast, you will be transferred to the airport to board your chartered flight to Manyara airstrip. At Manyara you will be met and transferred to Ngorongoro Sopa Lodge in time for lunch.

    Once lunch is finished you will commence your tour of Ngorongoro Crater, descending into the caldera itself for truly amazing game viewing. On the way back to your lodge visit a Maasai village to learn more about this ancient culture. Later on as the sun sets arrive back at the lodge to relax before dinner is served. Why not opt for sundowner cocktails whilst watching the sun set?

    Overnight: Ngorongoro Serena Lodge
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 3

    Ngorongoro Crater - Serengeti National Park

    After breakfast, begin the journey to Serengeti National Park. Before leaving the Ngorongoro conservation area you will visit Olduvai Gorge, the archaeological site that became well known after the discovery of human fossils in 1959.

    En route enjoy a game drive. Upon arrival at Serengeti Sopa Lodge, you will receive a welcome drink and fragranced towels to refresh yourself. There will be time to relax before lunch is served. Later on depart for your afternoon game drive on these famous plains.

    Early evening arrive back at the lodge in time for dinner.

    Overnight: Serengeti Serena Lodge
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 4

    Serengeti - Zanzibar

    After breakfast you will depart for your morning game drive. Return to your lodge to check out and have lunch, after which you will be transferred to Seronera airstrip to board your chartered flight to Zanzibar.

    Upon arrival you will be met and transferred to Karafuu Hotel Resort. After checking into your room the time is yours to refresh before dinner is served.

    Overnight:La Gemma Dell'Est
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 5

    Zanzibar

    Spend the next two days discovering everything that this beautiful island has to offer, from water sports and sensational diving trips to excursions to Stone Town to learn about Zanzibar's past. Peruse the antique shops in the vibrant town centre, sample the different spices and learn how Zanzibar locals make use of them in cooking and as a natural remedy for various ailments.

    In the evenings relax with a delicious cocktail made with the freshest fruits from the island while the fishermen's dhows sail across the horizon for the perfect photo opportunity.

    Optional Services:

    • Safari Blue with Adventure Afloat - swimming, snorkelling and looking for dolphins in the warm waters to the south of the island.
    • A morning Stone Town tour combined with an afternoon trip to the Slave Caves.
    • A morning Stone Town tour combined with an afternoon Spice tour.
    • A morning Stone Town tour combined with an afternoon Jozani Forest tour.

    After your full day, arrive back at your hotel late afternoon/early evening with time to relax and freshen up before dinner.

    Overnight: La Gemma Dell'Est
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 6

    Zanzibar

    As above.

    Overnight: La Gemma Dell'Est
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 7

    Depart Zanzibar

    After breakfast morning is at leisure. Lunch will be served at Karafuu Hotel Beach Resort before you are transferred to Zanzibar Airport to board your flight to Dar es Salaam.

    Upon arrival you will be transferred to Kempinski where you will be having dinner. After dinner is finished you will be driven back to Dar airport to connect with your flight home.

    Meal Plan: B,L,D

Luxury Tanzania

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 9 days

    Ideal for first time visitors to Tanzania, explore the country's wilderness and wildlife before relaxing in Zanzibar.

    Highlights: Arusha, Tarangire National Park, Ngorongoro Crater, Serengeti National Park, Zanzibar.

  • Day 1

    Arrive Arusha

    Upon arrival at JRO Kilimanjaro International Airport you will be met by your Tanzanian drivers and transferred to your hotel. Upon arrival to the hotel you will check into your rooms then you are at leisure before dinner is served.

    Optional Services:

    • African drummers and dancers
    • Non-alcoholic or alcoholic welcome drink

    Overnight: Kibo Palace Hotel
    Meal Plan: D

  • Day 2

    Arusha - Tarangire

    After breakfast you will be transferred to Tarangire National Park, famous for its large herds of elephants, and the characteristic landscape dotted with baobab trees most of them over 1,000 years old. Upon arrival at the lodge you will check into your rooms then lunch will be served.

    In the afternoon you will depart for a game drive, returning to the lodge with time to unwind before dinner is served.

    Optional Services:

    • Bush lunch
    • Bush dinner
    • Sundowners

    Overnight: Tarangire Sopa Lodge
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 3

    Tarangire - Ngorongoro

    After breakfast depart for your morning game drive. Lunch will be served back at the lodge, after lunch transfer to Ngorongoro Sopa Lodge.

    Upon arrival at Ngorongoro Sopa check into your room, then time is your own to relax and refresh before dinner is served.

    Optional Services:

    • Welcome cocktail party
    • African Night with kanga gift
    • Off site check in

    Overnight: Ngorongoro Sopa Lodge
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 4

    Ngorongoro Crater

    After breakfast you will commence your tour of Ngorongoro Crater descending into the caldera floor for truly amazing game viewing. Today a delicious picnic lunch will accompany you on your game drives. On the way back to your lodge you will visit a Maasai village to learn more about this ancient culture.

    Later on as the sun sets you arrive back at the lodge with time to relax before dinner is served. Why not opt for sundowners cocktails whilst watching the sun set?

    Optional Services:

    • Sundowners at Crater View
    • BBQ Dinner

    Overnight: Ngorongoro Sopa Lodge
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 5

    Ngorongoro - Serengeti

    After breakfast you will begin your journey to Serengeti. En route you will stop to visit Olduvai Gorge, the archaeological site famous for the discovery in 1959 of a humanoid fossil by Mary and Louis Leakey. The site offers a small but very informative museum.

    On your way to the lodge you will enjoy a game drive and a chance to spot wildlife. Upon arrival at the lodge check into your room then lunch will be served.

    In the afternoon you will embark on a game drive. Returning to the lodge, there is time to relax before dinner is served.

    Optional Services:

    • Bush Dinner
    • Sundowners
    • Off site check in

    Overnight: Serengeti Sopa Lodge
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 6

    Serengeti

    A wonderful full day of game viewing. After breakfast you embark on a morning game drive amongst these endless plains with acacia trees scattered across the taupe landscape. Hopefully you'll see everything from zebra, elephant and giraffe, to lion and cheetah.

    After lunch back at the lodge, set out on your afternoon game drive seeing more of the varied Tanzanian fauna and flora. On your return back to the lodge after your long day, you can freshen up and relax before dinner with your fellow guests.

    Optional Services:

    • Bush breakfast with/without Champagne
    • Bush lunch
    • Sundowners
    • Gala Dinner

    Overnight: Serengeti Sopa Lodge
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 7

    Serengeti - Zanzibar

    After breakfast you will depart for your morning game drive. Returning to the lodge you will check out of the rooms then your driver will transfer you to Seronera airstrip to board your plane to Zanzibar. A picnic lunch will accompany this day.

    Arriving in Zanzibar you will be transferred to La Gemma Dell'Est on the northwest side of the island. After checking into your rooms you can relax and enjoy the sunset with a refreshing cocktail before dinner is served.

    Optional Services:

    • Theme nights with special menu
    • Pianist
    • Snake charmer whow
    • Bali Zanzibari dance

    Overnight: La Gemma Dell'Est
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 8

    Zanzibar

    Today you have the opportunity to discover everything that this beautiful island has to offer, from water sports and sensational diving trips to excursions to Stone Town to learn about Zanzibar's past. Peruse the antique shops in the vibrant town centre, or sample the different spices and learn how Zanzibar locals make use of them in cooking and as a natural remedy for various ailments.

    In the evening relax with delicious cocktails made with the freshest fruits from the island while the fishermen's dhows sail across the horizon.

    Optional Services:

    • Safari Blue with Adventure Afloat
    • A morning Stone Town tour combined with an afternoon trip to Slave Caves
    • A morning Stone Town tour combined with an afternoon Spice tour
    • A morning Stone Town tour combined with an afternoon Jozani Forest tour

    Overnight: La Gemma Dell'Est
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 9

    Depart Zanzibar

    After breakfast morning is at leisure. Lunch will be served at La Gemma Dell'Est then in the afternoon you will be transferred to Zanzibar Airport to board your flight to Dar es Salaam.

    Upon arrival at Dar International Airport you will be transferred to Kilimanjaro Kempinski for dinner. After dinner is finished you will be transferred back to the airport in time to board your flight back home.

    Meal Plan: B,L,D
