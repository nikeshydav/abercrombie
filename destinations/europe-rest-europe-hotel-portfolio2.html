SUGGESTED AUSTRIA ACCOMMODATION

  • Hotel Imperial Vienna

    Built in 1869 as a private residence for the Duke of Wuttemberg, this impressive building was converted into a hotel in 1873. The grand marble staircase, the crystal chandeliers and the grand elegant salons are complemented by Imperial furnishings and portraits of members of the powerful Hapsburg family.

    The guest rooms range in size from cosy deluxe rooms to the newly refurbished suites and Royal Apartment, all equipped with modern amenities to provide the highest level of comfort. No visit to the Imperial should be complete without sampling the Imperial Torte with a cup of Viennese coffee.

    Grand Hotel Wien

    The Grand Hotel Wien offers a living reminder of the "Belle Epoque" of the Austro-Hungarian Empire combined with luxurious 5* accommodation. The hotel is located in close proximity to the Vienna State Opera and St Stephen's Cathedral.

    Offering 205 rooms and suites, all accommodations are luxuriously furnished and tastefully decorated. The hotel's rooftop restaurant, Le Ciel, offers unrivalled views across Vienna; and the Grand Café offers fine views of the Ringstrasse and traditional tortes and pastries. The Grand also offers a Japanese restaurant, a cocktail bar and a piano bar in the lobby. There is also a fully equipped fitness suite.

    The Levante Parliament

    The hotel is situated in a central location, behind the Parliament and the Viennese City Hall and offers 74 rooms and suites. This hotel is located in the heart of Vienna and the building dates from 1911.

    The emphasis is on rationalism, the elimination of ornament and the use of technological advances in materials that allow flexibility of design.
