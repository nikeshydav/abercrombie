    The Aubrey

    The Aubrey is Santiago's first design boutique hotel opened in 2010 and located in the bohemian neighbourhood of Bellavista, the cultural heart of the city.The hotel's owners, with the assistance of Chilean interior designer Luz Mendez, have created an elegant decorative theme that combines original period features with a playful contemporary flair.

    Rooms are light and airy, with white walls providing a backdrop for splashes of vivid colour provided by bed linen and soft furnishings. All feature original parquet floors, geometrically patterned linens, fine cotton sheets, feather beds and, in the bathrooms, invigorating Hansgrohe showers. Terrace Rooms are spacious and open onto a private outdoor seating area whilst Loft Rooms are cosy spaces with angular ceilings.

    Service at The Aubrey is impeccable - crisp and efficient, yet friendly and responsive.

    W Santiago

    W's first foray into South America offers a stunning high-rise hotel set against a panoramic backdrop of the snow-capped Andes. The hotel brings W's philosophy of 'an urban retreat and sweet escape' to the bustle of Santiago's skyscrapers, vibrant colonial cobblestone streets and excellent nightlife.

    Inside, the design is original, quirky and fun. Colours are bright and brash but not over the top. The rooms are well-equipped examples of superb interior design by Tony Chi and Sergio Echeverria. Inspired by the magnificent beauty of Chilean nature and landscape, they have used diverse colours, materials and textures - including velvet, leather, mirrors and floor to ceiling windows - to create sumptuous, super-chic spaces.

    All 196 rooms and suites feature the signature W Bed, fully-wired technology, the delightful Munchie Box and state-of-the-art entertainment.
