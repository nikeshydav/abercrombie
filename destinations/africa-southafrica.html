South africa safaris & luxury holidays


There is no match to South Africa's outright claim of being one of the most culturally-diverse and rich lands. Johannesburg which was once a gold mining town is now a growing commercial city. Cape Town on the other hand presents a classic image with its colourful harbour-dining, spectacular view from the Table Mountain, vast vineyards and Robben Island. You also have the ancestral home of the Zulu nation KwaZulu-Natal, ancient lava mountains of the Richtersveld, wondrous elephant-spotting in Kruger National Park and the Augrabies Falls to watch out for.

Adorned with natural beauty like a bride, South Africa is a mixture of spectacular landscapes and rich wildlife. Forests, savannahs, deserts and mountains bring about a colourful medley in front of the eyes. Give yourself nine days to experience a world undiscovered.
