    Atul Kochhar – “Simply India”.

At St.Regis, you will be spoilt by choice, be it wind and kite surfing or a relaxing spa session at the Iridium Spa featuring 12 private treatments rooms, or just simply sun bath by the luxurious pool or even better the natural wonder, the splendid beautiful beach itelf.

    CONSTANCE BELLE MARE PLAGE

    Constance Belle Mare Plage spreads along two kilometres of soft white sand, protected by an offshore reef making it ideal for swimming and water sports and is located on the eastern coast of Mauritius.

    Facilities include a wide choice of restaurants, the Shiseido Spa, two 18-hole championship golf courses and a multitude of land and water activities. Guests of Belle Mare Plage are also welcome to use the facilities and restaurants of nearby Le Prince Maurice.

    CONSTANCE LE PRINCE MAURICE

    Evoking the splendour of a distant era, Le Prince Maurice is named after Prince Maurice van Nassau, pioneer of the spice trade in the Indian Ocean. Surrounded by a protective reef of sand bars, the hotel sits in a calm turquoise lagoon with sparkling white beaches and luxuriant tropical vegetation. Nature is complemented by design with the thatched roofed dwellings blending perfectly with the surrounding natural beauty. A haven of luxury and unparalleled service awaits.

    All of the junior suites are decorated with wooden furnishings and colours reflecting the spice route. Each has a spacious bathroom with shower and a large terrace or veranda. Interconnecting rooms can be requested for families. If you are looking for a little more space, the larger villa-style Senior Suites have a bedroom and separate living room/dining area. Three of these sit on stilts over the reserve and have a Jacuzzi in the bathroom and outdoor soaking bath on the terrace. Nine further Senior Suites have direct access to the beach and a private pool.

    Golfers will be pleased to hear that green fees are complimentary. You will have access to the Golf Academy and two 18-hole championship golf courses at nearby sister hotel Constance Belle Mare Plage. Don't miss the opportunity for a spot of afternoon tea between rounds in the clubhouse. The hotel has complimentary Wi-Fi and a complimentary arrival and departure lounge with showers available.

    Enjoy a romantic evening feasting on the freshest seafood & delicious local delicacies at Le Barachois the only floating restaurant in Mauritius.

    DINAROBIN HOTEL GOLF & SPA

    An elegant five-star resort, Dinarobin Hotel Golf & Spa is a haven of peace and tranquillity - the ideal setting for a luxury holiday in Mauritius. Cascading pools dotted with islets bright with flowers, spacious wooden decks, terraces designed with sea views and tropical gardens planted with exotic trees make Dinarobin the symbol of tropical elegance in Mauritius.

    Located on the most scenic shoreline in Mauritius with the spectacular Le Morne Mountain acting as a dramatic backdrop, Dinarobin offers an intimate village setting. The resort features luxurious all-Suite accommodation that blends harmoniously into the lush tropical gardens. The resort's fabulous setting is complemented by world-class service. The ever-attentive staff attends to your every whim in the restaurants, in your Suite, or on the beach as you relax in the shade under your straw parasol.

    Stay in a Club Suite at the Dinarobin and have access to the exclusive "The Club at Dinarobin", which gives access to a private lounge and a gorgeous roof-top thatched terrace offering a selection of food and beverages throughout the day at set times.

    FOUR SEASONS RESORT MAURITIUS AT ANAHITA

    Cocooned by a private sanctuary on the east coast of Mauritius, the Four Seasons at Anahita embraces endless views of blue waters against a backdrop of green-blanketed mountains. Here you can enjoy an oceanfront finish on the Ernie Els 18-hole championship golf course, splash with the family on serene lagoon beaches or instil tranquillity with regionally-inspired treatments in the overwater spa. Fill every moment with fun and relaxation in this exquisite retreat.

    123 spacious villas have been designed to connect with the natural surroundings. Tranquil décor rich with natural woods, volcanic stone and other indigenous materials brings the flavour of Mauritius indoors. The Garden Pool and Mangrove Pool Villas sit on the mainland part of the resort, while Lagoon Pool Villas are located on the private Ile aux Chats and face the turquoise lagoon. Each villa offers the following facilities plasma televisions, DVD players, iPod docking stations, wireless Internet connectivity and tea/coffee-making facilities. If you are travelling as a family - or are a couple looking for more space - the Two and Three Bedroom Residence Villas include a fully-equipped kitchen, living area, dining room and study.

    Be inspired by your surroundings and try the Sugar Cane Therapy, an indigenous massage incorporating sugar cane sticks.

    LE TOUESSROK

    Le Touessrok is one of the world's great resorts -the height of cool, modern elegance, truly one of the "Leading Hotels of the World". It lies on a beautiful stretch of sandy coast, looking out over tranquil Trou d'Eau Douce Bay. The main resort area spreads out in two wings. The Hibiscus Wing has 37 Deluxe Rooms, 32 Junior Suites and the Royal Suite; while the Coral Wing contains 31 Deluxe Rooms and a Junior Suite. If you are looking for the most contemporary styled room, step over the footbridge to Frangipani Island. Here you will find designer Junior Suites with cool colours, plasma TVs, funky egg-shaped baths, butler service and beautiful ocean views.

    All rooms at Le Touessrok open onto furnished sea-facing terraces or balconies, feature en suite bathrooms with walk-in showers, and are equipped with the fullest facilities. For the utmost privacy and luxury, three private three-bedroom Villas are located at the end of Hibiscus Beach. Each spacious Villa comes with a chef and personal manager to look after your every need. Feel like Robinson Crusoe for the day and take a short boat-ride across to Ilot Mangenie, an island retreat exclusively reserved for Le Touessrok guests.

    In the evening experience Le Touessrok's own home-made rums along with fine rums from around the world at The Sega Bar with the rhythms of the resident jazz band taking you late into the night in a stylish atmosphere.

    LONG BEACH RESORT MAURITIUS

    Nestling on the edge of one of the Island's longest, widest and most beautiful natural beaches is the luxury Long Beach. All of the rooms have views over the crystal clear waters of the lagoon, and the contemporary décor reflects this harmonious atmosphere.

    Long Beach Resort has an extensive list of activities of which to enjoy. From the peaceful haven of the Spa with its many treatments to the golfing activities in the two championship golf courses available.

    For dining, guests have an array of options that will satisfy a diverse palate, all of which of course are supplied with fresh produce.

    Further activities such as yoga or aqua gym classes or a game on the floodlit tennis courts are available, or for the more adventurous guests mountain bike excursions and even a climbing wall! However, for those who simply wish to unwind the glistening white sand beach beckons.

    In the evening enjoy the lively central square built on the concept of 'Italian life' this area is the heart of the hotel and is surrounded by restaurants, bars, and boutiques.

    MARADIVA VILLAS RESORT & SPA

    An exclusive haven of luxury and solitude, Maradiva Villas Resort & Spa spreads through 27 acres on the west coast of Mauritius, overlooking the soft sands and clear blue waters of Tamarin Bay. No detail has been overlooked with interiors taking inspiration from African, Indian, Arabic and French colonial designIf you are looking to recharge the batteries, visit the magnificent Spa for a 'Mind, Body and Soul Experience'. Meet the

    Ayurvedic Doctor who will design a meal plan for your specific constitution and body type. This, combined with spa treatments, meditation or yoga, will help to rejuvenate the mind, body and soul. Once full of energy, there are plenty of activities on offer. Learn to water-ski, hire a bike to visit the town of Flic en Flac, visit the nearby Tamarina Championship Golf Course or take to the ocean in search of dolphins. As the sun goes down, the resort takes on a romantic atmosphere.

    Designed with an emphasis on calm and relaxation, the soft tones and natural materials ensure an exclusive haven of tranquillity. Each villa has a large bathroom with indoor rain shower and outdoor garden shower, 24-hour butler service, plasma TV, DVD music system and internet connection. To give yourself a little more space, Exclusive Suite Villas have a separate living room and a terrace with dining table and shaded sofa seating area. A few of the villas can interconnect. For the ultimate in luxury and privacy, the two Presidential Villas have their own access to the beach, feature two bedrooms and a spacious living area opening onto a private garden and swimming pool.

    Enjoy a candle-lit dinner for two in a Rajasthani-style tent on the beach, with only the sound of the waves playing in the background.

    ONE & ONLY LE SAINT GERAN

    Basking on the golden peninsular of Belle Mare on the island's northeast coast under the calm sway of countless coconut palms, the hotel is situated amidst 50 acres of lush tropical gardens that lead onto fine white sand and clear turquoise waters.

    All suites feature a private terrace facing the ocean, cove or tropical gardens. 148 Junior Suites are styled with Dutch and French colonial influence and have a king or two double queen beds. There are twelve Ocean Suites that encompass a separate lounge along with two secluded terraces that lead off the sumptuous master bedrooms. An exquisite choice of three restaurants and 2 bars serves everything from a light pool snack to a gourmet feast, Rasoi by Vineet features the contemporary Indian cuisine of the Michelin-starred Vineet Bhatia.

    The pinnacle of luxury is found in the Villa with its private swimming pool, separate entrance and relaxing private position in a peaceful corner of the estate.

    PARADIS HOTEL & GOLF CLUB

    Paradis Hotel & Golf Club has a superb location on the island's South-western tip and is a haven for sports lovers and families alike. The resort is beautifully spread out so there is plenty of space for everyone.100 Deluxe and Deluxe Beachfront rooms feature a large bedroom, bathroom and spacious terrace overlooking the lagoon.

    120 Junior Suites and 54 Beachfront Junior Suites, reflecting a nautical design, are of high quality with a spacious lounge area and balcony; bathrooms are large with separate shower, bath and a walk-in wardrobe. All include a television and mini-bar. For families we recommend the generously proportioned Family and Luxury Family Suites which can accommodate two adults and up to three children. These have been specially designed with little people in mind, featuring tailor-made bath amenities, small slippers, bathrobes and fun themes. If you are looking for privacy, 12 three-bedroomed Executive Villas and a Presidential Villa feature private club car, butler service, fully-equipped kitchen, dining area, barbecue, plasma screen and DVD player.

    Paradis features four of the eight restaurants located on the Morne Peninsula, providing a wide choice of cuisine and settings. Indulge in a gourmet dinner or simply enjoy fresh grilled seafood while looking out over the magnificent seascape. A must for golf lovers is a visit to the spectacularly located 18-hole championship golf course. Whether you aim to improve your swing or lower your handicap, pay a visit to the Golf Academy where golf pros are on hand to iron out your weaknesses with the aid of sophisticated computer software. There are lots of watersports on offer including diving, sailing, deep sea fishing and windsurfing. While there is plenty to keep you occupied at Paradis, you will also have access to all the facilities on offer at neighbouring sister hotel Dinarobin, including the gastronomic restaurant, Clarins Spa, tennis courts and a wide array of activities.

    Indulge yourself in one of the Clarins treatments at the Spa surrounded by reflecting pools decorated with flowers and warm colours to recreate the serene ambiance of Asian Spas.

    ROYAL PALM

    An oasis of luxury and calm, this exquisite five star luxury hotel is set in lush tropical gardens, overlooking turquoise seas and is on one of the finest white sandy beaches in Mauritius. This charming hotel is a blend of understated elegance, combined with impeccable service and traditional, friendly Mauritian hospitality. On arrival you will be spoilt for choice with places to eat. Choose from romantic La Goélette or the trendy oceanfront Natureaty, offering an alternative choice of creative dishes with an Asian twist. And don't forget Le Bar Plage, the perfect venue for a lazy lunch.

    There are 57 Junior Suites, in addition to the 27 Tropical, Palm, Garden, Senior, Penthouse, Presidential and Royal Suites. All have views through the lush tropical gardens towards the tranquil waters of the Grand Baie lagoon. The spacious suites are decorated in an elegant Mauritian style with warm colours and individual objets d'art. All have marble bathrooms with bath and shower, as well as a veranda or terrace. Interconnecting Junior Suites, or Junior and Senior Suites, are great for families, but if you would like a bit more space, the Garden Suites include a second bedroom with private bathroom, suitable for up to two children. The most luxurious of all is the one Royal Suite, comprising two palatial bedrooms, a generous living area and a magnificent terrace with plunge pool overlooking the ocean. Complimentary WIFI.

    Begin your holiday in style and arrive in a chauffeur-drive silver Rolls Royce Phantom the only one of its kind in Mauritius.

    SHANTI MAURICE, A NIRA RESORT

    Nestled between sugarcane fields and the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean, Shanti Maurice promises an indigenous Mauritian experience that will enlighten all your senses. The resort has exotic gardens next to the pristine white sands and a world class spa with a unique combination of therapies to promote a feeling of extreme wellness.

    Dining experiences at the oceanfront Stars Signature Restaurant, Pebbles all day dining or the casual Fish & Rhum Shack on the beach are simple yet exquisite offering Mediterranean, international or casual BBQ's respectively, with the chefs creating imaginative choices for the most discerning of palates. Most of the fresh produce used in the restaurants is grown on the property together with fish fresh from the ocean.

    The pride of the almost exclusively local staff at the resort is evident throughout the Shanti Maurice experience, manifesting itself in genuinely warm and caring service. The hotel has a Kids Club and a specially designed Kids Activities Programme, with recreational pursuits such as non-motorised water sports, tennis, and arts & crafts including shell-painting, clay-modelling and 'treasure hunts'. The Kids club is set within its own grounds and comes complete with its own toddler's pool, games room and outside play area. Spacious suites and villas are decorated with cool linens, calm colours and tropical woods, all with large double beds and discreet modern technologies. Junior Suites have a large balcony or terrace, sea-views and spacious bathroom with the ground floor junior suites having direct access to the beach and an outdoor shower. If you are looking for more privacy, the Luxury Villas or our favourite is the Luxury Suite Villa with Pool, facing directly onto the beach with beautiful ocean views and a 54sq metre swimming pool. The Shanti Villa comprises two bedrooms and a swimming pool with built-in Jacuzzi. All of the Villas have a private butler to look after your every need.

    Spend an evening in Grandma's kitchen, where you will be welcomed into a Mauritian home to taste authentic cuisine made with fresh local ingredients creating fantastic tasting food as well as learning about their lifestyle and hearing childhood stories, a truly unique dining experience.

    THE OBEROI

    Sitting on three crescents of soft white sand, overlooking the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean, The Oberoi Mauritius is surrounded by beautiful subtropical gardens. 20 acres of grounds include fragrant Frangipani trees, African tulip trees, bright red flame trees and an exotic bouquet of bougainvillea, hibiscus and oleander.

    This stylish resort reflects the island's rich multicultural heritage, in tandem with Oberoi's tradition of unparalleled hospitality and legendary service. Treat yourself from top to toe in The Oberoi Spa and float back to your room after enjoying an Ayurvedic or aromatherapy massage. Pick yourself up with a refreshing San Francisco cocktail en route to dinner. Each course at the restaurants resembles a work of art, which tastes as good as it looks. You may find yourself eating a decadent champagne risotto, topped with black truffle, or perhaps a delicately-infused snapper, freshly caught that day.

    The sugar-cane thatched Pavilions and Villas are decorated in beige and cream, with furnishings from Africa and Bali. Each features a beautifully carved wooden four-poster bed, sitting area and sunken marble bath, overlooking a private walled garden. 48 stylish Luxury Pavilions have an outdoor terrace with sun-beds, overlooking the gardens and Indian Ocean. But if you are looking for the ultimate in privacy, 24 Luxury Villas are dotted amongst the lush sub-tropical gardens and come with a private pool or garden, and outdoor dining pavilion. Two Royal Villas come with a private butler and have a separate bedroom and bath pavilion, a lounge pavilion and large private swimming pool. One of the Royal Villas has two bedrooms. All accommodation includes a television, DVD player and personal bar. For an extended family, the Royal Villa can be combined with a Luxury Villa with garden or with two Luxury Pavilions. The hotel is 15 kilometres away from the capital city of Port Louis and near the buzzing small town of Grand Baie.

    Experience a voyage of discovery with one of the daily activities from the 'Touching the Senses' programme. Our favourite is an evening of stargazing on the beach with astronomer Bhaskar Desai. He will guide you through the southern constellations and explain the significance of the stars in relation to Hindu mythology.

    THE RESIDENCE

    With a perfect combination of bygone charm, contemporary luxury and a reputation for impeccable service, The Residence exudes an atmosphere of grace and sophistication. Beach butlers are on hand to clean your sunglasses, ensure you have plenty of towels or bring you a fresh juice to quench your thirst. When the heat of the day gets too much, head to the Sanctuary Spa, a temple to relaxation hidden beneath the hotel. In the evening stroll across to the secluded Plantation restaurant. Sit on the colonial veranda overlooking the Indian Ocean and sample such delights as fresh prawns, Creole-style snapper and delicious guava ice-cream.

    163 Colonial Garden View, Ocean View or Ocean Front Rooms and Suites are spacious and attractively furnished with dark woods and cooling shades of white and beige. All feature a small living area, en suite bathroom with separate bathtub and shower, private terrace or balcony, flat-screen television, DVD player and internet connection. All rooms and suites come with a butler who will look after your every need including unpacking your luggage. Our favourite rooms for families are the Garden View Senior Suites, where you can shut the door between bedroom and living area. These suites can accommodate two adults and two children and have an outdoor terrace complete with sun loungers, representing the ultimate in expansive indulgence.

    Perfect for an afternoon treat the Gourmet Kiosque serves delicious sweet snacks such as waffles, pancakes and an assortment of ice creams.

    TROU AUX BICHES RESORT AND SPA

    Trou aux Biches is set on a 35 hectare estate with beautiful tropical gardens and a beachfront setting it is a sublime place to relax and be pampered in the Indian Ocean. A resort that is perfect for couples and families alike. Offering a wide range of complimentary land and water sports with a state of the art gym facility. All Villas, Beach Front Suites and Senior Beach Front Suites have private pools. The resort's junior suites, family suites and tropical suites are all housed in thatched chalets in a crescent shape overlooking a pool area.

    The rooms are contemporary in décor and are light and airy and all have their own private outside seating area. The Trou aux Biches has six restaurants, four of which are located in the complex of L'Oasis where the shops and main pool can also be found. Specializing in international cuisine are L'Oasis and La Caravelle. The smaller speciality restaurants, namely Blue Ginger, Mahiya and Il Corallo offer fine dining whereas Le Deck, open for lunch and dinner focuses on light meals and casual dining. The children's club has its own club house and outside area including a paddling pool and their own beach area. The modern teen's club consists of three different rooms and provides the perfect setting for teenagers to meet and make friends or socialize in peaceful comfort under the discreet supervision of the staff.

    For that extra special stay, book one of the pool villas located in a separate village on the resort offering privacy and exclusivity.
