Travel to Peru


Peru is a gold mine of enriching travel experiences. It has been for long drawing tourists and history buffs to its cities for cultural, gastronomic and ecotourism. The ancient ruins of Inca civilization, dense rainforests, the Andes and the noteworthy Lima, the City of the Kings have stroked the imagination of the whole world with their grandeur and mystic.

Peru is host to a diverse topography and landscape with desert in one part and a dense rainforest and the grand Andes mountain range in the others. In the renowned "Archeological Capital of America"; Cusco you start the discovery of the ever legendary Inca civilization which has mystified the whole world for centuries. Take a desert safari in Peru and discover the classic Inca Trails extending from Cusco to Machu Picchu left behind by one of the world's greatest civilizations or discover the origin of the Incas at Lake Titicaca.

Lima, the iconic metropolis and capital city of Peru is generally an entrance to the land. It is home to a number of colonial monuments some of which are UNESCO Cultural World Heritage Sites. With Abercrombie & Kent Luxury Tours to Peru, you can explore the mysterious Nazca Lines which takes you into a unique world of inexplicable and enigmatic structures, forms and figures left behind by the ancient Peruvian civilization. Then there is the vast plain of Paracas Wildlife Reserve where the desert and the sea come together and endows a natural ecological habitat to a diverse wildlife of the region. Also browse through the steep hollows of upto 3,400 metres at Colca Canyons in Arequipa.
