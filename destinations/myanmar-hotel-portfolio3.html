Suggested Mrauk U accommodation

  • Mrauk Oo Princess Resort

    Mrauk Oo Princess Resort is surrounded by magnificent views of the blue mountain ranges, the Mrauk Oo River and rice paddy fields. The architecture of Mrauk Oo Princess Resort was inspired by the traditions and culture of the Rakhine people.

    It has 21 "Village House" Style guest rooms with view towards the river, rice fields or garden. Inside the Village Houses are naturally light up and also provides you with fantastic views of the surrounding scenery. The hotel has its own jetty and its spacious restaurant offers various gourmet cuisines with good view.
