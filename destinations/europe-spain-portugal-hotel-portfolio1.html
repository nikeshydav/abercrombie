Suggested Barcelona and Northern Spain accommodation

  • Alva Park Resort & Spa

    Alva Park Resort & Spa, a member of 'The Leading Small Hotels of the World', is a wonderfully elegant property where modern clean lines mix with superb design to create the rarefied ambience of an exclusive residence.

    No stone has been left unturned and no detail left to chance in delivering a resort that somehow manages to combine high design and real comfort.

    The suites and junior suites too are absolutely beautiful, and awash with luxurious details - from fine wool carpets, fabric wall coverings to sycamore wood detailing and Roche Bobois cherry tree furniture. A single remote controls lighting, curtains, and air conditioning, whilst the TVs are, naturally, Bang & Olufsen. This is truly stunning accommodation, and each with a slice of outdoor living thanks to a private balcony or terrace with sea or garden views.

    Mandarin Oriental Barcelona

    Mandarin Oriental Barcelona is situated on the famous Passeig de Gràcia street, near the Casa Batlló by Gaudi. Its elegant mid 20th century building with a highly stylised modern interior was designed by Spanish born Patricia Urquiola. Centrally located, it is the ideal place from which to explore the artistic and cultural delights of this great city.

    The hotel's 98 luxurious guest rooms exude modern design, whilst incorporating delicate reflections of oriental heritage. Dominated by clean lines and 'white space' they are the very epitome of cool, modern chic - brilliantly brought to life with splashes of colour, stylish furniture and well chosen furnishings.

    The original touches are everywhere. The Bankers Bar - the place to be in the evening - has a display created from an original collection of bank security boxes. This hotel is a model of contemporary design surrounded by Barcelona's modernist architecture and art.

    W Barcelona

    Barcelona's newest hot spot is unquestionably the W Barcelona. W Barcelona rises high above the Mediterranean in Spain's most dynamic and stylish city.

    Designed by world-famed architect Ricardo Bofill and located on the famed boardwalk La Barceloneta, the W Barcelona is unprecedented - a sensory explosion of entertainment, recreation, culinary delights, nightlife, and hospitality in an extra-ordinary setting.
