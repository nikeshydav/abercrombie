SUGGESTED DUBAI ACCOMMODATION

  • Park Hyatt Dubai

    The resort hotel Park Hyatt Dubai is a waterfront retreat designed in Moorish style and adjacent to the world-famous Dubai Creek Golf & Yacht Club. Sleek stone walls inlaid with wonderfully detailing surround tranquil outdoor spaces dotted with Date Palms. Inside, the Moorish details continue, with stone arches, lanterns and beautiful wickerwork furniture leading the way.


    The rooms and suites are wonderful too. All are Moorish and Mediterranean in design, using muted pastels and natural light to complement elegant interiors, which include en suite bathrooms with deep soaking bathtubs and walk-in showers. Toned down and sleek, they are lovely, relaxed and homely spaces.

    The Ritz-Carlton, Dubai International Financial Centre

    The Ritz-Carlton, Dubai International Financial Centre offers a dynamic setting and opulent retreat for any traveller. Standing 14 stories high, the hotel epitomizes understated luxury and elegance behind its handsome limestone facade. Enjoy a string of sumptuous amenities, a direct walkway to The Gate which is the heart of the Financial Centre, plus the added convenience of the city of Dubai at your fingertips.


    Contemporary in style with floor to ceiling windows offering views of the hotel courtyard or of the Dubai International Financial Centre (DIFC) neighbourhood, all the rooms are equipped with the convenience of internet access and feature a dedicated work area, which includes a remote connectivity unit. Contemporary interiors influenced by French art deco and the subtle colour palette of the rooms offer tranquility and refined comfort.

    Emirates Palace

    An air of luxury fills every corner of this palatial paradise, a member of Kempinski Hotels & Resorts. Built to reflect the different shades of the Arabian Desert sands Emirates Palace is an enchanting landmark that's a wonder to behold - a perfect location for relaxation or a retreat after a safari exploring the local landscapes.


    Inside every room and suite, the décor is a masterful blend of Arabian regal splendor and the latest technology, featuring acres of gold leaf and the finest marble. A touch screen handset controls everything from lights and air conditioning to the in room entertainment system.

    Jumeirah At Etihad Towers

    World-class contemporary design and seamless detail is enhanced in this stunning complex by sweeping views of the Abu Dhabi coastline. Easily accessible to the airport (37 kilometres) and within minutes of the City's most exclusive shopping malls, the central business district and Ministries' Area. A stunning city skyline view awaits you from panoramic floor to ceiling windows while intelligently appointed space caters to your work and leisure requirements.


    A wealth of facilities are offered here, such as the exceptional 13-suite Talise spa, with both Hamman and Rasoul treatments, women's beauty salon, Six P gym, club executive lounge, which offers complimentary snacks and beverages throughout the day, and a Mezzoon ballroom, seating up to 1,400 delegates. 12 restaurants offer an eclectic mix of gourmet cuisine while both Ray's bar, high on the 62nd floor, and the Lobby bar boast chic, modern décor serving exquisite cocktails, fine wines and quality cigars to create a memorable evening of entertainment.

    Kempinski Hotel & Residences, Palm Jumeirah

    Just south of the Persian Gulf lies the iconic palm-tree shaped island of Palm Jumeirah, home of the picturesque Kempinski Hotel & Residences


    Experience Arabian luxury when staying in one of the Suites, Penthouses or Villas, all displaying a tasteful décor glowing with the golden hue of chandeliers, rich fabrics and light opulent furnishings. The elegant and spacious Imperial Villas are ideal for the whole family to enjoy with a private pool, rooftop terrace and full size billiard table in the games room. Sit back and unwind by the palm-fringed swimming pool before heading to the Cinq Mondes Spa for a relaxing massage.

    Mina A Salam

    Mina A' Salam aims to give you a taste of 'old Arabia' - a time when wind-towers defined the city's silhouette and provided a welcome sight to returning fishermen, travellers and tradesmen. The same sand coloured buildings still stand proud in parts of Dubai and are now joined by this authentically styled harbour-side hotel - where deign is also informed by Dubai's sea-faring tradition.


    The traditional theme continues inside, where the 292 rooms and suites are sumptuously appointed in Arabian style - decadent, woven fabrics, carved wooden furniture and wall hangings abound. The hotel has a number of temperature controlled outdoor pools. Other facilities and activities include desert safaris, yachting and a huge array of sports and leisure facilities.

    Qasr Al Sarab Desert Resort By Anantara

    Qasr al Sarab Desert Resort by Anantara not only has a spectacular location but it is an Arabian palatial wonderland. The hotel has been tastefully created to be a true reflection of Arabian style and elegance. Set in the unique Empty Quarter the largest uninterrupted body of sand in the world, but less than 200kms away from the centre of thriving Abu Dhabi.


    Accommodation is beautiful with a variety of rooms and suites available all furnished to the highest standards complete with a private balcony or terrace. For those travellers wishing to have more space and privacy there are a selection of one, two and three bedroomed villas boasting private pools and spacious living areas. The restaurants at Qasr al Sarab offer an array of Arabian and International cuisine in different venues or in the privacy of your own private balcony or terrace. This is a luxurious five star resort style hotel offering an exceptional desert experience.
