Suggested JEBEL AKHDAR MOUNTAINS accommodation

  • SAHAB HOTELS

    Opened in 2011 this property was designed and built in tribute to the charm of the mountain, life style and the modern way of life. The Sahab Hotel has entirely adopted this new tourism concept by blending the ancient landscape of the Jabal Al Akhdar Mountains with traditional Omani architecture and an incredibly spectacular view. Décor is rustic chic with traditional Omani touches to reflect Sahab's positioning of this distinctive experience.

    The infinity swimming pool and Jacuzzi with their aquamarine waters are coupled with panoramic views and a Poolside Restaurant serving light menus, refreshments and cocktails. The fossil garden boasts over 5000 sqm of native plants and 270 million years old marine fossils.

    Activities on offer really do make the most of the region with hiking trips into the heart of the Jabal Al Akhdar mountain range. Explore the most hair-raising caves as well as observe canyon wildlife in their natural habitat. A magnificent location for star gazing as the clear night sky has almost zero pollution so that the star gazing can be enjoyed in its full. Children will adore the donkey rides through the majestic countryside and finding out all about traditional village culture.

    TANUF MOUNTAIN CAMP

    Situated amongst some of Oman's most dramatic mountain scenery the camp evokes a traditional, luxurious Arabian 'encampment' and is designed in keeping with the ambience of the environment and the region.

    Tents are perfect for a couple and there is scope for up to two children on camp beds. Adjacent to your tent is a well-appointed shower and wash area open to the skies. To the rear of the camp and discreetly positioned are the toilets. Each tent is positioned with the greatest care to ensure maximum privacy and individual views and a verandah where you can recline in a canvas deck chair and enjoy the magic of sunset.

    Lighting is all solar, candles, oil lamps and fires in order to preserve the environment around you.

    Dine under the night sky whilst enjoying expertly prepared dishes made from the best fresh local produce. Menus are a blend of Middle Eastern, North African and Mediterranean cuisine.

    There are a host of activities on offer around the camp; walks for geology and flora enthusiasts, visits to traditional mountain villages and an opportunity to meet local people and gain an insight into the local way of life. Relax under the large 'majlis' with a pair of binoculars and a night sky print outs to starwatch. Perhaps play a game of boules or backgammon. For bird watchers spotting sheets are available in the camp.

    THE VIEW

    Located at a height of 1400 meters on a slope close to Jabal Shams and Al Hamra town, The View is a sanctuary of 15 eco-luxe tents that are environment friendly. The luxury en suite tents boast decks that offers a majestic view of the Hajar mountain range and the villages below at any time of the day or night.

    The View Restaurant is situated close to the canyon. Serving exotic meals for breakfast, lunch and buffet dinner the views of the canyon from the restaurant and the deck are stunning.

    Look up to the stars that you may never view while being in the city and check out your knowledge of astronomy. At the same time you will watch with amazement the town of Al Hamra lighting up with the nightlights. This is truly one of the best sights you will see here at The View.

    Just two and half hours from Muscat, The View's position makes it a superb base for further exploration. Visit the town of Al Hamra (a living museum of 400 year-old mud-brick mansions), and explore the dramatic chambers of Al Hoota Cave and its serene underground lake.
