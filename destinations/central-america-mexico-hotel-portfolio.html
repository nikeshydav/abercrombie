SUGGESTED BAJA CALIFORNIA ACCOMMODATION

  • CAPELLA PEDREGAL

    Capella Pedregal is a new addition to the luxurious resorts to be found around Los Cabos but in a unique location within easy reach of the Marina & small town of Cabo San Lucas for an experience of true 'Cabo' culture.

    The hotel is set between two cliffs with its own private cove and offers stunning ocean views of the Pacific where whales can be spotted in the winter months. Although the ocean currents of the Pacific are too dangerous for serious ocean swimming, the beach is very pretty and you can lounge on one of the cabanas before taking a dip in the stunning pools.

    All the rooms are quite spectacular with ocean views, terraces and private plunge pools. Dine at the superb Don Manuel's, there is also a beach bar and seafood restaurant nearby. The main pool leads out from the Auriga spa & fitness centre via a series of waterfalls to the decks overlooking the ocean and has been beautifully landscaped.

    LAS VENTANAS AL PARAISO A ROSEWOOD RESORT

    Blending the striking desert surroundings and México's unique flavours with lavish suites and al fresco communal areas, Las Ventanas is, as its name suggests, a 'window to paradise'. With fewer than one hundred rooms, the resort has an exclusive feel, which is accentuated by the wonderful, attentive service.

    All the rooms are superb, with secluded terraces, expansive bathrooms, personal telescopes and terracotta fireplaces for the cooler winter evenings. Inside, intricately carved furnishings and colourful native paintings vie for attention against a stunning décor backdrop in whites and blues.

    The public areas too are wonderfully stylish, particularly the pool area, with its superb views and relaxed bar.

    ONE & ONLY PALMILLA

    The resort and rooms or 'casas' reflect the style of old México with red-tiled roofs, whitewashed walls and burbling fountains, which perfectly compliment the modern touches evident in the restaurants, bars and spa.

    Beneath those red-tiles roofs, nearly 200 well-equipped guestrooms perch over the beach in one or three-storey buildings, all with balconies affording beach and ocean views. Inside, the rooms are beautifully appointed. Sharp and tasteful in every detail, they are wonderfully spacious and decorated with an array of handcrafted Mexican furnishings, whilst neutral décor and stylish furniture complete the elegant look.
