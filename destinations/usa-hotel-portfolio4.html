  • ARIZONA, ENCHANTMENT RESORT, SEDONA

    Enchantment Resort is surrounded by the unique natural beauty that makes up the Southwest desert. Each room offers viewing decks overlooking Boynton Canyon and the magnificent mountains.

    The spa, Mii amo presents a unique, full-service spa experience and takes its name from the Native American word for journey. This is a perfect place to stargaze at night, with clear skies and a perfect climate.

    ARIZONA, FOUR SEASONS RESORT SCOTTSDALE

    Each of the rooms at this magical Four Seasons Resort offer views of either the Sonoran desert or mountains. This is a genuine destination hotel offering sensational suites. Guests enjoy special privileges at both The Pinnacle and The Monument championship golf courses nearby.

    The scenery here is simply astonishing and with 350 a days a year on sun on average its the perfect place to slow things down, take it easy and consider the spa treatment menu.

    LAS VEGAS, ARIA

    Walking into your room at The Aria you'll immediately notice the automatic curtains opening to welcome you. The rest of the hotel is packed with thoughtful touches with every room having a corner view to maximise light and the sights of the entertainment paradise below.

    Located in the heart of the new CityCenter development, Aria is a dramatic addition to the skyline of Las Vegas. The rooms allow guests to customise their lighting, room temperature, music with the most advanced in room technology in the city.

    Read more

    LAS VEGAS, MANDARIN ORIENTAL

    The Mandarin Oriental Las Vegas is quite different to any other hotel in the city. The lobby and check-in are on the 23rd floor in the 'sky lobby'. Unlike other hotels, it doesn't have a casino.

    The environment is therefore one of calm, serenity and civility, allowing you to feel far from the madding crowd. The hotel is perfect if you want to have a taste of Las Vegas but also want the chance of some quiet relaxation surrounded by absolute luxury when the casinos, shows and bright lights have all been experienced.

    Read more

    LAS VEGAS, THE ENCORE AT WYNN

    With a soaring 50-storey tower and packed with luxurious boutiques, The Encore at Wynn Las Vegas is the only hotel on the Vegas strip with an 18-hole golf course. The resort prides itself on its intimate atmosphere, offering a comfortable, residential feel. The tower suites even provide a white-gloved butler service.

    All of the suites offer floor to ceiling windows with unforgettable views across the city or mountains. The full-service spa Salon was custom designed with meticulous attention to care and offers a huge amount of treatments and massages. Unlike some of the other hotels in Vegas, The Encore at Wynn is not built around a 'theme'. Instead it concentrates solely on sophisticated chic and is a haven for the most discerning visitor.

    UTAH, CANYON POINT, AMANGIRI

    As you approach the Amangiri, you'll notice that the resort is difficult to spot. Architecturally, it has been designed to blend into the landscape with natural hues, materials and textures. The result is breathtaking. A vast complex of stone and concrete with postcard style windows looking out onto the breathtaking desert scenery outside.

    Being an Aman Resort you can expect nothing less than absolute luxury. This is one of our favourite hotels in the United States.

    - Entry to each of the 34 suites its through its own private courtyard
    - The central pavilion is built around the main swimming pool
    - The spa offers brushed stone, windows providing streams of light and a number of unique treatment venues
    - The resort is close to the Grand Staircase - Escalante National Monument.
