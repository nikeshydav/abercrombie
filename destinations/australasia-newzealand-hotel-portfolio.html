Suggested North Island accommodation

  • Mollies Estate

    Mollies is a luxury boutique property hidden in the heart of Auckland. A member of 'Small Luxury Hotels' this charming gem offers 14 magnificently appointed suites with private terraces overlooking the magnificent harbour or the delightful garden.

    The Dining Room at Mollies is a gourmet delight, with tempting gastronomic menus that make it popular with locals. The East Day Spa at Mollies offers pampering treatments, from a jetlag recovery Apna massage to a relaxing pedicure whilst you indulge in one of Mollies famous high teas. The owner's passion for opera, attention to detail and fine antiques results in a wonderfully eclectic, yet soothing environment.

    Aachen House

    This grand building has been restored with all modern amenities but keeping the historic atmosphere and ambience.

    Suites are large, with many opening out onto patios and balconies. Each of the nine rooms are luxuriously furnished with antiques and fabrics that would grace the most exclusive home.

    The Langham, Auckland

    Within walking distance of Auckland's vibrant waterfront and shopping areas, The Langham exudes classical elegance. Enjoy a meal at Partingtons, the hotel's award-winning restaurant serving contemporary New Zealand cuisine which can be accompanied by one of the 400 wines available.

    If you are in Auckland at the weekend, then the seafood buffet served on a Friday night has over 30 dishes on offer or perhaps try the Langham's famous Sunday brunch. After a busy day sightseeing, why not treat yourself to a champagne afternoon tea or try out the excellent leisure facilities include the new Chuan Spa which uses the principles of traditional Chinese medicine practices around the five elements of metal, wood, fire, earth and water.

    Treetops Lodge and Estate

    Treetops Lodge & Estate offers the ultimate in world class luxury accommodation. This secluded retreat is nestled amongst 2,500 acres of native forest within the thermal region of Rotorua.

    The accommodation is luxurious whether you are staying in one of the suites or one of the more secluded villas. Integral to the Treetops experience is the cuisine. Local produce is complemented with a selection of fine New Zealand wines. With seven trout streams, lakes (fly fishing lessons available) and over 70 kilometres of trails suitable for hiking, horse riding or mountain biking, there is plenty to do without even leaving the estate but if you do want to explore, Treetops is conveniently located close to Rotorua.

    Bay of Islands Lodge

    Bay of Islands Lodge sits above one of the area's few remaining un-milled coastal rainforests. Surrounded by a native bush reserve, a few minutes' drive from Paihia, the bay's major township.

    The two-level, cedar-clad lodge overlooks the Veronica Channel and the regular ferry route between Paihia and the charming historic town of Russell. Your luxury accommodation for two in the Sea View or Bush View Suite includes full breakfast each morning and a complimentary open bar.

    Bolton Hotel

    The Bolton Hotel is the only, Qualmark five star rated luxury boutique hotel in Wellington, New Zealand. Located on the corner of Bolton and Mowbray Streets, it is within walking distance to the city's many theatres, restaurants, cafes, bars, retail hub, waterfront and stadium. The perfect base to explore the city and discover Wellington.

    With 142 contemporary-styled guest rooms and suites stylishly decorated in calming neutral tones and feature artworks by local artists and historical photographs of early Wellington. Luxurious white cotton bedding and velvety soft bathrobes ensure that guests can relax in comfort.

Set within the peaceful village of Russell and The Bay of Islands Flagstaff Lodge & Day Spa offers luxury accommodation.     Huka Lodge

    With unrivalled riverside location it is considered to be the iconic New Zealand retreat, offering an elegant and welcoming haven where guests can enjoy the highest standard of comfort and hospitality. Located in Taupo, in the centre of New Zealand's North Island, it is just over an hours drive from Rotorua.

    The 18 lodge rooms, one suite and the two exclusive Huka Lodge Cottages are harmonious and deliberately simple in approach. The soft light and layers of well appointed luxury complement the restful forest and river scenery and ensure that you have a luxurious base to return to after your day's activities.

    Solitaire Lodge

    Solitaire Lodge is one of the most beautiful lakeside retreats in New Zealand. The lodge nestles on an elevated promontory that provides breathtaking views across Lake Tarawera. Enjoy the simple pleasures of the lake-swimming, sailing and fishing or visit the hot springs at nearby Rotorua.

    The Boatshed

    Located just 35 minutes by fast ferry or 10 minutes by helicopter from Auckland, The Boatshed is a luxury designed boutique property on Waiheke Island. With all day dining available for guests, you can have complete flexibility and with just five suites, you can be assured of excellent personal service.

    Take a walk along the beach, explore the nearby vineyards and local artist galleries and studios or relax in your suite with panoramic ocean views. The Boatshed now has Mini Mokes available for rental, one of the best ways to explore the island and its hidden treasures. Waiheke is the perfect retreat from the hustle and bustle of city life.
