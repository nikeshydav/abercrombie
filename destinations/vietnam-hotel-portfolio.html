Suggested Hanoi, Halong and Sapa Accommodation

  • Halong Ginger Cruise

    Enjoy one or two nights discovering the splendours of Halong Bay aboard the Halong Ginger, a custom-built 35 metre junk with golden sails, polished woods and refined Asian décor.

    On-board you will find 10 elegant cabins with panoramic windows, natural marble bathroom and deluxe amenities, a restaurant (serving western and Vietnamese cuisine), two bars, a library and sundeck. Cruise to Bai Tu Long Bay where thousands of sculpted limestone islands rise from the waves. During the cruise you will have the opportunity for swimming, short treks, kayaking and visiting tranquil beaches and local villages. Finish the day with a cocktail on the sundeck, as the sunset bathes the rocks in a golden glow.

    Halong Jasmine Cruise

    At an impressive 55 metres in length Halong Jasmine is one of the largest junks on Halong Bay. The style of the boat, like its sister junks The Ginger and Violet, is 1930's Asian chic featuring 22 spacious cabins, a restaurant, lounge and two unique Jasmine Suites with their own Jacuzzi & balcony for uninterrupted views of the bay.

    All cabins aboard the Jasmine are air-conditioned with en-suite bathrooms offering twin beds in the premium cabins, queen sized doubles in the Jasmine Deluxe and king sized beds in the suites.

    Indochina Sails

    The 14 Cabin Indochina Sails is traditional Vietnamese with soft, muted colours giving the junk a welcoming feel. The interior is lined with a perfumed wood leaving a pleasant aroma whilst local granite has been used in the bathrooms which are warm in winter and cool in the summer.

    All cabins aboard Indochina Sails are air-conditioned with en suite bathrooms offering queen sized doubles and twins in both superior and deluxe.

    paradise cruises

    Paradise Cruise offers a unique, inviting design using only the finest natural materials-precious dark wood, creamy silk, crisp white linens, fine texture marble, delicate china and silver accessories bringing contemporary comforts blended with the richness of Vietnamese design.

    Sofitel Metropole

    The beautiful French-colonial style Sofitel Metropole is located in the heart of Hanoi only minutes walk from the vibrant Old Quarter of town. Sofitel Metropole wonderfully combines old and new with its colonial style rooms in the old wing and the contemporary rooms in the new wing.

    A spa and outdoor pool makes this the perfect retreat in the centre of Hanoi. After a day visiting museums, bartering in the shops and visiting Ho Chi Minh's Mausoleum, relax in a rattan armchair at the poolside bar Bamboo while sipping a signature cocktail. Enjoy an exquisite Vietnamese dinner at Spices Garden Restaurant in the courtyard, or sample the best French cuisine in town at Le Beaulieu.

    Valentine Cruise

    With just 5 rooms, this intimate, pleasantly designed wooden junk is perfect for small groups, incorporating all of the facilities of the larger junks in a more private setting. The interior is similar to its sister junk, the Indochina Sails.

    All cabins aboard The Valentine are air-conditioned with en-suite bathrooms offering queen sized doubles and twin beds in the deluxe cabins and a king sized double in the suite.

    Victoria Sapa Resort and Spa

    This leading luxury Resort & Spa in North -Vietnam takes architectural inspiration from a traditional mountain chalet - exuding a polished wooden interior, accented wooden décor and welcoming stone hearth fireplace. Situated amongst an abundance of untouched, tropical vegetation, the hotel has been carefully planned to allow guests a unique experience in close harmony with the natural surroundings and local tribe people.

    The epic scenery of Sapa and its mountainous terrain combines jungle, rural terraces and rich areas where natives harvest their crops. Featuring 77 elegantly adorned guestrooms where views of the beautiful gardens and Mount Fansipan can be enjoyed. Facilities include a tennis court, spa, swimming pool and fitness centre. Sapa Resort & Spa is the perfect choice for travellers who simply want to relax in refined luxury and soak up the unparalleled scenery.

    Violet

    One of the newest luxury junks to set sail on Halong Bay - the 6-cabin Halong Violet has generously sized cabins themed in classic Indochinese style.

    The 6 individually themed cabins are each fitted with a marble bathroom, Jacuzzi and a private balcony, the boat also features a boutique style dining room, library, lounge, spa and gym. The Violet's cruise program sails from the crowds to untouched areas of Halong Bay.
