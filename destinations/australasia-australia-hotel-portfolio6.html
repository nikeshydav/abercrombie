Suggested Western Australia accommodation

  • Cable Beach Club Resort

    The award-winning Cable Beach Club Resort & Spa is the only resort in Broome. Located on the spectacular Cable Beach it was built by Lord McApline and cleverly incorporates the diverse cultural influences of the region - which originally came together thanks to the 19th century pearl rush.

    Set in magnificent gardens, accommodation options include studios, bungalows, private villas and luxury suites complete with a private butler. All are wonderfully stylish, combining contemporary design with hints of rustic chic, evidenced by the wooden floors and plantation shutters. With colourful balconies housing comfortable daybeds, and overlooking tropical gardens, all encourage relaxed outdoor living.

    Cape Lodge Margaret River

    Surrounded by stunning forest, lake and parkland scenery Cape Lodge is only minutes away from pristine beaches, spectacular coastline and the sparkling waters of the Indian Ocean - and neighbours the great Margaret River Wine Estates of Mosswood, Vasse Felix, Cullen and Pierro.

    The Lodge has 22 beautifully appointed rooms and suites, with every detail -from flowers to champagne - enlisted to make staying here a luxurious experience. A member of Small Luxury Hotels of the World, its accommodation is set amid ten hectares of forest, parkland, vineyard, gardens and lakes.

    All rooms and suites are beautifully appointed, with neutral décor providing a backdrop for comfortable, contemporary furniture and furnishings and lovely en suite bathrooms.

    El Questro Homestead

    El Questro provides visitors with a unique chance to experience life in the outback, through activities such as barramundi fishing, horse trekking and guided visits or flights to natural gorges, springs and waterfalls.

    Accommodation ranges from the luxurious homestead to simpler bungalows and tented camps surrounded by lush tropical gardens. With interiors featuring modern pieces set against rustic authentic Australian furnishings, each room is air-conditioned with an en-suite and private balcony.

    This homestead is the perfect place from which to appreciate the untamed beauty of the Kimberley.

    Read more

    Hyatt Regency Perth

    Located close to the central business district in the East Perth area, the Hyatt Regency Perth stands above the city's natural heartbeat - the majestic Swan River. Located on the Adelaide Terrace close to the historic St. Georges Terrace and Murray Street, it is close to many of city attractions, be they art, shopping or cultural attractions - it is an ideal base from which to explore.

    The hotel offers 367 luxury rooms and suites boasting great views of Perth City and Swan River. All are elegantly appointed, decorated in wood and earth tones, and feature huge, comfortable beds with plush bedding. All rooms have 'mood' lighting and high-speed Internet access, along with a bath, shower and deluxe bath amenities.

    The Richardson Hotel & Spa

    The Richardson Hotel & Spa is a striking boutique hotel, designed by renowned London architect Sir Terry Farrell. Since opening in late 2006, this iconic hotel has rapidly become a Perth landmark, establishing itself as the city's premier five-star hotel thanks to its superb facilities, state-of-the-art technology and attentive staff.

    Spacious, elegant, and beautifully appointed, the rooms and suites at The Richardson offer the ultimate in boutique luxury accommodation. Every room and suite offers a private balcony to enjoy the temperate climate of Western Australia and a separate lounge and dining area.

    Each has been carefully designed with the modern traveller in mind. Rich textured contemporary décor creates a stylish interior with a strong sense of mood, luxury and intimacy. The plush room furnishings and sleek modern lines blend rich golden colours with beiges and beech wood.
