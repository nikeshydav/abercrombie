A&K Office In Botswana

Landlocked in the heart of Southern Africa, Botswana is a peaceful, stable nation blessed with awe-inspiring vistas of immense natural beauty, an astounding array of wildlife and welcoming people. From the magnificent Okavango Delta and lush riverine landscape of Chobe, to the stark rugged beauty of Tuli and the salt pans of Makgadikgadi. Botswana captures the visitor's heart, mind and soul. Committed to conservation and sustainable tourism, Botswana offers the finest authentic safari experiences in Africa.

Since 1982, Abercrombie & Kent Botswana has been creating inspiring experiences for discerning travellers throughout the Southern African region. We offer a uniquely regional service with our infrastructure that is well established in the region with offices and support in Johannesburg, Cape Town, Maun (Botswana), Lusaka (Zambia) and Victoria Falls (Zimbabwe).
