Travel To Turks & Caicos


Turks and Caicos Islands offer a perfect tropical retreat with their picture perfect white sand beaches, pristine blue waters and green fauna lining the elongated coast. The Abercrombie & Kent holidays in Turks and Caicos Islands are just the ideal breather of bliss and delight.

Aptly dubbed 'Beautiful by Nature', these flawlessly beautiful islands are located southeast of The Bahamas and flaunt a 65 miles across and 200 miles long coral reef system. These extensive reefs are not only world's least threatened but also some of the healthiest thanks to a low human interference in its surrounding ocean. While marine life enthusiasts can marvel themselves in the colorful underwater gardens, they can occasionally come across oceanic wildlife such the giant Humpback whale as they migrate towards Mouchoir Bank between January and April.

Those who travel to Turks and Caicos will also discover that many of the islands are protected habitats for regional wildlife such as iguanas, turtles and birds such as flamingos, pelicans and ospreys among 170 other species. Perhaps, this explains why only 8 islands out of 40 of this archipelago are inhibited by humans.
