BEST OF SOUTHERN AFRICA

Programme Overview

  • An ultimate holiday of Southern Africa with an amazing blend of dramatic scenery, rich culture, wildlife encounters and activities to keep you on the edge.

    Duration: 12 Days/11 Nights

    Highlights: Livingstone | Chobe National Park | Okavango Delta | Moremi Game Reserve | Cape Town

    A&K ADVANTAGE

    • Take a helicopter flight over the mighty Victoria Falls and enjoy the Flight of the Angels.
    • Enjoy Game drives and sun downers at Chobe National Park.
    • Experience a variety of game viewing activities in the Okavango Delta.
    • Set off on a spectacular game drive at the Moremi Game Reserve.
    • Visit a piece of history in Robben Island and the iconic Table Mountain.
    • Spend a full day exploring Cape Point, ascending the Cape of Good Hope and seeing endangered penguins at Boulders Beach.
    • Stay at A&K’s exclusive Sanctuary Sussi and Chuma at Livingstone.
    • Stay at A&K’s exclusive Sanctuary Chobe Chilwero in Chobe National Park.
    • Stay at A&K’s exclusive Sanctuary Baines’s Camp in the Okavango Delta.
    • Stay at A&K’s exclusive Sanctuary Chief’s Camp in Moremi Game Reserve.
    • Private transfers arranged for arrival and departure.
    • The services of an A&K Representative*

    * An A&K Representative is always at hand to provide unparalleled 24/7 support, be it local travel tips or reconfirming arrangement details, to almost anything. Should you ever need help or advice, dial your A&K Representative’s mobile phone for services at any time.
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE JOHANNESURG

    On arrival at Johannesburg, you will be met by an A&K Representative and privately transferred to your hotel. Explore the area around your hotel.

    Overnight: Gold Class – DaVinci; Silver Class – Radisson Blu Sandton
    Meal Plan: None

  • Day 2

    JOHANNESURG – LIVINGSTONE

    Early this morning, meet your personal chauffer and head to the airport for your flight to Livingstone. On arrival you will be met by a representative who will assist you with your transfer and check-in at the lodge. Settle in and then depart on a private helicopter flight over the mighty Victoria Falls and soak in the breathtaking sight of the stunning gorges and the Victoria Falls.

    Overnight: Gold Class – Sanctuary Sussi and Chuma; Silver Class – Zambezi Sun
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 3

    LIVINGSTONE – CHOBE NATIONAL PARK

    After breakfast, depart in your private vehicle for Chobe National Park. Later this afternoon, head out for a sunset cruise. Sip on cocktails while watching an African sunset.

    Overnight: Gold Class – Sanctuary Chobe Chilwero; Silver Class – Chobe Game Lodge
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 4

    CHOBE NATIONAL PARK

    Set off early this morning for an amazing game drive. This evening you could either head out for another game drive or take part in other activities available at your lodge. Either head out for a photographic safari cruise or a sun downer cruise. After lunch explore the park on foot along with your private armed guide.

    Overnight: Gold Class – Sanctuary Chobe Chilwero; Silver Class – Chobe Game Lodge
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 5

    CHOBE NATIONAL PARK – OKAVANGO DELTA

    Meet your lodge representative at the reception area who will transfer you to the airstrip for your light aircraft flight to the Okavango Delta, the largest inland delta in the world.

    Overnight: Gold Class – Sanctuary Baines’ Camp, Sanctuary Chief’s Camp; Silver Class – Xakanaka Camp Okavango
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 6

    OKAVANGO DELTA

    Famed for its big cat and bird population, the Okavango Delta is a peaceful heaven where animals have been protected for decades and know they will not be disturbed. You could explore the day at your pace.

    Overnight: Gold Class – Sanctuary Baines’ Camp; Silver Class – Xakanaka Camp Okavango
    Meal Plan: Meals: B,L,D

  • Day 7

    OKAVANGO DELTA – MOREMI GAME RESERVE

    Transfer to the airstrip for your light aircraft flight to the Moremi Game Reserve widely acknowledged as the “predator capital of the world. Game viewing in the Okavango Delta is nothing short of spectacular, you could enjoy a seasonal mokoro (dugout canoe) ride for a rare safari opportunity across the marshlands; you may see crocodiles and bathing hippos as well as ostrich, antelope and wildebeests.

    Overnight: Gold Class – Sanctuary Chief’s Camp; Silver Class – Camp Okavango
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 8

    MOREMI GAME RESERVE

    You have the opportunity to see animals up close with your expert guide on morning and evening game drives in open 4WD vehicles.

    Overnight: Gold Class – Sanctuary Chief’s Camp; Silver Class – Camp Okavango
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 9

    MOREMI GAME RESERVE – CAPE TOWN

    Meet your lodge representative who will transfer you to the airstrip for your light aircraft flight to Maun. On arrival at Maun you will clear customs and check-in for your flight to Cape Town via Johannesburg. On arrival at Cape Town you will be met by an A&K Representative who will assist you with your transfer and check-in at your hotel. Enjoy some time at leisure.

    Overnight: Gold Class – Table Bay Hotel; Silver Class – Victoria and Alfred Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 10

    CAPE TOWN

    Journey past the ‘bikini beaches’ of Clifton & Camps Bay and descend into Hout Bay Valley. Continue to Chapman’s Peak Drive and onto the Cape of Good Hope Nature Reserve at the end of the Cape Peninsula. Enroute visit Boulders, a lovely beach sheltered from the wind and famous for its colony of endangered Jackass Penguins. Pass through Simon's Town and learn about the famous hound called "Just Nuisance", the most renowned animal in the annals of the Royal Navy. Continue to Kalk Bay a quaint fishing hamlet on the False Bay coast.

    Overnight: Gold Class – Table Bay Hotel; Silver Class – Victoria and Alfred Hotel
    Meal Plan: B,L

  • Day 11

    CAPE TOWN

    Weather permitting, explore the infamous Robben Island. Post lunch, visit the famed Table Mountain, recently been named as one of the ‘New 7 Wonders of Nature’.

    Overnight: Gold Class – Table Bay Hotel; Silver Class – Victoria and Alfred Hotel
    Meal Plan: B,L

  • Day 12

    DEPART CAPE TOWN

    Meet your A&K Representative who will assist you with your transfer to the airport for your onward connections.

    Meal Plan: B

LEGENDS OF SOUTH AFRICA

Programme Overview

  • Get set for an exciting, fun filled holiday with adventure and leisure. Experience South Africa from the wildlife to the wine lands.

    Duration: 9 Days/8 Nights

    Highlights: Johannesburg | Sun City | Pilanesberg | Stellenbosch / Franschhoek | Hermanus | Cape Town

    A&K ADVANTAGE

    • Experience a mix of thrilling wildlife encounters, luxury accommodation and fine dining.
    • Enjoy your morning at the Valley of Waves located at Sun City.
    • Experience an adrenaline rush with a Hot air balloon ride over the Savannah followed by Game viewing at Pilanesberg.
    • Relish Wine Tasting and Cellar Tours in Stellenbosch / Franschhoek and indulge in gastronomical delights.
    • Get adrenaline rushes with a Whale watching cruise and Shark cage diving at Hermanus.
    •Visit a piece of history in Robben Island and the iconic Table Mountain.
    • Spend a full day exploring Cape Point, ascending the Cape of Good Hope and seeing endangered penguins at Boulders Beach.
    • Private transfers arranged for arrival and departure.
    • The services of an A&K Representative*

    * An A&K Representative is always at hand to provide unparalleled 24/7 support, be it local travel tips or reconfirming arrangement details, to almost anything. Should you ever need help or advice, dial your A&K Representative’s mobile phone for service at any time.
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE JOHANNESBURG – SUN CITY

    On arrival be welcomed by an A&K Representative and personal chauffer for your transfer to Sun City. Time is at leisure to enjoy the facilities and activities at your hotel.

    Overnight: Gold Class – The Palace of the Lost City; Silver Class – Cascades
    Meal Plan:None

  • Day 2

    SUN CITY

    Depart for the iconic Valley of Waves waterpark with the Roaring Lagoon. Evening is at leisure where you may explore a range of activities by yourself – a live performance by one of the many International stars; or a musically extravagant show at the theatre. There is a casino on the premises as well to try your hand with lady Luck.

    Overnight: Gold Class – The Palace of the Lost City; Silver Class – Cascades
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 3

    SUN CITY – PILANESBERG

    This morning at the crack of dawn, drive towards the world acclaimed Pilanesberg Game resort. Go on an exciting “Aerial Safari “ in a Hot Air Balloon with a lot of game spotting and celebrate your landing with a glass of sparkling wine. (This excursion is subject to weather conditions) Late afternoon, enjoy a game drive in the comfort of a game vehicle.

    Overnight: Gold Class – Ivory Tree Lodge; Silver Class – Bakubung
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 4

    PILANESBERG – STELLENBOSCH / FRANSCHHOEK

    Depart from Pilanesberg for Johannesburg airport in your own private vehicle for a scheduled flight to Cape Town. On arrival at Cape Town you will meet your personal chauffer for a scenic drive to the Cape Winelands. Explore the renowned region of Stellenbosch and Franschhoek. Enjoy a blend of modern French cuisine spiced with an Eastern influence at Monneaux restaurant (or similar) followed by fine cheese tasting. Then head for a final wine-tasting and cellar tour to the beautiful Plaisir de Merle estate.

    Overnight: Gold Class – La Residence; Silver Class – Oude Werf
    Meal Plan: B,L

  • Day 5

    HERMANUS

    Enjoy a scenic drive to Hermanus – the official Whale watching capital of the world. Head out on a whale watching cruise for a memorable trip. After this exhilarating experience, you could enjoy sun downers on your return to the hotel.

    Overnight: Gold Class – Birkenhead House; Silver Class – The Marine
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 6

    HERMANUS - CAPE TOWN

    After breakfast at your hotel, indulge in one of the most thrilling experiences - Shark Cage Diving. No prior diving experience is required, and the activity is extremely safe, making it one of your best lifetime memories. Diving equipment and gear is all provided, however the trips are based on weather and tide conditions. On reaching Cape Town after an hour and a half drive, stroll around the area that surrounds your hotel to enjoy some great shopping, dining and the amazing night life.

    Overnight: Gold Class – One and Only; Silver Class – Victoria and Alfred Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 7

    CAPE TOWN

    After breakfast explore the infamous Robben Island. Post lunch, visit the famed Table Mountain, recently been named as one of the ‘New 7 Wonders of Nature’. Weather permitting; you may ascend to its sky-high plateau by cable car for some spectacular views of the city below, the suburbs and the Atlantic Ocean.

    Overnight: Gold Class – One and Only; Silver Class – Victoria and Alfred Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 8

    CAPE TOWN

    After breakfast head out to discover the wonders of Cape Point. Pass and descend into the bucolic Hout Bay valley and from here we travel over the famous Chapman's Peak Drive (subject to road conditions) to join the sea again at the town of Noordhoek. Enjoy a visit to the Cape Point Ostrich Farm where you will enjoy a guided tour around the farm. Continue to the Cape of Good Hope Nature Reserve at the end of the Cape Peninsula. Enroute visit Boulders, a lovely beach sheltered from the wind and famous for its colony of endangered Jackass Penguins. Pass through Simon's Town and learn about the famous hound called "Just Nuisance", the most renowned animal in the annals of the Royal Navy. Continue to Kalk Bay a quaint fishing hamlet on the False Bay coast.

    Overnight: Gold Class – One and Only; Silver Class – Victoria and Alfred Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 9

    DEPART CAPE TOWN

    After breakfast, meet your A&K Representative for your transfer to the airport to board your scheduled flight to Johannesburg and beyond.

    Meal Plan: B

LEGENDS OF THE CAPE

Programme Overview

  • A classic combination of safari, gardens and sandy beaches of Southern Africa for you to explore, enjoy and return home with loads of unforgettable memories.

    Duration: 9 Days/8 Nights

    Highlights: Cape Town | Hermanus | George | Plettenberg

  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE CAPE TOWN

    On arrival in Cape Town, you will be meet an A&K Representative who will assist you with your transfer and check-in at your hotel. Time is at leisure and later you could take a walk around the hotel and its surroundings.

    Overnight: Gold Class – One and Only; Silver Class – Victoria and Alfred Hotel
    Meal Plan: None

  • Day 2

    CAPE TOWN

    After breakfast head out to discover the wonders of Cape Point. Pass and descend into the bucolic Hout Bay valley and from here we travel over the famous Chapman's Peak Drive (subject to road conditions) to join the sea again at the town of Noordhoek.

    Enjoy a visit to the Cape Point Ostrich Farm where you will enjoy a guided tour around the farm. Continue to the Cape of Good Hope Nature Reserve at the end of Cape Peninsula. Enroute visit Boulders, a lovely beach sheltered from the wind and famous for its colony of endangered Jackass Penguins. Pass through Simon's Town and learn about the famous hound called "Just Nuisance", the most renowned animal in the annals of the Royal Navy.

    Continue to Kalk Bay a quaint fishing hamlet on the False Bay coast.

    Overnight: Gold Class – One and Only; Silver Class – Victoria and Alfred Hotel
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 3

    Cape Town

    Your guide will meet you at the hotel lobby and take you for another amazing experience of this area – a visit to the famed Two Oceans Aquarium to see another aspect of the marine life.

    Get up close and personal with the rock hopper penguins. Watch some of the remarkable activities of creatures such as goose barnacles, cushion stars and sea cucumbers as they feed, breathe and even give birth. The Aquarium offers another unique opportunity to get close to the marine life - you could take a dive in and see the shoals of large predators such as yellowtail, the ragged-tooth sharks and the black and white mussel cracker. Leave with some amazing photographs and great memories.

    Afternoon is at your leisure. You could choose to either indulge in some retail therapy at one of the many amazing shops around the V&A Waterfront, or perhaps enjoy the spa facilities at your hotel.

    Overnight: Gold Class – One and Only; Silver Class – Victoria and Alfred Hotel
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 4

    CAPE TOWN – HERMANUS

    Meet your chauffer after breakfast and head out on the scenic Garden route. Enjoy this hour and a half scenic drive to Hermanus – the official Whale watching capital of the world!

    Check in to your hotel and head out for a whale watching cruise. Head towards the Bay and board your boat for a memorable trip. You could encounter two different whale species inhabiting these waters, Humpback and Bryde’s whales (July to November).

    Overnight: Gold Class – Grootbos Villa; Silver Class – Grootbos Private Reserve
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 5

    HERMANUS – GEORGE

    Meet your A&K Representative for your transfer to George via Cape Town Airport.

    Arrive at George and check into your hotel.

    Overnight: Gold Class – Fancourt; Silver Class – Hyatt Oubaai
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 6

    GEORGE

    Head out to explore a Natural Heritage site - the Featherbed Nature Reserve.

    In order to protect the reserve there is a limit to the number of people that are allowed daily. In the company of your personal guide, walk, trek and drive through this natural wonder.

    Return to your hotel for the night.

    Overnight: Gold Class – Fancourt; Silver Class – Hyatt Oubaai
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 7

    GEORGE – PLETTENBERG BAY

    Head off further down the Garden Route towards the town of Plettenberg Bay – considered to be the Jewel of the Garden Route.

    On arrival at Plettenberg, check into your hotel and proceed to visit the only primate sanctuary-the Monkey land. See over 550 primates comprising of squirrel monkeys, capuchin monkeys, Saki monkeys, ringtail and black-and-white ruffed lemurs and more in their forest home and also experience the 128-metre suspended canopy walk.

    Overnight: Gold Class – The Plettenberg Hotel - Villa; Silver Class – Hog Hollow
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 8

    PLETTENBERG BAY

    Other than the scenic beauty and pristine beaches, Plettenberg has a lot more to offer - from whale -watching to deep sea fishing to skydiving to hiking and other forest adventures, surfing, kayaking, bungee jumping, black water tubing to horse-riding; to mention just a few of the activities that you can choose from.

    We certainly recommend a bungee jump experience over the Bloukrans River Bridge for the ultimate thrill! It is the world’s highest commercial bungee bridge and is safe for all age groups.

    Overnight:Gold Class – The Plettenberg Hotel - Villa; Silver Class – Hog Hollow
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 9

    DEPART PLETTENBERG BAY

    Meet your A&K Representative who will assist you with your transfer to the airport to board your flight to Johannesburg and beyond.

    Meal Plan: B

  • A luxurious escape to explore the splendours of South Africa in all its glory ! The perfect combination of nature, culture, wildlife adventure and more…

    Duration: 9 Days/8 Nights

    Highlights: Johannesburg | Sun City | Kruger National Park | Cape Town

  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE JOHANNESBURG – SUN CITY

    On arrival be welcomed by an A&K Representative and personal chauffer for your transfer to Sun City.

    Time is at leisure to enjoy the facilities and activities at your hotel.

    Overnight: Gold Class – The Palace of the Lost City; Silver Class – Sun City Hotel
    Meal Plan:None

  • Day 2

    SUN CITY

    Depart for the iconic Valley of Waves waterpark with the Roaring Lagoon. Evening is at leisure where you may explore a range of activities on your own – a live performance by one of the many International stars; or a musically extravagant show at the theatre. There is a casino on the premises as well to try your hand with lady luck.

    Overnight: Gold Class – The Palace of the Lost City; Silver Class – Sun City Hotel
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 3

    SABI SANDS

    This morning, meet your personal chauffer who will drive you to Johannesburg airport. Board your flight to Sabi Sands air strip. On arrival at Sabi Sands, you will meet a representative and will be transferred to your lodge. Post lunch; take part in a game drive armed with your cameras for some spectacular images. Return to the lodge for the night.

    Overnight: Gold Class – Sabi Sabi Little Bush Lodge; Silver Class – Sabi Sabi Bush Lodge
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 4

    SABI SANDS

    Set off this morning at dawn and then again later in the afternoon with your experience tracker and guide for some amazing game drives and experiences in the bush.

    Overnight: Gold Class – Sabi Sabi Little Bush Lodge; Silver Class – Sabi Sabi Bush Lodge
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 5

    SABI SANDS

    Spend another day in this spectacular wildlife reserve. You could do a safari at dawn and then do the nocturnal drive for another unique experience of the bush. Alternately, you could go on a bush walk with experienced trackers and explore another aspect of the African bush.

    Overnight: Gold Class – Sabi Sabi Little Bush Lodge; Silver Class – Sabi Sabi Bush
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 6

    SABI SANDS – CAPE TOWN

    Meet your A&K Representative for a transfer to KMIA airport for your schedule flight to Cape Town. On arrival, check into your hotel and head out for another amazing experience – Cape Town City tour. Visit the famed Table Mountain named as one of the ‘New 7 Wonders of Nature’. Weather permitting; you may ascend to its sky-high plateau by cable car for some spectacular views of the city below, the suburbs and the Atlantic Ocean.

    Overnight: Gold Class – One and Only; Silver Class – Taj Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 7

    CAPE TOWN

    Drive down to the Harbour and board your boat where your equipment will be ready for your use. After the safety instructions, head out for a few hours at sea to enjoy the close sightings of the Great Whites through the safety of the shark cage.

    Overnight: Gold Class – One and Only; Silver Class – Taj Hotel
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 8

    CAPE TOWN

    Head out to discover the wonders of Cape Point. Pass and descend into the bucolic Hout Bay valley and from here we travel over the famous Chapman's Peak Drive (subject to road conditions) to join the sea again at the town of Noordhoek.

    Enjoy a visit to the Cape Point Ostrich Farm where you will enjoy a guided tour around the farm. Continue to the Cape of Good Hope Nature Reserve at the end of the Cape Peninsula. Enroute visit Boulders, a lovely beach sheltered from the wind and famous for its colony of endangered Jackass Penguins. Pass through Simon's Town and learn about the famous hound called "Just Nuisance", the most renowned animal in the annals of the Royal Navy.

    Continue to Kalk Bay a quaint fishing hamlet on the False Bay coast.

    Overnight: Gold Class – One and Only; Silver Class – Taj Hotel
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 9

    DEPART CAPE TOWN

    Meet your A&K Representative who will assist with your transfer to the airport for your flight to Johannesburg and beyond.

    Meal Plan: B
