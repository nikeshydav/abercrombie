Suggested South Luangwa accommodation

  • Kapani Safari Lodge

    Set in lush grounds Kapani means a place of mopane trees, Kapani Safari Lodge is set in the main Mfuwe area of the South Luangwa. Experience excellent safaris year round with your choice of game drives, night drives and game walks.

    Why not stay in camp by the pool and see what wildlife comes to you? According to the season enjoy a sundowner in the bar and then dine al fresco on the deck overlooking the Kapani Lagoon, close to the Luangwa River. Just eight stylish rooms and two substantial suites provide a high level of comfort. A thatched building in the centre of this luxury safari lodge houses the lounge and library

    Mchenja Camp

    Set on the banks of the Luangwa River, Mchenja Camp commands magnificent river views. The camp has five stylishly designed tents under thatch, each with outside shower and indoor bath.

    A small pool allows you to cool off after a busy morning of safari activities. Game viewing in this part of the park is exceptional and excursions are conducted on foot or by vehicle. Superb night game drives are available and well worth while as you are bound to see many of Luangwa's large pods of hippo - there are estimated to be at least 50 hippos per kilometre of river.

    Nsefu Camp

    The first photographic safari camp in Zambia, Nsefu Camp is located on a sweeping bend of the river and consists of six traditional rondavels, each with a great view of the river.

    One of only two camps in the Nsefu sector, it is very unlikely that you will see another person on game drives - your own private Zambia. In 2009 the camp will be opening in the Emerald Season, when the river is high and lagoons full. Access will be from Nkwali via boat, an exciting journey where you should see flocks of birds, hippo and crocodiles. Due to high water levels only boat and walking safaris will bring you close to nature.
