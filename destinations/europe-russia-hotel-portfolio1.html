Suggested St Petersburg accommodation

  • Grand Hotel Europe

    Follow in the footsteps of Tchaikovsky and George Bernard Shaw all of whom have graced the stately Grand Hotel Europe. A genuine historical, cultural and culinary landmark, it is ideally positioned on Nevsky Prospekt, and surrounded by cultural treasures including the Russian Museum and Mussorgsky Theatre.

    It is no surprise to find it has played host to some of the world's most famous names, including Tchaikovsky and George Bernard Shaw.

    In terms of design, the hotel's superb, impossibly grand façade gives way to a sumptuous interior - soaring ceilings, stained glass, art nouveau arches and detailing, and superb hard wood features abound. The 301 rooms and suites are equally wonderful, transporting guests back to the age of the Tsars with restored original features complemented by a butler service available on request. Guest rooms are generally large whilst suites feature rare antique Russian furnishings.

    Hotel Astoria

    Set in the heart of St. Petersburg, the Astoria is a member of the famed Rocco Forte collection. Hotel Astoria occupies an unrivalled setting in St Isaac's Square at the hub of St Petersburg's vibrant city centre.

    St Isaac's Cathedral stands opposite, and the renowned Hermitage Museum is only a short walk away. This elegant hotel is the perfect base from which to explore the magic and mystery of St. Petersburg.

    Moika 22 Kempinski

    Located in a charming and exclusive area, at the very heart of St. Petersburg, on the Moika River, opposite the renowned Hermitage Museum.

    Opened in August 2005, the Moika retains the wonderful façade of a historic building, designed by Dutch engineer, Basil von Witte during the years 1852-1853. Behind the façade, however, the building has been entirely reconstructed to create a slick and elegant interior - with marble floors and a huge, glass ceilings atrium at its heart.

    The Moika's 188 rooms and suites offer panoramic views of St. Petersburg's golden cupolas and gilded palaces and are calm, homely spaces. All have super-comfortable beds with the finest quality goose-down pillows and duvets, air-conditioning and separate bathrooms with heated floors.
