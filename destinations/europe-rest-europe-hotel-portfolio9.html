Suggested ICELAND accommodation

  • 101 HOTEL

    With a commanding presence in the oldest postal code of Reykjavík, the 101 Hotel is right in the centre of this vibrant city; its cultural institutions, main shopping district and the main hub of restaurants, bars and nightlife are all a short walking distance away. The 101 Hotel communicates refined elegance, with a stylish sense of comfort pervading throughout.

    Furnished with works by local artists, the 101 Hotel is the first boutique hotel in Iceland and exudes pure Nordic coolness. The heated oak floors, lounge with fireplace and the lively popular bar will keep you warm whilst the excellent service will ensure your stay here is purely about your enjoyment.

    HOTEL BORG

    Hotel Borg is a luxurious boutique hotel in the heart of Reykjavík. It overlooks the central square of Austurvollur, with a variety of cafes, restaurants and shops all on the doorstep. The Borg is a landmark in Reykjavik, built in 1930 and restored to its original Art Deco style in 2006-2007, the hotel is a showcase of sophistication.

    RADISSON BLU 1919

    The Radisson Blu 1919 Hotel is a uniquely designed 4 star lifestyle hotel in the heart of Reykjavik, offering outstanding service to leisure travellers.

    Built for office use in the year 1919 by the first shipping company in Iceland the hotel is one of the most beautiful historical buildings in Reykjavik and is the perfect blend of historic architecture and modern interiors.

    • RANGA LODGE

      Hotel Ranga is a luxury countryside hotel and the only 4 star hotel in South Iceland. It offers all the facilities and services of a modern resort and is located about an hour from Reykjavík between Hella and Hvolsvollur.

      It lies just off the main road right in middle of South Iceland's panoramic scenery, providing stunning picturesque surroundings, such as the famous volcano Mt. Hekla, several glaciers and the Westman Islands. It's ideal location offers something special for the visitor on a year-round basis and gives you a real taste of Iceland. Right on the banks of the Iceland's premiere salmon river, the Eystri-Rangá (world's #1 in 2007), the hotel offers an optimal atmosphere to meet every traveller's needs, whether they be relaxation, pleasure, adventure or business.
