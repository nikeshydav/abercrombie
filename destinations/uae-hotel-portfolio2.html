  • Al Maha Desert Resort And Spa

    The resort lies nestled in a verdant oasis in one of Arabia's largest nature reserves - home to gazelle, oryx, numerous species of birds, and some of Arabia's most impressive dune and desert landscapes.

    The Al Maha Desert Resort & Spa is secreted amongst 86 square miles of sand dunes and desert plain, with the Hajar Mountain providing a dramatic backdrop.


    Built in the style of an ancient Bedouin settlement, with the neutral toned 'tents' shading individual suites, this secluded, internationally-awarded hideaway resort is redolent with the history of the region. This sanctuary located in the sands, offers tented style suites featuring Arabian antiques and handcrafted furniture and are themed red for sand, blue for sky or green for oasis.

    Anantara Dubai The Palm Resort & Spa is located on the eastern crescent of Dubai's iconic Palm Jumeirah. This world famous landmark is located close to Dubai Marina and only 45 minutes from Dubai International Airport.


    Surrounded by lush gardens, 293 beautifully appointed rooms and villas are uniquely designed with contemporary Asian flair overlooking the lagoons or the Palm. Complementing such a prestigious destination, resort facilities include a private beach, three lagoons, water sports, a shoreline infinity pool, a fitness studio, two tennis courts and an Anantara Spa with 10 treatment rooms, all designed with relaxation and luxurious indulgence in mind.

  • Atlantis, The Palm

    Atlantis, The Palm is ocean-themed and the flagship resort at the apex of The Palm Jumeirah's Crescent. There are several unique elements at the resort which focus on the extensive marine habitats. At The Lost Chambers, you can meander through underground passageways with 20 fresh and sea water exhibits. At Aquaventure, the 42 acre water themed park, you can take the body slide into a tube and through the shark tank.


    The accommodation here is utterly sumptuous, from the bars, lobbies and restaurants to the rooms and suites. The huge ShuiQi Spa and Fitness set over two floors of the Royal Towers provides a wealth of treatments, bathing options and water therapies.


    Activities are in abundance whether it is the choice of two beaches at Aquaventure or the Royal beach or the two swimming pools one with families in mind. Coupled with the tennis centre, Sanctuary nightclub or the shopping offerings you are spoilt for choice from dawn to well into the night.

  • Banyan Tree Al Wadi

    Amid stretches of desert sand in tranquil Wadi Khadeja, an oasis of peace awaits at Banyan Tree Al Wadi. A sanctuary surrounded by the evergreen ghaf tree.     The activities on offer are outstanding with the world-renowned Banyan Tree Spa or a round of golf at the Al Hamra course to a class in falconry, horseback-riding, camel safaris and bike trails. An Arabian wildlife experience awaits within the nature reserve, home to local desert fauna such as Arabian gazelles, camels and oryxes, offering you a unique opportunity to interact with and observe these gentle creatures up close.

  • Burj Al Arab

    In its relatively short tenure on the Dubai coastline this instantly recognizable hotel has attracted international attention and awe, ensuring its place as one of the most photographed structures in the world. An extensive range of other facilities includes classic sports car rental, Rolls Royce chauffeurs, an outdoor pool, beach access, water sports, shopping and a fitness centre. The hotel operates the Sinbad's Kid's Club for children aged one to 12 years.
