Classic Bali

" Amandari's Classic Bali" offers a choice of three complimentary activities during a three-night stay, allowing guests to fully experience the vibrant soul of classic Bali.

Amandari, or 'peaceful spirits', is located just outside Bali's cultural capital of Ubud. Perched on an escarpment high above the meandering Ayung River Forge in the village of Kedewatan, Amandari's 30 free-standing suites are idyllic place from which to discover the essence of this extraordinary island. Explore rural villages filled with artisan workshops specializing in woodwork, painting, gold and silver jewelry, pottery and musical instruments. Hike or bike along the winding paths of terraced rice fields. Relax to the meditative sounds of gamelan players by the main pool or indulge in soothing spa treatments incorporating local wellness practices.

Amandari's Classic Bali includes:

. Three nights' accommodation

. Return airport transfers

. Daily breakfast

. One set dinner per stay (from 1 May 2012 onwards)

. Room upgrade upon check-in ( subject to availability)

. A choice of three activities out of below:
- A scenic Ayung River trek with a personal guide and picnic breakfast
- A Balinese home-cooking class in a traditional Balinese kitchen
- A one-hour Amandari message
- A visit to a well-known local healer
- A tour of Bali's craft villages (silverwork, wood and stone carving) or
- John Hardy's silver atelier
