Suggested Rome accommodation

  • Aldrovandi Villa Borghese

    A member of the Leading Small Hotels of the World, this superbly located hotel is situated in Rome's most upscale residential area.

    Hotel de Russie

    A member of the Rocco Forte Group, frequented by celebrities and Italian nobles alike, and furnished in a contemporary style, the Hotel de Russie is ideally located between the Spanish Steps. The Wellness Zone boasts a spa with marine hydropool, sauna, Turkish steam bath, treatments and gym - the Roman Ritual treatment is a must for tired feet.

    A unique luxury hotel in the heart of Rome, Hotel de Russie is set in extensive, terraced gardens - the 'Secret Garden', which provides a tranquil oasis amidst the bustle of central Rome. After dark, the garden is illuminated by soft lights and, during high season is home to the open air Stravinskij Bar.
