Travel To Mauritius


Mauritius greets you with a vibrant contrast of colors, cultures and landscapes in its beautiful islands. A melting pot of Indian, African, Chinese and European influences this island nation truly offers a much needed refreshing break from the demanding city life.

At a glance, Mauritius paints a colorful picture of saffron clad Buddhist monks strolling around the Banyan trees to Hindu temples teeming with devotees, to masses partaking in the Father Laval walk towards the blessed Blessed Jacques Désiré Laval.

Mauritius holidays are characterized by white sandy beaches, turquoise lagoon, hospitable locals and palatable regional cuisine. Take the historic tea route across the islands and visit plantations or hit the shores and swim among the crooning dolphins. Greet the wildlife in the Bel Ombre Nature Park on a quad bike or indulge in numerous water sports.

A travel to Mauritius evokes a string of memorable moments, whether you're a newlywed couple, family or a solo traveler.

A personalized holiday in Mauritius by Abercrombie & Kent is bound to rejuvenate your mind, body and soul with its shiny warm days, balmy evenings, tepid waters interposed with lush sand beaches and scent of tropical fruits.

Weather
Mauritius experiences a hot and humid summer from December to April and cooler, drier conditions from May to November. Under the influence of the prevailing south-easterly winds, the east coast is slightly cooler and wetter than the west. The winds can bring a welcome relief to the east coast in the hottest summer months but are more blustery here in July and August. The cyclone season lasts from December to March. Although a direct hit is rare, cyclones far out at sea can bring some days of squally rain.
