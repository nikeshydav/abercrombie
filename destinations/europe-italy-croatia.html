Travel To Italy & Croatia


Italy and Croatia make up for a great tourist spot. Where Italy attracts visitors to the pleasures of la dolce vita, Croatia has managed to sweep off hearts with its many natural and architectural treasures, making it a jewel of the Adriatic.

Try an eclectic cultural mix sparkled with love and romance in the luxury tours to Italy. Imagine a scenic experience of snow-capped Alps laden with hilltop villages in the Tuscany and Umbria area in the north and rugged terrain of Sicily in the south. Italy is characterised with vibrant and sophisticated cities that can be contrasted against the simple elegance of the countryside. As the country is relatively new with just 150 years on its chart, each region has a distinct cultural identity attached to it. Experience the Italian scenic splendour with its unique and rich heritage and for the love of Italy; imbibe in yourself their cuisine, music, art and beauty which make up as its core values.

Croatia speaks of having one of the spectacular coastlines in Europe. With more than 6000 kilometres of shoreline and over 1000 islands, reefs and cliffs; its coastline demands special attention. Sparsely populated, these islands have quaint set ups of stone built fishing villages and private coves for ultimate luxury amidst the shade of nature. You can enjoy a true Croatian holiday, the Mediterranean way. An unspoilt terrain, this Mediterranean land of Croatia extends from Istrain peninsula in the north to the Dalmatian coast which carries forward to Dubrovnik in the south.
