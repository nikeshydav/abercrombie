Suggested Sossusvlei Accommodation

  • KULALA DESERT LODGE

    Kulala Desert Lodge is a rustic tented camp, located on the Kulala Wilderness Reserve, offering inspiring views of the Namib Sand Dunes. The accommodation includes 19 en suite thatched Kulalas (which means "to sleep" in Oshimwambo the local language), all on wooden platforms, perfect to capture the magnificent views across the desert. The main area has a plunge pool, lounge, dining area and bar.

    Some of the amazing activities include nature walks and drives, or alternatively a horse riding safari, early morning hot air balloon safaris over the desert and trips to the world famous dunes of Sossusvlei.

    LITTLE KULALA

    Kulala means 'to sleep' and what better place to drift off, under a sky littered with thousands of stars. Little Kulala is a wonderful property furnished with exquisite fixtures and fittings, each of the eleven thatched villas are set amongst 21,000 hectares of the private Kulala Wilderness Reserve.

    Private plunge pools, bleached decks and rooftop 'skybeds' ensure a romantic, exclusive stay. Game drives and walks aim to share with you the solitude and beauty of the Namib Desert.Desert adapted wildlife such as ostrich, gemsbok may be seen by day and aardwolf and bat-eared fox by night.

    For the ultimate way to witness the sheer vastness of Namibia a morning hot air balloon ride can be arranged. (Closed 15 January - 15 February)

    Wilderness Camp

    Kulala Wilderness Camp is located on the private Kulala Wilderness Reserve which is some 37 000 hectares in size. The camp is made up of 10 comfortable Meru Style Tents with thatched roofs or "kulalas" (which means "to sleep" in Oshimwambo the local language) with en-suite bathrooms and verandas.

    All the rooms are built on elevated platforms with commanding views of the valleys and mountains in the distance. The main lounge/dining areas are also raised to take advantage of the endless views
