Suggested Cusco and Machu Picchu accommodation

  • Inkaterra Machu Picchu Hotel

    Inkaterra Machu Picchu is located in an enchanted five-hectare cloud forest garden alongside the Vilcanota River and on the edge of the village of Aguas Calientes. Located just a short shuttle ride from the entrance to Machu Picchu, the lodge serves as an excellent base from which to explore. Skilled artisans built the 85-room hotel using stone, adobe stucco and eucalyptus beams to create a delightful Andean village ambience.

    Rooms are large, well equipped and located in one or two-storey whitewashed cottages, reached via stone pathways weaving their way through the tropical gardens. All the rooms and villas feature thick alpaca blankets and handmade wool coverlets, crackling fireplaces and comfortable spaces for sitting and dining, as well as spa-style bathrooms.

    This hotel creates an authentic Andean ambience and offers a rare slice of barefoot luxury in this ancient location.

    La Casona Inkaterra

    La Casona is Cusco's first luxury boutique hotel. A former colonial manor house it was amongst the earliest Spanish constructions in Cusco, built over an Inca complex and occupied by the conquistadores themselves.

    Immaculately restored to exacting specifications, it has just 11 well-equipped suites circling a traditional central courtyard. Furnished with antiques and retaining original architectural detail, each suite features a sitting area with open fireplace and a plush spa-style marble bathroom.

    Attention to detail and a truly personalised experience can be felt in all aspects of the hotel - from the resident butler to the superb amenities.

    Machu Picchu Sanctuary Lodge

    Exclusively nestled adjacent to the awe-inspiring Machu Picchu Inca citadel, the Orient Express Sanctuary Lodge welcomes you to a place rich in ancient heritage, renowned spirituality and incredible natural natural beauty.

    It is the ideal base from which to explore the archaeological wonders, spot rare species of birds and flowers (especially orchids of which there are an estimated 300 species in the area) or unwind and enjoy breath-taking scenery from the lodge's garden.

    Monasterio Hotel

    Perfectly positioned in the centre of Cusco, this 16th century monastery serves as a museum hotel, resplendent with unique features and 'Cusco Baroque' architecture.

    Located right beside the lively Cuzco central square, Hotel Monasterio is a former monastery and national monument dating from 1592. Resplendent with unique features and 'Cusco Baroque' architecture, it was originally built on the site of the Inca Amaru Qhala Palace and, today, combines its monastic origins with the best of colonial style and a touch of Inca Peru.

    Mountain Lodge Trek to Machu Picchu

    A 'comfort seeker's alternative to the tents and sleeping bags of the standard Inca Trail', The Mountain Lodge Trek incorporates the first Trans-Andean lodge to lodge trek Aguas Calientes, the gateway to Machu Picchu.

    Departures are based on small groups of 10 and include six nights accommodation in wonderful, comfortable lodges set in remote, pristine locations.

    Tambo del Inka Luxury Collection

    The Tambo del Inka Luxury Collection Hotel is a new addition to the Libertador Group of hotels, located in the very heart of the timeless Sacred Valley of the Incas overlooking the Urubamba River.

    The Tambo del Inka Luxury Collection Hotel is a new addition to the Liberator Group of hotels. Nestled in the Sacred Valley, just 50 minutes from the city of Cusco, this intimate resort demands relaxation whilst offering a huge variety of opportunities to explore some of Peru's most magical landscapes and cultural centres - not least the spectacular Inca citadel at Machu Picchu.
