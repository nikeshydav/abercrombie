Suggested Grumeti Reserve accommodation

  • Faru Faru River Lodge

    Basking in the abundance of animal species and birdlife and inspired by a botanical theme, Faru Faru River Lodge offers luxury and elegance in a laidback, rustic setting. Built from local stone and rocks, shaded by sycamore trees and boasting of its own private beach, this intimate, six-suite property is one of the best lodges in Tanzania.

    The lines of the heated rim-flow swimming pool echo those of the watering hole lying just below. A reading area, en-suite bathroom, huge picture windows and an elevated observation deck adorn each suite. The viewing decks are carefully positioned to ensure prime, elevated game views and the rugged landscape from the luxurious comfort of your suite. A 'Swahili' inspired beach allows an opportunity for rest and relaxation.

    Sasakwa Hill Lodge

    An ideal place for those in search of solitude, the Sasakwa Hill Lodge is an incredibly opulent permanent camp situated on top of Sasakwa Hill, with awe inspiring views over Tanzania's Serengeti plains. Set in a 340,000 acre private concession, the lodge is built in the style of an Edwardian Manor House with stone walls, deep verandahs, high ceilings, stately furniture, silverware and ornamental pieces completing the picture.

    Six beautifully appointed air conditioned cottages display typical African influences with touches of wood, leather and grass along with crisp linens, imported chandeliers, hand-crafted furniture and a four-poster bed. Personal, dedicated service and relaxing spa facility with a range of indulgent therapies is another highlight. The area is home to large herds of game that provide incredible sightings all year round. Luxurious and comfortable stay in the middle of wilderness is what describes this Tanzania lodge best.
