The world famous EnotecaPinchiorri resides on the ground floor, and a visit to one of the most precious wine collections is a must for any diner.

The elegant and palatial lobby is only surpassed by the warm welcome by the hotel’s staff. There is no better place to relax after a ‘hard day’ of shopping and sightseeing than an hour or two in the TempiodellaSaluta spa.
