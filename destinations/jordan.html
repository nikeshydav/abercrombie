Jordan: Where history lies hidden

An epitome of faith, religion and belief; Jordan has the outlook of being a modern-meets-ancient civilisation. Unshaken by the ever-changing times, Jordan lives in a timeless-spiritual world being a proud beholder of a land that has seen the face of prophets, miracles and a holy river. It is intersected by two great civilisations, Egypt in the west and Mesopotamia in the east. Take a walk in the hills, enjoy the steepness of the valleys, rush through the clear streams and tread on a spiritual journey once covered by the great prophets of their time.

Jordan is a new world onto itself. Stand on the towering Mount Nebo and experience a spiritual urgency as you place your foot on the mountain from where Moses saw the Holy Land of Jordan. Keeping alive the dying era of Roman-rule in Jerash, have a light escapade into the rose-red city of Petra which became famous for featuring in the Indiana Jones movie. .
Try your hand at adventure and go camel riding through the locales of Wadi Rum which is often known for its varied and enthralling desert landscape. Also rich in traditional water sports; you could fish for tropical aquatic life in Aqaba and watch out for multi-coloured corals while floating on the dead waters of the quintessential Dead Sea.
Holidays in Jordan bring up a field trip and a spiritual excursion together. You can go comparing the Crusader castles with Arabic ones while feasting on special Bedouin food (Jordanian food) prepared using their special technique and compassion. Known for their hospitality, Jordanians will welcome you to enjoy a splendid stay with them while exploring this rich land.
