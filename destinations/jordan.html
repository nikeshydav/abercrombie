Jordan: Where history lies hidden

Explore exotic destinations on tours to the Middle East and North Africa with A&K. Jordan has the outlook of being a modern meets ancient civilization, an epitome of tremendous faith, religion and belief. Unshaken by the worldly chaos, Jordan remains a timeless and spiritual, a proud land that has seen the face of the prophets, miracles and a holy river. It is intersected by two great civilisations, Egypt in the west and Mesopotamia in the east.
Tourist attractions are in abundance here. On a Jordan private journey with Abercrombie & Kent India, stroll through the hills, enjoy exploring the steeping lush valleys, rush through the sparkling streams and tread on a spiritual journey once covered by the great prophets of their time.
Connect with your spiritual self as you stand on the towering Mount Nebo, the mountain from where Moses saw the Holy Land of Jordan. No visit to Jordan is complete without a light escapade into the rose-red city of Petra, renowned for featuring in the legendary Indiana Jones movie.
Adventure seekers can go camel riding through the locales of Wadi Rum, known for its diverse and enchanting desert landscape. Rich in traditional water sports, you could also fish for tropical aquatic life in Aqaba and watch out for multi-coloured corals while floating on the Dead Sea.
Holidays in Jordan are an amalgamation of a field trip and a spiritual excursion. You can go comparing the Crusader castles with Arabic ones while feasting on special Bedouin food (Jordanian food). Known for their hospitality, Jordanians will welcome you to enjoy a splendid stay with them while exploring this rich land.
