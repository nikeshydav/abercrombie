Suggested Chobe Accommodation

  • Sanctuary Chobe Chilwero Lodge

    Chilwero meaning 'a place of high view,' aptly describes this exquisite lodge. Sitting above an escarpment overlooking the Chobe River, this lodge offers unparalleled views of the islands and the flood plains that stretch into Namibia, making it the perfect base to start or end your Botswana Holiday. The cottages have been individually designed to fit in with the Chobe topography and vegetation. The world famous Victoria Falls are also within easy reach making this lodge a clear favorite of all other lodges in Botswana.

    Apart from that, it boasts of a full service spa, the first on the Botswana Safari circuit. Beautifully fashioned, it integrates authentic African chic design with nature. Features include an indoor spa bath, Vichy showers, hydrotherapy, plunge pool, relaxation area and private treatment rooms - one of which is elevated in the treetops.

    Food is a fusion of Western and African cuisine created by high gourmet standards and accompanied by the finest of wines from the lodge's own temperature-controlled wine cellar. Game drives with professional guides take place twice daily in open sided 4-wheel drive vehicles. Sunset cruises are particularly spectacular and offer awe-inspiring views of the Chobe River. Birding safaris, fishing expeditions and exploring the endless tributaries of this mighty river are other highlights.

    Savute Elephant Camp

    Aptly named, the Savute Elephant Camp offers birds-eye view of elephants in their natural habitat. Perched on the former banks of the now arid Savute channel, the camp promises outstanding game viewing without having to leave the confines of the camp itself. Accommodation is in form of twelve recently thatched luxurious tents. The tent interiors are fully appointed with private timber decks, four poster beds, spacious en-suite bathroom and dressing room area. Enjoy the starlit African night sky with a state-of-the-art telescope in the main lodge.

    Renowned for its excellent game viewing and exciting birdlife, Savute Elephant Camp lets you find yourself in harmony with a unique timeless place, chalked apart from city life.
