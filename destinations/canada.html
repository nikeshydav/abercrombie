Travel To Canada


The greatest charm of the Canadian wilderness is its rugged and raw beauty. Here lie opportunities galore to stroll through the lush alpine backwoods, row across the icy rivers or to drive along the far-stretched roads amidst the mesmerizing scenery of the snow capped Rockies. Whether you are an adrenaline junkie, looking for fulfilled family holiday or in pursuit of a serene retreat away from the city life, Canada offers something for everyone.

Here you can engage in adventure sports such as skiing, hiking, or salmon fishing, wildlife tracking in wilds or go sightseeing or dine in one of the world class fine dine restaurants in glamorous Canadian cities. With Abercrombie and Kent Holidays in Canada you embark upon a spectacular journey on a seaplane and catch an exclusive view of Vancouver's glittering skyline or the contrasting sheer splendor of the vast Canadian outback. Travel to Canada to enjoy an epic adventure tailored to your preferences that will leave you with memories of dramatic scenery, sightings of the grizzly bear, friendly locals and larger than life adventures in this glorious country.

Best time to go to Canada

The peak season is from June to September. Killer whale viewing is best in June and July.
