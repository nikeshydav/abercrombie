Wings over Zambia & Zimbabwe

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 7 Nights

    A safari in wild Africa. Prolific wildlife. Up close with nature on walking safaris. Mana Pools National Park in the heart of the Zambezi Valley. Water-based safari activities.

  • Day 1

    Arrive Johannesburg

    An A&K greeting on arrival into the safari gateway of Southern Africa, and assistance to your airport hotel.

    Overnight: InterContinental Johannesburg O.R. Tambo (Standard Room)

  • Day 2

    Johannesburg - Lusaka - South Luangwa National Park

    This morning, return independently to the international terminal for your flight* north to Lusaka, Zambia. A connecting light aircraft flight takes you further north to Mfuwe and the spectacular South Luangwa National Park. The next part of your adventure begins with an afternoon game drive

    Overnight: Sanctuary Puku Ridge Tented Camp (Safari Tent)
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 3 - 4

    South Luangwa

    You now have two days to explore the South Luangwa. Rise before dawn for a morning game drive: your safari guide will introduce you to this superb park and deliver some exciting photographic opportunities. Elephant, lion, wild dog, buffalo and a wide variety of antelope reside in this area, as does the regal leopard. Keep a lookout for the South Luangwa's endemic species, Thornicroft's giraffe and Cookson's wildebeest, and drive along the Luangwa River to see the vibrant carmine bee-eaters nesting in the riverbank walls; take an afternoon safari drive with sundowners, and a thrilling night drive; or, as the South Luangwa is 'home' of the walking safari, head out on foot with your armed guide to see the bush from a different perspective

    Overnight: Sanctuary Puku Ridge Tented Camp (Safari Tent)
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 5

    South Luangwa - Lusaka - Mana Pools National Park

    A light aircraft flight takes you from Mfuwe via Lusaka to Mana Pools National Park in Zimbabwe. On the middle reaches of the Zambezi River, a hundred kilometres below Lake Kariba is the Mana Pools National Park situated in the heart of the Zambezi Valley. Ringed by the far distant blue hills of the escarpment, this area is bursting with a profusion of birds and animals, especially during dry season from June to October, and it is Zimbabwe's second world heritage site

    Overnight: Ruckomechi Camp (Tented Unit)
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 6 - 7

    Mana Pools National Park

    Ruckomechi Camp lies on the banks of the Zambezi, shaded by a large grove of acacia and mahogany trees and with a superb view of the mountains of Africa's Great Rift Valley across the river in Zambia. Two full days at the camp to discover the fascinating surrounds. The area is renowned for large numbers of elephant, buffalo, hippo and eland, especially in the winter when they concentrate along the river. Predators such as lion, leopard and wild dog are also found. Birdlife is superb, particularly for both mopane woodland and riverine species, with numerous local species like Collared Palm-Thrush, Racket-tailed Roller, Purple-banded Sunbird and Black-throated Wattle-Eyes. Activities include wildlife viewing in open 4WD vehicles on motorised pontoon boats, on foot and in canoes

    Overnight: Ruckomechi Camp (Tented Unit)
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 8

    Mana Pools National Park - Lusaka

    A good-bye from A&K as you end your safari with a flight to Lusaka airport

    Meal Plan: B

ZIMBABWE EXPLORER

Programme Overview

  • Experience the awesome power of nature in comfort with this five day itinerary giving you the best Zimbabwe has to offer.

    Duration: 5 Days/6 Nights
    Highlights: Hwange | Victoria Falls

    A&K ADVANTAGE
    • Experience morning and night game drives at Hwange National Park.
    • Enjoy a Sunset Cruise on the Mighty Zambezi River.
    • Take the famed Steam Tram Bridge Tour over The Victoria Falls Bridge.
    • Get a guided Tour of the mighty Victoria Falls.
    • Private transfers arranged for arrival and departure.
    • The services of an A&K Representative*

    * An A&K Representative is always at hand to provide unparalleled 24/7 support, be it local travel tips or reconfirming arrangement details, to almost anything. Should you ever need help or advice, dial your A&K Representative’s mobile phone for services at any time.
    • Detailed Itinerary

  • Day 1

    ARRIVAL VICTORIA FALLS – HWANGE

    On arrival at Johannesburg, you will be met by an A&K Representative and assisted with your onward flight to the Victoria Falls Airport where you would be met again by a Representative and transferred by road to The Hide Safari Camp situated on the Eastern boundary of the famous Hwange National Park well known world over for its size and its wildlife population of buffalo, zebra, giraffe, rhino, all the big cats and the elephant population. Built in 1992 by the Preston family, who remain deeply involved to this day, the Hide Safari Camp has earned its reputation as a consummate safari destination, for both the exceptional wildlife experience and the hospitality it offers. As a guest, you will stay in one of the ten beautiful tented suites with views over the waterhole, where animals congregate day and night. Between game drives and walks in the National Park, there is plenty of time to relax or sit quietly to watch the wildlife. On arrival after check-in, you will have some time at leisure and then head out for a night game drive for a thrilling chance to see leopard and other nocturnal species.

    Overnight: Gold Class – The Hide Safari Camp; Silver Class – NA
    Meal Plan: None

  • Day 2

    HWANGE

    The waterhole at The Hide is particularly special, being the only source of permanent water for a good-sized area. So you will still be able to enjoy the daily visitors to the camp which often include elephant, giraffe, zebra, impala, waterbuck and kudu. Lions come in from time to time and wild dogs have even made kills in full view of the camp. Head out for an early morning game drive. Later, head back to the camp for lunch and later there is time at leisure to explore other activities the area has to offer. You could perhaps head out on a walking safari escorted by a professional guide or enjoy a sun downer from the camp deck.

    Overnight: Gold Class – The Hide Safari Camp; Silver Class – NA
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 3

    HWANGE – VICTORIA FALLS

    You will be met by your guide and depart Hwange by road to the Victoria Falls Hotel. The hotel has a charm and energy like no other built in 1904, the Victoria Falls Hotel was the very first hotel to be built in Victoria Falls. The hotel exudes original colonial Edwardian charm and recent refurbishments offer guests the modern comforts one would expect from a luxury hotel. It perfectly depicts the era of grand travel with corridors that reflect its historical link to an era in which it was created, with unobstructed views of the Victoria Falls Bridge and the spray of the Falls. A private path leads guests from the gardens to the entrance of the Victoria Falls rainforest in an easy 10 minute stroll. After check-in you have a few hours at leisure before heading out on an iconic Victoria Falls activity - a Sunset Cruise on the Mighty Zambezi River! What could possibly be a better way to end another glorious African day than with a leisurely cruise along with drinks and snacks.

    Overnight: Gold Class – Victoria Falls Hotel; Silver Class – NA

  • Day 4

    VICTORIA FALLS

    You check-in for The Victoria Falls Tram Morning Bridge Tour on board The Victoria Falls Steam Tram that goes over The Victoria Falls Bridge. Steeped in history, the Victoria Falls Tram Bridge Tours are a unique journey into the adventure and romance of a bygone age and a perfect way to end your Zimbabwean escapade. The Afternoon is open for optional activities. You could opt from some adrenaline rushes with some white water rafting, or bungee jumping from the bridge, a walk with the lions or a laid back canoe Safari. Alternately you could spend some time in a bit of retail therapy. Visit the local market, enjoy African culture and everything that an African hand can make and return with some great souvenirs. Perhaps head back to the hotel after enjoying dinner at the famed Boma restaurant.

    Overnight: Gold Class – Victoria Falls Hotel; Silver Class – NA
    Meal Plan: B, D

  • Day 5

    DEPART VICTORIA FALLS

    You check-out of the hotel and depart to Victoria Falls Airport for your onward flight.

    Meal Plan: B

ZIMBABWE – WHEN WILDERNESS CALLS

Programme Overview

  • A safari in wild Africa with prolific wildlife! Come up close with nature and experience some of the cultural experiences with the local famed Tonga tribes of Zimbabwe.

    Duration: 7 Days/6 Nights
    Highlights: Lake Kariba | Bumi Hills | Victoria Falls

    A&K ADVANTAGE
    • Enjoy an evening game drive and a morning game walk at Matusadona National Park while staying at The Changa Safari Camp.
    • Take an unusual transfer - via boat to Bumi Hills.
    • Indulge in a spa at the Bumi Hills Safari Lodge and Spa.
    • Experience a morning game drive and an evening safari cruise on the Lake Kariba.
    • Head out for a rich cultural experience and visit the Tonga tribe on your way to the Victoria Falls.
    • Take part in an iconic Victoria Falls activity - a Sunset Cruise on the Mighty Zambezi River !
    • Taka a guided tour of the mighty Victoria Falls.
    • Private transfers arranged for arrival and departure.
    • The services of an A&K Representative*

    * An A&K Representative is always at hand to provide unparalleled 24/7 support, be it local travel tips or reconfirming arrangement details, to almost anything. Should you ever need help or advice, dial your A&K Representative’s mobile phone for services at any time.
    • DETAILED ITINERARY

  • Day 1

    ARRIVAL HARARE – LAKE KARIBA

    On arrival at Harare International Airport you will be met by a representative and assisted for your charter transfer to Kariba. On arrival at Kariba, you will be transferred to the Changa Lodge in the Matusadona National Park. The Changa Safari Camp is Located on the shores of Lake Kariba in Zimbabwe and provides the unique experience of combined Game and fishing Safaris. Accommodation at Changa consists of luxury tented suites each individually displaying truly unique.

    African influences, from rustic wood, leather and weaves against crisp white linen and locally made furniture. Styled with complete comfort in mind, each tent has en-suite facilities and “under the stars” outside shower and bath. Whether you choose to relax in the comfort of your tent or enjoy spectacular lake vistas from your own private veranda you will be reminded time and again of the endless beauty around you. All tents are mosquito proofed and we have fans for your comfort during the hot months. After check-in you will have some time to unwind and later enjoy the afternoon tea followed by an evening game drive. A leisurely drive in an open safari vehicle is a perfect way to discover the area, and provides an opportunity to see a wider variety of game and scenery as you cover much more ground than on a game walk. Accompanied by your guide, a game drive is a lovely way to spend the evening. Return to your lodge for dinner and overnight.

    Overnight: Gold Class – Changa Safari Camp; Silver Class – NA
    Meal Plan: None

  • Day 2

    LAKE KARIBA

    You will spend the whole day at The Changa Safari Camp. Depart early this morning for a game walk, arguably the best way to experience and get a feel of the African bush. Our professional guides have thorough knowledge of the area and accompanied with them, you could experience Matusadona 'at its best'. Walks are conducted in the early mornings; and this gives you enough time with the rest of the day to enjoy this amazing area. You return to the lodge for lunch and this afternoon you could either choose to go boating, bird watching or try your hand at fishing at the Kariba Lake. Climb onboard the pontoon boat and together with your guide, you can explore the multitude of bays and river inlets where the birdlife is prolific and elephants and antelope abound. Alternately, Kariba is a fisherman’s paradise! You could try your hand at fighting the renowned Tiger Fish for which people from all over the world return time and time again.

    Overnight: Gold Class – Changa Safari Camp; Silver Class – NA
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 3

    LAKE KARIBA – BUMI HILLS

    You will be met by a guide at your lodge and head out for a boat transfer to Bumi Hills where you will stay at the Bumi Hills Safari Lodge and Spa. A wildlife gem with its luxury 20-rooms, Bumi Hills Safari Lodge and Spa, perched on a hill overlooks Lake Kariba and the game-rich shoreline, approximately 55 km south west of Kariba town. With the Zambezi escarpment as its backdrop, the well-known Bumi Hills Safari Lodge overlooks the foreshore below and the vastness of Lake Kariba. The incredible panoramic vista cannot be matched by any safari lodge in southern Africa, transforming all public areas, your room balcony and the Spa into pure WOW factors. You arrive late afternoon and after check-in enjoy the spa. It is pure ecstasy; as you enjoy your massage overlooking the massive lake and the game-rich shoreline. Thereafter relax on the platform near the shoreline for a few hours followed by dinner and overnight at the Camp.

    Overnight: Gold Class – NA; Silver Class – Bumi Hills Safari Lodge and Spa
    Meal Plan: B, D

  • Day 4

    BUMI HILLS

    Head out for an early morning game drive. With your guide, enjoy a 4WD game drive in an open, state-of-the-art Land Rover with the possibility of seeing four of the Big Five - elephants, lions, leopard, buffalo and more. In the nearby Matusadona National Park you may also walk with Bumi’s exceptional armed professional guides to track the rhino. You have some time at leisure and later indulge in another experience totally unique in this area-head out for a safari cruise on the lake.

    Overnight: Gold Class – NA; Silver Class – Bumi Hills Safari Lodge and Spa
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 5

    BUMI HILLS – VICTORIA FALLS

    One of the most appealing attractions of staying at Bumi Hills Safari Lodge and Spa is being so close (30-minute drive) to the sprawling mud-hut Tonga village under Chief Mola. After breakfast, with a guide head out for a rich cultural experience and visit the Tonga tribe on your way to the Victoria Falls. Visit the local school, clinic, village shop, their basic mill and meet the people and then proceed to the Victoria Falls Hotel in Victoria Falls. After check-in you have a few hours at leisure before heading out on an iconic Victoria Falls activity - a Sunset Cruise on the Mighty Zambezi River!

    Overnight: Gold Class – Victoria Falls Hotel; Silver Class – NA
    Meal Plan: B, D

  • Day 6

    VICTORIA FALLS

    After breakfast, you will go on a Guided Tour of the mighty Victoria Falls. The Victoria Falls present a spectacular sight of awe-inspiring beauty and grandeur on the Zambezi River, forming the border between Zambia and Zimbabwe. The afternoon is open for optional activities. You could opt from some adrenaline rushes with either white water rafting or bungee jumping from the bridge, a walk with the lions or a laid back canoe Safari.

    Overnight: Gold Class – Victoria Falls Hotel; Silver Class – NA
    Meal Plan: B, D

  • Day 7

    DEPART VICTORIA FALLS

    You check-out of the Hotel, meet your guide and head out for a road transfer to the Victoria falls Airport for your onward flight.

    Meal Plan: B
