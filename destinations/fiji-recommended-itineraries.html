Fijian Escape

  • Duration: 8 Days/7 Nights

    Highlights:Fiji | Taveuni

    Programme Overview

    Experience the mesmerizing natural beauty, cultures and areas that make Fiji an extraordinary Island and one of the most desirable luxury holiday destinations in the world.

  • Day 1

    ARRIVE FIJI

    Arrive at the Nadi International Airport. An A&K representative will welcome you with a “Bula” and escort you for your transfer to Denarau Marina.

    You will be privately transferred from Denarau Marina to Castaway Island on a luxury catamaran. Castaway Island Resort, located just west of the largest Island of Fiji is surrounded by white sand beaches, vibrant coral reefs with azure waters & also covered in rich tropical rainforest and is spread over 174 acres. Enjoy the amazing fresh sea breeze and breathtaking views along the way to the island.

    Overnight: Castaway Island, Fiji or similar
    Meal Plan: None

  • Day 2

    FIJI

    The day is at leisure. You could simply lay back on the beach or in a hammock and soak in the beauty and tranquility of the island which almost seems surreal. Alternately, you could enjoy many of the activities available for the young and the young at heart. With unrestricted use of all non-motorised water sports equipment including snorkeling equipment, catamarans, kayaks, windsurfers, paddleboards, spy boards and more, you would be spoilt for choice!

    You could also indulge in a catamaran sailing later this afternoon to view the coral through a glass bottom boat.

    This evening, a private dinner under the stars will be set up for you with local fire dancers & musicians who will serenade you through the evening, making this one of your most memorable dinners!

    Overnight: Castaway Island, Fiji or similar
    Meal Plan: B, D

  • Day 3

    FIJI

    This morning, join the island hopping excursion and a Dolphin Safari by speed boat exclusively for you. Enjoy a private picnic that would be set up for you at a secluded beach. Indulge in some local delicacies with champagne and enjoy some great quality time together.

    Enjoy a sundowner at the deck and return to your room for a relaxing massage to end a perfect day of fun in the sun!

    Overnight: Castaway Island, Fiji or similar
    Meal Plan: B, L

  • Day 4

    TAVEUNI

    After breakfast, it’s time to board a private charter flight from Castaway Island to Taveuni Island Resort & Spa, considered to be the Jewel of the Pacific. This will be an amazing opportunity for you to view the stunning scenes of the mainland from a perfect bird’s eye view.

    Known as the ‘Garden Island of Fiji’, Taveuni is Fiji’s third largest island. It is 62.4 kilometres long, 11.2 kilometres wide and 1,218 metres high and offers some amazing experiences like the Cascading waterfalls at Bouma and Levena, Scenic lookouts, Rainbow Reef corals with Great White Wall dive sites & Lagoons. In fact this is also famed for being the location of the second Blue Lagoon movie!

    There are blowholes and a fantastic natural rock waterslide - one will never cease to be amazed by the beauty of this island!

    Overnight: Taveuni Island Resort & Spa, Fiji or similar
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 5

    TAVEUNI

    Taveuni has the unique distinction of being the first place on this planet to experience the birth of the New Millennium. Start your day with a tour of the West Coast which begins with a visit to the dateline (180° meridian) - where you can stand in both today, and yesterday! Take a four wheel drive to Lake Tagimoucia which is an extinct volcanic crater at the highest point of Taveuni.

    This evening, you can choose to dine privately; enjoy a sumptuous dinner for two at the lagoon bure, under the stars by the pool, on the beach with a musician playing alongside, or in your own luxury bure.

    Overnight: Taveuni Island Resort & Spa, Fiji or similar
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 6

    TAVEUNI

    Explore the eastern side of the island today and visit the amazing Bouma National Heritage Park. Walk along to see three amazing waterfalls with lush vegetation and an unbelievably beautiful grotto. Take a swim in the huge natural rock pool: dive under, into and behind this large volume of cascading water. On your way up to the second waterfall, there is a spot which gives you a 360 degree view of the coast and the islands of Matagi, Qamea and Laucala, and up into the high peaks and ranges of Taveuni mountainous landscape creating a vista of lush tropical beauty.

    Enjoy a picnic lunch on the rock and make your way to the beautiful white sand beach of Lavena. You could hike through the stunning coastal scenery and enjoy the beauty of this wild foreshore. You come across a swinging bridge that crosses a deep river canyon. You could swim to a cool deep pool surrounded by high rock walls and lush rainforest. As you swim deeper into the grotto, two beautiful waterfalls cascade. You could climb up onto them and slide or jump into the deep water below.

    Overnight: Taveuni Island Resort & Spa, Fiji or similar
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 7

    TAVEUNI

    The day is yours to explore at your own pace! You could either choose to indulge in some more soft adventure activities or just relax to enjoy some of the resorts other facilities & features.

    Overnight: Taveuni Island Resort & Spa, Fiji or similar
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 8

    DEPART TAVEUNI

    Check out of the hotel and depart on a private charter for a 90 minute flight directly to Nadi Airport for your onward flight.

    Meal Plan: B
