Suggested Nha Trang Accommodation

  • Evason Nah Trang

    Ana Mandara, meaning "beautiful home for the guests" is reminiscent of an old Vietnamese village and is situated directly on the beach off the most famous thoroughfare, Tran Phu Boulevard.

    Furnished with native woods and rattan, the true image of Vietnam is reflected by warm hospitality, the rice culture, and unique tastes. Set on 26,000 square metres of private tropical gardens overlooking the sea, Ana Mandara Resort comprises 17 villas containing 74 well-appointed guest rooms, situated in either plush tropical gardens or with spectacular views of Nha Trang Bay.

    Six Senses Ninh Van Bay

    Feel luxuriously at one with nature in this stunning setting overlooking the South China Sea, with its inspiring rock formations, powder-white sand and towering mountains. Enjoy exclusivity and an architectural style that reflects the traditions of Vietnam in this prestigious, award-winning resort.

    Pool Villas - in which private swimming pools have been crafted - integrate perfectly with the environment and are situated on the beachfront, over rock formations and on the hillside; each provides generous personal space and an uncompromised standard of luxury.

    Don't miss the six senses spa where the rhythm of a gentle waterfall and aroma of essential oils will soothe and invigorate you.
