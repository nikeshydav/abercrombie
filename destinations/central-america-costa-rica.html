Travel to Costa Rica


Costa Rica, which means 'Rich Coast' in Spanish, extends from the tranquil tepid waters of the Caribbean to the vigorous enormous waves of the Pacific. The locals known as Ticos take pride in their land and bask in the glory of this nation having over 100 years of democracy. This land of peace boasts of a commendable political stability without any state army and has one of the most enlightened conservation policy in Central America aimed at preserving its natural treasures, from its ancient volcanoes, to rainforests, or marine and wild animals.

With Abercrombie & Kent holidays in Costa Rica, one is bound to enjoy the enriching and inspiring facets of this amazing land. Nature lovers can take delight in the stunning beauty that comes in many contrasting forms and shapes, be its active volcanoes, unspoiled beaches, or lush dense rainforest. Also its rich bio diversity has allowed for upto 91,000 species of flora and fauna to flourish within its compact land area. A naturalist's heaven, Costa Rica has 20 natural parks, 8 biological reserves, and a number of protected areas. Tortuguero National Park is particularly famous of the green turtles nestling every year, along with the rare long-tailed Quetza.

Adventure tourists also have activities galore to make most of Costa Rica's natural treasures. Explore the islands and forests of Osa Peninsula brimming with wild life or snorkel, scuba dive, Kayak or raft in its diverse water bodies or devour the sparkling fireworks of the Arenal volcano that looms large and ominous around its emerald green base. A luxurious and comprehensive travel to Costa Rica, the home of natural paradise is more than just a perfect getaway, rather a life altering experience.
