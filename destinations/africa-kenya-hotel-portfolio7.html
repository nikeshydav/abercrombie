Suggested Nairobi Accommodation

  • Giraffe Manor

    Dating back to the 1930's Giraffe Manor has become of Nairobi's most historical and iconic buildings in Nairobi. Although Giraffe Manor is reminiscent of the early days of Europeans in East Africa, very much present is the herd of Rothschild giraffe who spend their mornings and late afternoons up at the Manor greeting guests, while the rest of their day is spent roaming around their 140 acre sanctuary.

    Giraffe Manor has just six individually styled six bedrooms, one of which is a family suite. The interior of Giraffe Manor is stylish, yet still keeping the heritage of a bygone area. An original Art Deco bathroom within the manor is a feature of the house that never fails to amaze.

    Manor offers you, their guest a truly unique and wondering experience in the suburbs of Nairobi within easy reach of some of Nairobi attractions including the Daphne Sheldrick Elephant Orphanage.

    House of Waine

    The House of Waine is a boutique property in the heart of the Karen region of Nairobi. With just 11 individually designed rooms, it is the perfect place to relax before or after your safari.

    All rooms at the House of Waine have televisions with local and satellite channels, direct dial telephones and a mini bar.

    The house is built within 2 1/2 acres of lush gardens, providing a peaceful retreat from the bustling streets of Nairobi. Meals are served in various indoor and outdoor locations around the house with delicious international cuisine and a special traditional Kenyan meal prepared for those who wish to be a bit more adventurous. The House of Waine is within easy reach of Nairobi's attractions including the Daphne Sheldrick Elephant Orphanage.

    Ngong House

    The luxurious private Ngong House is located in the Langata in Nairobi, overlooking the Ngong Hills. Main accommodation includes 6 Luxury Tree Houses, but also a Private Cottage, Log Cabin and Suite in the main house, all being very comfortable, quiet and unique.

    Delicious dining is served in front of a grand fireplace, whilst breakfast and lunch is served outside under a canopy. A short distance from Ngong house is Nairobi National Park, an Elephant and Rhino Orphanage and a Giraffe Centre where you can spend time feeding the Giraffes.

    • Helpful, friendly efficient staff to welcome you and enjoy your stay
    • Only 15 minutes from Wilson Airport and 20 minutes from the city centre
    • Overlooks the stunning Ngong Hills

    IN THE KNOW
    Ngong House is only a few minutes' drive from Karen Blixen's coffee farm which was made famous in the book and film 'Out of Africa'.

    The Norfolk Hotel

    For more than 100 years The Norfolk Hotel in central Nairobi has played a part in Kenya's history. The hotel was traditionally the starting point of many of the early safaris around Kenya and retains this capacity today.

    Guest rooms and suites are luxuriously furnished and well equipped with direct dial telephone, satellite television, electrical mosquito repellent and air conditioning. An outdoor heated swimming pool is surrounded by lush gardens - the perfect escape.

    Norfolk Hotel has six restaurants serving cuisine to suit every mood and palate. The Tea Room offers traditional high tea in a comfortable setting, the Tatu Restaurant is an contemporary American styled steak house serving the finest of Kenyan Angus Beef. And a visit to The Norfolk is not complete without a drink at the Lord Delamere Bar. This has been a popular meeting place in Nairobi since it opened its doors in 1904.
