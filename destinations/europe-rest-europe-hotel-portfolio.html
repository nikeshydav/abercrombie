  • Schlosshotel Kronberg

    The Schlosshotel Kronberg, opened in 1954, is the only privately owned 5 star hotel in the region and member of "Small Luxury Hotels of the World". The castle was built 1889 by Empress Victoria and is now one of the most impressive remaining castles in Germany. Surrounded by a gorgeous parkway with an ambitious 18-hole golf course, the Schlosshotel offers living history to touch. An exclusive library, antiques and paintings formerly owned by Empress Victoria still review the authentic atmosphere. To our guests we offer complimentary LAN and in Taunus-LAN Internet connection, minibar, parking as well as transportation and entry to a fitnessclub and swimming pool nearby.

    Enjoy the excellent kitchen at the restaurant and its charming terrace with an imposing view to the golf course. We serve you a champagne breakfast, tasteful a la carte dishes and menus also an extensive wine list. On weekends we celebrate our traditional high tea accompanied by piano music in the imperial library and a historical tour through the castle. The Victoria Lounge is the new highlight at the Schlosshotel Kronberg. We offer you cherished drinks, tasty cocktails and small viands from the kitchen on the exquisite terrace location beneath a precious baldachin. Take a seat on the noble padded lounge-furniture and enjoy the grand view to the spacious garden. The Grand Hotel is situated at the resort Kronberg in Taunus. In few minutes by car you reach Frankfurt, Mainz and Wiesbaden. Frankfurt airport is 22 km away and to get to the fair ground you only need 18 minutes by train without change.

    Hotel de Rome, Berlin, Germany

    Hotel de Rome is located on Bebelplatz. It is one of the few luxury hotels in Berlin which is located in an original building using all of its architectural beauty. The original classic design of the building has been given a contemporary twist by designer Tommaso Ziffer.

    The hotel has 146 stylish rooms in total which all have a mixture of 'Berlin Style' glass and steel, individually controlled air conditioning, direct dial telephones, internet and fax connections, electronic safe, minibar and adjustable bathroom sound entertainment system. All bathrooms have separate walk in showers and mosaic marble detailing above double ended baths. The restaurant opens onto a large terrace in the summer months and boasts delicious, Mediterranean cuisine. The Bebel Bar serves fresh and delicious drinks and the Opera Court is the focal point for coffee and afternoon tea.

    Hotel Adlon Kempinski, Berlin, Germany

    Located on the historic Pariser Platz by Berlin's most famous sight, the Brandenburg Gate, and near to the elegant shops & boutiques of Friedrichstrasse and Museum Island, the recently redeveloped Hotel Adlon is once again the Grande Dame of Berlin hotels.

    The bustling lobby and public areas are elegantly decorated in a style reminiscent of the 1920s heyday of the hotel, and variety of restaurants offers a wide range of cuisine. The accommodations are furnished in the same elegant art deco manner, with many rooms enjoying outward-facing views. There is also a health club with indoor swimming pool.

    Hotel Torbräu, Munich, Germany

    Welcome to Munich's Oldest Hotel. The Torbräu with its unmistakable Munich charm has been offering a warm welcome to guests since 1490. Today, after more than a century under the continuous management of the Kirchlechner Family, the Torbräu not only represents a lovingly refurbished establishment with 90 tastefully appointed rooms and suites, but also stands for individual character and first-class service with a personal touch.

    The Kirchlechners follow one motto: "Our guests have always been more than a room number to us. We would like them to really feel at home at the Torbräu." With its perfect downtown location, the historic Torbräu is an excellent starting point for exploring Munich.
