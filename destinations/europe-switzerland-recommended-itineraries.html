SWITZERLAND AND NORTHERN ITALY

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 10 days

    A 10-day FIT itinerary showcasing the beautiful highlights of Switzerland and Northern Italy.

    Highlights: Geneva, Lausanne, Zermatt, Chillon, Lugano, Lake Maggiore, St. Moritz, Lake Como, Interlaken, Bern, Lucerne.

  • Day 1

    Arrive GENEVA

    Upon arrival at Geneva Airport you are met by your local representative and accompanied to your hotel in Lausanne, where you are assisted with check-in.

    Overnight: Beau Rivage Palace

  • Day 2

    LAUSANNE

    TToday you will enjoy a three hour city tour by private vehicle with your English-speaking guide. Your tour will include the Old Town. The Gothic Cathedral of Notre Dame, an architectural treasure, is Switzerland's largest church and probably its finest. The Hotel de Ville is the seat of municipal and communal councils, and the painted medieval Fontaine de la Justice draws people to lounge on its heavy rim.

    Overnight: Beau Rivage Palace
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    LAUSANNE - CHILLON - ZERMATT

    This morning, you will meet your driver-guide and transfer by private vehicle to Taesch. En route you will stop in Montreux for a guided tour of Chateau de Chillon, a 12th century castle that rises out of the water at Veytaux. The chateaux was once used as a prison and was the inspiration for Lord Byron's famous poem "The Prisoner of Chillon". Upon arrival in Taesch you will be met by your local representative and escorted by taxi and electric car to your hotel in Zermatt.

    Overnight: Grand Hotel Zermatterhof
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    ZERMATT

    Today enjoy a half day excursion with your English speaking guide up to Gornergrat (subject to weather conditions). The Matterhorn dominates the skyline, creating alpine vistas that are breathtaking. Have a quick stop before returning down to Zermatt and your hotel.

    Overnight: Grand Hotel Zermatterhof
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 5

    ZERMATT - LAKE MAGGIORE - LUGANO

    Leave Zermatt this morning with your driver and make your way across the border of Switzerland to Italy. First stop is in Stresa where you will enjoy a four hour private boat cruise during which you will visit the Borromean islands of Isola Bella and Isola Madre. Isola Bella is home to the 17th century Palazzo Borromeo and its splendid landscaped gardens, whilst much of Isola Madre is given over to a botanical garden. After the lake cruise continue onto your hotel located in Lugano.

    Overnight: Hotel Splendide Royale
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 6

    LUGANO - LAKE COMO - ST. MORITZ

    Today make your way back into Switzerland as you travel along the old roman road, the Via Regina. Admire the beautiful scenery, with roads lined by the clear lakes and majestic mountains of the Lake Como region. You will overnight in the luxury resort town of St. Moritz.

    Overnight: Badrutt's Palace Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 7

    ST. MORITZ - INTERLAKEN

    Board the Glacier Express in St. Moritz for the scenic train journey to Andermatt. First class seating will be reserved in the panoramic carriage. Sit back and relax as you take in the breathtaking mountain landscape of the Swiss Alps. You will be met in Andermatt and transferred by private vehicle to your hotel in Interlaken.

    Overnight: Victoria Jungfrau Grand Hotel & Spa
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 8

    JUNGFRAUJOCH

    Meet your English-speaking guide at the hotel lobby for a full day excursion to Jungfraujoch. This excursion takes you to the very heart of the Bernese Alps - from the green valleys to the glistening, eternal snow fields and glaciers. You will travel by private car from Interlaken to Lauterbrunnen and board the train up to Jungfraujoch, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known as “the roof of Europe” at over 11,000 feet.

    The railways of the Jungfrau region are under unified management and comprise an electrified, highly efficient transport system. For those technically interested: there are different gauges with gradients up to 25%. The highlight, of course, is the spectacular ride through the tunnel (4.4 miles long) cut into the rocks of the Eiger. The travelling time from Lauterbrunnen to the Jungfraujoch is approximately two hours each way. At Jungfraujoch, the highest railway station in Europe, you can visit the Ice Palace, which was hewn into the glacier in 1934, and the Sphinx Terraces - reached by lift affording breathtaking views of the Aletsch Glacier, the largest river of ice in Europe. Return to your hotel after the tour, first by train back to Grindelwald, then by private transfer to Interlaken.

    Overnight: Victoria Jungfrau Grand Hotel & Spa
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 9

    INTERLAKEN - LUCERNE

    Meet your driver-guide this morning for the direct transfer to Lucerne, where you check-in to your lakeside hotel. This afternoon soak up the atmosphere of Lucerne during a walking tour with your local guide. Along the way, visit the Altes Rathaus (Old Town Hall), the Hofkirche which was first part of a monastery founded in 750, the Lion Monument and the celebrated Glacier Gardens. Afterwards admire the views from the water with a relaxing public boat cruise on the lake. (5 hours, walking guide and public cruise)

    Overnight: Palace Hotel Lucerne
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 10

    DEPART ZURICH

    Private transfer with English-speaking driver to Zurich Airport for your return flight home.

    Meal Plan: B

TAILOR MADE SWITZERLAND

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 11 days

    This itinerary is ideal for first time visitors to the Alps and Switzerland, and those who enjoy picturesque views.

    Highlights: Geneva, Gothic Cathedral of Notre Dame in Lausanne, Gruyères Cheese Factory, Gruyères Castle, Montreux, Chateau de Chillon in Veytaux, Zermatt, Matterhorn, Lucerne.

  • Day 1

    DEPART USA

    Overnight flight

  • Day 2

    ARRIVE GENEVA/LAUSANNE

    On arrival at Geneva airport you will be met by your English-speaking guide for a short sightseeing tour of Geneva in your private vehicle. Nestled between the Jura and the Alps, on the southwestern tip of Lac Léman (Lake Geneva), Geneva was the home of the League of Nations. The Left Bank bristles with prestigious shops, monuments, parks and fountains. In the Old Town, visit the Church of St Peters and relax a little in one of the many sidewalk cafés before transferring to the lakeside town of Lausanne.

    Here your Guardian Angel will assist with check-in and run through the rest of your itinerary.

    Overnight: Beau-Rivage Palace – Deluxe Double/Twin with lake view
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    LAUSANNE

    This morning your driver and guide will take you up to the Old Town of Lausanne. The Gothic Cathedral of Notre Dame, an architectural treasure, is Switzerland's largest and finest church. The Hôtel de Ville is the seat of municipal and communal councils, and the painted medieval Fontaine de la Justice draws people to lounge on its heavy rim. This afternoon is at leisure for you to enjoy the relaxing facilities of the hotel, or pop along to the Olympic Museum, next door to the hotel. The more adventurous may like to take the ferry across the lake to the French town of Evian, famous for its water.

    Overnight: Beau-Rivage Palace – Deluxe Double/Twin with lake view
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    CHILLON & GRUYERES

    Today, enjoy a full day excursion to Montreux and Gruyères with your private vehicle and English-speaking guide. Tour through some of the picturesque cheese-making villages, and visit the Gruyères Cheese Factory and Gruyères Castle. Stop in Montreux for a guided tour of Château de Chillon, a 12th century castle that rises out of the water at Veytaux that was once used as a prison. This inspired Lord Byron to write his famous poem ‘The Prisoner of Chillon’.

    Overnight: Beau-Rivage Palace – Deluxe Double/Twin with lake view
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 5

    LAUSANNE –ZERMATT

    Today you will be met at your hotel and privately transferred with your Guardian Angel to Lausanne railway station. Here you will be shown to your pre-reserved 1st class seating and your luggage will be handled onboard for you.

    In Visp our Zermatt Guardian Angel and driver will wait for you and transfer you the rest of the way to Zermatt by private vehicle.



    CONCIERGE RAIL SERVICE

    Travel by modern express rail in Europe is an excellent way of traveling between destinations in comfort, style and at speed. The benefits of rail travel in Europe are numerous, and in many instances, rail travel can be quicker than air travel so no waiting in airport departure lounges or lost luggage!

    1st Class rail travel is also a very cost effective and comfortable method of covering mid-distance travel when compared to air travel or road transfers.

    Our unique Concierge Rail means that you will be accompanied to your reserved 1st class seating on departure and met on the platform at the arrival terminal, whilst your luggage will be handled for you at both ends of the journey. For your journey, you will be provided with a journey bag containing an English language newspaper, bottled water and some snacks.

    Overnight: Grand Hotel Zermatterhof – Deluxe Double/Twin
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 6

    ZERMATT & GORNERGRAT

    Enjoy a 4-hour sightseeing tour with your local English-speaking guide. Travel up the Gornergrat by the highest open-air cogwheel rail system in Europe. From the summit station there are majestic views of the Matterhorn, Monte Rosa and Corner Glacier. The remainder of today is at leisure.

    Overnight: Grand Hotel Zermatterhof – Deluxe Double/Twin
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 7

    ZERMATT

    Today is at leisure to enjoy the Alpine scenery and hospitality. You may want to take the cable car up to the Little Matterhorn, stroll through the Alpine meadows or relax and enjoy the facilities of your hotel which has an amazing spa with a large pool, steam room, sauna, Alpine and Ice grotto, fitness area and “Vita Borni” treatments (please pre-book).

    Overnight: Grand Hotel Zermatterhof – Deluxe Double/Twin
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 8

    ZERMATT – GLACIER EXPRESS – LUCERNE

    This morning you will be met by your Guardian Angel and privately transferred to Zermatt station. Here you will board the Glacier Express train bound for Andermatt. Enjoy the breathtaking scenery from the comfort of your reserved first class seats. Upon arrival at Andermatt train station, you will be met by your driver and taken by private vehicle to your hotel in Lucerne.

    Overnight: Palace Luzern – Deluxe Double/Twin with lake view
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 9

    LUCERNE

    After breakfast, you will take a walking tour of Lucerne with your local English-speaking guide. You will visit the Altes Rathaus (Old Town Hall), the Hofkirche that was first part of a monastery founded in 750, and the celebrated Glacier Gardens. The afternoon is at leisure, but on a nice day we suggest enjoying a relaxing public boat cruise on the lake.

    Overnight: Palace Luzern – Deluxe Double/Twin with lake view
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 10

    MOUNT PILATUS

    Meet your guide and driver this morning for your trip up Mount Pilatus. Your driver takes you to the station in Alpnachstad from where you and the guide take the world’s steepest cogwheel railway to Pilatus Kulm. During your journey, see lush meadows with Alpine flowers, mountain streams and fascinating rock faces. Your guide will point out anything of interest. Make your way back down the mountain by the aerial gondola. Those interested may like to visit the exhibition at Fräkmüntegg Station that tells the story of the gondola from concept through to completion. The remainder of today is at leisure for you to enjoy the lovely town of Lucerne, perhaps visit a museum or just sit along the promenade and watch the world go by.

    Overnight: Palace Luzern – Deluxe Double/Twin with lake view
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 11

    DEPART

    You will have a private car transfer to Zurich airport.

TUSCANY FINE WINE AND DINING

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 4 days

    A romantic 4 day break to the stunning Italian region of Tuscany. Relax and enjoy the luxurious spa facilities and indulge in a wine and dine experience.

    SPECIAL OFFER - Stay for an additional night at no extra charge throughout 2013.

    Highlights: Florence, Chianti, Tavarnelle, Passignano

  • Day 1

    ARRIVE TUSCANY

    You private chauffeur will drive you from Florence Airport to your hotel in the heart of the stunning Tuscan wine producing region of Chianti.

    The ideal choice for both relaxation and exploration, the luxury sanctuary Castello Del Nero is located within striking distance of Florence and Siena. The carefully restored 12th century castle offers the absolute latest in modern facilities whilst maintaining the atmosphere of its historic surroundings.

    Overnight: Castello del Nero

  • Day 2

    TUSCANY

    Today is at your leisure to enjoy the facilities of your hotel and the archetypal Tuscan village of Tavarnelle, just a short walk away. Your hotel is located in acres of secluded grounds and boasts a large outdoor pool, extensive ESPA signature treatments, thermal suites and a fitness centre as well as two floodlit tennis courts and a heated swimming pool. Other activities include trekking, hot air ballooning and golf.

    Overnight: Castello del Nero
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    TUSCANY - WINE AND DINE EXPERIENCE

    Your chauffeur will bring you into the heart of Chianti today and the fairy-tale location of Passignano. The finest of the renowned noble wines of the Antinori family are stored away in ancient cellars underneath the badia (monastery) which is entirely closed to the public - except for you!

    Enjoy an exclusive tour of the cellars followed by Michelin starred lunchtime dining with delicious accompanying fine wines from the Antinori cellar. The sommelier will be happy to share his in-depth knowledge with you. Fully satiated, your chauffeur will bring you back to the paradise of your hotel.

    Overnight: Castello del Nero
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    DEPART TUSCANY

    Your chauffeur will bring you back in style to Florence Airport today.

    Meal Plan: B

CROATIA BEACH

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 4 Days

    A luxurious and relaxing 4 day visit to the stunning Croatian coastal town of Split.

    Highlights: Explore the Ancient city of Split and enjoy plenty of time at leisure to make the most of your wonderful hotel facilities.

  • Day 1

    ARRIVE IN SPLIT

    Meet your Chauffeur at Split airport and transfer to your hotel which overlooks the bay of Split, the Adriatic sea and the iconic island of Brac.

    Le Meridien Lav, Split is located at Podstrana, five miles south of Split. The beautiful setting features 800 metres of beach frontage, gardens landscaped by award-winning designer Jim Nicolay of Hawaii, and fantastic sea views overlooking Split and the surrounding islands.

    Overnight: Le Meridien Lav

  • Day 2

    SPILT

    Today is at your leisure to enjoy the facilities of your beachfront hotel. The hotel facilities include an outdoor and an indoor pool, tennis courts and a Water Sports Centre which offers scuba diving, waterskiing, sailing, and more. The exclusive spa and wellness centre is a magnificent space offering treatment rooms, relaxation areas and a fitness centre, the perfect place to unwind and feel pampered.

    Overnight: Le Meridien Lav
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    SPLIT

    Today you may wish to explore the ancient city of Split, including the palace which was the 'retirement home' of the roman emperor Hadrian. Alternatively why not enjoy a romantic dinner of fresh oysters overlooking the marina.

    Overnight: Le Meridien Lav
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    SPLIT

    Your chauffeur will bring you back in style to Split Airport today.

    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 5

    Urubamba/Machu Picchu

    Board the deluxe Hiram Bingham train, the most luxurious way to travel the breathtaking landscape to Machu Picchu. Step on board, and you are immersed in an elegant world of polished wood, gleaming cutlery and glittering glass that evokes a romantic ambience. After a light lunch on board, arrive at the breathtaking Incan citadel and begin exploring the ruins in the company of your expert guide. Feel the pulse of this magical place as you visualize the lives of the priests, craftsmen, and servants who inhabited this seemingly uninhabitable citadel and then left, leaving no written records about its destruction. After a busy day of sightseeing, return to your beautiful mountainside retreat, high in the lush Andean clouds forest. It features the look and feel of a rural Andean village set in spectacular grounds with paths that wind past waterfalls and gardens, and trails that wander through wild forest. Snuggle under a thick alpaca blanket in your suite, which offers incredible views of the exotic gardens or the dramatic Vilcanota River.

    Overnight: Inkaterra Machu Picchu Pueblo Hotel
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 6

    Machu Picchu/Cusco

    Return to Machu Picchu with your guide this morning for a final look at the ruins - and for an even more meaningful event for you. A shaman joins you to perform a vow renewal ceremony for you. Stand before the spirits of the Incas and reaffirm your love in this magical place. Afterward, return to the hotel, renewed and refreshed, for lunch. This afternoon, return to Cusco by Vista dome train and settle in at your hotel, where you receive VIP amenities.

    Overnight: Hotel Monasterio
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 7

    Cusco

    A morning city tour takes you to the Cathedral on the Plaza de Armas and the Santo Domingo Temple, a Dominican church and convent built on the foundations of the Koricancha (place of gold), where the Incas worshipped the sun. Continue just outside Cusco to the ruins of Sacsayhuaman, a testimonial to the Incan architectural skills. Try to slip a piece of paper between the cracks of the massive stone blocks, cut and fitted together with incredible precision without a single drop of mortar to hold them in place. Your afternoon is at leisure. Make sure to visit the hotel spa for a relaxing couple's massage. This evening, enjoy a romantic dinner at Limo restaurant, overlooking the main plaza.

    Overnight: Hotel Monasterio
    Meal Plan: B,L

  • Day 8

    Cusco/Lima

    Fly to Lima and transfer to your hotel. The remainder of the day is at leisure to do some last-minute shopping, take a walk along the ocean or enjoy the views from your suite.

    Overnight: Miraflores Park Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 9

    Lima/India

    Transfer to the airport &Board your international departure flight.

    Overnight: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
    Meal Plan: B,L,D
