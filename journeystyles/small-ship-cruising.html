An adventure cruise immerses you into the exotic and the unspoiled. At Abercrombie and Kent, each trip is a fresh, spontaneous, unscripted journey and an intimate experience. Our range of small ship cruises offer the highest standard of accommodation, guiding, dining, exceptional service and fabulous on-board camaraderie. Whether barging in France, cruising the Nile or onboard ‘Road to Mandalay’ in Myanmar, A&K’s world on the water is a truly world class experience to cherish.

Adventure Cruising

Fresh, exotic, unspoiled. These probably describe an adventure cruise best. Available in all shapes and sizes, from intimate, small boat cruises with small crew-to-passenger ratio to larger cruise ships equipped with all amenities, these can vary from basic to luxury and short cruises to longer ones. Each trip is a fresh, spontaneous and unscripted journey that is fundamentally different from a traditional cruise itinerary in all ways but one – comfort and service.

Whatever be your choice, with A&K, you are sure to have an experience that will be long etched in your memory.

Canal & River Cruising

Cruising along rivers and canals is the most relaxing way to see a country in absolute comfort while enjoying the constantly altering scenery. Abercrombie & Kent offers river cruises on hotel vessels on escorted itineraries throughout France, Holland and Belgium, Germany, Britain and Ireland that includes the most popular excursions and sightseeing these places have to offer.

Barge cruises hold great value in today’s European travel market, taking you beyond the city and introducing you to each region in a relaxed and novel way. Expect to be pampered as you glide through ancient villages with guided excursions to lush vineyards, majestic chateaux and colorful local markets. Also, a canal or luxury river cruise will not pinch your pockets as pricing includes everything you need to make your holiday complete, right from accommodations to three mouth watering daily meals with wine, as well as guided sightseeing and entry fees. Transfers and bar drinks are included on most vessels too.

A&K offers special theme cruises as per your interests. Wine lovers might want to take the Wines of Burgundy (Grand Cru) Cruise aboard 'Amaryllis' and sample the 33 Grand Crus of Burgundy. If gardens is what you fancy, a luxury river cruise on 'Magna Carta' in England, passing through lush green countryside and quaint towns along the Thames sounds perfect.

Whatever be your fanciful ventures, discover the best of travel only with A&K.
