ROMANTIC MOROCCO: THE LAND OF THE KASBAHS

Programme Overview

Casablanca, Fès, Erg Chebbi Dunes, Ait Benahddou and Essaouira

  • Duration: 8 Days/ 7 Nights

    Explore Morocco in a romantic and luxury journey!

    Highlights: Dinner at Rick's Café, Casablanca Horse riding on the beach along with a picnic lunch Kasbah style dinner at a private beach on La Corniche Reservations service at special restaurants Visit to an isolated marabout’s tomb at the edge of the ocean and the salt flats Horse-drawn carriage ride while visiting the garden Reservations for a 1001 night dinner show Night on the Sahara Desert at the Erg Chebbi Dunes Special dinners/lunches organized History and culture discovery of the Portuguese city of Essaouira

    A&K Advantage:

    Breeze through customs and immigration formalities upon arrival with special VIP assistance Gain an insider's perspective on Andalusian garden owner’s private secret grounds and discover his studio where you can design your very own perfume
    A private visit to the Photography House along with special wine & dinner
    Explore beautiful Kasbahs and a private lunch near Ait Benahddou, a part of the World Heritage List of UNESCO Trip to Ounagha, where you will enjoy Olive Oil & Argan oil
    Wine tasting at ‘Val d’Argan’ French owned winery

    Private arrival and departure airport transfers The services of an Abercrombie & Kent Representative

    * On arrival in each country, you are welcomed by your A&K Representative who conducts a briefing on your time in the country. An A&K Representative is always at hand to provide unparalleled 24/7 support, be it local travel tips or reconfirming arrangement details, to providing recommendations for restaurants and shopping. Should you ever need help or advice, dial your A&K Representative’s mobile phone for prompt and courteous service at any time.


    • Detailed itinerary

  • Day 1

    CASABLANCA–Sightseeing & Dinner at Rick's Café

    On Arrival you receive VIP airport assistance and are quickly whisked through customs and immigration. You will be met by your A&K Representative and transferred to your hotel by a private vehicle where you will be assisted with check-in. Originally settled by Berbers in the seventh century; Casablanca today is a bustling, French-influenced center.

    This afternoon, join your private guide for a tour of this port city, stopping at the formidable Hassan II Grand Mosque. Its minaret is the tallest religious structure in the world, and part of the mosque's floor is glass, enabling worshippers to kneel directly over the sea.

    Enjoy dinner at Rick's Café, housed in a 1930's mansion. It is the mythical saloon from the 1942 film "Casablanca".

    On return to the hotel room you will be welcomed with candles and roses petals a perfect setup for a romantic ambience.

    Overnight: Le Doge
    Meal Plan: D

  • Day 2

    CASABLANCA–Horse riding on the beach, private picnic lunch and Kasbah style private dinner setting

    Your A&K Representative will accompany you in your private vehicle to Club Hippique le Barry for Horse Riding on the beach. A private romantic lunch picnic will be organized for you to enjoy the ambience.

    Stroll hand in hand on the beach and spend the day at leisure.

    Enjoy a romantic private dinner organized specially for you in a Kasbah style at a private beach on La Corniche.

    Overnight: Le Doge
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 3

    CASABLANCA–FES–ERG CHEBBI DUNES–Lunch at La Maison Bleue & night at a Moroccan caidal styled tent

    Indulge in a complimentary rose petal bath and breakfast in bed.

    Your private vehicle will take you for a lazy lunch to La Maison Bleue, Fès. Built in 1915, this exquisite residence still bears the mark of its first and eminent owner, Mohammed El Abbadi, an astrologer, man of law and professor at the University of Qaraouiyine. His library remains and bears witness to the culture and knowledge that prevailed in this dwelling. The dishes are prepared according to the purest traditions. The Moroccan traditional cuisine is subtle and elegant, and is a worthy match for this privileged venue.

    A private charter flight will transport you to Er-Rachidia. Hop into your private four-wheel-drive vehicle for a drive across the stunning Erg Chebbi Dunes to your exclusive A&K Desert Tented Camp in a remote area of the Sahara Desert. Enter the wilderness of the desert, then what lay in front of you is your private camp settled exclusively for you. You are welcomed with mint tea and pastries and led to your private Moroccan caidal-styled tent that combines elements of Berber and Bedouin styles. Decorated with colorful carpets, it comfortably sleeps two, and has its own private bathroom with a shower and toilet next door. Climb the highest dune, camel ride like Laurence of Arabia and watch the colors of a Sahara sunset.

    Enjoy a wonderful Bedouin dinner around the fire and overnight in a luxurious tent.

    Overnight: Desert Tented Camp
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 4

    ERG CHEBBI DUNES–MARRAKECH–Explore Andalusia Garden, insider access to the owner’s private grounds where you can design your own perfume

    Your private charter will bring you back to civilization at Marrakech.

    Enjoy a horse-drawn carriage ride to the Andalusia gardens. Discover the amazing gallery exposing a collection of more than fifty Moroccan artists. Behind the gardens push a small door, and reveal the owners private secret grounds. Here in his studio discover musk, lavender and jasmine to design your very own perfume under, the direction of Mr. Benchabane the artist who created the famous “Soire de Marrakech” perfume.

    Later in the evening, a private visit to the Photography House opened just for you, animated by the curator of this fabulous place. A romantic dinner & wine on the terrace will be organized with animated by lights.

    Overnight: Taj Palace
    Meal Plan: B,D

  • Day 5

    MARRAKECH–AIT BENAHDDOU–MARRAKECH–Sightseeing and private lunch

    Explore beautiful Kasbahs and a delightful private lunch near Ait Benahddou. You will travel by a private helicopter from Marrakech. Fly above the Atlas Mountains to the South of Morocco. Discover the village of Ait Benahddou, one of the jewels of Morocco, which is part of the World Heritage List of UNESCO, and where many movies have been filmed.

    Enjoy a private lunch in this amazing setting with a wonderful view. Return back to Marrakech in the late afternoon.

    On return to the hotel you will be welcomed with champagne, fruits to a candlelit room for a romantic ambience.

    Overnight: Taj Palace
    Meal Plan: B,L

  • Day 6

    MARRAKECH–OUALIDIA–MARRAKECH–Sightseeing & 1001 night dinner show

    Your private vehicle will transport you to Oualidia for an Atlantic Ocean escape, a boat trip along the lagoon. Cruise through a series of narrow channels to an isolated marabout’s tomb at the edge of the ocean then your boatman will take you further ahead to see the salt flats.

    Enjoy a romantic lunch picnic on the beach.

    Reservations for a 1001 night dinner show with a romantic setting will be in order. Comfortably seated on rich cushions under one of the various "Caïdales" tents you will taste the dishes that made Moroccan cuisine world famous. During this delicious meal enjoy folkloric troops, musicians, and acrobats from all over Morocco. They will entertain, astonish, captivate and surprise you. A calligrapher will write your names in Arabic to offer you a most beautiful souvenir.

    Overnight: Taj Palace
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 7

    MARRAKECH–ESSAOUIRA–MARRAKECH–Olive oil, Argan oil & wine tasting at “Val d’Argan” and explore Essaouira

    You will be driven down to Ounagha in a private vehicle where you will enjoy Olive Oil & Argan oil and also wine tasting at ‘Val d’Argan’ French owned winery.

    Later discover history and culture in the Portuguese city of Essaouira. A delightful sea food lunch served in a neighboring restaurant. And what better way to finish this incredible day than with a camel ride on the beach or a kite surf lesson.

    Return to Marrakech by the evening.

    Overnight: Taj Palace
    Meal Plan: B,L

  • Day 8

    DEPART MARRAKECH

    You will be transported to the airport by your private vehicle; our A&K representative will assist you during the check-out formalities.

    Clients should be at the airport 2h00 before take-off time.

    Meal Plan: B

LUXURY HONEYMOON IN MOROCCO RED GREEN & BLUE

Programme Overview

Marrakech, Atlas Mountain, Oualidia, Ounagha and Essaouira

  • Duration: 8 Days/7 Nights

    Explore Central–West Morocco in a romantic and luxurious journey.

    Highlights: Reservations service at special restaurants
    Horse–drawn carriage ride while visiting the gardens
    Reservations for a 1001 night dinner show
    Complimentary Bubble bath with rose petals, and candle at Oualidia

    A&K Advantage : Breeze through customs and immigration formalities upon arrival with special VIP assistance
    Gain an insider's perspective on Andalusian architecture during an informative discussion on the majestic, intricate details that are the hallmark of the style and design your very own perfume
    A private visit to the Photography House along with special wine & dinner
    Visit to a remote Berber village along with a stay at Kasbah Tamadot
    Trip to Ounagha, where you will enjoy Olive Oil & Argan oil
    Wine tasting at ‘Val d’Argan’ French owned winery
    History and culture discovery of the Portuguese city of Essaouira

    Private arrival and departure airport transfers
    The services of an Abercrombie & Kent Representative

    * On arrival in each country, you are welcomed by your A&K Representative who conducts a briefing on your time in the country. An A&K Representative is always at hand to provide unparalleled 24/7 support, be it local travel tips or reconfirming arrangement details, to providing recommendations for restaurants and shopping. Should you ever need help or advice, dial your A&K Representative’s mobile phone for prompt and courteous service at any time.

    • Detailed itinerary

  • Day 1

    MARRAKECH–1001 night dinner show

    MARHABA! The traditional greeting used in Morocco which expresses the meaning of a warm Hello or Welcome. On Arrival at Marrakech, you will be welcomed by your A&K Representative, who will provide VIP service for customs & immigration. You will then be privately transferred to your hotel and be assisted with check–in.

    Based on your time of arrival, you may freshen up and enjoy a little tour in horse carriage to have an overview of the Pearl of the South.

    Reservations for a 1001 night dinner show with a romantic setting will be in order. Comfortably seated on rich cushions under one of the various "Caïdales" tents you will taste the dishes that made Moroccan cuisine world famous. During this delicious meal enjoy folkloric troops, musicians, and acrobats from all over Morocco. They will entertain, astonish, captivate and surprise you. A calligrapher will write your names in Arabic to offer you a most beautiful souvenir.

    On return to the hotel room you will be welcomed with candles and roses petals for a romantic ambience.

    Overnight: Taj Palace
    Meal Plan: D

  • Day 2

    MARRAKECH–Shopping & dinner reservations at a traditional Moroccan restaurant

    Meet your English–speaking local guide today to discover the Mysteries of Marrakech. You will visit the Bahia Palace, a small piece of folly ordered in 1880 by the great vizir Sidi Moussa and the Ben Youssef Medersa, one of the jewels of Marrakech.

    Post Lunch, discover the intricate souks of the old town and be prepared for shopping! This maze of colourful alleys and small squares are home to a mystifying array of stalls and ateliers devoted to specific crafts. You also explore Djemaa El Fna, the city’s main square famous for its orange juice stalls. No one can really be certain how it came into being, but over the years the square has become the heartbeat of Marrakech, where fire–eaters, mime artists, snake–charmers and street musicians perform at every turn.

    Reservations for a romantic candlelit dinner in a traditional Moroccan restaurant will be in place for you.

    Overnight: Taj Palace
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    MARRAKECH–Horse drawn carriage ride to the gardens, design your own perfume & private visit to the Photography house

    Enjoy a horse–drawn carriage ride, you start with the Menara Gardens, where serenity and comparative coolness makes it one of the most pleasurable and romantic places for taking a stroll in Marrakech. Planted with olive–trees, some of which are more than 100 years old, the appeal of the Menara garden lies in its central part. Its large central lake was dug in the 12th century at the time of Almohade. On one side, there is a harmonious Saadian style pavilion that was built at the end of the 19th century.

    Continue to the Majorelle Gardens, created in the 1920's by the French painter Jacques Majorelle. Built around a small Art Deco workshop and painted a bright blue. Majorelle is a very unique and special blue and is now a trademark name. It also contains a memorial to the Algerian born French designer Yves Henri Donat Mathieu–Saint–Laurent, also known as Yves Saint Laurent. He often visited the gardens to find inspiration and refuge, following his requests he was cremated and had his ashes dispersed there.

    Move ahead to the Andalusia gardens. Discover the marvelous gallery exposing a collection of more than fifty Moroccan artists. Enter the beautifully designed enchanting Andalusia gardens. Behind the gardens push a small door and the owner’s private secret grounds are revealed. In his studio discover musk, lavender and jasmine to design your very own perfume, under the direction of Mr. Benchabane the artist who produced the famous “Soire de Marrakech” perfume.

    Later in the evening, a private visit to the Photography House opened just for you, animated by the curator of this fabulous place. A romantic dinner & wine on the terrace will be organised with animated by lights.

    Overnight: Taj Palace
    Meal Plan: B,D

  • Day 4

    MARRAKECH–ATLAS MOUNTAINS–Sightseeing

    Discover wonderful landscapes of the Atlas Mountains and experience Berber traditions. Your day begins by travelling to Asni (by 4x4 1h drive or helicopter 15 minute flight) admiring stunning views, continue by road to the village of Imlil on the foot hill of the Djebel Toubkal, the highest mountain in the Atlas. From Imlil you will take mules and walk or ride for about 15 minute to reach a remote village for an authentic Berber breakfast (olive oil, homemade bread, olives and mint tea) inside a Berber house with a fabulous view over the mountains.

    Walk or ride back to Imlil and drive down to Sir Richard Branson’s Kasbah Tamadot, for an exquisite lunch! The Kasbah is snuggled at the top of the hills with a panoramic view across the Atlas Mountains and Berber villages. Have time at leisure to relax on the terrace or by the pool, read a book in the gardens or just stroll through the property to discover its wonders and enjoying its serenity.

    Alternative choose a more exciting way to spend the afternoon such as hike, trek or a ride through the scenic mountains.

    Later in the evening a Candlelit dinner by your private Jacuzzi overlooking the amazing landscapes would have been organised.

    Overnight: Kasbah Tamadot
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 5

    ATLAS MOUNTAINS–OUALIDIA–Scenic drive

    Depart by private 4WD with your guide to enjoy the drive through the high Atlas Mountains as you reach the calming and enchanting Oualidia situated on the Atlantic Coast, well known for its famous oysters. Stop at panoramic sights to enjoy beautiful views.

    You will be welcomed at La Sultana in a classic Moroccan style, with mint tea and pastries. There will be a bottle of wine or champagne in your room to celebrate. While away a day with a drink in one hand and your partner in other, or watch the colourful fishing boats and flamingos at the water's edge together.

    Relax and unwind with a Hammam & angel’s skin just for you two (Relaxing massage '30min’ in duo with essential oils)

    Overnight: La Sultana Resorts
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 6

    OUALIDIA–Atlantic ocean escape

    Morning at leisure
    Later you will depart for your Atlantic Ocean escape, a boat trip along the lagoon. Cruise through a series of narrow channels to an isolated marabout’s tomb at the edge of the ocean then your boatman will take you further ahead to see the salt flats.

    Tonight you will be on a romantic cruise on the lagoon.

    Overnight: La Sultana Resorts
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 7

    OUALIDIA–OUNAGHA–ESSAOUIRA–Enjoy Olive Oil & Argan oil and also wine tasting at Val d’Argan

    Enjoy a complimentary Bubble bath with rose petals and candle. To make the day special you may enjoy your breakfast in bed or on the terraces.

    You will be driven down to Ounagha where you will enjoy Olive Oil & Argan oil and also wine tasting at ‘Val d’Argan’ French owned winery. Later discover history and culture in the Portuguese city of Essaouira.

    A Delightful sea food lunch served in a neighboring restaurant. And what better way to finish this incredible day than with a camel ride on the beach or a kite surf lesson. Return to Oualidia by the evening.

    Overnight: La Sultana Resorts
    Meal Plan: B, L

  • Day 8

    DEPART OUALIDIA

    You will be transferred in a private vehicle from Oualidia to Casablanca airport; our A&K representative will assist you during the check–out formalities.

    Clients should be at the airport 2h00 before take–off time.

    Meal Plan: B

TURKEY: TURKISH DELIGHT

Programme Overview

Istanbul, Izmir, Ephesus, Sirince and Cappadocia

  • Duration: 7 Days/6 Nights

    Share gorgeous sunsets, and celebrate your love on this romantic holiday.
    Discover Turkey's amazing history together, and interact with local people who share stories about their lives.

    Highlights:Reservations service at special restaurants
    Buyukada trip in a private boat
    Hands on Turkish cooking lesson with a local family
    Renewal of marriage vows in front of the Celsus Library
    Hot-air balloon ride over Cappadocia
    Dinner at Sur Balık Restaurant, Cappadocia
    A special massage in one of the hidden and mystic valleys of Cappadocia

    A&K Advantage:

    Private sunset Bosphorus cruise followed by drinks and dinner on cruise with dinner
    Picnic barbecue lunch in Cappadocia via horse cart

    Private arrival and departure airport transfers
    The services of an Abercrombie & Kent Representative

    * On arrival in each country, you are welcomed by your A&K Representative who conducts a briefing on your time in the country. An A&K Representative is always at hand to provide unparalleled 24/7 support, be it local travel tips or reconfirming arrangement details, to providing recommendations for restaurants and shopping. Should you ever need help or advice, dial your A&K Representative’s mobile phone for prompt and courteous service at any time.

    • Detailed itinerary

  • Day 1

    ISTANBUL–At leisure

    On Arrival at Istanbul, you will be met by your A&K Representative, who will provide VIP service for customs & immigration. You will then be privately transferred to your hotel and be assisted with check-in. En route to your hotel drive by the periphery of the world’s most striking city walls. Constructed between 413 and 447 by Theodosius II, they were the greatest fortifications built up to their time, stretching more than four miles from the Golden Horn in the north to the Sea of Marmara in the south. Four Seasons, Bosphorus is a palace beautifully modified into a chic retreat, stunningly landscaped & surrounded by the azure waters and the mountain tops.

    Day at leisure

    Dinner reservations can be made at Maiden’s (or Leander’s) Tower, where you may enjoy drinks and dinner on the first-floor restaurant or the café, Kiz Kulesi. Dinner could be followed by some wine with your loved one on the top floor of this light house with awe inspiring views against the dark at the Kuledebar. Rising up on an islet in the middle of the Bosphorus, this former Ottoman lighthouse has inspired not one, but two legends. We strongly recommend a visit for truly romantic experience.

    Overnight: Four Seasons Hotel, Bosphorus
    Meal Plan: None

  • Day 2

    ISTANBUL–Sightseeing & Private sunset cruise

    Explore some of Istanbul's masterpieces of Byzantine and Ottoman architecture, beginning with the sixth-century Basilica Cistern. Visit the timeless Hagia Sophia, one of the greatest living examples of Byzantine architecture. Originally constructed as a church in 360, it has been a museum since 1935. The Turks governed most of the Mediterranean and western Orient from the 15th-century Topkapi Palace. Later proceed to the Pera district, the present-day heart of Istanbul.

    Early this evening, share memorable moments on a private sunset cruise of the Bosphorus. As you cruise along, view of the beautiful skyline is stunning with its splendid mansions, Judas blossoms, forts and palaces. To complete the day enjoy each other’s company over some wine, followed by dinner and entertainment all specially arranged for you on the cruise.

    Overnight: Four Seasons Hotel, Bosphorus
    Meal Plan: B, D

  • Day 3

    ISTANBUL–Trip to Buyukada & sightseeing

    You will be transported to Buyukada in a private boat. Buyukada is one of the Princes Islands in the Sea of Marmara. Cuddle up with your sweetheart in a horse-drawn buggy (private vehicles are not permitted on the island). Or stroll along the leafy boulevards lined with Ottoman-Victorian colonial houses. Enjoy a glass of wine at the café on the grounds of the famous St. George’s Church.

    After lunch, you will be taken on a private guided half day sightseeing tour (4 hour) with your A&K Representative. The 16th century was the Golden Age of the Ottoman Empire, and the era's master architect was Sinan. Visit his Suleymaniye Mosque, dominating the skyline with its magnificent dome. In the Ottoman world, pious mosque-school-bathhouse complexes sustained the social life of the community, and magnificent markets and bazaars powered the financial life. Visit Sinan's splendid Rustem Pasa Mosque, well-known for its Iznik tiles, located close to the eventful Spice Bazaar. Perhaps you can locate a souvenir or two in its 88 vaulted rooms. After strolling around the bazaar, stop for a special treat, taste of Istanbul's best baklava and the chance to meet the master pastry chef. No mall is as alluring as the 3,500-stall Grand Covered Bazaar, and you have an opportunity to walk through it looking for your own treasures.

    This evening, you could venture out to enjoy the famed nightlife of Istanbul.

    Overnight: Four Seasons Hotel, Bosphorus
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    ISTANBUL–IZMIR–EPHESUS–SIRINCE–Sightseeing, local cooking & renew your vows

    Fly to Izmir, the birthplace of Homer, and visit the remains of the great Ionian city of Ephesus, lying at the foot of Mount Pion. Unequaled as an archaeological site, the absolute size and grandeur of the ruins make it the best-preserved classical city in the eastern Mediterranean. Wander around the archaeological park, local museum and temple, and attend a private opening of the breathtaking Terrace Houses.

    For lunch, join a local family in their country restaurant for a hands-on lesson in Turkish cooking.

    Move ahead to the beautifully preserved village of Sirince, resting on a curved gorge that overlooks never-ending olive groves extending towards the Aegean Sea. Spend your night in an authentic cottage with brilliant views over the town, valley and surrounding mountains.

    Return to Ephesus in the evening for a delightful opportunity and poignant event: a chance to renew your marriage vows in front of the Celsus Library. Authenticated by a local priest with the Izmir State Symphony in the background, your wedding blessing becomes a romantic and memory to be cherished for a lifetime.

    Overnight: Nisanyan Houses
    Meal Plan: B,L

  • Day 5

    SIRINCE–IZMIR–CAPPADOCIA–Picnic lunch & romantic dinner

    Your private chauffer will drive you to the airport in Izmir for your private charter flight to Cappadocia. Millennia-long volcanic activity and erosion turned the volcanic remains into a soft porous stone named tuff, which form the stunning landscape of Cappadocia. The valleys of tuff occupied with "fairy chimneys" were developed by expert carvers into cave dwellings, monasteries, chapels and cellar villages.

    On arrival, you would be met & assisted to your luxurious traditional Cappadocia hotel, which is also partly carved into the tuff. Appreciate the antiques, elegant furniture, handmade wool and silk carpets that form a part of the interior décor throughout the property.

    At your own pace sightsee the rock-carved churches of Göreme, now an open-air museum displaying fine examples of ninth- and tenth-century Byzantine wall paintings.

    Lunch, ride by horse cart to a quaint place in the country for a picnic barbecue. Feast on a mezze of salads, kebabs, fried chicken and lamb chops.

    We suggest a romantic dinner at Sur Balık Restaurant, which offers new tastes in Cappadocia. Talented executive chefs concoct the Anatolian specialties beside the famous Red River of Avanos.

    Overnight: The Museum Hotel
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 6

    CAPPADOCIA–Hot air ballooning

    Wake with sunrise and get on board for a thrilling hot-air balloon ride over Cappadocia, looking down over the magical landscapes. When you land, celebrate with a champagne toast. After a late breakfast, explore another wonder of the region: the "underground city" at Kaymakli. The wide network of rock-carved subterranean tunnels and hallways, storage and sleeping cells, airshafts and chapels, is more than 140 feet deep. With your private guide, follow a lighted route and descend to 70 feet to explore the mysterious passageways and dwellings.

    Lunch; join a local farmer and his family at their home for a taste of authentic and rustic cuisine.

    This afternoon, visit Göreme Village Weavers Association, where young girls weave traditional rugs at home.

    Or
    Evening, you can experience a special private massage session in one of the hidden and mystic valleys as part of our Valley Experiences. At offer are a variety of exclusive massages, such as our Ehl-i Massage, Cariye's Secret, Asian Silence, Sultan's Pleasure, Secret of Aphrodite, and Cappadocian Roses from menus (placed in your room)—all carried out with a professional and personal touch.

    Overnight: The Museum Hotel
    Meal Plan: B,L

  • Day 7

    DEPART CAPPADOCIA

    Board a private charter flight to Istanbul, where you connect with your departure flight home

    Meal Plan: B

Egypt: Love on the Nile (2012)

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 7 Nights

    Relive the romance of Cleopatra and Mark Anthony's era on an adventure to unforgettable, eternally beguiling Egypt. The pharaohs of a bygone time were considered god-kings, and you too will feel like royalty as you cruise down the Nile on a tranquil four-night cruise on board a privately chartered dahabieh (traditional Egyptian sailboat). Throughout the journey, discover Egypt's most evocative and enduring monuments, including the Pyramids of Giza, the Temple of Kom Ombo and the Valley of the Kings and Queens.

  • Day 1

    India/Cairo, Egypt

    Board flight to Cairo, where the history and romance of ancient Egypt still lives. Upon arrival, you are met by an Abercrombie & Kent representative and transferred to your hotel, where you are assisted with check in. The remainder of your day is at leisure.

    Overnight: Four Seasons Hotel Cairo at the First Residence
    Meal Plan: None

  • Day 2

    Cairo

    This morning, visit the world-famous Egyptian Museum of Antiquities, home to the relics of Egypt's glorious, bygone era. The pink-granite building was constructed in the 19th century, and original Pharaonic statues and monuments pepper the lush gardens on museum grounds. Tour this impressive museum, home to relics dating back to 4000 BC, among them the treasures of Tutankhamen and the Mummy Room. After lunch, begin your tour of the breathtaking pyramids. The Pyramid of Cheops, the largest of the three, was built around 2690 BC and is one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World. Marvel at the mysterious Sphinx, 190 feet long and 65 feet high, carved out of a single piece of rock. This evening, enjoy a private dinner on board a felucca (traditional sailboat), listening to the soft sounds of the oud (Egyptian lute) as you glide along the magical Nile.

    Overnight: Four Seasons Hotel Cairo at the First Residence
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 3

    Cairo/Abu Simbel/Aswan

    Early this morning, fly south to Aswan and then board a short flight to Abu Simbel. Abu Simbel is home to the magnificent temple of Ramses II, with its four 60-foot-high statues of the pharaoh. After exploring this stunning temple, fly back to Aswan and continue overland to your private sailing vessel, the 'Zein Nile Chateau.' With its airy white sails and elegant interiors, this dahabieh (traditional Egyptian sailboat) is the perfect setting for a romantic getaway. Enjoy a lovely lunch on board as you set sail toward Aswan's High Dam, from which you can gaze down the huge lake created to control the floodwaters of the Nile. Continue on your gentle cruise to the exquisite Temple of Philae on the island of Agilika. End the afternoon at the granite quarries, which supplied the ancient Egyptians with most of the hard stone used in pyramids and temples. Here you will find the 1,500-ton Unfinished Obelisk, a glorious example of the technique used by the ancient Egyptians in producing these enormous monoliths.

    Overnight: Sanctuary Zein Nile Chateau
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 4

    Aswan/Kom Ombo/Edfu

    Enjoy a leisurely breakfast on board your dahabieh as you continue cruising the river, en route to Kom Ombo. Disembark and explore the lovely Greco-Roman temple dedicated to Haroeris (the Sun God) and Sobek (the Crocodile God). The temple, built in the third century BC, stands at a bend in the Nile where, in ancient times, sacred crocodiles basked in the sun on the riverbank. Afterward, return to your vessel for lunch in the shade as the 'Zein Nile Chateau' sets sail for Edfu. Arrive in the early evening at the well-preserved Temple of Horus (the Falcon God), built in 230 BC by Ptolemy III.

    Overnight: Sanctuary Zein Nile Chateau
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 5

    Edfu/Esna/Luxor

    Today, travel to Esna, home to the Greco-Roman Temple of Khnum. The beautifully preserved Great Hypostyle Hall was built during the reign of the Roman Emperor Claudius. The walls of the temple are covered with inscriptions, most of which date back to the Roman period. After contemplating this architectural marvel. Return to your vessel to enjoy another romantic lunch. The balance of your afternoon is at leisure to sit back and enjoy the breathtaking scenery passing by as the 'Zein Nile Chateau' makes her way along the Nile toward Luxor. You also catch a glimpse into daily life on the river's banks, which in many areas has remained almost unchanged for centuries.

    Overnight: Sanctuary Zein Nile Chateau
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 6

    Luxor

    This morning, your journey takes you to the famous Valley of the Kings and Queens, where over 60 tombs have been discovered. Afterward, pause by the lovely terraced funerary temple of Queen Hatshepsut, Egypt's first female pharaoh, and pass by the fabled Colossi of Memnon. Following lunch on board the 'Zein Nile Chateau,' pay a visit to the awe-inspiring Temple of Karnak, a huge complex where 134 great columns once supported an enormous roof, an extraordinary engineering feat for the 12th century BC. Continue to the strikingly graceful Temple of Luxor, built by Amenhotep III, Tutankhamon, Haremhab and Ramses II during their respective reigns and dedicated to the god Amun. Along the front of the temple, three large statues remain of Ramses II, while the Colonnade of Amenophotep III features 14 towering papyrus-headed columns. Walk hand in hand amid these timeless temples, reflecting on a breathtaking culture and its legacy. Return to your dahabieh in the afternoon for some time at leisure before dinner.

    Overnight: Sanctuary Zein Nile Chateau
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 7

    Luxor/Cairo

    This morning, bid farewell to your 'Zein Nile Chateau' crew as you disembark and are transferred to the airport for your flight back to Cairo. The balance of the day is at leisure. You may wish to arrange a private car and driver for further explorations of the city or to complete some last-minute shopping. Tonight, dine privately in the Churchill Gardens of the historic Mena House Oberoi Hotel, where your table overlooks the magnificent pyramids.

    Overnight: Four Seasons Hotel Cairo at the First Residence
    Meal Plan: B,D

  • Day 8

    Cairo/India

    After Breakfast, Transfer to the airport for your homebound flight.

    Meal Plan: B
