EXOTIC HONEYMOON IN CUBA & MEXICO

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 13 DAYS

    Highlights:MEXICO CITY, HAVANA & RIVIERA MAYA OFFERS MIX OF CITY, HERITAGE & BEACH

    • ITINERARY

  • Day 1

    India/Mexico City

    Depart from India to Mexico City and Arrival transfer to The Four Seasons Hotel for three nights, an oasis of calm in the centre of the city.

    Overnight Stay:Four Seasons Hotel

  • Day 2

    Mexico City// Ancient Aztec waterways of Xochimilco by boat

    Spend a leisurely morning plying the ancient Aztec waterways of Xochimilco by boat before driving to the delightful colonial district of Coyocan to follow in the footsteps of Surrealist artist Frida Kahlo and her muralist husband Diego Rivera. Visit Casa Azul, Frida Kalho's home and Diego Rivera's Studio Museum and the colourful Saturday Bazaar at San Jacinto.

    Overnight Stay: Four Seasons Hotel

  • Day 3

    Mexico City// Visit to astonishing archaeological zone of Teotihuacan

    Morning visit to the astonishing archaeological zone of Teotihuacan, famous for the world's third and fourth largest pyramids, the Pyramids of the Sun and the Moon.Afternoon guided tour of Mexico City's historic centre including the main square, one of the largest in the world and a UNESCO World Heritage site, the Metropolitan Cathedral and Temple Mayor, the most important Aztec temple in their capital city of Tenochitlan.

    Overnight Stay: Four Seasons Hotel

  • Day 4

    Mexico City/Havana

    Midday flight to the vibrant city of Havana and transfer to the Saratoga Hotel for four nights. The famous Saratoga is an iconic landmark in the centre of Havana, recently transformed to reflect its 1930s glory. Enjoy wonderful views of the city from the rooftop pool.

    Overnight Stay:: Saratoga Hotel

  • Day 5

    Havana//Walking tour of Habana Vieja

    Morning walking tour of Habana Vieja, the UNESCO preserved old town. Observe the faded elegance of the city including the Gran Teatro and one of Ernest Hemingway's favourite haunts - La Bodeguita. Afternoon at leisure. You may wish to spoil yourself with a 'mojito' or daiquiri in one of the shady plazas and look out for a spontaneous burst of music or dancing.

    Overnight Stay: Saratoga Hotel

  • Day 6

    Havana//Visit to Cigar Factory

    Learn how cigars are made with a morning guided visit to a cigar factory. Afterwards enjoy a tour of the city in a classic car including the seafront 'Malecon' and Revolution Square, home to a large image of Che Guevara. This evening, experience local flavours with dinner at a private 'paladar' restaurant

    Overnight Stay: Saratoga Hotel

  • Day 7

    Havana//Day at Leisure

    Day at leisure to enjoy your last full-day in this fascinating city or relax on the hotel's rooftop terrace

    Evening visit to the world-famous open-air Tropicana Cabaret, a timeless show of glitz and glamour dating back to the 1930's

    Overnight Stay: Saratoga Hotel

  • Day 8

    Havana//Riviera Maya

    Morning flight to Cancun and transfer along Mexico's Caribbean coast to the Riviera Maya and the Rosewood Mayakoba Resort & Spa

    Overnight Stay: Rosewood Mayakoba Resort & Spa

  • Day 9, 10, 11 & 12

    Riviera Maya/ enjoying the legendary Rosewood style

    Spend four days enjoying the legendary Rosewood style, service and delicious cuisine. Also on offer is a championship golf course, spa island, lecture programme on culture and ecology, children's activity club, diving on the world's second largest coral reef and even tequila tasting sessions, not to mention luxurious suites and several swimming pools.

    Overnight Stay: Rosewood Mayakoba Resort & Spa

  • Day 13

    Riviera Maya/India

    Transfer to the airport for flight back to India.

Antarctica, South Georgia & the Falkland Islands (2012-13)

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 17 Nights

    Discover the unique wildlife and remote landscapes of the Southern Ocean on this sweeping panorama of South Georgia, the Falkland Islands and the Antarctic Peninsula. Accompanied by naturalist guides and leading experts, scan the seas from 'Le Boreal' and set out on twice-daily excursions to learn about the remote and fragile environment that unfolds before you during this journey of a lifetime.

  • Day 1

    India/Santiago, Chile

    Arrive in Santiago, where an A&K representative meets you at your gate and assists you through arrival formalities. Then transfer to your hotel, where immediate check-in has been arranged. Relax before this afternoon's optional tour of the city's highlights, including the La Moneda Palace and the Plaza de Armas. Get to know your fellow travellers this evening at a welcome cocktail party.

    Overnight: Grand Hyatt (Santiago)
    Meals: Breakfast

  • Day 2

    Santiago/ Ushuaia, Argentina

    Fly to Ushuaia at the southern tip of South America. Enjoy some sightseeing (time permitting) and a traditional Patagonian barbecue lunch. This afternoon, board your ship and settle in for a first night on the water. Familiarize yourself with your luxury cruiser; you may even want to toast your journey in the bar.

    Overnight: Le Boreal
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 3

    At Sea

    As you sail east toward the Falklands, your lecturers begin their informal series of enlightening discussions on the flora, fauna and the history of the region. A professional staff photographer is on hand to assist you with specific camera questions and, throughout your cruise, leads lectures focusing on various skills. The waters here are rich in krill, making them a favorite with local wildlife searching for food. Alongside your naturalist guides, spot whale and seabirds from the cruiser's deck.

    Overnight: Le Boreal
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 4

    Port Stanley, Falkland Islands*

    Today, you have the opportunity to step back in time to a place where nature reigns supreme. Spend your day walking to penguin rookeries and other unspoiled wildlife habitats, or perhaps strolling the charming streets of the village of Stanley, the Falklands' capital.

    Overnight:Le Boreal
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 5-6

    Cruising the South Atlantic

    As the ship approaches South Georgia, deck-side sightings of whale, seal and other wildlife become more frequent. The time spent cruising to South Georgia is filled with informative lectures - as well as casual talks with your guides - that soon have you scanning the seas like an expert.

    Overnight: Le Boreal
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 7-8-9

    Cruising the South Atlantic

    South Georgia is actually a nearly submerged continuation of the Andes mountain range, and your days here are spent exploring its glacier-carved coves and rugged shores. Visit the impressive Salisbury Plain, St. Andrews Bay or Gold Harbour, all home to vast colonies of king penguin as well as fur and elephant seal. You also have the opportunity to retrace part of the route taken by Ernest Shackleton during his historic crossing of South Georgia Island in 1916. Weather permitting; you may visit his gravesite in Grytviken.

    Overnight: Le Boreal
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 10-11

    Cruising the Scotia Sea

    During your time cruising the deep-blue Scotia Sea - rich with marine mammals and fish - expert lecturers recap your adventures in South Georgia.

    Overnight: Le Boreal
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 12-15

    Antarctica

    The ship's itinerary calls for landings on the Antarctic Peninsula and a number of South Shetland Islands, which may include Half Moon, Penguin and Deception. Other possible island visits may include Cuverville, Paulet and Goudier. There are also plans to sail through Paradise Bay and the Lemaire Channel, with additional opportunities for exploration, including a stop at a scientific research station. While on board, you may view whale and birdlife from the deck or from your own cabin's private balcony.

    Overnight: Le Boreal
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 16-17

    Drake Passage

    Return through Drake Passage, where there are more chances to observe marine and birdlife. Compare notes on your incredible journey with your fellow travellers, either on deck or in one of 'Le Boreal's comfortable gathering spots.

    Overnight: Le Boreal
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • 18

    Ushuaia/Santiago, Chile/India

    Disembark after breakfast and transfer to the airport.
