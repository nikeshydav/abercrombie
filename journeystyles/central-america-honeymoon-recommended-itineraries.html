EXOTIC HONEYMOON IN CUBA & MEXICO

Programme Overview

Mexico City & Riviera Maya offers mix of city, Heritage & Beach

  • Duration: 8 Days/7 Nights

    Highlights
    Ply the ancient Aztec waterways of Xochimilco by boat
    Visit Casa Azul, Frida Kahlo’s home
    Visit to the astonishing archaeological zone of Teotihuacan - the ‘City of Gods’
    Riviera Maya
    Swim through a 600-meter route full of beautiful stalactites and stalagmites on this private tour.
    A private swim with the dolphins
    Enjoy the Esencia de Amor Couples Massage at Esencia Estate
    Swim with the great whale in Isla Holbox
    Visit one of the Seven Wonders of the World - Chichen Itza

    A&K Advantage

    Private arrival and departure airport transfers
    The services of an A&K Representative

    * On arrival in each country, you are welcomed by your A&K Representative who conducts a briefing on your time in the country. An A&K Representative is always at hand to provide unparalleled 24/7 support, be it local travel tips or reconfirming arrangement details, to providing recommendations for restaurants and shopping. Should you ever need help or advice, dial your A&K Representative’s mobile phone for prompt and courteous service at any time.

  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    MEXICO CITY

    On arrival in Mexico City, you would be met by a A&K Representative and transferred to The Four Seasons Hotel. The Four Seasons hotel is situated in the heart of Mexico City & offers amazing service in all areas of hospitality; guaranteeing you a memorable stay!

    Overnight: Four Seasons Hotel

  • Day 2

    MEXICO CITY - ANCIENT AZTEC WATERWAYS OF XOCHIMILCO BY BOAT

    Spend a leisurely morning plying the ancient waterways of Xochimilco - one of the last traces of the Aztec era - by boat before driving to the delightful colonial district of Coyocan to follow the footsteps of Surrealist artist Frida Kahlo and her muralist husband Diego Rivera. Visit Casa Azul, Frida Kahlo’s home and Diego Rivera's Studio Museum. The Casa Azul is where she was born and returned to for the last years of her life and contains a large assortment of pre-Hispanic artifacts, Mexican folk art, and indigenous jewelry from her personal collection and is an absolutely stunning sight! And if you happen to be in the city over the weekend, a visit to the colourful Saturday Bazaar at San Jacinto is a must!

    Overnight: Four Seasons Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    MEXICO CITY - VISIT TO THE ASTONISHING ARCHAEOLOGICAL ZONE OF TEOTIHUACAN

    This morning, visit the astonishing archaeological zone of Teotihuacan, famous for the world's third and fourth largest pyramids, the Pyramids of the Sun and the Moon. Known as the ‘City of Gods’ Teotihuacan, preserves the remains of one of the most important civilizations and you will be amazed by the beautiful constructions within the wall of the Pyramid of the Sun, the Temple of Quetzalpapalotl, the Avenue of the Dead and the buildings surrounding them.

    Afternoon enjoy a guided tour of Mexico City's historic centre including the main square, one of the largest in the world and a UNESCO World Heritage site, the Metropolitan Cathedral and Temple Mayor, the most important Aztec temple in their capital city of Tenochtitlan.

    Overnight: Four Seasons Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    MEXICO CITY - RIVIERA MAYA

    Morning fly to Cancun and transfer along Mexico's Caribbean coast to the Riviera Maya and the Rosewood Mayakoba Resort & Spa.

    En route, you could go underground and explore Rio Secreto. This is a surreal locale near Playa del Carmen where one can observe natural history dating back millions of years and you can hike and swim through a 600-meter route full of beautiful stalactites and stalagmites on this private tour.

    Later on, enjoy another marvelous experience where you and your partner will enjoy the private swim with the dolphins! This intimate dolphin program in Cancun is designed for the best interaction with dolphins in a personalized and more intimate swim.

    Check in and relax at your amazing hotel.

    An impressive collection of eclectic wines are housed in the resort’s glass-walled wine room called La Cava. This evening, enjoy a romantic dinner in this private dining space.

    Overnight: Rosewood Mayakoba Resort & Spa
    Meal Plan: B, D

  • Day 5

    RIVIERA MAYA - ENJOYING THE LEGENDARY ROSEWOOD STYLE

    Spend four days enjoying the legendary Rosewood style, service and delicious cuisine. Also on offer is a spa island, lecture programme on culture and ecology, diving on the world's second largest coral reef and even tequila tasting sessions, not to mention luxurious suites and several swimming pools.

    Indulge in another authentic Mexican ritual & try a few rounds of the famed tequila!

    In the evening we will make reservations for you to enjoy the Esencia de Amor Couples Massage at Esencia Estate, 15 miles south of Playa del Carmen. This experience includes exfoliation massage and private Jacuzzi salts and scents aphrodisiac. A ritual to celebrate any special event or to the joy of being together!

    Overnight: Rosewood Mayakoba Resort & Spa
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 6

    RIVIERA MAYA - ISLA HOLBOX

    Early this morning after breakfast, we would highly recommend you undertake another experience of a life time – swim with one of the most amazing creatures of the sea – the great whale! Take your private guide & driver to Chiquila and the board your private ferry to the tranquil & ecofriendly Isla (island) Holbox. Arrive and embark on a private tour snorkeling to swim with the elusive whale sharks – and if you are lucky, you may even have some manta rays, green turtles and playful dolphins as company!

    After this exhilarating experience, enjoy your lunch at a private secluded spot on the beach. On your return, visit the Isla Pajaros (Bird Island) and admire a phenomenal variety of birds in their natural habitat. Take the late afternoon ferry back to Chiquila and enjoy a sundowner witnessing an amazing sunset over the water. Head for your private flight; back towards the Rosewood Mayakoba Resort for the evening.

    Relax after the day’s adventurous experience & enjoy a fabulous meal at one of the restaurants.

    Overnight: Rosewood Mayakoba Resort & Spa
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 7

    RIVIERA MAYA - CHICHEN ITZA

    This morning after breakfast, meet your private guide at the hotel lobby & be escorted on a private flight to one of the new Seven Wonders of the World – Chichen Itza.

    Witness the amazing achievements of the Mayan civilization and learn about the different theories of the rise and fall of the dynamic Mayan civilization.

    Visit ‘Ikkil’ - a beautiful limestone sinkhole nearby that is great for an afternoon swim! End the amazing afternoon and enjoy a local fare at a quaint restaurant. Return to the hotel in time for dinner.

    Overnight: Rosewood Mayakoba Resort & Spa
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 8

    DEPART RIVIERA MAYA

    Transfer to the airport for your onward connections.

    Meal Plan: B

  • Optional extensions:

    5 DAYS MEXICO TO HAVANA

    Day 1

    MEXICO CITY - HAVANA

    Morning at leisure & later depart for your flight to the vibrant city of Havana and check into the Saratoga Hotel. The famous Saratoga is an iconic landmark in the centre of Havana, recently transformed to reflect its 1930s glory. Enjoy wonderful views of the city from the rooftop pool.

    Overnight: Saratoga Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 2

    HAVANA - WALKING TOUR OF HABANA VIEJAS

    This morning you will be taken on a walking tour of Habana Viejas, the UNESCO preserved old town. Observe the faded elegance of the city including the Gran Teatro and one of Ernest Hemingway's favourite haunts - La Bodeguita.

    Afternoon would be at leisure. You may wish to spoil yourself with a 'mojito' or daiquiri in one of the shady plazas and look out for a spontaneous burst of music or dancing.

    Overnight: Saratoga Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    HAVANA - VISIT TO CIGAR FACTORY

    Learn how cigars are made with a morning guided visit to a cigar factory. Try your hand at rolling a few cigars & these would then be labeled especially for you – personalised to commemorate your special event! Afterwards enjoy a tour of the city in a classic car including the seafront 'Malecon' and Revolution Square, home to a large image of Che Guevara.

    This evening, experience local flavours with dinner at a private 'paladar' restaurant and later return to your hotel for the night.

    Overnight: Saratoga Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    HAVANA - DAY AT LEISURE

    At leisure to enjoy your last day at this fascinating city or relax at the hotel's attractive and romantic rooftop garden.

    This evening visit the world-famous open-air Tropicana Cabaret, a timeless show of glitz and glamour dating back to the 1930's. The show mostly reveals the Cuban folklore under the starry skies and warm tropical moon, a surreal and an exotic experience.

    Overnight: Saratoga Hotel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 5

    DEPART HAVANA

    This morning after breakfast, your private chauffer will meet you at your hotel & assist with your transfer to the airport for your onward connection.

    Meal Plan: B

MEXICO: MOST ROMANTIC AND LUXURIOUS GETAWAYS

Programme Overview

Puerto Vallarta, Costa Alegre and Comala

  • Duration: 11 Days/10 Nights

    Highlights
    Rhythms of the Night show
    Humpback whale viewing
    Dolphin belly ride
    Spa & Gourmet dining
    Private picnics
    Twilight horseback ride
    Horseback tour of a coffee plantation
    Archaeological sites sightseeing - El Chanal and La Campana
    Helicopter ride to view the twin volcanoes
    Sightseeing of the Nevado de Colima Volcano National Park

    A&K Advantage
    Dinner at Frida, one of the top gourmet restaurants in Puerto Vallarta, recipient of Four Diamonds by AAA
    Private sunset sailing

    Private arrival and departure airport transfersA&K Advantage
    The services of an A&K RepresentativeA&K Advantage

    * On arrival in each country, you are welcomed by your A&K Representative who conducts a briefing on your time in the country. An A&K Representative is always at hand to provide unparalleled 24/7 support, be it local travel tips or reconfirming arrangement details, to providing recommendations for restaurants and shopping. Should you ever need help or advice, dial your A&K Representative’s mobile phone for prompt and courteous service at any time.

  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE PUERTO VALLARTA–Rhythms of the Night show

    Upon arrival you will be welcomed by an A&K Representative and privately transferred to your boutique hotel. Hacienda San Angel is one of worlds most sophisticated and romantic boutique luxury hotels.

    Snuggled high above the narrow cobblestone streets of Puerto Vallarta, one of the worlds most sophisticated and romantic boutique luxury hotel experiences awaits you. Mexican Colonial architecture charmingly dominates this stunningly seductive hotel adorned with turn of the century furnishings. Lush green lawns with fragrant flowers, tropical flora and trickling fountains meander through this exquisite setting. Three pools, a fine dining restaurant perched at the roof top and nightly cocktail hours only enhance the romantic escape.

    You will enjoy a chilled bottle of sparkling wine upon arrival to help you relax and unwind by the pool.

    At sunset, you are transported for an adventurous evening by a private ocean cruise across Banderas Bay to the isolated cove of Las Caletas for “Rhythms of the Night”. As night falls, the secluded haven of Las Caletas transforms into an enchanting land that will entrance you and take you on a breath taking trip to a mystical and spiritual world that time forgot.

    A private beachside candlelit table will ensure you enjoy every moment of this unique dining experience. Concealed at the corner of the tropical jungle, a torch-lit pyramid creates the dreamy and enchanting atmosphere for a hypnotic and breathtaking theatrical act.

    After the show, the memorable evening will come to an end with a nightcap and dancing beneath the stars on the short cruise back to your point of departure.

    On return to the hotel room you will be welcomed with candles and roses petals for a romantic ambience.

    Overnight: Hacienda San Angel
    Meal Plan: D

  • Day 2

    PUERTO VALLARTA–Whale watching, Dolphin swim, spa & dinner in a 4 diamonds by AAA restaurant

    Morning after breakfast you will be whisked away in a private boat to the Banderas Bay for a memorable experience of whale watching. You will be taken close to with the majestic Humpback Whales also known as yubarta, jibarte or xibarte. You will enjoy observing their actions and get fascinated with their remarkable jumps, tale hits and playing with their lovely cabs. The beautiful melodies created by these wonderful cetaceans are hypnotizing. Listen to them on the hydrophone and get mesmerised.

    Afternoon, you head for a Dolphin swim adventure which includes getting close to these smart and passionate creatures. You will be amazed by their strength and energy as you take a belly ride with a dolphin.

    Late evening you will visit the Grand Velas Riviera Nayarit for a special treat to their exclusive spa and a gourmet dining experience. Soothe your tired muscles and enjoy rejuvenating spa treatments designed for you to experience the benefits of water, earth, fire, air and the native therapeutic arts of the Huichol people.

    Continue your luxurious experience and get treated to traditional Mexican cuisine at Frida where every meal is reflective of Mexico's culinary past. Located on the second floor of Grand Velas Riviera Nayarit hotel, Frida is one of the top gourmet restaurants in Puerto Vallarta. It is the recipient of Four Diamonds by AAA, endorsing the high standards and fine dining experience delivered by this Puerto Vallarta restaurant.

    After dinner you will be transferred back to your hotel for the night in a private vehicle.

    Overnight: Hacienda San Angel
    Meal Plan: B, D

  • Day 3

    PUERTO VALLARTA–Private lunch & Sunset sailing

    Morning, you will be transferred to your private boat for a cruise to explore the Marietas & neighbouring Islands at your pace.

    Enjoy a private idealistic lunch on a secluded beach organised especially for you.

    For an unforgettable experience, a private luxurious Beneteau sailboat awaits to sail you into the Pacific Ocean. Sail along the shoreline near the Malecon, where the delicate blend of hues painting Puerto Vallarta give you the chance to capture the Old Town shining in vivid colours. Enjoy a panoramic view of the emblazoned horizon and get fascinated seeing the sky change colours as the sun sets. Wine, cheeses and an open bar will be on offer to add to the romance and beauty of the cruise. As the sunset dissolves into a starlit night, you can share a romantic kiss or two as you sail across Banderas Bay.

    Overnight: Hacienda San Angel
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    PUERTO VALLARTA–COSTALEGRE–At leisure

    Depart from Puerto Vallarta for Costalegre by your private charter flight. You will be welcomed by your A&K Representative and be assisted with check-in. Las Alamandas Resort is an Eco-Chic Boutique Hotel, an unspoiled tropical paradise, located between Puerto Vallarta and Manzanillo along the famed Costalegre Coast of Jalisco.

    Relax by the poolside sipping Margaritas and relishing tropical fruit.

    Evening, celebrate your honeymoon with a bottle of chilled sparkling wine and dine al fresco under a sky of countless stars. Contemporary Mexican Cuisine prepared with the best and freshest local produce and from the resort’s own organic garden.

    Overnight: Las Alamandas Resort
    Meal Plan: D

  • Day 5

    COSTALEGRE–Relax day

    Enjoy a complimentary bubble bath with rose petals, candle and a lazy romantic breakfast in bed.

    Day at leisure

    Relax in true Mexican style by taking a siesta together in a hammock on the beach listening to waves crashing along the shore

    Explore the local surroundings at your pace

    Rejoice a romantic candlelit dinner, set up elegantly with flowers with private service.

    Overnight: Las Alamandas Resort
    Meal Plan: B, D

  • Day 6

    COSTALEGRE–Horseback ride at twilight & a gourmet picnic

    Rise early for a horseback ride on the sand at twilight for a truly romantic experience and stunning views of the sunrise on the ocean.

    After breakfast you depart for light adventure experience of surfing and kayaking on the serene and tranquil waters of the lagoons.

    A gourmet picnic would have been organized for you at a private beach. The romantic setting and secluded alcove on the private beach will be perfect for the exclusive time that you would like to spend with each other and relax on the soft white sand.

    Evening at leisure

    Overnight: Las Alamandas Resort
    Meal Plan: B, L

  • Day 7

    COSTALEGRE–Shopping & spa

    Enjoy your Bubble bath with rose petals, candle and lazy romantic breakfast in bed.

    Do a self-drive of the beautiful coastal town or go on your private vehicle to the town of Barra de Navidad, located only 20 minutes away. It has a blend of shops selling local and regional crafts, pottery & ceramics, and other goods from the artisans.

    On return unwind with a massage to the soothing sounds of the waves at the oceanfront Spa Palapa. Indulge in private couple’s massages at the water’s edge.

    Overnight: Las Alamandas Resort
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 8

    COSTALEGRE–COMALA–At leisure

    Today you will be privately transferred to an eye-catching quaint town of Comala, Colima also known as “Pueblito Blanco” where the buildings are painted white with red tile rooftops and adorned in bright bougainvillea. The name Comala originates from the Náhuatl word, comalli and translates to a place where comales are made, the clay griddles that are still used today.

    Hacienda de San Antonio is located at the base of an active volcano in Mexico’s picturesque highlands. It offers lush gorgeous gardens and immense expanses of unspoiled natural beauty. The eternal spring-like climate is suitable for enjoying a wide range of outdoor activities.

    Spend the day at leisure to wander in the narrow cobblestone streets of Comala, where you will discover a host of attractions like local handicrafts, folk art and well known ceramic replicas of the perros cebados.

    Overnight: Hacienda de San Antonio
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 9

    COMALA–Coffee plantation tour and tasting & sightseeing of El Chanal and La Campana

    Comala’s soil, altitude and climate are ideal for coffee plantations. This morning ride on horseback to a coffee plantation for a tour and tasting of the regions finest coffees.

    After lunch, you will leave for a half day sightseeing of Colima’s most famed archaeological sites with your private guide. Colima's culture is deep rooted and is strongly prevalent at El Chanal and La Campana. Amongst other buildings of commercial importance at the pre-Hispanic time, is the evidence of a pre-Hispanic ball game. Ball game practice was as much about religious worship as it was about physical activity. Xolotl, a deity related with this game, as per Pre-Hispanic mythology was the companion of the sun during his rest in the underworld at night. Venus, the morning star, heralded the victory of Tonatiuh, the sun god daily.

    Overnight: Hacienda de San Antonio
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 10

    COMALA–Helicopter view of twin volcanoes & Nevado de Colima Volcano National Park tour

    Post breakfast you will depart for your private helicopter ride to Suchitlán Canyons for stunning views of the twin volcanoes. Enjoy the magnificent view of Nevado de Colima and the Volcan de Fuego de Colima.

    A private guide will accompany you to explore Nevado de Colima Volcano National Park which offers a vast expanse of flora and fauna.

    Evening at leisure

    Overnight: Hacienda de San Antonio
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 11

    DEPART COMALA

    You will be transferred by a private vehicle in style to the airport for your flight.

    Meal Plan: B
