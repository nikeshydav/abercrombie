Extreme Adventures

Extreme Adventures are for the traveller ready to meet a different kind of challenge.

No matter what goals we attain in our lives and careers, nature remains the ultimate benchmark of what we can achieve. An Extreme Adventure reveals exactly who you are, demanding the most of your physical, psychological and perhaps even spiritual selves.

Face the challenge of freezing temperatures, soaring altitudes, pounding G forces and barren deserts. Venture to places that test your ability to persevere; push your body to the limit in the harshest conditions and with the most demanding activities. Savor the rewards that only come from experiencing nature at its most extreme.

Choose Your Extreme Adventure

From the polar ice caps to the deserts of Africa and all points between, these adventures are sure to challenge and change you.

Arctic Expeditions

Set foot where few humans dare go. Feel icy air lashing every inch of exposed skin and face winds that buffet the sturdiest tents and blow the blinding snow sideways.
  • Alaska: Trails of the Iditarod
  • Iceland: Tracks Across the Glacier
  • Norway: On the Trail of the Polar Bear
  • The South Pole: Conquering the Final Degree

Desert Expeditions

In this timeless world of shifting sands, behold history's fragments and taste life in a place where survival demands the utmost resourcefulness.
  • Australia: Desert Eco Survey
  • Egypt - Traces of Lost Civilizations
  • Jordan: Ancient Strongholds & Desert Camps
  • Mali: The Jackal's Road
  • Mongolia: The Gobi Desert
  • Mongolia: The Thousand Camel Festival
  • Morocco: The Desert Road Rally

Multi-sport

From the mist-shrouded landscapes of Bhutan to the rolling rapids of New Zealand, explore your perfect playground by bike, camelback, raft, zip line and more.
  • Bhutan: Land of the Dragon
  • Costa Rica: Jungle Canopy to Surging Rapids
  • Mexico: Kayaking the Baja Coast
  • Mongolia: The Golden Eagle Festival
  • Norway: Lillehammer Olympic Challenge

Mountain Trekking

Scale the heights of earth's grandest peaks, proclaiming your ability to set foot where you choose - a raw triumph over nature.
  • Borneo: From the Rainforest to Mount Kinabalu
  • Greece: Ascent to the Home of the Gods
  • Mount Kilimanjaro: The Lemosho Route to the Summit
  • Mount Kilimanjaro: The Roof of Africa by the Umbwe Route
  • Nepal: Ascent to Everest Base Camp
  • Nepal: Conquering Everest Base Camp

Tailor Made

These independent Extreme Adventures are for those longing to experience the thrill of nature on their own schedule. Design your own expedition for you or your private party.
  • Tailor Made Argentina: The Ice Fields of Patagonia
  • Tailor Made Bolivia: Lake Titicaca & the Uyuni Salt Flats
  • Tailor Made Ecuador: From Jungles to Mountain Peaks
  • Tailor Made Greenland: Trek to the Igloo Village
  • Tailor Made New Zealand: Adrenaline Rush
  • Tailor Made Peru: Hiking the Inca Trail to Machu Picchu

Extensions
