HONEYMOON AT ZIMBABWE

Programme Overview

Imbabala, Victoria Falls and Hwange

  • Duration: 7 Days/6 Nights

    Highlights
    Game drive & Safaris
    Bush walks
    Adventure activities
    Special sundowners
    Private Cruises

    A&K Advantage
    Private pontoon boat cruise
    Dinner at Boma
    Victoria Falls Tram & Bridge Champagne Sunset Tour

    Private arrival and departure airport transfers
    The services of an A&K Representative

    * On arrival in each country, you are welcomed by your A&K Representative who conducts a briefing on your time in the country. An A&K Representative is always at hand to provide unparalleled 24/7 support, be it local travel tips or reconfirming arrangement details, to providing recommendations for restaurants and shopping. Should you ever need help or advice, dial your A&K Representative’s mobile phone for prompt and courteous service at any time.

  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE IMBABALA–Private boat cruise & sundowner

    On arrival at Victoria Falls airport you will be welcomed by an A&K Representative and privately transferred to Imbabala Zambezi Safari Lodge and assisted with check-in. The lodge is an un-spoilt wilderness, unfenced located on a riverine fringe of the mighty Zambezi River where Zimbabwe, Botswana, Zambia and Namibia come together.

    Later in the evening, explore Zambezi's natural beauty on a private pontoon boat cruise. You may even see an elephant swimming by, as they so often do. Complete your beautiful evening with a glass of champagne, cruising down the river and watch the sunset.

    Dinner is at the Lodge.

    Overnight: Imbabala Zambezi Safari Lodge
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 2

    IMBABALA–Fishing and game walk

    After breakfast, head out with your private guide on a speed boat to try a hand at angling. Imbabala is the only fishing and photographic lodge for a long stretch of the Zambezi River and has near exclusivity of the Zambezi & for many years it has been an excellent ground for the legendary Tigerfish angling, which has a well-deserved repute as one of the best fighting fresh water fish in the world.

    This evening you could leave for your walking safari and explore the wilderness in a memorable hand on experience or head out on a game drive.

    Dinner is around a bon fire at the lodge.

    Overnight: Imbabala Zambezi Safari Lodge
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 3

    VICTORIA FALLS–Elephant Camp & sunset cruise

    After Breakfast you depart the lodge in a private vehicle for The Elephant Camp. En-route, enjoy the spectacular view of the gorges that separate Zimbabwe and Zambia. On arrival at the camp you will be assisted with check-in.

    Afternoon is at your pace at this luxurious and intimate lodge.

    Early evening at the camp, get an opportunity to get introduced to and interact with the Wild Horizons elephants as you will be taken on a private interaction, followed by a romantic sunset Cruise on the Mighty Zambezi River.

    Dinner is scheduled at Boma where you will be dressed in traditional robes called chitenges, for an exciting evening. You will be treated to chilled local millet beer and a grand buffet with local delicacies. Enjoy live local entertainment like Amakwezi traditional dances and drumming, face painting, hair braiding, storytelling and fortune telling by a sangoma.

    Overnight: The Elephant Camp
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 4

    VICTORIA FALLS–Helicopter ride and the Victoria Falls Tram & Bridge Champagne Sunset Tour

    After breakfast, depart on a private helicopter flight over the mighty Victoria Falls and soak in the breath taking sight of the stunning gorges and the Victoria Falls. The flight provides a delightful view of the Devil’s Cataract, Eastern Cataract and swirling mist.

    The afternoon is open for adventure activities for you, which include white water rafting, bungee jumping from the bridge, a canoe safari, or a micro flight for the brave at heart.

    Enjoy the Victoria Falls Tram & Bridge Champagne Sunset Tour this evening. The Bungee Flying Squad will make an electrifying demonstration of stunts before the champagne refreshment. In a short, theatrical presentation you will be introduced to the history of the bridge. Relax and appreciate the stunning views of the magnificent African sunset overlooking the gorges and the falls.

    Later in the evening, relax with a special private couple Spa Treatments specially organized in the privacy of your suite.

    Savour African delicacies around a romantic bonfire dinner at the camp.

    Overnight: The Elephant Camp
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 5

    HWANGE–Game drive & sundowner

    Enjoy a bush walk with the lions before returning to the camp for your breakfast.

    After breakfast you will be transferred in a private vehicle to Hwange. Located on the eastern periphery of the Hwange National Park, The Hide Safari Camp is renowned world over for its expanse and wildlife population of buffalo, zebra, giraffe, rhino, elephant and all the big cats; making the game drives so popular. Hwange is a massive playground worth discovering & is also cushioned by a private concession which allows activities in the national park that may not otherwise be permitted.

    Evening, your private guide will ride along with you in your private vehicle for an exclusive experience of the Game Drive as you drive around in search of predators and prey alike. Sundowners are an essential part of the adventure so relax with a glass of chilled wine and enjoy the view the national park has to offer at sunset - calm, serene, dusky and simply romantic!

    Overnight: The Hide Safari Camp
    Meal Plan: L, D

  • Day 6

    HWANGE–Bush breakfast, waterhole hideaways & game walk

    Enjoy your breakfast especially laid out for you in the bush.

    Relax and spend time in the hides, a vital part of the safari tradition. Stay close to the watering hole, which is particularly the only permanent source of water for a large area and look out for the various birds and animals who visit. It is a memorable experience, watching them arrive in batches to take a drink, stand around, graze, do their herbivore activity or just have a good time.

    This evening you may explore the wildlife in their natural world on foot along with your personal guide who are trained, armed and always on a look out for you. There is an ultimate thrill and an adrenaline rush if you encounter a big game on foot and an experience you miss out on in a 4WD safari!

    Enjoy drinks and dinner around a bonfire tonight.

    Overnight: The Hide Safari Camp
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 7

    DEPART HWANGE

    After Breakfast you will be assisted with check-out and privately transferred to Victoria Falls Airport for your flight.

    Meal Plan: B

  • Possible Extension:
    3 Days KARIBA

    Day 1

    VICTORIA FALLS–KARIBA–Game drive

    After breakfast, you will be transferred by a private charter to Kariba. On arrival, you are welcomed by an A&K representative and assisted with check–in.

    The Changa Safari Camp is Located on the shores of Lake Kariba in Zimbabwe and offers an exclusive blend of Game and Fishing Safaris.

    Afternoon at leisure, cherish the time on your private deck with a chilled cocktail in the company of your loved one. Appreciate the splendid views of enigmatic Matusadona Mountains across the inland sea of Lake Kariba.

    Leave in your private vehicle along with your highly trained & professional guide for a game drive this evening. During the drive you may sight the predators, cheetah, leopard, lion and hyena as well as antelope, buffalo and elephant who are commoners to the shoreline. You could also come across hippos and crocodiles that are in abundance & if luck is by your side, you may even get a glimpse of the last remaining Black Rhinos.

    Dinner would be organized at the camp on your return.

    Overnight: Changa Safari Camp
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 2

    KARIBA–Canoe ride and fishing

    After breakfast you and your partner will leave for an explorative game walk in the enchanting Matusadona national park.

    After lunch at the camp, enjoy a canoe ride on the lake Kariba and take in the spectacular view of the lake and its surroundings.

    Fishing on Lake Kariba in a private pontoon boat is always a pleasurable experience. Try fighting Tiger Fish, which is renowned world over or alternatively try a hand on bream fishing which is also an amazing experience and your catch can be served as 'a la' snacks.

    Specially arranged romantic candle lit dinner under the starlit sky in the open private deck.

    Overnight: Changa Safari Camp
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 3

    DEPART VICTORIA FALLS

    After breakfast, check-out and be transferred by your private charter to Victoria Falls Airport for your flight and onwards connections.

    Meal Plan: B

Luxury Honeymoon in Zimbabwe

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 7 Days

    Highlights
    Enjoy the romantic Sunset cruise at the Zambezi River, experience the smoke that thunders at Victoria Falls & relax at the luxury lodges i.e. Victoria Falls, Hide Safari Camp & Changa Safari Camp and experience the best game drives safari experience with bush walk.

  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    Arrival at Victoria Falls Airport

    On arrival at The Victoria Falls Airport off a flight to be advised you be road transferred to The Victoria Falls Hotel.

    Later in the afternoon go for a romantic Sunset Cruise at the Mighty Zambezi River & Dinner at the Hotel.

    Overnight: The Victoria Falls Hotel

  • Day 2

    Visit Victoria Falls & Enjoy Optional Activities

    After breakfast, you will go on a guided tour of the falls.
    The Mighty Victoria Falls: -Victoria Falls present a spectacular sight of awe-inspiring beauty and grandeur on the Zambezi River, forming the border between Zambia and Zimbabwe. It was described by the Kololo tribe living in the area in the 1800’s as ‘Mosi-oa-Tunya’ – ‘The Smoke that Thunders’. In more modern terms Victoria Falls is known as the greatest curtain of falling water in the world.

    The Afternoon is open for Optional Activities
    • White water rafting
    • George swing/Zipline/Fly fox
    • Bungee jumping from the bridge
    • Canoe Safari
    • Walk with the lions
    • Helicopter flights

    Later in the evening dinner at the Hotel

    Overnight: The Victoria Falls Hotel

  • Day 3

    Victoria Falls//Hwange

    After breakfast and check-out at the Hotel, you will be Road Transferred to Hwange where you will stay at The Hide for 2 nights.

    The Hide Safari Camp:- The Hide Safari Camp is situated on the Eastern boundary of the famous Hwange National Park well known world over for its size and its wildlife population of buffalo, zebra, giraffe, rhino, all the big cats and the elephant population hence the game drives are the order of the day.

    Later in the afternoon tea you will go on a Game Drive & Dinner at camp then enjoy a night safari.

    Overnight: The Hide Safari Camp

  • Day 4

    Game driver & Bush walk

    Today you may do a bush walk in the morning along with a bush breakfast

    You will spend the whole day at The Hide Safari Camp. Enjoy Game Drives and Game walks, and spend time at the ‘hides’ where you can observe the animals at the watering hole.

    Spend the night at the tree house called Dove’s Nest & later enjoy the game drive with dinner at tree house.

    Overnight: The Doves Nest

  • Day 5

    Hwange//Kariba//Game Drive

    On this day after breakfast you will be Charter Transferred to Kariba where you will stay at The Changa Safari Camp for 2 nights on fully inclusive basis.

    The Changa Safari Camp: -is Located on the shores of Lake Kariba in Zimbabwe and provides the unique experience of combined Game and fishing Safaris.

    After check-in you will have afternoon Tea then go on evening Game Drive & Dinner at Changa Safari Camp

    Overnight: Changa Safari Camp

  • Day 6

    Enjoy Canoe Rides, Game Walk & Drives and Fishing on Lake Kariba

    You will spend the whole day at The Changa Safari Camp, enjoying canoe rides, Game Walks, Game Drives and Fishing on Lake Kariba.

    Overnight: Changa Safari Camp

  • Day 7

    Depart

    After breakfast at a time to be advised you will be charter transferred to Harare International Airport for your outbound flight.

    ***************************************End of A&K Services************************************************

LUXURY HONEYMOON IN KENYA

Programme Overview

Lewa Downs, Masai Mara and Diani Beach

  • Duration: 10 Days/9 Nights

    Highlights
    Game drives to explore the wilderness and sight the Big Five
    Bush & nature walk to explore the various plants, other plains animals and get educated on how to identify animal signs
    Horse ride
    Introduction to the Samburu clan & culture
    Hot air balloon safari over the plains, concluded in a tradition initiated by the Montgolfier brothers
    Trip to Kisite Marine Park
    Adventure sports at Diani beach

    A&K Advantage
    A romantic sundowner at Lewa Downs
    Special sundowner and dinner at Masai Mara
    “Out of Africa” picnic lunch
    Specially organised dinner at Masai Mara with local entertainment
    Sundowner cruise at Diani Beach

    Private arrival and departure airport transfers
    The services of an A&K Representative

    * On arrival in each country, you are welcomed by your A&K Representative who conducts a briefing on your time in the country. An A&K Representative is always at hand to provide unparalleled 24/7 support, be it local travel tips or reconfirming arrangement details, to providing recommendations for restaurants and shopping. Should you ever need help or advice, dial your A&K Representative’s mobile phone for prompt and courteous service at any time.

  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE NAIROBI–LEWA DOWNS–Game drives and romantic dinner

    On arrival at Nairobi you will be welcomed by an A&K representative and transferred by a private charter flight to Lewa Downs.

    An A&K representative will meet you on arrival, transfer you to the camp in a private vehicle and assist with check-in. En-route to the camp, enjoy a game drive. You may be rewarded with sightings of predators like lion as well as elephant and plenty of other game of the plains.

    Lewa Safari Camp located on Lewa Wildlife Conservancy is famed world over for its community conservation efforts and the fruits of those efforts are visible in the vast quantities of game that live on the 65,000 acre farm. Rarities like rhino and the splay-footed sitatunga antelope are easy to see at Lewa.

    Be welcomed with a cool beverage at hand after which you will be escorted to your luxurious tent to relax. Enjoy the stunning beauty of the conservancy’s landscape and Mt Kenya at a distance from the privacy of your own veranda.

    A romantic candle lit dinner will be served by the fireplace in the main lounge.

    Overnight: Lewa Safari Camp
    Meal Plan: D

  • Day 2

    LEWA DOWNS–Bush walk and horse ride

    On the second day of your special honeymoon enjoy a private breakfast served on your tent deck. Mornings at Lewa are special; you can appreciate spectacular views of a snow-capped Mt Kenya across at a distance.

    Game driving in Lewa is outstanding as it is home to 10 per cent of Kenya’s black rhino population and the single largest population of Grevy’s zebra in the world. You will be taken on a private guided bush walk. Get close to the plains game and smaller beasts as you explore their habitat.

    Between your bush walk, stop over at a spot specially selected and setup for you to relish an authentic picnic in the company of your loved one.

    Evening, trot around on a horse around the picturesque conservancy and be astonished by its beauty.

    Later enjoy a private romantic dinner served by the pool side, as you watch the sun set over the horizon.

    Overnight: Lewa Safari Camp
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 3

    LEWA DOWNS–Introduction to the Samburu clan & culture and a romantic sundowner

    A bush breakfast awaits you. Take the time to sit back, enjoy the spectacular views, the smells, colours and the gentle breeze. Bush breakfasts are a chance to enjoy a full breakfast in the middle of the plains, surrounded by wildlife.

    This day you have the chance to interact with your hosts – the Samburu people. Lewa Conservancy borders the Samburu community living in bordering conservancies in the arid lowlands of the north. The Samburu are one of the few remaining communities who still practice their traditional culture despite the fast changing world. You will visit a local Samburu village, where you will be introduced to the culture and also have the opportunity to buy hand-crafted beadwork and carvings which are offered for sale. Remember to bargain!

    As the day draws to a close, your private guide will drive you into the bush where a private and secluded vantage point will be all set for a romantic sundowner. Watch the spectacular sunset as you enjoy a nice drink alongside your loved one.

    A sumptuous dinner will be laid out on your tent verandah on return.

    Overnight: Lewa Safari Camp
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 4

    LEWA DOWNS–MASAI MARA–Game drive

    After breakfast you will be transferred for your private charter flight to Masai Mara’s Kichwa Tembo airstrip.

    As you approach the breathtakingly magnificent Masai Mara the wilderness and the freshness of air that comes with it will leave you awestruck. In this vast wilderness, culture blends with the wild with ease and the Mara is home to not only the Maasai people but also the numerous games that include the Big Five.

    A flower girl and your private guide will welcome you on arrival and transfer you to the camp in a private vehicle. En-route enjoy a first glimpse of the Masai Mara’s wildlife, you may be pleased with sightings of elephant, buffalo, giraffe and a host of plains game.

    On arrival at Sanctuary Olonana you will greeted with scented towels and chilled welcome drink. Relax at your large river view veranda and enjoy the stunning view.

    After lunch take a dip in the pool or relax by the pool.

    Evening you will be taken in your private vehicle with your highly trained and professional guide for a game drive in Maasai Mara. Maasai Mara is a fabulous place for seeing big cats rather all of the big five, a variety of plains game and over 300 species of birds. The plains offer a rich variety of diet for the dominant wildebeest and Topi, Thompson’s and Grant’s gazelle, eland and buffalo. In the Mara River, hippos dip to sniffle and grumble while crocodiles sunbathe, mouths agape, on the riverbanks. Around July to October, you could witness the most spectacular and greatest show on earth which Mara plays host to – the wildebeest migration.

    A private candle lit dinner served on your deck under the African stars will be set on your return.

    Overnight: Sanctuary Olonana
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 5

    MASAI MARA–Hot air balloon safari, nature walk, spa, special sundowner and dinner

    This morning gently float over the plains with a bird’s eye view of the game below as the sun rises slowly over the horizon. After landing enjoy a full breakfast including exotic Kenyan fruits, homemade pastries and other delicacies that are served in the middle of the bush with glasses of bubbly to toast your flight.

    Take a private walk with the naturalist and get educated about plants, trees, insects and even how to approach animals on foot, as your private guide shows you how to spot animal signs which are everywhere but not that easily noticed.

    Post lunch you have an exclusive appointment at the mini spa for a special private romantic couple’s treatment.

    After a revitalizing spa treatment join your private guide for a short game drive before driving to a specially chosen vantage point overlooking the Mara plains, where you have the most spectacular view of the rapid sunset colouring the acacia dotted plains and transforming the world. Together, romance by the log fire with the outstanding view of the setting sun on the horizon along with your sundowner drinks.

    Proceed to the bush where a private and secluded clearing has been prepared for a sophisticated dinner in the middle of the wilderness. Under the African starlit sky enjoy a delicious, warm and private candlelit dinner.

    Overnight: Sanctuary Olonana
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 6

    MASAI MARA–Explore Victoria Lake, “Out of Africa” picnic lunch and special dinner with entertainment

    Watch the rising African sun; listen to the sounds of the cascading river, the singing of birds and the hippos snorting in the river as you sip your bed tea.

    Take an early flight by a private aircraft to the Victoria Lake. Try your hand at fishing for the legendary giant Nile perch found in these waters as you discover the numerous tiny islands or stop by at a traditional fishing village and learn about the lives of the villagers and their day to day chores, before flying back to the Maasai Mara.

    Sanctuary Olonana is located in almost exactly the same spot where the Masai Mara scenes in “Out of Africa” were filmed. Experience a special “Out of Africa” picnic lunch in homage to that magical scene in the film with Meryl Streep, Robert Redford and that wonderful gramophone! Out in the bush on a rise of the escarpment, we recreate a memorable traditional picnic lunch whilst overlooking some of the most spectacular, game-filled scenery on the continent.

    Return to camp later in the afternoon. There is time relax by the pool or in your tent.
    Evening, go for a short game drive.

    On your last evening in Masai Mara, you will be picked up by your guide and driven for a private, secluded and an elegant dinner by a log fire. After an enjoyable silver service dinner, you retire to the crackling campfire where night caps are served. Quietly absorbing the peace and beauty of an African night, romancing under the open sky you will be amazingly surprised as chaos suddenly erupts with the sound of the Maasai battle cry. From trees and scrub leap long-limbed ochred warriors all brandishing long gleaming spears so prized by the Masai tribe. This is a mock charge by a group of Masai Moran (warriors) breathtaking in its authenticity! As you settle down, after absorbing the shock with your glasses refilled and laughter abated, the Moran demonstrate some traditional dances and their legendary gravity-defying leaps, their proud profiles and spearheads reflecting the amber light of the flames.

    Return to a candlelit room for the night.

    Overnight: Sanctuary Olonana
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 7

    MASAI MARA–DIANI BEACH–At leisure

    After a sumptuous breakfast at Sanctuary Olonana, bid the staff farewell and depart on a private charter to Diani Beach - one of the major beach resorts on Kenya’s South coast.

    You will be met by an A&K representative and transferred in style to Kinondo Kwetu, your dwelling for this last leg of your honeymoon. Kinondo Kwetu is a collection of five houses on Galu Beach south of Mombasa. Run by a Swedish family, it boasts of what may be the only sauna on the Kenyan coast! Surprisingly pleasant despite the high temperatures found here.

    Day at leisure, relax with glass of champagne lazing around the pool.

    Stroll along the coast line arm in arm with your loved one enjoying the gorgeous view of the Indian Ocean, pleasant winds which blow onshore from the North and commonly known as the Baridi (Cold) in Kiswahili.

    Enjoy a romantic evening by the beach where dinner will be served in a special private candlelit setting.

    Overnight: Kinondo Kwetu
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 8

    DIANI BEACH–Adventure sports or scuba diving & a romantic sundowner with horse ride

    Enjoy a scrumptious breakfast by the pool.

    The winds at Diani beach are highly suitable for surfing. You may choose to be adventurous and go for kite surfing or wind surfing. Take a short lesson and enjoy the experience.

    Alternately, you may be taken for a private scuba diving trip. This will guarantee you see a spectacle of fabulous coral reefs and an enormous marine population.

    Evening, go on the beach for some horse riding, followed by a special sundowner in a private secluded spot with some wine and tropical fruits.

    Overnight: Kinondo Kwetu
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 9

    DIANI BEACH–Trip to Kisite Marine Park and sundowner cruise

    After breakfast, depart for a half day trip to Kisite Marine Park, Shimoni in a private boat for an unforgettable experience. Shimoni is Kenya’s famous archipelago in the South coast with spectacular coral reefs, sighting of dolphins and tropical fish in the most beautiful and attractive colours. Enjoy your time with your loved one in exploring the reefs and marine life by snorkeling and spot dolphins.
    Savour a snack and a chilled drink on your way back on your private boat.

    After lunch, you are scheduled for a private cruise around the coast to explore the smaller islands around the area. Enjoy some champagne to celebrate your honeymoon on this sundowner, as you watch the sun setting at a far end over the horizon.

    Return to your hotel and either enjoy the bartenders specially prepare healthy, fresh tropical drinks for you or make a selection from the wine list of South African vintages. A 3-course dinner will be served at one of the charming spots in the garden or on the beach.

    Overnight: Kinondo Kwetu
    Meal Plan: B, D

  • Day 10

    DEPART DIANI BEACH

    After nine glorious nights in Kenya it is time to bid the country good bye. After breakfast you will be transferred by private charter and vehicle to the airport for your homeward flight.

    Meal Plan: B

SOUTH AFRICA: THE SIX-CARAT SAFARI

Programme Overview

Cape Town, Stellenbosch, Paarl, Hermanus, Johannesburg, Thorny bush Private Game Reserve

  • Duration: 11 Days/10 Nights

    Highlights
    Visit the beautiful garden of Cape Town and other places of importance
    Stellenbosch and Paarl Winelands experience
    Hermanus, whale watching
    Game drives to explore the wilderness and sight the Big Five
    Hot air ballooning over Thorny bush Private Game Reserve
    Bush walk to explore the various plants, other plains animals
    Special meals and sundowner experiences

    A&K Advantage
    Whale watching and a sundowner cruise at Hermanus
    Chef’s table dinner at Johannesburg

    Private arrival and departure airport transfers
    The services of an A&K Representative

    * On arrival in each country, you are welcomed by your A&K Representative who conducts a briefing on your time in the country. An A&K Representative is always at hand to provide unparalleled 24/7 support, be it local travel tips or reconfirming arrangement details, to providing recommendations for restaurants and shopping. Should you ever need help or advice, dial your A&K Representative’s mobile phone for prompt and courteous service at any time.

  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE CAPE TOWN–At leisure

    On arrival an A&K Representative will welcome and transfer you in a private vehicle to your hotel and assist with check-in.

    Afternoon is at leisure to relax or perhaps explore Cape Town, a city set on a soaring, rocky peninsula punctuated by lush valleys.

    Overnight: Cape Grace
    Meal Plan: None

  • Day 2

    CAPE TOWN–Sightseeing

    This morning, meet an A&K representative for a tour of charming Cape Town in your private vehicle. Drive through the suburb of Tamboerskloof ("Drummers' Ravine") to the lower cable car station on the slopes of Table Mountain. Weather permitting; ascend by cable car to the top of this famed mountain, which offers splendid view of the sea and the city from its summit.

    Proceed to historic Cape Town, where you start with a walk through the beautiful Company Gardens – which are remnants of the Dutch East India Company's fruit and vegetable gardens. Strut past the House of Parliament; Tuynhuis ("Townhouse," or Cape Town's "White House"), National Art Gallery and National Library. Move ahead to the 17th-century Castle of Good Hope. The last stop for the day will be the bustling Victoria & Alfred Waterfront, a working harbor surrounded by eclectic shops and restaurants.

    This is the perfect setting for a romantic waterfront dinner.

    Overnight: Cape Grace
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    CAPE TOWN–Cape Point Peninsular tour

    After breakfast you set off for a Cape Point Peninsula tour. Drive past the "bikini beaches" of Clifton and Camps Bay, located at the base of the towering Twelve Apostles Mountains. Continue to the Cape of Good Hope Nature Reserve and the Cape Peninsula. Ride “the Flying Dutchman”, a funicular railway to the top or if you would like you could climb up the steps for unparalleled views.

    Your touring continues to Kalk Bay, a quaint fishing hamlet on the False Bay. Around lunchtime here, fishermen offload their catch and it is possible to buy your supper straight from the boat. Travel back toward Cape Town, passing through the elegant suburb of Constantia, the cradle of the South African wine industry.

    In the evening stroll through Kirstenbosch, the world-renowned National Botanical Garden. A private guide will show you around this fascinating, romantic garden. Return to your hotel, passing the University of Cape Town, Rhodes Memorial and Mostert's Mill, a Dutch windmill dating back to 1797.

    Overnight: Cape Grace
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    CAPE TOWN–STELLENBOSCH–PAARL–CAPE TOWN–Explore the Winelands

    After Breakfast, explore Cape Town's famous western region, blessed with ideal wine-growing climate, beautiful natural scenery and historic estates - not to mention a bevy of top-notch restaurants.

    Start your gastronomic journey at Stellenbosch. Stellenbosch is undoubtedly the most beautiful and scenic wine land in South Africa, home to some of the finest wines of the world. Visit the top of the line wine estates for a cellar tour and sampling of wine and olive oil (also produced here). Explore this lovely town's history and shops on your own as you meander through the charming streets.

    Enjoy lunch at the critically acclaimed Cape Winelands restaurant Terroir, on the grounds of the Kleine Zalze winery. Dine on a Provençal-inspired chalkboard menu dominated by seasonal ingredients and local wines.

    After lunch, visit Waterford Wine Estate and sample some more award-winning wines complemented by a chocolate tasting.

    End the day at Anura Estate, to sample the perfect couple: award-winning wines and handcrafted Forest Hill cheeses.

    Overnight: Cape Grace
    Meal Plan: B, L

  • Day 5

    CAPE TOWN–HERMANUS–At leisure

    Enjoy a leisurely breakfast this morning.

    Afterward, you are privately driven to Hermanus, a town on the southern coast of the Western Cape, known as the perfect spot for whale watching.

    Check in to your luxury retreat and evening is at leisure or you may explore the wide sprawling beautiful beaches of Hermanus. We suggest you sample some fine cuisine at the sophisticated beach side restaurant like The Rock or The Burgundy.

    Overnight: Birkenhead House
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 6

    HERMANUS–Whale watching

    Spend the morning at leisure, soaking in the beauty of your surroundings with a relaxed breakfast in bed.

    You will later be privately driven to various vantage points for whale watching along with a private guide. Watch the mammals play around and you may watch out for the sea-spray.

    Evening, you will be onboard a private cruise to add to the experience of whale watching and complete the experience with a romantic sundowner.

    Overnight: Birkenhead House
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 7

    HERMANUS–CAPE TOWN–JOHANNESBURG–Chef’s table

    After breakfast, drive a couple of hours overland, returning to Cape Town to catch your flight to Johannesburg.

    An A&K representative will meet and transfer you to your boutique hotel, situated on 10 acres of beautifully landscaped gardens.

    The rest of your day is at leisure to enjoy the hotel's amenities or explore Johannesburg’s shopping destinations at your pace. You may also visit the Africa Craft Market of Rosebank for renowned quality African artifacts and crafts.

    Enjoy a chef’s table for a very special dinner showcasing the freshest ingredients and creative presentation.

    Overnight: Saxon Boutique Hotel & Spa
    Meal Plan: B, D

  • Day 8

    JOHANNESBURG–THORNY BUSH PRIVATE GAME RESERVE–At leisure

    After a romantic breakfast at your hotel, take a short private charter flight to Thorny bush Private Game Reserve, located in the world-famous Kruger National Park.

    Upon arrival, you will be received by an A&K representative and transferred in a private vehicle to Royal Malewane.

    Royal Malewane is a gorgeous safari lodge in the heart of a private game reserve boasting of an amazing species of birds and animals including the Big Five. Your luxurious accommodation offers unparalleled views of the African bush, coupled with warm hospitality and award-winning cuisine.

    Evening is at leisure.

    Overnight: Royal Malewane
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 9

    THORNY BUSH PRIVATE GAME RESERVE–Hot air ballooning and bush walk

    Early morning you leave for your Hot Air Balloon flight & watch the sun rise as you overlook the habitants of the reserve from high up in the sky. Apart from the renowned Big Five the reserve is also home to wild dog, wildebeest and plenty of giraffe. Celebrate your flight on landing with a special champagne breakfast in the bush.

    Afternoon is at leisure or you may visit the Hoedspruit Endangered Species Centre and discover rare species of birds and animals.

    This evening you will be on a guided bush walks with expert guides and trackers. Enjoy the sightings as you discover the reserve’s habitat.

    Rekindle your love over delicious, candlelit dinner and South African wines, and sleep under a blanket of the brilliant stars.

    Overnight: Royal Malewane
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 10

    THORNY BUSH PRIVATE GAME RESERVE–Bush breakfast, spa, game drive and special dinner

    Enjoy a specially planned and sophisticatedly set bush breakfast laid out especially for you. Relish the breakfast in the fresh open air along with the natural beauty and picturesque views.

    Indulge in a luxurious relaxing couple’s spa in a private casita on this last day of your honeymoon. Feel rejuvenated and revitalized in the lodge’s spa offering the best of treatments inspired by indigenous ingredients & amidst the serenity and harmony of the bush.

    Leave for a game drive at dusk. As the sun sets appreciate the beauty of the reserve and nature as you see the animals returning to their resting place for the night - a paradise for the shutter bug in you.

    Return to a specially organised dinner in the wilderness - a graceful setting with burning torches, twinkling stars, traditional African music and a private butler to serve you. A beautiful setting indeed for a romantic dinner with your loved one!

    Overnight: Royal Malewane
    Meal Plan: B, L, D

  • Day 11

    DEPART THORNY BUSH PRIVATE GAME RESERVE

    Bid farewell to this paradise of flora and fauna with a final morning game drive. Fly back to Johannesburg in your private charter & you will be met by an A&K representative and escorted to the international airport for your ongoing flight.

    Meal Plan: B
