Family China & Yangtze

Programme Overview

Visit the Great Wall and ride a toboggan down from the top, walk in the footsteps of emperors in the Forbidden City, visit pandas up close, cruise the Yangtze River and enjoy a lesson in Mandarin.

  • Duration: 12 days/ 11 nights

    Highlights: Beijing | Xian | Chengdu | Shanghai

    A&K advantages

    • Embark on a three-night cruise upon the Yangtze River in a deluxe cabin on board boutique, all-balcony Sanctuary 'Yangzi Explorer,' the finest vessel on the river
    • Cruise with a dedicated Young Explorers Guide
    • Enjoy a noodle-throwing and dumpling-making demonstration for a unique Chef's Table experience
    • Stay at the region's most luxurious hotels, including two on the 2014 Condé Nast Gold List
    • Walk the Mutianyu section of the Great Wall
    • Stop at a pottery factory where you make your own miniature terra cotta soldier on a fun Artisan Visit
    • Ride Like a Local in a pedicab in Beijing through a hutong
    • View giant pandas roaming freely in their natural habitat
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    Arrive Beijing

    On your arrival in Beijing, you will be welcomed by an A&K Representative, who will meet and assist with transfer to the hotel.

    Overnight: Four Seasons Hotel Beijing

  • Day 2

    Beijing

    Today, begin to explore China’s political and cultural hub with a walk or cable car ride to the top of the Great Wall. Hop on a toboggan for an exciting ride back down. Continue to visit the magnificent Beijing National Stadium, also called the “Bird’s Nest”.

    Later, visit the National Aquatics Center, one of the most dramatic and exciting sporting venues constructed for the 2008 Beijing Olympics. After an afternoon at leisure proceed for a cocktail and welcome dinner at the hotel.

    Overnight: Four Seasons Hotel Beijing
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 3

    Beijing

    This day stroll through the culturally significant Tiananmen Square. Next, hop on a pedicab through a hutong, filled with winding alleyways that harbor a fast-vanishing way of life.

    Overnight: Four Seasons Hotel Beijing
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 4

    Xian

    This morning, depart for Xian and enjoy lunch after arrival. Next, set off to view the Ancient Gate of Xian’s City Wall, the most intact ancient Chinese wall in existence, where you can cycle along its ramparts.

    This evening, partake in a noodle-throwing and dumpling-making demonstration at a local restaurant.

    Overnight: Shangri-La Hotel, Xian
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 5

    Chengdu

    Today, marvel at the legendary Terra Cotta Warriors, the most significant archeological excavation of the 20th century. Appreciate the thousands of life-sized ceramic soldiers and horses “guarding” the tomb of China’s first emperor.

    Later, learn the art of pottery to make your own terra cotta soldier. Then, depart for Chengdu, the capital of Sichuan province known as the "Heavenly State" the capital of Sichuan province.

    Overnight: Shangri-La Hotel, Chengdu
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Days 6

    Chengdu

    Start the day with a visit to the Chengdu Panda Breeding and Research Center to observe China’s living national emblem in its natural habitat. Later, visit the Sichuan Opera to view one of China's oldest and most popular opera forms. Attend a mystifying face-changing performance. Then, visit the green room to meet and interact with the actors, learn their art of make-up and view their costumes closely.

    Overnight: Shangri-La Hotel, Chengdu
    Meal plan: B

  • Day 7

    Sanctuary 'Yangzi Explorer’

    Start the exploration today with a transfer to the Dazu Caves, a series of historic Chinese religious culptures and carvings. Get deep insights into the culture by visiting a nearby family home.

    Later, travel to the stunning city of Chongqing. After arrival embark on your luxury river cruiser for a three-night journey through the scenic Three Gorges.

    Overnight: Sanctuary 'Yangzi Explorer'
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 8-9

    Sanctuary 'Yangzi Explorer’

    These two days are scheduled for exploring the shore aboard on the extravagant Sanctuary 'Yangzi Explorer’.

    Relax onboard as the young explorers enjoy age-appropriate fun and learning activities such as crafts, games, calligraphy and Mandarin lessons with a dedicated Young Explorers Guide.

    Overnight: Sanctuary 'Yangzi Explorer'
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 10

    Shanghai

    Enjoy an early lunch before you disembark the cruise in Yichang.

    Later, travel to Shanghai, where you enjoy a Scenic Sundowner with rooftop panoramic views of Shanghai.

    Overnight: The Peninsula Shanghai
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 11

    Shanghai

    Today, explore the magical Bund Sightseeing Tunnel, a voyage full of entertainment. Then, Proceed to the stunning glass Skywalk atop the World Financial Center for extraordinary views of Shanghai.

    Later, enjoy a leisurely walk in the streets of Old Town and continue to visit the Yu Garden, an extensive Chinese garden located in the heart of bustling Shanghai. Later this evening, bid farewell to shanghai with a gala dinner.

    Overnight: The Peninsula Shanghai
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 12

    Depart Shanghai

    After breakfast, you will be assisted with transfer to the airport for your onward flight

    Meal plan: B

Family Galápagos

Programme Overview

Cruise the Galápagos aboard eco-friendly 'Eclipse,' where you walk among giant tortoises, marine iguanas and blue-footed boobies; and share kid-friendly activities led by an A&K Young Explorers Guide.

  • Duration: 10 days/ 9 nights

    Highlights: Guayaquil | The Galápagos

    A&K Advantages

    • Cruise for seven nights in the Galápagos on board 'Eclipse,' a Readers' Choice for Condé Nast Traveler readers, enjoying its fully air-conditioned staterooms and common areas
    • Discover the Galápagos on twice-daily island excursions that include nature walks limited to 12 guests for more interaction with your naturalist guide
    • Kayak wildlife-rich waters or snorkel amid sea lions and fish
    • Learn how the seafood appetizer ceviche is prepared and sample the result during a fun Chef's Table experience
    • Enjoy a wide range of on-board activities, including stargazing, whale watching and an equator-crossing celebration
    • Ride Like a Local when you board a panga to go ashore and explore island coastlines
    • Enjoy VIP lounge access in Baltra and Guayaquil for maximum comfort prior to your departure flights
    • A Children's Activity Coordinator provides a range of child-friendly programs and activities, including wildlife photography, scavenger hunts, Spanish lessons and art-making
    • Enjoy the convenience of earlier seating and kid-friendly menus at meal times
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    Arrive Guayaquil

    On your Arrival in Guayaquil, Ecuador´s largest city and main port, you will be welcomed by an A&K Representative, who will assist you with transfer to the hotel.

    Overnight: Hilton Colón Guayaquil

  • Day 2

    Guayaquil

    Enjoy a private breakfast before you transfer to the airport for your flight to Galápagos. After your arrival, you will be escorted by a naturalist guide to embark on ‘Eclipse’, dazzling with the essence of luxury, for a seven-night cruise. The ‘Eclipse’ is a vessel built for adventure, offering a stylish and relaxed atmosphere. ‘Eclipse’ has been awarded the “Smart Voyager” seal of approval by the Rainforest Alliance, certifying it as eco-friendly and socially responsible.

    Overnight: 'Eclipse'
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 3-8

    The Galápagos

    Today, begin to explore the Galápagos Islands, haven for some of the world’s most varied wildlife and also called a unique ‘living museum and showcase of evolution’.

    Here, you may get the opportunity to view some of the unique and unusual animal life – such as the land iguana, the giant tortoise and many types of finch – that inspired Charles Darwin’s theory of evolution by natural selection following his visit in 1835.

    Because of constant change in the weather, water and wildlife conditions, the islands exact itinerary is not fixed but you will embark on two daily excursions to the main sites and islands. Explore landmarks such as Punta Espinoza on Fernandina Island, El Barranco on Genovesa Island, Pinnacle Rock on Bartolome Island and the Charles Darwin Research Station on Santa Cruz Island.

    Begin your each cruise day with an early breakfast inclusive of specially arranged healthy children’s meal-time menu choices. The sightseeing activities are well planned and at a pace that is right for young explorers.

    Enjoy the morning with a shore excursion with a naturalist guide on a sturdy rubber raft- Panga. View sunbathing sea lions; skittering Sally Lightfoot crabs; tangles of marine iguanas; and amusing blue-footed boobies, as well as penguins, finches, and terns; even a century-old tortoise diligently trudging over the sand.

    Later, return to ‘Eclipse’ for a lunch on-board, followed by time at leisure to nap or socialize on deck. Enjoy the cruise with scavenger hunts and other games specially arranged for young explorers. You may perhaps get the opportunity to sight whales or seabirds, by binoculars. In the afternoon, return to the pangasfor the second excursion of the day, perhaps with an opportunity to swim, kayak or snorkel, where you may meet face to face a water-dwelling inhabitant, such as a curious dolphin or a sea lion.

    Further, return to ‘Eclipse’ in the evening. Later, gather in the dining room for a gala meal. Continue for an evening lecture by one of your guides or learn about the mysteries of the night sky with a fascinating astronomy lecture on deck; or gather for cocktails in the lounge to cap a day of extraordinary discoveries.

    Overnight: 'Eclipse'
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 9

    Guayaquil

    Today, disembark The ‘Eclipse’ and depart for Guayaquil. After arrival, enjoy your lunch at leisure and later visit the Ecua-Andino Panama Hat Factory, where you are welcomed by a refreshing cocktail. Here, observe the fine craftsmanship involved in making this iconic headgear.

    Tonight, share your experiences with fellow guests over the farewell dinner at your hotel’s elegant Portofino Restaurant.

    Overnight: Hilton Colón Guayaquil
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 10

    Depart Guayaquil

    After breakfast, you will be assisted with transfer to the airport for your onward flight.

    Meal plan: B

  • Machu Picchu Post-Tour Extension

    Available on the Jun 12, Jul 17, Aug 7, Dec 18, and Dec 25 departures.

    Day 1

    ARRIVE LIMA

    Arrive in Lima, where an A&K representative will welcome you to the capital of Peru and assist with transfer to the hotel.

    Overnight: Hotel Monasterio
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 2

    Cusco

    Today, explore Sacsayhuaman, one of the most amazing Incan constructions for tourists. Marvel its monumental stones precisely cut and fitted together without mortar.

    Overnight: Hotel Monasterio
    Meal plan: B

  • Day 3

    Machu Picchu

    Begin your day with a beautiful journey to Machu Picchu on a Vistadome train. Explore the place in depth as you journey through this breathtaking Incan citadel.

    Overnight: Inkaterra Machu Picchu Pueblo Hotel
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 4

    Cusco

    This day, you may choose to hike Inti Punku, Huayna Picchu or explore the ruins with your guide to experience the stunning panoramic views.

    Later, travel back to Cusco

    Overnight: Hotel Monasterio
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 5

    Depart LIMA

    Depart for Lima to explore the amazing archaeological treasures and cultural heritage of the place.

    Begin the city tour with a visit to the historic Plaza Mayor and the majestic Cathedral. Later, see the extraordinary Presidential Palace and the famed Larco Herrera Museum.

    This evening, you are assisted with transfer to the airport for your onward flight.

    Overnight: Miraflores Park Hotel (day room)
    Meal plan: B

Italy: A Family Journey

Programme Overview

Explore Italy, enjoying activities for all ages: gladiator school in Rome, lunch with a family that produces balsamic vinegar, and a master class in making Venetian carnival masks.

  • Duration: 10 days/ 9 nights

    Highlights: Rome | Siena | Florence | Italy | Venice

    A&K Advantages

    • Learn to fight like a Roman gladiator and engage in mock combat in an exclusive training session
    • Take a pizza-making lesson and enjoy the fruits of your labor
    • Board a train to visit the Cinque Terre villages along the coast
    • Stop in a local gelateria for a taste of delicious Italian ice cream
    • Enjoy a private audience with a "duke" or "duchess" at the Museum of Renaissance Civilization
    • Sit down to lunch with a family that produces celebrated balsamic vinegar and sample this local favorite
    • Attend a master class in mask painting and create your own Venetian carnival mask
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE ROME

    Upon your arrival in Italy’s ancient capital, Rome, you are greeted and escorted to your hotel.

    After enjoying the day at leisure, join your fellow guests in the evening for a festive welcome dinner.

    Overnight: Boscolo Exedra Roma
    Meal plan: D

  • Day 2

    ROME

    Today, explore the Imperial City, starting with the Palatine Hill. Visit the Colosseum, a majestic amphitheater where 50,000 spectators once watched gladiatorial battles.

    Complete the cultural excursion with a visit to a Roman gladiator school, where you take a lesson and engage in a live mock combat experience.

    Overnight: Boscolo Exedra Roma
    Meal plan: B

  • Day 3

    ROME

    Begin your day with a tour of the Vatican City. Visit the Sistine Chapel with its elegant ceiling painted by Michelangelo. Marvel at the magnificent St. Peter’s Basilica, a late renaissance church and one of the largest in the world.

    This evening, enjoy a pizza-making lesson and savor the results for dinner.

    Overnight: Boscolo Exedra Roma
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 4

    SIENA

    This day, you travel to explore the stunning Tuscany, En route visiting Siena and the Piazza del Campo, site of the Palio race featured in the James Bond movie, Quantum of Solace.

    Continue to visit Siena’s Duomo, an Italian cathedral church. Observe the beautiful frescoes full of bright colors and gold details in the stupendous Piccolomini Library.

    Later, Continue to Montecatini Terme, revered for its curative waters since at least the 16th century.

    Overnight: Grand Hotel & La Pace Spa
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 5

    ITALY

    This morning, begin to explore Paris, where you enjoy the stunning sight of the Eiffel Tower. Continue to visit Arc de Triomphe, the most monumental of all triumphal arches and Champs Elysées, probably the most famous avenue in the world. Later, visit the Notre Dame Cathedral, the most famous of the Gothic cathedrals.

    Spend the rest of the day to explore Paris at your own convenience.

    Overnight: Hotel du Louvre
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 6

    PARIS

    Travel today to explore the extraordinary beauty of the Cinque Terre (“Five Lands”), a rugged portion of coast on the Italian Riviera .View the scenic villages built along the steep coastal cliffs of Liguria on the Italian Riviera.

    Next, board a train to visit Vernazza, a gorgeous seaside town located on the west coast of Italy consisting of tall, colorful houses and a church set beside a sheltered cove. Continue to the Manarola coast, a fishing village nestled amid terraced vineyards.

    Later, if time permits visit Riomaggiore, a secluded medieval village, before you travel to Florence.

    Overnight: Hotel Brunelleschi
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 7

    FLORENCE

    Today, travel to the Uffizi Gallery to closely observe grandeur Renaissance artworks. Then, visit the Palazzo Vecchio, a symbol of the civic power of Florence for over seven centuries.

    Later, visit the Theatre of the Museum of Renaissance Civilization for a private audience with Renaissance-era royals impersonated by actors – a lively travel moment designed especially for children.

    Finally, visit the Boboli Gardens of the Pitti Palace.

    Overnight: Hotel Brunelleschi
    Meal plan: B

  • Day 8

    VENICE

    Experience the local culinary with a private tasting of balsamic vinegar in an aceitaia (vinegar cellar) at a top local producer. Later, relish a lunch of local specialties with the host’s family .

    Then, set forth for a travel to the charming Venice.

    Overnight: Hotel Palazzo Giovanelli
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 9

    VENICE

    This day, experience one of the most interesting and lovely places in the world. Begin by visiting the famed St. Mark’s Basilica. Continue to the Doge’s Palace, built in a Venetian Gothic style.

    Later, visit the bustling Rialto district before you attend a Venetian Carnival mask-making class, fashioning your own to take home.

    This evening, celebrate with your fellow guests at the farewell dinner.

    Overnight: Hotel Palazzo Giovanelli
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 10

    DEPART VENICE

    After breakfast, you will be assisted with transfer to the airport for your onward flight.

    Meal plan: B

    Aug 6, 2015 - Aug 15, 2015 $5,795

Peru: A Family Journey

Programme Overview

Discover Peru in a kid-friendly way -- explore Machu Picchu; visit a center for alpacas and llamas; kayak on an Andean Lake; meet with local children; and swim with sea lions off Palomino Island.

  • Duration: 9 days/ 8 nights

    Highlights: CUSCO | MACHU| PICCHU| LIMA

    A&K Advantages

    • Rise early to watch the sun rise over spectacular Machu Picchu
    • See alpacas, llamas and other camelids at a center in Awana Kancha, and raft the exciting Urubama River
    • Visit with local Andean people to learn the differences in traditional clothing between one community and the next
    • Learn about 3,800 varieties of potatoes at a local market and enjoy a cooking class on how to make empanadas
    • Swim with sea lions off San Lorenzo Island
    • Stand in awe of the massive stones of Sacsayhuaman
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE LIMA

    Arrive in Lima, where an A&K representative will welcome you to the capital of Peru and assist with transfer to the hotel.

    Overnight: Ramada Costa del Sol Lima Airport

  • Day 2

    CUSCO

    This morning, Fly to Cusco and experience the culture with lunch at a local restaurant offering Novo Andino cuisine. Continue with a drive to the sacred valley of the Incas, Urubamba. Here, you get an opportunity to meet and interact with the members of the camelid family (llamas, alpacas, guanacos and vicuñas) to learn about the fabrics made from their shaggy coats using ancient weaving techniques.

    Later, take a tour with your guide to meet the locals, wearing their colorful traditional clothing and discover the different communities and cultures. Further, is a lesson in the Quechuan Language.

    Transfer to your hotel for dinner with the fellow guests

    Overnight: Aranwa Sacred Valley Hotel & Wellness
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 3

    CUSCO

    Today, travel to visit Huayoccari Hacienda, a private residence to closely observe a collection of Incan artifacts. Further, raft through the Urubamba River through the breathtaking Sacred Valley.

    Enjoy lunch in the gardens of the Kuychi Center, a project supported by A&K Philanthropy to help Niños del Arco Iris (Children of the Rainbow). Also get a chance to learn and play a traditional Andean game.

    Later, visit an intricately designed magnificent church built over Incan ruins in Chincheros. Enjoy a kite flying session before you head forward to a Pachamanca Dinner, part of Peru culture for centuries now. End your day stargazing beneath the sparkling skies.

    Overnight: Aranwa Sacred Valley Hotel & Wellness
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 4

    MACHU PICCHU

    Begin your day with a beautiful journey to Machu Picchu on a Vistadome train. Explore the place in depth with an expert guide, who will guide you through this breathtaking Incan citadel.

    After discovering the mystical place, enjoy lunch at Machu Picchu Sanctuary Lodge Hotel.

    This evening, enjoy dinner at the hotel.

    Overnight: Inkaterra Machu Picchu Pueblo Hotel
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 5

    MACHU PICCHU

    Today, Awaken to sunrise and take a stroll through Machu Picchu to witness the beautiful sunrise views.

    You may also choose to hike Inti Punku, Sun Gate, or Huayna Picchu with your guide to experience the stunning panoramic views.

    Enjoy your lunch and head for a return travel to Cusco.

    Overnight: Aranwa Cusco Boutique Hotel
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 6

    CUSCO

    This day, tour Cusco, visiting the Cathedral on the Plaza de Armas and the Santo Domingo Temple. Experience the cultural fusion of the Spanish and the Incan by interacting with the locals at the market of San Pedro. Continue to visit Peru’s famed potato market to learn about the 3,800 potato varieties.

    Later, travel to Sacsayhuaman, one of the most amazing Incan constructions for tourists. Marvel its monumental stones precisely cut and fitted together without mortar.

    This evening, enjoy an Andean music presentation by Tito Leon. Continue for diner at one of the city’s finest local restaurant; also receive a lesson in making empanadas.

    Overnight: Aranwa Cusco Boutique Hotel
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 7

    LIMA

    Today, take a morning flight to Lima. After your arrival you will be assisted with a transfer to your hotel. Begin your exploration with a city tour, starting with the Plaza Mayor. Visit the historic Cathedral and appreciate its architectural intricacies. En-route view the Presidential Palace, Continue to visit the Church of San Francisco.

    After the historic exploration, experience the modern Lima .Tour the fascinating Larco Herrera Museum to view an extensive private collection of pre-Inca ceramics ,one of the largest collection of its kind in the world!

    Overnight: Casa Andina Private Collection Miraflores
    Meal plan: B

  • Day 8

    LIMA

    Begin your day with a transfer to Palomino Island, a huge sea lion rookery. Jump into the water with life vests and wet suits and enjoy the pristine beauty.

    This evening, discover an extraordinary archaeological site. Later, enjoy a farewell dinner comprising of the contemporary Peruvian cuisine at nearby Huaca Pucllana Restaurant.

    Overnight: Casa Andina Private Collection Miraflores
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 9

    DEPART LIMA

    After breakfast, transfer to the airport for your onward flight.

    Meal plan: B
