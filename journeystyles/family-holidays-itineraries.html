Australia & New Zealand: A Family Journey

Programme Overview

Explore Australia and New Zealand, enjoying fun activities for all ages: learn to surf at Bondi Beach, swim or snorkel the Great Barrier Reef, see an endangered kiwi and visit enchanting Hobbiton.

  • Duration: 16 days/ 15 nights

    Highlights: Sydney | Melbourne | Uluru | Queensland | Cairns| Queenstown | Rotorua | Auckland

  • A&K advantages

    • On Phillip Island, observe little penguins waddling out of the surf and koalas dozing in the sun, in a beautiful, natural setting
    • Marvel at the beauty of the Great Barrier Reef
    • Learn to surf along the golden sands of iconic Bondi Beach
    • Journey to the real Middle Earth with a tour of Hobbiton
    • Crew an America's Cup yacht on Waitemata Harbour

  • Day 1

    ARRIVE MELBOURNE

    On your Arrival in Melbourne, you will be welcomed by an A&K Representative, who assist you with transfer to the hotel.

    Gather tonight for a reception and welcome dinner.

    Overnight: Grand Hyatt or Similar
    Meal plan: D

  • Day 2

    MELBOURNE

    Begin exploring Melbourne today, visit St. Paul’s Cathedral and the Royal Botanic Gardens, world-renowned scientific center for the study of plants, their diversity and conservation.

    Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant and proceed to Phillips Island, where you sight little penguins coming ashore and koalas, living emblems of Australia, dozing in the sun

    Tonight, relish a meal of seafood delicacies before you travel back to Melbourne.

    Overnight: Grand Hyatt or Similar
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 3

    ULURU

    Take a short flight this day via Alice Springs to Uluru for a night in the heart of the Outback. Spend time at the Iconic Uluru to explore its famed surface alive with the colors of the setting sun, perhaps to form your own connection.

    Tonight, enjoy gourmet dining under a desert sky at the charming Arnguli Grill

    Overnight: Desert Gardens Hotel or Similar
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 4

    CAIRNS

    Take a walk through Uluru along with an indigenous guide and learn to throw a boomerang and spear

    Later fly to Cairns and on arrival you will be assisted with transfer to your hotel for a dinner with your fellow guests.

    Overnight: Shangri-La Cairns or Similar
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 5

    CAIRNS

    Today, embark the historic Kuranda Scenic Railway for an exciting ride through a tropical rainforest paradise framed by rugged mountains with waterfalls,

    Later, glide just above the canopy on the Skyrail Rainforest Cableway to enjoy the scenic panoramic views.

    Overnight: Shangri-La Cairns or Similar
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 6

    CAIRNS

    Aboard a catamaran proceed to Moore Reef and transfer to a pontoon for a relaxing afternoon cruise across the Great Barrier Reef. Enjoy a seafood buffet lunch, a marine life touch tank.

    Later, get the opportunity to snorkel, swim and enjoy presentations by reef naturalists at an underwater theater.

    Overnight: Shangri-La Cairns or Similar
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 7 & 8

    SYDNEY

    Depart from the Coral Sea and take off for Sydney. Check into your hotel, which is strategically located near shopping-hubs, parks and museums. In the morning, receive a lesson in surfing at famed Bondi Beach, on an all-ages outing.

    Overnight: Sofitel Wentworth Sydney or Similar
    Meal plan: B, D (Day7), B, L (Day8)

  • Day 9

    SYDNEY

    This day, explore the world's most beautiful harbor with a Sydney Harbour cruise.

    Overnight: Sofitel Wentworth Sydney or Similar
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 10

    QUEENSTOWN

    This day, you take a flight to the next stunning destination, Queenstown, New Zealand.

    On arrival you will be assisted with transfer to your hotel.

    Overnight: Crowne Plaza Queenstown or Similar
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 11

    QUEENSTOWN

    Today, after your arrival in Glenorchy, indulge in a truly unique adventure on a “funyak” safari.

    Later, enjoy lunch in the spectacular Mt. Aspiring National Park, a wonderful mixture of remote wilderness, high mountains and beautiful river valleys

    Overnight: Crowne Plaza Queenstown or Similar
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 12

    QUEENSTOWN

    This day, explore Queenstown at leisure with your family.

    Overnight: Crowne Plaza Queenstown or Similar
    Meal plan: B

  • Day 13

    ROTORUA

    Fly to Rotorua, En-route visiting the little village of Ohinemutu, for a private tour led by a local Maori guide.

    Later, visit Rotorua Museum of art and history. Proceed to your hotel for a Maori kapa haka (performing arts) concert and Hangi feast.

    You will also have the opportunity to meet New Zealand’s endangered national emblem at Kiwi Encounter, an organization supported by A&K Philanthropy.

    Overnight: Millenium Hotel Rotorua or Similar
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 14

    AUCKLAND

    Travel to Matamata and enjoy lunch at a local dairy farm. Proceed for an experience of the real Middle Earth on a tour of the Hobbiton movie set, inspired by “The Lord of the Rings” trilogy.

    Overnight: Stamford Plaza Auckland or Similar
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 15

    AUCKLAND

    Take a private guided tour of the “Scars on the Heart” exhibit at the Auckland Museum, which honors the contributions and sacrifices made by New Zealand during the two world wars.

    Later, join the crew of an actual America’s Cup yacht for an unforgettable experience on Waitemata Harbour.

    This evening enjoy a specially organized farewell dinner of regional cuisines

    Overnight: Stamford Plaza Auckland or Similar
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 16

    DEPART AUCKLAND

    After breakfast, you will be assisted with transfer to the airport for your departure flight.

    Overnight: Crowne Plaza Queenstown or Similar
    Meal plan: B

Family Alaska

Programme Overview

Share a private wildlife rafting trip, watch bears fish for salmon, learn about Alaskan huskies from a four-time Iditarod champion, and land on a glacier during a flight over Denali National Park.

  • Duration: 8 days/ 7 nights

    Highlights: Anchorage | Talkeetna

  • A&K Advantages

    • Ride Like a Local aboard the Alaska Railroad GoldStar Dome train from Seward to Girdwood
    • Visit the Alaska Wildlife Conservation Center and take a float trip through Kenai National Wildlife Refuge accompanied by a Youth Explorers Guide
    • Embark on a private fishing trip on the Susitna River
    • Flight-see over Denali National Park, landing on a glacier at the base of Mount McKinley
    • Learn about Alaskan huskies and dog sledding with Martin Buser, a four-time Iditarod champion
    • Walk with your guide through the arts-oriented shops in Talkeetna on a uniquely Alaskan Market Visit

  • Day 1

    Arrive Anchorage

    After your arrival in Anchorage, transfer to Girdwood, carved out by glaciers into one of the most beautiful cities in Alaska.

    Tonight, enjoy the hospitality with a warm welcome followed by a dinner.

    Overnight: Hotel Alyeska or similar
    Meal plan: D

  • Day 2

    Anchorage

    Today, travel to the Kenai River, a heavily-fished river. En-route explore the awe-inspiring Alaska Wildlife Conservation Center, the perfect place for a wildlife adventure. This nonprofit organization rehabilitates injured or orphaned animals and returns them to their natural habitat. Look out for sights of bears, moose, bison, elk and a variety of birds.

    This afternoon, embark on a float trip through Kenai National Wildlife Refuge for breathtaking views and an unforgettable experience.

    Tonight, savor a barbecue dinner, followed by a campfire and s’mores roast.

    Overnight: Hotel Alyeska or similar
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 3

    Anchorage

    This day, cruise the Kenai Fjords National Park, offering an incredible and impenetrable wilderness. Here, you may catch sight of humpback whales, orcas, puffins and sea lions at close range, and glimpse bald eagles overhead.

    Ride the Alaska Railroad GoldStar Dome train to enjoy spectacular views of the countryside from its open-air platform and a glass-covered observation deck.

    Overnight: Hotel Alyeska or similar
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 4

    Talkeetna

    Today, fly by float plane over Cook Inlet to Redoubt Bay Bear Sanctuary, located in remote wilderness in prime Alaska bear-viewing country. Watch the bears safely from your pontoon boat as they snatch salmon from the water - sometimes in mid-leap.

    Further, discover the quaint downtown of Talkeetna. Continue to end your day and relax at the lodge.

    Overnight: Talkeetna Alaskan Lodge or similar
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 5

    Talkeetna

    Start this morning with a visit to Kahiltna Birch Works to see how birch sap comes out of trees and then enjoy a tasting of birch candy, ice cream and mustard. Return to your lodge and set off for a geocaching adventure, a high-tech treasure hunt using handheld GPS devices. Later, enjoy lunch before you head for a fishing session at the Susitna River, its waters teeming with salmon and trout, amid the splendid landscapes.

    Conclude your day with a Scenic Sundowner as you take in spectacular views of the Alaska Range from the relaxing deck of your lodge.

    Overnight: Talkeetna Alaskan Lodge or similar
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 6

    Talkeetna

    Embark on a Kayak trip to explore the Denali area through the tranquil waters of Byers Lake. Further, enjoy hiking on a well-maintained forest trail, where you will be guided along Alaska’s plant and animal life.

    Later, climb aboard a nimble aircraft for an exciting flight over the peaks and glaciers of Denali National Park, guided along by your pilot. After landing on the glacier’s surface, disembark to view splendid snow-veiled peaks stretching in every direction.

    Overnight: Talkeetna Alaskan Lodge or similar
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 7

    Anchorage

    This day, travel to Anchorage, en-route get the opportunity to meet and interact with four-time Iditarod champion Martin Buser and his Alaskan husky team.

    Continue for a tour of Anchorage and later enjoy a sumptuous farewell dinner.

    Overnight: Hotel Captain Cook or similar
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 8

    Depart Anchorage

    After breakfast, transfer to the airport for your onward flight.

    Meal plan: B

Family Costa Rica

Programme Overview

Fly through the rainforest on a thrilling zipline adventure, explore the lagoons of Tortuguero National Park in search of native wildlife, kayak through a flooded forest and view the Arenal volcano.

  • Duration: 7 days/ 6 nights

    Highlights: San Jose | Tortuguero | LA Fortuna

    A&K Advantages

    • See both the Arenal Volcano and the Caribbean coast, as well as the remarkable habitats in between, and travel in comfort between Arenal and Tortuguero by private air charter
    • Walk above the rainforest canopy on suspended bridges, viewing this natural wonder from a unique vantage point
    • Gain firsthand knowledge of Costa Rica's ecological wonders and habitats as you explore with your Young Explorers Guide.
    • Choose from a motorized boat or a sit-on-top kayak to explore the creeks and lagoons of Tortuguero National Park in search of diverse Costa Rican wildlife
    • Stay in outstanding hotels and eco-lodges near Costa Rica's verdant national parks

  • Day 1

    Arrive San José

    Upon your arrival in San José, Costa Rica, you will be met by an A&K representative and assisted with transfer to your hotel in Santa Ana, a beautiful suburb near San José with a charming local ambiance and atmosphere.

    Spend the remaining day at leisure.

    Overnight: Studio Hotel or similar
    Meal plan: None

  • Day 2

    Tortuguero

    Today, travel for an exhilarating excursion to the Atlantic Forest Zipline Adventure, a 1,200-acre nature reserve adjacent to Braulio Carrillo National Park. Fly through the forest canopy to enjoy an enchanting panoramic view.

    Further, you may also opt for a ride on an aerial tram through the rainforest canopy, en-route viewing hanging gardens of plants and animals hidden high in the tree tops.

    In the afternoon, enjoy your lunch and later continue by boat to experience a true paradise at Tortuguero. End the day in your beautiful lodge at Tortuguero.

    Overnight: Tortuga Lodge or similar
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 3

    Tortuguero

    Begin your day early for a guided birding walk to sight a multitude of avian species. Continue to journey through the slow-moving tropical rivers that define Tortuguero National Park, an absolute wildlife haven. En-route view a spectacular array of wildlife in the thick flora, perhaps monkeys, sloths, river otters, caimans, iguanas or poison dart frogs.

    Later, explore the village of San Francisco, which beguiles with natural beauty, vibrant neighborhoods and contagious energy.

    If time permits, you may also form a neighborhood connection by visiting a local school (when in session) or perhaps join local children in a game of soccer.

    Overnight: Tortuga Lodge or similar
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 4

    Tortuguero

    This day, travel northwards by a motorized boat to Caño Palma (Palm Canal) for a thrilling kayak adventure, paddling to enchanting places in the richest biological ecosystem in Costa Rica, led by an expert local guide. According to your preference, you may also opt for the optional motorized boat excursion.

    Later, stop at the Sea Turtle Conservation Museum, where you learn about the Atlantic green sea turtles that thrive on Tortuguero’s shores. Explore the beach to view the thousands of these gentle vegetarians return to nest each year on the same stretch of sand where they were born.

    Enjoy lunch at the lodge and spend the remaining day at leisure to relax or enjoy a garden walk with your family.

    Overnight: Tortuga Lodge or similar
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 5

    La Fortuna

    Today, depart for Arenal. After arrival, drive to Finca Burio (Burio Farm), where you embark on a tour of the plantations to learn the benefits and uses of rare and exquisite fruits.

    Enjoy the sight of vibrant local bird species and later, learn to process sugar cane at a trapiche (sugar mill).

    This afternoon, enjoy a specially organized organic lunch, prepared by the farm owner’s family.

    Later, proceed to your hotel at the base of the impressive Arenal Volcano. Here, appreciate the views of the famous volcano’s near-perfect cone, 5,357 feet above sea level.

    Overnight: Arenal Nayara Hotel & Garden or similar
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 6

    La Fortuna

    This morning, enjoy your breakfast and proceed to the unique and charming Arenal hanging bridges with your private guide. Tour this beautifully designed sight blending with the natural surroundings. As you walk, observe the diversity of flora and wildlife, set against awe-inspiring views of the volcano.

    Continue to visit the Hidalgo family’s hot springs, a less-populated alternative to the overcrowded springs found elsewhere in the area. Tucked nearly a mile off the main road between Lake Arenal Dam and La Fortuna, the springs consist of five free-flowing pools amid a natural river gorge.

    Afterward, refresh yourself with refreshing smoothies and cocktails made with delicious local ingredients.

    This evening, enjoy an authentic home-style meal for your farewell dinner at the on-site restaurant.

    Overnight: Arenal Nayara Hotel & Garden or similar
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 7

    Depart San Jose

    Today, travel back to San José. Later, you are assisted with transfer to the airport for your onward flight.

    Meal plan: B

Family Kenya & Tanzania Safari

Programme Overview

On this Family safari, a Young Explorers Guide leads the way for children with a customized program of activities including games, catching butterflies, mask-making and kite-making.

  • Duration: 12 days/ 11 nights

    Highlights: Nairobi Amboseli National Park | Lake Manyara National Park | Ngorongoro Crater | Serengeti National Park | Masai Mara Game Reserve

    A&K Advantages

    • Travel with a Young Explorers Guide, who provides age-appropriate activities throughout the journey
    • Embark on a family treasure hunt through a local market in the Ngorongoro Highlands
    • Visit a Maasai village where you meet with a village elder who shares more about his traditional, nomadic people and watch a demonstration of traditional bead work
    • Children may participate in a special game drive during their stay in the Serengeti
    • Meet the chef in his kitchen at Ngorongoro Serena Safari Lodge for a demonstration on cooking local dishes

  • Day 1

    Arrive Nairobi

    Upon arrival in Nairobi, you are met by your A&K representative and assisted with transfer to your hotel.

    Depending on arrival time, meet your Young Explorers Guide, who may share Swahili, the local language, lessons.

    Overnight: Nairobi Serena Hotel or similar
    Meal plan: None

  • Day 2

    Amboseli National Park

    Begin your exploration with an overland journey to Kenya’s Amboseli National Park at the foot of snow-capped Mount Kilimanjaro, Africa’s highest mountain and home of the nomadic, vibrantly dressed Maasai tribe.

    Join a safari briefing before you begin to explore the acacia woodland, freshwater swamps and open plains in search of wildlife.

    This evening, join your fellow guests for a welcome dinner.

    Overnight: Amboseli Serena Safari Lodge or similar
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 3

    Amboseli National Park

    The morning and afternoon game drives today, give you an opportunity to closely observe the lions, elephants, leopards, cheetahs and a prolific bird population. View the beautiful mirages formed in the dried lakebed at the reserve. The young explorers enjoy specially organized Family-friendly conservation talks and swimming, led by the Young Explorers Guide.

    Overnight: Amboseli Serena Safari Lodge or similar
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 4

    Lake Manyara National Park

    Travel by road to Arusha, crossing the border of Tanzania and stopping at the colorful Mto wa Mbu market, where children can participate in a treasure hunt. Continue to your next lodge, positioned 2,000 feet above the atmospheric Great Rift Valley and freshwater Lake Manyara. Families may set off on a mountain biking adventure. Later, children may participate in kite flying, crafts or a swimming competition at the lodge pool.

    Overnight: Lake Manyara Serena Safari Lodge or similar
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 5

    Ngorongoro Crater

    This morning, embark on a game drive in Lake Manyara, a scenic gem, on a lookout for nonchalant baboons, large herds of buffalo and zebra, and even storied tree-climbing lions.

    This afternoon, enjoy your lunch and proceed to explore the magnificent Ngorongoro Crater, a UNESCO World Heritage site, an extinct volcano and the largest caldera in the world. The crater today is a bucolic sanctuary supporting a wide array of wildlife including lions, giraffes and the elusive black rhino.

    Later, you may also enjoy a family experience, where you learn how local dishes are prepared by joining the Chef’s Table in the kitchen.

    Overnight: Ngorongoro Serena Safari Lodge or similar
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 6

    Ngorongoro Crater

    Today, continue with your exploration of this vast land of animals, traversing the prairies for elephants while flamingos paint the shimmering water.

    Afterward, visit a Maasai village, where you experience its culture and witness a demonstration of traditional bead work.

    Further, the children enjoy a special visit to a local school (when in session).

    Overnight: Ngorongoro Serena Safari Lodge or similar
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 7

    Serengeti National Park

    Begin to explore the Serengeti National Park, recently proclaimed a 7th world wide wonder. During the journey take a privately guided tour of the Olduvai Gorge, home to many significant archaeological discoveries. Settle down in your accommodation, which offers commanding views of true safari country.

    Overnight: Serengeti Serena Safari Lodge or similar
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 8

    Serengeti National Park

    This day, embark on a game drive searching for the “Big Five” — lions, leopards, Cape buffalo, elephants and rhinos. Here you may witness wildebeests and zebras in abundance in search of fresh grass during the migration periods. The children may enjoy their specially organized hour-long game drive with their Young Explorers Guide.

    Overnight: Serengeti Serena Safari Lodge or similar
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 9-11

    Masai Mara Game Reserve

    Today, depart for the Masai Mara Game Reserve, Arriving at one of the best known and most popular reserves in the whole of Africa, rich in plains game and houses a prolific population of predators.

    Further, drive through the famed reserve overseen by the Maasai. After arrival, settle in your luxurious tented accommodations.

    The next two days are for you to explore this wonderful region, bestowed with over 500 square miles of savannas, woodlands and rivers. Between game drives, join the Young Explorers Guide for tree and bird identification, bush treasure hunts and fire-making techniques. You may also pay a visit to a local primary school, which is supported by A&K Philanthropy.

    Enjoy the sunset as you raise a toast with family-friendly “mocktails” on the Scenic Sundowner. Celebrate the last night with a special farewell dinner.

    Overnight: Mara Intrepids or similar
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 12

    Depart Nairobi

    This day, you travel back to Nairobi, where you may relax in your dayroom before you transfer to the airport for your flight home.

    Overnight: Nairobi Serena Hotel (dayroom) or similar
    Meal plan: B
