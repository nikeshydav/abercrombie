Elegance in the Air: The Private Jet Experience

Privileged Access

Private jet travel allows you to experience far more than you can on a conventional itinerary - without ever sacrificing comfort and convenience. Our private jet can land where the large airlines cannot, and a small group size allows us to pack your itinerary with exclusive, invitation-only events.

Expert Guidance: Your A&K Tour Staff

Leading your journey is a Tour Director who oversees the itinerary from start to finish, relying on a lifetime's worth of knowledge to show you the sites only a local knows. A Tour Manager keeps everything running smoothly, while local guides lend additional insight throughout the journey.

Luxury in the Skies

Our private jets provide relaxing accommodation for you and your small party. Comfortable business-class seats let you relax between destinations. A bar stocked with premium brands (along with a well-chosen wine list) provides the finishing touch to an elegant onboard experience.

A&K Exclusive: Travelling Bell Boy

With A&K's exclusive Travelling Bell Boy service, you simply leave your packed bags behind in your room as you walk out the door, and find them waiting inside your room at your next accommodation.

One-of-a-Kind Accommodations

Private jet journeys feature hotels, resorts and lodges chosen by our experts for their authentic reflection of each destination's style and spirit. With our small group size, we can often secure exclusive access to these properties, making your journey that much more intimate and rewarding.

Fine Dining & Entertainment

On an A&K private jet journey, meals are more than just a chance to dine at the end of the day - they are a complete feast for the senses, with local entertainment and stunning natural backdrops that turn a meal into an event.
