Suggested Ecuador Accommodation

  • ATHALA II

    The luxury Catamaran Athala II was specifically designed and built to cruise the Galapagos Islands in style. The elegant design provides more comfort, superior speed, excellent stability and meets all the latest safety standards.

    The Athala is unrivalled in offering the generous, comfortable accommodation and amenities of a larger boat while still preserving the relaxed exclusivity of a smaller vessel. All cabins on board are of a luxurious standard following a new refurbishment from its new owners. Each cabin has a well-appointed private bathroom, water, air conditioning and wall to wall carpeting. All have ocean views with large portrait windows. This spacious catamaran accommodates 16 guests in 8 spacious cabins, four of which have private balconies.

    From your comfortable base aboard the Athala II, you will be able to explore the best visitor sites in the islands. There are many opportunities to hike, swim, snorkel, kayak, bird-watch and take great close-up photographs of the flora and fauna. The M/V Athala II anchors off-shore at two visitor sites per day. Passengers are divided into groups of a maximum of twelve people, each with its own naturalist guide. All guides are certified Class II or III naturalists and speak fluent English. On the islands you will follow marked trails, walking at a leisurely pace, with your guide explaining the unusual sights you see. You will spend 3 to 4 hours at each site, allowing plenty of time to explore the abundant wildlife. The snorkelling schedule allows several immersions during the cruise. The use of snorkelling equipment and wetsuits is complimentary. Most of the snorkelling activities take place before or after island excursions, so that you will not miss the hikes.

    GALAPAGOS GRAND ODYSSEY

    This new luxury cruise vessel has been introduced to Galapagos waters as of spring 2011. With an exclusive small yacht feel and a combination of luxury, privacy and comfort, the Grand Odyssey offers stylish accommodation for up to 16 passengers and is also ideal for chartering the whole vessel for families or groups of friends wanting an exclusive yacht from which to explore the islands.

    It features eight spacious Galapagos & matrimonial suites and one Odyssey Suite, all with large panoramic windows to enjoy the wonderful landscapes of the islands, private bathroom and air conditioning, decorated in elegantly understated neutral tones. There are also connected suites for families. Take in the fabulous sunsets, relax in one of two jacuzzis or dine on gourmet cuisine alfresco from the splendid shaded sundecks. There is also a small spa and a beautiful lounge with library and indoor dining. The ship has one dedicated naturalist guide who is certified and speaks fluent English.

    GALAPAGOS SAFARI CAMP

    This luxury tented camp emulates the African safari tradition and is a pioneering project in Latin America. Guests enjoy total comfort under canvas while experiencing nature in its purest form. The Galapagos Safari Camp borders the National Park in the highlands of Santa Cruz Island and is close to the giant tortoise reserve.

    The camps' nine spacious tents are constructed on wooden platforms and all have a large balcony with a hammock and ensuite bathrooms with hot power showers. There is also a beautiful central lodge perched on the hill with panoramic views from its spacious veranda. Inside is a lounge and bar with cosy fireplace and a dining room where fresh home-grown and home-cooked dishes are served.

    VILLA ESCALESIA

    Villa Escalesia offers spectacular, exclusive accommodation overlooking Academy Bay and Santa Fe Island. It is an ideal base for day trips to explore the islands by private yacht or simply enjoy the dazzling views from the sundeck and unwind in the infinity pool.

    In terms of architecture, the villa is a superb, modern take on familiar themes of exposed wood and rough hewn stone - but all coming together to create, stunning, slick accommodation. With superb service from your chef and concierge, guests can enjoy fine dining under the stars on the villa's roof terrace.

    The villas wonderful interiors incorporate three large bedrooms and bathrooms, along with kitchen, dining and living spaces. A self-contained suite provides accommodation for four people.
