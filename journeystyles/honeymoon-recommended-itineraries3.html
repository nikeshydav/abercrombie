FINE FOOD AND ART OF BERLIN

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 4 Days

    A luxurious 4 day tour of Berlin, capturing the main sights of this beautiful and historic city. From horse drawn carriage rides and food and arts tours to historic museums, this tour includes something special for everyone. You will also be treated to a complimentary upgrade*, free international phone calls and complimentary Wi-Fi at your hotel.

    *offer subject to availability
    Highlights: Brandenburg Gate, Kaiser Wilhelm Memorial Church, Charlottenburg Palace, Museum Island, Potsdamer Platz, Marheineke Halle, Tachles, Hackesche Hofe

  • Day 1

    ARRIVE BERLIN

    Upon arrival at Berlin airport you will be met in the arrivals hall by your driver and privately transferred to your hotel. Located on the historic Pariser Platz by Berlin's most famous sight, the Brandenburg Gate, and near to the elegant shops & boutiques of Friedrichstrasse and Museum Island, the recently redeveloped Hotel Adlon is the Grande Dame of Berlin hotels. The Adlon Kempinski also offers a wonderful spa with heated indoor pool, Jacuzzi, saunas, steam baths and a whole menu of different treatments to add that special touch to your honeymoon.

    During your stay you will be treated to a complimentary upgrade (subject to availability), free international phone calls so you can easily contact your loved ones back home, and complimentary Wi-Fi.

    overnight: Hotel Adlon Kempinski Berlin

  • Day 2

    BERLIN

    Today you will enjoy a half-day overview sightseeing tour (4 hours) with your private vehicle and local English-speaking guide giving you a wonderful introduction to the sights of Berlin. Begin your tour at the newly created Potsdamer Platz, dubbed "Europe's largest construction site" during the mid 1990s and see the nearby Checkpoint Charlie, the famous crossing point between East and West Berlin. Continue to the Kaiser Wilhelm Memorial Church, whose ruin stands as a dramatic reminder of World War II.

    Before you return back to your hotel enjoy a romantic horse drawn carriage ride, sipping a glass of champagne as you are carried along Unter den Linden, see the Gendarmenmarkt and return to Brandenburg Gate through Berlin's most beautiful alleys. (Warm blankets will be provided depending on the weather)

    This afternoon is at your leisure to relax and enjoy the facilities of your hotel.

    Alternatively you may like to visit the Charlottenburg Palace. Dating from 1695, the Palace was restored to its original elegance following World War II. The vast and beautiful palace is now home to the most richly decorated interiors and artifacts in Berlin.

    As another option you may enjoy a short walk to the Museum Island, for a visit to one of the world's greatest museums; the Pergamon Museum which houses a vast collection of ancient artifacts and antiquities. You may also like to visit Berlin Cathedral with its vast dome before walking back to your hotel.

    Overnight: Adlon Kempinski Berlin
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    BERLIN

    Today enjoy a full day with a guide and driver (8 hours) on a food & art tour of Berlin. Start with a visit to the Turkish market in Kreuzberg on the banks of the canal (Tuesday + Friday only).

    Alternatively, visit the Marheineke Halle, an indoor, organic food market also in Kreuzberg which sells flowers, meat, fruit and veg as well as typical Berlin snacks (open Monday - Saturday) You will also take a trip to the Bonbon Macherei, a traditional sweet making shop, in the Heckmann Höfe - one of the hidden courtyards in the Mitte district.

    Finally, pay a visit to 'KaDeWe' or Kaufhaus des Westens, a prestigious department store in West Berlin that was opened in 1907. KaDeWe has a mouthwatering food court on the 6th floor with a selection of food and drink to rival Harrods in London. During your tour your guide will treat you to some delicious snacks along the way, such as a traditional Berlin currywurst (the best of these are found in Kreuzberg apparently), Berliner Weiße (beer with red or green syrup) as well as some patisserie or petit four from kadewe.

    This afternoon you may like to pay a visit to Tacheles, a wacky, colourful artists residence which houses a number of quirky modern works of art. The building is a former department store in the Jewish quarter (Scheunenviertel) of Berlin. It served as a Nazi prison during the Second World War and recently it was taken over by artists who called it Tacheles which is Yiddish for "straight talking." The building is a popular place for visitors, however the lease with the property owner expired at the end of 2008 and the future of the art house is now uncertain.

    Finally, visit the 'Hackesche Höfe,' one of the most famous and attractive of Berlin's interconnecting courtyards in the Mitte district. Formerly a rather neglected area, Hackescher Markt has developed into a cultural and commercial centre since the German reunification and has become famous for its nightlife. Return to your hotel from here.

    Overnight: Hotel Adlon Kempinski Berlin
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    DEPART BERLIN

    Today you will be transferred to the Berlin airport by private vehicle to board your flight.

    Meal Plan: B

TUSCANY FINE WINE AND DINING

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 4 days

    A romantic 4 day break to the stunning Italian region of Tuscany. Relax and enjoy the luxurious spa facilities and indulge in a wine and dine experience.

    SPECIAL OFFER - Stay for an additional night at no extra charge throughout 2013.

    Highlights: Florence, Chianti, Tavarnelle, Passignano

  • Day 1

    ARRIVE TUSCANY

    You private chauffeur will drive you from Florence Airport to your hotel in the heart of the stunning Tuscan wine producing region of Chianti.

    The ideal choice for both relaxation and exploration, the luxury sanctuary Castello Del Nero is located within striking distance of Florence and Siena. The carefully restored 12th century castle offers the absolute latest in modern facilities whilst maintaining the atmosphere of its historic surroundings.

    Overnight: Castello del Nero

  • Day 2

    TUSCANY

    Today is at your leisure to enjoy the facilities of your hotel and the archetypal Tuscan village of Tavarnelle, just a short walk away. Your hotel is located in acres of secluded grounds and boasts a large outdoor pool, extensive ESPA signature treatments, thermal suites and a fitness centre as well as two floodlit tennis courts and a heated swimming pool. Other activities include trekking, hot air ballooning and golf.

    Overnight: Castello del Nero
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    TUSCANY - WINE AND DINE EXPERIENCE

    Your chauffeur will bring you into the heart of Chianti today and the fairy-tale location of Passignano. The finest of the renowned noble wines of the Antinori family are stored away in ancient cellars underneath the badia (monastery) which is entirely closed to the public - except for you!

    Enjoy an exclusive tour of the cellars followed by Michelin starred lunchtime dining with delicious accompanying fine wines from the Antinori cellar. The sommelier will be happy to share his in-depth knowledge with you. Fully satiated, your chauffeur will bring you back to the paradise of your hotel.

    Overnight: Castello del Nero
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    DEPART TUSCANY

    Your chauffeur will bring you back in style to Florence Airport today.

    Meal Plan: B

South Africa: The Six-Carat Safari (2012)

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 10 Days

    Rekindle the spark of romance in South Africa, land of exotic wildlife, renowned cuisine and lively culture. Enjoy cellar and vineyard tours at top South African wineries, cast your eyes upon seas as endless as your love in quaint Hermanus and observe the region's wildlife from up close on a safari drives led by a private guide in the unspoiled Thorny bush Private Game Reserve.

  • Day 1

    India/Cape Town

    Arrive at Cape Town International Airport, where an Abercrombie & Kent representative will meet and transfer you to your hotel. The remainder of the afternoon is at leisure to settle in to your hotel or perhaps explore Cape Town, a city set on a soaring, rocky peninsula punctuated by lush valleys.

    Overnight: Cape Grace
    Meal Plan: None

  • Day 2

    Cape Town

    This morning, meet an A&K representative for a tour of charming Cape Town. Drive though the suburb of Tamboerskloof ("Drummers' Ravine") to the lower cable car station on the slopes of Table Mountain. Weather permitting; ascend by cable car to the top of this famous mountain, which affords magnificent views of the sea and the city from its summit. Then head to historic Cape Town, starting with a tranquil walk through the lovely Company Gardens, the remnants of the Dutch East India Company's fruit and vegetable gardens. Stroll past the Houses of Parliament, the Tuynhuis ("Townhouse," or Cape Town's "White House"), the National Art Gallery and the National Library. Then continue to the 17th-century Castle of Good Hope. End your explorations at the bustling Victoria & Alfred Waterfront, a working harbor surrounded by eclectic shops and restaurants. This is the perfect setting to arrange for a romantic waterfront dinner.

    Overnight: Cape Grace
    Meal Plan: B,L

  • Day 3

    Cape Town

    After breakfast, set off for a day of discoveries en route to Cape Point. Drive past the "bikini beaches" of Clifton and Camps Bay, located at the base of the towering Twelve Apostles Mountains. Continue to the Cape of Good Hope Nature Reserve at the end of the Cape Peninsula. The reserve converges in the south at Cape Point, a great pinnacle of rock. Climb the steps or ride a funicular railway - the "Flying Dutchman" - to the top for unparalleled views poised for contemplating with your loved one: southern right whale in spring and dolphin and a variety of seabirds in early summer. The journey continues to Kalk Bay, a quaint fishing hamlet on the False Bay. Around lunchtime here, fishermen offload their catch and it is possible to buy your supper straight from the boat. Travel back toward Cape Town, passing through the elegant suburb of Constantia, the cradle of the South African wine industry. Stop at Kirstenbosch, the world-renowned National Botanical Garden, where a private guide shows you around this fascinating, romantic garden. Return to your hotel, passing the University of Cape Town, Rhodes Memorial and Mostert's Mill, a Dutch windmill dating back to 1797.

    Overnight: Cape Grace
    Meal Plan: B,L

  • Day 4

    Cape Town/Cape Winelands/Cape Town

    Today you explore the Cape Town's famous culinary scene. The Western Cape region is blessed with ideal wine-growing climate, beautiful natural scenery and historic estates - not to mention a bevy of top-notch restaurants. Start your gastronomic journey at Morgenster Estate for a cellar tour and sampling of wine and olive oil (also produced here). Enjoy lunch at the critically acclaimed Cape Winelands restaurant Terroir, on the grounds of the Kleine Zalze winery. Dine on a Provençal-inspired chalkboard menu dominated by seasonal ingredients and local wines. After lunch, visit Waterford Wine Estate, where you sample award-winning wines complemented by a chocolate tasting. Continue to picturesque Stellenbosch, the second oldest town in South Africa. Explore this lovely town's history and shops on your own as you meander its charming streets hand in hand. End the day at Anura Estate, to sample the perfect couple: award-winning wines and handcrafted Forest Hill cheeses.

    Overnight: Cape Grace
    Meal Plan: B,L

  • Day 5

    Cape Town/Hermanus

    Enjoy a leisurely breakfast this morning. Afterward, you are driven to Hermanus, a town on the southern coast of the Western Cape, known as the perfect spot for whale watching. Check in to your luxury retreat and evening at leisure.

    Overnight: Birkenhead House
    Meal Plan: B,L

  • Day 6

    Hermanus

    Spend the day at leisure, soaking in the beauty of your surroundings. Choose to sample excellent Cape cuisine at its fine restaurant or walk hand in hand along its secluded, romantic beach, watching for the spray of whales.

    Overnight: Birkenhead House
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 7

    Hermanus/Cape Town/Johannesburg

    After breakfast, drive a couple of hours overland, returning to Cape Town in time to catch your flight to Johannesburg. An A&K representative will meet and transfer you to your boutique hotel, situated on 10 acres of beautifully landscaped gardens. The rest of your day is at leisure to enjoy the hotel's amenities together or explore cosmopolitan Johannesburg on your own.

    Overnight: Saxon Boutique Hotel & Spa
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 8

    Johannesburg/Thorny bush Private Game Reserve

    After a romantic breakfast at your hotel, perhaps overlooking the terrace and lush gardens beyond, take a short flight to Thorny bush Private Game Reserve, located in the world-famous Kruger National Park. Upon arrival, you are picked up and whisked away to Royal Malewane, a beautiful safari lodge in the heart of a private game reserve. Your luxurious accommodations offer unparalleled views of the African bush, coupled with warm hospitality and award-winning cuisine.

    Overnight: Royal Malewane
    Meal Plan: B,L,D

  • Day 9-10

    Thorny bush Private Game Reserve

    Spend two full days on safari in search of the "Big Five" (elephant, rhino, buffalo, lion and leopard). This unspoiled area is also home to wild dog, wildebeest and plenty of giraffe. Set out on morning and evening game drives, as well as guided bush walks with expert guides and trackers. Rekindle your love over delicious, candlelit meals and South African wines, and sleep under a blanket of the brilliant stars.

    Overnight: Royal Malewane
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 11

    Thorny bush Private Game Reserve/Johannesburg/India

    Bid farewell to this paradise of flora and fauna with a final morning game drive. Fly back to Johannesburg, where you are met by an A&K representative and escorted to the international airport for your return flight home.

    Meal Plan: B

Italy: Flavors of Venice, Tuscany & Rome (2012)

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 7 Nights

    Italy captures classic romance like no other place in the world. This journey is a toast to your love as you traverse the canals of Venice in your own gondola, steal a kiss on Juliet's balcony in Verona, savor a private picnic in the heart of an Umbrian vineyard and explore Rome, a city so beautiful it gave romance its name. Fall in love all over again as you live the dolce vita of this sensual, beguiling country.

  • Day 1

    India/Venice

    Begin your romantic adventure by boarding an flight to Venice, one of the world's loveliest cities. On arrival, you are met by an Abercrombie & Kent representative and transferred to your hotel. Speed across the lagoon to your palace hotel, with a convenient location right on the city's Grand Canal. Arrive at the hotel - a 14th-century Venetian palace adorned with masterpieces by the likes of Tiepolo - by private jetty. This evening, take to Venice's famous canals as you cruise past the Santa Maraidella Salute Church and into the lagoon to toast the sunset and your romantic holiday with a glass of Veneto Prosecco. Continue to the beautiful island of Torcello, where a table is reserved for you in the delightful gardens at the LocandaCipriani. Savor one of their specialties: Adriatic fresh oysters.

    Overnight: Ca' Sagredo
    Meal plan: D

  • Day 2

    Venice

    Explore the less-visited side of "La Serenissima" (a fitting nickname for this most serene of Italian cities) alongside your expert local guide today. Lose yourselves in the charming labyrinth of narrow streets and passageways in the old artisans' quarter, where postcard-perfect scenes await around every corner. Wander through the Frari Church, which houses the breathtaking masterpieces of Titian, Bellini and Canova. This evening, your private gondolier rows and serenades you through the Grand Canal and under the Rialto Bridge on to your restaurant of choice.

    Overnight: Ca' Sagredo
    Meal Plan: B,D

  • Day 3

    Venice/Verona

    Travel overland to the Roman city of Verona, the setting for Shakespeare's Romeo and Juliet. The family homes of the famous fictitious lovers are still standing, and today you have the chance for a private moment together on Juliet's balcony, perhaps even the opportunity to renew your wedding vows in this truly special location. Cap a lovely day with front-row seats at an epic opera performance al fresco in the Roman Arena, such as La Traviata (summer only). Tonight, overnight in the luxurious surroundings of a 13th-century palace.

    Overnight: Due Torri
    Meal Plan: B,D

  • Day 4

    Verona/Assisi

    Travel through central Italy and the stunning Tuscan countryside, a land of rolling vineyards and charming villas. Explore the region around Cortona, synonymous with the romantic novel and film Under the Tuscan Sun. Gaze at the tranquil beauty of Tuscany's Val d'Orcia, a spectacular sight to behold with your beloved. Enjoy a leisurely afternoon in medieval Assisi. You may wish to spend time in your hotel's spa, which sits within the 2,000-year-old Roman structure of the building. Your accommodations this evening are within a beautifully renovated convent, with incredible views over the dramatic Umbrian countryside.

    Overnight: Nun Assisi Relais & Spa Museum
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 5

    Assisi

    This morning, you are at leisure to explore the fascinating town of Assisi on your own. In the afternoon, visit a family-run Umbrian vineyard, where a gourmet picnic awaits you amid the serene countryside setting of the vineyard. Dine under the shade of a tree with the medieval city of Assisi as your backdrop. Take your time - your personal waiter will continue to serve estate wines as long as you wish to linger.

    Overnight: Nun Assisi Relais & Spa Museum
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 6

    South Georgia

    Today, travel deeper into the heart of the Umbrian countryside for one of the most memorable meals of your life. Dubbed the world's smallest restaurant, the one-of-a-kind Solo Per Due is dedicated to hosting and serving just you and your partner a dining experience tailored around your tastes and preferences. Your waiter serves on the call of your bell; otherwise, the restaurant and terrace are just for you, offering stunning views through Umbria's olive-tree clad hills. Continue to legendary Rome. Many say that the "Eternal City" is even more stunning as the sun goes down and the cityscape lights up. Alongside your guide and chauffeur, experience Italy's capital at night, touring the beautifully lit Trevi Fountain, Baths of Caracala and ancient Roman Forum before heading up to a lovely villa in the Borghese Gardens for the finale. At the top of the villa, champagne and canapés await you and your loved one to share in private as you gaze from your terrace out into the city that gave romance its name. Your romantic boutique hotel enjoys one of Rome's premier addresses, just across from the stunning Roman Colosseum.

    Overnight: Palazzo Manfredi
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 7

    Rome/India

    After breakfast, transfer to the airport for your departure flight.

    Meal Plan: B

    •  

    OPTIONAL EXTENSION: -SVETI STEFAN

  • Day 1

    Rome/Sveti Stefan

    Fly across the Adriatic Sea to Podgorica, the capital of Montenegro. Upon arrival, you will be met by an A&K representative and transferred to the magical island of Sveti Stefan, one of Europe's newest and most unique luxury destinations. As soon as you arrive, enjoy a relaxing couple's massage in one of the private cottages on the island. The evening is at leisure to explore the island and sample one of its restaurants in the charming Piazatta, with romantic views over the crystal clear waters of the Adriatic. Overnight in your deluxe cottage, or upgrade to a suite, which features a working fireplace for a romantic evening in?

    Overnight: Aman Resort Sveti Stefan
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 2

    Cruising the Adriatic

    Set off out to sea on a romantic private cruise on board your luxury yacht and personal skipper. The day is yours to discuss with your captain, whether your wish is to find a sheltered beach cove to explore, admire the majesty of the Kotor fjord or even cruise up to Dubrovnik to enjoy a romantic lunch within the city walls. Returning to Sveti Stefan in the afternoon, the evening is at leisure, perhaps to dine in nearby medieval Budva.

    Overnight: Aman Resort Sveti Stefan
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 3

    Skadar Lake

    Enjoy the peace and beauty of Skadar Lake National Park this morning. For a true getaway, rent one of the small boats or canoes available to explore the lake on your own. Skadar is a haven for wildlife and nature, with 270 bird species calling the lake their home. In the nearby surrounding region, there are 20 monasteries, churches, villages, fortresses and sacred monuments. Later this afternoon, return to Sveti Stefan, where you can while away the rest of the day in the resort's private beach cove.

    Overnight: Aman Resort Sveti Stefan
    Meal Plan: B

  • Day 4

    Depart Sveti Stefan

    Privately transfer to Podgorica Airport for your onward flight.
