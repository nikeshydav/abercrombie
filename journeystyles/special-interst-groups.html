Luxury Small Group Journeys

For groups of 12 to18 travelers, our Luxury Small Group Journey tours are a cut above the rest. The small and limited group size combined with our extensive and long-established local ties allows us to acquaint our guests with people and places which are far beyond the reach of any other traveler.

If you choose to take a Family Journey, be sure to be accommodated in private, boutique properties or for that matter, even private islands which don't accommodate large groups and ensure more one-on-one interaction with our award-winning Tour Directors.

Connect to your destination and transform your understanding of it as you opt for an A&K's Connections Journey, each journey reflecting a distinctive aspect of the local culture.

Every journey designed by us is carefully crafted, thanks to the insight of a tour leader and a phalanx of local guides which guarantees to take you well beyond the guidebook, for an authentic understanding of a country. A journey for every interest, Abercrombie & Kent's group travel department creates rewarding journeys for any interest, from art and architecture to history and contemporary culture.
