Barges , France

Cruise the Canal du Midi and skirt the sunbathed shores of the Mediterranean before winding inland through ancient villages and Roman fortifications in the Languedoc wine region.

The eight passenger Anjodi was specifically rebuilt to navigate this 300 year old canal. With four air-conditioned en suite bedrooms and a Jacuzzi on the deck, she has been refurbished with African hardwoods, handcrafted panelling and shining brass to create a comfortable and charming ambience.

Relax as you meander along tree lined routes from the lakeside fishing town of Marseillan home to Noilly Pratt Vermouth via Béziers taking the world's oldest canal tunnel at Malpas through the hills. Visit Narbonne, enjoy private wine tastings and discover Cathar history before visiting Carcassonne
