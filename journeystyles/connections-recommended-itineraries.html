Amsterdam, Paris & London

Programme Overview

Cruising through each city's waterways, visit the Anne Frank House in Amsterdam, stroll Montmartre as well as visit the Louvre in Paris. View the Crown Jewels in London before a ride on the London Eye.

  • Duration: 10 Days/ 9 Nights

    Highlights: Amsterdam | Paris | Belgium | London

    A&K Advantages

    • Cruise on the canals and rivers of these three world capitals
    • Sample Dutch pancakes for lunch, a local specialty that makes for a tasty Culinary Connection
    • For a local Market Connection, join your Tour Director for a walk in a charming food market and meet the vendors
    • Join a local artist in Paris who teaches you how to sketch for a lively Artisan Connection
    • Travel in comfort on the Eurostar between Paris and London
    • Take a flight on the London Eye for a spectacular view of the city
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE AMSTERDAM

    Upon your arrival in Amsterdam, you are met and assisted with transfer to your hotel by an A&K representative. The rest of the day is at leisure.

    Tonight, you and your fellow travelers receive a warm welcome followed by dinner.

    Overnight: Sofitel Legend The Grand Amsterdam
    Meal plan: D

  • Day 2

    AMSTERDAM

    Begin your exploration with a walking tour of the beautiful city of Amsterdam. Later, cruise through its canals. Continue to visit The Anne Frank House, a historic house and biographical museum dedicated to Jewish wartime diarist Anne Frank.

    Begin your exploration with a walking tour of the beautiful city of Amsterdam. Later, cruise through its canals. Continue to visit The Anne Frank House, a historic house and biographical museum dedicated to Jewish wartime diarist Anne Frank.

    Overnight: Sofitel Legend The Grand Amsterdam
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 3

    AMSTERDAM

    This morning, tour the Rijksmuseum, one of Amsterdam's grandest museums, to appreciate works by Rembrandt, Vermeer and other Dutch masters.

    The afternoon is at leisure, which you may choose to spend exploring the Van Gogh Museum on your own.

    Overnight: Sofitel Legend The Grand Amsterdam
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 4

    PARIS

    Today, travel to Ghent, Belgium. After your arrival, enjoy lunch at a local restaurant. Explore the place with a short tour before you set off for Paris, the City of Light.

    Overnight: Hotel du Louvre
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 5

    PARIS

    This morning, begin to explore Paris, where you enjoy the stunning sight of the Eiffel Tower. Continue to visit Arc de Triomphe, the most monumental of all triumphal arches and Champs Elysées, probably the most famous avenue in the world. Later, visit the Notre Dame Cathedral, the most famous of the Gothic cathedrals.

    Spend the rest of the day to explore Paris at your own convenience.

    Overnight: Hotel du Louvre
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 6

    PARIS

    Start your day with a visit to the awe-inspiring Louvre, originally a royal palace but now the world's most famous museum. Later, stroll through a local food market to experience the culture of the place.

    The afternoon, unwind at the Tuileries Gardens at leisure.

    Later, board to cruise through the Seine River followed by dinner at a local restaurant.

    Overnight: Hotel du Louvre
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 7

    LONDON

    Today travel the north of Paris to reach the Montmartre District, Paris's most charming and quirky places to wander. Take a leisurely stroll in its winding back streets and later, join a local artist for a sketching lesson.

    Further, board the high-speed Eurostar to London.

    This evening, relish the famous fish and chips for supper at a local restaurant.

    Overnight: St. James Court, A Taj Hotel
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 8

    LONDON

    Visit the famed Tower of London and catch a glimpse of some of the dazzling jewels. Continue to view the Tower Bridge, one of the most recognizable bridges in the world, before visiting St. Paul’s Cathedral.

    Enjoy lunch at leisure after a stroll in the lively Covent Garden. Later, visit Westminster Abbey, a Gothic church and continue to explore the Houses of Parliament and Buckingham Palace.

    Spend some time at the Trafalgar Square, located at the very heart of London, and move further to take a flight on the London Eye for spectacular panoramas of the city.

    Overnight: St. James Court, A Taj Hotel
    Meal plan: B

  • Day 9

    LONDON

    Begin with a morning cruise along the Thames River to Greenwich. Further, explore the sleek Cutty Sark, the world’s sole surviving tea clipper ship displaying a fusion of old and modern art. Continue to view campus of Greenwich University, admired for its architecture.

    Later, board for a return cruise to London or you may choose to spend leisure time in Greenwich on your own.

    Overnight: St. James Court, A Taj Hotel
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 10

    DEPART LONDON

    After breakfast, you are assisted with transfer to the airport for your onward flight.

    Meal plan: B

Australia & New Zealand: Two Worlds of Wonder

Programme Overview

Visit Australia & New Zealand, exploring their nature, culture and stunning sites like the Sydney Opera House and Hobbiton.

  • Duration: 16 Days/15 Nights

    Highlights: Sydney | Melbourne | Uluru | Queensland | Great Barrier Reef | Milford Sound | Queenstown | Rotorua | Auckland

    A&K Advantages

    • Learn about life in the bush with a visit to a family-run sheep ranch
    • Make a Spiritual Connection at Uluru (Ayers Rock), and enjoy an authentic Australian "barbie" and stargazing with an expert
    • Marvel at the beauty of the Great Barrier Reef, with insights from a qualified marine biologist
    • Enjoy a personally guided tour of the Sydney Opera House
    • Journey to the real Middle Earth with a tour of Hobbiton
    • Partake of a traditional Maori Hangi on a Culinary Connection
    • Crew an America's Cup yacht on Waitemata Harbour
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE MELBOURNE

    On your Arrival in Melbourne, you will be welcomed by an A&K Representative, who assist you with transfer to the hotel.

    Gather tonight for a cocktail reception and welcome dinner.

    Overnight: Grand Hyatt or Similar
    Meal plan: L

  • Day 2

    MELBOURNE

    Begin exploring Melbourne today, visit St. Paul’s Cathedral and the Royal Botanic Gardens, world-renowned scientific center for the study of plants, their diversity and conservation. For an exquisite experience proceed to the Thomson family farm and relax over a cup of Australian hot tea while getting to know more about the life on the land in rural Australia. Also, learn about Australia’s most significant industries, sheep-shearing.

    Enjoy your lunch at a nearby organic farm featuring local produce and wine. Take a leisure tour of the property, observing the native flora and fauna and keeping watch for kangaroos.

    Return to Melbourne and rest of the evening is at leisure.

    Overnight: Grand Hyatt or Similar
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 3

    ULURU

    Take a short flight this day via Alice Springs to Uluru for a night in the heart of the Outback. Spend time at the Iconic Uluru to explore its famed surface alive with the colors of the setting sun, perhaps to form your own connection.

    Savor sparkling wine and canapés, followed by an authentic Australian barbecue. This evening, let an expert lead you on a tour of southern stars.

    Overnight: Desert Gardens Hotel or Similar
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 4

    CAIRNS

    Take a walk through Uluru along with an indigenous guide and learn about the legends that feature the monolith and how the traditional owners of the land survived in the arid Outback.

    Later fly to Cairns and on arrival you will be assisted with transfer to your hotel for a dinner with your fellow guests.

    Overnight:Shangri-La Cairns or Similar
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 5

    QUEENSLAND

    This day you travel north to reach Daintree Rainforest, an emerald set in Tropical Far North Queensland. Begin your exploration with a local indigenous guide. Walk around the forest paths trodden for centuries by the Kuku Yalanji people, who have generously granted access to their tribal lands.

    Cruise through the Daintree River to observe wildlife with an expert guide, keeping a lookout for “salties” — Australia’s infamous saltwater crocodiles. Later, take a tour of the Daintree Township while the afternoon is at leisure.

    Overnight: Shangri-La Cairns or Similar
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 6

    GREAT BARRIER REEF

    Aboard a catamaran proceed to Moore Reef via Fitzroy Island and shift to a pontoon for a relaxing afternoon cruise across the Great Barrier Reef. Enjoy a seafood buffet lunch, a marine life touch tank, and opportunities to snorkel, swim and enjoy presentations by reef naturalists at an underwater theater.

    Overnight: Shangri-La Cairns or Similar
    Meal plan:B, L

  • Day 7

    SYDNEY

    Depart from the Coral Sea and take off for Sydney, arriving in the afternoon. Check into your hotel, which is strategically located near shopping-hubs, parks and museums.

    In the evening, enjoy the dinner specially arranged for you at a local restaurant.

    Overnight: Sofitel Wentworth Sydney or Similar
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 8

    SYDNEY

    This morning along with a personal guide take a tour of one of Australia’s iconic sights, the Sydney Opera House.You have the opportunity to go behind the scenes at a venue the UNESCO World Heritage Committee has called “one of the indisputable masterpieces of human creativity.”

    Overnight: Sofitel Wentworth Sydney or Similar
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 9

    SYDNEY

    The morning is at leisure for you to explore Sydney at your own pace.

    Later, board a cruiser on Sydney Harbour for stunning sights of some of the city’s spectacular landmarks, including Harbour Bridge, the Opera House and Luna Park.

    Dinner will be served on board.

    Overnight: Sofitel Wentworth Sydney or Similar
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 10

    QUEENSTOWN

    This day you take a flight to the next stunning destination, Queenstown, New Zealand.

    On arrival you will be assisted with transfer to your hotel. Enjoy the evening dinner at the hotel itself with your fellow guests.

    Overnight: Crowne Plaza Queenstown or Similar
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 11

    QUEENSTOWN

    Admire the crystal clear alpine lakes, lupine-fringed meadows and pristine waterfalls on an enchanting day tour. Sip a cup of tea at Te Anau and enjoy a drive through snow-covered peaks and verdant rainforest to embark on a lunch cruise of spectacularly scenic Milford Sound. Set in a glacier-carved fjord, which boasts of deep blue waters, cascading waterfalls and rare wildlife.

    Overnight: Crowne Plaza Queenstown or Similar
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 12

    QUEENSTOWN

    You will visit the Gibbston Valley Winery, renowned for its Pinot Noirs for a wine cave tour. The cave provides the perfect environment that is conducive for ideal maturation of fine vintages. Proceed to the Kawarau Bridge, made famous by bungee jumpers, before you explore the historic Arrowtown which was the gold-mining settlement.

    You will have the opportunity to take a ride on a Skyline Gondola and appreciate the panoramic scenic views of Lake Wakatipu from Bob’s Peak.

    Overnight: Crowne Plaza Queenstown or Similar
    Meal plan: B

    • Day 13

    ROTORUA

    Fly to Rotorua, visiting the little village of Ohinemutu, for a private tour led by a local Maori guide.

    Later, proceed to Rainbow Springs for a tour of the national carving and weaving schools, followed by a view of bubbling mud pools and steaming geysers.

    This evening, join a local family for a home-cooked dinner and an amazing opportunity to learn what it’s like to be a New Zealander.

    You will also have the opportunity to meet New Zealand’s endangered national emblem at Kiwi Encounter, an organization supported by A&K Philanthropy.

    Overnight: Millenium Hotel Rotorua or Similar
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 14

    AUCKLAND

    Travel to Matamata and enjoy lunch at a local dairy farm. Proceed for an experience the real Middle Earth on a tour of the Hobbiton movie set, inspired by "The Lord of the Rings" trilogy. Learn how the Hobbit holes, Green Dragon Inn and other structures in The Shire were created for the films.

    Move ahead to Auckland to retire for the day.

    Overnight: Stamford Plaza Auckland or Similar
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 15

    AUCKLAND

    Take a private guided tour of the "Scars on the Heart" exhibit at the Auckland Museum, which honors the contributions and sacrifices made by New Zealand during the two world wars.

    Later, join the crew of an actual America’s Cup yacht for an unforgettable adventurous experience of a lifetime. Take the helm, man the grinders or simply sit back and enjoy the action, as you ply the waters of beautiful Waitemata Harbour.

    This evening enjoy a specially organized farewell dinner of regional cuisines

    Overnight: Stamford Plaza Auckland or Similar
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 16

    DEPART AUCKLAND

    After breakfast you will be assisted with a transfer to the airport for your departure flight.

    Meal plan: B

North America: Canadian Rockies

Programme Overview

  • Duration: 8 Days/7 Nights

    Appreciate the scenic beauty around the Emerald Lake, learn the art of nature photography, and experience nature in all its glory.

    A&K Advantages

    • Enjoy a warm Canadian welcome in Calgary with a special ceremony on arrival.
    • Learn the art of photography from a professional photographer at one of the most picturesque and scenic Moraine Lake.
    • Hike Emerald Lake with one of the country's best naturalist guides.
    • Savor a barbecue picnic lunch and enjoy a lecture by an expert park naturalist with Sunwapta Falls in the background.
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE CALGARY

    On arrival you will be greeted by an A&K Representative who will assist with transfer to your hotel. Later, enjoy a welcome dinner along with your fellow guests.

    Overnight: Fairmont Palliser Hotel or similar
    Meal plan:D

  • Day 2

    LAKE LOUISE/PRISTINE WATERS

    Kick start your day with an adventure filled tour in the scenic beauty of the place at the famous lakes of the Canadian Rockies. Take a drive around the Bow Valley to Johnston Canyon, with its sheer walls, waterfalls, tunnels and pools formed by erosion over thousands of years. Take a leisure walk along the beautiful walkway .Then, set off for Moraine Lake, a glacially fed lake in Banff National Park. Next is an opportunity to learn the art of wildlife and landscape photography under the guidance of a professional photographer. Experiment with your newfound skills on one of the most picturesque lakes in the Canadian Rockies. Wrap up the day at your luxury hotel on Lake Louise (5,700 feet).

    Overnight: Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise
    Meal plan: B

  • Day 3

    CALGARY

    Start your day with a serene drive around the Yoho National Park leading to the Emerald Lake, which gets its vibrant color due to the glacial sediment in its waters. Here, you will be led by an expert guide, who reveals the geological and natural factors behind this magnificent lake, and provides a glimpse of the breathtaking natural surroundings and habits of the endangered local grizzly bear.

    After lunch if the weather permits, continue via the old railroad town of Field, stopping by to get a view of the Takakkaw Falls, Spiral Tunnel and Kicking Horse Natural Bridge.

    The remaining day is at leisure to explore Lake Louise (5,700 feet) at your convenience.

    Overnight: Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 4

    JASPER/THROUGH THE ICEFIELDS

    Take a journey into the picturesque Icefields Parkway, along the backbone of the Canadian Rockies. Enjoy a drive along sprawling icefields in the area being one of the world's great drives, with roadside wildlife a common sight and vistas of verdant forests and soaring peaks all around. Take a break at the massive Athabasca Glacier (7,200 feet) for an exhilarating ride across its surface on a specially designed vehicle.

    After the adventure ride, continue to magnificent Jasper National Park via Peyto Lake and Crowfoot Glacier.

    End your day by relaxing at the lodge.

    Overnight: Fairmont Jasper Park Lodge
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 5

    JASPER/CANYON SKYSCRAPERS

    Give your day a beautiful start with a cruise along the mile-high Maligne Lake to the famous Spirit Island, a tiny island surrounded by the towering snow-capped Rockies. You may opt to join your fellow travelers for a guided hike through the Maligne Canyon, with its impressive 200-foot-tall limestone walls.

    After the beautiful experience return to your lodge and spend your remaining day at leisure to experience the natural surroundings at your own pace.

    Overnight: Fairmont Jasper Park Lodge
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 6

    BANFF

    This morning, depart from the Jasper National Park, stopping at Athabasca Falls, one of the most powerful and breathtaking falls in the Rocky Mountains.

    Later visit the lovely Sunwapta Falls, relish a barbecue lunch followed by an information session by a local park naturalist in this beautiful, remote ambience. Set off your journey to Banff National Park - a UNESCO World Heritage Site, established in 1885 as Canada's first national park.

    End the day at your extraordinary hotel, often called the "Castle in the Rockies."

    Overnight: Fairmont Banff Springs Hotel
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 7

    BANFF/NESTLED IN NATURE

    Nestled amongst the peaks of the Canadian Rockies, Banff is known as a traveler’s mecca for a good reason, a national park resort community surrounded by spectacular nature, home to roaming deer, elk and other wildlife. Start your day with an enchanting float trip down the majestic Bow River. During the rest of the day you may perhaps explore the community's restaurants, galleries and unique shops at your own pace. Join your fellow travelers for a gala farewell dinner.

    Overnight: Fairmont Banff Springs Hotel
    Meal plan: B, D

  • Day 8

    DEPART BANFF

    Enjoy the breakfast and you will be assisted with transfer to Munich airport for your onward flight.

    Meal plan: B

    Pricing & group size

    As a guide this 8 day journey in Canada will cost from £3,027 per person based on two adults sharing excluding flights. The maximum group size for this itinerary will be 24.

Along the Danube

Programme Overview

Cruise the Danube through Budapest by night, enjoy an A&K-exclusive visit to Nuremberg and its Imperial Castle, attend a concert in Vienna at the Kursalon and walk the charming streets of Munich.

  • Duration: 10 Days/9 Nights

    Highlights: Budapest | Bratislava | Vienna | Austria | Wachau | Linz Germany,Munich

    A&K Advantages

    • Cruise for seven nights on the Danube River and enjoy one night each in Budapest and Munich in centrally located hotels
    • Explore Budapest on both sides of the river, and cruise beneath the illuminated Chain Bridge, taking in floodlit views of the city
    • Tour a World War II art bunker - an A&K-exclusive experience
    • Enjoy a "Sound of Vienna" concert in the Kursalon
  • Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    ARRIVE BUDAPEST

    On your Arrival in Budapest, you will be met by an A&K representative, who will assist with your transfer to the hotel.

    This evening, join your host and interact with your fellow travelers at a welcome dinner in the evening.

    Overnight: Corinthia Hotel Budapest
    Meal plan: D

  • Day 2

    AMADEUS SILVER II

    Visit a Baroque castle and its enormous botanical gardens in Gödöllö. Enjoy your lunch and head for priority boarding on the Amadeus Silver II.

    This evening, cruise through Budapest, crossing the illuminated Chain Bridge, for floodlit views of the city. Enjoy a refreshing welcome cocktail followed by dinner, Proceed to attend a gypsy band perform on board.

    Overnight: 'Amadeus Silver II' or 'Amadeus Brilliant'
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 3

    AMADEUS SILVER II

    This morning, tour "Budapest", the combination of the cities "Buda" and "Pest" situated on the west bank of the Danube.

    Explore Buda with its cobbled streets and medieval architecture and Pest, with its wide avenues and 19th-century buildings.

    Enjoy your lunch on board before taking an optional excursion to charming Szentendre, a riverside town.

    Overnight: 'Amadeus Silver II' or 'Amadeus Brilliant'
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 4

    AMADEUS SILVER II

    Today, arrive in Bratislava, Slovakia and set off to visit its old quarter. Continue to view St. Martin’s Cathedral, one of the most significant symbols of Bratislava and Grassalkovich Palace, residence of the President of Slovakia.

    Return on board and enjoy an exclusive pre-dinner cocktail party. Later, you may choose for a cruise to Vienna for an evening tour.

    Overnight: 'Amadeus Silver II' or 'Amadeus Brilliant'
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 5

    AMADEUS SILVER II

    Take a drive through the Ringstrasse to discover the Gothic St. Stephen's Cathedral, whose magnificent structure enshrines the heart of Vienna.

    This afternoon, you may choose an optional afternoon excursion to Schönbrunn Palace, former summer residence of the imperial family and one of Europe's most impressive Baroque palace complexes.

    Afterward, attend a “Sound of Vienna” concert (Category A seating) in the splendid Kursalon.

    Overnight: Shangri-La Cairnsor Similar
    Meal plan: B, L

  • Day 6

    AMADEUS SILVER II

    This day, experience Dürnstein, a winegrowing region situated in the heart of the magnificent Wachau with an optional city walk. Here, enjoy tasting the local vintages. Continue for an optional excursion to visit Melk Abbey, one of the biggest and most beautiful European Baroque Ensembles.

    Overnight: 'Amadeus Silver II' or 'Amadeus Brilliant'
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 7

    AMADEUS SILVER II

    Today, you get to choose from two wonderful excursions. Either set off on a walking tour in Linz and Passau and a full-day excursion to enchanting Salzburg, the birthplace of Mozart, including Salzkammergut, a spectacular region of alpine and subalpine lakes, picturesque valleys, rolling hills and rugged steep mountain ranges.

    Or you may opt for an exploration of the fascinating Ceský Krumlov in the Bohemian region of the Czech Republic.

    End you day with a gala dinner on board.

    Overnight: 'Amadeus Silver II' or 'Amadeus Brilliant'
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 8

    GERMANY

    Embark on an A&K-exclusive tour to Nuremberg, Germany, where you privately tour a World War II art bunker.

    Explore the city’s altstadt (old town) and the historic Imperial Castle. Continue to visit the Palace of Justice, site of the War Crimes Tribunal of 1946. Tour the city of Regensburg and later, return to the ship to enjoy the evening with a Bavarian-style celebration.

    Overnight: 'Amadeus Silver II' or 'Amadeus Brilliant'
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 9

    MUNICH / BAVARIA’S CHARMING CAPITAL

    End your cruise in Passau and embark on a drive to Munich for a walking tour of the old town exploring Marienplatz, Munich's most famous square in the heart of the Old Town and the majestic Frauenkirche, the city’s landmark cathedral.

    This evening, join your fellow guests for a farewell dinner.

    Overnight: Hotel Torbrau
    Meal plan: B, L, D

  • Day 10

    DEPART MUNICH

    Depart Munich

    Enjoy the breakfast and transfer to Munich airport for your onward flight.

    Meal plan: B



    NOTE: September 02, September 23 and October 17 departures run in reverse, Munich to Budapest.
    Note: Timings and pre-/post-cruise sightseeing may vary. Please contact A&K for details.
