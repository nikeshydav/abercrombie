Suggested Italy Accommodation

  • BORGO EGNAZIA

    Inspired by Apulia's rich traditions, yet thoroughly contemporary in its style, Borgo Egnazia is a true masterpiece of architecture where the main element is the typical white tufo stone. The narrow alleys of the Borgo Village are reproduced according to the traditions of the most characteristic Apulian historical centers with a magic stretch stolen by the most enchanting Arabian landscapes.

    The Restaurants, surrounded by different and always evocative landscapes and atmospheres, offer very high quality products and cuisine. A diverse menu based upon local produce, mainly home-grown follows the philosophy of the Mediterranean Diet. Children are well catered for with the Trullalleri kids club and Fichi d'India teens club offering a wide range of daily activities.

    Sporting activities on offer include two Tennis courts, a grass multiuse court and three swimming pools, one of them indoor. Cala Masciola Beach Club is located inside the property and features typical seaside low rocks.

    For those who want to discover the area and local customs enjoy a cooking class or hop on a horse or bicycle. Alternatively take to the water and sail along the coast or for those looking for more adventure why not partake in a bit of scuba diving, kiting or windsurfing?

    Considered one of the top golf courses in Italy, San Domenico Golf is an 18 hole championship course bordering the crystal azures of the Adriatic Sea. The course is spread over 6300 metres of lush land featuring undulating bermuda grass with sea views from every vantage point. Against a backdrop of the cobalt blue sky and the sea, the Clubhouse is situated a few metres from the Adriatic Coast and the ancient Roman City of Egnazia.

    GRAND HOTEL VILLA CORA

    Located on a tranquil hilltop position, the Grand Hotel Villa Cora reopened in 2010 after a detailed restoration When Baron Oppenheim built this handsome parkland villa in the late 19th-century he dedicated it to his wife. This romantic gesture echoes throughout the villa with its 46 rooms; 30 in the main villa and 14 in the stables plus Villino Eugenia the gatehouse. The interiors reflect the eclectic passions of the aristo and his belle - namely roses, the Orient, ornate parquet, and spectacular frescoes and statuary.

    Authentic period and antique pieces adorn both the halls of the Villa and the bedrooms. Explore the connecting rooms on the ground floor that lead you through a journey from the Salone degli Specci resembling a mini Versailles to the Sphynx room with its Moorish influences to the White room the perfect location to lounge.

    The gourmet restaurant is complimented by a wine cellar and bar. In the summer dining al fresco is the norm at Le Pool with Tuscan cuisine on offer. We particularly enjoy the tasting menu. In winter the indoor restaurant Il Pasha is your home for dinner. Take a table in a booth for added privacy. The spa is located under the gatehouse of the villa and is accessed via a tunnel from the villa itself. Sauna, Hammam, a gym and a selection of treatments are all available.

    The hotel's shuttle service will take you to the heart of the city and pick you up when you cannot walk anymore and return you to the peace of the villa and its gardens to enjoy a well-earned glass of Prosseco.

    HOTEL CASTELLO DI CASOLE

    Hotel Castello di Casole offers 41 suites in a range of styles and sizes to accommodate visitors in grand style. From junior suites in the main hotel building to apartment-style olive grove suites a short walk away, these enticing spaces have been painstakingly restored using original materials found on the estate. Authentic details - wood-beamed ceilings, stone walls and hand-plastered finishes - are gently coaxed into the modern era with luxurious fabrics, fixtures and furnishings, an alluring blend of old and new.

    In addition to the suites, choose from The Bargagli Penthouse - a highlight of the main hotel. A two-story, 3-bedroom masterpiece where every detail is a work of art. Families may be interested in renting one of the Hotel Villas just steps away or for a bit more privacy, the Farmhouses, set on hillsides throughout the 4,200-acre property. No matter the choice, one will enjoy the comforts of home, amazing views of the Tuscan hills and exceptional services.

    Castello di Casole has everything to make life in the countryside a delight. There's fine and casual dining, a luxurious spa, pools and fitness centers, and adventures throughout the estate during every season.

    Home to remarkable dining experiences, a collection of fine and casual dining venues serving traditional Italian cuisine with a twist. From a perfect cappuccino as the Tuscan sun sets, to a romantic picnic lunch in the vineyards, to dining al fresco with friends and family, food and drink are an essential part of everyday.

    The focus is on simple, rustic food in the classic Italian tradition. Expect handmade pastas, fresh organic vegetables, fresh fish and game from local sources - all paired with fine wines from the best wineries in Italy and around the world. Whether you choose to dine in your private residence, order room service at the hotel, take Italian cooking classes from master chefs, or visit one of the restaurants, every day in Tuscany stirs the soul in some new way.

    HOTEL DANIELI

    Of the three palaces that make up the hotel, the expertly restored 14th century Palazzo Dandolo was once the residence of the Doge Dandolo family. Murano glass chandeliers, ornately carved pink marble columns, stained glass, delicately woven rugs and dramatic high ceilings adorned with golden leaves frame the majestic staircase within the palace.

    During the summer, allow Hotel Danieli to offer an alternative to exploring the city by taking you to the islands of Venice Lido where guests can enjoy water sports, tennis, and golf along with privileged beach access.

    Hotel Danieli's 225 luxuriously decorated guest rooms and suites feature beautiful decorations and exquisite antique furnishings that reflect the rich cultural heritage of Venice.

    Indulge, while in an extraordinary setting, in a luxurious personalised in-room spa treatment.

    At the Hotel Danieli, you are surrounded by unique dining options. Enjoy an al fresco drink on the Bar Terrazza Danieli, experience afternoon tea at the Bar Dandolo surrounded by it's Gothic splendour or savour a delicious formal dinner at the rooftop Restaurant Terrazza Danieli. Whichever dining venue you choose, ranging from formal to casual, indoor to outdoor, they each incorporate Venice's extraordinary blend of history, artistic beauty, and worldly sophistication.

    HOTEL SPLENDIDO

    This Orient-Express hotel is situated on Northern Italy's Ligurian coast high above Portofino and the Mediterranean. For nearly a century the Splendido has been meeting the needs of a distinguished guest list, drawn by its high standards. The hotel has just the right blend of beauty, style, comfort and service. The Hotel Splendido has 64 beautiful rooms including 35 junior suites and suites.

    Leisure facilities on offer apart from the pool are the all-weather tennis court and the fitness centre both offering lessons.

    Enjoy the very best of fine Italian cuisine at Hotel Splendido's La Terrazza Restaurant, where you can enjoy magnificent views of the bay, or dine indoors in elegant splendour. For more relaxed, al fresco dining, opt for the Pool Restaurant, where the lunchtime salad buffet is legendary.

    The brand new Smile Club for chidren aged four to 12 years offers arts and crafts and scavenger hunts. The treatments on offer at the Wellness Centre are wide and varied and make use of the garden for massages - drift off among the flowers in a secluded corner of the terraced gardens as your aches and pains are expertly pummelled. Children can join in and enjoy Kid's Yoga or perhaps a family session is more to your taste.

    If you prefer to stay a little closer to the action, sister hotel Splendido Mare stands beside the renowned Piazzetta, close to the resort's lively quay. Hotel Splendido and Splendido Mare are connected by a scenic path, taking just 10 minutes to walk. A complimentary shuttle service will also whizz you between them.

    HOTEL SPLENDIDO MARE

    The Splendido Mare is located on Portofino's picturesque waterfront overlooking the Piazzetta in the heart of Portofino. The hotel is smaller than its sister hotel, the Hotel Splendido, with only 16 rooms and suites. All rooms are light, airy and elegant, and most offer balconies with superb views of the village, the harbour and the Castle of San Giorgio. The Junior suites all have terrace or balcony to enjoy your surroundings. The Ava Gardner Suite is the epitome of luxury. Occupying the entire top floor the magnificent terrace has the most stunning views.

    Splendido Mare shares all the facilities of the Hotel Splendido, including a spectacular open-air-heated pool and all-weather tennis courts. A scheduled shuttle bus and scenic path connects the two properties.

    The Chuflay Restaurant specializes in local and regional Italian dishes. Dine indoors or al fresco overlooking the piazzetta. Seek out the best vantage point from which to enjoy the jet-set bustle of Portofino harbour from the terrace.

    IL SALVIATINO

    The new, ultra luxurious Il Salviatino is a 14th Century villa which has been lovingly restored over the years. The villa façade gives way to a simply astonishing interior, where grand architectural touches like scrolled stone banisters, vaulted ceilings and sweeping staircases exist side by side with rustic-chic décor.

    The sumptuous suites and rooms are individually decorated, respecting the many particularities of the villa and its gardens. All are an elegant blend of local artisanship and the latest technologies. Handmade authentic Tuscan linen, lovingly selected artwork and the finest leathers abound, to create genuinely breath-taking accommodation.

    The sense of innovation does not stop with the accommodation itself, but also informs the service ethic. At Il Salviatino, service ambassadors cater for guests' every whim, instead of the more traditional reception or concierge.

    MASSERIA TORRE COCCARO

    Located down in the heel of Italy in the Apulia region the Masseria Torre Coccaro is a family-friendly boutique property. There is a wealth of secluded spots to enjoy that are dotted around the property: the old vegetable garden, the olive groves, the walking path through the olive groves to Maizza, the Olive Mill set in an ancient cave, the old haylofts with fireplaces there is so much to explore and enjoy.

    Whether you prefer to relax by the outdoor heated pool or head to the private sandy beach the activities on offer are second to none. Enjoy horse rides along the beach or through the countryside. Accompany the chef to the kitchen garden and choose your delights for your use in the cooking school. Children can enjoy the mini-club and beach volleyball. Ensure you take a trip on the private motor yacht either to Polignano a Mare to the sea caves or the antique port of Monopoli for diving and swimming in the secluded bays.

    The Aveda Spa is resplendent in natural materials with views of the olive groves. There is a gym, indoor pool, whirpool and Turkish bath with eucalyptus oil. Treatments on offer make use of the organic produce from the Masseria and are tailored to your specific need.

    The Masseria is proud of its zero kilometre 'food miles' philosophy whereby the collaboration with local farms ensure that the best produce is sourced. Cheese and meat are a large part of the local produce coupled with wild offerings such as porcini mushrooms, wild asparagus, snails and wood berries. Fish of the day is sourced from the nearby village of Savalletri. All fruit and vegetables are grown on site in the botanical garden.

    The produce can be sampled in four restaurants at the Masseria. Restaurant Egnathia serving Mediterranean cuisine under a vaulted ceiling is located in the old stables that date back to 1600. Enjoy dinner on the terrace under the magnificent bouganivillae and jasmine heavy pergola taking in the countryside views. A light lunch is served at Cabana by the pool. Le Palme is situated at the adjacent property Maizza and serves creative cuisine overlooking the pool. Head to the Coccaro Beach Club, the exclusive sea restaurant and sample delicacies in the sushi and oyster bar.
