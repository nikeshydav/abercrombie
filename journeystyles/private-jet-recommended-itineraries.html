Africa: Across a Continent by Private Jet
With Special Guest Geoffrey Kent

March 2-20, 2014 | Limited to 40 guests
$79,995 per person, double occupancy | Single supplement $14,995

 

Immerse yourself in the wonders of a continent on an inspiring journey by private jet, led by the company with an unrivaled on-the-ground presence in Africa.

Africa: Across a Continent by Private Jet takes you to seven countries in 19 days, revealing destinations as diverse as the Serengeti, Victoria Falls and ancient Ethiopia at their most exclusive and authentic. Travel in comfort aboard a privately chartered Boeing 737 with a small party of just 40 guests, landing at airports where larger commercial flights can't go and arriving and departing on your own schedule. Thanks to A&K's 11 African offices and longstanding local connections, your itinerary is packed with unique encounters and insider-access opportunities - none more remarkable than the chance to spend three days in the Serengeti on safari with Geoffrey Kent, A&K's founder and CEO.

Africa: Across a Continent by Private Jet Highlights


Ethiopia | Ancient Churches

March 3-4 (2 nts.)

Explore the remarkable rock-carved churches of Lalibela. Meet with local dignitaries at a private cocktail reception.

Uganda | Mountain Gorillas

March 5-7 (3 nts.)

Come face to face with the endangered mountain gorilla. Meet the local Batwa tribe and discover their way of life.

Tanzania | On Safari with Geoffrey Kent

March 8-10 (3 nts.)

Search for game in the company of A&K founder Geoffrey Kent. Witness a Maasai tribal dance. Meet researchers helping to conserve the cheetah.

Zambia | Victoria Falls

March 11-13 (3 nts.)

View Victoria Falls on foot and from a helicopter. Learn the local ways at Nakatindi Village. Pay an up-close visit to habituated elephants.

Botswana | Wildlife Along the River

March 14-16 (3 nts.)

Search for wildlife in Africa's most elephant-rich park. Cruise the Chobe River at sunset.

Namibia | Desert Dunes

March 17-18 (2 nts.)

Cruise Walvis Bay on a private boat. Ride the desert dunes in an all-terrain vehicle. Dine under the desert stars.

South Africa | The Finale

March 19-20 (1 nt.)

Take in the gorgeous views from atop Table Mountain. Gather for a gala farewell dinner at Cape Town's Two Oceans Aquarium.
