Irrwaddy Princess II

The Irrwaddy Princess II has been designed to access the shallow parts of the fascinating Irrwaddyriver. A cruise on this famous river is The facilities on board include a spacious dining room, a bar, solarium, a spa room and a sun deck area. The three-deck vessel has a total of 20 cabins, all with en-suite facilities, air-conditioning and a mini bar. The experienced chefs create a mix of local, Chinese and Western menus to delight guests on board. The riverboat drifts along the majestic Irrawaddy River affording unforgettable views and providing chances to visit small villages untouched by the modern world.

HIGHLIGHTS

  • A traditional Myanmar riverboat
  • 20 en suite cabins complete with air conditioning
  • Weekly departures
  • Cruise between Mandalay and Bagan on the famous Irrwaddy River
  • A selection of one and two night journeys are available
