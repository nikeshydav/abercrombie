For over 40 years, Abercrombie & Kent has been setting the standards for luxury world travel by providing our clients with unparalleled service. In 1982, we brought this experience to Egypt to share the 7,000 years of history, distinctive atmosphere and remarkable sights with the luxury traveller.

We will prepare for you a holiday in which the bustle of Cairo and its awe-inspiring pyramids are perfectly matched by the supreme relaxation of a romantic Nile cruise - the natural vantage point for visiting temples and tombs in Upper Egypt. In Sinai and on the Red Sea, we have chosen the finest resorts surpassed only by the pristine sandy beaches and excellent diving and snorkelling. For those with a love of adventure, we can offer invigorating activities such as quadrunning and camel trekking in the desert.

We currently have nine offices throughout Egypt, with over 400 full time staff including airport representatives, drivers and Egyptologist guides. Our pride and joy are our three Nile cruise boats, as well our own vehicles. As a destination management company, we provide a diverse range of services for many different types of business, including FITs, Tour Series, Ad-Hoc Groups, Incentive Travel, Special Interest Groups and Conferences and Events.
