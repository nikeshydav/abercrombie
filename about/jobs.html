Abercrombie & Kent is the world's leading luxury tour operator with over 2,000 staff and over 50 offices worldwide. We are committed to being the market leader in luxury travel through innovation, excellent product and exemplary customer service. Share your passion for travel by embarking on a career journey with the world's leading tour operator Abercrombie & Kent.

A&K Travel Specialists

We believe travel should stimulate and inspire and our employees must be able and willing to inspire our clients with travel expertise while providing impeccable service. Our Travel Consultants are encouraged to develop knowledge of their region of specialisation with familiarisation trips, in house training from colleagues and also training from our fellow A&K employees working in destinations around the globe. Because we have our own people on the ground we can access areas that only the locals know about.

Our team in India creates tailor made itineraries to:
  • Africa
  • North Africa
  • Arabia
  • North America
  • Orient
  • Antarctica
  • Europe
  • Latin America
  • Australasia & South Pacific

Why join A&K?

A career with A&K means joining a worldwide family, it means joining a company that truly makes a difference. A&K people are special, they contribute to the success of a company that continues to evolve and grow, they are proud to work for a company that has heritage and yet is constantly innovating, that is worldwide and yet local and is dedicated to exceeding every client's expectations.

Whether you work as a travel specialist or an expert in marketing, technology, sales or administrative functions, we invite you to consider becoming part of the Abercrombie & Kent family and sharing your enthusiasm for travel with us. Our specialists combine passion for travel with excellence, integrity and responsibility for our guests, our products and our suppliers. To secure experts of this calibre, we seek a special blend of professionalism and knowledge, patience and understanding. We value the information provided by our guests and those we come in professional contact with and consider it an opportunity to learn.
