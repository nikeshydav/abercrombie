Makgadikgadi Pans

The Kalahari Desert offers the spirituality of the Makgadikgadi Pans and the humbling experience of spending time with the nomads of Kalahari. The Makgadikgadi Pans are the surreal remains of the world's largest super lake next to which is the Makgadikgadi Pans National Park, a grasslands area attracting thousands of animals after the rains.

Antarctic Adventure

Tour operator Abercrombie & kent is offering four expeditions to Antarctica aboard luxury vessel MV Le Boreal. With a team of elite and highly-qualified naturalists at the helm of the operations, you can be sure that your trip will be informative, educational and enjoyable.

Travel Hot List 2012

The Amazon offers an unforgettable wilderness experience. It is the longest river in the world (7000km) and at the heart of a rainforest which covers over a billion acres. In peru, the Amazon represents 60% of the entire country and is a must-see destination rest experienced on a luxury cruise aboard MV Aqua or MV Aria where naturalist guides point out the extraordinary wildlife and natural phenomena of the area.
